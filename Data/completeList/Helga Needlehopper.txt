'''Helga Needlehopper''' is a sweet, yet very dangerous little girl who was taken care of by [[Donald Duck]] in the ''[[Quack Pack]]'' episode "[[Take My Duck, Please]]".
In the episode, Donald gets a prim and proper girl in exchange and the boys get to have fun. But when Donald finds out that she's not the sweet angel the ads make her, the situation turns awkward around the house.
