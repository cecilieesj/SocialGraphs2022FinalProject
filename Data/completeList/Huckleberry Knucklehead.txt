'''Huckleberry Knucklehead''' is a character in [[Wander Over Yonder]]. He is a giant baby that first appeared in "[[The Toddler]]". He is voiced by Owen Faust, the nephew of producer and story editor [[Lauren Faust]].

== Background ==
He is a giant pink baby whose parents are two tiny bugs who accidentally lose him in a mall.
