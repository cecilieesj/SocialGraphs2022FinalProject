'''Christopher Robin's Babysitter (Tammy)''' is rarely seen, as her face is usually never shown, so little is known about her personality. She first appeared in the episode, "[[Babysitter Blues]]". She is played by Jackie Gonneau.

== Appearance ==
Christopher Robin's Babysitter (Tammy) is a ginger girl who has a ponytail, who wears a yellow sweater, a teal skirt, and some teal shoes.
