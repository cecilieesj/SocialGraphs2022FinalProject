'''Carolyn "Mouse" Rose Adrienne Smith'''<ref>[[Pilot (Big Shot)]]</ref> is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]''. She is currently a student at Westbrook School for Girls. She is the Point Guard on the Westbrook School Basketball Team.

==Background==
===Personality===
Carolyn is smart, loyal, kind, friendly with a heart of gold. She tends to be violent since she suggested she and the other girls take out Ms. Grint, advanced towards Kelsey to attack her after she insulted Louise, suggested they beat Carlsbad up in the night, and said not even their own mothers will be able to identify them after they beat Carlsbad.

==Trivia==

*Her and [[Destiny Winters]] are the only characters so far whose middle name has been revealed.
*She has two middle names "Rose" and "Adrienne".
* Her nickname is "Mouse" because she is the smallest and youngest player on the Basketball Team.<ref>[https://meaww.com/big-shot-star-tisha-custodio-carolyn-mouse-intimidated-john-stamos-at-first-shared-music-interest 'Big Shot' star Tisha Custodio says she was 'intimidated' by John Stamos at first, but music helped them bond | MEAWW]</ref>
*She is an Honor Student.
*Her Jersey Number is 10.
*She loves Sushi.<ref>TCKS</ref>
*It was revealed in TCKS she has a crush on Harper Schapira and her friends on the Westbrook School Basketball Team know about it. That proves she is lesbian.
*Her parents want her to get into West Point because they are in the Military.<ref>This is Our House</ref>
