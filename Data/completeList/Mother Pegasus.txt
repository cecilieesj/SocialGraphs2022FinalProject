|status = Active}}'''Mother Pegasus''' is one of the main protagonists in the [[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]] segment of the [[1940]] film ''[[Fantasia]]''.

==Role in the film==
Mother Pegasus is shown to be a patient and kind mother toward her family. When [[Peter Pegasus]] tries to fly on his own and falls to the ground, she quickly flies to his side and helps him up again. She holds him up in the air as he learns how to fly. When [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]] sends a thunderstorm down from [[Mount Olympus]], she quickly leads her foals back to their nest and shelters them from the rain beneath her wings. During nighttime, she sleeps with them in the same tree where her husband rests on the higher branch.
