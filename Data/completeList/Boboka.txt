==Role in the Series==
===''[[Beshte and the Hippo Lanes]]''===
Along with her son, and the other members of her herd, Boboka stands in water, <span>which has flooded their grazing grounds from a previous storm.Â </span>

The Lion Guard arrives, and attempts to ask them to leave, but Bupu stubbornly refuses.

Boboka requests the chance to get her child out of the water, and Bupu relents.

After some delay, they make it to the Flood Plains, where Beshte has created hippo lanes. Boboka and her son are the first to enter the Flood Plains.

Ono informs the group of a slight mishap, and Kion and Ono go into the Flood Plains to help Boboka and her son.
