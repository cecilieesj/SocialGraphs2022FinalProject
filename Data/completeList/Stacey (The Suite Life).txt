'''Stacey''' is a minor character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's original series]] ''[[The Suite Life of Zack & Cody]]''.

==History==
She is a beautiful blonde girl that stayed at [[Tipton Hotel]] in the episode "[[Sleepover Suite]]". [[Cody Martin|Cody]] has a crush on Stacey.
