'''Savannah Grace O'Neal''' (played by [[Mary Mouser]]) is one of the main characters in the film, ''[[Frenemies (2012 film)|Frenemies]]''.

== History ==
It's a girl who loves skateboarding. She has three brothers and lives with her father. She gave refuge to Murray when he fled from the house of [[Jake Logan|Jake]], who she likes, and found that [[Julianne Bryan|Julianne]] was taking advantage of Jake and two other boys to do their homework. While she went shopping she found a girl like her, [[Emma Reynolds]], with whom she exchanged to live a life of the other.
