'''Vincent Callisto''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. He is the paternal grandfather of [[Miles Callisto|Miles]] and [[Loretta Callisto]], as well as a retired captain of the Tomorrowland Transit Authority. He was known in his younger days for confidently throwing himself into his missions and he continues to live a thrilling life. Vincent, [[Gong Gong and Po Po]] all served together on the same ship, ''The Explorer'', before it was lost in ice.

==Trivia==
*Vincent's voice is provided by [[Jonathan Frakes]], who is best known for his role as [[w:c:memory-alpha:William T. Riker|Commander Riker]] in ''[[w:c:memory-alpha:Star Trek: The Next Generation|Star Trek: The Next Generation]]''.
