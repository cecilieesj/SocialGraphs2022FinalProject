'''Kragthar '''is a minor antagonist from ''[[Wander Over Yonder]]''.

==History==
He is first seen in "[[The Fancy Party]]" with other villains only to be thrown out after unintentionally insulting the Queen.

He was seen again in "[[The Battle Royale]]" with other villains fighting for a ring of power (which turned out to be a candy ring set up by Wander).

In "[[My Fair Hatey]]", he is captured by [[Lord Dominator]] with many other villains.

It was revealed in "[[The Bad Neighbors]]" that he along with other villains somehow escaped and joins Lord Hater at his neighborhood barbecue.
