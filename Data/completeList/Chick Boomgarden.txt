'''Chick Boomgarden'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/boomgarden.htm</ref> is a minor character and antagonist from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
Chick Boomgarden is the owner of Boomgarden Electronics across the road at Gramp's shop, [[Canal Street Electronics]], and his archrival.
