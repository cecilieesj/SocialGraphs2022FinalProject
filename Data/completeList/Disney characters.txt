The '''Disney Characters''' are characters created, developed, and/or adapted by [[Walt Disney]] and others at [[The Walt Disney Company]]. Disney characters are the most popular and recognizable set of characters in the world, with [[Mickey Mouse]] standing as the most famous and as Disney's mascot. Some of the other most well-known Disney characters range from films such as, [[The Lion King]], ''[[The Little Mermaid]]'', ''[[The Jungle Book]]'', ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]'', ''[[Mary Poppins]]'', ''[[The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh|Winnie the Pooh]]'' and more.
