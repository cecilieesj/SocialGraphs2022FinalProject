'''Spin''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Lab Rats]]''. He is one of the bionic soldiers that made up Victor Krane's bionic army. He picked the name Spin for himself because of his ability to spin like a cyclone, a bionic ability that resembles a spinning top.

He is portrayed by [[Max Charles]].
