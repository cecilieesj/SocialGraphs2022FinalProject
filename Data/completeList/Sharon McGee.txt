'''Sharon McGee''' is a recurring character in the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Sharon is a middle-aged Thai-American woman with a stout physique. She has brown hair and wears it in a loose bun, and dark brown almond-shaped eyes. Her attire includes a blue green jacket and a white T-shirt under it, along with blue jeans. She also wears a yellow necklace.

==Trivia==
*Despite having a name, the credits simply list her as "Mom".
*In the episode "[[The Best of Nin-tensions]]", Sharon and Pete revealed they eloped and did not have a traditional wedding.
*Sharon was originally going to have a beauty mark below her right eye just like [[Molly McGee|Molly]].
*Sharon was originally going to be named Janice and would have been African-American rather than Thai-American.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pe1-XelDNtQ</ref>
