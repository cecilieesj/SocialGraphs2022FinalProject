'''Henry Andrew "Ham" Mack''' was a recurring character on the [[Disney Channel]] television show ''[[Andi Mack]]''. He was portrayed by Stoney Westmoreland.

Ham is [[Andi Mack (character)|Andi]]'s grandfather/adoptive father and the lone male in the Mack household. He is overjoyed that his family has fully reunited.
