==Role in the series==
Doc Hogg is the kindly country doctor on ''Bear in the Big Blue House''. He's fond of conversations (although he has a tendency to finish other peoples' sentences) and pie. He's also fond of jokes based on wordplay. [[Tutter]] seems somewhat in awe of him and refers to him as "Doctor Hogg."

Doc Hogg is also fond of social games, such as card games and chess. As a social animal and the community physician, he's friends with most of the residents in Woodland Valley, including [[Bear (Bear in the Big Blue House)|Bear]] and [[Jeremiah Tortoise]]. He first appeared in [[The Big Blue Housecall]] when he came over to give everyone a checkup.
