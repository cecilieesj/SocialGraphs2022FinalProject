'''Large Marvin''' is the fattest [[w:c:muppet:Fraggles|Fraggle]] in ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Fraggle_Rock_(location) Fraggle Rock]'', due to his love of snacking. He was performed by [[Dave Goelz]]. He is a bit dimwitted, but is also athletic and a feared swimming opponent of [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Red_Fraggle Red Fraggle].

His first appearance was in the ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Fraggle_Rock Fraggle Rock]'' episode "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_123:_The_Beast_of_Blue_Rock The Beast of Blue Rock]."

Large Marvin Fraggle made a non-speaking cameo in ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/A_Muppet_Family_Christmas A Muppet Family Christmas]'' during the "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Pass_It_On Pass It On]" number.

In ''Muppets Tonight'', Large Marvin was later seen as an audience member.
