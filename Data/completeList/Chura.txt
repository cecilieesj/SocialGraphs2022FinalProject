'''Chura''' is a character that appears in ''[[The Lion Guard]]''. 

== Background Information ==

=== Personality ===
Much like Mtoto, Chura deeply respects and admires [[Beshte]], and is quick to defend the young hippo against the uncertainty of others.

== Role in the Series ==

=== "[[Beshte and the Hippo Lanes]]" ===
When Beshte, the strongest hippo in the Pride Lands, and a member of the [[Lion Guard]] is tasked with creating the hippo lanes, he is joined by several animals, Chura amongst them.

After a young aardvark questions his capabilities, Chura defends the hippo, and comforts Beshte.

Upon noticing Beshte heading in the opposite direction of his destination, Chura kindly corrects him, and once Beshte drops him off, thanks Beshte for the ride.
