==Personality==
Sigourney starts out as a very mean woman. She is vain, greedy, lazy, and tends to be snarky toward Ellen, she is also idiotic, hypocritical, insensitive, and has a very arrogant attitude. She mentioned she bullied a pregnant lady who started crying as a result.

However, it turns out, she envies Ellen's life because she has a family. She pretended she threw her back out to be her family, she even broke down crying, implying she was faking.

==Trivia==
*She is the second member to be introduced, followed by [[Nora James]].
*According to Stan, she is hyper critical, insensitive, and "better than you", and that's from reading her online dating profile.
