|children = [[Ian Calloway]] (son)}}'''Gloria Calloway''' is [[Ian Calloway|Ian]]'s mother in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Role in the series==
Gloria was introduced when Ian found Heather's ad about giving Stan's puppies, Freddy and Gracie, away while he was staying with here in Glendale.

Gloria thought Ian was crazy when he thought Stan was a talking dog. She believed it was all the cartoons but Ian made a deal he could watch them when he eats his brussel sprouts.

Bennett and Ellen thought up a way to get rid of him because they thought he was crazy, so while Bennett distracted him, Gloria brought her to their house and told her son leave the family and their dogs.

==Trivia==
*She thinks Ian is crazy.
*Ellen wished Gloria could be her mother.
*Ian made a deal about watching cartoons by agreeing to eat his brussel sprouts.
*She lives in Glendale, possibly in California.
*She may be a widow.
