'''Frank''' and '''Esther Exposition''' are supporting characters in [[Disney Junior]]'s [[2017]] television series, ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]''.

==Background==
===Personalities===
Frank and Esther are constantly seen doing various activities around the world. They somehow are always wherever [[Bingo (Puppy Dog Pals)|Bingo]] and [[Rolly (Puppy Dog Pals)|Rolly]] travel to.
