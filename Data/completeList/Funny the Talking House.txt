'''Funny''', a blue talking funhouse, is the titular main location and [[The Sensational Six]]’s hangout/place/friend in ''[[Mickey Mouse Funhouse]]''.

==Personality==
Funny is a fun loving adventurous talking funhouse, who helps [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] and the gang on amazing adventures. As his name suggests, he is funny, caring, happy, and clever.

==Physical Appearance==
Funny is a house with blue coloring on the face, a red roof, and yellow lining. He has the ability to transform himself into anything when taking others on adventures.

==Trivia==
*In "[[Homesick]]", it is revealed that Funny is allergic to sunflowers.
