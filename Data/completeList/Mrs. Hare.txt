'''Mrs. Hare''' is the mother of [[Thumper]] and [[Thumper's sisters|his sisters]] and a friend of [[Bambi's mother]]. In the [[Bambi (film)|first movie]] she appeared for two brief times, during each of those times making [[Thumper]] say a quote of his father, [[Mr. Hare]]. In the [[Bambi II|sequel]] she pretty much has the same role as the first film, only once this time. She also appeared in the Disney book series Disney Bunnies but is still not given a name.
