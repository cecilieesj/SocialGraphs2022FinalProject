==History==
He despises dogs due one bit off half his thumb in fourth grade. His cat, Tallulah knows that Scott is really a dog by always hissing and scratching whenever he is near. He will not rest until he has made Fala D. Roosevelt the best school in EastWestland. He is constantly trying to avoid lawsuits. He is a strict but fair administrator. He also is revealed to be odd as he suffers from chapped lips, has a high addiction to sugar, uses pink lemonade lip gloss, and secretly wears bikini briefs. He also might be smitten with Mrs. Helperman.

==Trivia==
*Strickler shares the many traits, occupation, qualities, and voice actor of [[Principal Mazur]] in ''[[A Goofy Movie]]''.
*He was voiced by Wallace Shawn who is also known for doing [[Rex]] in the ''[[Toy Story ]]'' Films
