'''Anya Corazon''' (also known as '''Spider-Girl''') is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]'', voiced by [[Melanie Minichino]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]''===
Anya is a student at Horizon High along with [[Spider-Gwen|Gwen Stacy]], [[Miles Morales]] and [[Spider-Man|Peter Parker]]. Anya is a tomboyish girl obsessed with science and gives Peter a lot of flak (for being late). She is super smart, intellectual and a bit of a know-it-all. Anya takes her studies real seriously. She is shown to be very protective of her inventions and their true functions like the time she was mad at Peter Parker for re-purposing her Vibranium machine to put out a fire that her machine started. In spite of that, she does care about others, including her best friend Gwen Stacy.

She developed a scrambler that can cut the power to anything mechanical and, with it, she helped Spider-Man destroy the [[Spider-Slayers|Spider-Slayer]].
