'''Beep and Priscilla''' are [[Camilla]]'s fellow chicks and [[Miss Nanny]]'s pets. Beep is a yellow chicken and Priscilla is a brown chicken.

==Description==
Introduced in the 2018 series, Beep and Priscilla are round-as-a-ball, real baby chickens who are brown and yellow. Resembling Camilla, the biggest of the three multi-colored chicks who live in the backyard chicken coop, medium-sized Priscilla talks in clucks, and Beep, as the name implies, makes the sound of a car horn.
