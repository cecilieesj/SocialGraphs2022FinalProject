'''Greg''' is the protagonist of the [[2020]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]•[[Pixar]] short film ''[[Out]]''. He is a gay man who is struggling with coming out to his parents.

==Background==
===Personality===
Greg is usually a well-adjusted young man with a very caring heart and personality. However, he is also unsure of being open with those close to him. Based on what his mother says, he used to be very open with his parents and tell them everything. It is heavily implied that upon him coming to terms with his homosexuality, that he started to retreat somewhat. It is only through the intervention of the cosmic animals that he is finally able to confront his feelings and emotions to his parents.

===Physical appearance===
Greg is a tall Caucasian man with reddish-brown hair and a beard. He wears a yellow flannel shirt and denim jeans for much of the short. Accompanying his look is a blue ushanka that he apparently loves to wear.

==Trivia==
*Greg is the first openly gay protagonist in a Pixar film and, for that matter, the first openly gay protagonist in a Disney production.
**He is the second LGBT protagonist in Disney media, after [[Luz Noceda]] from ''[[The Owl House]]''.
**He is also the second LGBT character in Pixar films, after [[Specter]] from ''[[Onward]]''.
