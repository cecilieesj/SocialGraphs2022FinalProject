==Role in the film==
Cliff is the Sheriff of Briarville. He punishes for whatever Kenny plans with [[Ernest P. Worrell|Ernest]]. He does not believe in trolls until [[Trantor]] and other trolls attack Briarville's citizens near the tree house. Kenny eventually becomes one of Trantor's wooden dolls after the trolls disappeared forever when Ernest, [[Mike Murdock]], and other children either squirted or poured milk at them. After Ernest defeats Trantor, he and Amanda reunite with Kenny who is a normal person once again.
