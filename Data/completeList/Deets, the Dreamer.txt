Originally named Sarah, she once worked for Bedlam because he captured her parents, then Ol' Skool helped her change and save them both. She wears a pink costume flies with a jet glider pack. Deets seems to have a crush on Ed, and the two have kissed several times throughout the series.

Deets is the one on the left where the Jetix sign is.

She is younger than Loogie and Burn, but older than Fizz and Ed.
==Trivia==
*She is voiced by Megan Fahlenbrock
*She is the tallest member
