'''Dot''', a.k.a '''Agent D''', (full name "'''Dot the Disguise Gal'''") is a constant character in ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force]'' for Nintendo DS. You meet her in the first mission and she guides you along the rest of the game. She is an [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Elite_Penguin_Force_Agent Elite Penguin Force Agent]. She wears the color [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Purple Purple] and wears a blond [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Wigs wig]. She always wears a lilac coat. She is really good in her disguises, as her name says. She appears disguised in two missions: in the first mission, she was hiding in many disguises such as a mailbox, [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Inner_Tube Inner Tubes] and a barrel. In the second one, she is disguised as a snowman, because she wanted to catch the Snowman Thief, which didn't exist. In ''Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force'' for Nintendo DS, Dot gives you the key to the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffle_Training_Room Puffle Training Room] when you use a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Pin pin] to solve a puzzle after you meet her in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Command_Room Command Room] which comes after collecting all the map pieces. Dot is also seen in the mission called [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/The_Veggie_Villain The Veggie Villain], which was her first online appearance. In the Mission, she appears coming out of the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Command_Room Command Room] in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/HQ HQ] after the color puzzle on the door is completed. In the second game, Dot appears as more as a friend to the player, in comparison to the original where she is a mentor. Dot seemed to completely change her clothes in "[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/The_Veggie_Villain The Veggie Villain]" and ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force:_Herbert%27s_Revenge Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force: Herbert's Revenge]''.One other thing to note is that there was a Beta tester named Dot but since Club Penguin updated, Dot's name got changed to her id which allowed Club Penguin to use Dot as a character on [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin Club Penguin].
