[[File:Herbert the Goblin.jpg|thumb|238px]]'''Herbert'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/herbert.htm</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0388163/?ref_=tt_cl_t5</ref> is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. He appears in the episode "[[Dragon Breath]]". He is voiced by [[John DiMaggio]].<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0224007/</ref>

==History==
Herbert is a Goblin with a lizard-like tail, purple hair, and a keen sense of smell. He has an Ogre with horns and caught Fu. [[Fu Dog]] was going to pay the dog biscuits back to Herbert, but Fu isn't early, so Herbert got revenge on Fu. [[Jake Long|Jake]] turns into the American Dragon and beats up the Horned Ogre. Herbert ended up getting grabbed by Jake and was thrown with his Ogre.<ref>[[Dragon Breath]]</ref> Herbert later appears in "Bodyguard Duty". He and his army of goblins kidnapped Sara and Kara. Herbert was defeated by fish dump which is the worst smell to goblins. Herbert kidnaps [[Oracle Twins|Kara and Sara]], but Herbert ends up crashing into a bunch of Ogres.
