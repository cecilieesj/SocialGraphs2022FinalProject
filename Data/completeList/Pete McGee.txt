'''Peter "Pete" McGee''' is a recurring character in the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Pete is a middle aged man who is taller than the rest of his family. He has messy red hair and facial hair. His attire includes a green sweater over a white button down shirt. He wears blue pants and has a watch on his right arm.

==Trivia==
*Despite having a name, the credits simply list him as "Dad".
*He bares a slight resemblance to his voice actor, Jordan Klepper.
