'''Thundra''' is a recurring character from ''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin: The Series]]''. She is a rain bird who controls the world's weather and [[Iago]]'s love interest.

==Background==
===Personality===
Thundra is a feisty, hardworking rain bird who is in charge of the rain forest. She speaks with a Spanish accent and frequently says a number of Spanish phrases.

===Physical appearance===
Thundra is a green rain bird with a golden hat with green hair at the top, a gold necklace, and a yellow tail that makes a rainbow when she flies.
