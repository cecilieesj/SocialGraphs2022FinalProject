'''Daimon Helstrom''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in, ''[[Helstrom]]''. He is a professor of ethics who moonlights as a demon hunter. In his battle against a hidden world, Daimon is determined to root out demons as they arise, and will not stop until they’re vanquished. He is portrayed by Tom Austen.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.denofgeek.com/us/tv/283715/marvel-helstrom-hulu|title=Marvel’s Helstrom TV series reveals its cast|website=Den of Geek|date=[[October 8]], [[2019]]}}</ref>
Daimon Hellstrom was created by Roy Thomas and Gary Friedrich.
