'''Dok-Ondar''' is a character at ''[[Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge]]'' that first appeared in [[Star Wars (Marvel Comics)|''Star Wars'' Marvel Comics]].

==Background==
Dok-Ondar is an Ithorian alien who is a collector and shopkeeper. He runs a shop known as Dok-Ondar's Den of Antiquities in the Black Spire Outpost on the planet of [[Batuu]]. He is known for his love of collecting, having started during his childhood with rocks and other uncommon items he could found. Unfortunately, his parents were mysteriously murdered after they found artifacts that contained secrets of the [[Sith]] and the [[Jedi]].  

He is known to have prominent connections throughout the galaxy, particularly to criminals such as the Crimson Dawn, [[Jabba the Hutt]], [[Han Solo]], and [[Aphra|Doctor Aphra]].

==Film Appearances==
===''[[Solo]]''===
In this film, Dok-Ondar is mentioned by [[Qi'ra]]. During a party held by the Crimson Dawn syndicate, Qi'ra refers to him as someone who is needed to be well treated.
