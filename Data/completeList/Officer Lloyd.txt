|status = Defeated}}'''Officer Lloyd''' is an animal control officer and a minor antagonist in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2008]] animated feature film, ''[[Bolt (film)|Bolt]]''.

==Role in the film==
When [[Bolt (character)|Bolt]] and [[Rhino]] sneak into the shelter to free [[Mittens]], the trio attempt to escape afterwards, but Lloyd tries to intercept Bolt, Rhino, and Mittens by blocking the exit. Rhino then comes in at high speed in his plastic ball and hits Lloyd in the eye, causing him to collapse to the ground unconscious. When [[Officer Ester|Ester]] later gets back, she notices an unconscious Lloyd and accuses him of destroying her truck in an explosion.
