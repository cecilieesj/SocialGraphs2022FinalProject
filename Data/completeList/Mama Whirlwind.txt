'''Mama Whirlwind''' is a full-sized tornado who comes to the rescue when [[Little Whirlwind|her child]] is being chased by [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] in the [[1941]] animated short, ''[[The Little Whirlwind]]''.

==Trivia==
* Much of the animation of Mama Whirlwind is reused from ''[[The Band Concert]]''.
