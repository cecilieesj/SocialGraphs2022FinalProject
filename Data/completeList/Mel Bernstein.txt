[[File:Mel Bernstein.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Mel Bernstein''' ([[Mary Birdsong]]) is the mother of Wyatt, [[Amanda Bernstein|Amanda]], [[Cleo Bernstein|Cleo]], and Jasmine. She hates [[Crash]] and even threatens to destroy him, and turn him into an ugly purse, but she won't because of Wyatt. She calls Wyatt, "WyGuy" in ''[[Crash & Bernstein]]''. She would do anything for her little "WyGuy."
