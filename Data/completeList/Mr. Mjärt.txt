He's the owner of the [[Mjärt Mart]] and [[Pickle]] and [[Peanut]]'s boss.
==Background==
Originally from the "Old Country", Mr. Mjärt escaped to America to prevent himself from getting eaten by a puma -- which has happened to his family for generations.
