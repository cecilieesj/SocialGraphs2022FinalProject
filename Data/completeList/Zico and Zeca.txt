'''Zico and Zeca''' are two parrot twins who are the nephews of [[José Carioca]]. They were created in [[1962]] as replacements for [[Morty and Ferdie Fieldmouse]] in [[Mickey Mouse]] comics stories that were adapted to star José at the time.
