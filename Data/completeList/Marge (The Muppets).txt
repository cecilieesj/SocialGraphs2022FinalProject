[[File:Marge_waitress.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Marge''' is a waitress working at Mel's Drive-In in [[Hollywood]] in ''[[The Muppets (film)|The Muppets]]''. She was played by [[Aria Curzon|Aria Noelle Curzon]].

When [[Mary (The Muppets)|Mary]] spends a day alone in the city sightseeing, she stops to eat and asks for a table for one. Confronting her fears about being on her own, she sings "[[Me Party]]" in the middle of the restaurant. The song ends when Marge asks her if she's expecting anyone else.
