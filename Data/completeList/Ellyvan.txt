|alignment = Good}}'''Ellyvan''' is one of the main characters and the best friend of [[Zooter]] from the [[Playhouse Disney]] (now [[Disney Junior]]) show ''[[Jungle Junction]]''. He is voiced by [[Billy West]].

==Biography==
Ellyvan carries the deliveries around the jungle. His name is a compound word of "elephant" and "van." He is able to suck improbable quantities of liquids up his trunk and spray them distances of up to a half a mile. He lives in the base of a big hollow tree some distance out of town. Ellyvan is very naive, and throws himself into any situation, especially if there is food involved. Ellyvan is a cheery and optimistic character, and rarely becomes upset.

==Appearance==
Ellyvan is a blue elephant with a US accent. Ellyvan is the biggest animal in the jungle and Evelyn's boyfriend.
