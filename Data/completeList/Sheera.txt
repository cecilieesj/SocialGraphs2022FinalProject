
[[File:Sheera_looks.png|thumb]]'''Sheera''' is a baby lamb introduced in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode "[[Shear Madness]]".

==Appearance==
She is a white colored lamb with a pink nose and green eyes. She wears a purple collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol on it and a white diaper with purple trim and patterned with barns and corn cobs. At the beginning, she looks like the other lambs on the show, but with a tuft of wool covering her eyes and then is sheered down to what she looks like the thoughtout the entire episode.

==Trivia==
*She was voiced by Charlie Townsend, who also voiced [[Mia (T.O.T.S.)|Mia the Kitten]] and [[Octivia|Octivia the Octopus]].
