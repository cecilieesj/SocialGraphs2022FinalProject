[[File:Maid_Marzipan.png|thumb|Maid Marzipan]]'''Maid Marzipan''' is a character appearing in ''[[The 7D]]'' season 2 episode "[[Nicely Done & The 7D]]". She is known for making delicious marzipan, which is enjoyed by The 7D, [[Nicely Done]] and His Really Nice Guys and the [[Sheriff Of Plottingham]] and she is voiced by [[Tress MacNeille]].{{Infobox_character}}
