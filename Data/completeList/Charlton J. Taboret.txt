|status = Active}}'''Charlton J. Taboret''' is an artist member of the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]] that serves as Lord Henry Mystic's portrait artist.

His illustrations of the adventures of [[Henry Mystic]] and [[Albert the Monkey|Albert]] can be found throughout the queue of Mystic Manor, with one piece showing that he joins the two while out on in the field.

==Trivia==
*Charlton's illustrations were created by Imagineer Chris Turner, one of the primary art directors for the Mystic Manor project.
