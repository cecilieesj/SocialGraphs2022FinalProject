==Biography==
Chelsea is a mean girl at Lake Monroe High School. She considers herself to be popular. Her dream is to be elected as the homecoming queen. She harasses Carter since the beginning of the movie. When Rosie arrives at the school and quickly gains popularity, she sees Rosie as a threat and and starts hating her. When Carter and Rosie being nominated for the homecoming queen, she hates them even more. With the help of her "best friend", Brooke, they do whatever it takes to get rid of them out of the contest, She and Brooke are no longer friends because she was being snobby to her.
