'''Wong''' is a character in [[Marvel Comics]] who appears in the multiple films of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], debuting in ''[[Doctor Strange (film)|Doctor Strange]]''.

The [[Ancient One]] assigns Wong to become [[Doctor Strange|Stephen Strange]]'s teacher, and has helped Strange master his skills and become one of Strange's allies. Wong is an expert magic user and a master martial artist, who later becomes the Sorcerer Supreme on a technicality after Strange blips for five years.

Wong was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Steve Ditko.

==Personality==
Wong's main development is being very wise, and very serious and possessing a no-nonsense attitude. However, he is known for allocating some of his time for other activities such as listening to Beyoncé or participating in drunk karaoke. Other than that, not much else is known about his character.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Spider-Man: No Way Home]]''====
Because Wong survived [[Thanos]]'s snap, he is now the new Sorcerer Supreme following [[Doctor Strange]] having blipped for 5 years between the events of ''[[Avengers: Infinity War]]'' and ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]''.

== Disney Parks ==
Wong will appear in Disney Parks with his friend [[Doctor Strange]] at [[Avengers Campus (Disney California Adventure)|Avengers Campus]].
