'''Judge Glamis Cutler'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/cutler.htm</ref> is a minor character from the episode "[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]" from the television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
He is the food court judge for the area, and a good report from him is important. He comes with his son, Leonard for his birthday. Glamis Cutler tells [[Arthur Spudinski|Spud]] that as long as Leonard is happy, he’s happy.
