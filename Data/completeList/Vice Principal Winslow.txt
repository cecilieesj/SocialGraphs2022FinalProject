'''Principal Winslow''' is the  Vice Principal at [[John Hughes High School]] who appeared in the ''[[Shake It Up]]'' episode "[[Wild It Up]]".

==Background==
Before she gave [[Rocky Blue|Rocky]] detention for making fun of her accent, she seemed to like Rocky a lot because of her perfect record attendance and great grades in school.
