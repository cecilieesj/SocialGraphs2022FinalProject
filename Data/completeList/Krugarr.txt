'''Krugarr''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]''===
Krugarr is a lem, a race of beings that have red skin and a worm-like body. He is a [[Masters of the Mystic Arts|Master of the Mystic Arts]] who left [[Kamar Taj]] to become one of the members of [[Stakar Ogord]]'s [[Ravagers|Ravager]] Clan, and he participated to many adventures and fight across the galaxy. Since [[Yondu Udonta]] was exiled on the Clan, the team itself was disbanded and Krugarr never saw his team-mates again for many years.

Some years later, after the battle on Ego's Planet, Yondu died and sacrificed for [[Star-Lord]]. Stakar finally forgived Yondu and later the Clan reunited and assembled again as a team to steal some stuff.

==Trivia==
*In the [[w:c:marvel:Earth-691|alternative 31st Century of Earth-691]], Krugarr is a Lem sorcerer and the disciple and successor of [[Doctor Strange]] as the Sorcerer Supreme.
