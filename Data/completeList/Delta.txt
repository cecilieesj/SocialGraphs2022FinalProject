|status=Active}}'''Delta''' is a minor character in ''[[Beverly Hills Chihuahua]]'' and [[Beverly Hills Chihuahua 2|its sequel]]. She is absent from the [[Beverly Hills Chihuahua 3: Viva la Fiesta|third film]] along with [[Bimini]].

==History==
Similar to [[Sebastian (Beverly Hills Chihuahua)|Sebastian]] and Bimini, she is only seen at the beginning and end of the first film. Delta is first seen at the dog groomer with [[Chloe]] and Bimini, and then again when the three of them are relaxing by the pool. She is seen again at the end of the film, having gotten a spot on a dance show with Sebastian and Bimini.

Her role is slightly enlarged in the second film. Here, she attends [[Papi]] and Chloe's wedding with Sebastian and Bimini, and was seen watching the dog show on TV.
