'''Ruby Mendel''' is a supporting character from the ''[[Even Stevens]]'' television show and subsequent feature film, and is played by Lauren Frost. Ruby is [[Ren Stevens]]' best friend. She loves to gossip, usually about school crushes and who's dating who within her school.

While under the spell of a lucky penny (Episode 2x01 - "[[Starstruck (2010 film)|Starstruck]]"), Ruby developed a crush on [[Louis Stevens]], and later in the episode, on the boy band BBMak. She also has a fear of apricots.
