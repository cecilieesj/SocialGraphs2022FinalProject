}}'''Boppy''' is a dog-shaped punching bag from the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. He is voiced by [[James Arnold Taylor]].
==Role in the series==
Boppy is generally self-confident and kind-hearted but he doesn't like being injured. In "[[A Bad Case of the Pricklethorns]]", Boppy is quickly taken in by [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]] to get healed, and Doc finds a hole in him and puts a band-aid on him, then she gives him a quick check-up. But when Boppy goes back out, he gets another hole, and Doc quickly puts another band-aid on him. [[Stuffy]] tells her how Boppy got his second injury and Doc finds out that he got popped by a rose thorn and calls her diagnosis the 'Pricklethorns'. She tells Boppy to be more careful and stay away from the rosebush.
