'''Ali''' is a character in the [[Disney+]] original series ''[[Baymax!]].'' He is a young boy who is friends with [[Sofia (Baymax!)|Sofia]] and tries to reassure her in her situation.

==Background==
===Personality===
Ali is an excitable young boy, but is also slightly nervous and not at ease as his friend Sofia. He has trouble assessing situations and tends to become a nervous wreck whenever he is on his own. It is only when he is with Sofia that he attains any sort of confidence.

===Physical appearance===
Ali is of average pre-teen height with dark brown skin and short hair. He wears a grey shirt with a flower pattern and short violet sleeves, orange-beige shorts, violet socks and light grey shoes. Additionally, he wears matching wrist bands and a headband that are orange with a white stripe going through it.

===Abilities===
Ali is a master of performing yo-yo tricks with his friend Sofia.

==Trivia==
