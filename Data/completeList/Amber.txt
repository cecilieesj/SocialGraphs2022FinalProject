'''Amber''' was a student at Bayside High School in ''[[That's So Raven]]''. She is played by [[Brenda Song]].

==Season 1==
Amber met up with [[Raven Baxter|Raven]] and [[Chelsea Daniels|Chelsea]] in the hallway to invite them to her party. She pointed out that she and a handful of other students would be going with their dates and pointed out that Raven and Chelsea were dateless.
