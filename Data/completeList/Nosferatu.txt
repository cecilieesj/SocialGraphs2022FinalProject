'''Nosferatu''' (better known as '''Nosy''') is [[Vampirina Hauntley|Vampirina's]] baby cousin in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Vampirina]]''.

==Physical appearance==
Nosferatu is an infant vampire. She has periwinkle skin, pointy ears, one fang, purple eyes, and black hair in bat-wing pigtails. She wears light amber onesie pyjamas, with orange bats on them and yellow paw prints on the bottoms of the feet.

==Trivia==
*She's the first vampire in the series to not have violet eyes. Her eyes are purple.
*Her name comes from both the Hungarian word for "vampire" and the 1922 silent horror film of the same name.
