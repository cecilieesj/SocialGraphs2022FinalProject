|fate = Ends up married to Miss Starter}}'''Reuben Stoppable''' is a minor character from the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[Bad Boy]]".

==History==
Reuben is the bridegroom at the wedding. He only appears on screen for about a second, pulling his bride, Miss Starter, to safety. He is a cousin of [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]], in what way is not specified, but since the wedding is specified as "the Stoppable-Starter wedding" he is from the father's side.

==Family==
Reuben is the husband of Miss Starter, the nephew to [[Mr. Stoppable]], and cousin to [[Ron Stoppable]], he now has a new adopted baby cousin called [[Hana Stoppable]]. His relationship to Ron's other cousin, [[Shaun]], is unclear.
