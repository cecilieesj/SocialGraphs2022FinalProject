|status=|siblings=Jeb (brother)|family=Matilda (cousin)}}'''Ed''' is a giant [[South America]]n river otter who lives in the Amazon rain forest. He is [[Mr. Whiskers]]' best friend.
==Personality==
Ed is sympathetic, talkative and a little ignorant. He and Whiskers usually come up with silly plans, but it's usually Ed that does all the thinking.

==Physical description==
Ed is known to look like a blue otter, with a large snout, a big black nose, big teeth and small fingers and ears. Ed is rather slouched.

==Relationships==



===Mr. Whiskers===
''Main article: [http://brandyandmrwhiskers.wikia.com/wiki/Mr._Whiskers Mr. Whiskers]''

Mr. Whiskers is Ed's best friend. They usually come up with silly plans. In the episode "The No Sleep Over", Ed and Whiskers were playing pranks on Brandy and her friends, while they played pranks back on them.

== Love interests ==
Lola Boa: Ed have a crush for Lola Boa and we discover that in the episode *A Really Crushing Crush*
