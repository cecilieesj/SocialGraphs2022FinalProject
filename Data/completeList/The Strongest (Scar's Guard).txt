The '''Strongest Member of Scar's Lion Guard''' is a character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[The Lion Guard]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
Not much of this member's personality is known. This member of Scar's Guard appeared to be the strongest before his death. When Scar was alive as the leader of his Guard, he was devoted in protecting the Circle of Life from threats. However, he refuses to take over [[Mufasa]]'s place.

===Abilities===
*'''Animal Strength:''' The Strongest is the strongest member in Scar's [[Lion Guard]].

==Role the series==
Aside from his previous appearance in a painting, the only time the Strongest member appears in a physical form was in the Season 3 episode, "[[Battle for the Pride Lands]]". His only appearance was during Scar's flashback in the musical number "[[When I Led the Guard]]". When Scar lost control of the Roar, he destroyed all the members of his Lion Guard, losing the [[Mark of the Guard]].
