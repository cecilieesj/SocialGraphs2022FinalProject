Betty is a baby bear cub who made her appearance along with her twin brother [[Bobby (T.O.T.S.)|Bobby]] in the episode "[[The Great Robot Race]]."

== Appearance ==
She is a peach colored bear with purple eyes and a pink nose. She spots a swirl of hair on her head. She wears a yellow collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol and a white diaper with yellow trim and evergreen tree and mountain patterns on it.
