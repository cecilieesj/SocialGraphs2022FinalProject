|alignment = Good|family={{Scroll|[[Reginald Fletcher]] (father-in-law)<br>[[Winifred Fletcher]] (mother-in-law)<br>[[Lawrence Fletcher]] (brother-in-law)<br>[[Linda Flynn-Fletcher]] (sister-in-law)<br>[[Ferb Fletcher]] (nephew)<br>[[Phineas Flynn]] (step-nephew)<br>[[Candace Flynn]] (step-niece)<br>[[Adrian Fletcher]] (husband)<br>[[Eliza Fletcher]] (daughter)<br>Five sons}}}}'''Lucy Fletcher'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398900/</ref> is a minor character from ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]'', only appearing in "[[My Fair Goalie]]". She is the wife of [[Adrian Fletcher]] and sister-in-law to [[Lawrence Fletcher]]. She is very proud of her husband for winning several competitions against his brother. Her sons are named after famous footballers (soccer players) David Beckham and Pelé.

==Background==
===Children===
*'''Pelé Fletcher:''' The tallest of all his brothers; he wears a red t-shirt with a white collar and pink stripes, white shorts and black sneakers.
*'''Pelé Fletcher:''' He has spiky blond hair and black sneakers.
*'''Beckham Fletcher:''' Straight brown hair and half moon glasses.
*'''Beckham Fletcher:''' Unkempt brown hair square green glasses.
*'''Beckham Fletcher:''' Straight blond hair that covers one eye, black sneakers.
*'''[[Eliza Fletcher]]:''' The only girl of Lucy; she is [[Candace Flynn|Candace]]'s height and has brown hair in a small ponytail. She was named after Eliza Doolittle and her middle name, Beckham, is a reference to Victoria Beckham, a former member of the Spice Girls.

===Physical Appearance===
She has light brown hair and blue eyes and wears a brown headband and belt, beige shirt, and gray pants.
