'''Tommy Howard''' is a minor character in Disney's ''[[Fillmore!]]''. He is voiced by Josh Uhler.<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Tommy/</ref>

==Biography==
He is a member of the art club along with [[Jean Geary]]. They are possible suspects of graffiti in the school's toilet stalls.
