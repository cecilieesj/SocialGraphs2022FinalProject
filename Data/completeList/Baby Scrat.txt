'''Baby Scrat''' is a main character of ''[[Ice Age: Scrat Tales]]''. He is the son of Scrat.

==Background==
===Personality===
Baby Scrat loves his father, Scrat, but his love for the acorn often gets in the way.

===Physical appearance===
Baby Scrat is a young saber tooth squirrel with less developed fangs an orange-fur pattern, pink eyes, and white underbelly, as well as dark brown stripes on his tail. He also has some facial traits of his father.
