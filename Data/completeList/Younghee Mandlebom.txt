|quote = "No Leonard No! Don't do that you'll kill yourself"||parents=Younghee's Dad}}'''Younghee Mandlebom'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0311295/</ref> is a Korean girl who is [[Leslie Dunkling|Leslie's]] best friend. She is usually mean to [[Leonard Helperman]]. But she actually cares for him, even showing that she had a crush on him at least twice. She doesn't want anything bad to happen to Leonard and does not appreciate it when he does something dangerous, scary, or foolish.
