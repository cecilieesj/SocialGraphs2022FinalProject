'''Clementine'''' is the main character, and narrator of the book series ''[[Clementine (book series)|Clementine]].'' She's a charismatic 8 year old with a lot to say. She tackles 3rd grade, and is usually on the top of her game. She's often misunderstood because of her unique character qualities, and is sent to the principal's office to have a "chat" with Principal Rice. She loves helping others, especially her family.
