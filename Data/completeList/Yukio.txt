'''Yukio''' is a character in the [[Disney+]] Original series ''[[Baymax!]].'' He is a young man who is close friends with Mbita and shows an interest in him that is mutually reciprocated.

==Background==
===Personality===
Yukio is a kind hearted and laid back young man. He is very friendly, not afraid to approach others and always seems to know exactly what to say to those around him.

===Physical appearance===
Yukio is a tall and well-built man of Japanese descent with short black hair. He is seen wearing a light blue shirt, a yellow apron with several notable white powdered spots because to indicate he has grined apple cores before. He also wears grey pants and always wears a grey newsboy cap.

==Trivia==
*Yukio bears a slight resemblance to [[Tadashi Hamada]].
*While Yukio is an LGBTQ+ character, his voice actor, [[Brian Tee]], is not.
*One out of all the kinds of apples Yukio sells, he has so far presented a common apple such as a [https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sciros Pacific Rose] (cultivar: ''Sciros'') while Mbita asks if he has something other than fish (when he started hating fish due to his fish-allergies). Even though it is in a fact a Chilean type of apple, the Japanese stores should refer to it as “Fuji”.
