==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
The Living Laser is a prisoner of the Vault until the Breakout.

He later joins the Masters of Evil and guards one of the Norn stones. He is defeated when Iron Man blasts him into space. At some point, however he did make it back to Earth by unknown reasons.

When the Enchantress decides to dispose of the Masters of Evil, she has the Living Laser frozen in light form.

===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===
The Living Laser takes control of the Iron Man armor, but is defeated by [[Spider-Man]].
