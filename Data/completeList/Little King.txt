==''Planes''==
Little King is an Irish racing competitor in the Wings Around the Globe competition.

Little King is seen in many shots of the racers throughout the movie, including in the pit row at John F. Kennedy International Airport. He was selected as one of the planes on the show "World's Top Ten Air Crashes" for the Wings Around the Globe, which he was #9. When [[Dusty Crophopper|Dusty]] is broken and needs repairs, Little King gives him supplies.

==Trivia==
*His design is a combination of a Extra EA-300's fuselage, and a Cessna 402's cockpit windows and vertical stabilizer.
