|alignment = Bad}}'''Nodoff''' is a minor antagonist from the [[The Disney Afternoon|Disney Afternoon]] show ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''. He is the ruler of Dream World and uses his sleep sand to put others to sleep. He only appeared in the episode "[[Ghoul of My Dreams]]".

==Role in the episode==
In order to obtain enough money to pay off her student loan, [[Morgana Macawber]] struck a deal with Nodoff. He would help her out by using his sleep sand to lull the denizens of [[St. Canard]] to sleep. While asleep, Morgana and her familiars flew over the city in a balloon to get the money in any way they could.
