|alignment=Neutral}}'''Nate''' is one of [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]]'s friends and [[Lee]]'s best friend, and is usually seen with him. He appears in the episode "[[The Inconveniencing]]".

==History==
Nate is first seen with the rest of [[Wendy Corduroy|Wendy's]] friends. He comes along to the abandoned Dusk 2 Dawn store. He gives [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]] the name "Dr. Funtimes" after he opens the door. Later, when he is headed out the door and the door closes, he yells at the ghosts to let him out. Then the ghosts turn him into a hot dog. He is the fourth of Wendy's friends to have something happen to. He is rescued by Dipper along with the rest of Wendy's friends. Lee and Nate do not have enough money to leave.

In "[[Double Dipper]]", he and Lee are seen attempting to escape from Stan's party. They get trapped, due to not having enough cash to pay Stan's "exit fee". Nate can be seen celebrating Pioneer Day when [[Quentin Trembley]] returned to Gravity Falls. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-0">[http://gravityfalls.wikia.com/wiki/Nate#cite_note-0 [1]]</sup>

In "[[The Time Traveler's Pig]]", Nate is seen riding the Ferris wheel with Lee at the Mystery Fair.

==Personality==
Nate is a stereotypical carefree, laid back teenager. He can be sarcastic and loves partying and doing stupid things with his friends.

==Appearance==
He has dark skin, brown hair, a small beard, and dark aqua green and white cap and earrings. He wears a black shirt. He has several tattoos on his arms.
