'''Booker Carter''' is the male protagonist of the [[Disney Channel]] show, ''[[Raven's Home]]''. He is [[Raven Baxter|Raven]] and [[Devon Carter|Devon's]] psychic son, the twin-brother of [[Nia Baxter-Carter|Nia]] and the best friend of [[Levi Grayson]].

==Trivia==
*It is revealed he has his [[Raven Baxter|mother]]'s precognition abilities.
*Outside of his mother, father and sister, and maternal grandfather, the only people to currently know about Booker's psychic abilities are Chelsea (Raven's best friend), Levi (his best friend/Chelsea's son), Tess (Nia's best friend), Ivy and Neil (his friends/classmates).
