[[File:Blinky.jpg|thumb|220x220px]]Blinky is a female baby bunny who first appears in [[Bunny Bonanza|Bunny Bunanza]].
==Appearance==



Blinky is a light purple rabbit with a light blue bow with stars patterns on it. She wears a dark green collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol and a white diaper with dark green trim and evergreen patterns on it.
