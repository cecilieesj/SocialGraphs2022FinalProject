The '''Leaf Monster '''is another antagonist shown in ''[[The 7D]]'' episode "[[Leaf It to Sneezy]]". It was created by [[Hildy Gloom]] out of a huge pile of leaves at the Fall Into Fall Day festival and instructed to throw the Queen away to Gusty Gorge. The Leaf Monster was stopped several times by Sir Yipsalot, and The 7D (except Doc and Sneezy) but to no use, until Sneezy gave out his gigantic sneeze that blast the leaves on him away and vanished for good.
