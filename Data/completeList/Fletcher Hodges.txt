'''Prof. Fletcher Hodges''' is a character from the [[Adventurers Club]] of [[Pleasure Island (Downtown Disney)|Pleasure Island]].

== History ==
In the early 20th century, Fletcher Hodges was an esteemed but absent-minded professor of antiques and archaeology.  By around [[1932]], he became a member of a group called The Adventurers Club which made its headquarters on Pleasure Island in [[Lake Buena Vista]], [[Florida]].  Under club founder and [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers|SEA]] member [[Merriweather Adam Pleasure]], Hodges managed the club's collection of rare and supernatural antiques whim the professor claiming to have mounted every artifact in their collection.

== Appearances ==

=== [[Disneyland]] ===

==== [[Trader Sam's|Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar]] ====
In this bar is a message sent from Fletcher to one Trader Sam via the Jungle Navigation Company.  The message reads as, "''Sam, Beware of the giant Piranha Butterfly! Don't say I didn't warn you- Fletcher Hodges''".

=== [[Walt Disney World Resort|Walt Disney World]] ===

==== Adventurers Club ====
Hodges was one of the main performed characters in this restaurant.
