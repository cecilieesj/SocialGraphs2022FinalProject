|alignment = Good}}'''Coco Banana''' is a character in ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is the founder of [[Club Banana]].

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
He is African American, and has brown eyes, black hair, a black mustache, black goatee, purple business suit, and teal shirt.

==Appearances==
===''[[Kim Possible]]''===
In the episode "[[Homecoming Upset]]", he takes [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]], [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]], and [[Bonnie Rockwaller|Bonnie]] to Venice, [[Italy]] in his personal plane. During the trip, he comforts Bonnie, and is moved by her story.
