|status = Deceased}}The '''Cavendish's Gang''' are minor antagonists of the [[2013]] film ''[[The Lone Ranger]]''. They collaborated with [[Butch Cavendish|Butch]], for the people of Colby think the Comanches attacked and burned their farms.

==Members==
*'''Frank''': He is portrayed by Harry Treadaway.
*'''Jesús''': He is portrayed by Joaquín Cosío.
*'''Ray''': He is portrayed by Damon Herrimnan.
*'''Skinny''': He is portrayed by Matt O'Leary.
*'''Barret''': He is portrayed by James Frain.
*'''Collins''': A traitorous deputy ranger secretly working with Butch. He is portrayed by Leon Rippy.
