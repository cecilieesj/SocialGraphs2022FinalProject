Devils are a group of demons that live in hell. These devilish-like creatures have appeared in many shorts and movies in the Disney canon.

== History ==
the devils first appeared in [[Hell's Bells|Hells Bells]] where they serve Satan until they kill him. A [[Pluto's Devil|demon version]] of [[Pluto]] first appeared in Mickeys Pal Pluto who appears many times throughout time. [[Chernabog]] is a demon who have a group of [[Chernabog's Minions|minions]] in the movie Fantasia. [[J. Thaddeus Toad|Mr. Toad]] also goes to hell where he sees many demons in [[Mr. Toad's Wild Ride|Mr. Toads Wild Ride.]]  In the [[The Haunted Mansion (attraction)|Haunted Mansion]], a wailing devil appears on the ride's plaque/sign, and the ride was once planned to feature a portrait depicting a demon spiriting away a miser's soul to Hell. [[Hades]] is a demon in the movie [[Hercules]] with his minions [[Pain and Panic]]. Chernabog reappeared in a Mickey Mouse short in which he is friends with Pete and The Beagle Boys.  Many demons appear in the show [[The Owl House]], most notably [[King (The Owl House)|King]].
