'''Miss Piggy's Receptionist''' is a woman who worked for [[Miss Piggy]] whilst she worked as the Plus Size Editor at ''[[Wikipedia:Vogue Paris|Vogue Paris]]''. She was reluctant to allow [[Kermit the Frog|Kermit]] and the rest of the Muppets into Miss Piggy's office to see her, but she was ultimately duped by a "Muppet Man."
