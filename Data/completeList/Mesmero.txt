|quote=}}'''Mesmero''' (first name '''Vincent''') is a mutant super-villain with the power of hypnosis. He's an enemy of the [[Wolverine]] and [[Spider-Man]].

==History==
===[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]===
After [[Spider-Man]] and [[Wolverine]] stop Mesmero's hypnotized citizens and send him to jail, he uses his powers to make the heroes switch bodies. After a difficult battle against [[Sabretooth]], Spider-Man, and Wolverine (in each other's body) go to Mesmero's cell and treathen him into changing them back.
