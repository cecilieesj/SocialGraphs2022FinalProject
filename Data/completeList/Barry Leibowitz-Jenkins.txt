'''Barry Leibowitz-Jenkins''' is a new character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder]]''. He is the police officer husband of [[Randall Leibowitz-Jenkins]] and the adopted father of [[Maya Leibowitz-Jenkins|Maya]] and [[KG Leibowitz-Jenkins|KG]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Warm and strong, Barry is a devoted husband to Randall and father to Maya and KG. He is a late '30s detective with an infectious laugh. Big and athletic, when he slaps Oscar on his back he sends him flying for miles.<ref>[https://d23.com/meet-the-new-characters-of-the-proud-family-louder-and-prouder/ Meet the New Characters of The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder]</ref>

===Physical appearance===
Barry is a tell, well built Caucasian male with a buzzcut blond hair and green eyes. He is typically seen wearing a light yellow polo shirt, black belt, blue pants and shoes.
