'''Reggie''', is a minor character in [[Gravity Falls|''Gravity Falls'']]. He is [[Soos Ramirez|Soos]]' older cousin.

==Role in the series==
===Season 1===
He was first mentioned in "[[Fight Fighters]]", where according to Soos, a teenager broke both of Reggie's arms, both of Reggie’s legs, and maybe even killed him, however Soos then says that he and Reggie were just talking about it.

===Season 2===
Reggie makes his first in-person appearance in "[[Soos and the Real Girl]]", when he is having an engagement party in a week and wants Soos to come. He and his fiancée are later seen at the mall but Soos doesn't want Reggie to see that he still single and hides.

He has a small cameo in "[[Blendin's Game]]", were he is seen as a kid was a young Soos telling not sweat it since his dad wasn't coming.
