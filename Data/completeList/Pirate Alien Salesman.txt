|alignment = Neutral}}The "'''Pirate Alien Salesman'''" is an alien dressed in pirate attire from an futuristic time period who appears in [[Future-Worm!]]. He sold [[Danny Douglas|Danny]] and [[Future-Worm|Fyootch]] the [[Crystal (Future-Worm!)|crystal]], but not before warning them about it's side-effects.
==Appearance==
The alien has jungle green skin, a brown-grey long-sleeved outfit, a black pirate hat and five eyes, with the middle one being the only that isn't covered by an eyepatch.
