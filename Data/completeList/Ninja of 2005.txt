The "'''Ninja of '05'''" was the Norrisville Ninja in [[2005]].

==Appearance==
The Ninja of '05 has a huge top body, legs half of the height, and a large chin. He also has blue eyes, lightly tanned skin, and large arms. For the outfit, he wears long boots, gauntlets, a red belt, a mask, and red scarf. He also has a Norisu Nine symbol on his left shoulders and two red lines on both left arm and his top and bottom.

==Personality==
He is humorous as he makes bad puns and skeptical as he questions often. Like all the Ninja's (including Randy) he is pure heart and very humble.
