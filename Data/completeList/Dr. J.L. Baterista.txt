|friends = S.E.A. and its members}}'''Dr. J.L. Baterista''' is a character affiliated with [[Disney Parks]]' ''[[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]''.

==History==

===Background===
Dr. J.L. Baterista was a member of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers in the late-nineteenth and early-twentieth century. In 1899, he appeared alongside [[Harrison Hightower III]], Lord [[Henry Mystic]], Captain [[Mary Oceaneer]] and [[Maestro D'Elfman]]. In 1906, he went on a river journey along the Congo.

===Developmental History===
Dr. J.L. Baterista was created for the attraction [[Mystic Manor]] as a member of SEA. He was modelled after [[Joe Lanzisero]] who was the Senior Vice President of Walt Disney Imagineering when the ride was being developed. " J.L." are made to represent Lanzisero's initials.

==Appearances==

===Mystic Manor===
Two portrait appearances of the doctor are visible in Mystic Manor. One is a profile portrait of him, while the other shows him at an 1899 S.E.A. meeting.

===[[Disney Cruise Line]]===
A hat in Mary Oceaneer's collection is slated as coming from Baterista's collection and was an apparent gift to Mary.

===[[The Tropical Hideaway]]===
An oar commemorating Baterista's 1906 journey on the Congo is on display at the Tropical Hideaway.

==Trivia==
*The name ‘baterista’ means drummer in Portuguese.
