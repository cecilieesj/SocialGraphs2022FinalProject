'''Hoagie, Knuckles, and Chops (aka The Three Big Pigs)''' are three Toon pigs in ''[[Bonkers (TV series)|Bonkers]]'' who appear as the main antagonists in the episode "[[Never Cry Pig]]". They are a trio of shady contractors who build shoddy houses and frame [[The Mean Old Wolf]] for the collapse of said houses. Hoagie is voiced by [[Rob Paulsen]]. Knuckles is voiced by [[Pat Fraley]] and Chops is voiced by [[Rodger Bumpass]].

==Personalty==

===Hoagie===
Hoagie is the pig in a purple suit, wear sunglasses and has a mohawk.

===Knuckles===
Knuckles is the pig in a blue suit and wears glasses.

===Chops===
Chops is the youngest of the three. He wears a green suit and a hat. He’s not the brightest than the his brothers.

==Roles==

==Trivia==
*Hoagie, Knuckles, and Chumps are based on the main characters from the ''[[The Three Little Pigs]]''.
