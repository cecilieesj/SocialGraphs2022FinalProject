[[File:Atog.png|thumb|250px]]'''Atog''' is one of the Kinkow giants. He is used as the final exam at Fight School. He is shown in the episode, "Fight School". [[Boomer Parker|Boomer]] and [[Brady Parker|Brady]] lost to him in a fight. They later participated in a rematch and defeated him.

==Appearance==
*He is a giant.
*He is played by [[Dalip Rana|The Great Khali]].
