[[File:Vidshot-2016-09-09-17h21m35s151.png|thumb|250px]]'''The Really Nice Guys''' are minor protagonists of the animated TV series, ''[[The 7D]]''. They appeared in ''[[Nicely Done and The 7D]]'' and are named Nice, Nicer, and Nicest. They were locked in a tower by the Sheriff of Plottingham but free by Nicely Done and the 7D.
