|alignment = Good|voice=Jack Mather}}'''J. Harold King''' is a character in the [[1945]] [[Donald Duck]] cartoon ''[[Duck Pimples]]''. He is the author of the book featuring [[Paddy the Cop]], [[Pauline]], [[Leslie J. Clark]] and [[Dopey Davis]].
==History==
As Paddy is about to cut Donald open to find the pearls, J. Harold stopps him and confirms that Donald is innocent as the thief is revealed to be H.U. Hennessy a.k.a. Paddy. After Paddy "shoots" Donald, he and Pauline return to the book.
