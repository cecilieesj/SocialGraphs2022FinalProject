|quote=}}The '''Wizard''' (real name '''Bentley Wittman''') is the master of anti-gravity.

The Wizard was created by [[Stan Lee]], [[Jack Kirby]], and Larry Lieber.

==History==
===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===
[[Doctor Octopus]] contacted the Frightful Four to kidnap [[Spider-Man]]. With the help of [[Trapster]], Wizard and the Frightful Four (minus Trapster) tracked Spider-Man to Midtown High School. He got distracted by Peter, who changed to his spider costume. he fought with Spider-Man for a while and escaped before the police arrived.

After Spider-Man left [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]], the Frightful Four (minus Trapster again) ambushed Spider-Man for a rematch. They got the tide of the battle until Spider-Man's [[White Tiger|previously]] [[Sam Alexander|rejected]] [[Luke Cage|team]][[Iron Fist|mates]] arrived and saved him. Wizard's gloves exploded after Spider-Man webbed them together.

===''[[Avengers Assemble]]''===
[[Hawkeye]] is first contacted by [[Iron Man]] right after he stopped [[Trapster]], [[Ulysses Klaw|Klaw]], and Wizard from committing a robbery.
