'''Carolina Grant-Gomez''' is a main character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is the mother of Kevin and Gretel and. the wife of [[Dave Grant-Gomez|Dave]]. She is from Venezuela.

==Background==

===Physical appearance===
Carolina Grant-Gomez has tan skin with brown hair. She wears a red shirt and blue pants.

==Trivia==
