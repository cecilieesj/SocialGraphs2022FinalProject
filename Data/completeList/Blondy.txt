'''Blondy''' is a blond baby bunny from ''[[T.O.T.S.]]''.

==Appearance==
Blondy is a blonde rabbit who has blue eyes and a pink nose. She has a fluffy tail and her hair is straightly smooth. She has a pink collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol on it and she has a pink diaper with leaves and mountains on it.
