'''Clerk Penny''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC citrine [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Duck Duck] and clerk in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/The_Brrrgh The Brrrgh's] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Goofy%27s_Gag_Shop Goofy's Gag Shop].

==SOS Toon==
﻿Clerk Penny is also an [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon]. She is a 4-star [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Trap_Gags Trap ]SOS Toon. When called upon, she will deploy [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Trapdoor Trapdoors] in front of all [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs cogs] in play. She will not deploy Trap Gags on trapped-cogs or soon-to-be trapped.

==Trivia==
﻿*All Trap SOS Toons are clerks working in Goofy's Gag Shop; [[Clerk Will]], [[Clerk Clara]], and [[Clerk Ray]] are among these.
