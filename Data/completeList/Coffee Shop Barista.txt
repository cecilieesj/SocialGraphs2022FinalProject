|friends = Pizza Chef (possibly), Sport Shop Owner, Gift Shop Owner, Pet Shop Owner||image = OOTCS.png}}The '''Coffee Shop Barista''' is a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Brown Brown] [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Penguin penguin] in [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin Club Penguin] who works at the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Coffee_Shop Coffee Shop]. She always wears a green [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Coffee_Apron Coffee Apron]. She has appeared in two [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Missions Missions], and in ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force]'' for Nintendo DS.

She is friends with [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Flare Flare], saying that the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffle Puffle] was the only one who could boil water hot enough to make hot chocolate.

==Involvement in Missions==

*[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Mission Mission 5] - When [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Herbert Herbert] and [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Klutzy Klutzy] attacked the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Coffee_Shop Coffee Shop], the Barista believed that they were some type of hairy mutant crab. She gives you a picture He drew describing it. You must then fix the Coffee Machine so you can make the hot chocolate.
*[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Mission Mission 8] - When Herbert makes another tremor with his [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Earthquake_Driller Earthquake Driller], the Coffee Shop is messed up. You must help her clean up the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Cookie cookies]. If you do that the Barista will give you a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Cookie cookie] so you can cheer up the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Green_Puffle Green Puffle] outside.
*In [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Elite_Penguin_Force_Missions Elite Missions] 1, 5, 10, and the summer mission, the Barista is in the Coffee Shop.
