'''Lady Stella Albright''' is the headmistress of Starling Academy and the deuteragonist in Disney's ''[[Star Darlings]]''.

==Appearance==
Lady Stella has the appearance of an aged woman with white hair with pink highlights and wrinkles around her face. She wears a white dress uniform.

She is the Principal and headmistress of Starling Academy as well The most powerful being Starland next to Lady Rancora herself. In [[The Power of Twelve]], she discovered along with the twelve-star darlings, she managed to confront her and face her to save her home from her negative wish powers.
