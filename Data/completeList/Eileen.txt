
[[File:Char_36817.jpg|thumb]]'''Eileen''' (voiced by Michelle Horn)<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Lloyd-in-Space/Eileen/</ref> is a supporting character of the [[Disney's One Saturday Morning]] show ''[[Lloyd in Space]]''.

==Biography==
Eileen is a female alien with three eyes and a student at Luna Vista Middle School. She appears recurringly in the series and she first speaks in the episode [[The Big Sleepover]] along with the other girls that Lloyd infiltrated the party with a spy camera.
