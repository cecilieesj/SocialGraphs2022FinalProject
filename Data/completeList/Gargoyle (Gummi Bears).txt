The '''Gargoyle''' is a minor antagonist who appeared in the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' episode "[[Night of the Gargoyle]]".

==Role in the episode==
The stone gargoyle is given to [[King Gregor]] by [[Duke Igthorn]] and [[Toadwart]], guised as a Monarch's Day present from the [[Gummi Bears]].

When the light of the full moon shines on the gargoyle, he comes alive and starts causing mischief.

[[Zummi Gummi]] uses a sunlight spell that reverts the gargoyle back to stone, and the Gummies return him to [[Drekmore Castle]].
