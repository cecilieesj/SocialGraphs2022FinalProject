'''Mark Garrison''' is the protagonist of [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s ''[[The Ugly Dachshund]]''. He is an artist, [[Fran Garrison|Fran]]'s husband, and the owner of the Dachsies: [[The Dachsies|Danke, Wilhelmina, Heidi, and Chloe]] and [[Brutus]] the Great Dane.

==Role in the film==
Mark Garrison is first seen waking up early in the morning to go to the hospital with his wife, [[Fran Garrison|Fran]]. He calls [[Dr. Pruitt]] to tell him they are on their way.
