'''Regina Honey''' is a guest character and antagonist in ''[[K.C. Undercover]]''. 

== History ==
She kidnaps and holds Kira and Craig hostage, then later plans to auction of her new heat, solid melting laser; and use Craig and Kira as live test targets for the laser to show possible criminal buyers, thus killing the Cooper parents.

She is portrayed by [[Diane Delano]].
