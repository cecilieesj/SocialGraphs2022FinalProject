'''A-Spen''' is a non-binary alien from the ''Disney's'' ''Zombies'' franchise. A-Spen is one the main characters in the [[Disney+]] original movie [[Zombies 3|''Zombies 3'']]. They are portrayed by Terry Hu.

==Background ==
A-Spen is a super-intelligent, telepathic alien who, after arriving on Earth, finds an uncommon emotion entering their bloodstream: Love.
===Personality===
A-Spen is very exciteable, they like to feel many emotions.

===Physical Appearance===
A-Spen has oval shape brown eyes, short blue hair and pale skin. They wear a blue and silver jumpsuit, blue vest, silver belt, Luma Lens bracelet and silver boots.

==Trivia==

*A-Spen is the first non-binary character in a Disney Channel Original Movie.
**They are also the second Disney character to be non-binary after [[Raine Whispers]] from ''[[The Owl House]]''.
*They are [[Willa Lykensen]]'s lover.
*Their name is a pun on the term "aspen".
