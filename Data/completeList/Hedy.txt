}}'''Hedy''' is a baby hedgehog from ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode, "[[Daddy Delivery]]".

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Hedy is a brown hedgehog with tan skin, light blue eyes and a red nose. She wears a red collar with the T.O.T.S. icon on it, and a white diaper with green trim and leaves patterns.
