==History==
He is seen with Wendy's friends, driving the van they use to get around [[Gravity Falls (location)|town]]. He drives them, [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]], and [[Mabel Pines|Mabel]] to the [[Dusk 2 Dawn]] store to party. He cheers Dipper on when he gets the door open. In the store, he is seen letting the guys stuff his pants with ice and playing Dancy Pants Revolution.

When the ghosts attack, he is sucked into Dancy Pants Revolution, which starts piercing him with multicolored arrows and taunting him.

Thompson is seen again in "[[Double Dipper]]" and in one scene, he is dancing with someone. He appears again in "[[Irrational Treasure]]", watching the wagons circle around [[Stan Pines|Grunkle Stan]]'s car at Pioneer Day. In "[[The Time Traveler's Pig]]", Thompson is seen riding the Ferris wheel with Tambry at the Mystery Fair.

==Personality==
Thompson is a stereotypical teenager, laid-back, fun-loving, and friendly. He enjoys playing video games and messing around with his friends, Nate, Robbie, Wendy, Lee, and Tambry. 

==Appearance==
Thompson is a teenage, light-skinned boy with chocolate brown hair, four small hairs above his lip, wears a lime green shirt with a small bunny logo on the right side of his shirt, has brown shorts, white socks, a black and gold watch, and brown and gray shoes.
