|rides = [[Kitchen Kabaret]]}}The '''Boogie Woogie Bakery Boy''' is a trumpet-playing piece of toast who performed in the former [[Kitchen Kabaret]] show at [[Epcot]].

He accompanied [[The Cereal Sisters]], who told his story in song as he appeared in several onstage locations, first in a cabinet and then rising from the toaster above them.
