'''Parker Rooney''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney Channel]] original comedy series ''[[Liv and Maddie]]''. He is also the youngest sibling between [[Liv Rooney|Liv]], [[Maddie Rooney|Maddie]], and [[Joey Rooney|Joey]]. This character is probably between the ages of 7 and 11. He is portrayed by [[Tenzing Norgay Trainor]].
