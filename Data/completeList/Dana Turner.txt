'''Daniella (Dana) Turner''' is a character who appears in ''[[Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam]]''. She is the love interest of [[Nate Grey|Nate]] and the daughter of [[Axel Turner]], the owner of Camp Star.

==<span style="font-size:18px;">Story</span>==
Dana first appears at the bonfire along with her fellow campers including [[Luke Williams]] she flirts with Nate first and her bracelet, the clasp not tightened on her wrist leads to falling on his eyes which she calls "pretty".

===<span style="font-size:16px;">''Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam''</span>===
While Camp Star and Camp Rock are battling, Dana and Nate are secretly seeing each other, because her father told her to not talk to him. Nate writes "Introducing Me" for Dana so she can know the real him. The next year, Dana joins Camp Rock.
