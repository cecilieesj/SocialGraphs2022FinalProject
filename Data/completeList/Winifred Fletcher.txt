[[Adrian Fletcher]] (son)|parents=Unnamed parents|siblings=UNKNOWN}}'''Winifred Fletcher'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0352308/?ref_=tt_cl_t6</ref> is a recurring character of the animated series, ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. She is the wife of [[Reginald Fletcher]], mother of [[Lawrence Fletcher]], and grandmother of [[Candace Flynn|Candace]], [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]], and [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]]. She lives in London with Reginald Fletcher.
==Trivia==
*She has a great passion for tea.
*She is a great fan of the [[Wikipedia:Sherlock Holmes|Sherlock Holmes]] book series and has the complete set of the book series. ("[[Elementary My Dear Stacy]]")
*She wears plugs in her nose because of the smell of her [[Reginald Fletcher|husband]]'s feet. ("[[Picture This]]")
*She is nicknamed "Winnie" by [[Reginald Fletcher|Reginald]] because of her first name, Winifred.
