'''Gilgamesh''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by Don Lee.

Gilgamesh was created by [[Jack Kirby]].

==Trivia==
*In the comics, Gilgamesh is an identity assumed over the centuries by an Eternal known as the "Forgotten One," also known as the "Hero," who once joined the Avengers.
