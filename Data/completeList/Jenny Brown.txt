'''Jenny Brown''' is a recurring character in ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]'', and the older sister of [[Django Brown]].<ref name="Correspondence">User:Marekos1996/Dan Povenmire Correspondence</ref> With a history of participating in various protests along with an inherent desire for world peace, she can be characterized by her hippie-like nature.

==Background==
===Personality===
She appears to be somewhat of a hippie, based on her clothing and the Peace symbol she wears, along with her hope and desire for world peace. Jenny says she doesn't like persimmon. She likes pigeons and likes the idea of being one of [[Candace Flynn|Candace]]'s best friends along with [[Stacy Hirano|Stacy]].

===Physical appearance===
Jenny has long brown hair decorated by a white daisy. Her usual clothing consists of a yellow turtleneck sweater, a red skirt decorated with wavy orange lines, yellow socks, sandals with orange straps and an orange Peace Sign necklace.
==Appearances==
===''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''===
Jenny is a somewhat close friend of Candace as well as [[Stacy Hirano|Stacy]], having been seen hanging out with or conversing with them over the phone from time to time. She first appeared in the episode "[[Lawn Gnome Beach Party of Terror]]" when she took an interest in [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]] and [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]]'s activities when they built an [[Backyard Beach|artificial beach]] in their backyard and came over to spend the day there with [[Stacy Hirano]]. She appears to own and care for a dog with a resemblance to a [[wikipedia:Komondor|Komondor]]. She also appears in other episodes helping or hanging with Candace.

===''[[Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension]]''===
Jenny plays a role in the film, during the [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz (2nd Dimension)|second dimension Doofenshmirtz's]] invasion when she took control of the Candace Crusher to assist in defense against the attack.

==Trivia==
*She is named after series creator [[Jeff "Swampy" Marsh]]'s daughter, Jenny.
*[[Dan Povenmire]] revealed that she is the sister of Django Brown.<ref name="Correspondence">Marekos1996/Dan Povenmire Correspondence on the Phineas and Ferb wiki</ref>
