'''Ironheart''' (real name '''Riri Williams''') is a superheroine from [[Marvel Comics]] who dons a high-tech suit of armor modeled after [[Iron Man]]. She made her animated debut in the special ''[[Marvel Rising: Secret Warriors|Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron]]'' in the ''Marvel Rising'' franchise.

She made her live-action debut in the [[2022]] [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] film ''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]'', portrayed by [[Dominique Thorne]].

Ironheart was created by Brian Michael Bendis and Mike Deodato.
