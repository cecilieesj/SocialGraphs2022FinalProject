[[File:Brad_Parnassus.jpg|thumb]]'''Brad Parnassus''' is a guest character of [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s ''[[Fillmore!]]'' and is voiced by Michael Welch.<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Brad-Parnassus/</ref>

==Role in the series==
Brad was a member of the Safety Patrol until [[Ingrid Third]]'s arrival.<ref>[[Ingrid Third, Public Enemy Number One]]</ref> He was the smartest kid in X Middle School and join the art club for his college applications. He has joined the Safety Patrol while [[Wayne Liggett]] departed, thus becomes [[Cornelius Fillmore|Fillmore]]'s temporary partner.
