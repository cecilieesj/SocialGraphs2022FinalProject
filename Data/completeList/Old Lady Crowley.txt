'''Old Lady Crowley''' is one of the [[Corona Castle|castle]] servants who appears in the 2017 Disney Channel television series, ''[[Tangled: The Series|Rapunzel's Tangled Adventure]]''. She is a grumpy old lady who is known to never smile, earning her the nickname "Scowley Crowley". She is often seen sweeping the castle floors. The one and only thing that puts a smile on Crowley's face is peace and quiet.

==Role in the [[Tangled: The Series|series]]==
In "[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]", Crowley points out the mud that Rapunzel led into the castle.

In "[[One Angry Princess]]", Crowley talks to Rapunzel and says that she saw Attila picking flowers. However, the man turned out to be Big Nose picking the flowers.
