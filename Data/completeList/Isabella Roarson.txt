'''Isabella Roarson''' is a monster pop star who comes to Roarsville looking for talent to join her in her show. [[Summer Hugglemonster]] is among her biggest fans. Isabella first appeared in the episode "[[Number One Fan]]".

== Appearance ==
Isabella is pink-skinned and has striped horns and a violet stomach. She wears a golden hairbow and skirt, star necklace and boots.

== Trivia ==

* Isabella's voice actress is a member of well-known musical group Spice Girls.
