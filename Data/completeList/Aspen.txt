|dislikes=Bugsy, being a housekeeper, Skeeter Bronson}}'''Aspen''' is a character in ''[[Bedtime Stories]]''. She is a former employee of the Nottingham Hotel.

==Role in the film==
Aspen works as a front desk receptionist, and is attracted to Kendall, as they are frequently seen flirting with each other.

After [[Skeeter Bronson]] becomes the new manager of the hotel, he keeps Aspen as an employee, but hires her as a housekeeper instead of a receptionist.
