''Not to be confused with the V.P. rewards, [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon (VP)].''

[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Field_Offices Field Office] SOS Toons are a new, weaker set of SOS Toons in the discontinued online video game, ''[[Toontown Online]]''. These SOS Toons can be earned from the "caged-friend" inside a Field Office. When the friend is released from its cage, the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] will allow you to call one of the new SOS Toons.

There are currently 12 Sellbot Field Office SOS Toons.

===Toon-Up===
[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon-Up_Gags Toon-Up] SOS Toons merely just heal Toons in battle. Depending on the Toon depends on the amount of Laff Points healed.

[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Phil_Bettur Phil Bettur], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Emma_Phatic Emma Phatic], and [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/GiggleMesh GiggleMesh] are the Toon-Up SOS Toons.
===Lure===
﻿[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Lure_Gags Lure] SOS Toons lure all [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs Cogs] in battle. As with any Gag, even an SOS Toon, these Toon's Gag are not guaranteed.

[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Des_Traction Des Traction], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Dee_Version Dee Version], and [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Bo_Nanapeel Bo Nanapeel] are the Lure SOS Toons.
===Sound===
[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Sound_Gags Sound] SOS Toons attack all Cogs in battle.

[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Bea_Sharpe Bea Sharpe], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Otto_Toon Otto Toon], and [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Al_Capella Al Capella] are the Sound SOS Toons.
===Drop===
[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Drop_Gags Drop] SOS Toons will attack all unlured Cogs in battle. As a Drop Gag, the Gag will '''always''' miss Lured Cogs.

[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Anne_Ville Anne Ville], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Bud_Erfingerz Bud Erfingerz], and [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/J.S._Bark J.S. Bark] are the Drop SOS Toons.
