|fate = Being knocked out with [[Ra]] and [[Khnum]]}}'''Bastet''' is a character in the TV series ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series|Hercules]]''. She is the goddess of warfare with the head of a cat. She appeared in the episode, "[[Hercules and the Romans]]".
==Trivia==
*Bastet is both one of Ra's offsprings and one of Ra's consorts along with Hathor, Sekhmet, and sometimes Satet.
*In some versions of mythology, Khnum is Ra's father which makes him being both Bastet's grandfather and father in law.
