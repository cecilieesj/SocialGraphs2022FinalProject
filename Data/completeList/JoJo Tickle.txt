'''JoJo Tickle''' is the title character of ''[[JoJo's Circus]]''. She is an inquisitive and active six-year-old clown whose parents are famous circus clowns. She's kind-hearted, has a great sense of humor, boundless curiosity, and excitement about life; and she loves to clown around. Quite often she talks to the audience. JoJo attends Clown School with her various friends, including her best friend [[Skeebo Seltzer]]. They are taught by [[Mrs. Kersplatski]]. She is voiced by [[Madeleine Martin]].
