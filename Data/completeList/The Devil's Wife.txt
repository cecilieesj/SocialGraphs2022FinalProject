'''"The Devil’s Wife"''' (also known as '''"Medusa"''') is a minor character in ''[[Hocus Pocus]]''. She is the wife of the [[The Devil (Hocus Pocus)|man]] whom the [[Sanderson Sisters]] initially assume to be the devil. They also think she's Medusa because of her snake-like haircurlers.
 
==Appearances==
===''[[Hocus Pocus]]''===
"The Devil’s" wife is first seen when her husband introduces her to the [[Sanderson Sisters]], who think she is Medusa. She later chases them out of her house when she catches her husband dancing with [[Sarah Sanderson]].

===''[[Hocus Pocus 2]]''===
She and her husband make a cameo in this film on a TV. There are also some citizens of Salem dressed up as them.

==Trivia==
* Penny Marshall and [[Garry Marshall]], the actors who portrayed her and her [[The Devil (Hocus Pocus)|husband]] were siblings in real life.
