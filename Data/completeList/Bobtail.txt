[[File:Profile - Bobtail.png|thumb]]Bobtail is a female baby bunny who first appears in [[Bunny Bonanza]].

==Appearance==
Bobtail is an orange rabbit who has brown eyes and a peach nose. She has a fluffy tail. She has an orange collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol on it and she has an orange diaper with leaves and mountains on it. {{T.O.T.S.}}
