'''Shroom''' is a character from the [[Touchstone Pictures]] film ''[[Gnomeo & Juliet]]'' and its [[2018]] Paramount Pictures sequel ''Sherlock Gnomes''. He is a shiitake mushroom.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
ï»¿Shroom is a short and rather stout plaster shiitake mushroom. He is blue, and has white spots on his "hat".

==Role in the [[Gnomeo & Juliet|film]]==
Shroom is a very good friend of [[Gnomeo]]. Shroom might even be considered to a be like a pet to Gnomeo. Shroom does not have the ability to talk, and communicates by little squeaks and funny noises. When Gnomeo is in his statue form, he leans on Shroom. When Gnomeo almost gets run over by a car, Shroom follows the scent and finds Gnomeo.
