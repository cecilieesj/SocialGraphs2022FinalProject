==History==
Pain King visited the [[Middleton Mall]] in the town of [[Middleton]] with the Global Wrestling Association. They were holding a wrestling event there and were in the middle of the match when the wrestling promoter, Jackie Oakes, bitter at not being able to be taken seriously as a wrestler because of his short stature, used a magic talisman to turn himself into a monster,

Team Possible were able to defeat the monster, and with Steel Toe beat up Jackie.

==Trivia==
