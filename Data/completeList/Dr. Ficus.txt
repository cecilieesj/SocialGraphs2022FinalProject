[[File:Drficus.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Dr. Ficus''' appears in the ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_307:_Germ_Warfare Germ Warfare]." His face was operated by [[Bruce Lanoil]], his body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Pons_Maar Pons Maar] and his voice was provided by [[Charles Kimbrough]].

==History==
Possessing a resonant voice and authoritative manner, he attempts to diagnose [[Baby Sinclair|Baby Sinclair's]] unusually extreme illness and prescribes pricey blue and orange medicines as miracle cures. The doctor's placid indifference to the failure of the blue medicine causes [[Frances Sinclair|Fran Sinclair]] to lose faith in the medical establishment and seek help fromÂ [[Zabar]] instead.
