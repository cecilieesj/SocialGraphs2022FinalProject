'''Leilani''' is a character from the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. She appears in the sixth episode of the second season of the series, "[[Leilani's Luau]]". She is a hula girl toy from [[Hawaii]] that hula dances when the sun hits her until been in a box for so long, she becomes slow and weak Doc learns the hula from Leilani.

==Appearance==
Leilani is a hula girl toy from Hawaii with black hair, a pink shirt, a tan-colored grass skirt, rainbow-colored leis and bracelets.

==Trivia==
*"Leilani" is a Hawaiian name that means ''heavenly lei''.
*She sort of resembles [[Lilo Pelekai|Lilo]] from ''[[Lilo & Stitch]]''.
*She could possibly be named after Leilani Jones, the audio describer for [[Disney DVD]]s.
*Her voice actress, [[Liza del Mundo]], has also voiced [[Hay Lin]] in ''[[W.I.T.C.H. (TV series)|W.I.T.C.H.]]''.
