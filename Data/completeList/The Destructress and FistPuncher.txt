'''The Destructress''' (real name '''Lauren''') and '''FistPuncher''' (real name '''Lyle''') are the two main antagonists of the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''.  They are a pair of siblings who were given superpowers by the same aliens who gave Hamster and Gretel their powers, but they were told to use their powers for evil.
