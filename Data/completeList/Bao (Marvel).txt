'''Bao'''  is a minor character in ''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]''. Bao is an anthropomorphic bao bun, and the God of Dumplings.

==Appearances==
===''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]''===
Bao was present at the gathering of the council in the audience of [[Zeus (Marvel)|Zeus]]. Bao was pointed out by [[Thor Odinson|Thor]] as he observed the various gods in attendance. When Zeus begun his showy entrance, Bao was one of the many gods chanting his name in anticipation. Bao witnessed the following proceedings, including Thor's failed call to arms and violent incapacitation of Zeus himself, followed swiftly by his escape.

==Trivia==
*The character and their name is most likely a reference to the Disney and Pixar short film ''[[Bao]],'' which features an [[Dumpling (Bao)|anthropomorphic dumpling]].
