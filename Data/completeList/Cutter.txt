'''Katherine "Cutter" Sterns''' is a mechanic in the [[Disney Television Animation|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated series, ''[[Monsters at Work]]''.

== Physical Appearance ==
Cutter is a teal three-eyed crab-like monster. She wears a builder jacket and helmet with a Monsters Inc. symbol. She has four crab-like legs. She also has blue arms.

==Trivia==
*Cutter is supposed to resemble a safety cone in reference to her work.
*She has a habit of reminiscing on prior MIFT workers who died in terrible, yet ridiculous, accidents.
*She used traps to catch her ex-husbands which ironically none of them succeeded in doing so.
