[[File:Lionel_Cook.jpg|thumb]]'''Lionel Cook '''(voiced by [[Dorian Harewood]]) is [[Taranee Cook|Taranee's]] father, and a very good cook. It seems he works as a chef. He is allergic to fur, and by extent, Mr. Huggles. Yet he thinks animals are very cute and regrets his allergy. Lionel is a little bit overweight and is an African-American.
