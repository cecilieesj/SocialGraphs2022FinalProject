'''Marci''' is a supporting character that appears in the Playhouse Disney animated series [[Stanley (TV series)|''Stanley'']].

==Biography==
Marci, unlike her twin sister Mimi, is a tomboy, She is not doing things other girls participate in. Marci has a tomboyish personality, as she likes sports.
