{{Character|image = Rip.png|films = ''[[The Country Bears]]''|actor = [[Alex Rocco]]|alignment = Bad}}'''Rip Holland''' is the secondary antagonist of ''[[The Country Bears]]''.
==Role in the film==
He is an out-of-work concert promoter who is called on to promote the Country Bear comeback tour by Henry. He accepts to promote the show which would bring him back in business but at the end, he neglects to do it when [[Reed Thimple]] pays him not to promote the show; stating that "it's nothing personal, it's just business." They are both defeated when a large crowd shows up to enjoy the concert.
