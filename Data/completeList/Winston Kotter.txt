
[[File:Winston_Kotter.jpg|thumb]]'''Winston Kotter''' (voiced by [[Adam Wylie]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Winston/</ref> is a one-time guest character of ''[[Fillmore!]]''.

==History==
He is a Canadian ambassador and his henchmen was dealing with counterfeit baseball cards. Unlucky for [[Cornelius Fillmore|Fillmore]], Winston has a full history of diplomatic immunity and plans to pin the crime on [[Penny Madrid|Penny]] but was stopped and captured by Fillmore and the Safety Patrol.
