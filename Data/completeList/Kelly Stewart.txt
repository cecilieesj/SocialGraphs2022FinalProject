'''Kelly Stewart''' is the wife of [[Rick Stewart|Rick]] and mother of [[Darcy Stewart|Darcy]]. She's portrayed by Keli Daniels.

==Character Description==
Rick and Kelly are best friends of [[Ellen Jennings]] and [[Bennett James]].

Rick and Kelly brought their daughter to help them bring their girls closer together by pretending to end their friendship with Ellen and Bennett.
