|friends = Other Elite Puffles}}'''Flit''' is a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffle#Green_Puffle green] [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Elite_Puffles Elite Puffle]. He appears in [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force] and in the exclusive Christmas Book called "[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffle_Pal_Adventures:_Music_Makes_Magic Puffle Pal Adventures: Music Makes Magic]". He has the ability to fly and retrieve objects that [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Penguins penguins] can't reach. Although his normal 'flying into enemies' attack is weak, he can cut the ropes of things on balloons like anvils and coffee bags to drop on enemies' heads.

==Trivia==
*In the ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force]'' game, [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Jet_Pack_Guy Jet Pack Guy] said in Mission 8: "Well, Flit here flies faster '''than a snail tied to a rocket''' or even a penguin with a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Jetpack jetpack]. It's impressive, really!". This was a reference to [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Rocketsnail_Games Rocketsnail Games].

*In mission 13, there may be a glitch that makes Flit not cut the ropes off the balloon and drop the anvil on the Ultimate Protobot.
