|shows = ''[[House of Mouse]]''}}'''The Hippos''' are the servants and back-up dancers of [[Hyacinth Hippo]] in the 1940 feature film ''[[Fantasia]]''.
==Appearances==
===''[[Fantasia]]''===
They are seen attending their boss, Hyacinth Hippo, as she is prepared for the noon, and dances until she gets tired. They help her to a couch, and disappear, and are not seen until the end of the segment. As part of their routine, they are seen swinging the Alligators by the tails

===''[[Mickey's Twice Upon a Christmas]]''===
They appear in the first segment, "Belles on Ice." They serve as Daisy's back-up skaters, while the Gators serve as Minnie's back-up skaters.
