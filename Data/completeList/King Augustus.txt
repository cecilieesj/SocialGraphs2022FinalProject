'''King Augustus''' is a minor character who appeared in the animated television series ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]''.

== Role in the series ==
King Augustus is ruler of Olympia, a neighboring kingdom of [[Atlantica]]. Olympia is known for its strong warriors, though the kingdom had actually never seen a full war. When King Augustus's son, [[Thor (The Little Mermaid)|Prince Thor]], returned to the palace after having been mocked by [[Ariel]] and her friends, King Augustus declared war on Atlantica. It wasn't until [[Urchin]] was injured that Augustus ended the battle, willing to discuss peace with [[King Triton]].
