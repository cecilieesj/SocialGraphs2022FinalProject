'''Coach Wallace''' is an alien coach from ''[[Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero]]''. He coaches the red Blargtarbs. Unknown to the Blargtarbs, he has an interest in [[Coach Jackie]], the coach of the opposing team. He appears in the episode "[[Ultrahyperball]]".
