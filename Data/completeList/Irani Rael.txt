'''Irani Rael''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who is the leader of [[Nova Corps]]. She is portrayed by [[Glenn Close]] in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]''.

Irani Rael was created by Dan Abnett, Andy Lanning, Scott Hanna, Geraldo Borges and Dan Buckley.

==Appearances==
===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]''===
