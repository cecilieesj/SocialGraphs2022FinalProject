[[File:Phil_Trummer.png|thumb]]'''Phil Trummer''' is the James/Jennings neighbor who's mean and nasty. His nickname is Nasty Old Man Trummer, he even refers it to himself. He is portrayed by Jeff Doucette.

==History==
Phil is the James-Jennings' neighbor. He has some sort of a sarcastic personality, and he is sometimes mean to the kids. He came over to complain about Avery, Tyler, and Chloe doing a riot about Stan being banished from the inside of the Jennings' house.
