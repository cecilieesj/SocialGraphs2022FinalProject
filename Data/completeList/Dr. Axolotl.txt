'''Dr. Axolotl''' is the main antagonist of the ''[[TaleSpin]]'' episode "[[Bullethead Baloo]]".

== Biography ==
He is a psychotic lizard inventor who sought revenge against [[Shere Khan]] with his robot, the Mechanical Electric Laborer, which he reprogrammed to be highly destructive and nearly unstoppable. This crazy scientist is the creator of MEL. When he tried to sell the robot to Shere Khan, it made a mess, causing Khan to call Dr. Axolotl mad. Dr. Axolotl vowed revenge.

== Appearance ==
He is a very slender necked and body wrinkled light green monitor lizard with red hair and wears a top hat, and a white-violet colored scientist suit, plus blackish pants.

== Personality ==
He is often seen as crazy and vengeful. Despite his engineering skills, he seems unable to get his robot to be competent enough.

== Trivia ==

* Despite his name, axolotls are salamanders, not lizards.
