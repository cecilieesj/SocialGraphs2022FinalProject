'''Gloom''' is the main antagonist in an early version of [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]]'s [[2015]] animated feature film, ''[[Inside Out]]''. He was going to grow bigger in size as he becomes more powerful inside Riley's mind.

Early concepts of him can be seen in ''[[The Art of Inside Out]]'' book.
