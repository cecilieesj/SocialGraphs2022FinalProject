'''Gregoria''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Vampirina]]''. She is a 473 year old gargoyle who, along with [[Demi]], helps take care of and guide [[Vampirina Hauntley]] when her parents are away. She is often grumpy and sarcastic, but loves her friends deep down and enjoys spending time with them.
