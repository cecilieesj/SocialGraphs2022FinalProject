'''Bert''' is a gorilla and an employee of Ajax Gas Station in ''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]''. He usually sits on a chair and just blinks his eyes and does nothing. He made a few appearances in a few ''Mickey Mouse Works'' shorts, including "[[Mickey's New Car]]", "[[Donald's Valentine Dollar]]" and "[[Pit Crew]]".
