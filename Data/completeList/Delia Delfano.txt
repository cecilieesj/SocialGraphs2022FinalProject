'''Delia Delfano''' is a main character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's original series]] ''[[I Didn't Do It]]''.

[[File:DeliaPhotoshoot.jpg|thumb]]
==Biography==
Delia is a 16-year-old girl who is best friends with [[Lindy Watson|Lindy]], [[Jasmine (I Didn't Do It)|Jasmine]], [[Logan Watson|Logan]], and [[Garrett (I Didn't Do It)|Garrett]] and has known the four since third grade in the early 2000s and they are inseparable.

==Personality==
Delia is a lovable, eccentric person. She is very outspoken and if there's something that shouldn't be said out loud, she will go ahead and say it anyhow.

==Physical Appearance==
Delia has curly brown hair, hazel eyes, and also wears glasses.

==Relationship==
===[[Lindy Watson]]===
She and Delia are friends. Delia was upset that Lindy took her for a job in Fireman Freddy's Spaghetti Station to forgive her.
===[[Jasmine (I Didn't Do It)|Jasmine]]===
Delia and her are best friends. In The Pilot, she was jealous that Delia already had four boys and Jasmine has none In the same episode Jasmine tells Delia that Lindy took her job as a hostess.

==Trivia==

*She is the part of the theater department.
