'''Dr. John Watson''' is character in the Sherlock Holmes stories by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. Watson is [[Sherlock Holmes]]' friend and assistant, and is the first person narrator of all but four stories in the Sherlock Holmes canon. He is a minor character from [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[1986]] animated feature film, ''[[The Great Mouse Detective]]''.

==Role in the film==
Watson appears with Sherlock as silhouette cameos in the film. When introduced, Watson is observed by [[Basil of Baker Street|Basil]] and his friends while the former was discussing a case with Sherlock, and they soon depart their home to continue the investigations.

==Trivia==
*[[David Q. Dawson]] was based on Watson.
*Unlike Basil Rathbone, voice clips of Nigel Bruce (Watson's actor in the ''Sherlock Holmes'' films) were not used for the cameo part of Watson.
*[[Jude Law]], who played Dr. Watson in [[Guy Ritchie]]'s ''Sherlock Holmes'' and ''Sherlock Holmes'': ''A Game of Shadows'' and [[Martin Freeman]], who played Dr. Watson on the BBC television series ''Sherlock'', both starred in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. Law played [[Yon-Rogg]] and Freeman played [[Everett Ross]].
