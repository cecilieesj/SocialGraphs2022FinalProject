==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
In Ultron-5, he and the rest of the Serpent Society battled the Avenges in a subway. Unfortunately, Hank Pym's pacifist decisions caused the rest of the Serpent Society to escape. Death Adder was captured.

==Powers and Abilities==
He uses his tail to lift people into the air or hit them.
