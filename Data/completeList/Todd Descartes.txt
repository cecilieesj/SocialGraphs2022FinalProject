'''Todd Descartes''' was a character voiced by [[Cree Summer]]<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/The-Weekenders/Todd-Descartes/</ref> in the television series ''[[The Weekenders]]''. He is [[Carver Descartes|Carver]]'s obnoxious younger brother, who, although 3 years old, is not potty trained. He has a habit of causing trouble wherever he goes, and was ranked as Bahia Bay's greatest babysitting nightmare.
