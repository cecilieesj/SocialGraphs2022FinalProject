'''Sheriff Dan''' (formerly '''Sheriff Bob''') is a character from the ''[[Air Bud (series)|Air Bud]]'' film series. He is the Sheriff of Fernfield, [[Washington]], and the owner of [[Deputy Sniffer]] in the ''[[Air Buddies]]'' film series.
