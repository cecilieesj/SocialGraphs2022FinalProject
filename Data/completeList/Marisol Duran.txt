'''Marisol Duran''' is a famous Spanish singer who befriends The Cheetah Girls's Chanel. She is controlled by her mother throughout her life. She is fluent in Spanish. Duran appears in ''[[The Cheetah Girls 2]]''.

==Background==
Marisol was born and raised in Spain. She was famous around Barcelona, for her singing. Her mother took note and used Marisol to rise them to fame.

==Events==
===The Cheetah Girls 2===
There they meet Spanish pop artist Marisol who will also compete in the Music Festival, and her manager/mother, [[Lola Duran]], who plans a scheme to break up the Cheetah Girls, as they pose a threat to her daughter's chances in the competition, and she starts making Marisol distract Chanel from The Cheetah Girls.

Right before Chanel is going to get changed to perform with Marisol, the Festival Director informs that the Cheetah Girls are able to perform after getting a tip. Everyone is surprised when they see that the informer was his nephew, Angel. He informed that Lola tried to sabotage the Cheetahs, and his uncle reinstates the girls as the Cheetah Girls. Lola tries to dispute, but the Director will not hear it. Marisol finally tells off her mother, saying she is quitting the competition because she loves to sing and her mother is just desperate to make her a star. The Cheetah Girls then perform "Amigas Cheetahs", and as a surprise, bring Marisol onto the stage, along with Joaquin's dancing crew, Angel on the guitar and the Director on the trumpet. Their song is a hit with the crowd.

==Physical Appearance==
Marisol is a Spanish female. She was long honey-colored hair. She is slim and tall.
