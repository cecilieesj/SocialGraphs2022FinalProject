'''Sam''' is a minor
character in Disney's [[2007]] American fantasy-musical movie ''[[Enchanted]]''.

==Role in the film==
At [[Robert Philip]]'s law firm, Sam is Robert's secretary and tries to find [[Giselle]]'s transportation home, but can not locate [[Andalasia]].

== Trivia ==
* Sam is played by Jodi Benson, who is the official voice actress for [[Ariel]].
