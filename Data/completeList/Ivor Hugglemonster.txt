'''Ivor Hugglemonster''' is a character in the [[Disney Junior|''Disney Junior'']] show [[Henry Hugglemonster|''Henry Hugglemonster'']]. He is [[Momma Hugglemonster|Momma]] and [[Daddo Hugglemonster|Daddo's]] youngest child, and the younger brother of [[Cobby Hugglemonster|Cobby]], [[Summer Hugglemonster|Summer]], and [[Henry Hugglemonster (character)|Henry]].
