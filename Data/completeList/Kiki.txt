'''Kiki''' is a thirteen-year old witch-in-training who first appeared in [[Studio Ghibli]]'s ''[[Kiki's Delivery Service]]'' as the main protagonist. She is voiced by [[Kirsten Dunst]] in the Disney dub and Lisa Michelson in the Streamline dub.

==Background==
===Personality===
Kiki is upbeat, cute, curious, kind-hearted, friendly, outspoken, a little naïve, independent, responsible, and enterprising when she goes on an adventure. At the end of the movie, Kiki is becoming wiser after she rescued [[Tombo]] from the air balloon.

===Appearance===
She is 13 years old, has short dark brown hair, pale skin, a purple dress, white bloomers, red flat shoes and a red headband in the shape of a bow.
