'''Le Plume''' is a protagonist in ''[[Beauty and the Beast: Belle's Magical World|Belle's Magical World]]'', the third film installment in the ''[[Beauty and the Beast (1991 film)|Beauty and the Beast]]'' franchise. He is one of the [[Beast]]'s many servants who was turned into a quill by the [[Enchantress]]' curse.
