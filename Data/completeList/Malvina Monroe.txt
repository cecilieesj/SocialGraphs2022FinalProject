'''Malvina Monroe''' is the main antagonist from the upcoming [[2022]] Disney’s feature animated/live-action film, ''[[Disenchanted]]''. She is the local queen bee of Monroeville, who is infected by [[Giselle]]’s wish to live a fairytale life.
