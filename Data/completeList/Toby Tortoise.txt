He is the primary rival of speedy [[Max Hare]], but he finds a way to beat him by taking his time. He also makes a cameo appearance in the [[1988]] [[Touchstone Pictures]]/Amblin entertainment film, ''[[Who Framed Roger Rabbit]].'' He was voiced by Eddie Holden. Although he did not appear in ''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]'', his name was mentioned on a punch card in "[[Donald's Rocket Ruckus]]".

Toby is often represented musically by a slow theme played on a [[wikipedia:Slap tonguing|slap tongue]] [[Wikipedia:bass clarinet|bass clarinet]].

== Trivia ==
In the 1966 vinyl record "Thumper's Great Race" and book version "Thumper's Race" from 1970. Toby is friends with [[Bambi (character)|Bambi]], [[Thumper]], and [[Flower]]. In the story, Toby participates in a race against Thumper and Flower hosted by [[Friend Owl]] (known as "The Wise Old Owl" in both versions).
