'''Mimi''' is a supporting character in the Playhouse Disney animated series ''[[Stanley (TV series)|Stanley]]''. She is also the twin sister of [[Marci]]. In the Christmas episode, it is stated that the twins celebrate Kwanzaa.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Throughout the series, she wears a blue T-shirt with purple overalls with a flower and sneakers, a baton twirling uniform, purple one-piece swimsuit, blue ballet leotard, pink ballet leotard, cowgirl outfit, and a figure skating outfit.

===Powers and Abilities===
Mimi has a very good talent for baton twirling in Honest Ostrich. She is also great at figure skating and ballet.

==Role in the series==
Mimi, unlike her sister Marci, does girly things. Mimi has a feminine personality, as she likes flowers. Like the other main child characters in the series, she is a good friend of Stanley and often joins him on his adventures in ''[[The Great Big Book of Everything]]'' and is aware that his pets can talk.
