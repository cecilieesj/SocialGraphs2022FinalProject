'''Professor Jakus Maezono''' is a character and antagonist from the TV series ''[[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]]''. 

==History==
He was one of the human scientists who worked on the Prometheus Project and assisted in the creation of the [[Super Robot]]. He wanted to transfer his consciousness into the [[Prometheus Five]] model, thus allowing him to live forever as the ultimate super-weapon. [[Dr. Takeuchi]], his friend and fellow scientist, attempted to stop him, but he was killed in the process.
