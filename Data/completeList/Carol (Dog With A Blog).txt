[[File:CarolDWaB.jpg|thumb]]'''Carol''' is a retired actress in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. She's portrayed by Mary Pat Gleason.

==Character History==
Carol is a retired actress who Avery and Tyler hired, along with her husband, [[Emerson]], to play as their grandparents to turn down [[Tom Fairbanks]]' offer of buying Stan.
