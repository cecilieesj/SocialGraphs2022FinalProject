[[File:Chase.jpg|thumb]]'''Chase''' is a cheetah cub who refused to take his nap. He appeared in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode "[[Cheetah Chase]]".


==Personality==
Chase is an energetic cheetah that loves to play tag.
