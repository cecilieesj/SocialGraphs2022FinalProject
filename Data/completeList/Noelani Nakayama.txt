==Background==

===Personality===
Noelani Nakayama is a fun, vivacious, happy-to-be-there doctor. She is as local as they come. When she isn’t working as a doctor, she’s fostering her friendship with Charles, teaching him a little bit about culture in Hawaii, his new home during his residency.<ref>https://d23.com/meet-the-characters-of-doogie-kamealoha-m-d/</ref>
