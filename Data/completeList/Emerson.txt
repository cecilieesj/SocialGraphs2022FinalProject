[[File:EmersonDWaB.jpg|thumb]]'''Emerson''' is a retired actor in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He's portrayed by Michael Kagan.

==Character History==
Emerson is a retired actor who Avery and Tyler hired, along with his wife, [[Carol (Dog With A Blog)|Carol]], to play as their grandparents to turn down [[Tom Fairbanks]]' offer of buying Stan.
