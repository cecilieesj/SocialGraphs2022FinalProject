|powers = Fire, flight|enemies = [[Iron Man]]|personality = Evil, dark, ominous}}'''Fin Fang Foom''' is a dragon-like alien who is one of [[Iron Man|Iron Man's]] enemies.

Fin Fang Foom was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Iron Man 3]]''====
The dragon tattoos on [[Aldrich Killian]]'s chest are a reference to Fin Fang Foom.

===Marvel Animated Universe===
====''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]''====
He was summoned to battle the hulks by [[Sauron]].<ref>http://www.thefutoncritic.com/listings/20140124disneyxd07/</ref>  According to Sauron he is one of the few creatures he could not control until he drains Impossible Mans powers.
