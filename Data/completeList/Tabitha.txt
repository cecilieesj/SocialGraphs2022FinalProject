}}'''Tabitha''' is [[Tiberius]]' baby sister and a tiger princess who appears in the episode "[[Adventures In Tigersitting]]".

==Appearance==
Tabitha is a tiger cub with gray fur and purple stripes. She has purple eyes and a dark pink nose. She also wears a collar with the T.O.T.S symbol. She sports a diaper with yellow trim and leaf symbols on them. At the end of "[[Adventures In Tigersitting]]", she wears a gold crown on her head.
