'''Hilda Hippo''' is an anthropomorphic purple hippopotamus who appears in ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]'', ''[[Mickey Mouse Mixed-Up Adventures]]'', and ''[[Mickey Mouse Funhouse]]''. She is voiced by [[April Winchell]].

== Trivia ==
* She is inspired by, but not to be confused with, [https://everythingbusytown.fandom.com/wiki/Hilda_Hippo the Richard Scarry character of the same name] and [[Hyacinth Hippo]] from ''[[Fantasia]]''.
