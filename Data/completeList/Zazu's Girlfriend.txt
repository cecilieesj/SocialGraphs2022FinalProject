}}'''Zazu's Girlfriend''' is a female hornbill in the comic ''[[A Girlfriend for Zazu]]''.

==Background==
===Appearance===
She had blue feathers and black tipped wings with the feather's themselves being purple. Her eyes are green.

===Personality===
Despite being flashy and showy, she is not haughty and stuck up. She likes and takes [[Zazu]] for who he is, as opposed to what [[Simba]] and [[Nala]] try to make him be.

==Information==
Zazu and two other hornbills spot her and are immediately infatuated with her. Simba and Nala pick up on this and try to get the shy Zazu together with her. All their attempts end up with Zazu hurt or embarrassed and in the end, when Zazu is really in trouble, she comes to his rescue and becomes his girlfriend.
