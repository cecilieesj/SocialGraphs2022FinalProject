|fate = Defeated by Beck and his allies}}The '''Apaches''' are the main antagonists of [[Disney]]'s [[1963]] live-action film ''[[Savage Sam]]''. They are a tribe of unscrupulous Indians who have in their number one slightly wiser Comanche Chief.

==Role in the film==
[[Travis Coates|Travis]], [[Arliss Coates|Arliss]], and [[Lisbeth Searcy|Lisbeth]] are taken captive by Apaches stealing horses. Travis is left behind when the Apaches are chased by a cavalry patrol and is found by [[Savage Sam (character)|Savage Sam]] (thanks to his keen sense of smell).

Beck gathers up a search party including [[Bud Searcy]] and neighbor, Wiley Crup. Led by Savage Sam, who tracks the Apaches despite an injured paw and a hailstorm, they find and rescue Arliss and Lisbeth.
