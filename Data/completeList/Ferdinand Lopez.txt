}}'''Ferdinand Lopez''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and antagonist. He is a mercenary working for [[Batroc]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Captain America: The Winter Soldier]]''===
Ferdinand Lopez is the right-hand of Georges Batroc, whom helped to hijack the [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] ship, the Lemurian Star. Eventually, he and his comrades were capturades when a rescue team of SHIELD came to help the hostages, including Jasper Sitwell.
