==Appearance==

==History==
She has a one-word speaking role in Test of the Tested. When [[Ingrid Third|Ingrid]] is part of a circle with other students, as part of a support group and the '''Facilitator''' asks them to use an adjactive to describe the person behind the theft of the S.A.T.T.Y. 9 test sheets, Nerdy Girl answers "villainous".
