'''Tutterly T. Tutter''' is [[Tutter]]'s great-grandfather in ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. He was a famous explorer who collected cheese from around the world, and buried his pile. He appears in flashbacks in the book ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Raiders_of_the_Lost_Cheese Raiders of the Lost Cheese]'' and is mentioned by Tutter in [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_202:_Raiders_of_the_Lost_Cheese the episode on which the book is based] when Tutter and [[Ojo]] find a map for location of Tutterly's cheese trove.
