|status = Presumably Deceased}}'''Desiree D'Allure''' is a one-time character and the main antagonist of the ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]'' episode "''[[Love is a Many Splintered Thing]]''".

==Background==
She is the leader of a gang which makes its living by stealing antique furniture.
===Physical Appearance===
She is a tall and slender cream mouse with brown hair, light lavender eyelids, aqua-green eyes, red lips, a pink nose and wears a black hat, a dark magenta turtleneck long-sleeved sweater and a purple midi-slip skirt.

==Trivia==
*Desiree is an anglicization of the French name Désirée meaning desired, and how it reflects her stealing antique furniture, and her liking of jewels and art.
*Allure reflects on how she used her charm to manipulate Monty into getting what she wants.
