'''Thad Ross''' is a character in the television series ''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]''. He is the future son of [[Hulk]] and [[Betty Ross]], and also has a twin sister named [[Betts Ross]], to which they call themselves the Gamma Twins.
==Background==
===Powers and Abilities===
He possesses superhuman physical abilities as a result of inheriting gamma irradiated DNA. He has considerable muscle and bone mass which grants high levels of superhuman strength. Since he is probably [[Red Hulk]]'s grandson, he can probably heat up like him.
