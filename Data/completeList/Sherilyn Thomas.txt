'''Sherilyn Thomas''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series [[Big Shot (TV series)|''Big Shot'']]. She is currently the Principal at Westbrook Schools for Girls.

==Background==
===Personality===
Sherilyn is tough as nails that wants what is best for the school and her students. She cares about her students but, she has a heart of gold.
