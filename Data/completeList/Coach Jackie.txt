'''Coach Jackie''' is an alien coach from ''[[Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero]]''. She coaches the blue Tarbgarbs. Unknown to the Tarbgarbs, she has an interest in [[Coach Wallace]], the coach of the opposing team. She appears in the episode "[[Ultrahyperball]]".
