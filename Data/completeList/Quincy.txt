'''Quincy''' is one of two the tritagonists (alongside [[Annie]]) of ''[[Little Einsteins]]''. He is the talented musician of the group. 

==Background==
===Personality===
Quincy is a kind, sweet, fun, loyal, and trustworthy little boy. He is also very talented in playing all types of musical instruments. He is around five years of age.

===Friends===
His friends are [[Leo (Little Einsteins)|Leo]], [[June (Little Einsteins)|June]], [[Annie (Little Einsteins)|Annie]], and [[Rocket (Little Einsteins)|Rocket]]. He also has other friends, but they are not human, like a car named Carmine and an ivory-billed woodpecker named Tapper.

==Appearances==
===''[[Little Einsteins]]''===
''Little Einsteins'' is the only franchise he has ever been shown. He appeared in every episode.

===Disney Parks===
He made an appearance at Disney World as a puppet in Disney Junior Live on Stage! in Disney's Hollywood Studios Theme Park and Animation Courtyard. Also, he meets and greets people, along with the other Little Einsteins.

==Trivia==
*He is named after famous composer Quincy Jones. 
*He is the second character of Little Einsteins who has a solo mission, the first being Annie.
*He might have arachnophobia (fear of spiders).
*He is also afraid of the dark.
*His pajamas are green.
*In ''Rocket's Firebird Rescue'', Quincy was given Music Power and may have it permanently.
*In ''A Galactic Goodnight'', he has his instruments blanket to hug at bedtime. One time, he gave Rocket the blanket to hug, but that did not make Rocket fall asleep at all.
