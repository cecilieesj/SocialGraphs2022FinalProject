'''Gooti Terez''' is a character, who appears in the [[Disney XD]] animated series, ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''. She is a female Theelin who is a member of Iron Squadron, alongside with [[Mart Mattin]], Jonner Jin, and R3.

==Trivia==
*Gooti was named after and designed to resemble Andi Gutierrez, a digital communications manager at [[Lucasfilm]], the host of Rebels Recon, and the co-host of The Star Wars Show.
