'''Hallie''' is one of [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]]'s friends in the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
Hallie is a sassy purple hippopotamus with a Texas accent. She is Doc's nurse in the toy clinic. Hallie likes and knows how to dance in "[[Break Dancer]]", and she was great at jump rope as she seems to be a little tomboyish by the way she talks.

===Physical appearance===
Hallie is an obese (Rude) stuffed purple hippo with red glasses, a red and white striped apron with a yellow tag, a white shirt, and a red and white hat.

==Trivia==
*She's named after the "Nurse Hallie" incarnation of the "Hallie Hippo" toy series.
