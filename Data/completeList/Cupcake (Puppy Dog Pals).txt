Cupcake is a recurring character in ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]'' and a minor antagonist as well. She is often found at the dog park with her henchman Rufus She becomes more friends after "The Fang Fairy".

== Appearance ==
She is a lavender/white Maltese dog, with cupcake shaped hair ties that hold her hair in pigtails. She has a very small tail and a golden pup tag (also shaped like a cupcake.)

== Personality ==
She is rather snooty and also gets annoyed by the pugs' mischievous antics. She shares similarities with [[Hissy]], though she has much less tolerance for [[Bingo_(Puppy_Dog_Pals)|Bingo]] and [[Rolly_(Puppy_Dog_Pals)|Rolly]]. She’s also a terrible singer. Later she does warm up to the pugs.
