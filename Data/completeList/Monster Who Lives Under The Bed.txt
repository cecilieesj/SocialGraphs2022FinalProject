The '''Monster Who Lives Under The Bed '''is a minor character from ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''.

==Background==
===Physical Description===
This monster is rarely seen with only his red eyes and sharp teeth visible.

===Personality===
He is scary and creepy.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''===
The monster is seen briefly during the opening song "[[This is Halloween]]" while he's under the bed saying the line "I am the one hiding under your bed, teeth ground sharp and eyes glowing red!".

==Disney Parks==

===[[Haunted Mansion Holiday]]===
The Monster under the bed makes a physical appearance for the queue of this ride, being shown guiding guests to the load area with a sign for, "Sleigh Rides". He is also depicted as having tentacles with candy-cane like patterns on them.

The monster is also featured throughout the ride in artwork such as in a stretching room portrait, the load area's "Scary Christmas" display, and one of [[Madame Leota]]'s tarot cards.
