'''Fahari''' is a leopard who appears in ''[[The Lion Guard]]''. He is part of [[Makucha's Army]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Muck like [[Makucha]], Fahari is arrogant and unfriendly and is ready to fight any one who come into his territory. However he knows when to back down from a challenge.

==History==
===[[The Harmattan]]===
Fahari first appears in "[[The Harmattan]]" along side Makucha and Jiona when steps into Back Lands on the way to the [[Tree of Life (The Lion Guard)|Tree of Life]]. They start to attack the guard when a huge dust storm hits. Makucha's leap finds the Lion guard in a cave.

=== [[Triumph of the Roar]] ===
Fahari helps Makucha's Army fight the Night Pride and the Lion Guard. He gets defeated because Kion uses the [[Roar of the Elders]] on him and blasts him far far away from the Tree of Life.

==Relationships==
===Friends===
====[[Makucha]]====
Fahari is good friends with Makucha. They work together to try and get rid of the Lion Guard.

====[[Chuluun]]====
Fahari and Chuluun are on good terms. They work together to stop the Lion Guard and the Night Pride.

====[[Ora]]====
Fahari and Ora are on good terms. They work together to stop the Lion Guard and the Night Pride.

====[[Mama Binturong (character)|Mama Binturong]]====
Fahari and Mama Binturong are on good terms. They work together to stop the Lion Guard and the Night Pride.

====[[Smun]]====
Smun and Fahari are on good terms. Smun finds Fahari and sends him to Makucha to help get rid of the Lion Guard.

=== Enemies ===
====[[Kion]]====
Fahari hates Kion Kion uses the Roar of the Elders and defeats him along with the rest of Makucha's Army.

====[[Bunga]], [[Fuli]], [[Beshte]], [[Ono]] And [[Anga]]====
Fahari hates the rest of the members of the Lion Guard. He tries to get rid of them so they can go to the Tree of Life.

====[[Makini]]====
Fahari hates Makini since she's friends with the Lion Guard.

====[[Rani (The Lion Guard)|Rani]]====
Fahari hates Rani since she is the queen of the Tree of Life.

====[[Baliyo]], [[Nirmala]] And [[Surak]]====
Fahari hates the rest of the Night Pride. He trapped them inside the Tree of Life.
