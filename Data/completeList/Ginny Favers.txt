'''Ginny Favers''' is the tritagonist of the film ''[[Song of the South]]''. She is a poor girl who lives with her mean brothers, [[Jake and Joe Favers|Jake and Joe]], and their mother. She is friends with Johnny and has a pet puppy named Teenchy whom she gives to Johnny after her brothers threaten to drown it. She is usually barefoot and wears a dress with blue collar and sleeves apart from when she is preparing to go to Johnny's birthday party where she wears a white dress fashioned from her mother's wedding dress.
