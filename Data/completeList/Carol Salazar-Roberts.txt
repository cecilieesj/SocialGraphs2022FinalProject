== Biography ==

=== Early Life ===
At some point Carol married her wife Dana before the events of Season One.

=== Season One ===

==== [[The Auditions]] ====
On Nini's first day of school Carol and her wife Dana go get Donuts for their family.

==== Blocking ====
Her daughter Nini's ex-boyfriend Ricky Bowen is having issues at home with his parents getting separated so, he goes over to Nini's house where he feels safe. Carol lets him spend the night and gives him a T-Shirt to sleep in that says "SLC Pride" that is from a Salt Lake City Pride Parade.

=== '''Season Two''' ===

==== Valentine’s Day ====
Carol and her wife Dana Salazar-Roberts go Ice Skating on Valentine’s Day and she twisted her ankle by trying not to “steamroll” a child.

==Trivia==

* She gives [[Ricky Bowen]] her "SLC Pride" T-Shirt to sleep in when he comes over to spend the night at her house where he feels safe to get away from his problems at home in Blocking.
* It is unknown what Carol's last name is but since her daughter Nini has a hyphenated last name that is probably the last name she uses.
* Carol and her wife Dana are the first same sex couple to be parents of a main character in a Disney TV series.
