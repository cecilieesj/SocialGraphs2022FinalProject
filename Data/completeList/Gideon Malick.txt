'''Gideon Malick''' is a leader of [[HYDRA]] and a member of [[World Security Council]] in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] and first appeared in the [[2012]] [[Marvel Studios|Marvel]] action film, ''[[The Avengers (film)|The Avengers]]'' and frequently appeared in the [[Marvel Studios|Marvel]] television series ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''.

==[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]==
===''[[The Avengers (film)|The Avengers]]''===
Gideon Malick in 2012 served on the World Security Council, that oversees S.H.I.E.L.D and served as [[Nick Fury]]'s advisors' during the Battle of New York, however secretly a HYDRA infiltrator.

===''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''===
After the fall of [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]], and the death of [[Doctor List]] and [[Wolfgang von Strucker]], the heads of HYDRA, Gideon Malick became the new head of HYDRA. Gideon Malick was responsible for the threat of manipulating the AUTC for collecting powerful [[Inhumans]] as he was an advisor to the [[President Matthew Ellis|the president]] and serving on the [[World Security Council]] he was to powerful even too be taken down by the president. He also joined with [[Grant Ward]] in destroying SHIELD.
