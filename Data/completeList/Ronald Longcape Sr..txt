'''Ronald Longcape Sr.'''(portrayed by Maurice Godin) is a character and antagonist in ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]'' . He is [[Ronald Longcape Jr.]]'s father. He is evil, though he's not very good at it. He enjoys [[Jell-o]], as seen in "[[Saving WizTech]]" Parts 1 and 2.

He is portrayed by Maurice Godin.
