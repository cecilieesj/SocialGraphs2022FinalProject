'''Bonnie''' is a female rabbit who appeared on ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. She lives with her son [[Billy (Bear in the Big Blue House)|Billy]] and their home was damaged by flooding from the storm in "[[Welcome to Woodland Valley]]." She reluctantly, but gratefully accepts help from [[Jeremiah Tortoise]] in getting back on her feet. She later appeared in the episode "[[A Trip to the General Store]]."
