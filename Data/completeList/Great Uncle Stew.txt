'''Great Uncle Stew''' is a supporting character in ''[[Stanley's Dinosaur Round-Up]]''. He is the great-uncle of [[Stanley Griff|Stanley]] and [[Lionel Griff]] because he is the uncle of [[Mark Griff]], but it is unknown which of ''his'' parents he is related to, or whether he is the widower of either parent's sister. He owns a dude ranch and owes money before [[Rockin' Rory]] converts it into a parking lot for an amusement park.

== Trivia ==
* This was John Ritter's final acting role as he passed away 2 years before the movie's release. The movie is dedicated to him.
