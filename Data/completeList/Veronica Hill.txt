'''Veronica Hill''' is a recurring character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is the news reporter for the Action News.

==Background==

===Physical appearance===
Veronica Hill has tan skin with dark brown hair. She wears a formal blue suit with a white undershirt and a blue skirt.
