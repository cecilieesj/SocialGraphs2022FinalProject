'''Summer Hugglemonster''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney Junior]] show ''[[Henry Hugglemonster]]''. She is voiced by [[Hynden Walch]] .

==Personality==
Summer is the only daughter, second eldest of the Hugglemonster family, and Henry's older sister. She sings and dances almost everywhere she goes. She often gets upset and overreacts when things are hopeless. She also hates disappointing her fans. She is one year older than her brother, Henry Hugglemonster (aged 6), and one year younger than her older brother, Cobby Hugglemonster (aged 8). She is around seven years of age.

==Appearance==
Summer wears red shoes throughout the entire series as well as a yellow hairbow, although she takes off the shoes when she's swimming. She often wears an aqua tutu for ballet, sometimes with a silver tiara.
