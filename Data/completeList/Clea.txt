'''Clea''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]'', played by [[Charlize Theron]]. She is a sorceress with vast amount of magical power.

Clea was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Steve Ditko.
