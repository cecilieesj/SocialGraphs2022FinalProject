|quote = "I, the amazing Madame Credenza, see that your future is uncertain."}}'''Madame Credenza''' is a gypsy moth from the ''[[Timon & Pumbaa]]'' TV series who appears in "[[Beetle Romania]]".

==Role in the series==
Madame Credenza lives in Romania. When [[Timon]] and [[Pumbaa]] go to [[Madame Credenza's Place|her place]], she tells Pumbaa that his future is uncertain. When Madame Credenza is about to tell Timon his future, the meerkat eats the moth. After Pumbaa makes Timon spit her out, Credenza puts a curse on the meerkat, which will not take effect until the next morning.
