==Personality==
Timothy is a kind and loving 10-year-old boy. He loves his family and making friends.

==Relationships==
===[[Joni Jerome]]===
Joni is an older girl that Timothy likes. They met when Timothy was getting beat up by the school bullies. Later, they meet at the bullies' house where he tries to get a kiss from her. However, she rolls down his socks and sees his leaves. Timothy panics and kicks her in the head.
