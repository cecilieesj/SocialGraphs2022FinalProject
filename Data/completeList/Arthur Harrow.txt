'''Arthur Harrow''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Moon Knight (TV series)|Moon Knight]]'', played by [[Ethan Hawke]].

Arthur Harrow was created by Alan Zelenetz, E.R. Cruz and Chris Warner.

A former vessel of Egyptian god [[Khonshu]] turned religious zealot and cult leader associated with the goddess [[Ammit]] who sees [[Moon Knight]] as an obstacle to him "healing the world", and who encourages Marc Spector to embrace his inner darkness.
