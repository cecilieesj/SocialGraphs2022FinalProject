|quote = }}'''Amanda Lopez''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series, ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''. She is a Hispanic student in [[Milo Murphy|Milo's]] class who is a perfectionist.
==Background==
===Personality and Traits===
Amanda is obsessed with order and perfection, and sometimes worries when Milo is about as things can definitely go wrong when he is around. As the series progresses however, she has shown to have become more calm towards Murphy's law, but still remains insecure. Amanda has also started to grow on Milo, developing a possible crush on him. Evident when she allowed Milo to part take in the last round of a cooking competition with her,<ref>"[[Cake 'Splosion!]]"</ref> kissed him on the cheek,<ref>"[[Managing Murphy's Law]]"</ref> and became jealous when she saw Joni having a moment with Milo.<ref>"[[Spy Little Sister!]]"</ref>

===Physical Appearance===
Amanda has tan skin and a slender figure. Her hair is dark violet and shoulder-length with a dark magenta headband. She wears pink pearl earrings and has dark purple lips. Amanda's standard wardrobe is a pink jacket with magenta striped lining on the collar, a pair of red violet pants with pink cuffs and pink flats.
