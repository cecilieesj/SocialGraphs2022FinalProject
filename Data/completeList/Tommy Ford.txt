'''Tommy Ford''' (also known as '''Captain Cole''' in an alternate timeline) is the male protagonist in the graphic novelization of ''[[Space Mountain]]''. According to [[Anne Krell]], "Cole" had gone back in time to have an adventure with his younger self. Along with his alternate self, Stella Macri, Desoto, [[Renard]], and [[ARTIE]], he saves existence from Krell.
