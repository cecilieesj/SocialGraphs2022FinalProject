|quote = |status=Active}}'''Natalie Bagley''' is a reoccurring character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''. Natalie was a sophomore at [[East High School]] in Season 1 and Season 2. Natalie is the Stage Manager for the [[East High School]]'s Drama Club.<ref>[[The Auditions]]</ref> 

==Background==

===Personality===
Natalie is known for being sassy and sarcastic. As the stage manager, Natalie shows confidence and critique very commonly. At times, she may need her emotional support hamster to comfort her during hard times. She can be described as needy, as Miss Jenn mentioned in Blocking. She is often sick or going through some randomly dramatic life event.

==Trivia==

* She missed The Read-Through because her Glands were swollen.
* She has a emotional support hamster named Elliott.
* Her last line is "Buckle up Wildcats" in the first episode of both Season 1 and Season 2.
* She says the last line in both the Season 1 and Season 2 finale.
* She is the only character who has never been a main character to have a confessional.
