==History==
He is [[Penny Proud|Penny]]'s surfer friend. Duke originally came to the show as a flat character, but later grew into a bigger role as the show progressed. He was a rival to [[Sticky Webb|Sticky]] after [[Dijonay Jones|Dijonay]] used him to make Sticky jealous, but later he dumped Dijonay, and he and Sticky became good friends.<ref>Surf and Turf</ref>

==Physical Appearance==
He has crinkly hair, necklace choker, white no sleeve v-neck shirt, red Hawaiian shorts with sunscreen, and some sandals.
