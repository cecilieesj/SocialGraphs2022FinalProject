|occupation = Lawyer|home = [[Paris]]|likes=Providing Skinner some wisdom|dislikes=Skinner going crazy|status=Active}}'''Talon Labarthe''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]•[[Pixar]]'s [[2007]] animated feature film, ''[[Ratatouille]]''. He is [[Skinner]]’s lawyer and confidant.
==Appearances==
===''[[Ratatouille]]''===
Talon Labarthe is [[Chef Skinner|Skinner]]'s slick and sophisticated lawyer who is first seen when Skinner shockingly finds out [[Alfredo Linguini|Linguini]] is [[Auguste Gusteau|Gusteau]]'s son. Finding a hair in the late chef's hat, Labarthe performs a DNA test and confirms that Linguini is Gusteau's son. He was able to keep calm when Skinner loses control of himself after hearing about that situation, and is very stride and confident to do so. He advises Skinner to wait out the will's two year deadline and he can fire Linguini and nobody shall ever know about it. He is not seen anywhere else in the film.
