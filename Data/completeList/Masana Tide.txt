'''Masana Tide''' is a character in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' universe. She is first introduced in the comic series ''Darth Vader: Dark Lord of the Sith''.

After the Great Jedi Purge, Masana joined the [[Galactic Empire]], becoming an inquisitor known as the '''Ninth Sister'''.

==Background==
===Powers and Abilities===
====Powers====
*'''[[The Force]]:''' Masana has a very strong connection to the Force.
**'''Mind Probe:''' Masana can telepathically sense and sift through the thoughts and memories of other sentient beings.
**'''Force Sense:''' Tide can sense another sentient being's emotions, the future, ripples in the Force caused by momentous or traumatic events, impending danger, and the presence of other light or dark side Force-users.
*'''Advanced Strength:''' Tide utilizes her Force powers to amplify her physical strength, this made her physically stronger than most other sentient beings.

====Abilities====
*'''Expert Lightsaber Duelist:''' Masana is a highly skilled lightsaber duelist.

===Weapons and Equipment===
====Lightsaber====
Taron's valued weapon and possession is her red-bladed lightsaber.
*'''[[Lightsaber|Double-bladed spinning lightsaber]]:''' Masana had built a red double-bladed spinning lightsaber.
