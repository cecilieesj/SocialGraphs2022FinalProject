'''Alan Diaz''' is the protagonist of ''[[Mighty Med]]''. He is played by Devan Leos.
==Background Information==
Alan's uncle Horace is the hospital's Chief of Staff. Alan was sent to Mighty Med to learn the profession. Upset that “normos” has discovered [[Mighty Med]], Alan has made it his life’s work to make [[Kaz]] and [[Oliver (Mighty Med)|Oliver]] miserable and get them fired.
