|status = Deceased}}'''Lord Ascot''' is a minor character of the [[2010]] film ''[[Alice in Wonderland (2010 film)|Alice in Wonderland]]''. He is portrayed by Tim Piggott Smith.

==Appearances==
===''[[Alice in Wonderland (2010 film)|Alice in Wonderland]]''===
He is shown to be a very rich man in the movie. At the start of the movie he is seen in a meeting with Alice's dad Charles Kingsleigh, who explains to Lord Ascot and a couple of other men about an expedition to China. The meeting is interrupted by a very young Alice who woke up from a bizarre dream.

Fast forward 13 years, Alice attends a party held at Lord Ascot's mansion. This party is a surprise proposal from Lord Ascot's son, Hamish, which everyone but Alice knew about. Alice declines the proposal and, after a discussion with Lord Ascot, goes on an expedition to China.

===''[[Alice Through the Looking Glass]]''===
Prior to the events of ''[[Alice Through the Looking Glass]]'', during Alice's three year trip to [[China]], Lord Ascot died from unknown causes.
