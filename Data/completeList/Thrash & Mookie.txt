
[[File:Thrash.jpg|thumb|256px]]'''Thrash & Mookie''' (voiced by [[Jeff Bennett]] and [[April Winchell]])<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0071818/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0934589/?ref_=tt_cl_t11</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Mighty-Ducks-The-Animated-Series/Thrash/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Mighty-Ducks-The-Animated-Series/Mook/</ref> are [[Nosedive Flashblade|Nosedive]]'s seemingly only human friends who dress like punk rockers in the television show ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]''. They run the comic store called "Captain Comics" of which Nosedive is a frequent customer. They were the first people that they met on Earth. Thrash is male and Mookie is a female.
