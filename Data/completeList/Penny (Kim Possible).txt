'''Penny''' (voiced by [[Tara Strong]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Kim-Possible/Penny/</ref> is a one-time guest character of the [[Disney Channel]] [[Disney Channel Original Series|Original Series]] ''[[Kim Possible]]''.

==Biography==
Penny was briefly in love with [[Ron Stoppable]] when he saw her eyes. However, Ron was under the bad influence with [[Dr. Drakken]]'s Truth ray device which forces Ron to tell the truth, but also told a lie that he no longer has feelings for her.
