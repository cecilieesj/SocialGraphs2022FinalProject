'''90's Adventure Bear''' is a character featured in the [[Disney XD]] show, ''[[Pickle and Peanut]]''. He is the title character of his own TV show and the leader of the 90's Adventure Team. [[Pickle]] and [[Peanut]] cherish him as their favorite childhood hero.

==Role in the series==
90's Adventure Bear first appears in the [[90's Adventure Bear|episode of the same]] name. While Pickle and Peanut are attending an outdoor garage sale, Nineties shows up eating a jar of mixed fruit. When Nineties spills fruit juice from jar onto his bathrobe and says "Ronkledonk", Peanut recognizes this as Nineties' signature catchphrase from his TV show. Pickle and Peanut introduce themselves, telling Nineties about how much they enjoyed his show. Nineties tells them he hasn't been doing any adventures nowadays and that he's down on his luck. In an attempt to get Nineties' spirit of adventure back, Pickle and Peanut pretended to be Zip Skyboard and Major Manslaughter.
