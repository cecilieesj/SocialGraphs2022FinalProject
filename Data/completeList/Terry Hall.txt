'''Terry Hall''' is a hockey player from [[Minneapolis]], [[Minnesota]]. His first and only appearance was in ''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''. He played for the [[District 5 Ducks]], along with his older brother [[Jesse Hall]]. Terry plays at the forward position and wears the number 1. He is portrayed by Jussie Smollett.
