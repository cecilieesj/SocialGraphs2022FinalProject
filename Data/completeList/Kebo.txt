'''Kebo''' is a supporting antagonist of the [[Marvel Television|Marvel]] series, ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'', being a minor antagonist in Season 2 and a major antagonist in Season 3. He was an enforcer who worked for [[HYDRA]] and later became the right-hand-man of [[Grant Ward]].

He was portrayed by Daz Crawford.
