Savannah is a student at Ellie Blake's school and the main antagonist of the 2018 Disney Channel original movie, ''Freaky Friday''.

<br />

== Background ==
Savannah is the typical high school mean girl. Cruel, mean and selfish. She is obsessed with winning the hunt. She seems to especially have a hatred for Ellie, and personally attacks her severally.
