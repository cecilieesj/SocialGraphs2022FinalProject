'''Joe''' is an construction man in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Character History==
Joe worked with Bennett on building the trampoline. Bennett hid him because he does not want admit to Ellen that he asked for help. When the parents found out the presents are missing, Bennett thinks Joe stole them.

==Trivia==
*He was not credited in the episode.
*When Ellen asked who Joe was, Bennett told her the elf was Joe.
