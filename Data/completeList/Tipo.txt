|siblings=[[Chaca]] (older sister)<br>[[Yupi]] (younger brother)<br>[[Kuzco]] (adoptive brother)}}'''Tipo''' is a supporting character in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2000]] animated feature film, ''[[The Emperor's New Groove]]''. He is the oldest son of [[Pacha]] and [[Chicha]].

==Appearances==
===''[[The Emperor's New Groove]]''===
He is the son of [[Pacha]]. While his dad is taking [[Kuzco]] to the palace, he has a vision where his dad went over a waterfall. His mother calms him down. After [[Chaca|his sister]] tells them about her dream, the two siblings get into an argument over which dream could actually happen. This scene implies that they are psychic.

===''[[Kronk's New Groove]]''===
TIpo appears in this movie as one of the campers at Camp Chippamunka. Toward the end of the memory, he follows [[Kronk]]'s advice which was, "Do whatever it takes to win." He put itching powder in [[Ms. Birdwell]]'s troop's hand chalk. When Birdwell finds out and scolds Tipo, Kronk defends him but also alienates his true love. Later, Tipo feels guilty and runs off to find Birdwell, after which she and Kronk are brought back together.
