|home = PNK house}}'''Carrie Williams''' is a character in the [[2013]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated film, ''[[Monsters University]]''. She is the fearless leader of the [[Python Nu Kappa]] sorority, who goes against the [[Roar Omega Roar]] fraternity in the [[Scare Games]]. She is exactly the same as her sorority sisters physically, and differs only in her coloration. She is the reddest of the PNKs, and the only one who has bright red hair combined with pink skin.
