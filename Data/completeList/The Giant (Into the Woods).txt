}}'''The Giant's Wife''' (or '''The Giant''') is the main antagonist of the [[2014]] [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] film, ''[[Into the Woods]]''.

==Role in the film==
The Giantess was mentioned by [[Jack (Into The Woods)|Jack]] when he journeyed into the castle of her husband, the male Giant, where she kept Jack hidden from him. After her husband dies from falling down Jack's beanstalk, she enters the woods below intending to get vengeance against the boy. She appears in the woods demanding where Jack was to the group of people, killing Jack's mother in the process. The Baker's Wife falls off a cliff and dies while running from her. To stop her rampage, [[Baker (Into the Woods)|the Baker]], Jack, [[Cinderella (Into the Woods)|Cinderella]], and [[Little Red Riding Hood (Into the Woods)|Little Red Riding Hood]] fake Jack's death to the Giant while she gets hit by the Baker and Jack and falls over, resulting in her death.

==Trivia==
*She is from the original tale "Jack and the Beanstalk" as the wife of the Giant. However, this version is based more closely on the one from the original musical of ''Into the Woods.''
*The Giant was the only giant alive after her husband died
*In the original production, she is an unseen character.
