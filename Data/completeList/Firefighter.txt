[[File:Firefighter.jpg|thumb]]'''Firefighter''' is a random Firefighter who helps get a dog, Toto, get out of a hole in the ground.

==Physical Appearance==
This Firefighter is a white male. He has red hair and has a slight build.

==Events==
===The Cheetah Girls (Movie)===
When [[Galleria Garibaldi]]'s dog, Toto, is stuck in a hole he comes to help her. She then meets up with the other Cheetahs, then they help Toto out of the hole.
