|enemies = [[Wildwing Flashblade]]}}'''Phineas P. Viper''' is an industrialist who appears in the ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]'' episode "[[Beak to the Future]]". 

==Role in the episode==
He helps [[Lord Dragaunus|Dragaunus]] with his plan to create a Super Raptor. In the future, he rules Earth with an iron fist while the Saurians are off conquering the galaxy, and he genetically emerges himself with his pet snakes in that time. He is mentioned again in "[[Bringing Down Baby]]" when [[Wildwing Flashblade|Wildwing]], Duke, and Tanya find out that he is still helping Dragaunus with his plans.
