|status= Active}}'''Rosa''' is one of the main characters of the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Alice's Wonderland Bakery]]''. She's the daughter of the [[Queen of Hearts (Alice's Wonderland Bakery)|Queen of Hearts]], the Princess of Hearts, one of [[Alice (Alice's Wonderland Bakery)|Alice]]'s best friends and her most artistic friend.   

==Physical Appearance==
Rosa has tanned skin, black hair in big curled pigtails with red heart-shaped hair ties at the ends, and wears a golden crown that has four hearts for the top, a sleeveless pink and purple dress with a red heart in the center and little red hearts on the purple stripes, pink leggings patterned with red hearts, and indigo slippers with red hearts on the tips. 

At bedtime she wears a red nightgown with pink buttons, short pink pajama bottoms patterned with red hearts, and pink slippers with red hearts that each have a pink heart in the center.
