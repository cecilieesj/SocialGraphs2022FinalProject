'''Kingo Sunen''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by [[Kumail Nanjiani]].

Kingo was created by [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appeearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]''====
Kingo is one of ten superpowered Eternals sent by the Celestial Arishem to Earth to exterminate the invasive Deviants. The last Deviants are apparently killed in 1521. However, he and the rest of the Eternals wait for Arishem to tell them that they can leave.

During his stay on Earth, Kingo became a big Bollywood movie star.

====''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]''====
While Kingo doesn't appear in the series, he is mentioned by [[Ms. Marvel|Kamala]] and [[Kamran]], as they discuss Bollywood movies.
