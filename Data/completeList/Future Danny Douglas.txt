|alignment = Neutral}}"'''Future Danny Douglas'''", otherwise known as "'''Future Danny'''", is a version of [[Danny Douglas]] from an unknown future, and a character in ''[[Future-Worm!]]''.
==Appearance==
Being a different version of Danny Douglas, Future Danny holds a lot of resemblance with his present counterpart: he is just as short as him and has a similar physical appearance, but his hair is grey, he wears a green jacket, a hybrid set of glasses made out of a normal set of glasses and a monocle, grey pants, black shoes, and a white shirt reading "Future Science!".
