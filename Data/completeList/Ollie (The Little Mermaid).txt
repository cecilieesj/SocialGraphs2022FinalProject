|likes=Being with Gabriella, having muscles|status=Active}}'''Ollie''' is a blue vocal octopus who appears in two episodes; "[[Wish Upon a Starfish]]" and "[[Ariel's Treasures]]".
==Appearances==
===''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid: The Series]]''===
He is the close friend and interpreter of [[Gabriella]], a deaf mermaid. He has spots on the sides of his head and a patch on his left eye. In his first appearance, he is shown with Gabriella, watching [[Ariel]] sing. After Ariel notices them, he explains Gabriella's disability and introduces himself. He is also the one to tell the tale of the [[The Magical Wishing Starfish|Magical Wishing Starfish]], and he accompanies them along the way. In his second appearance, he is visiting [[Atlantica]] with Gabriella and helps her stop [[Ursula]]'s plot to take over the kingdom.
