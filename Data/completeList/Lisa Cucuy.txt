'''Lisa Cucuy''' is the daughter of two wealthy parents, Carlos and Julie Cucuy in ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]''. Her first and only appearance was in the episode "[[Alex Gives Up]]", where she was shown to have a crush on Max. But when she saw Mason and learned that he was a werewolf she chose him instead. Then Alex saw her flirting with Mason and got jealous, when Lisa's parents found out Mason was a werewolf they jumped out of the boat. When Lisa saw that she couldn't get Mason, she jumped out of the boat with her parents.

==Personality==
Lisa is shown to be selfish and will do everything to get what she wants such as bribing Mason just to be her boyfriend and she is shown aggressive when she dumps Max after seeing Mason.
