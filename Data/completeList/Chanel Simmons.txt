'''Chanel "Chuchie" Simmons''' is one of the main characters in ''[[The Cheetah Girls]]'' series. She is of Puerto Rican, African, Cuban, and Dominican descent and is noted for her trademark mini-micro braids. Chanel has a flair for designer clothing and once charged hundreds of dollars on her mother's credit card. She is fluent in both English and Spanish. Her interests include hair and singing. She lives with her mother and brother, Pucci Simmons, in SoHo. In the films, she is portrayed by [[Adrienne Bailon]].



==Background==

===Physical Appearance===
Chanel has brown skin, brown eyes, and curly light brown hair. She always wears the most fashionable things.
