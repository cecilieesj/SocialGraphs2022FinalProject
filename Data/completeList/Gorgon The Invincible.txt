{{Quote|I'm many things. I'm the voice in your head, the Dark One's powers inside you, inside all the Dark Ones. Rumplestiltskin is but one of many. Perhaps I can be another. How about Gorgon the Invincible?|The Darkness.}}

'''Gorgon the Invincible''', formerly known as the '''Dark One''', is a minor antagonist of [[ABC]]'s ''[[Once Upon a Time]]'' and he appears in four episodes of the fifth season.  Gorgon has the abilities to look like a [[Bandersnatch]].

==Background==
Gorgon was one of the previous [[Dark One|Dark Ones]], he stabbed a Dark One and gains the power of the Dark One and the [[Dark One's Dagger|Dark One Dagger]], becoming Gorgon the Invincible. he began to shapeshifted into a fire-breathing Bandersnatch, which is unknown that that is a way of a Bandersnatch. Gorgon seeks to steal the [[Sorcerer Hat|Sorcerer's Hat]], but failed, but in fact he does not want to give up his power. Gorgon was stabbed by [[Zoso]] who had become the Dark One.

==Role in the series==

===Before the Third Curse===
After becoming the Dark One, [[Emma Swan]] sees the Darkness in the forms of [[Rumplestiltskin|Rumpelstiltskin]] and Gorgon for it is all Dark Ones, but the Darkness prefers to look like Rumple.

===After the Third Curse===
Gorgon is present with all past Dark Ones lead by [[Nimue (character)|Nimue]], appearing in [[Emma Swan|Emma]]'s head to watch her reunite [[Sword in the Stone (object)|Excalibur]] with the [[Dark One's Dagger]].

After [[Captain Hook|Hook]] creates a portal to the [[Underworld]], Gorgon, along with the other Dark Ones all return in their physical form to [[Storybrooke]] to snuff out the light.

[[Nimue (character)|Nimue]], Zoso, Gorgon and the Dark Ones marked the people in Storybrooke so they can take their place in the Underworld, just has it was the time, Hook absorbs Nimue, Gorgon and all the Dark Ones into Excalibur and Emma kills Killian and destroys Excalibur and the Darkness. But then, Gold used Hook loophole and created a new Dark One Dagger and absorbs all the combined powers of all the Dark Ones and became much stronger than Emma.

==Trivia==
*Gorgon makes his first appearance in "[[The Dark Swan]]", then second in "[[Nimue (episode)|Nimue]]", fourth in "[[Broken Heart]]" and a final appearance in "[[Swan Song]]".
