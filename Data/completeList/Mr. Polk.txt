|quote = "[[Candy Cho|Candy]]'s right. Tonight, everybody is a winner."}}'''Mr. Polk''' is a science teacher at [[John Hughes High School]] in the live-action series ''[[Shake It Up]]''. He is [[Rocky Blue|Rocky]] and [[CeCe Jones|CeCe]]'s homeroom teacher, and also watches the students in detention ("Wild It Up"). He seems to adore [[Candy Cho|Candy]] ("Show It Up"), and is annoyed by Rocky ("Egg It Up").
