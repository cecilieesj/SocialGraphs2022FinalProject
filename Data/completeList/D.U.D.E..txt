{{NeedImage}}'''D.U.D.E.''' is a rapping computer. He appears in the Mickey's Starland (predecessor to [[Mickey's Toontown Fair]], which is now dysfunction.) in a show called [[Mickey's Starland Show]], in which is a surprise birthday for [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]]. It is hosted by him and his "deejay protégé", C.J. In order to turn him on, the audience must yell "Hey D.U.D.E., what's happening?"

For every variant of Mickey's Starland Show, his voice and a few of his lines change.
==Quotes==

We're gonna have all kinds of fun
