*[[Yeti]] (Fantasyland)
*Dragon (Fantasyland)
*Esmerelda (Main Street USA)
*[[Shrunken Ned]] (Adventureland)
*Gator Waiter (New Orleans Square)
*Captain Blackheart (Adventureland)
