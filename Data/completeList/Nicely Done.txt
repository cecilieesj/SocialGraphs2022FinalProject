[[File:Nicely_Done.png|thumb]]
'''Nicely Done''' is a minor character and one of the protagonists of ''[[The 7D]]'' Season 2 episode, "Nicely Done and the 7D". He helps the 7D rescue one of their friends from the Sheriff of Plottingham. He is voiced by [[Rob Paulsen]].
