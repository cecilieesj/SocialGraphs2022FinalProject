|quote = "Out with the old, in with the new."}}'''Oliver Warbucks''' is the deuteragonist of ''[[Annie (1999 film)|Annie]]''. He is the resident of the mansion where [[Grace Farrell]] takes [[Annie (character)|Annie]] to visit for Christmas. He is a billionaire, so it is he who announces a $50,000 reward to the couple who can prove they are Annie's parents. He is in love with Grace, both of whom act as surrogate parents.
[[File:1.169789.jpg|thumb|Oliver Warbucks and Grace Farrell]]

==Notes==
Warbucks is one of five Disney characters to appear on the Forbes Fictional 15 list at least once (although he has been on the list a few times). The other four are [[Scrooge McDuck]], [[Flintheart Glomgold]], [[Cruella De Vil]], and [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]] (although he is a Marvel character).
