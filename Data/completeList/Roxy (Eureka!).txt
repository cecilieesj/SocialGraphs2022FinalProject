'''Roxy''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Eureka!]]''. The mother of [[Eureka (character)|the titular character]], Roxy is the owner of Paleo, the only restaurant in Rocky Falls.

==Background==
Roxy lives during the Stone Age, specifically in Rocky Falls. At some point prior to the start of the series, she met and fell in love with Rollo, with whom she would have a daughter, named Eureka. Roxy would also go on to found Paleo, the only restaurant in Rocky falls, where she also serves as the chef.
