|quote = }}'''Ray Schoonover''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and antagonist who appears in ''[[Daredevil (TV series)|Daredevil]]'', played by [[Clancy Brown]].

Ray Schoonover was created by Carl Potts, John Wellington and Jim Lee.
==History==
He is an United States Marine colonel who is secretly a drug lord. He was also Frank Castle's commanding officer during the war. He later became a drug lord known as the blacksmith and His actions led to the mob war that led to the death of Frank's family. After Frank became the [[Punisher]], Ray hired a sniper to [[Matt Murdock]] and [[Foggy Nelson]] in order to frame Punisher for it, but Matt and Foggy managed to escape alive. Afterwards, [[Karen Page]] met with Ray and questioned him about him and his past with Punisher, but Punisher showed up and shot Ray in the head, killing Ray.
