[[File:Cynthia.jpg|thumb]]'''Cynthia''' is a background character in the [[Disney XD]]'s series ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''.

==Biography==
She is a student who attends [[Norrisville High]] School.

==Trivia==
*She is based on the producer of RC9GN, [[Jackie Buscarino]].
*We learn her name in "[[Randy Cunningham and the Sorcerer's Key]]" when Flute Girl notices that Stevens gave her a pin.
*Cynthia, [[Heidi Weinerman]], and [[Cass Simonson]] are talking with each other during the montage of Randy wasting [[Smoke Bombs|smoke bombs]]  in "[[Silent Punch, Deadly Punch]]".
*Jackie Buscarino was also part of the crew of Disney Channel's hit series ''[[Gravity Falls]]''.
*She had her first and so far only speaking role, talking about Howard, in "[[Gossip Boy]]", saying "He saved us from eating chipmunk-changas!".
*She is a girl that [[Dickie]] checked and threw because she wasn't Tawny in "[[Stank'd to the Future]]". Randy caught her before she slammed into the ground.
*In "[[McFreaks]]", in the far tree in the first shot of the Norrisville Pet Park, she's hanging upside with her pet, a lemur.
