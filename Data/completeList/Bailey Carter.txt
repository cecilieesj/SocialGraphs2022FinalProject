'''Bailey Carter''' is a recurring character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is the best friend of Gretel.

==Background==

===Physical appearance===
Bailey has brown skin and wears red glasses. She wears a yellow long sleeve sweater with a green skirt and yellow shoes and socks.
