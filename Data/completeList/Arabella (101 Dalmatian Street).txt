'''Arabella''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. She is a friend of [[Clarissa]].

==Appearance==
She is a Lhasa Apso, with long, cream and golden fur. The hair on her head is tied with a pink headband.

==Personality==
She is as snobby as her friends.
