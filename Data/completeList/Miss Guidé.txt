[[File:Miss Guide.png|thumb|250px]]'''Miss Guidé'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398764/?ref_=tt_cl_t14</ref> is a minor character on the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. Her first appearance was in the episode "[[Trading Faces (Kim Possible)|Trading Faces]]". She was voiced by [[April Winchell]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Kim-Possible/Ms-Guide/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0934589/</ref>

==History==
She is [[Jim & Tim Possible|Jim and Tim Possible]]'s S.K.I.P. counselor, at [[Middleton High School]].
==Equipment ==
Miss Guide is rarely seen without her clipboard and pencil taking notes on all that she observes.
==Relationships==
She seems to have a good relationship with the students under her guidance.
