|status = Active}}'''Spencer''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is one of [[Portia Poodle]]'s sidekicks.

==Appearance==
He is a small grey dachshund (as his nickname suggests) with a brown underbelly and dark grey spots. He wears a small black jacket with a white collar.

==Personality==
Spencer is a snobby, mischievous, sarcastic and snobbish dachshund who sees himself as having a certain air about himself, and, despite the fact that he is the sidekick of Portia Poodle, he is very loyal to her.
