'''Nanette''' is a character in the [[2011]] [[Touchstone Pictures]] film ''[[Gnomeo & Juliet]]''. She is inspired by the character referred to as [[Wikipedia:Nurse (Romeo and Juliet)|Nurse]] in [[Wikipedia:William Shakespeare|Shakespeare]]'s ''[[Wikipedia:Romeo and Juliet|Romeo and Juliet]]''.

==Role in the [[Gnomeo & Juliet|film]]==
Nanette is a green water spout frog, who is best friends with [[Juliet (Gnomeo & Juliet)|Juliet]].

She lives in [[Mr. Capulet]]'s garden along with the Red Gnomes.

Later in the film, [[Paris (Gnomeo & Juliet)|Paris]], who is assigned by [[Lord Redbrick]] to be Juliet's suitor, is admired by Nanette.

At the end of the film, Nanette starts a relationship with Paris.
