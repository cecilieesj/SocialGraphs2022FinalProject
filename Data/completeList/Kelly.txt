'''Kelly''' is the owner of her own hardware store in Sheet Rock Hills. She helps [[Manny Garcia|Manny]] and the Tools with repairsÂ and makes sure they always have what they need.

==Background==
===Personality===
Kelly is a sweet, fun, heart-warming, and funny woman. She works in a hardware store and has nearly everything that Manny needs to repair anything. In addition, she has an obvious huge crush on Manny.

===Physical appearance===
Kelly has red hair with green eyes, sky blue earrings, a pink shirt, blue trousers, a beige fanny pack around her stomach, and light pink slippers.
