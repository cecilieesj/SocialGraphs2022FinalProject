'''Mrs. Carter'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/carter_mom.htm</ref> is a minor character from the animated television show, ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
Mrs. Carter is [[Trixie Carter|Trixie]]'s mother and apparently an airline hostess. Not much else is known about her.
