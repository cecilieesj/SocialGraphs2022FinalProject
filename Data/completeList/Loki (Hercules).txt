'''Loki''' is a minor antagonist in ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]''. He is the Trickster God of the Asgardian deities.

==Background==
===Personality===
Taking inspiration from Hades' sleazy car-salesman characterization in the film, Loki's personality is inspired by slick Hollywood agents.

===Powers and Abilities===

*Standard powers of an Asgardian/Aesir deity.
* Ability to change size at will.
*Ability to conjure and throw energy balls.
* Ability to teleport short distances.

==Appearances==
===''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]''===
Loki appears as the villain-of-the-week of the episode "[[Hercules and the Twilight of the Gods]]", where he gets Hercules a position in the Asgardian pantheon so that with the threat of Ragnarok looming ever closer, he would be able to succeed in his plots to conquer Asgard.
