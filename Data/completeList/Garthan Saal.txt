'''Denarian Garthan Saal''' is a high ranking member of the [[Nova Corps]].

Garthan Saal was created by Ralph Macchio, Mark Gruenwald, Bob Hall and Don Heck.

==Appearances==
===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]''===
Saal with [[Rhomann Dey]] presented and read the criminal history of the future Guardians of the Galaxy. Saal informs to [[Irani Rael]] the capture of [[Gamora]], the adopted daughter of [[Thanos]].

When Dey reports that Quill and the others help to stop Ronan, he shows skeptical. During the Battle of Xandar, Saal with the rest of Nova Corps defending [[Xandar]] but when Ronan uses the power of the [[Orb]], he and his teammates die.
