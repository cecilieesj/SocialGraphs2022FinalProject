The '''Royal Recordkeeper''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2000]] animated feature film, ''[[The Emperor's New Groove]]''.

== Appearances ==
=== ''[[The Emperor's New Groove]]'' ===
He is in charge of notifying [[Kuzco|Emperor Kuzco]] that the time has come to look for a wife, among several candidates that he chose for him. However, Kuzco rejected all of them, scolding him for not finding better candidates. 

=== ''[[The Emperor's New School]]'' ===
He appears from time to time reminding [[Kuzco]] that he must graduate from the Academy to become emperor, and making sure Kuzco fulfills his duties. In the series his blue suit from the film is changed to a red one.

=== Other appearances ===
In ''[[House of Mouse]]'', the Royal Recordkeeper makes a cameo appearance in the episode "[[Pluto vs. Figaro]]".
