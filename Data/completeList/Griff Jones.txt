|quote = |status=Active|parents=Gerald McCormick (adoptive father)<br>Mrs McCormick (adoptive mother)|siblings=[[Xander McCormick]] (adoptive brother)|partner=[[Zuri Ross]] (girlfriend)}}'''Griffin "Griff" Jones-McCormick''' is a recurring character in [[Bunk'd]]. He's a 12 year old kid who's a reformed juvenile delinquent. His parents abandoned him and he was forced to steal things to survive, which got him arrested and sent to juvie. He was also in and out of foster homes. He was sent to Camp Kikiwaka to help him stay out of trouble and to help him reform. He had fun making new friends and doing the activities there.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Griff is a 12-year-old Caucasian boy with brown neck-length hair and brown eyes. He is relatively skinny and has an average build. He usually wears t-shirts and jeans, sometimes with a jacket. Described by some of the kids there, he is cute and has great hair.
