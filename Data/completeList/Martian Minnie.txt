'''Martian Minnie''' is an alien counterpart of [[Minnie Mouse]], who appears in the ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]'' episodes "[[Mickey's Sport-Y-Thon]]" and "[[Martian Minnie's Tea Party]]". She would later return in the ''[[Mickey Mouse Funhouse]]'' episode "[[Spaced Out!]]" and "[[Polka Dots and Don'ts]]".
