|fate = Brought back home by Bill and Judy Burke}}'''Prince Terrien''' is [[Leslie Burke]]'s pet dog in ''[[Bridge to Terabithia]]''.

==Role in the film==
Prince Terrien was given to Leslie for Christmas by [[Jess Aarons|Jess]]. He is the guardian and jester of Terabithia. He follows Jess and Leslie throughout their adventures there. He is also the only witness to Leslie's death and becomes a way for other characters like Jess and her father, Bill, to stay connected to her afterward.
