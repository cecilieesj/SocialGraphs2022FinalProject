'''Fisherman Droopy''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cat Cat] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/NPC NPC] in the discontinued online video game, ''[[Toontown Online]]''.

Like all Fisherman NPCs, you can talk to him to sell fish in your fishing bucket. For every 10 new fish species you sell, he will award you with a trophy and a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Laff Laff boost].
