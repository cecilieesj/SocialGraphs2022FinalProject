'''Khonshu''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Moon Knight (TV series)|Moon Knight]]'', voiced by [[F. Murray Abraham]]. Much like the [[Asgardians]] are the gods from the Norse pantheon, Khonshu is a god from the Egyptian pantheon, being the Egyptian god of the Moon and the source of [[Moon Knight]]'s power.

This version of the mythological Khonshu was created by Doug Moench, Bill Sienkewicz and Frank Springer.

== Trivia ==
*The Egyptian pantheon is the third mythological pantheon that is introduced in the MCU, after the Norse pantheon and the Greco-Roman pantheon.
