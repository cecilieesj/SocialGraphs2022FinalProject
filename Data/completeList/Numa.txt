'''Numa''' is a recurring character who appeared in ''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]'' and reappeared in ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''. She is a female Twi'lek who is a part of [[Cham Syndulla]]'s Ryloth cell movement.{{CharacterStub}}
