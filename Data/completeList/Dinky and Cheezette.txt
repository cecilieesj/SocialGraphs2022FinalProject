'''Dinky and Cheezette''' are characters from the animated series ''[[Dave the Barbarian]]''. They are [[Candy|Princess Candy]]'s best friends, who are sometimes unsupportive - and wisely so - toward her actions. They are as obsessive about fashion, clothes and social lives as Candy is.
