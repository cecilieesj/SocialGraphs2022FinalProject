== Appearance ==
Robo-Chef usually wears a white chef hat mask, white coat, and black pants. He also has a mustache.

His robot has one eye. It usually wears a chef hat. It has a claw for the right arm and a mixer for the left arm.

== Relationships ==

=== Frenemies ===

==== [[Gum Girl (Book Character)|Gum Girl]] ====
Robo-Chef wanted revenge on his baking event due to gum. His evil plan was to steal the bank and destroy Gum Girl, but his plan was foiled, got tripped by Gum Girl, and got arrested by the police for his crimes. In Stick Together, Gum Girl helped him defeat Mr. Hansen.
