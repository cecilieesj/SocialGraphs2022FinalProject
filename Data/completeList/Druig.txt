'''Druig''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by Barry Keoghan.

Druig was created by [[Jack Kirby]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Druig stands out from his fellow Eternals because of his disagreement on how the Eternals protected mankind from the Deviants while choosing not to interfere with human conflicts. However, despite disagreeing with the Eternals' withdrawl and isolation, Druig shows some pacifistism when it comes to ending conflicts.
