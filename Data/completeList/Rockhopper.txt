Captain '''Rockhopper''' (or '''RH''' to abbreviate) is a friendly penguin pirate and sailor from ''[[Club Penguin]]''. He docks his own ship, [[w:c:clubpenguin:The Migrator|the Migrator]], at [[w:c:clubpenguin:Club Penguin Island (location)|Club Penguin Island]], where he sells items he finds in his travels, and visits as a [[w:c:clubpenguin:Famous Characters|mascot]].

Rockhopper and [[w:c:clubpenguin:Blue (penguin)|Blue Penguin]] appeared as costume characters various times:
*From [[April 30]], [[2009]] until [[May 13]], 2009, in scheduled meet-and-greets at a themed spot in [[Disney's Hollywood Studios]].
*On a parade float for the [[Disney Parks Magical Christmas Celebration|Christmas Day Parade]] of 2009 and the [[Disney Parks Magical Christmas Celebration|Frozen Christmas Celebration]] in [[2014]] at [[Magic Kingdom]].
*At [[Wikipedia:Times Square|Times Square]] in [[December 2]], [[2011]], as the reward for "Billybob's Art Challenge"<ref>[https://youtu.be/cm0smGUyL00?t=37 Club Penguin: Billybob's Art Challenge for Warm Coats Warm Hearts! - YouTube]</ref>, partnered with ''[[ABC]]''<nowiki/>'s ''[[Wikipedia:Good Morning America|Good Morning America]]'' for their fifth annual ''Warm Coats & Warm Hearts Drive''.

==Appearance==
*A red anthropomorphic penguin wearing a large black pirate's hat, bushy black eyebrows and beard, along with a jacket.

==Trivia==
*His favorite food is stinky cheese.
*[[w:c:clubpenguin:Yarr|Yarr]] is his pet [[puffle]] and first mate, who was stranded at sea until rescued by Rockhopper.
*His favorite Club Penguin game is Mancala, and snowball fight is his favorite sport.
*According to his journal, he invented the Club Penguin dance.
*His own igloo on Club Penguin melted.
*Pineapples remind him of himself.
*He wears a coat that he found abandoned on an island.
