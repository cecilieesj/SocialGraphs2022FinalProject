[[File:Hatching_pete02.jpg|thumb]]'''Pete Ivey''' is the main protagonist character of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Hatching Pete]]''. He is portrayed by [[Jason Dolley]].
==Personality==
He is known to be an extremely shy at the beginning of the movie, but gains confidence in the films. He is shown to be very nice and helpful person.
