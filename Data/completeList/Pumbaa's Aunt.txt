{{Infobox_character|image = Pumbaa aunt.png|name = Auntie|family = [[Pumbaa]] (nephew)|shows = [[Timon & Pumbaa]]|alignment = Neutral|dislikes = breaking her stuff}}'''"Auntie"''' is Pumbaa<nowiki>'s aunt, who appeared in the ''</nowiki>[[Timon & Pumbaa]]<nowiki>''</nowiki> episode "[[Don't Wake the Neighbear]]".

== History ==
[[Timon]] and [[Pumbaa]] arrive at Pumbaa's Aunt's house, with Timon holding a boom box. Timon is excited about having a party at Pumbaa's aunt's house, but Pumbaa tells his friend that he has to be careful not to break any of his aunt's stuff. But at the end of the episode Pumbaa's aunt shows up and is about to hurt Timon and Pumbaa for what just happened.
