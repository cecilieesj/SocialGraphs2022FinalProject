'''Sweet Pea''' is a garden-talent fairy who appears exclusively in the Disney Fairies. Her friends have said that she always seems to be hyper about something, whether it be planning a party, blogging about upcoming events in the Pixie Postings, or even sitting back and watching the flowers bloom.
