==SOS Toon==
﻿'''Moe Zart''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/VP VP] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon] reward that you can receive by defeating the VP. This SOS is a rare, five-star SOS. It is [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Sound_Gags Sound] and will do 80 damage to all [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs cogs] that you are battling at the time. (Not including boss (aka VP, [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/CFO CFO], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/CJ CJ], or [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/CEO CEO]) cogs.) His Fog Horn has a damage of 80, while the regular fog horn only has 50 damage. His gag is the most powerful sound gag

==Animation (as an S.O.S)==
1. ﻿A [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Dog dog] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon toon] will pop up out of the ground using a "black hole teleport" and say "Hi (Your toon's name)! Glad to Help!

2. He will pull out a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Fog_Horn Fog Horn], doing 80 damage to all cogs you are fighting. If more than just the SOS toon is using a sound gag, he will use sound with you.

3. He will then say "See you later!" and jump back into his teleport hole.

==Trivia==
*His name is a pun of the famous musician Mozart.
