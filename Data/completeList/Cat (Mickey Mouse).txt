The Cat is a cat who is always trying to eat [[Gubbles]].
==Physical appearance==
The cat is a black cat with black and white hair that looks like [[Figaro]].
==Appearances==
===[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)]]===
He first appeared in the episode [[Gasp!]] sleeping on [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey's]] apartment. When he finds out that a fish named [[Gubbles]] leaves in Mickey's apartment he opens the window while Mickey is in a call and takes Gubbles out of his fish tank and eats him. When Mickey finds out that he tried to eat Gubbles he spits out Gubbles when Mickey hits him with the broom and chases him out of his apartment and closes the window. He then returns through the window of the bathroom and eats him again. Mickey then chases him again in an epic battle to get back Gubbles. When Gubbles is in his stomach he sees that the Cat has eaten a lot of fish before him. But Mickey tell Gubbles to do tricks for a treat inside his stomach Gubbles jumps a out and barks at the Cat leading to him jumping out the window. In the episode [[No]] when Mickey can't say no he takes the opportunity to ask Mickey if he can eat Gubbles and Mickey can't say no so he eats him after Gubbles escapes and he chases him. At the end of the episode he comes and gives him with a female fish and joins Mickey Mouse, [[Minnie Mouse]], [[Pluto]], [[Gubbles]], the Female Fish, [[Goofy]], [[Scrooge McDuck]], [[Mortimer Mouse]], [[Pete]], [[Huey, Dewey and Louie]], [[The Three Little Pigs]] to watch a movie. His last appearance was in the episode [[Flushed!]] when Mickey thinks Gubbles is dead he invites the Cat to Gubbles funeral with Pluto.
