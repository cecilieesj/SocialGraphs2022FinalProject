Tamka is a crocodile who is one of [[Kiburi]]'s sidekicks and one of [[Scar]]'s minions who appears in [[The Lion Guard]].

==Background==
He used to be part of [[Makuu's Float]].

===Personality===
He is dim-witted and like his boss, he is also overconfident. He even admitted he was not very smart to the Lion Guard. He is also ignorant as he ignores the Lion Guard's warnings every time they confront him.

===Physical appearance===
He is an olive-green crocodile with yellow eyes and an underbelly. He is also overweight.

==Appearances==
====[[Let Sleeping Crocs Lie]]====
He first debuted after the Lion Guard woke him and the rest of [[Makuu's Float]] up by mistake. He then attempts to take over one of the water holes in the [[Pride Lands]] under [[Kiburi]]'s orders. He, Nduli and another crocodile later attempt to murder [[Simba]]. When he and the other crocs are banished as punishment, [[Ushari]] shows him and the other three to [[Janja's Den]] where they meet [[Scar]], [[Janja]], [[Cheezi]], and [[Chungu]] and listen along with them and Ushari to Scar singing [[I Have A Plan]].

=== [[The Little Guy (The Lion Guard)|The Little Guy]] ===
When a geeko named [[Hodari]] wants to join a crocodile float [[Shupavu]] tells him to join Kiburi's. Kiburi welcomes him into his float. He thinks that Hodari can get them passed the Lion Guard, However Kiburi's plan doesn't work and they fight against Makuu's float. Hodari decides to help Makuu and wins the fight against Nduli and his float.

=== [[The Scorpion's Sting]] ===
Nudli and the rest of the his float attack the Lion Guard and [[Makini]]  before they can reach the top of the volcano. Later they trap the guard again with the rest of [[Army of Scar|Scar's Army]].

=== [[The Kilio Valley Fire]] ===
Kiburi's float helps attack Killo Valley where [[Ma Tembo]]'s herd used to live.

=== [[The Zebra Mastermind]] ===
In the Outlands Tamka and Nduli are talking about what food they wanna eat while Kiburi talks to Scar. They soon notice [[Thurston]] the zebra. Thurston tells him that he's not going to miss the big party, Tamka assumes he means the big meeting, and Thurston explains that he's the guest of honor due to his Lion Guard knowledge, and assumes that they too, wish to hear about the Guard's weaknesses. He tells them that rubbing their tummies will cause them to go to sleep, and the crocodiles decide to bring back the Lion Guard to Scar in the hopes that they'll get to go to the next big meeting.

However their plan is ruined because Kion uses the [[Roar of the Elders]] on them.

=== [[The Hyena Resistance]] ===
Tamka helps Scar's Army try to get rid of [[Jasiri]] and the Hyena Resistance.

=== [[Beshte and the Beast]] ===
The Army of Scar faces off agaisnt Basi's Pod and so they can take over Big Springs. However a gorilla warrior [[Shujaa]] comes to stop them.

=== [[Pride Landers Unite!]] ===
The Army of Scar fights off agaisnt Pride Landers.

=== [[The Fall of Mizimu Grove]] ===
The Army of Scar tries to take over Mizimu Grove during Makini's Mapando Mpaya.

=== [[Battle for the Pride Lands]] ===
The Army of Scar has their finale battle against the Pride Landers. When Scar is defeated, Jasiri becomes in charge of the Outlands.

==Relationships==
== Friends ==
===[[Kiburi]]===
Tamka is good friends with Kiburi. He follows Kiburi's every order and tries to help him win the battles.

===[[Nduli]]===
Tamka and Nduli are good friends. They both are part of Kiburi's float.

===[[Scar]]===
Tamka and Scar are good friends. Scar welcomes Kiburi and his float to the Outlands when they were banished from the Pride Lands.

==Acquaintances==

===[[Ushari]]===
Tamka and Ushari are on good terms. They work together to destroy the Lion Guard.

===[[Reirei's Pack]]===
Tamka's on good terms with Reirei's pack. They work together to destroy the Lion Guard.

===[[Mzingo's Parliament]]===
Tamka's on good terms with Mzingo's Parliamanet. They work together to destroy the Lion Guard.

==Enemies==
===[[Kion]] and the Lion Guard===
Tamka doesn't like Kion and the Lion Guard. He helps the Army of Scar defeat them. Kion even uses the Roar on him.

===[[Makuu]]===
Tamka used to be part of Makuu's float until Kiburi called for a Mashindano. After Kiburi lost Makuu kicks him and his followers out of his float.

===[[Simba]]===
Tamka doesn't like Simba. Tamka and two other crocodiles try to get rid of the king. Simba banishes him and the crocodiles to the Outlands.

===[[Hodari]]===
Tamka dislikes Hodari. When Hodari wanted to be a crocodile Kiburi welcomes him in his float. It was only so he can get past the Lion Guard.
