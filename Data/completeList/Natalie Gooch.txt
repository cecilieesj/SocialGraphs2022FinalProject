*Ninja-Rina|enemies = *Gum Girl (at times)|alignment = Bad, then good}}'''Natalie Gooch''' was a secondary protagonist of ''[[The Gumazing Gum Girl! (Book Series)|The Gumazing Gum Girl!]]'' series. She was normally [[Gum Girl (Book Character)|Gum Girl]]'s school bully. She was a student in Ms. Smoot's class.

== Appearance ==
[[File:Gum Girl Natalie Gooch.jpg|thumb]]
Natalie is a stereotypical boyish-looking girl who was a school bully. She usually wears a white shirt, pink vest, pink pants, and pink shoes.

== Relationships ==

=== Friends ===

==== [[Gum Girl (Book Character)|Gum Girl]] ====
Natalie bullied Gabby, and insulting her for how she looks with her at school. In ''Gum Luck'', It appeared to be that Natalie had been bullying Gabby, and so, she had actually always thought of Gabby as her friend.
