|occupation = Ruler of the Terra-Firmians}}The '''Terra-Firmie King''' is the ruler of the [[Terries and Fermies|Terra-Firmians]]. He appeared in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[Earth Quack]]".

==Other appearances==
He also appears in the video games ''[[DuckTales (video game)|DuckTales]]'' and ''[[DuckTales Remastered]]'' as the boss of the African Mines level. In these appearances, he was given a long white beard.
