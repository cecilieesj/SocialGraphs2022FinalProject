'''Pluto Junior''' is one of the fun-loving [[Pluto's Quin-puplets (characters)|children]] of [[Pluto]] and [[Fifi the Peke|Fifi]]. Originally a unnamed puppy, who debuted in [[Pluto's Quin-puplets]], Pluto Junior is by far the only one of his four siblings, to have a name.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Pluto Junior's design is much like Pluto Senior's, though basically a smaller version of Pluto. Though Jr has a more circular head, and is chubbier. Almost all other things has him resemble his father. Jr's ears also droop down starightly, and of course are much shorter.
