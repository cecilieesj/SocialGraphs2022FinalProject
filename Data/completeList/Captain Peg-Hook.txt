'''Captain Peg-Hook''' is a pirate dog that is a boss in the game ''[[Disney's Magical Quest 3 starring Mickey and Donald|Disney's Magical Quest 3]]''. His first battle takes place in The Airship, he is later fought as a mini-boss in the King Pete's Palace.
