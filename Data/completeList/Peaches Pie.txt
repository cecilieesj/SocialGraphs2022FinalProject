'''Peaches Pie''' is a peach-scented doll with a country girl accent who loves dancing and playing with her new friends as she never worries about anything (except the scent of her dress being smelly) as [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]] and her toys help her.

==Appearance==
Peaches Pie has blonde hair, blue eyes, white stockings and wears a pink and orange dress and matching hat.
