|quote = '''Red-Haired Lady:''' "Kill him! Kill the umpire!"}}'''Ladies from the Stand''' are a trio and a group of women and one of the supporting characters of the second segment, ''[[Casey at the Bat]]'', in Disney's [[1946]] animated feature, ''[[Make Mine Music]]''.

==Background==
They are a trio of ladies from the stand with [[Casey (Make Mine Music)|Casey]] charming and swooning at them during the game.
===Personality===
They're dreamy, gorgeous, beautiful, adoring, lovely, sweet, cheerful, cute, affectionate, kind and silly, but when the red-haired lady doesn't like the Umpire which she has shouted at and who tries to kill him with a pin from her hat before Casey raised his hand to prevent violence and chaos from happening, she was serious, angry and feisty.
===Physical Appearance===
They are slender with fair skin, thin black eyebrows, black eyes, red lips and blush on their cheeks.
