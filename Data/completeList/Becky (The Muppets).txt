'''Becky''' is a young woman and [[Fozzie Bear]]'s girlfriend living in the [[Los Angeles]] area in the [[2015]] TV series ''[[The Muppets (TV series)|The Muppets]]''.

She was played by [[Margo Harshman]] in the original network presentation pilot only to be recast by [[Riki Lindhome]] in the actual series.
