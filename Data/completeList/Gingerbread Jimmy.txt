}}'''Gingerbread Jimmy''' is a minor character from the [[Disney Junior]] show ''[[Goldie & Bear]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[Gingerbread Jimmy's Fantastical Forest]]".
==Role in the episode==
This gingerbread cookie resides in Gingerbread Jimmy's Fantastical Forest, one of [[Magic Gnome]]'s board games. When [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie]], [[Bear (Goldie & Bear)|Bear]], [[Red (Goldie and Bear)|Red]] and [[Humpty]] are transported inside the game, Jimmy appears to them and explains to them how the game works.
