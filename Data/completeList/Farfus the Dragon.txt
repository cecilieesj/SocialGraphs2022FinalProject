|status=Active}}'''Farfus the Dragon''' is a purple dragon from ''[[Mickey Mouse Funhouse]]''. He was seen as a pie stealer from the episode "[[Mickey the Brave!]]".

==Personality==
According to [[Mickey Mouse]] and friends, he was described as a bad dragon. However, his actual personality is a playful dragon, who is in the Kingdom of Majestica.

==Appearance==
'''Farfus''' is a purple dragon, with a white underbelly, has light green ears, spikes, and wings, and light green scales on some parts of his body.
