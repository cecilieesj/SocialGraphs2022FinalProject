'''Chips''' is a minor character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is a seagull who lives in Cornwall.

== Personality==
He is a playful bird but he is also helpful.

==Appearance==
He is a white seagull with huge eyes and feathers on his head which resemble hair.
