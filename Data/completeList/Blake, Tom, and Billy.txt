==History==
They are the [[Hollywood]] counterparts of [[Joey, Vinnie, and Bobby]] and acted like Hollywood writers with stereotype cliche, with Billy like their assistant. Fans of the actor dog [[Bolt (character)|Bolt]], they help him to find his owner [[Penny (Bolt)|Penny]] when he returned to Hollywood, in exchange if Bolt incorporate aliens in his TV show.

==Trivia==
*One of the pigeons references [[Nemo]], the titular character of the [[Pixar]] film ''[[Finding Nemo]]''.
