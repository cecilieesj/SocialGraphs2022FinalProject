'''Mbita''' is a character in the [[Disney+]] Original series ''[[Baymax!]].'' He is a food vendor who realizes that he has an allergy to fish, ruining his business, but learns to move on with the help of [[Baymax]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Mbita is a down-to-earth young man who embraces his culture, family heritage and orientation. However, he feared change and was worried about losing the thing that he loved most following the revelation about his allergy. He eventually comes to accept change and tries to make himself a better person while also becoming more confident in social skills and abilities.

{{quote|His whole life his mom and dad have served fish soup to the masses, and now he's finally taken over the family business... You want to feel for him while being able to laugh at the absurdity of [his allergies].|Dan Abraham<ref name="PromoSheet" />}}

===Physical appearance===
Mbita is a tall slender African-American man with tall flat hair. He usually wears either an orange, or purple shirt, denim jeans and black sneakers. When working, he wears a beige apron and a paper hat.

==Role in the series==
Mbita comes from a family who serves and cooks fish, but he had developed a fish allergy at some point. Despite that, he continues family heritage and orientation, and kept his allergy hidden.
