'''Paul ''' is a character in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. He made his debut in the [[Generation Why|first episode]] of the [[Disney+]] series ''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]'' and is portrayed by Paul Kim. Paul is a student at Coles Academic High School.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]''====
He is first seen in the [[Generation Why|first episode]], talking with Miguel in the schools hallways, when [[Ms. Marvel|Kamala]], [[Nakia Bahadir|Nakia]] and [[Bruno Carrelli|Bruno]] pass them by.

Later on, Paul is conversing with Miguel by the lockers, when Kamala greets them, as she walks by.

When footage circulates online of [[Zoe Zimmer|Zoe]] being saved by a young cosplayer with powers at AvengerCon, Paul is among the students at school, surrounding her, as she recounts the events from her perspective. Paul questions the identity of the young girl who saved her, and Zoe dubs the heroine as "Night Light". She then proceeds to invite her classmates to party on Friday in celebration of the "fragility of life", as well as her half-a-million followers.

Paul is in attendance at the party, when they hear the sound of sirens. He alerts the patygoers that the police are arriving, and warns them all to leave. Paul begins to worry he will be arrested, but the new student [[Kamran]] offers to drive Paul and a few other clasamates away, before they get in trouble. Paul, Miguel, Nakia, Bruno and Kamala all get into Kamran's car, and are dropped of at Cirlce Q, before heading off in their seperate ways.
