[[File:Bobby_point.png|thumb]]Bobby is a bear cub who makes his first appearance with his twin sister [[Betty (T.O.T.S.)|Betty]] in the episode "[[The Great Robot Race]]."






==Appearance==
Bobby is a light brown-ish bear cub with blue colored eyes. He sports a tuft of hair on his head. He wears a mint colored Collar with T.O.T.S. logo and a diaper with green trim and evergreen tree and mountain patterns of them.
