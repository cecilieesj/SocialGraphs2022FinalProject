'''Nigel Nightshade''' is the main antagonist of the [[Quack Pack|''Quack Pack'']] episode, "[[Pride Goeth Before the Fall Guy]]".
==Background==
==='''Personality'''===
Like all thieves, Nigel Nightshade is known to be conniving, sneaky, clever, lying, greedy, selfish, tricky, deceiving, mean, cunning, and slick in many ways when it comes to stealing and tricking people to gain their trust and get exactly what he's after.
==='''Physical Appearance'''===
Nigel Nightshade is a short man with fair skin, black hair, thick black eyebrows and wears a gray suit, red bow tie, and black shoes.
