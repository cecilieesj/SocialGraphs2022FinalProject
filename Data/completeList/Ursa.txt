'''Ursa''' is a recurring character in the TV series ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''.

Ursa is a friend and possible love interest of [[Bear]].

She is fluent in Spanish, as she was originally from Mexico, and often greets Bear by saying "Hola Bear!"

Ursa lives on a farm on a hill in Spain and grows various berries.

She appears in the episodes, "[[And to All a Good Night]]", "[[Clear as a Bell]]", and "[[You Never Know]]".
