|alignment = Neutral}}'''Susan''' is a character in ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]''. She is one of [[Justin Russo|Justin]]'s friends from his advanced math class.

==Role in the series==
[[Alex Russo|Alex]] grows fond of [[Zeke Beakerman|Zeke]] and Susan, and tries to be their friend, making Justin jealous because he was friends with them first.

Susan says that [[Ruby Donahue]] is her role model. She plans on following in her exact footsteps.

When Susan invites Alex to watch a movie with them, Alex finds out that she is under the age restriction, and uses magic to get into the movie, literally.
