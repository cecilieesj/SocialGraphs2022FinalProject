He is a precursor of [[Chip and Dale]], but with less personality. He decides to make his home out of Pluto's buried bones in "[[Bone Bandit]]", helps himself to the produce in "[[Donald's Garden]]", and digs up [[Minnie Mouse]]'s flower garden and gets Pluto blamed for that.
