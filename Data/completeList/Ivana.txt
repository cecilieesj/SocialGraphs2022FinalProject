[[File:Ivana_the_hairdresser.jpg|thumb]]'''Ivana''' the hairdresser, is [[Juanita Simmons]] and [[Chanel Simmons]]'s hairdresser.

==Physical Appearance==
Ivana is possibly a German female. She has red shoulder-length hair and is pale. She is also skinny, her work outfit requires her to wear a purple vest.

==Events==
===[[The Cheetah Girls]] (Movie)===
Ivana is fixing both, Juanita and Chanel's hair. When Chanel sees [[Galleria Garibaldi|her best friend]]'s dog, [[Toto (The Cheetah Girls)|Toto]], on TV. Chanel rushes out with her hair still undone, and so does her mother. Ivana yells to them not to tell them that Ivana's store did their undone hairstyle.
