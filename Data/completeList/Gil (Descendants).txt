'''Gil''' is a character who appears in the Disney Channel films ''[[Descendants 2]]'' and ''[[Descendants 3]]''. He is the son of [[Gaston]] and starts as an antagonist but later reforms.

==Trivia==
*Due to his low intelligence, he is often used solely for brute force by [[Uma]], and forgets not to use her hated nickname "Shrimpy".
*He thinks penguins can be found in a jungle.
*Gil likes pets, having only the stag head in his dad's man cave as a poor stand in.
*Has no filter, he says whatever he wants whenever he wants.
*As of the third film, Gil decides to explore Auradon with Jay, having inspired him to see more the world.
*In ''[[Rise of the Isle of the Lost: A Descendants Novel]]'', it is revealed that Gil is the younger brother of Gaston, Jr. and Gaston III.
*Gil was seen kissing [[Harry Hook]],<ref>[https://68.media.tumblr.com/10e3c44e93b27a5e7e5539d2d4c3faf8/tumblr_otgrcvgDT41vzeht5o1_1280.jpg]</ref> however, it was never scripted or directed and was an improvised scene between the two actors.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qAnbPzj-HgM]</ref> But it was mentioned in the novelization of the film [[Descendants 2]].
