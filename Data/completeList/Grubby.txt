'''Grubby Barbic''' is a character from the animated television show ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''.

==History==
Grubby is [[Ursa (Gummi Bear)|Ursa]], [[Gritty]], and the other Barbic's cook, he is introduced in the episode "[[Return to Ursalia]]".

When welcoming the [[Gummi Bears]] from [[Gummi Glen]] to [[Ursalia]], he serves his old Barbic recipes on flaming arrows, shish-kebab-style.

After a small battle between the two tribes over the [[Ursalia Horn]], he and his fellow Barbics join forces with the Glen Gummies, in a fight against [[Duke Igthorn]] and his [[Ogres]], with Grubby making sure that [[Sunni Gummi|Sunni]] and [[Buddy (Gummi Bears)|Buddy]] are safe.
