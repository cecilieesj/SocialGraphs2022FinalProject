'''Claire Temple''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who mostly appears in the [[Netflix]] shows set in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. Claire Temple is one of the Night Nurses. As such, she has dedicated her life to use her vast medical skills and knowledge of medicine to heal superheroes.

She is a recurring ally of [[Defenders (team)|the Defenders]]Â ([[Daredevil]], [[Luke Cage]], [[Iron Fist]], and [[Jessica Jones]]).

As befitting a Netflix character, she can be quite violent and aggressive when the occasion calls for it, being able to handle herself quite well in a fight.

Claire Temple was created by Archie Goodwin, George Tuska and John Romita Sr.

==Trivia==
*''[[The Punisher]]'' is the only [[Marvel Cinematic Universe|Marvel]] [[Netflix]] series not to feature Claire.
