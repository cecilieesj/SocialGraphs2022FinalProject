'''Dustin Dreamlake''' (voiced by [[Jason Marsden]]) is a character from the Disney Channel animated series ''[[The Replacements]]''. He is a parody of a singer [[Justin Timberlake]]. He is [[Riley Daring|Riley]]'s idol who danced at Riley's 13th birthday party as well as in the Christmas Special episode. {{CharacterStub}}{{The Replacements}}
