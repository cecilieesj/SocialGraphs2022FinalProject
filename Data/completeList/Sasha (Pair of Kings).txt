[[File:Sasha_Arrested.jpg|thumb]]'''Sasha''' is the ex-girlfriend of [[Boz Parker]]. She appeared in the episode "Heart and Troll".
==History==
Sasha is Boz's ex-girlfriend. She lies to him, telling him she is a queen. She goes to The Castle and pretends to get back together with him, in order to steal. She is caught, arrested by [[Mikayla Makoola|Mikayla]] and put behind bars. (Heart and Troll)
==Appearances==
Heart and Troll
==Personality==
Sasha is very deceptive, and could trick Boz easily. She is just a normal girl, a deceptive liar.
