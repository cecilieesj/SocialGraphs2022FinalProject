|status=Arrested}}'''Dominic Badguy''' (surname pronounced /bædgee/), also known as "[[I'm Number One|Number Two]]" and "The Lemur", is [[Constantine]]'s henchman who helps in the plot to steal the crown jewels of [[England]]. He is the secondary antagonist of ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''. He poses as CEO and international talent manager of Badguy Talent Management and signs [[the Muppets]] to a world tour.
