|alignment = Good}}'''Madam Ubetcha''' is a fortune-teller who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Sofia the First]]'', voiced by [[Nika Futterman]]. In her debut appearance "[[Two to Tangu]]", she meets [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]] and [[Princess Amber|Amber]] in the kingdom of [[Tangu]], and offers them some cryptic advice to find Tangu Peek and their [[Magic Carpet (Sofia the First)|Magic Carpet]].
She appeared again in "[[Finding Clover]]" where Sofia asked if they'd met before but Madam Ubetcha dismissed this question and showed Sofia and her friends, [[Minimus]], [[Crackle]], [[Robin (Sofia the First)|Robin]] and [[Mia (Sofia the First)|Mia]], how to find their friend [[Clover]]. She appeared again to help Sofia save Clover by giving her a cloak for a disguise.
