'''Liz''' is a minor character on the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. She is a student and [[Middleton High School Cheerleaders|cheerleader]] at [[Middleton High School]]. She has brown eyes, red hair and wears a purple headband. She wears a purple and red cheerleader uniform with yellow pom poms.

When she's not in her cheerleader outfit, Liz wears a purple short-sleeved shirt and blue jeans.

==Trivia==
*When she went missing with [[Marcella]], her name was revealed in "[[Sink or Swim]]".
