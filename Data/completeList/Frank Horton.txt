'''Officer Frank Horton'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398799/</ref> is a character in ''[[Lloyd in Space]]''. He is the chief of police in the Intrepidville Police force, husband to [[Mrs. Horton]] and father to [[Eddie Horton]]. He is voiced by [[Clancy Brown]].

==Background==
Frank Horton was born several years before the start of the series. When he was a young boy, he had always wanted to join the police force like his own father. One day, his dad caught him glancing at the steering wheel in his car and decided to let his son drive home. Frank then earned enough qualifications to become a police officer and where he met his long time partner: Officer Bork.

==Personality==
Frank is a peaceful, laid-back man who enjoys enforcing the law and protecting the citizens of Intrepidville. He is also a loving father and husband and takes pride in that his son Eddie wants to become a police officer like him. In his police work, Frank is a very clever detective who can track down even the most slippery criminals. He is also very loyal to his friends and family and will never think twice before stepping into a dangerous situation to save someone in need.
