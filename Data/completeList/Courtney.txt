|imagesize = 203}}'''Courtney'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/courtney.htm</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0387989/</ref> is a secondary character in the ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]] ''animated series.

==Physical Appearance==
[[File:Courtney.jpg|thumb|left|Courtney appears only in season one]]Courtney is a very tall and skinny young girl, long brown hair with fringes and she is only the tallest girl in [[Millard Fillmore Middle School]]. She stands about half a head taller than [[Rose]]. She is always seen wearing a collared soft purple dress shirt with sleeves rolled up to just under the elbow to end at the thigh, matching skirt connected with a left side in the pocket matched-up her sleeves that ends around her waist with a wide belt in dark purple. She also wears brown shoes without socks.

==Personality==
[[File:Courtney_and_Rose.jpg|thumb|left|Courtney stand taller than her size Rose]]Courtney is a friendly student that seems to get along well with other girls. However, she seems to have issues with boys, especially the handsome player kind (as seen from her first reaction when [[Jake Long|Jake]], disguised as [[Jaren MacArthur]], attempts to start a conversation with a flirty remark<ref name="shape">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>. Since she is rarely seen and has no important role in the series, not much else is known about her. She and her friend [[Rose]] were drinking hot coffee<ref name="ski">[[The Ski Trip]]</ref>. Courtney was in her cat costume invited to Jake's Halloween party<ref name="bash">[[The Halloween Bash]]</ref>.
==Relationships==
===[[Rose]]===
She and a new school student [[Rose]] have a close relationship and seem to get along well. She started to have trouble with the boys, and started a comment on Teen Pop Sensation [[Jaren MacArthur]]<ref name="shape">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>. Sometimes when [[Rose]] not around with her when she's busy with [[The Huntsman]] and [[The Huntsclan]].

==Trivia==
*She first appears in the premiere episode<ref>[[Old School Training]]</ref> and returns in four more episodes<ref name="shape">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref><ref name="ski">[[The Ski Trip]]</ref><ref name="bash">[[The Halloween Bash]]</ref><ref name="ring">[[Ring Around the Dragon]]</ref>, and only has minor roles.
*Courtney speaks in the premiere episode and only has two lines in the entire series<ref name="shape">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>.
*When Courtney speaks, she is usually heard off-screen, except for one episode<ref name="bash">[[The Halloween Bash]]</ref>.
*Courtney is a fan of [[Jaren MacArthur]]<ref name="shape">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>.
*In an animation error, Courtney's hair is raven several times and back to brunette<ref name="bash">[[The Halloween Bash]]</ref>.
*Courtney bears a resemblance to Charley Davidson from ''Biker Mice from Mars''.
*Courtney is taller than [[Nona]] from ''[[The Weekenders]].''
*She has not reappeared in Season Two.
