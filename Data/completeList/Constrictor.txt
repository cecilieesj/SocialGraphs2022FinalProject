|powers = Has metal cables which come out of his wrists, and they have the strength to lift a person into the air|alignment = Neutral}}'''Constrictor''' is a supervillain with long, wrist-mounded metal coils. He was among the prisoners escaping the Big House when the Breakout happened.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
He was one of the prisoners of the Big House until the Breakout. During the escape, he grabbed one of the S.H.I.E.L.D. agents and tried to avenge himself, before being taken out by Wasp.

He appeared again as a member of the Serpent Society in their successful attempt to free Madame Hydra and King Cobra from S.H.I.E.L.D. custody despite intervention from Captain America and Spider-Man.

==Powers and Abilities==
He has metal cables which come out of his wrists, and they have the strength to lift a person into the air.
