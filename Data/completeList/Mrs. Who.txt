'''Mrs. Who''' is a character in the 2003 movie ''[[A Wrinkle in Time (film)|A Wrinkle in Time]]'' and in the [[A Wrinkle in Time (2018 film)|2018 remake]].

==Appearances==
===[[A Wrinkle in Time (2003 film)|''A Wrinkle in Time'' (2003)]]===
Charles Wallace hears Mrs. Who calling him in his mind just as he and Meg wander what is going on just as he and Meg wander outside about the strange thing. After discovering a normal crow in an abandoned cottage, Mrs. Who's pair of glasses watch over Calvin and Meg return home.

As Meg and her friends arrive at the Uriel, the third star of the planet Malik, Charles encounters Mrs. Who, to which she is happy to meet him and it is revealed that she is the one who called Calvin. Whatsit explains to the trio that she and Who are still young, explaining that she finds it easier to quote others just as Meg tells her to find her missing father. Later when the children oversee the mysterious dimension where Meg and Charles' father would be at, Mrs. Who gives a warning about the universe in danger of being engulfed by darkness. She later talks with Mrs. Whatsit and Mrs. Which about the possibilities of saving Mr. Murry and bringing him back to the real world from the tyranny of It to which Meg overhears.

After Meg and her friends are sent to the [[Happy Medium]]'s lair, she appears next alongside Mrs. Whatsit where she explains to him that she and her friends have arrived to ask him for advice to help them seek help that no one else can to which he agrees to help Meg and her friends complete the task. She discusses with her to that she has much to be proud of in hoping to defeat the evil, much to H.M.'s reaction to their sadness. After seeing footage of Meg's mother grieving over her husband's disappearance, leading Meg and the rest of the trio to find her missing father. Later before transporting Meg, Charles, and Calvin to Camazotz, Mrs. Who is present with Mrs. Whatsit and Mrs. Which approaching the trio, where Mrs. Which explains to them that they will be watching them when they arrive, but they will be unable to see them as they cannot help them to which when Charles explains to himself that he is not afraid of anything, she tells him not to be too arrogant in facing fears just before the three arrive at their destination while Mrs. Who gives her glasses to Meg.

Back at the Happy Medium's cave, Mrs. Who, alongside Mrs. Which and Mrs. Whatsit, watch footage of Meg and her group entering the Central Central Intelligence headquarters via footage tuned in by H.M.'s crystal ball. Later when Charles is dealing with the Man with Red Eyes, H.M. fearfully reacts to what he did with Calvin, alongside Mrs. Who, Mrs. Which, and Mrs. Whatsit.

Later at Ixchel, she alongside Mrs. Which and Mrs. Whatsit appear at the spot where Meg, her father, and her friend Calvin are at after Mrs. Which has studied Meg's human passion in an attempt to help Meg rescue Charles Wallace. She, Mrs. Whatsit, and Mrs. Which appear in the spot where Aunt Beast rescued Meg where Meg, her father, and Calvin ask her about the possibility of bringing Charles back. Later, it is explained that grace would be the only hope of rescuing Charles Wallace thanks to advice from Mrs. Whatsit, Mrs. Who explains to Meg not to give up faith in herself in order to accomplish her plans of defeating the Man with Red Eyes and rescuing Charles after Mrs. Which explained to Meg that she prided much on her wisdom, but she has taught her much.

After Meg returns to the real world after rescuing her missing father as well as saving Charles Wallace from the forces of It, Mrs. Who suddenly appears in front of Meg's mother alongside Mrs. Whatsit and Mrs. Which just as Charles Wallace introduces his mother to the three of them where she explains that she needed adjustments. After Mrs. Whatsit bids Meg a farewell, Mrs. Who then plans to leave but before she can leave, Meg returns her glasses to her. After Mrs. Which leaves, Mrs. Who then happily leaves after Meg is praised for her heroism, followed by Mrs. Who,  leaving with the other two Mrs. Ws.
