|home = Denver, [[Colorado]]|alignment = Good|status=Active|likes=Emmet|dislikes=Coach Hammerstone, Ivy|partner=[[Emmett Heglin]] (possible boyfriend)}}'''Vonnie'''  is a minor character in ''[[Good Luck Charlie]],'' [http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Teddy Teddy]'s teammate on the volleyball team, played by Cyrina Fiallo. She believes that [http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Kelsey Kelsey], a member of the volleyball team, is too over-dramatic. She absolutely hates [http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Mr_Hammerstone Coach Hammerstone], who nicknamed her "Velociraptor". She doesn't like working on projects and it has been revealed that she picked Teddy as her partner only so she didn't have to work on it. Vonnie and Teddy have appeared to be close friends. In ''[http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Rat-A-Teddy Rat-A-Teddy]'', Vonnie told Teddy that she thinks [http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Ivy Ivy] doesn't like her because Ivy said so right to her face. She also might like [http://goodluckcharlie.fandom.com/wiki/Emmett_Heglin Emmett] because she freaked out when he gave her 'the look' and thought about getting married to him.

==History==
She is [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]]'s teammate on the volleyball team.
