'''Brian Patrick Kameāloha''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Doogie Kameāloha, M.D.]]''. He is the youngest child and son of [[Clara Hannon|Dr. Clara Hannon]] and [[Benny Kameāloha]]. He is [[Kai Kameāloha|Kai]] and [[Lahela Kameāloha]]'s younger brother.

==Background==
===Personality===
Brian Patrick is sociable and curious, ready to discover the wacky world around him.<ref>https://d23.com/meet-the-characters-of-doogie-kamealoha-m-d/</ref> Brian is very goofy and silly and loves his family very much. As the youngest he tried hard to keep up with the rest of the family, which results in him often pretending to be older and hoping to hit puberty and become a man as fast as he can.

==Biography==

===Early Life===
Brian Patrick Kameāloha was born and raised in Oahu, Hawaii. He is the youngest child of Dr. Clara Hannon and Benny Kameāloha. He is also the younger brother of Kai and Lahela Kameāloha, and he has a crush on Lahela's best friend, Steph Denisco.
