'''Wolf Spider''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''. Wolf-Spider is the evil counterpart to the real [[Spider-Man]]; unlike the heroic original, he is a villain in his world who wanted to obtain all the shard of the Siege Perilous to conquer the entire multiverse.

==Trivia==
*The name "Wolf Spider" is derived from the [[Wikipedia:Wolf spider|wolf spider]], spiders that belongs to family ''Lycosidae'', named due to their predatory hunting behavior.
*Wolf Spider is voiced by [[Christopher Daniel Barnes]] who previously voiced Spider-man in "Spider-man: The Animated Series" and then later voiced Spider-man Noir in the video game "Spider-man: Shattered Dimensions."
