'''Morbius''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and supervillain who appeared inÂ ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', voiced by [[Ben Diskin]].

Morbius was created by Roy Thomas, John Romita Sr., Gil Kane and Frank Giacoia.

==History==
He was a [[HYDRA]] scientist who was assigned to be a lab assistant for [[Doctor Octopus]] to help with creating the Anti-Venom, and later got turned into a human-vampire bat creature by Doc Ock.

==Trivia==
*The character was supposed to appear at the end of ''Blade'' (1998) where he was portrayed by director Stephen Norrington. He eventually made his theatrical debut in ''Morbius'' (2022) played by Jared Leto.
