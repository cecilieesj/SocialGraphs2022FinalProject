'''Shriek''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and supervillainess who appeared in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''. She was voiced by [[Ashley Eckstein]].

==History==
After she was exposed to the energy from the Darkforce Dimension, Shriek gained the ability to control sound, the ability to generate fear, anger, and sadness, the ability to control minds, telepathy, and the ability to fly. She decided to use her power as a supervillainess to try and take over [[New York City]] and kill [[Spider-Man]].
