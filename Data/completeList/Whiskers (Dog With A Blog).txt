[[File:WhiskersDWAB.jpg|thumb]]'''Whiskers''' is a cat that made an appearance in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Character History==
Ellen was babysitting her for a friend. Bennett, Avery and Ellen wanted to keep her away from Stan. Once he knows a cat is inside a house, he attempted to get rid of it. However, Stan discovered he may like a cat.

Stan thought he could like a cat like an individual but not as group. At the end, her owner came to take her back, once Stan was about to say goodbye, Whiskers scratched, restarting Stan's dislike for cats again.

==Trivia==
*She's the only cat that is named.
*Before her name was confirmed, Bennett suggested naming her Marjorie, to which Ellen opposed about naming her after her mother.
