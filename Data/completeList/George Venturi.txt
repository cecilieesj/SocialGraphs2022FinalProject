'''George Venturi''' is the father of the Venturi family and step-father to the McDonald family. He was first married to a woman named Abby till they got a divorce and he remarried toÂ [[Nora McDonald]].

==Character Description==
George is a laid-back kind of guy and plays the electric guitar and does home renovation, both fairly badly. He also likes to joke around, cook, and watch sports on TV with his sons [[Derek Venturi|Derek]] and [[Edwin Venturi|Edwin]] . He is an average driver, and his license was suspended numerous times. Before marrying Nora, George raised his three children; Derek, Edwin, and [[Marti Venturi|Marti]], all by himself. It is unknown why he is no longer with his first wife Abby or how he got permanent custody over his the 3 children he is also very protective of his children and wants to be able to look after all 3 of his children.
