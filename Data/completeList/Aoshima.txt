'''Aoshima''' is a made up creature seen in the ''[[Gravity Falls]]'' episode "[[The Inconveniencing]]", After eating "bleventeen" packs of [[Smile Dip]], [[Mabel Pines|Mabel]] sees Aoshima in a hallucination. It appears as a flying dolphin with a small yellow bow tie, and two muscular humanoid arms. After spinning around, it grows another dolphin-like head and two more muscular humanoid arms. On each palm of its hands are dolphin faces, which shoot rainbow-colored rays from their mouths. While doing so, a car alarm can be heard.
