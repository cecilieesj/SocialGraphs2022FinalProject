'''Ravager K''' is a character from the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], who appeared in ''[[What If...?]]'', voiced by Tanya Wheelock. She is a member of the [[Ravagers]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]''===
====''[[What If...?]]''====
In alternative timeline, were [[Black Panther|T'Challa]] became Star-Lord, K a member of the [[Ravagers]], was involved with the Central Bank heist of Tarnax IV. She worked along side of T'Challa, [[Yondu Udonta]], [[Taserface]], and [[Kraglin Obfonteri]]. She battled [[Skrulls]] and successfully pulled off the heist along with the rest of the Ravagers.

Following the Battle of Morag, the Ravager group met at the Iron Lotus where they shared a drink with K and talked about the Central Bank heist of Tarnax IV. She laughed along with the others as she remembered how that job paid for Kraglin Obfonteri's new teeth.

In alternative timeline, were [[Thor Odinson|Thor]] was an only child, she was invited to Thor's party, K went along with the Ravagers to attend his party on Earth. She arrived in Las Vegas in time for the party to begin and partied with Thor and his compatriots all night. In the morning, she woke up passed out on the floor of [[Jane Foster|Jane Foster's]] hotel room. Later, in [[Paris]], Ravager K, [[Ayesha]] and her maid, took a selfie with Thor, but they were interrupted by [[Fandral]] and [[Sif]], who alerted Thor of [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]].

==Trivia==
*Unlike other characters, she never appeared in the live-action media of [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], she was created specifically for ''[[What If...?]]''.
*The character's likeness is based off of Magdiela Hermida Duhamel, the production supervisor of ''What If...?''.{{Citation needed}}
