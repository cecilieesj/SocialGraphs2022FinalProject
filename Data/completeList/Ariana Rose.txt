'''Ariana Rose''' is a minor character in the Descendants franchise. She is the cousin of [[Audrey (Descendants)|Audrey]] and appeared in [[Escape from the Isle of the Lost: A Descendants Novel]]. 

== Personality ==
She behaves as a snobby and self absorbed girl who shows dislike towards Audrey. She was rude with [[Evie]] and told her it was naive that she said that Audrey does not need a prince to be happy. She didn't seem to be friends with [[Mal]] either and went right passed her without talking. 

== Printed Media ==

=== [[Escape from the Isle of the Lost: A Descendants Novel]] ===
She is first seen in chapter thirty-eight. She had to get her dress from Evie because the the [[Flora, Fauna, and Merryweather|Three Good Fairies]] were making Audrey's gown at the time. Ariana did not believe Evie's saying that Audrey doesn't need to a date a prince to be happy.<br />
