}}'''Harry Handbag''' is a Toon bag in the TV series ''[[Bonkers]]''. He appears in the episode "[[In the Bag (Bonkers)|In the Bag]]" and is voiced by [[Frank Welker]].
Harry was feeling rather alone and without any company. He figured that he wouldn't be so lonesome if he stored people and objects inside of him.

==Trivia==
*Harry the Handbag is one of the four criminals that [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]] needs to arrest in the Sega Genesis game.
