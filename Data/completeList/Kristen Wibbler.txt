[[File:Th_555780660_CarolineSunshineSammiHanratty_ShakeItUpS03E1613_122_368lo.jpg|thumb|280px]]'''Kristin Wibbler''' is a one-time character and [[Tinka Hessenheffer]]'s rival who appears in the "Bag It Up" episode of ''[[Shake It Up]]''. She was played by [[Sammi Hanratty]].

==History==
She first appeared after mocking Tinka's fashion where afterwards Tinka, Rocky and Cece agreed to rent a designer purse. Tinka challenged Kristin to a fashion showdown where, At first Tinka thought she got the upper hand as she got the designer purse however it turns out that Kristin has the same purse but with a matching wallet and shoes, in which she caused Tinka to faint and lose the fashion showdown and walked out laughing in victory.
