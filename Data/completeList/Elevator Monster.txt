[[File:Elevator_monster.png|thumb]]'''The Elevator Monster''' is a psychic being from [[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]] that serves [[the Skeleton King]]. He looks like an old man, except that below his waist is an elevator-like protrusion instead of feet. He has the ability to transport anybody who comes into the elevator to a completely different spot. He has disguised himself as an old man in an elevator, leaving many people inconspicuous.
