'''Jack Bolton''' is a minor character in the ''High School Musical'' trilogy. He is the basketball coach at [[East High School]] and is the father of [[Troy Bolton]].

==''High School Musical''==
The Coach does not initially approve of Troy and Gabriella's relationship, worrying that Gabriella might distract Troy from the basketball game. Eventually, though, he accepts her. 

==''High School Musical 2''==

==''High School Musical 3: Senior Year''==
Jack watches as the Wildcats win the game in the opening song (Now or Never), and later watches his son graduate from East High.

==[[High School Musical on Stage!]]==
Jack's character also appears in the stage adaption of the first film, having a very similar role to the movie.

==Trivia==
*It's revealed in the first film that Jack was student at East High (he graduated in 1984) and had also played on the school basketball team.
