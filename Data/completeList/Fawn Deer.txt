}}'''Fawn Deer ''' is an anthropomorphic deer featured in television shows ''[[Raw Toonage]]'' and ''[[Bonkers]]''. She is [[Bonkers D. Bobcat]]'s main love interest and co-star when he was a cartoon star. 

==Personality==
Fawn is very sweet and passionate as well as friendly and likable. She cares a lot about those close to her, especially Bonkers.

==History==
Fawn is [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]]' main love interest and co-star when he was a cartoon star. Bonkers is willing to do just about anything to please and impress her. Fortunately for Bonkers, Fawn clearly reciprocates his love for her, as she has proven it on numerous occasions by kissing him on the cheek, and sometimes on the lips. She appears mainly in the "Miranda Episodes."
