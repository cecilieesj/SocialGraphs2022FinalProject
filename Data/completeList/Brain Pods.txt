|status = Active}}'''Brain Pods''' are Cybernetic brains in mobile jars, who serve [[Emperor Zurg]] as scientists and researchers. Although they take pride in their creations, they secretly harbor a desire to escape from Zurg's control (two of which are shown succeeding in doing so on-screen).

[[Jeff Bennett]] voiced #18 and #81. [[Dan Castellaneta]] and [[Sean Hayes]] voiced #13. [[Tress MacNeille]] voiced #94. [[Jeremy Piven]] voiced #57. [[Paul Rugg]] voiced an unidentified Brain Pod. [[Frank Welker]] voiced Brain Pods #17, #26, #39, #99, and one unidentified pod.
