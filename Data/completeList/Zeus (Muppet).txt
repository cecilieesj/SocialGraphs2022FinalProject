'''Zeus''' is king of the gods and patriarch of many in [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Greek_Mythology Greek Mythology]. Traditionally bearded and occasionally able to shoot thunderbolts, Zeus is also known for his many affairs, often by taking the form of wildlife. His siblings included [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Hades Hades] and Poseidon.

Zeus appeared on ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Muppets_Tonight Muppets Tonight]''. In a "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Thor,_God_of_Thunder Thor, God of Thunder]" sketch from [https://muppet.fandom.com/wiki/Episode_108:_Jason_Alexander episode 108], the two deities battle it out in a library and vaporize Ernest Pleth.
