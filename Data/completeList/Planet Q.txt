[[File:Planetoid_Q.png|thumb]]'''Planetoid Q''' is a living planetesimal with a jack o' lantern-esque face, in ''[[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]]'', ordered by [[The Skeleton King|the Skeleton King]] to collide with [[Shuggazoom]]. Later, it is convinced by [[Chiro]] to make its own decisions and do what it wants, instead of listening to the Skeleton King's commands. Planetoid Q appeared as an aspect of the Dreamlands in a later episode.
