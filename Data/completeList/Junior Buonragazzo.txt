
[[File:Junior Buonragazzo.PNG|thumb|200px|right]]'''Junior Buonragazzo''' is a supporting character in [[Geppetto (film)|''Geppetto'']]. He is the son and apprentice of child-maker Professor Buonragazzo. He assists in handling the machine that makes "perfect children".

Junior helps his father out in his store by supplying the children to make perfect.
