'''Meena Paroom''' is the main character of [[Disney Channel]]'s original series ''[[Cory in the House]]''.

== Biography==
She is the daughter of the ambassador of Bahavia, a fictional country sharing elements of India, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan and Bolivia. She loves to wear American outfits and listen to hard rock music.
==Trivia ==

*Cory previously had a crush on Meena. But Meena never found out
*Meena lives two different lives(American life and Bahavian Life)
*Meena is the Singer of DC3 band
*Meena's father is the Ambassador of Bahavia
*She's similar to Eddie from That So Raven
**Both like to sing
**Both are a different gender of a group of 3 friends
