'''King Groucharooney''' is [[Grumpy]]'s cousin and ruler of Grumpylvania in the ''[[The 7D]]'' episode "[[Game of Grumpy]]". He was voiced by [[Jay Mohr]]. Groucharooney welcomed Grumpy to his kingdom to celebrate his "accomplishments" as "leader" of the 7D (which is false).
==Personality==
Despite his name, Groucharooney is actually nice. He was later disappointed when Grumpy lied to him.
