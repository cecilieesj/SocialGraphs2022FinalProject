'''Frida Liang''' is [[Miles Callisto|Miles']] free-spirited aunt and [[Phoebe Callisto|Phoebe's]] sister who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated seires ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''.

==Background==
As an artist Frida loves to paint, especially while standing on one hand. As part of the Martians Artists Commune on [[Mars]], she lives a very simple life with less technology, such as transportation and communications. Frida has a pet robot porcupine named [[Pollock]], who carries quills that are paint brushes when she does her paintings.

==Trivia==
* Frida is the second member of [[Miles Callisto|Miles]]' family to own a robot pet, first was Miles with [[M.E.R.C.]]
* Frida is possibly inspired by the Mexican painter Frida Kahlo.
