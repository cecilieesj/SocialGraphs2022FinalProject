The '''FPS Robot''' is a character in ''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]''. It appears in the arcade game, ''[[Hero's Duty]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
It seems to take its role seriously. It also gets upset with Ralph.

===Physical appearance===
It has silver skin, wheels for feet, and a screen head.

==Appearances==
===''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]''===
As the [[Moppet Girl]] prepares to play "[[Hero's Duty]]", the FPS Robot appears during gameplay as the girl begins her duty playing the arcade game. During gameplay, [[Wreck-It Ralph (character)|Ralph]] constantly interrupts by going into the robot's screen as the girl is tasked to fight against the [[Cy-Bugs]] in order to progress through gameplay. The girl's gameplay, however, was unsuccessful when one of the Cy-Bugs flies toward the robot, causing her to lose the game. Afterwards, the Robot then leaves after being bothered by Ralph, indicating that the game is over.
