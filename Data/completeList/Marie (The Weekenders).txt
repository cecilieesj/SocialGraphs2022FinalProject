[[File:Marie_(The_Weekenders).jpg|thumb]]'''Marie''' is a friend of the gang, who likes "girly things" and a character in ''[[The Weekenders]]''. In order to become popular, they consider ditching Marie on her birthday for [[Pru]], but end up ditching Pru for Marie. Marie was briefly Pru's friend, when Pru tried to make her friends jealous for not giving her an Arbor Day present.
