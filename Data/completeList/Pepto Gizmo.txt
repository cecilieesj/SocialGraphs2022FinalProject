'''Pepto Gizmo''' is a bird doctor and a shaman in the Kiwi tribe. He helps the chief to lead the tribe. He appears in the ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers|Rescue Rangers]]'' episode "[[Kiwi's Big Adventure]]". [[Jim Cummings]] voiced him.

Throughout the episode, Pepto is trying to return the kiwis to the sky using magic and the [[Ranger Plane]]. With the other members of the tribe, he pursues the Rescue Rangers in order to take away the Ranger Plane, which the Kiwi tribe consider a flying deity. At the end of the episode, Pepto is seen treating Dale's broken toe.

==Trivia==
*His name is likely a parody of the medicine Pepto-Bismol.
