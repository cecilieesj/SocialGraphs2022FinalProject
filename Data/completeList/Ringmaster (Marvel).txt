The '''Ringmaster''' is the head of [[Circus of Crime]]. He steals and uses tech from other people like [[Stark Industries]]. He has a flamethrower on his palm of hand, and is able to teleport. He acts as the announcer for the circus calling out his criminals to take on the heroes. He appeared in Avengers Assemble. After his defeat, him and his organisation are all arrested by the SHIELD.

The Ringmaster was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].
