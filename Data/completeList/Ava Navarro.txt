'''Ava Navarro''' is a new character that was first introduced in Season 2 of [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]''. She is one of the main characters in Season 2 of [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]''. She is the daughter of Julie Navarro. She is a former Beach Volleyball Player who gets kicked off the team and [[Marvyn Korn]] recruits her to join the Westbrook Sirens Basketball Team. She started attending Westbrook School for Girls in Ava Fever after being homeschooled and has been a student ever since then. She is currently a Starter for the Westbrook School Basketball Team.
==Background==
Ava is a gutsy beach volleyball phenom whose public tantrum got her ousted from her own sport.<ref>[https://www.disneyplusinformer.com/new-details-revealed-for-big-shot-season-2/ New Details Revealed For ‘Big Shot’ Season 2 - Disney Plus Informer]</ref>

==Trivia==

*She used to play Beach Volleyball before she was kicked off the team in Ava Fever.
*She used to be homeschooled before she came to Westbrook.
*Her Basketball Jersey Number is 19.
*She is most likely replacing [[Olive Cooper]].
*She is similar to [[Marvyn Korn]].
**Both Marvyn and Ava had a Public Tantrum that got them kicked off a team. Marvyn had a public tantrum at a University of Wisconsin-Madison Men's Basketball Game that got him fired for throwing a chair at a referee. Ava had a public tantrum that got her kicked off the Beach Volleyball Team.
