'''Queen Zonthara''' is an antagonist from the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Dave the Barbarian]]''.
==History==
Zonthara is the ruler of Hyrogoth, and keeps on trying to teach Irmaplotz to be more evil. Her father is good, making her half-evil.
