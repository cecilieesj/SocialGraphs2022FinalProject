'''Katie''' is a supporting character of the animated series ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. She is the blonde-haired member of [[Fireside Girls]] Troop 46231.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Katie has blond hair in two low pigtails with red elastics, green eyes and is light-skinned. She wears a uniform with a red collar with large shoes and no socks, but in winter, she wears a red sweater with orange kerchief, yellow boots, has brown elastics in her hair, and wears an orange hat. She does not wear leggings in winter, unlike the other girls.  

==Trivia==
*She and [[Gretchen]] are the only Fireside Girls who appear in the [[Phineas and Ferb (video game)|''Phineas and Ferb'' Nintendo DS game]].
*Her first speaking role was interrupting Isabella in "[[Out to Launch (Phineas and Ferb)|Out to Launch]]", just as she was about to ask Phineas to the dance.
*She may be ambidextrous and can play the guitar, the violin, and the trumpet. (“[[Zubada]]”)
*A running gag in "[[Bee Story]]" is Katie saying or doing unusual things only to get a glance from another Fireside Girl.
