'''Able''' is a minor character in the [[Disney XD]] series, ''[[Tron: Uprising]]''. He owns a successful [[Light Cycles|light cycle]] garage, called Able's Garage, in [[Argon City]]. His employees include [[Beck]], [[Zed]], [[Mara]], and [[Link (Tron: Uprising)|Link]]. He acts as a grandfather figure to Beck. He is also one of the few programs who know that [[Tron (character)|Tron]] is still alive. He seems to be good friends with him, and supports the renegade. After saving [[Zed]] and [[Mara]] from [[Cyrus (Tron: Uprising)|Cyrus]]'s bombs, one of them attaches itself to him, and in the ensuing explosion, he gets derezzed.
