'''Nikki''' is an supporting character on the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[Raven's Home]]''. She is young adult resident of [[San Francisco]], that becomes [[Raven Baxter|Raven]]'s unpaid assistant. She comes from a wealthy background, but her father restricted access to her trust fund unless she has a job.

==Trivia==
*She was originally called "Drew" and was described as "EARLY 2000'S CELEBUTANTE VIBES."
*Raven acknowledges that Nikki is transgender in [[Raven and the Fashion Factory]] which Raven states makes her amazing, not different.
*Nikki knows pop star Ariana Venti (the former Ariana Murphy).
*She also knows Raven's former acquaintance J.J., from Boyz N' Motion.
*Juliana Joel is the first openly trans actress on Disney Channel.<ref>[https://www.instagram.com/p/CfwdiPYrffF/ 1st out Trans character on Disney Channel? I can finally share that I’ve joined the season 5 cast of Raven’s Home on Disney Channel !]</ref>
*Nori Reed, the writer of the episode where Nikki was introduced, has confirmed that she now envisions her as a transgender character, saying, "I think I may have created the first ever trans character for the Disney Channel!"<ref>https://www.washingtonblade.com/2022/07/13/ravens-home-introduces-first-trans-character-in-disney-channel-history/</ref><ref>https://www.mic.com/culture/ravens-home-disney-channel-first-trans-character</ref><ref>https://hollywoodunlocked.com/raven-symone-home-disney-channel-trans-character-nikki/</ref>
