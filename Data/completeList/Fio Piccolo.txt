'''Fio Piccolo''' is a character who appears in the japanese animated film ''[[Porco Rosso (film)|Porco Rosso]]''. Despite being a girl and a child in the twentieth century who is often discriminated against, she is one of a kind air plane engineer who is determined to prove her capabilities.
