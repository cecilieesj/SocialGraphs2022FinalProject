|fate = Watches Simba reclaim his throne}}'''Daabi''' is an aardvark character who was set to appear in ''[[The Lion King]]'', when it was initially titled ''[[King of the Jungle]]''.
==Personality==
Daabi is a rather dopey animal, and even speaks in a dopey manner. She has a crush on Simba despite the difference in species and on the food chain, but is loyal and loving friend. It is she who warns Nala that [[Banagi]] and [[Baasho]] are onto her plan to seduce [[Scar]] and eventually be rid of him.

==Appearance==
Daabi is described as having a wet square nose, kangaroo-like eyes and a long whip-like tongue.

==Information==
===In King of the Jungle - The Script===
Daabi is a childhood friend of Simba and Nala, but she is more Simba's friend than Nala's. Later she becomes one of the main reasons Scar is overthrown by Simba and Nala.
