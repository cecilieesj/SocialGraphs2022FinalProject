'''Sid''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is one of the forest animals.

== Appearance ==
Sid has gray and brown fur and pink eyes.

== Personality ==
Sid is a cheeky trickster who is obsessed with nuts. He is a crazy and highly energetic squirrel. Sid is very athletic, seeing himself as a parkour expert, and is very obsessed with the hoarding and eating of nuts, seeming to twitch whenever nuts are referred to.

== Trivia ==
*Fergus referring to Sid as "Fifty Nuts" may have been a reference towards the rapping industry towards the American Rapper [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/50_Cent 50 Cent].
