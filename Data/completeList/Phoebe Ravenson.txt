'''Phoebe Ravenson''' is a recurring character in [[Disney Junior]]'s ''[[Vampirina]]''. She is a young witch and the eponymous girl in the episode "[[The Little Witch (Vampirina)|The Little Witch]]".

In the episode, she and her parents visit [[The Scare B&B]] where she befriends [[Vampirina Hauntley]], who, along with [[Demi]] and [[Gregoria]], help her improve her witch skills so she can be as good as her parents.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Phoebe Ravenson is a young witch with a small pointy nose and pale green skin with faint freckles matching her parents. She has braided pigtails with an orange hair color. She wears a black witch hat with a pink strap and a star on it, a moon pendant, a white and grey cat sweater with striped sleeves, a red belt and pants, purple leggings with small pink polka-dots, and brown boots with two black straps each.

As a cat, she is small and has black fur, while retaining her hat and pendant.
