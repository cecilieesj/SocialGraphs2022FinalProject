{{NeedImage}}'''Professor Frederickson''' is a minor character in ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids]]''. He is part of the conference [[Wayne Szalinski]] attends. He doesn't believe in the theory of shrinking, but he does say Wayne managed to shrink one thing: the size of the audience, and he proves it by going "to lunch".
