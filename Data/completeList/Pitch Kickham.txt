'''Pitch Kickham''' is a superstar soccer athlete from the [[Disney XD]] series ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''. His name is based on former professional soccer player [[David Beckham]]. He is voiced by [[Simon Pegg]].

==Role in the series==
Kickham first appears in the episode "[[The McHugger Games]]", in which he is seen in a television advertisement, stating whomever blocks his shot wins a limousine trip to the airport with him.
