|siblings=Younger Sibling (born in episode)}}'''Emma Sue '''is a one-time secondary antagonist in ''[[Gravity Falls]]'', who only appeared in the episode "[[Roadside Attraction]]".

==Role in the series==
During the episode, Emma is seen attending Upside-Down Town at the same time [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]], [[Mabel Pines|Mabel]], [[Stan Pines|Grunkle Stan]], [[Candy Chiu|Candy]], and [[Grenda]] are there. Dipper flirts with her and she gives him her email when she has to leave the attraction as her mother goes into labor and her father says that the family must rush off to Canada for her to give birth. 

She is later seen at [[Mystery Mountain]], after her mother has given birth where Emma and the other girls that Dipper has flirted with over the course of the trip confront him, asking which one of them he likes. When Dipper explains that he was just trying to learn how to talk to girls, and didn't like them, Emma Sue and the other girls leave, upset with him.

==Appearance==
Emma has freckles and wears a white tank-top covered by a light pink crop top, red shorts, and short brown boots. Her hair is light brown, worn in pigtails held up by pink scrunchies.
