'''Vincent Liotta''' is a character in ''[[Jessie (TV series)|Jessie]]''. He is a bad boy and the former boyfriend of [[Emma Ross|Emma]]. He first appeared in the episode, "[[Badfellas]]." He is portrayed by Garrett Backstrom.

==Personality==
Vincent is a bad boy who manipulates people to get everything he wants.
