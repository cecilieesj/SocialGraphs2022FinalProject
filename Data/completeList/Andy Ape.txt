'''Andy Ape''' is the star of the film Wacky Times, where his job is to babysit the destructive Toddler Timmy, whose arsenal ranges from baby bottles to anvils. His personality is friendly, excited and nervous. Whenever someone does harms to his feet, he destroys anything in his path. This was used by [[Darkwing Duck]] to fend off a zombie. He is a parody of [[Roger Rabbit]], like [[Bonkers D. Bobcat]].
