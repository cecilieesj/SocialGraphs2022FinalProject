'''Helen Stacy''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared as supporting character in ''[[Spidey and his Amazing Friends]]'', voiced by [[Kari Wahlgren]]. She is a detective for the New York City Police Department and [[Spider-Gwen|Gwen Stacy]]'s mother.

Helen Stacy was created by Howard Mackie and Dan Fraga.
