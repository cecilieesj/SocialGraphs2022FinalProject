'''Millie''' is a minor character in the [[2022]] [[Disney+|Disney+ Original]] film ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers (film)|Chip ‘n Dale: Rescue Rangers]]''. She is [[Chip and Dale|Chip]]'s pet dog.

==Role in the film==
Millie is Chip’s only companion, after Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers was canceled after just under of year of airing time and the cast ultimately went their separate ways. She lives in a quiet dwelling with her owner, he turning down numerous offers from his co-workers to join them in events at their homes with her.

When Chip and Dale go on an adventure to find Monterey Jack, Chip is constantly worrying about Millie, since he left her home alone. At the end of the film she is at Monterey Jack’s party welcoming him back from his surgery with Dale eating pizza.

In the end credits, Millie can be seen running to catch a falling pizza at a party with the Rescue Rangers.
