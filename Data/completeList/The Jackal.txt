'''Jack "Jackal"''' is an antagonist in ''[[K.C. Undercover]]''. As an enemy of the Cooper Family, he appeared in the Season 2 premiere, "Coopers Reactivated!" In the episode, Jackal vowed to get revenge on the Coopers for locking him up in prison. He was defeated by [[K.C. Cooper]] with the help of [[Judy Cooper]] and he fell to his death. He is portrayed by stuntman/actor [[James Logan]].
