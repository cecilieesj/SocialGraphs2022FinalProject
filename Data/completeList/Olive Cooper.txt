'''Olive Cooper''' was one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]'' in Season 1. She is the daughter of Dawn and Timothy Cooper. She is a former student at Westbrook School for Girls. She was a Small Forward for the Westbrook School Basketball Team.
==Background==
===Personality===
Olive is very unapologetic who isn’t initially impressed by Coach Korn’s dismissive behavior and isn’t afraid to set him in his place when he goes overboard. She is very joyful and loves to bring happiness on the court.

==Biography==
===Early life===
Olive Cooper was born to Dawn and Timothy Cooper. Olive comes from a middle class family. Her father owns a Bakery and her mother used to worked overtime to pay for her tuition to go to Westbrook School for Girls.

==Trivia==
*She has a 149,000 followers on Instagram.<ref>The Marvyn Korn Effect</ref>
*She loves Social Media.
*She is a Social Media Influencer.
*It was revealed in Great in the Living Room, that her mother works overtime to pay for her tuition at Westbrook School for Girls. When [[Marvyn Korn|Coach Korn]] hears about this he decides to give the Full Ride Athletic Scholarship to Olive that he was going to give to Savannah Gibson to convince her to play for Westbrook School Basketball Team so they can beat Carlsbad Prep in a few weeks.
*Her father owns a Bakery.<ref>Great in the Living Room</ref>
*She is one of the nine characters that has appeared in every episode so far along with [[Holly Barrett]], [[Samantha Finkman]], [[Louise Gruzinsky]], [[Emma Korn]], [[Marvyn Korn]], [[Carolyn Smith]], [[Sherilyn Thomas]] and [[Destiny Winters]].
