}}'''Lacey Ladybug''' is a snobbish ladybug and antagonist from ''[[The Buzz on Maggie]]''.

==History==
Lacey transferred to Buzzdale Academy from her old school in Beverly Gardens. She is snobby, pompous, and enjoys bossing other students around and acting as though she is better than them. She claims that she lived a life of luxury in Beverly Gardens and that her dad is the mayor. However, [[Maggie Pesky|Maggie]] revealed all this as a fraud to the public.
