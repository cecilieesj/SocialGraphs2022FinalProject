'''Bal Boa''' is a character from the children's series ''[[JoJo's Circus]]''. He is a contortionist snake with a talent for making number and letter shapes and one of [[JoJo Tickle]]'s classmates. Bal Boa also has a knack for troublemaking. He thinks it's fun to coax JoJo and others into doing what they aren't supposed to do, and the kids are sometimes susceptible to his charms. Bal Boa is also an expert on harvesting squirting flowers by doing a trance dance.
