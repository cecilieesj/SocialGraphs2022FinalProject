==Appearances==
===''[[Iron Man 3]]''===
One night, Harley Keener felt the presence of someone in his garage, the intruder turned out to be Tony Stark. The boy and Stark were at the site of a thermal explosion and then went to visit a woman, whose son was causing the explosion. the meeting does not end because Stark meets [[Ellen Brandt]] and [[Eric Savin]], with whom they fight to stay alive.

Afer Brandt's death, Savin takes Harley hostage but the child escapes through a flash grenade given to him by Tony. One day coming home from school, Harley finds his garage was full of electronic equipment which with filled him joy. On a table was a personal gift with a sign that read: "Potato Gun Mark II" and regards from "The Mechanic".

===''[[Avengers: Endgame]]''===
An older Harley attends Tony's funeral.
