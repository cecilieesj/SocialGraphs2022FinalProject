|alignment = Good}}'''Gillian''' is a character on ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. She first appeared in "[[A Giant Save]]".

Gillian is a big Irish-accented doll based on a character [[Professor Hootsburgh]] loves known as ''Gillian the Giant''.

Gillian is sweet by nature and loves to have fun with others but her size sometimes causes problems. In fact, she is as tall as [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc McStuffins]] herself, taller than [[Niles]].
