|quote = }}'''Ruby Hale''' is a character featured on the [[ABC]] series ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''. She is the daughter of General Hale, and the product of genetic experimentation by [[Daniel Whitehall]], who worked on enhanced humans. She made her first appearance in "[[Rise and Shine (Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.)|Rise and Shine]]".
Ruby was raised from birth to be HYDRA's ultimate weapon, leaving her socially stunted. Despite a romantic relationship with Warner Strucker, she was unable to fully reciprocate his feelings.
