'''Kumiko "Kennedy Van Buren" Hashimoto''' is a main antagonist and a recruiting character of the [[Disney Channel Original Series]] ''[[A.N.T. Farm]]''. She is portrayed by [[Piper Curda]].

==History==
She is a student at Z-Tech Prodigy School that she be running for president in 2032. She started a political party to stay in touch with the voters. She was dating [[Fletcher Quimby|Fletcher]] until the episode of "FinANTial Crisis", where she reveals herself as the daughter of Hashimoto (who first appeared in ''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''), sent to destroy the Z-Phones.
