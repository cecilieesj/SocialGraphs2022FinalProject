'''Caretaker''' is a character of the Marvel [[Hulu]] series, [[Helstrom]]. He is a guardian of knowledge of the occult. He uses his knowledge and his relationship with Ana to help keep demonic forces at bay.

Caretaker was created by Howard Mackie and Andy Kubert.
