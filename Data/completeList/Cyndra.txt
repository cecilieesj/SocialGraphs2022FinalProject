'''Cyndra''' is a female [[w:c:high-school-musical:East High|East High]] student in ''[[High School Musical]]'' who also auditions for the winter version of ''[[w:c:high-school-musical:High School Musical|High School Musical]]'' (Twinkle Towne). She is played by Falcon Grace. She is one of the three East High students who auditions for the Winter version of High School Musical (Twinkle Towne), besides East High School's Susan as a female East High School student, and Alan, who is a male East High School student.
