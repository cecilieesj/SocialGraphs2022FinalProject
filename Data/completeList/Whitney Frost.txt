'''Whitney Frost''' is a Marvel Comics character played by [[Wynn Everett]] (who reprised her role in ''[[Avengers Assemble]]''), Ivy George and Olivia Welch in ''[[Agent Carter]]''. She is the daughter of a mob boss who decided to become a supervillainess, using her skills at martial arts and her proficiency with firearms to become one of the first supervillains in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]].

Whitney Frost was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Gene Colan.
