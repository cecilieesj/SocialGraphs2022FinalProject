'''Priya''' is a supporting character in ''[[Mira, Royal Detective]]''. She is [[Mira]]'s creative cousin and a friend of [[Prince Neel]].

==Appearance==
Priya is a slender girl with olive skin, brown eyes, and long dark brown hair with small white flowers. She has gold hoop earrings, a light green dress, scarf and leggings, a light blue apron, and light blue sandals.

==Personality==
Despite her cuteness and beauty, Priya is clever and generous. She is a sweet-spirited, caring young girl and modest. She is very witty with a dry sense of humor.
