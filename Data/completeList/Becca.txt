'''Becca''' is the main protagonist of [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[2022]] Halloween comedy-fantasy film, ''[[Hocus Pocus 2]]''. She is a 16-year-old teenage girl from high school, alongside her friends, [[Izzy (Hocus Pocus)|Izzy]] and [[Cassie Traske|Cassie]]. She is aspiring to become a witch. By using the [[Black Flame Candle]], she accidentally resurrects the [[Sanderson Sisters]] after 29 years.

==Background==
===Powers and abilities===
Because she is a witch, Becca gets her powers on the night of her sixteenth birthday. She can perform actual magic and cast spells. Her magic shows when her hands glow blue.
