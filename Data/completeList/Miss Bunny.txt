'''Miss Bunny''' is [[Thumper]]'s love interest and mate in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[1942]] film, ''[[Bambi (film)|Bambi]]''. She appears in only one scene when she spots him and falls in love with him after she tricks him into noticing her. She sings and walks up to him and says her only line: "Hello" and is last seen petting his ear.

==Appearances==
===''[[Bambi (film)|Bambi]]''===
Right after [[Bambi (character)|Bambi]], Thumper and [[Flower]] claimed that they will never fall in love, Miss Bunny notices them and fashions herself up making her look flirtatious and noticeable. She struts up close to Thumper and bats her eyelashes at him, saying “hello”. Thumper eventually spots her and she woos him with charm and a kiss.

===''[[House of Mouse]]''===
Miss Bunny was seen in the episode "[[Jiminy Cricket (House of Mouse)|Jiminy Cricket]]", sitting with Thumper and his friends, while staring at Thumpes appearances for meets and greets at [[Disney's Animal Kingdom]] at Walt Disney World with Thumper (formerly at [[Camp Minnie-Mickey]]). In [[Japan]], she is very popular not just for Disney but other things as well, except she has pink cheeks and often wearsthe film, in ''[[Kingdom Hearts χ]]'' (as a costume) and many merchandise and the Disney Parks, she is referred to as "Miss Bunny." Miss bunny soon became an inspiration for Warner Bros. When ''Space Jam'' first came out they modified "Lola Bunny" after her.

== [[Disney Parks]] ==
When [[Thumper]] was reintroduced to the Disney Parks in 2006, he was joined by Miss Bunny. She made her first live appearance at the former Camp Minnie-Mickey area at Walt Disney World. She's more prominent at Tokyo Disney Resort and Disneyland Paris where's she more prominent at the parks. 

=== [[Walt Disney World Resort|Walt Disney World]] ===
Miss Bunny debuted at the [[Camp Minnie-Mickey]] area at Animal Kingdom before it's closure in 2014. She currently makes occasional appearances at Animal Kingdom (such as appearing at [[Rafiki's Planet Watch]] for the 75th Anniversary of Bambi) and appears during Magic Kingdom's annual Easter pre-parades.

=== [[Disneyland Paris]] ===
Miss Bunny makes frequent appearances at Disneyland Paris and very prominent during the resort's seasonal events. Mainly during the Spring and Easter seasons alongside her mate Thumper.

=== [[Tokyo Disney Resort]] ===
At [[Tokyo Disneyland]], she was featured during the park's [[Re-Villains! Halloween Parade|Re-Villains Halloween Parade]] where she's on the large float of [[The Evil Queen|The Evil Queen's hag]] form alongside [[Minnie Mouse]]. 

Beginning in 2011, both Miss Bunny and Thumper become very prominent during the park's easter events. Such as [[Disney's Easter Wonderland]] and [[Usatama on the Run!]] alongside other famous Disney Bunnies (such as [[Rabbit]], [[Br'er Rabbit]] and [[White Rabbit|The White Rabbit]]). Their most prominent appearance was the Easter parade [[Hippity Hoppity Springtime|Hippity-Hoppity Springtime]] where both were on the parade's title float between 2014 and 2015. She alongside Thumper also hosted the show stop section of Hippity-Hoppity Springtime parade.
