'''Lady Rancora''' is the main antagonist of the ''[[Star Darlings]]'' book ''[[Star Darlings: Good Wish Gone Bad (Stellar Edition)|Good Wish Gone Bad]] ''and the TV special ''[[The Power of Twelve]]''. When she was a student at Starling Academy, Rancora was once known as Cora. For most of the franchise; she disguised herself as Lady Cordial, the head of admissions at Starling Academy.
