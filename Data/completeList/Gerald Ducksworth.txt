'''Gerald Ducksworth''' is the tertiary antagonist of the [[1992]] hockey film, ''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''. He is Gordon Bombay's former boss at the Ducksworth Law Firm, a friend of Adam Banks' father and the un-official founder of the Mighty Ducks.
