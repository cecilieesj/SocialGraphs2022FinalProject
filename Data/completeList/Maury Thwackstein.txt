'''Maury Thwackstein''' is a minor character featured in the [[Darkwing Duck (Boom! Studios)|2010]] and the [[Darkwing Duck (Joe Books)|2016]] ''[[Darkwing Duck]]'' revival comic series. He is an elderly Quackworks employee who previously worked in the security department and, after a brief retirement, was named the new CEO of the company.
