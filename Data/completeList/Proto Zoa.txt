'''Proto Zoa''' is one of the main characters in the ''[[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century]]'' trilogy. He is a famous rock star that is the lead singer of the band called “Microbe” that [[Zenon Kar]] and [[Nebula Wade]] are fans of.

== Biography ==

=== Early Life ===
Proto Zoa was born somewhere between 2022-2023 somewhere on Earth. At some point he became a famous and became the lead singer for Microbe. He wrote the song “Super Nova Girl” which became a major hit. In 2049, Proto Zoa’s band Microbe is the first Rock Band to have a Rock concert in Outer Space.

=== Appearances ===

=== [[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century|''Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century'']] ===
In April of 2049 Proto Zoa’s band Microbe is about to become the first rock band to have a rock concert in Outer Space. Right before he becomes the first rock star to have a concert in Outer Space he holds a contest called “Dance with Me” where he chooses one lucky fan from Wyndham Space Station to dance with him at his first rock concert in Outer Space. Both [[Zenon Kar]] and [[Nebula Wade]] who are huge fans of Microbe enter the contest. Proto Zoa picks 13 year old Zenon Kar as the winner of the “Dance with Me” contest.
