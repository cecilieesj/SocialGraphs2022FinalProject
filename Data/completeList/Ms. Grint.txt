'''Terry Grint''' is a reoccurring character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]''. She is a Political Science Teacher at Westbrook School for Girls.

==Background==
===Personality===
Ms. Grint is a tough Political Science Teacher at Westbrook School for Girls who is not very likable. According to the faculty at Westbrook "No one can handled Ms. Grint."

====Everything to Me====
Ms. Grint mentions @KalmK0rn in a condescending tone, then adds “I never received my invitation to your Friday soirée, or is Club Korn strictly 17 and under?” Marvyn Korn tells her the party just happened and she tells him as the adult of the house, the responsibility lies with him.
