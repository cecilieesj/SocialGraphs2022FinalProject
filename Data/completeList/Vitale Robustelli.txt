[[Camellia Falco]]|image = Vitale Robustelli.jpg}}'''Vitale Robustelli''' is a character from [[Soarin']] affiliated with the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]].

== History ==

=== Background ===
Vitale Robustelli was the president of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers in the early 19th-century.  In 1851 he officially brought in the first female S.E.A. member in the form of [[Camellia Falco]].  At an unknown point of time he was removed from his position as president and was succeeded by former club-secretary, [[Jason Chandler]].

== Appearances ==

=== Soaring: Fantastic Flight ===
Vitale appears in portrait form, bringing Camellia Falco into SEA.  The portrait also features her pet falcon Aletta and [[Lord Henry Mystic]] visible in the crowd.  He is identified by a plaque as being Vitale Robustelli and the president of SEA at the time.{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}
