[[File:Zack.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Zack''' is a one-time guest character in ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''. He is portrayed by Chad W. Smathers.
==History==
He is the guy that [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]] went out in Doppel Date. He works at a mall. The main reason Teddy went out with him was because he looked like [[Spencer Walsh|Spencer]].

== Appearance ==
Zack is very tall, as he towers over both Teddy and Ivy. He is probably taller than Spencer and presumably towers over Bob and the rest of the family. He is muscular and has a chubby face.
