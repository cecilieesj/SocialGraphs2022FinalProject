'''Ezra''' is one of the characters in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[2003]] [[film]], the ''[[The Haunted Mansion (2003 film)|Haunted Mansion]]''. He was the Gracey Mansion's footman prior to his death.

== Background ==

===Personality===
Ezra is a cowardly and neurotic, but still loyal, yet bumbling, ghost footman who worries about getting into trouble and facing Ramsley's wrath.

==Role in the film==
In the film, Ezra is one of the minor characters who help out [[Jim Evers]] to dispel the mansion's curse. He first happens upon [[Michael Evers|Michael]] and [[Megan Evers|Megan]] after they follow a ghostly orb up to the attic. He tells them they aren't supposed to be in the attic when Emma comes along carrying a tray of cookies.

==Trivia==
*His line "there's always...my way," comes from [[The Haunted Mansion (Disneyland)|the Haunted Mansion ride]] when the narrator points out there's no way out of the gallery room, except for his way.
