'''Xerxes''' is the familiar of the sorcerer, [[Mozenrath]]. He is a floating eel/lamprey-like creature, who speaks in broken English. He appears in [[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]].

==Personality==
Xerxes is endlessly loyal to his master, though he has the habit of saying the wrong thing and taking a beating for it. And yet despite the abuse, he gets from Mozenrath, Xerxes remains faithful to his boss. He can also be even as sadistic as Mozenrath.
