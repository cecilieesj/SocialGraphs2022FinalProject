|inspiration = [[Little Richard]]|games = [[Walt Disney World Explorer]]}}'''Richard''' is a character in the attraction [[Food Rocks]] at [[Epcot]].

== Appearances ==

=== Food Rocks ===
A pineapple caricature of [[Little Richard]], he sings a healthy eating themed version of "[[Tutti Frutti]]" and reappears to join the cast in "[[Choose Before You Chew]]".

=== [[Walt Disney World Explorer]] ===
A dancing sprite of Richard appears on the program's map of Epcot's [[Future World]] to represent the show.

== Trivia ==
* According to a [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PIDqHaDjDBI Tiara Talk Show podcast interview with the creative team behind Food Rocks], Little Richard kept slipping into the original lyrics of the song during recording sessions out of habit since it had been such a staple of his performing career for years.
