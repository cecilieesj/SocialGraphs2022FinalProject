'''Chloe''' is a female character of the tween hit series ''[[Mackenzie Falls]]''. She is portrayed by [[Chastity Ann DeWitt]].

==History==
The new girl at the Falls, Chloe comes from a less wealthy background than most of her peers-economically. Chloe's family only has one summer home, and her family owns the smallest yacht in town.

==Trivia==
*She replaced [[Portlyn]] as Mackenzie's love interest.
