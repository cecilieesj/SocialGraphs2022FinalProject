'''Cleopatra "Dirtbike" Funnie''' is the baby sister of the Funnie family in the series ''[[Disney's Doug]]'', appearing only in the Disney series. She debuts in the episode ''Doug's Secret Christmas''. She gets her name from [[Judy Funnie|Judy]] snatching [[Doug Funnie|Doug]]'s Christmas list and making fun of the names of what was on it and then sarcastically making relevant suggestions, one of them being Cleopatra<!-- This last sentence doesn't make sense-->. In the end, [[Theda Funnie|Theda]], who had been present at the time, says that she took a name suggestion from each of them. The name was decided via a Disney Adventures contest, with the winner receiving a drawing of himself with Doug and his friends.
