'''Odin''' is the King of the Norse Gods. He appears in the ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]'' episode "[[Hercules and the Twilight of the Gods]]".
==Appearance==
Odin is a blue muscular giant with long white hair and beard. He wears a black eyepatch over his right eye and purple robe over his right shoulder. He also wears a two-horned helmet on his head.
==Powers and Abilities==
As a god, Odin possesses the natural powers and abilities of a NorseÂ deity such as immortality, omnipresence, vast strength, and the potential to do magic such as the ability to shape-shift, to teleport and to manifest objects. However, as the King of the Norse Gods, he has greater powers and abilities compared to the other deities.
