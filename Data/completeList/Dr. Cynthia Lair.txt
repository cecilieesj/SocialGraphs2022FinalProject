'''Dr. Cynthia Lair''' is the main character of [[Epcot]]'s [[Body Wars]] attraction.
During a routine expedition studying the immune system's efforts to prevent an infection from a splinter, she and a retrieval team run into trouble when she is pulled into a capillary, starting a wild ride through the human body.

==Trivia==
*Her first name is only mentioned in the ride's pre-show.
