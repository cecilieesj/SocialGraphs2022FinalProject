[[File:IvanMcCorkleDWaB.jpg|thumb]]'''Ivan McCorkin''' is a minor character that appears in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He's portrayed by Lyle Kanouse.

==Character History==
Ivan appears at the McCorkin reunion at Christmas. When they thought Avery and Tyler are family members, Ivan came to give them a big hug.
