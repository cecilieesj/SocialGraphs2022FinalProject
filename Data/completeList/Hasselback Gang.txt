'''The Hasselback Gang''' is a family of outlaws that appear as the main antagonists of the ''[[Amphibia]]'' episode "[[The Ballad of Hopediah Plantar]]".

==Role in the  series==
The Hasselback Gang first appears when member, Judro starts bullying the citizens of Bittysburg into giving him their money.
