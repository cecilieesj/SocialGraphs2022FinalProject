'''Lucas Edward Friar''' is one of the main characters in ''[[Girl Meets World]]''.

==Information==
Lucas is a native of [[Wikipedia:Austin, Texas|Austin, Texas]], and is a "cowboy in the city." He is the love interest of [[Riley Matthews]] and the former love interest of [[Maya Hart]].
