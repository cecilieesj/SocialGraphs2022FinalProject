}}'''Mack and Diesel''' are characters from Disney's [[2002]] movie, ''[[Snow Dogs]]''. They are strong, powerful, and well trained sled dogs. As part of the middle of the sled dog team, its up to them keep the pace and pull the weight of the sled. They also ensure that Scooper and Duchess, keep the the same pace, nipping them when they need encouragement.
