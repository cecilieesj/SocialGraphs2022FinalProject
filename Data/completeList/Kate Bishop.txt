'''Kate Bishop''', also known as '''Hawkeye''', is a [[Marvel Comics]] character. She made her debut in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] via the [[Disney+]] original series ''[[Hawkeye (series)|Hawkeye]]'', with her and [[Hawkeye|Clint Barton]] being the titular protagonists of the series.

Kate Bishop was created by Allan Heinburg and Jim Cheung.

==Background==
===Personality===
Kate Bishop is an extremely confident, brave, and smart individual with a great sense of justice, who has a tendency to see things others do not due to her high intellect. After Hawkeye saved her life at a very young age, Kate was inspired to take a stand for whom she deeply loves and cares about, desiring to become a hero to protect those she loves.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Hawkeye (series)|Hawkeye]]''====
Kate Bishop appears in this [[Disney+]] series, set after the events of ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]''.

===Marvel Animated Universe===
====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====
Kate Bishop makes a cameo in the thirteenth episode of the third season, "[[Into the Future]]". She resides in a dystopian world in the 30th century and is part of a rebellion to take down [[Kang]].

== Disney Parks ==
Kate Bishop aka Hawkeye/Lady Hawkeye/Hawkette will appear with [[Hawkeye|Hawkeye (Clint Barton)]] with other heroes in [[Avengers Campus (Disney California Adventure)|Avengers Campus]] at [[Disney California Adventure|Disney's California Adventure]].
