The '''Fractured''' are the main enemies that the Guardians fights in the video game, ''[[Disney Mirrorverse]]''. They are warrior creatures (some of which are copies of the Disney heroes and villains) created by the Fractured Mirror and serve whoever Villain with it under their possession.
