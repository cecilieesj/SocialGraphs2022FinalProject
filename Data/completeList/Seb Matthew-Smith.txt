|quote = |status=Active|parents=Unnamed parents|partner=[[Carlos Rodriguez]] (boyfriend)}}'''Sebastian "Seb" Matthew-Smith''' is a character from the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''. Seb was originally a recurring character in Season 1.  Then Seb became a main character in Season 2.<ref>[https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/tv/story/2019-12-05/high-school-musical-the-musical-the-series-disney-plus-joe-serafini 'High School Musical' on Disney+ ups Joe Serafini to series regular - Los Angeles Times (latimes.com)]</ref> Seb will be a guest star in Season 3. Seb was a sophomore at [[East High School]] in Season 1 and Season 2. In Season 1, Seb was casted as the gender-bent version of [[Sharpay Evans]] in [[East High School]]'s fall production ''[[High School Musical]]''. In Season 2, Seb was casted as [[Chip Potts]] in East High School's spring production ''[[Beauty and the Beast (musical)|Beauty and the Beast]]''.

==Background==

===Personality===
Seb is known for being caring, friendly and kind to others. Despite a show-stopping performance as Sharpay, Seb happens to be quite introverted. His polite and caring nature makes him fairly likable among his peers. At times, Seb can be a little na√Øve. Recently, he has shown signs of only talking about cows and animals. The fact that he lives on a farm is becoming a large part of his personality.

===Physical appearance===
Seb has blonde hair, blue eyes and light skin. He sometimes wear glasses.

=== Season One ===

==== [[The Auditions]] ====
On the first day of school Seb is sitting next to his friend Natalie Bagley at lunch. Seb can be seen in line for people that want to join the musical. Miss Jenn thinks he'll audition for the role of Ryan. Carlos says that he would rather play Sharpay. Seb nods and Miss Jenn agrees that the gender-bent version of the role would be interesting for the show.

==== [[The Read-Through]] ====
Seb gets up from his seat at the read-through to talk to Ashlyn. He compliments her glasses, saying that he has the same ones at home. After Ashlyn asks him why, he tells her it's to help him see better when he's milking one of his cows. This cuts to a confessional where Seb explains to the audience that he stands out in his farming family.

==== Homecoming ====
Carlos helps Seb with the Sharpay choreography via a private dance session. Afterwards, Carlos asks him to homecoming. Seb agrees, but arrives three hours late after his family loses one of their cows.

==Trivia==

* Seb is named after Peter Matthew Smith, an actor who played the role of King George in ''Hamilton'' and is an old theater friend of the creator ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]'' Tim Federle.<ref>[https://twitter.com/TimFederle/status/1239983275591909377 Tim Federle Tweet on Twitter]</ref>
* Tim Federle the creator of ''High School Musical: The Musical: The Series'' made the character Seb specifically for Joe Serafini who originally auditioned for [[E.J. Caswell]].<ref>https://www.post-gazette.com/ae/tv-radio/2021/07/30/tim-federle-joe-serafini-disney-plus-high-school-musical-series-pittsburgh-clo-olivia-rodrigo/stories</ref>
* In ''[[The Read-Through]]'', Seb compliments [[Ashlyn Caswell|Ashlyn]]'s Grandma Glasses by saying they are cool and he has the same ones at home. [[Ashlyn Caswell]] asks him "Why?" and he replies that he wears them so he can see when he's milking.
* He stands out in his farm family because he's obsessed with musical theater.<ref>[[The Read-Through]]</ref>
* He was late to Homecoming because his family lost one of their cows.
* He's gay and his family is very supportive about his sexuality as shown in Act Two.
* He is allergic to Cashmere.
* He has been electrocuted/ struck by lightening.
* He has a sheep called Jimmy who has social anxiety.
* He wanted the next year's musical to be ''Dear Evan Hansen''.
* He is a fan of Greta Gerwig.
