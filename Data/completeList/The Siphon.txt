The Siphon is one of the main antagonists in the movie ''[[Race to Witch Mountain]]''. It is a trained bounty hunter.

== Appearance ==
It is a tall armored alien. Without its helmet, it has hideous features.

== Personality ==
It was merciless and aggressive.
