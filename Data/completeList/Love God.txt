The '''Love God''' is a character from the ''[[Gravity Falls]]'' episode [[The Love God|of the same name]].

==History==
He uses potions to make people fall in love. He has taken on a second career as a freelance musician, since nowadays the Internet has been doing the same duties as he.

[[Mabel Pines]] nabs one of his love potions in order to take on the role of a matchmaker. Mabel also steals a potion to undo the love she has started.
