'''Rowley Jefferson''' is the deuteragonist of [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]'s [[2021]] animated film, ''[[Diary of a Wimpy Kid]]''. He is the best friend of [[Greg Heffley]].

== Background ==
Rowley first appeared riding a big wheel when Greg aims with the football to throw.

==Trivia==
*Rowley has been portrayed by two actors prior in live action. First by [[Robert Capron]] (2010-2012) and then by Owen Asztalos (2017)
