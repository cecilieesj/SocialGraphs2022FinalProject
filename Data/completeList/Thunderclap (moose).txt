'''Thunderclap''' is the main antagonist of the animated shortÂ ''[[Morris, the Midget Moose]]''.

==Role in the film==
He is a wild moose who has a reputation for being the toughest of all moose in the forest with a desire to challenge other moose into battle. He does not hesitate to deliver fights, and every time he wins, he laughs sisterly at the loser. He is eventually beaten by [[Morris the Midget Moose]] and [[Balsam]] (disguised as a monster) and he runs away scared forever.
