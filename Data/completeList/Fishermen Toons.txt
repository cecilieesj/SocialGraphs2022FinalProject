'''Fishermen Toons''' are NPC Toons that you can sell Fish to, in the discontinued online video game, ''[[Toontown Online]]''.

==How to interact with Fisherman Toons==
*Walk up to any Fisherman Toon (Like Fisherman Droopy) with at one or more fishes in your bucket

*The menu will pop up. And you can have the choice to sell them or to not.

*Press any of the two of the options and the menu will close.

*The fisherman toon will say "''Thanks! The Pet Shop will like this species''" or ''"Wow you got (your total of fish) out of 70 fish! That deserves a laff boost and a throphy!"''
