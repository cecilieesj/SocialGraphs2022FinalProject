==History==
He disguised himself as a puppet in the show "Pooka Pooka Fun Fun Farm" and used mind control spells disguised as his "show songs" to control the minds of children to cause chaos, including even [[Haley Long|Haley]]. When [[Jake Long|Jake]] tells his parents that Haley is breaking the rules they gave them, Haley is brought out of mind control and becomes angry at Pooka Pooka and attacks him.

During the credits, it's revealed that the show has been taken off the air for good.
