'''Gregarius''' is a character who appeared in the ''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]]'' episode "[[I Never Mechanism I Didn't Like]]", where he served as a minion for [[Mechanicles]]. He hypnotised [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]], [[Jasmine]], [[The Sultan]], [[Iago]] and [[Abu]]. [[Genie]] and [[Carpet]]  were immune to his hypnotism.

When voicing the character, [[Jim Cummings]] speaks in a tone of voice where he actually does sound like a recording.
