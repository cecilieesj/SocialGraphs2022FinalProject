'''James Taylor "J.T." James''', better known as '''Hellfire''', is a fictional supervillain appearing as a supporting antagonist in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] TV series ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''.

He is a former mercenary who was swayed by the ancient Inhuman [[Hive]] and forced to follow every command and assist him in his plans. After Hive's death, James, tormented by the after-effects of being brainwashed, allied himself with the Watchdogs and desired revenge on [[Daisy Johnson]] for letting Hive control him.

He is portrayed by Axle Whitehead.
