|alignment = Good}}'''Trevor''' is a protagonist and character of tween hit drama ''[[Mackenzie Falls]]''.

==Role in the film==
Trevor is loyal, strong, funny, and tall. All of those traits make up for the fact Trevor isn't the shiniest gold coin in the vault. Trevor has been at [[Mackenzie]]'s side for years. And as far as they are concerned their friendship can withstand anything. Nothing shall come between them. No girl, no scheming half-brother, or joke that goes over Trevor's head.
==History==
He is [[Mackenzie]]'s best friend and partner find and help to fight evil.
