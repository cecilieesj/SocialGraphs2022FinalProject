{{Infobox_character|films = ''[[Nikki, Wild Dog of the North]]''|actor = Robert Rivard|personality = |alignment = Neutral|home = Canadian Northwest|pets = Taao (dog)|friends = [[Andre Dupas]], [[Nikki]], [[Makoki]]|enemies =[[Jacques Lebeau]] |likes = |dislikes = Dog-fighting|weapons = Gun|image = Nikki-Wild-Dog-of-the-North-1961-2.jpg|fate = }}'''Durante''' is a character in the [[1961]] live-action film, ''[[Nikki, Wild Dog of the North]]''.

==Role==
After [[Jacques Lebeau]] and [[Makoki]] capture [[Nikki]], Durante tells Lebeau that dog-fighting is now illegal, but Lebeau refuses. After Nikki's fight with another dog, Andre arrives and is pushed into the ring by Lubeau and Nikki remembers him. However, Lebeau ties Nikki to a cage and fights Andre until he kicks him in the chest. However, Lebeau refuses to accept defeat and tries to stab Andre. However, Makoki frees Nikki and he attacks Lebeau, causing him to accidentally stab himself in the chest. As Durante was about to shoot Nikki, thinking he killed Lebeau, Makoki reveals the knife in his chest, realizing that Lebeau accidentally killed himself.
