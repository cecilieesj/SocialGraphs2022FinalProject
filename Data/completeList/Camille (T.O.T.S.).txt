'''Camille''' is a baby camel who first appeared in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode "[[A Penguin in the Desert]]".

==Appearance==
Camille is a light tan colored camel with a light brown tuft of curly hair on her head. She has green eyes and a light colored muzzle. She wears a light orange collar with the T.O.T.S. logo on it and a diaper with light orange trim and cactus prints on it.

==Trivia==
*She is voiced by Charlie Townsend, who also voiced [[Mia (T.O.T.S.)|Mia the Kitten]] and [[Octivia|Octivia the Octopus]],
*She speaks in babbles and coos, but does say "Mama, Dada" like a lot of the other babies.
