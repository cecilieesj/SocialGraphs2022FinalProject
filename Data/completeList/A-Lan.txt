'''A-Lan''' is an alien from the ''[[Zombies]]'' franchise. A-Lan is one of the main characters in ''[[Zombies 3]]''. He is portrayed by [[Matt Cornett]].

==Background==
===Personality===
A-Lan is another member of the Alien trio, A-Lan enjoys competition, especially against the less intelligent and less athletic students at Seabrook High.

=== Physical Appearance ===
A-Lan is a male alien with short blue hair styled in a quiff, teal eyes and light skin. A-Lan wears a blue and silver jumpsuit, blue vest, Luma Lens bracelet and blue boots. Like all Aliens, A-Lan has silver markings on the side of his face.

==Trivia==

*He is the second Disney character that [[Matt Cornett]] has played followed by [[E.J. Caswell]] from the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''.
*He is similar to [[E.J. Caswell]] from the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''.
**Both E.J. and A-Lan are portrayed by [[Matt Cornett]].
**Both E.J. and A-Lan are friends with [[Meg Donnelly]]'s character. In ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]'', E.J. and [[Val]] ([[Meg Donnelly]]) are childhood friends. 
**Both E.J. and A-Lan are smart. 
**Both E.J. and A-Lan are competitive.
**Both E.J. and A-Lan are athletic.
**Both E.J. and A-Lan love to play sports.
