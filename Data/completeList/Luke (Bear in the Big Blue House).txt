'''Luke''' is a glasses-wearing mouse who attends [[Mouse School]] with [[Tutter]]. In "[[Tutter's First Big Sleepover Bash]]," Luke attended his very first sleepover, but may not have been quite ready. He missed his parents and needed a drink of water, a lullaby, a special pillow and a vaporizer and he likes Tutter but he also appears in a [[Bear in the Big Blue House]] episode called "[[A Strange Bird]]".
