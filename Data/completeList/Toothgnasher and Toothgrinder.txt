'''Toothgnasher and Toothgrinder''' are [[Marvel Comics]] characters that appear in ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]'' and ''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]''. They are [[Thor Odinson]]'s mystical asgardian goats.

They were both created by Steve Englehart and John Buscema.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]''====
Both Toothgnasher and Toothgrinder appear in the film, in supporting roles.

===[[Marvel Animation]]===
====''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''====
They helped him get [[Beta Ray Bill]] back to his ship, the Skuttlebutt, and later guided Thor back home after battle with a possessed [[Amora the Enchantress|Enchantress]].

==Trivia==
*Toothgnasher and Toothgrinder are based on [[Wikipedia:Tanngrisnir and Tanngnj贸str|Tanngrisnir and Tanngnj贸str]], the goats of Norse mythology that pull Thor's chariot.
