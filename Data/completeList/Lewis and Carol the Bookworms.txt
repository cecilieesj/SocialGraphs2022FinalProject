'''Lewis and Carol the Bookworms''' are major, supporting characters in ''[[Sing Me a Story with Belle]]''. They inhabit [[Belle]]'s bookshop. Lewis is the yellow worm, and Carol is the green one.

Lewis is voiced by [[Jim Cummings]] (also the voice of Big Book in the same program), and Carol is voiced by the late [[Christine Cavanaugh]].

==Trivia==
*Both worms were named after the famous British author Lewis Carroll, who wrote the original ''[[Alice in Wonderland]]'' book.
