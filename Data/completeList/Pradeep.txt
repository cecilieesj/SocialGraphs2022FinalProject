'''Pradeep''' is a minor character featured on the [[Disney XD]] series ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''. He is a student of [[Norrisville High]] and plays the french horn in the school marching band.

==Personality==

==Appearance==
Pradeep is a lanky Indian boy with long hair and tan skin. He originally had eyelashes, implying that the character was meant to be a female at first. When not in a band uniform Pradeep wears a teal shirt, with a long sleeve yellow shirt underneath, with grey pants. His shoes match his shirt colors.

==Trivia==
*Pradeep was suppose to be a female in "[[Got Stank?]]", having eyelashes. However, in "[[Bash Johnson: 11th Grade Ninja]]", it is revealed that he's a boy.
*In the episode "[[McFear Factor]]", it's revealed that Pradeep has a fear of spiders.
*In "[[Everybody Ninj-along]]", it's shown he also plays the bongo drums.
*In "[[Bash Johnson: 11th Grade Ninja]]", he mistakes Bash for the Ninja after he destanks.
