[[File:386672.jpg|thumb|Monotone Woman]]'''Monotone Woman''' is played by Amanda Tepe who has portrayed multiple characters in the first season of ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]''. She had one appearance in the second season as a hot dog vendor. Her name was Elaine in the first season finale, but its been Amanda in some of the others.
