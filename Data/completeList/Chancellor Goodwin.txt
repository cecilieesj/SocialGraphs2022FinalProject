[[File:Goodwin.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Chancellor Goodwin''' is one of the main antagonists in ''[[Return to Halloweentown]]''. She's the headmistress of [[Witch University]] and a member of [[The Dominion|the Dominion]], an evil organization who wants to rule [[Halloweentown (place)|Halloweentown]].
