'''Master Phineas''' is a minor character in the [[2015]] adaptation of ''[[Cinderella (2015 film)|Cinderella]]''. He is portrayed by Rob Brydon.

==Appearances==
===''[[Cinderella (2015 film)|Cinderella]]''===
In this version, Master Phineas is the royal painter who paints portraits of royalty. He only appears briefly during a bumbling attempt to paint [[Prince Charming|Kit]]'s portrait, which would then be shown to other royalty prior to the ball. After a few disagreements with the raising and lowering of his seat, Phineas ends up on the ground which he is then content to being a good angle for Kit.
