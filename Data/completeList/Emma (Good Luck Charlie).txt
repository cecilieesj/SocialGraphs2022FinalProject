'''Emma''' is a teenage girl that PJ met and developed a crush on in the second episode of the first season, and accidentally switched strollers, as they were identical, and PJ wound up taking home her baby brother and her leaving with [[Charlie Duncan|Charlie]].

== Personal life ==
She meets [[PJ Duncan]] in the park, and while talking, they accidentally swap babies. Fortunately, they were able to retrieve them back ("[[Baby Come Back]]"). [[Amy Duncan|Amy]] and [[Bob Duncan|Bob]] found out. Bob paid less attention to it.

== Relationships ==
=== PJ Duncan ===
PJ has a crush on Emma, a girl he met at the park, who gives him his phone number, indicating that ''she'' may like PJ back. Because of this, he accidentally swaps babies with her. Fortunately, he and his siblings were able to retrieve Charlie back.

PJ also got angry at Emma because he thought she gave him the wrong phone number, but Emma explains that he just got it wrong. PJ replies with an "Oh...". PJ asks what Emma is having, and Emma leaves ("Baby Come Back").

== Background Information ==
* Emma is PJ's first crush to appear in the series.
