'''Denzel Dugglemonster''' is [[Henry Hugglemonster (character)|Henry Hugglemonster]]'s best friend. He loves to dig and have fun with Henry.

==Appearance==
Denzel is a little taller than Henry. He is a gray monster. He is seven years old.

==Personality==
Denzel is a nice monster. He loves to dig and have a lot of fun with Henry and his other friends.
