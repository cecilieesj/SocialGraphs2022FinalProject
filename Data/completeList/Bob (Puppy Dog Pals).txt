'''Bob''' is a main character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]''. He is an inventor and the owner of [[Bingo (Puppy Dog Pals)|Bingo]], [[Rolly (Puppy Dog Pals)|Rolly]], [[Hissy]], [[A.R.F. (character)|A.R.F.]], and Olivia.
==Background==
===Personality===
Bob is a kind, caring, and generous man. Examples of this are how he makes various toys for Bingo and Rolly to play with while he is away at work, and when he gave his childhood toy Ruff Ruff to his young neighbor Chloe in the episode [[Design-A-Dog]]. He is also smart as he is capable of inventing various devices.
===Physical appearance===
Bob is tall, slender, has fair skin, red hair, and green eyes. He wears a green sweater vest and red bow tie over a white shirt along with blue jeans and orange and white sneakers.


==Trivia==
His voice actor is Harland Williams who also plays Carl from Meet The Robinsons and Captain Frost & Cecilia from Jake and the Never Land Pirates.
