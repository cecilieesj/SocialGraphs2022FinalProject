'''Alex Wilder''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in the Hulu series ''[[Runaways (TV series)|Runaways]]'', played by Rhenzy Feliz. He ran away from his home and formed the superhero team known as [[the Runaways]] alongside other teenagers who ran away from their supervillain parents, protecting the innocent while constantly running away from law enforcement. Alex uses his scientific skills and his high-tech inventions and gadgets to fight crime and the forces of evil. He is also a great leader whom the rest of the team can rely on.
