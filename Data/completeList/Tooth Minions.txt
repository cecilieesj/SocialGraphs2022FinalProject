'''Tooth Minions''' are white monsters that are made of teeth. They appear in ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==Physical Appearance==
They are completely white with yellow teeth and red eyes.

==Magical powers==
Anyone who uses the [[Tooth Fairy (American Dragon: Jake Long)|Tooth Fairy]]'s wand will be able to control these creatures, thus they have no true alliance to the Tooth Fairy herself. If they get destroyed, they will put themselves back together.
