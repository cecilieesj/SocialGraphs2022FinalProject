'''Mrs. Booby''' was a minor character from ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]''. She was the Rescue Rangers' client in the episode "[[Three Men and a Booby]]".

She happened upon the Rangers while they were shopping in the supermarket. She told the Rangers that her baby had been "egg-napped". The Rangers later reunited her with her baby.
