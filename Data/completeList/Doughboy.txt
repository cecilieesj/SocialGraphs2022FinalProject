Doughboy is the indestructible [[HYDRA]] creation of the mad scientist [[Arnim Zola]]. Capable of taking any hit or explosion it is a creature made of a dough like substance. Doughboy has appeared in two current Avengers series, in [[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]] they where created by Arnim Zola has a indestructible assistant. It was sent out to Avengers Mansion to destroy the Avengers. In [[Avengers Assemble]], Arnim Zola used a small group of them to prevent [[Scott Lang|Ant Man]], [[Hawkeye]] and [[Black Widow]] from rescuing [[Captain America]] on Dimension Z. They were destroyed by Extreme heat and lost signal to their master Arnim Zola.
