'''F.R.I.D.A.Y.''' is a character from [[Marvel Comics]] and first appeared in the film ''[[Avengers: Age of Ultron]]''. She is [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]]'s backup A.I. in case her predecessor [[J.A.R.V.I.S.]] is destroyed. F.R.I.D.A.Y. is programmed to serve the same functions as J.A.R.V.I.S. As such, she is the A.I. that control all the technology Tony uses, including the [[Iron Man (armor)|Iron Man armors]], and she runs Stark Mansion. She sees Tony as a father figure and is eager to help Tony in his time of need. F.R.I.D.A.Y. speaks with an Irish accent.

F.R.I.D.A.Y. was created by Mike Grell, Derek Fridolfs and Ryan Odagawa.
