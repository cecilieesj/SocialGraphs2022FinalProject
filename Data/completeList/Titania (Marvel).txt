'''Titania''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character.

Titania was created by Jim Shooter, John Beatty and Mike Zeck.

==Background==
===Official Description===
:''Titania is a narcissistic social media influencer who also happens to have superhuman strength. Her misguided effort to garner attention leads to an unfortunate confrontation with She-Hulk, kicking off an adversarial relationship that confounds Jennifer/She-Hulk while seemingly energizing Titania.''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://assets.gettyimages.com/bf-boulder-whitelabelbucket-getty-prod/4xxxgnn4m57prckqcfb9bn/v/1055462855/original/SHE%20HULK%20ATTORNEY%20AT%20LAW%20Production%20Brief%20FINAL%2008-08-22.pdf|title=She-Hulk: Attorney at Law Production Brief|website=|date=}}</ref>

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]''====
Whilst Titania doesn't physically appear in the series, an image of her can be seen in the post-credits of the [[Generation Why|first episode]], hung on the board, in the office of the [[Damage Control|Department of Damage Control]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedirect.com/article/ms-marvel-post-credits-scene-she-hulk-characters|title=Ms. Marvel's Post-Credits Scene Includes She-Hulk Characters We All Missed|website=The Direct|date=[[August 21]], [[2022]]}}</ref>

====''[[She-Hulk: Attorney at Law]]''====
Titania appears in the  [[Disney+]] series, portrayed by [[Jameela Jamil]].

===[[Marvel Animation]]===
====''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]''====
Titania is a super-powered criminal in a relationship with Absorbing Man. She is also and associate member of the Wrecking Crew The team consists of Wrecker, Bulldozer, Piledriver, Thunderball. Titania and Absorbing Man work with the Wrecking Crew to start a riot at the Vault. The Wrecking Crew is defeated by the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. Titania later appears as a member of the Leader's Agents of C.R.A.S.H., and once again battled She-Hulk, but was defeated. Even after their defeat, Titania and her teammates served their purpose of ruining the Hulks' reputation in the process. The Wrecking Crew are shown to be inmates of the Vault section that Abomination presides over. Titania was an inmate in the part of the Vault that Abomination presides over and received She-Hulk as a cellmate. When Abomination "purges" his part of the Vault by launching it into space with a gamma bomb to destroy it, Titania convinces Absorbing Man to help the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. disarm it. Following S.H.I.E.L.D. finding out the truth of Leader and the Agents of C.R.A.S.H. masterminding the destruction of Vista Verde and framing the Agents of S.M.A.S.H., Titania, Absorbing Man, and the Wrecking Crew are transferred to another prison facility.

====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====
She was being transferred in a S.H.I.E.L.D. convoy until Absorbing Man attacks to spring her out. She and Absorbing Man were defeated by Iron Man, Hawkeye, and Thor.
