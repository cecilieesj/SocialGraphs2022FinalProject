'''Iceman''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who is a superhero and a founding member of the [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]] along with [[Cyclops (Marvel)|Cyclops]] and [[Beast (Marvel)|Beast]]. He has the ability to manipulate ice and cold by freezing water vapor around him. This allows him to freeze objects, as well as turn his body into ice.

Iceman was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Television Appearances==
===''[[Marvel Disk Wars: The Avengers]]''===
The [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]] arrive in the Blackbird: Iceman, [[Storm]], and [[Colossus (X-Men)|Colossus]], as well as the DISK containing [[Beast (Marvel)|Beast.]] After Beast is attached to Jessica's wristband, he explains how it is possible to use Cerebro and the energy from Ōkuma's teleporter to follow him to [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]].
