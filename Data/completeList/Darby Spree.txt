Darby is the leprechaun king. He hails from northern [[Ireland]]. He was the only one of his kin who has avoided being captured by the witch [[Banshee (Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers)|Druella O'Midas]].

==Trivia==
*Darby is the second leprechaun king to appear in a Disney Afternoon series. The first was in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[Luck o' the Ducks]]" by the name of King Brian.
**Darby's overall design resembles Far Darrig from that same episode, but with a beard.
*Darby shares his first name with [[Darby O'Gill]] from the film, ''[[Darby O'Gill and the Little People]]''.
