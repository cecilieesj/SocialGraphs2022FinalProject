'''Margaret''' is a beautician introduced on ''[[Muppets Now]]''.

==Appearances==
===[[Muppets Now]]===
She appears in "[[Due Date]]" during the "Lifesty" segment to administer a face slapping massage. Beginning on Taye Diggs, Margaret delivers a series of gentle taps. Her method with [[Miss Piggy]] is more aggressive, which is met with a series of karate chops.
