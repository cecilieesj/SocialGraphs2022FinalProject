|friends = [[Mr. Smee]], [[Captain Hook]], [[Sharky and Bones]], [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], [[Cubby]], [[Skully]]}}'''Blinky''' is a minor supporting character in the ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]'', he's [[Mr. Smee]]'s pet chameleon.
==Role in the series==
Blinky first appeared in the episode "[[Mr. Smee's Pet|Mr. Smee's Pet".]] Smee left the little lizard in the care of [[Captain Hook]], [[Sharky and Bones|Sharky, and Bones]] while he prepared Hook his favorite dish: codfish casserole. Blinky decides to go ashore, sneaking off of the [[The Jolly Roger|Jolly Roger]] into the [[Neverland]] jungle under Hook and his crew's watch. Knowing that Smee would soon finishÂ cooking his meal, Hook, Sharky, and Bones head ashore to find the reptile. Hook and his crew join forces with [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]] and his crew to find Blinky, returning him back to the Jolly Roger. They get back just in time so that Smee never knew Blinky was missing.
