}}'''[[Minnie Mouse|Minnie's]] Scooter''' is a sentient vehicle and [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey's]] scooter's girlfriend that appears in the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode "[[Amore Motore]]".

==Role in the series==
As Mickey's scooter happily strolls across the streets of Rome, he suddenly sees Minnie's scooter and is attracted to her and as Mickey sees his thought of dating that scooter, Mickey decides to get on his scooter and catch up with her so that Mickey's scooter can get her attention.  Mickey's scooter tries to immitate the grocer in the marketplace to attract to which his plan was unsuccessful, due to Mickey's scooter tripping over a pigeon. Mickey's scooter does another attempt to get her attention at a nearby park where Mickey introduces Minnie's scooter to talk to him during a picnic to which the plan was not successful, causing Minnie to leave with her scooter.

However, as Mickey's scooter accidentally causes the columns to topple, Mickey and his scooter plan to save Minnie and her scooter to which  Mickey and Minnie hold on tight on their scooters, causing them to end up at the Fountain of Trevi. Minnie's scooter happily praises Mickey's scooter, just as she kisses him just before Mickey and his scooter celebrate their success in trying to get Mickey's scooter attracted to Minnie's scooter.

==Trivia==
*At one point in "Amore Motore", Minnie's scooter is seen reading a book with illustrations of Johnny Fedora and Annie Blue Bonnet from from ''[[Make Mine Music]]''.
