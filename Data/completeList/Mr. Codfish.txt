'''Mr. Codfish''' is a minor character of [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[1971]] musical hybrid film ''[[Bedknobs and Broomsticks]]''.

==Role in the film==
He lives on the seabed next to [[the Isle of Naboombu]]. He thinks that the surface dwellers on the isle have many problems. Mr. Codfish is the first character the protagonists meet when they arrive at the place. He then acts as the judge in the underwater dance contest.
