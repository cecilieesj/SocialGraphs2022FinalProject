==History==
Dawn has two friends who always follow her. She is vain, shallow, bad-tempered, selfish, and is the school's most popular girl. Dawn doesn't care about the others and only cares about herself. The Gym Shorts Kid is her biggest fan and often gives her his shorts as gift for respect. Dawn and Maggie are rivals.
