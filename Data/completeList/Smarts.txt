'''Smarts''' is the later half of the cartoon duo "Pitts and Smarts" in the animated show, ''[[Bonkers]]''. He is a Toon cat, who, while in character behaves like a ordinary house pet, is actually sophisticated, friendly and eloquent. His off-stage persona is based on Bing Crosby. He is voiced by [[Maurice LaMarche]].{{Bonkers}}
