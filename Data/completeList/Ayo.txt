'''Ayo''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Captain America: Civil War]]'', ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'', ''[[Avengers: Infinity War]]'', ''[[The Falcon and The Winter Soldier]]'' and ''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]''. She is portrayed by [[Florence Kasumba]]. 

Ayo is a member and the second-in-command of the [[Dora Milaje]], the elite team of enforcers tasked to protect [[Black Panther|T'Challa]] and [[Wakanda]]. She is kind, noble, brave, and loyal to the ruler of Wakanda. She is a skilled strategist and politician and an expert at hand-to-hand combat. 

Ayo was created by Al Ewing and Kenneth Rocafort.
