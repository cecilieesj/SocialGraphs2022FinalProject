|quote = }}'''Blood Spider''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appeared in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', voiced by [[Benjamin Diskin]]. He is a superhero from an alternate universe overrun by monsters. He has superhuman strength, speed, durability, the ability to stick to walls and ceilings, and a spider-sense, and he is armed with web-shooters. He can perfectly mimic the fighting style of every opponent he encounters. Spider-Man helps him in his quest to free his dimension from monsters by developing a way of turning monsters back into humans.
