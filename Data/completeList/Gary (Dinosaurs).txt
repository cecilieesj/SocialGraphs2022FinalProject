'''Gary''' is a one-time character from the TV show, ''[[Dinosaurs]]''. He is a Dilophosaurus approximately fifty feet in height who appeared only in the episode "High Noon". After meeting Fran Sinclair in the market and helping her carry her groceries, he becomes interested in her and decides to challenge [[Earl Sneed Sinclair|Earl]] for her affection.

==Trivia==
*Gary's face (briefly visible through a hole in the roof) is the same puppet used for [[Roy Hess]].
