'''T'Chaka''' was the original [[Black Panther]] and king of [[Wakanda]]. He is the father of [[Black Panther|T'Challa]] and [[Shuri]].

T'Chaka was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
T'Chaka was the ruler of Wakanda until one night, during a ceremony, [[M'Baku]] arrived and challenged him to a duel. His son, T'Challa advised not to accept, he instead decides to accept the challenge. T'Chaka shows his skills and in a couple of moves manages to defeat M'Baku. However, M'Baku stands up and uses Klaw's Sonic Weapon to confuse T'Chaka and beat him to death. Despite his physical death, he stills lives in the memories of his son.

===''[[Captain America: Civil War]]''===
After an incident, in Lagos a place in [[Wakanda]], T'Chaka called for a system to put in place to regulate the actions of the Avengers. However, during the United Nations world meeting with his son T'Challa despite his son's best efforts to save him, he died in a bomb that was set by [[Baron Zemo]].

===''[[Avengers Assemble]]''===

During World War II, T'Chaka teamed up with [[Captain America]] and the Allies to stop [[Red Skull]] and Hydra from invading Wakanda in a later invasion when [[Baron Zemo]] went after a powerful spiritual amulet of the Crocodile. It took T'Chaka and Baron Zemon to return the amulet to its sacred holding.

==Trivia==
*In the comics, T'Chaka was killed by [[Ulysses Klaw]].
