}}'''Mayor Jeff''' is the mayor of the community of Lake Hoohaw. It is unknown if his position is elected or appointed, though he is a generally well-liked figure. In addition to his mayoral duties, Mayor Jeff is an avid follower of history and also has great nautical prowess, owning his own submarine. In the episodeÂ "[[A Very Surprising Party]]", it's revealed he can play banjo.
Mayor Jeff is a generally sociable figure. Lake Hoohaw being the small community that it is, he is generally easily accessible and approachable, even to the kids, who sometimes ask him for advice.
