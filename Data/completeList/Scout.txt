}}'''Scout''' is a character in ''[[Fantasia: Music Evolved]]''.
==Background Information==
Scout is a former apprentice of [[Yen Sid]], and accidentally creates the villain of the 
game, "The Noise".

==Personality==
Scout is a friendly and enthusiast student. She yearns to solve problems on her own, and grows frustrated if she finds herself unable to complete a task by herself.
