==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
Bushmaster and the Serpent Society, led by [[King Cobra]], battled [[Avengers|the Avengers]] in a subway. Unfortunately, [[Henry Pym|Hank Pym]]'s pacifist decisions cause the Serpent Society to escape.

In the wake of the [[Skrull]] invasion, Bushmaster and the rest of the Serpent Society face [[Captain America]] and [[Spider-Man]] to free King Cobra and [[Viper]]. One of his cybernetic arms is torn by Spider-Man.
