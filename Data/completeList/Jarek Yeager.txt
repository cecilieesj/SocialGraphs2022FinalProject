'''Jarek Yeager''' is a veteran pilot who appears in the animated series ''[[Star Wars Resistance]]''.
He was once a pilot who fought in the rebellion against the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], and is an old friend of Commander [[Poe Dameron]]. Following the Empire's defeat, Yeager retired to run a repair shop on the [[Colossus (location)|Colossus]] refueling station. He enjoys the solitude of being far from the central systems of the galaxy. He is quiet, hardworking, and wise, having experienced much in his lifetime.

==Trivia==
*Scott Lawrence, who provided the voice of Jarek Yeager, previously voiced [[Darth Vader]] in ''[[Star Wars]]'' games from 1994 to 2006, at which point he was replaced by Matthew Sloan, who continues to voice Vader in all non-movie and television projects.
*His name is possibly a play on Eren Yeager, the protagonist from the anime ''Attack on Titan''.
