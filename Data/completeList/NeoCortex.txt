'''NeoCortex''' is a superhero on ''[[Mighty Med]]'' with the ability of Telepathy, the ability to read minds and communicate with them or even manipulate the minds. He was turned evil by [[Skylar Storm|Skylar]], but after [[The Annihilator]] was defeated he and the other superheroes were turned good again.
