'''Hina Kuluua''', the Goddess of Rain, is one of the Tiki Goddesses in the pre-show of [[Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room]].

==History==
===Background===
According to the sign near Hina Kuulua, it is said that there is no gloom for Hina's mists which makes orchids blooms and whenever she is happy, a magic rainbow would be brought to cherish each day.

==Appearances==

====[[Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room]]====
After [[Tangaroa-Ru]] begins her introduction, Hina Kuluua introduces herself to the guests as the Goddess of Rain. Tangaroa-Ru continues her introduction that she and Hina travel together through the tropics across the seven seasto which Hina tells guests to come closer of what magic may be seen in fantasy. After Hina finishes her sentence, [[Tangaroa]] begins his introduction.

===Other appearances===
Hina Kuluua also makes a cameo appearance in Episode 5 of ''[[It's a Small World: The Animated Series]]'', "Tropical Hideaway".
