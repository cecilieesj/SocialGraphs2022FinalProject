[[File:RobWingstan.jpg|thumb]]'''Rob''' is a minor character that made an appearance in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He's portrayed by Gabriel Suttle.

==History==
Not much is known about him. He's one of [[Bennett James|Doctor James']] patients, when he left with his mother, Bennett realized he stole his watch, hinting that he has this habit of taking things from others.
