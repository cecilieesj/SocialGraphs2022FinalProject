[[File:Bandicam_2013-07-26_18-51-45-355.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Layla''' is a character and antagonist of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Biography==
She is a member of the cheerleading squad, like the other cheerleaders, dislike and distrust Lindsay and [[Avery Jennings]] (but not as much). Her BFF are Tiff, and another cheerleader with raven hair.

==Physical Appearance==
Layla has brunette hair that held back a ponytail, and wears her blue and red cheerleading outfit
