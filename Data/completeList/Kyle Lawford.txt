'''Kyle Lawford'''(voiced by James Snyder) is a rich boy who dated [[London Tipton]]. His only appearance is in the episode, "Maddie Checks In".

==Story==
Kyle Lawford checked into The Tipton Hotel in the episode, "Maddie Checks In". He is fairly wealthy; the Tipton is a very expensive hotel. In the episode there was minimum details about Kyle's family. Additionally, Kyle seems like a nice person. He dated London Tipton for short time while [[Maddie Fitzpatrick]] dated Jason. Kyle is known to be best friends with [[Jason Harrington]], who's family is incredibly rich. Kyle is a fan of Usher, just as the rest of the group is.

==Appearance==
Kyle Lawford is of an average height. He has brown, spikey hair. Despite being rich, Kyle wears average yet trendy pants and t-shirts. When he goes out he wears better quality clothes.
