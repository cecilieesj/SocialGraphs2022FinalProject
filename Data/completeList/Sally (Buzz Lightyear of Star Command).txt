'''Sally''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney Television Animation|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated series ''[[Buzz Lightyear of Star Command]]''. She is a multi-armed waitress that works at Cosmo's Cosmic Diner. 

Sally is voiced by [[Kerri Kenney]], who also voices [[Gravitina]] in the series.

==Physical Appearance==
From an unknown species of aliens, Sally is a yellow-skinned woman with multiple arms (four of them) and four fingers on each hand, long, slender legs, and a nice physique. Her lips are a luscious pink and her eyelids match, bringing out the blue in her eyes. Her ears are vaguely cat-like in nature and she keeps her brown hair into a stylish ponytail.

==History==
Sally is only seen working at Cosmo's Cosmic Diner as the waitress and she has been in several episodes. In all of them, she is servicing Team Lightyear as well as the diner's other patrons.

==Personality==
Sally is a pleasant person to have as your waitress and fairly proficient at her job. She sometimes flirts with [[Buzz Lightyear]] and seems rather fond of Team Lightyear in general.

==Abilities==
Given she has four arms, she can carry multiple trays at once. She also has an impeccable memory and can recite regular customers' usual orders with ease.
