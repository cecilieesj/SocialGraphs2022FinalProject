'''Snook''' is a skunk who lives in [[Woodland Valley]] on the seriesÂ ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. He may be somewhat stinky, but that doesn't stop him from being a good friend to [[Bear (Bear in the Big Blue House)|Bear]] and the kids from the [[The Big Blue House|Big Blue House]]. He is very sensitive to being startled.

Snook appeared in the third season episode "[[w:c:muppet:Episode 312: Smellorama|Smellorama]]".

The puppet skunk was originally featured as [[w:c:muppet:Stinky the Skunk|Stinky the Skunk]], a regular character on ''[[w:c:muppet:The Animal Show|The Animal Show]]'' and returned as the [[w:c:muppet:Skunk (Sesame Street)|generic skunk]] on ''Sesame Street''.

"Snook" was later used as the name for the main character, a sloth, in another Mitchell Kriegman creation, ''It's a Big Big World''.
