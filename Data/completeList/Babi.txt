|family = Unnamed nephew}}'''Babi''' is a character who appears in ''[[Treasure Buddies]]''. He is a capuchin monkey who likes to steal valuables. He assists the Buddies on their expedition and helps [[Cammy]] find her mother.

==Trivia==
*Babi is one of the few Disney characters to refer {{WikipediaLink|Illeism|to himself in the third person}}, the others being [[Toadwart]] from ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears|Gummi Bears]]'', [[Gurgi]] from ''[[The Black Cauldron]]'', [[George of the Jungle (character)|the titular character]] from the ''[[George of the Jungle]]'' [[George of the Jungle 2|films]], [[Jumba Jookiba|Jumba]] and sometimes [[Stitch]] from the [[Lilo & Stitch (franchise)|''Lilo &amp; Stitch'' franchise]], and [[Shujaa]] from ''[[The Lion Guard]]''.
*It is unknown how he came to [[United States|America]] at the end of the film and got back to ancient Egypt.
