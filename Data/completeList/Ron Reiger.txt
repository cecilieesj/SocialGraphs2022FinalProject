'''Ronald "Ron" Reiger ''' is a secondary character from [[Disney Channel|Disney Channel's]] original series ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is voiced by [[James Arnold Taylor]].

==History==
The first time that he surfaced was in the episode "[[Gorilla Fist]]". According to Ron Stoppable, he regularly gets out of P.E., and stays home from school, as mentioned in that episode.

He appeared in this episode in "[[Homecoming Upset]]", when he waved at Bonnie. He also was using a computer in "Homecoming Upset", which is how the ballots were counted for Homecoming King and Queen.

In the series finale "[[Graduation]]", he was marching behind Bonnie and his cap got nailed by her and he was sitting next to [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]]. In "Graduation Part 2", he was pushed off the staged by [[Bonnie Rockwaller|Bonnie Rockwaller.]] When [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]] and [[Jim & Tim Possible]] used the gizmo to destroy Mr. Barkin's van, they were being chased by Mr. Barkin.
