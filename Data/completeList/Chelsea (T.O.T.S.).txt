Chelsea is an energetic chinchilla from the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode "[[Woodbird's Wish]]".
==Personality==
She is very fast and energetic. She had an exercise wheel specially built for her by Mr. Woodbird.

==Appearance==
Chelsea is a baby chinchilla with gray fur surrounding her body, along with light gray fur going from her mouth to the underside of her body. She has cerulean blue eyes, a pink, heart shaped nose, and a tuft of hair on the top. Her ears are also pink. She has a purple T.O.T.S. collar, and a purple trimmed diaper with a leaf pattern.
