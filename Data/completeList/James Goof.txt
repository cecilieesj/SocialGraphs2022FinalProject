}}'''James Goof''' is [[Goofy]]'s superhero alter ego, a parody of James Bond. 

==History==
James Goof made his debut on April 21, [[1987]]. The character was the star of a comic series series in [[France]] from April 1987 to May [[1997]]. Several titles of the stories were inspired by James Bond films.

He confronts [[Pete]] and his sidekick Lafouine repeatedly.

He is often dressed in a tuxedo and sometimes wears a long, thin mustache.

Moreover, this character may have inspired the creation of [[Double Duck]].
