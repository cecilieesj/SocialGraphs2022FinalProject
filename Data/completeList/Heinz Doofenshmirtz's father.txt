'''Heinz Doofenshmirtz's Father''' is the father of [[Roger Doofenshmirtz|Roger]] and [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz]] and the husband of his mother from the animated series ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. He usually appears in flashbacks in certain occasions in certain episodes.

==Background==
===Early Life===
His early life is unknown. It is possible that he was born in Drusselstein.

==Relationships==
===Mrs. Doofenshmirtz===
He really loves his wife.
===Heinz===
He is the main reason why Heinz became a evil scientist due to the fact he had no use in caring for him and is always neglecting him.
=== Only Son ===
He won him as a prize and decided to name the dog Only Son, because the dog was a son like he never had.
=== Roger===
He and Roger do not interact frequently, though the two get along.

==Trivia==

* Many of the viewers hate him and his wife because their mistreatment and negligence  towards Heinz.
*In "[[Father's Day]]", Heinz's father had brown beard and his hair became gray, and he was able to communicate with Heinz in English. In most of his appearances, he communicated with his son in German.
*His attire is almost similar to a Viking attire.
