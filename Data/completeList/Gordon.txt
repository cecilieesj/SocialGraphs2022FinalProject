'''Gordon''' is a minor character is the [[2012]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated film, ''[[Brave]]''.

==Role in the film==
He is seen along with [[Martin]] at the main door to the Great Hall of the castle, announcing the arrival of the Lords, until he gets slammed by the door before he could finish. He assists [[King Fergus]] into killing a bear in the castle, unaware it is [[Queen Elinor]] until they were stopped by [[Merida|Princess Merida]]. He also attempted to attack [[Mor'du]], but the giant bear proved to be too powerful for him. However, he survived like everyone else.

==Trivia==
*This is one of [[John Ratzenberger]]'s least recognizable [[Pixar]] roles because he speaks with a Scottish accent in the film.
