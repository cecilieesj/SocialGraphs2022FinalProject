The '''''Sugar Bees''''' are a group of scout girls featured in the [[Pickle and Peanut]] episode "[[Cookie Racket]]", where they serve as the main antagonists.
==History==
A couple of scout girls belonging to the group meet up with [[Pickle]] and [[Peanut]] and try to get them to buy some of their cookies. However, when the boys try to sale cookies made with Pickle's grandma recipe, the girls fight against the boys in order to defend their turf.
