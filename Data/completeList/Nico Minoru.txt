'''Nico Minoru''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in the Hulu series ''[[Runaways (TV series)|Runaways]]'', played by Lyrica Okano. She is the daughter of [[Tina Minoru]] and is a powerful sorceress like her mother. She is tough, intelligent, and independent. Her magic only activates once her blood has been shed. When Nico finds out that her mother is a supervillainess, she joins the team of superhero fugitives known as [[the Runaways]]. 

Nico Minoru was created by Brian K. Vaughan and Adrian Alphona.

==Trivia==
*Nico is confirmed bisexual, having dated [[Alex Wilder]] and her current girlfriend, [[Karolina Dean]].
