The "'''Manhugger'''" is one of the nine man-eating giants that live in Giant Country. He is very tall and slim with short, dark hair and wears a vest and shorts. Surprisingly, the Manhugger is the tallest giant of the gang, surpassing even the Fleshlumpeater in terms of height.

He along with the [[Fleshlumpeater]], [[Bloodbottler]], [[Meatdripper]], [[Gizzardgulper]], [[Maidmasher]], [[Bonecruncher]], [[Childchewer]], and [[Butcherboy|Butcher Boy]] enjoy snatching and gobbling up little children at night while [[The BFG (character)|The BFG]] delivers dreams to the children.
