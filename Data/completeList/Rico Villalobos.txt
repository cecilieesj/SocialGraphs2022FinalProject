'''Lt. Rico Villalobos''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series, ''[[Elena of Avalor]]''. 

==Role in the [[Elena of Avalor|series]]==
In his first appearance, he had a non-speaking role. He is usually shown with the rest of the royal guards. He makes major appearances in the episodes "[[The Curse of El Guapo]]" and "[[Olaball]]." He later appears in "[[A Spy in the Palace]]", where Carla as Rita tries to trick him into opening the door to the Royal Treasury.

==Trivia==
*In his major episodes, Rico is shown getting himself hurt.
*In "[[Changing of the Guard]]", he is implied to have two relatives named Rocco and Nacho. How they are related to him currently remains unknown.
