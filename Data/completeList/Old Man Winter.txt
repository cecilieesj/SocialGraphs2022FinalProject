'''Old Man Winter''' is a character from the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Goldie & Bear]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[Winterchime Day]]" and is a yearly resident of [[Fairy Tale Forest (Goldie & Bear)|Fairy Tale Forest]]. He uses a special snow globe that creates snow and apses with getting the perfect amount of snow.
