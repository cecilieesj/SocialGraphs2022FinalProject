'''Aneka''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character. She first appeared in the [[Marvel Animation|animated series]], ''[[Avengers Assemble]]'', debuting in the episode "Panther's Rage", voiced by [[Erica Luttrell]]. She made her live-action debut in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], and will appear in the upcoming, [[2022]] film, ''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]''. She is portrayed by Michaela Coel.<ref name="PK">{{Cite web|url=https://assets.gettyimages.com/bf-boulder-whitelabelbucket-getty-prod/8q7trq7fk9rbg3q558wm3jb/v/1068614892/original/BLACK%20PANTHER%20WAKANDA%20FOREVER%20press%20kit%20FINAL%2011-2-22.pdf|title=Black Panther: Wakanda Forever - Press Kit}}</ref>

Aneka was created by Jonathan Maberry, Reginald Hudlin and Will Conrad.

==Background==
Aneka is a high-ranking leader of the [[Dora Milaje]] who leads by example. Highly skilled in various fighting styles, Aneka has a bit of a rebellious streak. Aneka and Ayo are called to take on a bigger role in the Dora Milaje.<ref name="PK"/>
