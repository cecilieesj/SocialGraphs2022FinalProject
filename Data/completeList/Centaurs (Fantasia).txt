The '''Centaurs''' first appeared during the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" in ''[[Fantasia]]''. They were very handsome, dashing, charming and noble and debonair horse-like men of different colors who were in love with the female horse-like [[centaurettes]].

==Role in the film==
The centaurs are first seen by a river and a waterfall. They first met the centaurettes when the [[Cherubs|cherubs]] introduced them, they all get girlfriends, all excluding [[Brudus]], who is sitting under a tree. The centaurs later attend a wine festival with their girlfriends, [[Bacchus (Fantasia)|Bacchus]], [[Jacchus]], the [[Satyrs (Fantasia)|satyrs]], the cherubs, the [[Unicorns|unicorns]], and the zebra centaurettes. It wasn't until when [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]] came to interrupt the party, they had to hide in trees and in a cave, one Centaur is seen up the hill telling them to hide somewhere by blowing his horn before joining the others, the centaur later tells everyone to come out when the storm is over, finally they watch the sunset before they go to sleep.
