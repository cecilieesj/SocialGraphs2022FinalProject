With Lucky|parents=[[Lucky Piquel]] (father)<br>[[Dilandra Piquel]] (mother)}}'''Marilyn Piquel''' is [[Lucky Piquel]]'s child genius prodigy daughter. She is an aspiring artist as well as script/story writing and has deep connection to toons such as [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]], with her favorite toon being TV star [[Skunky Skunk]]. She is more than capable of taking care of herself and aids her father in a number of his cases often being the words of wisdom or source of knowledge to Lucky. Although looking like her mom [[Dilandra Piquel]] ("Dil"/"Dyl"), aside from her big round glasses and freckled face; she also shares her father's hair color. She's voiced by [[Sherry Lynn]].

==Trivia==
*Marilyn Piquel is based on the character of [[w:c:scoobydoo:Velma Dinkley|Velma Dinkley]] from ''[[w:c:scoobydoo:Scoobypedia|Scooby-Doo]]''.
*[[Gretchen]] from the [[Fireside Girls]] in ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]'' is also based on Marilyn.
