'''Agent B''' (voiced by [[Carolyn Seymour]]) is the mother of [[Agent K]], mother-in-law of [[Dick Daring]], and adoptive maternal grandmother of [[Todd Daring|Todd]] and [[Riley Daring]]. She is the headmistress of Royal Spy Academy and the wife of [[Agent G]].

She appeared in "[[London Calling]]" and "Dick Daring's All-Star Christmas Spectacular".
