'''Johnny Wang''' is a male Arachne who is seen in a story that [[Fu Dog]] talked about.

==History==
He serves as a minion of the [[The Dark Dragon|Dark Dragon]] and [[Luong Lao Shi|Lao Shi]], Fu, and [[Councilor Chang]] encountered him and they know he has secrets about the Dark Dragon. Johnny then decides to do a contest dance with Lao Shi. Whoever falls will lose. Fu and Lao Shi won, but Johnny and the multi-legged woman failed.

==Episode Appearances==
===Season One===
*[[Hong Kong Nights]] (only appearance)

==Trivia==
*Unlike [[Veronica (American Dragon: Jake Long)|Veronica]] and the other Arachnes, Johnny only has six legs instead of eight legs.
