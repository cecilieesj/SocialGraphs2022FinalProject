'''Hazel''' is an antagonist from the series, ''[[A.N.T. Farm]]''. She is an aggressive orphan who Fletcher and Cameron used to get into a Stinkle movie without looking like little kids in the episode Creative ConsultANT. She was shown to be very aggressive and threatened Cameron and Fletcher several times. At the end of the episode, she and the other orphans tackled Cameron, Fletcher and Lexi for holding out on Nummy Bears. Hazel returns in AmusemANT Park, still with her aggressive nature.
