[[File:Walter_(Good_Luck_Charlie).png|thumb]]'''Walter''' (portrayed by Doug Haley) is a geeky recruiting character who is multiple classes with [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]] and possibly [[Ivy Wentz|Ivy]]. He attends South High School and is the same grade as both Teddy and Ivy.
==Personality==
He is a quirky 17 year old. Not so much is known about him or his personal life
==Trivia==
*He played [[Bob Duncan|Bob]] in Pushing Button.
*He lost his gavel in his judge robe
