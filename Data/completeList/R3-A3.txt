'''R3-A3''' is an Astromech Droid who appears in the [[Disney XD]] animated series, ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''. He is a member of Iron Squadron who is somewhat of a temperamental and territorial droid. He is responsible for maintaining the systems aboard the [[Sato's Hammer]] and customize them to his own specifications.
