}}'''Mr. Pettibone''' is [[Goofy|Goofy's]] pet kitten who appeared in the short "[[Goofy's Big Kitty]]" and in ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]''.

==Appearances==
===[[Mickey Mouse Works]]===
Mr. Pettibone debuted in the short "[[Goofy's Big Kitty]]" where Goofy mistakes [[Louie the Mountain Lion]] for him.

===[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]===
Mr. Pettibone would go on to make recurring appearances in the CGI animated TV series. His first appearance was in the episode "[[Mickey Goes Fishing]]" where Goofy goes off on a safari and asks Mickey and Pluto to watch over him. Mickey agrees and when Pettibone gets hungry, they take him to Star Lake to fish for his favorite meal: Big Red Gooey Fish.
