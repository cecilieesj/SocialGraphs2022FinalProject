'''Hernan "Shades" Alvarez''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and supervillain who appears in ''[[Luke Cage (TV series)|Luke Cage]]''.

Shades was created by Archie Goodwin, Billy Graham and George Tuska.

==Background==
He is a ruthless mob enforcer and hitman who is undyingly loyal to [[Mariah Dillard]], and acts as Mariah Dillard's number one man and as [[Luke Cage]]'s archnemesis. His history with Luke found him jailed at Seagate Prison with Luke when he was still known as Carl Lucas, and he beat him up there. He was originally one of [[Diamondback]]'s henchmen who was placed with [[Cottonmouth]] until he began questioning Diamondback himself.

He is a master at hand-to-hand combat and an expert marksman.

==Trivia==
*He was born on [[May 11]], [[1977]] in Ponce, Puerto Rico.
