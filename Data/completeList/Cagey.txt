'''Cagey''' is a recurring character in ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]''. He is a friend of [[Bingo (Puppy Dog Pals)|Bingo]] and [[Rolly (Puppy Dog Pals)|Rolly]].

== Appearance ==
He is a small guinea pig.

== Personality ==
He is a playful character and always willing to help his friends.
