'''Dr. Droid'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398750/</ref> is an antagonist in the animated TV series ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|The Mighty Ducks]]''. He is voiced by [[Charlie Adler]].

==History==
He was formerly a human named Otto Maton, until he replaced most of his body parts with machinery, for no particular reasons. He wants to turn the rest of the world into robots. He expresses a strong hatred for anything and everything organic. The Ducks tangle with him twice in "[[MicroDucks]]" and "[[The Return of Dr. Droid]]".
