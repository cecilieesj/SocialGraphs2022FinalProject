|family = [[Artie Smarty]] (son)|status=Active}}'''Martin Smarty'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0085610/?ref_=tt_cl_t11</ref> is a minor character on the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is the owner of [[Smarty Mart]] and [[Club Banana]].

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
He has white skin, bald head brown eyes, black business suit, purple shirt, and a brown tie.

==Appearances==
===''[[Kim Possible]]''===
He has a rebellious son, [[Artie Smarty]], who seems to be a real brat, but is an excellent skateboarder and also likes animals (in particular, otters and [[Rufus (Kim Possible)|Rufus]]).

He is the hero of [[Ron Stoppable]], who has read his book three different times, without it ever being assigned.

In the episode "[[The Big Job]]", it is discovered that he is one of the richest five people on the planet. He was kidnapped, without knowing that he was kidnapped, along with the other 4 world's wealthiest people, when [[Se√±or Senior, Junior]] enlisted [[Shego]]'s help in order to pull off a successfully executed Evil Plot for his father's birthday, but was rescued by Ron and [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]].

He gave Ron a job in the Pet Department at Smarty Mart, located in [[Middleton]], as a reward for saving him.
