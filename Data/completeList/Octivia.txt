
[[File:Octivia.jpg|thumb]]'''Octivia''' is a baby octopus who appears in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode "[[A Splashy Delivery]]". She is delivered in a special crate full of water.

==Appearance==
She is a pink octopus with a light blue headband, purple eyes, a purple bow on the upper right side of the head and she wears a teal collar with T.O.T.S. logo and a white diaper with teal lining and T.O.T.S. symbols.

==Trivia==
*Her name is often misspelled as Octavia.
*Her voice actress is Charlie Townsend.
