'''Harold Hatchback''' is one of [[Pete|Pete's]] rival car dealers and the father of [[Coop Hatchback]] from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] 1992-1993 animated series, ''[[Goof Troop]].''




==Background==
He, along with [[Pete]], tries to get a celebrity to appear in one of his commercials. He appears in only one episode.

===Personality===
Harold appears to have good business skills, as he is quite successful as a car dealer. He also is very competitive, as he tries to include celebrities in his advertisments when he can.

===Physical appearance===
Harold is an averagely built anthropomorphic dog with red fur. He wears a striped green tank top, blue shorts with light blue stripes down the sides, and yellow flip-flops. When doing commercials, he wears a red tuxedo with a black bow-tie.

===Role in the series===
Harold's only appearance is in the episode "[[Buddy Building]]". He tries to out-do [[Pete]] by finding celebrities to appear in his car commercials. Meanwhile, his son Coop Hatchback bonds with Pete's son, [[P.J.]], and [[Max Goof|Max]].

==Trivia==
*Harold uses [[Biff Fuddled|Biff Fuddled's]] animation model.
*Harold's voice actor, Patrick Duffy, has a son who provided the voice for Harold's son.
