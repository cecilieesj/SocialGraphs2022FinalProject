The '''parrot''' is a wandering parrot who stumbles into [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey's]] [[Mickey's House|home]] one stormy night in the 1938 animated short, ''[[Mickey's Parrot]]''. He was voiced by [[Walt Disney]].
