|status=Active}}'''Rupert Brooks''' is a minor character from [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s live-action film, ''[[Snow Dogs]]''. He is [[Ted Brooks]]' foster cousin. He is portrayed by Sisqó. He can be very nosey, intruding in people's private conversations. He also is big believer in technology and comfort, as he got his cousin a temperature controller neck brace. In the end of the movie, he gets his secret wish. To be the most famous dentist in all of Miami.
