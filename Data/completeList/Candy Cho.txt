'''Candy Cho''' is the head cheerleader at John Hughes High School and appeared in the episode "[[Show It Up]]". She is portrayed by Charlet Chung.

==Personality==
She is shown to always get first place at anything [[Rocky Blue]] does while Rocky only gets second place. Rocky seems to have a strong dislike for Candy, only because Candy always has to one-up her in everything she does. She and her partner Randy also shown to be very rude toward everyone in their school, and has no shame in manipulating people (The Hessenheffers, Mr. Polk). She also makes fun of Gunther and Tinka to the point where Gunther gets really mad. Candy tricked [[Gunther Hessenheffer|Gunther]] and [[Tinka Hessenheffer|Tinka]] into thinking that they would perform with them at the talent show, so that Rocky, [[CeCe Jones|CeCe]], Gunther, and Tinka wouldn't win. She is very popular and her best friend is shown to be a fellow cheerleader named Randy.
