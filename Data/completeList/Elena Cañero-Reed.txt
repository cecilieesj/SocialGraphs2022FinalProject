'''Elena Cañero-Reed''' is the protagonist from the [[Disney+]] original series ''[[Diary of a Future President]]''. Portrayed by Tess Romero, Elena is a confident and strong-willed 12-year-old Cuban American girl with a desire to become a future president of the United States.
