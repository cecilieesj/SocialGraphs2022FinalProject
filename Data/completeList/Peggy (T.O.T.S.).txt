'''Peggy''' is a young polar bear who appears in the episode "[[Bringing Baby Back]]."

==Appearance==

Peggy is a white polar bear with blue eyes and a grey nose. She wears a purple scrunchy in her fur and a purple pair of overalls with a snowflake on the front pocket. She is voiced by Angelica Hale.
