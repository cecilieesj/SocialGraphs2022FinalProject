}}'''Asteroth'''<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Mighty-Ducks-The-Animated-Series/Asteroth/</ref> is an evil wizard from the ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]'' TV series. He is from another dimension, which is actually a supernatural version of [[Anaheim]], [[California]]. His title is "Lord of the Red Dragon", since he can morph into a large red dragon when enough power is in his possession. The Ducks fight him twice: first in the alternate Anaheim, and then in the "real" Anaheim.
