'''Franky''' is a character from the online game ''[[Club Penguin]]''. He is the latest member to join the [[Penguin Band]]. He plays the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Acoustic_Guitar acoustic guitar] and the banjo. He is the author of the book ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Franky%27s_First_Show Franky's First Show]'', which you can read in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Book_Room Book Room]'s [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Library Library]. He is not an original member of the Penguin Band.

==Trivia==
*If he weren't a member of the band, he would still be working in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Pet_Shop Pet Shop]
*Puffles love him.
*Due to his shoes being available at the Stadium in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Snow_and_Sports Snow and Sports] catalog, Penguins can now pretend to be Franky, provided they have a cowboy hat and a guitar.
*Franky is the most famous penguin in the band.
*He is an [[Elite Penguin Force|EPF]] agent.
*He likes chocolate as he said in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Music_Jam_2011 Music Jam 2011].
