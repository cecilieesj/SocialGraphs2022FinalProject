'''Soggy Nell''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Dog Dog] and a shopkeeper toon who owns [http://toontown.wikia.com/index.php?title=Melted_Ice_Cubes&action=edit&redlink=1 Melted Ice Cubes] on [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Walrus_Way Walrus Way], in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/The_Brrrgh The Brrrgh].

==SOS Toon==
﻿Soggy Nell is also an [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon]. She is a 4-star Toons Hit. When called upon, she will use [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Pixie_Dust Pixie Dust] on all toons in battle and guarantees them the ability to hit cogs, no matter what accuracy the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Gag Gag] is.

''Toons Hit ''lasts 1 round.
