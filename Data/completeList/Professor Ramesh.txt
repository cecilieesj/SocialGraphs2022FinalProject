|quote = }}'''Professor Ramesh''' is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is voiced by [[Brian George]].

==History==
Professor Ramesh is [[Dr. James Possible|James Possible]]'s old college buddy. Like his other friend, [[Bob Chen]], Professor Ramesh majored in astronomy. They both work at the Mount Middleton Observatory. Professor Ramesh has crossed paths twice with [[Dr. Drakken]]. The first was when Drakken kidnapped him. The second involved a plot to turn the world's top scientists into idiots by using a brain control hat.
