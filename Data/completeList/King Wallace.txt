'''King Wallace the First''' is the first king of Rodigan and the ancestor of King Wallace II and his son [[Prince Wally]]. He appeared in in the flashback in the Season 1 episode "[[Royal Pain (episode)|Royal Pain]]".

King Wallace told [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]] about his ancestor ruling the kingdom with cruel arrogance. In the story, a man begged the king that his whole flock ran away and he needed a few days to pay his taxes, but the king sent him to the tower. He was disliked by his subjects, and even his own knights.
