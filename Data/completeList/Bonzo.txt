'''Bonzo''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney Channel]] movies ''[[Zombies (film)|Zombies]]'', ''[[Zombies 2]]'', and ''[[Zombies 3]]''. He is a kind hearted zombie and the boyfriend of [[Bree]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Bonzo is the sweetest and most kind zombie in Seabrook and Zombietown. He is creative and willing to help anyone regardless of who they are and where they came from. While it might appear that he is oblivious to the negativity and prejudice aimed at him and his fellow zombies, he is very much aware and does not care, preferring to act kind in order to make progress. He is only capable of speaking zombie, yet most people seem to understand what he is saying. He has a big heart and shows is not afraid to show his skills.

===Physical appearance===
Like his fellow zombies, Bonzo is pale white with dark green hair which is long. He wears trashy greyish-brown clothes, a turquoise green jumpsuit underneath and black boots.

=== Powers & Abilities ===
As a zombie Bonzo possess many special powers from his zombie bloodline. Therefore, Bonzo is capable of the following:

* '''Super speed''': This grants him speed.
* '''Super Strength''': Has twice the strength of a human.
