'''Jenna''' was a minor character on [[I Didn't Do It]]. She was portrayed by [[Cameron Inman]].


==History==
She has appeared in Lindy Nose Best, shown to be a crush of [[Logan Watson]]. The went on a date at the end of the episode just as [[Jasmine Kang|Jasmine]] was about to ask Logan out. Its unknown what happened after Jenna's date with Logan, but its assumed at some point things didn't work out somehow since she is never seen or mentioned again.
