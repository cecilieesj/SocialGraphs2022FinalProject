'''Joseph "Robbie" Robertson''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]'', voiced by [[Ernie Hudson]].

Robbie Robertson was created by [[Stan Lee]], John Romita Sr. and Mike Esposito.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
Robbie was seen at the Daily Bugle when [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]] visited [[J. Jonah Jameson]].

===''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]''===
Robbie first appeared in "[[Screwball Live]]", where he was holding a press conference for the construction of the New Media Building while not mentioning things under orders of his publisher which is disrupted by Screwball as part of a prank on Spider-Man that involved the use of a Worker-Bot that was loaned to the construction site by Oscorp.
