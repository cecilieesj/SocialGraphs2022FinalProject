'''Snitch''' is a cartoon rat who works in [[Hollywood]]'s junkyard and is [[Miranda Wright|Miranda]] and [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonker]]'s friend who helps them sort out clues from garbage and other things people had left behind. 

==History==
He is a friendly and helpful cartoon rat who became friends with Bonkers and Miranda during a case and shows a passion for his job, calling himself a garbage scientist rather than a junkman and knows all the ins and outs about his research in his career.
