The '''Horned Bullfrog Family''' were cannibals that appeared in the ''[[Amphibia]]''  episode "[[A Night at the Inn]]".

== Role in series ==

==Trivia==
*Bullfrogs in real life actually do eat other frogs.
*It is unknown if Juliet and Juniper are related to either Teddy or Martha.
*Teddy is the only male member of the family.
