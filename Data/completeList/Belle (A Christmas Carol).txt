==Appearances==

===[[A Christmas Carol|''A Christmas Carol'']]===
During Scrooge's visit with [[The Ghost of Christmas Past]], it is shown that Scrooge first meets Belle at [[Fezziwig]]'s party. Throughout the whole dance they shared, they felt a spark and get engaged. However, at another Christmas, Belle calls off the engagement following Scrooge's obsession with moneylending and finances. She is voiced by [[Robin Wright Penn]], who also voiced Scrooge's sister [[Fan]].
===[[The Muppet Christmas Carol|''The Muppet Christmas Carol'']]===
A friend of the [[Fozzie Bear|Fozziwig family]], Belle first meets the young Ebenezer at the annual Christmas party. The two fall in love and become engaged. However, the relationship is ruptured by Scrooge's increased focus on business, as the wedding date is continually put off. Belle leaves Scrooge, explaining her reasoning through the song "[[When Love is Gone]]".
