'''Vanna''' is a new character who appears in ''[[Zombies: Addison's Moonstone Mystery]]''. She is a teen vampire. The characters see her for the first time in the Seabrook High cafeteria.

<br />
==Appearance==
Vanna has dark hair with small streaks of red on the sides. Her eyes are lined with purple eyeliner and she wears a pendant around her neck. She wears a leather jacket and her eyes are dark and violet.
==Personality==
Vanna can come off as sweet, but, as revealed in the first season, she has a dark and fierce side. When she doesn't feel safe, or is embarrassed, she can be moody.
==Trivia==

*Unlike typical vampires, she may not be affected by the sun as evidenced by her being at the football field outside of school. 
*She caused an earthquake to happen.
*She can hypnotize others and calls it "Vanna-tizing".
**Somehow, she is not able to hypnotize Addison.
*Vanna has a crystal on her chest which protects her from sunlight and it's been in her family for hundreds of years.
*Vanna was woken by the comet, but her family was not.
*Vanna stated she is not a fan of people.
*Vanna reveals that she Vanna-tized Addison's friends so she could find her lost family.
*She can turn into a bat which is what vampires do.
*She really cares about her family and did anything in her power to awaken them from sleep.
*At the end of Season 1, her and her family fly away into the night sky.
