==Background==
Morovich is the leader of the K.G.B. pursuit squadron, the Prognovieh who is assigned by Krokov to return [[Natalia Rambova]] to him as she is trying to defect to the U.S.A. He is known the be fearsome, ruthless and known to have a glass eye which he sometimes covers with an eye patch.

His first attempts to capture her fail, since she's being escorted by [[Woodrow Wilkins]] a comic book writer.

He succeeds in his third attempt and nearly kills Woody and Harry in the process. When Woody and Harry rescue Natalia he sends is squadron after them as they attempt to escape by boat. He attempts to stop them from escaping but instead ends up crashing to a cliff after Krokov chickens out.
