|status = Active}}'''Ripcord McQuack''' is the father of [[Launchpad McQuack]]. He, [[Birdie McQuack|his wife]], and [[Loopey McQuack|daughter]] make up a team of stunt-pilots called the ''Flying McQuacks. ''It appears they make their living traveling to different air shows, preforming stunts. He makes his first appearance in ''[[DuckTales]]'' when the ''The Flying McQuacks ''are coming to [[Duckburg]] for an air show, where Launchpad is flying an experimental jet. He was voiced by [[Robert Ridgely]].

==Appearances==
===''[[DuckTales]]''===
Ripcord appears in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[Top Duck]]". He and the rest of the McQuack family save [[Scrooge McDuck]]'s money bin, from the [[Beagle Boys]]. He later attended Scrooge's wedding in the season one finale "[[Till Nephews Do Us Part]]".

===''[[Darkwing Duck]]''===
Ripcord makes a cameo appearance in the ''[[Darkwing Duck]]'' episode "[[U.F.Foe]]", during Launchpad's flashback.
