'''The Great Escapo''' is a prisoner from [[Gulag 38B]] in the [[2014]] film, ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''. He was played by [[Tom Hiddleston]]. He tries to escape the gulag during "The Annual Gulag Revue Show" but was stopped by [[Nadya]] and her taser.
