'''Mr. Bannister''' is a minor character from ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''. He's an English teacher at [[Norrisville High]].

==Appearance==

==Trivia==
*He has only appeared in "[[Last Stall on the Left]]", "[[Sorcerer in Love]]" and [[To Smell and Back]] with a speaking role.
**He also appears without speaking in "[[Stanks Like Teen Spirit]]".
*He's somewhat of a freestyle teacher as he accepts Randy's ripped-up essay, calling it a good use of metaphor, as an acceptable B grade.
*He is shown in the [[What the Juice?|opening theme]] speaking to [[S. Ward Smith]].
*In "[[Shloomp! There It Is!]]", it was revealed that Randy and Howard both have him for 1st Period.
*He is the first teacher to appear in the show.
*His name was revealed in Sorcerer in Love, when Howard Rand dashed out of the class saying "No time Mr. Bannister!".
*He gave Bash Johnson his first "B" at Norrisville High.
