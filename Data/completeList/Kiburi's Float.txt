|fate = }}'''Kiburi's Float''' is a group of [[Crocodiles (The Lion King)|crocodiles]] that appear in ''[[The Lion Guard]]''.



== History ==

=== [[Let Sleeping Crocs Lie]] ===
After [[Kiburi]] challenges [[Makuu]] to a <span>Mashindano, [[Scar]] instructs [[Ushari]] to inform Kiburi of a plot to ambush his nephew, [[Simba]]. </span>

Whilst Kiburi fights Makuu, his three followers surround Simba, but are noticed by [[Ono]], who informs [[Kion]]. Kion and the [[Lion Guard]] stop the plot, and Simba realizes the situation.

Makuu defeats Kiburi, and his plot is exposed. After Makuu banishes the group from [[Makuu's Float|his float]], Simba exiles them from the [[Pride Lands]].

Once in the [[Outlands]], the group is approached by Ushari, who brings them to Scar.

=== [[The Little Guy (The Lion Guard)|The Little Guy]] ===
When [[Hodari]] wants to become a crocodile Kiburi welcomes him into his float. Kiburi thinks that Hodari will get them passed the Lion Guard. However his plan fails and Hodari joins Makuu's side.

=== [[The Scorpion's Sting]] ===
Kiburi and his float help out with Scar's newest plan. They trap the Lion Guard with the rest of [[Army of Scar|Scar's Army]]. However Kion uses the [[Roar of the Elders]] and escapes.

=== [[The Kilio Valley Fire]] ===
Kiburi's Float helps Scar's Army take over Kilio Valley. 

=== [[The Zebra Mastermind]] ===
While Kiburi is at a big meeting with Scar, [[Janja]] and [[Reirei]] [[Tamka]] and [[Nduli]] talk to [[Thurston]] the zebra. 

=== [[The Hyena Resistance]] ===
Kiburi and his float attack [[Jasiri's Clan]].

=== [[Beshte and the Beast]] ===
Kiburi and his float help attack Big Springs. They get defeated by a giant Gorilla named [[Shujaa]].
