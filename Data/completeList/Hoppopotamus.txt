|affiliations = Wuzzles}}'''Hoppopotamus''' or Simply '''Hoppo''' is one of the main characters of the 1985 disney show [[The Wuzzles]], [[Jo Anne Worley]] voices her.
==General Description==
===Physical Appearance===
'''Hoppopotamus''' is half rabbit, half hippopotamus. She is called '''Hoppo''' by her friends. She is a Wuzzle who is a hippopotamus with a rabbit's ears, buck teeth and a fluffy tail. She has blue fur with a purple tummy, and she loves to sing and act.
===Personality===
She is a pushy demanding diva, but she can be sweet. However, when toughness is called for (particularly in dealing with the regular villain, [[Crocosaurus|Crock]]), she is the toughest Wuzzle of all. She has a crush on [[Bumblelion]], but he has his heart set on [[Butterbear]].
