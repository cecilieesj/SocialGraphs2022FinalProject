==History==
She is friends with [[Bonnie Rockwaller]], [[Tara]], [[Hope]], and the other girls.

Bonnie notes in ''So the Drama'' that she is dating [[Steve Farley]] the baseball captain.

==Appearance==
Jessica is blue-eyed with straight blonde hair and light freckles. She is taller than the rest of the cheer squad.

When not in her cheer outfit, her street clothes are comprised of an orange blouse with an orange and brown plaid skirt.
