'''Neeku Vozo''' was a Kadas'sa'Nikto mechanic who appears in the animated series ''[[Star Wars Resistance]]''. He is best friends with [[Kazuda Xiono]].

==Background==
Neeku is a trustworthy and kind-hearted member of the [[Colossus (location)|Colossus]] community. He speaks honestly and takes everyone quite literally, sometimes making what he has to say unintentionally hilarious. But despite his quirks, Neeku's logical way of thinking makes him one of the best mechanics on the platform.
