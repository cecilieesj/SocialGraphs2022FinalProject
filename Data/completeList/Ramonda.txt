'''Queen Ramonda''' is a character from [[Marvel Comics]] featured in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], first debuting in the [[2018]] film ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'', and subsequently appearing in ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]'' and ''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]''. She is portrayed by [[Angela Bassett]].

Ramonda is the queen of [[Wakanda]]. She is also [[T'Chaka]]'s widow and the mother of [[Black Panther|T'Challa]] and [[Shuri]].

Queen Ramonda was created by Don McGregor, Gene Colan, and Tom Palmer.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]''====
Ramonda appears in the film.

====''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]''====
In 2024, Ramonda learns of T'Challa subduing to an unspecified illness. Despite Shuri's best attempts to create the Heart-Shaped Herb to cure her brother, T'Challa subdues to the illness and dies, leaving Ramonda to inform Shuri of his death.

===[[Marvel Animation]]===
====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====
Whilst Ramonda doesn't make an appearance, she is mentioned in the episode "[[The Panther and the Wolf]]".
