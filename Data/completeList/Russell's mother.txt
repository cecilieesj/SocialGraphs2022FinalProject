'''Russell's mother''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]]'s [[2009]] animated feature film, ''[[Up]]''.

==Role in the film==
She only appears at the end of the film during the Wilderness Explorer ceremony with [[Dug]], celebrating on [[Russell]] getting his "Assisting the Elderly" Wilderness merit badge. She had a husband, but they were divorced.
