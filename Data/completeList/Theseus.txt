He was voiced by Eric Stoltz.

==Role==
After the Minotaur escapes from the Labyrinth, he helps Hercules face it. Theseus has a double identity, his second self being the superhero "Grim Avenger", whose costume resembles that of DC Comics' character [[wikipedia:Doctor Fate|Doctor Fate]], though his personality and back story are more inspired by [[wikipedia:Batman|Batman]]. As the Grim Avenger, Theseus is constantly narrating his every move aloud. He was once one of [[Philoctetes|Phil]]'s former students as well as mentioned in [[Hercules|the movie]].

== Trivia ==
* In the original myths Theseus was the son of [[Poseidon]] and Aethra, wife of Aegeus King of Attica.
* It is unknown if Theseus was later killed by the Minotaur.
