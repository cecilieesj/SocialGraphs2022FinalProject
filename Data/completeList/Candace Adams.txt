'''Candace Adams''' is a character from ''[[K.C. Undercover]]''. She is the main antagonist of her debut episode "[[Enemy of the State (K.C. Undercover)|Enemy of the State]]". 

== History==
Candace is an evil spy who got Kira Cooper framed for being a rogue of state. She posed as Maggie Summers so she can trick the Coopers and frame Kira so that she would end up in jail forever as revenge on her for saying she wasn't ready to become a spy. At the end of the episode she was captured after a fight with K.C. and later arrested.
