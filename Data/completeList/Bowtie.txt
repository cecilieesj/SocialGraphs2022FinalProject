He was one of the Robo-Penguins stationed on [[Tempestoro]], designed and programmed to collect weather data for the Tomorrowland Transit Authority, until he befriended Mirandos and at some point became her pet. Bowtie gets his name from wearing the blue bow tie on his chest.
