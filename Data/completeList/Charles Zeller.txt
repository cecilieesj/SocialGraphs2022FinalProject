|quote = |status=Active|parents=Unnamed Mother|siblings=Unnamed Older Brother|partner=Trent (ex-boyfriend)}}'''Dr. Charles Zeller M.D.''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Doogie Kameāloha, M.D.]]''. He is a medical doctor at Oahu Health Medical Center.

==Background==

===Personality===
Charles Zeller is from Chicago who is doing his residency on the island of Oahu. Much of his insight into island life comes from his friend, a fellow resident at the hospital, Noelani Nakayama, who helps the fun-loving Charles adapt to his new home.<ref>[https://d23.com/meet-the-characters-of-doogie-kamealoha-m-d/ Meet the Characters of Doogie Kamealoha, M.D. - D23]</ref>

==Biography==
===Early Life===
Little is known about Charles' early history, other than at some point he became a doctor at the Oahu Medical Center. Here, he met Lahela and became good friends with her, but is often seen at Noelani's side and the two are very close.

===Season One===
====Aiding Lahela====
Charles is often seen aiding Lahela whenever she needs help, such as with cases or with her love life. When Lahela begins doubting her feelings for Walter due to his behavior, he is the first to suggest that she give him a chance rather than follow Noelani's advice and give up on him.
