'''Cut Lawquane''' is a clone trooper of the Grand Army of the Republic. He would become a deserter to the Republic after the Battle of Genosis where the transport he was in gunned down by a Separatist gunship but he escaped in time, knowing he will not survive with his wounded brothers. After that, Cut met and fell in love with a female Twi'lek named Suu. He would soon become the adoptive father of Suu's children, Shaeeah and Jek.
