==Role in the episode==
Ka-Hoo-Fu is a mummy and an old friend of the king spirit, Garbled One. He is seen awakening from his casket by a spell from the evil priest [[Sarkus]], who command him destroy [[Donald Duck|Donald]]'s boat. After the Garbled One releases Ka-Hoo-Fu, he is seen flying away with him forever in eternal silence, which it leads the treacherous Sarkus without a minion to do his evil dirty works and got Sarkus arrested.
