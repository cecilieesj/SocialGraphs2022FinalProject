'''Lop''' is an Lepi rabbit-like humanoid girl from the episode "''[[Lop & Ochō]]"'' of ''[[Star Wars: Visions]]''.<ref>https://www.starwars.com/news/anime-expo-lite-star-wars-visions</ref>

==Background==
===Personality===
While being adopted by Ochō and Yasaburō, Lop can be highly empathetic when the two have themselves on opposite sites of the Tao rebellion. 

==Trivia==
*She is heavily inspired by Jaxxon by her species known as Lepi.
*Lop's English Dubbed voice, Anna Cathcart who played as the daughter of [[Drizella Tremaine]], [[Dizzy Tremaine]] from ''[[Descendants 2]]''.
