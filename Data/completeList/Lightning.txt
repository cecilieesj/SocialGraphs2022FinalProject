|status = Active}}'''Lightning''' is a mule in Disney's [[1960]] film, ''[[Swiss Family Robinson]]''.

==Role in the film==
Lightning is one of many farm animals found on the [[Ship (Swiss Family Robinson)|ship]] that the Robinson family sailed on.

Upon being brought to the island, Lightning is used to help the Robinsons build their [[Swiss Family Treehouse (Swiss Family Robinson)|treehouse]].

On the day of the Robinsons' new holiday, Lightning is one of the animals who participate in the race, with [[Fritz Robinson (Swiss Family Robinson)|Fritz]] riding him, along with [[Duke and Turk|Turk]] (ridden by Francis' monkey), [[Rocky (Swiss Family Robinson)|Rocky]] the elephant (ridden by [[Francis Robinson|Francis]]), [[Clementine]] the ostrich (ridden by [[Ernst Robinson|Ernst]]), and [[Duchess (Swiss Family Robinson)|Duchess]] the zebra (ridden by [[Roberta]]).
