[[File:Bushy.png|thumb]]Bushy is a male baby bunny who first appears in [[Bunny Bonanza]].

==Appearance==
Bushy is a light blue bunny with blue eyes and a pink nose.He has a fluffy tail. He has white hair. He wears a light red collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol on it and a white diaper with purple trim and patterned perennial leaves on it{{T.O.T.S.}}
