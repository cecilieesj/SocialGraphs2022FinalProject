'''Annette''' is an armadillo who appeared in the fourth season of ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. She was performed by [[Vicki Kenderes-Eibner]].

She works at the [[Woodland Valley Cinema]] and is extremely shy, to the point that she hides at random moments during contact with other people.

The puppet was first used as [[w:c:muppet:Dooley the Armadillo|Dooley the Armadillo]] in ''[[w:c:muppet:The Animal Show|The Animal Show]]'', and was also used for [[w:c:muppet:Joe the Armadillo|Joe the Armadillo]] on the ''[[w:c:muppet:Kermit's Swamp Years|Kermit's Swamp Years]]'' DVD, performed by [[w:c:muppet:Joey Mazzarino| Joey Mazzarino]]. The puppet also appeared in some ''[[w:c:muppet:Puppet Up|Puppet Up]]'' shows.
