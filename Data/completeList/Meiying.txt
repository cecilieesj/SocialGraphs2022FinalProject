'''Meiying''' (美莹; Měi Yíng) is a character from the [[China|Chinese]] animated series ''[[Stitch & Ai]]'', a spin-off of the [[Lilo & Stitch (franchise)|''Lilo &amp; Stitch'' franchise]]. She is [[Wang Ai Ling]]'s rival in the series.
She serves as this series' counterpart to [[Mertle Edmonds]], [[Penny (Stitch!)|Piko/Penny]], and [[Jessica (Stitch!)|Reika/Jessica]]. However, unlike Mertle and her hatred of [[Lilo Pelekai]], Meiying does not completely dislike Ai and is slightly friendlier towards her in comparison.
