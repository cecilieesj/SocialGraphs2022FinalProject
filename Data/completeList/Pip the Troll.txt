'''Pip Gofern''', also known as '''Pip the Troll''', is a [[Marvel Comics]] character, who appeared in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], in the post-credits scene of ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by [[Patton Oswalt]]. He is a creation and friend of [[Starfox]].

Pip was created by Jim Starlin.

==Appearances==
===''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]''===
Pip makes his first appearance in the post-credits scene of the Eternals. In late 2023, Pip and [[Starfox]] learn of Arishem's abduction of the majority of the surviving Eternals on Earth, following their saving of the planet by preventing the Celestial Emergence. Pip transported to the Eternal starship Domo where Starfox makes his dramatic entrance as he lists his various epithets.

==Trivia==
*In the comics, Pip Gofern was a Laxidazian nobleman-turned-troll who helped [[Adam Warlock]] to defeat his evil counterpart, known as the [[w:c:marvel:Magus|Magus]].
*Pip the Troll is [[Patton Oswalt]]'s sixth character in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], the first five characters are from [[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]].
