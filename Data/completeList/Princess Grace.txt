'''Princess Grace''' of [[Walla-Walla-Bing-Bang]] is a white swan from the TV show ''[[TaleSpin]]''. She is one of the chief characters of the episode "[[Waiders of the Wost Tweasure]]". She is close friends with [[Airplane Jane]].

Grace had summoned Airplane Jane on a matter of great importance. She was to be crowned queen the following day, but her rule had been challenged by [[Prince Rudolf]]. To rule the kingdom, one needed to wear the fabled [[The Ruby Wings|Ruby Wings]] of Queen Maureen.

After [[Baloo#TaleSpin|Baloo]] and Jane had outwitted Rudolf and recovered the Wings, Grace was crowned as the new queen of Walla-Walla-Bing-Bang.
