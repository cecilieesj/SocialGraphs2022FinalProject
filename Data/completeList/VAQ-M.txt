|voice = [[Carlos Alazraqui]]}}'''VAQ-M '''is a character in [https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Disney Disney]/[https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Pixar Pixar]'s 2008 animated feature film, [https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/WALL-E WALL-E].
==''WALL•E''==
Two VAQ-M units were seen in the movie. One was cleaning [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/EVE EVE] probes with [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/M-O M-O], [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/SPR-A SPR-A] and [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/BUF-4 BUF-4].The second is a reject bot, who is able to function more or less as normal, but appears to have a cold-like malfunction that results in him sneezing with enough force to blast him several yards backwards. This comes in handy when battling the [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/SECUR-T SECUR-T] bots.
==Other Appearances==
*VAQ-M also appears in the [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/WALL%E2%80%A2E:_The_Video_Game video game] where he sneezes junk out for [https://pixar.fandom.com/wiki/WALL%E2%80%A2E_(character) WALL•E ] to turn into cubes.
==Trivia==
*His name is a pun on the word "vacuum".
