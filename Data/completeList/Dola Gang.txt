The '''Dola Gang''' is a group of air pirates led by [[Captain Dola]]. They try to chase Sheeta and Pazu, but later end up teaming up with them. At first, they are the antagonists, but soon show that their nice side, most of them falling for Sheeta.

==Notable Members==
===Louis===
Louis is one of the gang and sons of Dola. Louis was voiced by Takumi Kamiyama, Colin Phillips, and Mandy Patinkin. Louis has brown beard and brown hair he has gray eyes. Louis wears a brown outfit and orange hood and pink pants

===Charlie===
Charlie is Dola's second son in her gang from Castle in the Sky. He was voiced by Takumi Kamiyama, Bob Stuart, and [[Michael McShane]]. Charlie has a brown hair and gray eyes he wears a orange hood and brown sweater and pink pants with black belt.

===Henry===
Henry is Dola's third son in her gang. Henry wears white hat with green line and white coat and pink underneath. He is voiced by Sukekiyo Kameyama, Ernest Fessler, and [[Andy Dick]].
