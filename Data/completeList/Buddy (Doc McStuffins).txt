'''Buddy''' is Donny's yellow toy dump truck from the Disney Junior show ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. He speaks with a Brooklyn accent. He is voiced by [[Jess Harnell]].

==Personality==
Buddy is cool, easy-going and very friendly.
