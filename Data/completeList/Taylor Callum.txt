|image = taylorcallum.jpg}}'''Taylor Callum''' is the protagonist of the [[2006]] [[Disney Channel Original Movie]] ''[[Cow Belles]]''.

==Personality==
Taylor is a spoiled girl. She lives with her father and [[Courtney Callum|sister]]. She loves shopping, and she doesn't care about anyone. Later, when her father tells her to work in his company, she meets people and learns that they have difficult lives. She helps her father's company. She likes singing with her sister.
