'''Clan Dingwall''' is a Scottish clan in ''[[Brave]]''. It is one of the four clans that have united to form the [[DunBroch]] Kingdom. Its leader is called the [[Lord Dingwall]]. The clan's tartan is of dark and pale yellowish shades of green, with orange. Its emblem is the rock.
