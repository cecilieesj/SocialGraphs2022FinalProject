}}'''Flippy Doggenbottom''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Dog Dog] NPC in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontown Toontown]. He is located in Mickey Toon Hall in the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Playground Toontown Central Playground].

Flippy is also an NPC that you may have to rescue in the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/VP Sellbot VP] Battle ''(Therefore, he is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toons SOS Toon] as well as a NPC)'' Completing the battle and rescuing him will give you his SOS Card, which is a 5-star [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon-Up_Gags Toon-Up] SOS.

Flippy is also the supposed President of [[Toontown]]. In the Toontorial of the game, he introduces Chat and Speedchat to you. This plays a VERY important part of the game, especially on Halloween. When he asks you to speak, say "Toontastic!" on Halloween if you're a cat and you'll be turned into a Black Cat!

==SOS Toon==
﻿Flippy is also an SOS Toon. He is a 5-star Toon-Up SOS. When called upon, he will use the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Juggling_Balls Juggling Balls] to heal all toons in battle.

As with all All-Toon Toon-Up gags, the healing points will be divided evenly to all toons in battle.

==Trivia==
*Flippy is one of the few NPC Toons that has a one-word name.
*Flippy plays an important role in the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontorial Toontorial] ''(He introduces [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Speedchat Speedchat], and assigns the first non-Toontorial [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontask Toontask] in the game)''.
*Flippy has the "rarest" and most [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Laff Laff]-replenishing Toon-Up SOS Card. Flippy's Toon-Up SOS is capable of healing up to 124 Laff to all the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toons Toons currently] in a battle with a cog or cogs.
*Players have attempted to create Toons that look identical to Flippy ''(But the majority of these Toons do not have the name Flippy, as the Toon Council rejects anyone requesting to use this name. The nearest they can get is either fllipy or flippy.). ''
*Flippy is the mayor of Toontown.
*Flippy's office got a major revamp when the "Silly Meter" was introduced.
*His last name was from a trading card.
*The last [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Bossbot_Cog_Suits Bossbot Cog Suits] Toontask is the only Task that [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Shep_Ahoy Shep Ahoy] is not involved in.
*It is also the last major Toontask in Toontown, and the only Toontask that doesn't involve defeating Cogs.
**It is also the shortest Task, just making a visit to Flippy for the last Bossbot Cog Suit Piece.
