'''Bor''' is the father of [[Odin Borson|Odin]], paternal grandfather of [[Thor Odinson|Thor]] and conquerer of the [[Dark Elves]]. He is portrayed by Tony Curran.

This version of the mythological Bor was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Thor: The Dark World]]''===
Bor led the Asgardians troops during the first Battle of [[Svartalfheim]], to try to stop the Dark Elves who wanted to use the power of the [[Aether]] to return to the universe to an eternal darkness. Years later, while [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]] and Thor were going to Svartalfheim, the Bor statue is destroyed by his grandson.
