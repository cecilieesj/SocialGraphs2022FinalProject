[[File:Cloud-9-december-2-2013-3.jpg|thumb]]'''Nick Swift''' is a character and the secondary antagonist of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Cloud 9]]''. He is portrayed by [[Mike Manning]].
==History==
He is the leader of the competitive team of Swift Snowboarding Team, Nick Swift will do whatever it takes to win.
==Personality==
Nick is very competitive and leader of the Swifts, a snowboarding group. He will do whatever it takes to rule out all other competitors.
