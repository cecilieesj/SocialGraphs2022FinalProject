The Who|occupation = Musicians}}'''The Beets '''are a popular band in the animated television show ''[[Disney's Doug|Doug]]''. They are made up of four band members and they are very popular in Bluffington. They are [[Doug Funnie]], [[Porkchop]], and [[Skeeter Valentine]]'s favorite band.

The band is made up of Munroe Yoder, Chap Lipman, Flounder, and Wendy Nespah. Munroe plays the bass, Chap plays the guitar, Flounder plays the drums, and Wendy is a multi-instrumentalist. Wendy is lead vocalist and also plays keyboard and tuba.

While Doug Preis is listed as voicing Monroe, the other band members do not really have speaking roles. Wendy is said to be the lead vocalist, but Monroe is the one singing the band's songs within the show.
== Songs ==
Some of the songs are performed by the band, some are sung non-professionally by characters in the show, and others are only mentioned.

* I Need Mo' Allowance
* Shout Your Lungs Out
* I Sneezed on My Face
* My Foot's Asleep
