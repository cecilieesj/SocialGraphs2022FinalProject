|dislikes = Giving up}}'''Glenda Wilkins''' is a character that appears in "[[Last Train to Bustville]]," voiced by guest star [[Joan Cusack]]. She is an old friend of [[Betty Jo Flynn|Betty Jo]] and [[Clyde Flynn]].

She decides to make one last attempt of reaching Gearhart Summit with her steam locomotive before the engine is retired and takes Betty Jo, [[Linda Flynn-Fletcher|Linda,]] and [[Candace Flynn|Candace]] along for the attempt. Due to not bringing enough coal, the engine slows down before reaching the summit. Candace dismantles the passenger cars in order to provide more fuel for the engine and the train does reach the top.
