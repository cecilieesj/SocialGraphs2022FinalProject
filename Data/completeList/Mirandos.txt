|affiliations = Tomorrowland Transit Authority}}'''Mirandos Ariellian''' is a character, who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. She is a young intelligent Ariellian from the planet [[Tempestoro]]. Later in the series, she becomes a member of [[Mission Force One]].

==Background==
===Personality and Traits===
Much like the rest of her people, Mirandos likes computers, and like her best friend Loretta is a genius at computer coding with the use of her Calibandroid (similar to Loretta's [[Bracelex]]). Common for her species, Mirandos speaks with a computerized-like voice.

==Trivia==
*Mirandos is modelled and named after Miranda from Shakespeare's ''The Tempest''.
**Mirandos' species is also named after the character [[Wikipedia:Ariel (The Tempest)|Ariel]] from Shakespeare's play.
*Ivy Bishop, who provides the voice of Mirandos is the sister of Fiona Bishop, who provides the voice of [[Loretta Callisto]].
*Although Mirandos made her debut appearance in "[[The Quantum Cup]]", her first meeting with the Callistos was in "[[The Discovery Expedition]]".
*As of "[[Unfair Getaway]]", she is now an official Cosmic Explorer.
*It is shown that she has a close friendship with Loretta.
*In "[[Operation Groovestar]]", she sings for the first time.
