'''Ares''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character based on the Greek God of the same name.

This version of the mythological Ares was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
Ares appears as sculpture at [[Sakaar]] in the film ''[[Thor: Ragnarok]]''.

===[[Marvel Animation]]===
====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====
He appears in this series and is voiced by [[Trevor Devall]]. He is a ruthless, merciless and cruel god of war, he is an enemy of [[Hercules (Marvel)|Hercules]] and [[Avengers|the Avengers]]. He is an expert at hand-to-hand combat and has mastered both the sword and the axe as his preferred weapons in combat.

Ares tried to steal the Key of Tartarus away from Hercules, but thankfully Hercules and the Avengers managed to defeat Ares. Later, the Beyonder summoned Ares to capture [[Captain America]] and [[Iron Man]].
