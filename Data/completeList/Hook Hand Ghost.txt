==History==
He is a Civil War soldier who died in prison. His ghost haunts at [[Camp Mugwomp|camp]] with his leader [[Shackles Jack]].

His hand was cut off and was replaced with a steel hook. He is one of Shackles Jack's prisoners and minions. He doesn't have eyes just like Shackles Jack, [[Solitary Sam]], [[Chain Gang Chuck]], and [[Peg Leg Pat]].
