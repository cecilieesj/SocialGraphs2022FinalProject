'''Melinda''' is one of the minor characters in the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" segment of the [[1940]] film ''[[Fantasia]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Fantasia]]''===
Melinda is first seen looking lonely without a [[Centaurs (Fantasia)|centaur]] to mate with, and then some [[Cherubs|cherubs]] bring her together with [[Brudus]], a lonely centaur. Next, she is seen attending the Wine Festival with Brudus, when [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]] comes in and ruins everyone's fun. Afterwards after the storm, she and Brudus exit from their shelter, and as the night falls, they go to sleep together.
