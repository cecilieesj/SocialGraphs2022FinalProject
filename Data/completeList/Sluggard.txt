|voice = [[Frank Welker]]}}The '''Sluggard''' is the primary antagonist of the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' episode "[[A Recipe for Trouble]]".

He is an [[Ogres|Ogre]]-sized, nasty tempered, slug-like beast that pesters the Gnomes Nemo, Norm, and Nester. He makes them feed him gnome root, and in the episode, the occasional [[Gummi Bears|Gummi Bear]].

When Grammi realizes that her bad cooking could work against the bully, she goes full force against him, and chases the monster away for good.
