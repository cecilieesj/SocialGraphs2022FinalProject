'''Commander Cody''' (birth number '''CC-2224''') is a clone marshal commander who fought alongside Jedi General [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] and is the leader of the 212th Attack Battalion. He is also commander of the Third Systems Army, 7th Sky Corps, and Ghost Company.

==Background==
===Abilities===
*'''Expert Hand-To-Hand Combatant:''' As a Clone Commander, Cody is extremely skilled in unarmed combat.
*'''Expert Marksman:''' As a Clone Commander, Cody is extremely skilled in utilizing blaster pistols and blaster rifles.
*'''High-Level Intellect/Expert Tactician/Leader:''' Cody is highly intelligent. As a Clone Commander, Cody is an extremely skilled tactician and a capable leader.

==Appearances==
===''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]''===
Like most clone troopers, Cody was born on [[Kamino]]. Cody was involved in many battles and conflicts in the war though he did not participate in the First Battle of Geonosis but he did participate in many others alongside [[Obi-Wan Kenobi|Obi Wan Kenobi]].  Such as the Battle of Christophisis, Battle of Teth, Second Battle of Geonosis, Battle of Ryloth, Battle of Selucime, Battle of Umbara, and Battle for Anaxes.

===''Revenge of the Sith''===
Later in the Clone Wars, during the Battle of Utapau, [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] and Cody lead the 212th Attack Battalion with the aim of capturing/destroying [[General Grievous]]. After Obi-Wan destroyed the General, Cody received Order 66 a transmission from [[Palpatine|Darth Sidious]] that triggered his inhibitor chip to turn on the Jedi and ordered a AT-TE to fire on Obi-Wan.

===''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''===
Cody was mentioned by Captain [[Rex (Star Wars)|Rex]], where he and Rex made up a bunch of protocols back in the Clone Wars which the Empire still uses. He was also mentioned, when Rex accidently called Kanan "Cody", when they were captured by [[Kalani]] and his [[Battle Droids|droid]] [[Droidekas|army]].
