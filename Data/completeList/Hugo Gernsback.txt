'''Hugo Gernsback''' is one of the two tertiary antagonists from ''[[Tomorrowland]]''. He is an [[Audio-Animatronics|Audio-Animatronic]] who runs the Blast From the Past comic book store.

==Role in the film==
Hugo is <span>first seen </span>with his "wife" [[Ursula (Tomorrowland)|Ursula]]. at his shop when [[Casey Newton]] arrives with her [[1964 World's Fair Vintage Pin|Tomorrowland pin]] to see if they know anything.

However, as Casey is about to leave the store, they suddenly bring out laser guns, she thinks they're just joking with her until Hugo blasted the room.

The two then attend to kill Casey but [[Athena (Tomorrowland)|Athena]] shown up and save her from them Ursula later tries to attack her with a spear but accidentally hits Hugo instead and Athena then rips Ursula's head off.

The Gernsbacks are revealed to be robots, after being defeated they both blow up in huge exposing destroying the whole shop with them, shortly after the other robots learn about Casey at the crime seen of the shop than kill all of the police officers and inform [[David Nix]] that they have what their after.

==Trivia==
*His name is a reference to a science fiction magazine writer of the same name and namesake of the Hugo Awards, with his wife Ursula taking her name from Ursula K. Le Guin, one of the earliest female winners of the Hugo.
