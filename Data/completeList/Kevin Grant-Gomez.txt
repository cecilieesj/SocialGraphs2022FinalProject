'''Kevin Grant-Gomez''' is the deuteragonist of the [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. He serves as the brains in the trio.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Kevin has light skin and dark brown hair in an angular style. He wears an unbuttoned orange shirt with a brown undershirt.

==Trivia==
*Kevin bears a resemblance to [[Jeremy Johnson]], a character from ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]'', another show created by [[Dan Povenmire]].
*Kevin is similar to [[Candace Flynn]] given that both the sullen older siblings of the protagonist.
