'''Esther''', named during her later appearance on the ''[[Muppets Now]]'' episode "[[Socialized]]," first appeared as Betsy Ross in a 1993 [[w:c:muppets:Muppet Meeting Films|Muppet Meeting Film]], and the next year as a patron of [[w:c:muppets:The Muppet Theatre|The Muppet Theatre]] in the opening titles and framing sequences of ''[[w:c:muppets: Muppet Classic Theater|Muppet Classic Theater]]''.

She later appeared as a senior citizen [[w:c:muppets:Whatnots|Whatnot]] during [[w:c:muppets:The Muppet Show Theme#The Muppets|''The Muppet Telethon'' opening theme]] in 2011's ''[[The Muppets (film)|The Muppets]]''.

She appeared again, in her spot as one of the  chorus girls during the different countries' [[w:c:muppets:The Muppet Show Theme#Muppets Most Wanted|''Muppet Show'' opening themes]], in ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]'' in 2014.
