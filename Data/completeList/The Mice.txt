|status=Active}}'''The Mice''' are a trio of sycophantic mice in ''[[G-Force]]''. The mice always agreed with [[Bucky (G-Force)|Bucky]], supporting him about not being part ferret until the end when the three and Bucky are adopted. [[Hurley]] even pointed out the mice will agree to anything, they even agreed when Hurley called them all idiots.

Each mouse is voiced by [[Tracy Morgan]], Hoyt Yeatman IV, and Max Favreau.
