|enemies = [[Thunderclap (moose)|Thunderclap]]|voice = [[Dink Trout]]}}'''Balsam''' is a character from the animated [[Silly Symphonies]] short, ''[[Morris, the Midget Moose]]''. He is a large moose with little antlers. Balsam is voiced by Dink Trout.

==Role in the film==
Balsam makes friends with another moose, who has a small size and large antlers named [[Morris the Midget Moose]]. Together, they face the terrible [[Thunderclap (moose)|Thunderclap]], terror of all moose, and they take away the fight, by disguising themselves as a monster to scare Thunderclap away forever.
