'''Prince Justin''', also known as '''Turniphead''', is a major character in ''[[Howl's Moving Castle]]''. He is the brother of the King of Ingary. He was cursed and turned into Turniphead aka that scarecrow. [[Sophie Hatter|Sophie]] kissed him and broke the curse.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Justin has blond hair and brown eyes. He wears a red bow tie, white shirt, pink vest and beige sport coat.
