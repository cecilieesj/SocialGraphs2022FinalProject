'''Dr. Julius Strangepork''' is an elderly Muppet pig who serves as the resident scientist aboard the spaceship Swinetrek in the "Pigs in Space" sketches on ''[[w:c:muppets:The Muppet Show|The Muppet Show]]''. His fellow crew members include [[Link Hogthrob|Captain Link Hogthrob]] and [[Miss Piggy|First Mate Piggy]]. The German-accented "sesquipedalian" performed routine repair work as well as offer diagnostics on planets or approaching spacecrafts.

Strangepork was occasionally seen outside the Pigs in Space skits as well.
