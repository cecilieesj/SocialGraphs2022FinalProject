'''Miss Rhonda Hightower''' is a recurring character fromÂ [[Disney Channel]]'s ''[[The Proud Family]]'' and the [[Disney+]] revival ''[[The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder]]''.

==History==
She is the principal of Willie T. Ribbs Middle School. She usually speaks in a meek, quiet voice, but then switches to a loud, authoritative voice when the kids won't listen to her, hence her name. When she yells, its usually insults about dim possibilities for the kids' futures.

==Appearance==
Miss Hightower is a short middle aged woman with brownish auburn hair. She wears glasses with round lenses. She wears a brown blouse with yellow flowers and a orange skirt. She wears sandals.
