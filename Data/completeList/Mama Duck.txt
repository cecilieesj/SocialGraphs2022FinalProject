'''Mama Duck''' is the mother of [[Harry the Duck]] and his new little sister, [[Hallie Duck]]. She normally lives with them both at the Otter Pond, which they call the Duck Pond. Mama Duck apparently comes from [[Russia]], as evidenced by her accent and her occasional use of phrases. She is normally seen wearing a red babushka with white flower patterns.
