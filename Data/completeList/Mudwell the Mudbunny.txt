'''Mudwell the Mudbunny''' was a brown rabbit-like creature who appeared in the ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Fraggle_Rock Fraggle Rock]'' episode "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_507:_Gone,_But_Not_Forgotten Gone, But Not Forgotten]". He was performed by [[w:c:muppet:Richard Hunt|Richard Hunt]]. Mudwell dies in the episode, leaving his friend [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Wembley_Fraggle Wembley] to learn about the cycle of death and rebirth, through his subsequent reincarnation in the form of a [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Lizard_(Fraggle_Rock) Lizard]. In ''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]'', Mudwell appears as one of the pirates on [[Long John Silver (Muppets)|Long John Silver's]] side and operates Dead Tom. He ironically cries over Dead Tom's "death" at the hands of Silver. Walleye Pike then reminds him that Dead Tom is already dead. In this appearance, Mudwell is performed by [[Bill Barretta]]. Mudwell was later seen as Wally on ''[[w:c:muppet:Muppet Time|Muppet Time]]'' and other characters asÂ [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Flash_Harry Flash Harry], [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Mr._Hoofit Mr. Hoofit], and [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Rudy_Monster Rudy Monster] in ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Mopatop%27s_Shop Mopatop's Shop]''.
