'''Proxima Midnight''' is a [[Marvel Cinematic Universe|Marvel Comics]] character and supervillainess who appeared as one of the antagonists of both ''[[Avengers: Infinity War]] ''and ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]''. She is a high-ranking member of the [[Black Order]] who has undying loyalty to [[Thanos]].

Proxima Midnight was created by Jonathan Hickman and Mike Deodato Jr.

== Background ==

===Personality===
Proxima Midnight is fiercely loyal to [[Thanos]]. Her harsh, unforgiving training at his hands has rendered her fierce, arrogant, and sadistic.
