{{Infobox_character|image = PaddyPig.jpeg|name = Paddy Pig|shorts = ''[[The Band Concert]]''<br>''[[Mickey's Grand Opera]]''<br>''[[All Together]]''<br>''[[Mickey's Christmas Carol]]'' (cameo)|shows = ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' (cameo)|alignment = Good|friends = [[Mickey Mouse]], [[Donald Duck]], [[Goofy]], [[Clarabelle Cow]], [[Horace Horsecollar]], [[Peter Pig]], [[Clara Cluck]], [[Pluto]]}}'''Paddy Pig''' is a character that debuted in the [[Mickey Mouse]] cartoon, [[The Band Concert|''The Band Concert'']] as the tuba player in Mickey's orchestra. His only other appearances in classic animation again are in the [[1936]] short, [[Mickey's Grand Opera|''Mickey's Grand Opera'']], and in the [[1942]] wartime short, [[All Together|''All Together'']].

He has also made cameo appearances in [[Mickey's Christmas Carol|''Mickey's Christmas Carol'']] and the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode "[[The Adorable Couple]]".
