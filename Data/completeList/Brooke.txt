{{Infobox_character|image = Brooke in Austin and Ally.PNG|actress = Carrie Wampler|shows = ''[[Austin and Ally]]''|alignment = Bad|friends = [[Austin Moon]] (formerly)}}'''Brooke''' is [[Austin Moon|Austin Moon's]] ex-girlfriend. Brooke first appeared in the episode, [[Magazines & Made-Up Stuff]]. Her last appearance on the show was [[Mysteries & Meddling Kids]].
== Trivia ==
* Her last name is unknown.
* Brooke made imaginary dates with Austin in her scrapbook from her dreams.
* She says she went on 37 dates with Austin, but only went on 2-3.
