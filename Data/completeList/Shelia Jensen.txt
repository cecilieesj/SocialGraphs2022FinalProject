[[File:Sheila_walks_away_leather_jacket.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Shelia Jensen''' is one of the comic characters of the [[W.I.T.C.H.]] of the comic book series
==History==
She is the Daughter of Kevin Jensen is the owner of Jensen Dance School of Academy and a friends with [[Taranee Cook|Taranee]] and [[Luke Pradd|Luke]]. She appears in 9 comic books issues.
