|partner = [[Star-Lord]] (ex-boyfriend)<br>Lunatik (ex-boyfriend)}}'''Lucy''' is a character and supervillainess from [[Marvel Cinematic Universe|Marvel Comics]]. Lucy is an A'askvarian a humanoid race with octopoid traits.
==Appearances==
===[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]===
Lucy the A'askvarian was one of [[Star-Lord]]'s ex girlfriends. Their relationship ended badly for Star-Lord "lying" about being a prince and never calling, she has resented him ever since. She constantly teams up with Star-Lord's other ex girlfriend [[Supergiant]] to get revenge on Star-Lord.
