'''Juanita Simmons''' is the mother of [[Chanel Simmons]] and [[Pucci Simmons]]. She, unlike her friend Dorothea Garibaldi, she is more care-free of what her children do. She is very happy with her life. She is currently married [[Luc]].

==Background==
Not much is know about Juanita, except that she lives in the Upper New York, since she has a lot of money.

===Physical Appearance===
Juanita is a Spanish female. She had dark long hair in the first movie and the beginning of the second, but it is know a sort of highlighted hair-style.

In the books, Galleria narrates that Juanita looks like a "glamapuss" and is slim as a model.

==Trivia==
*Chanel once charged hundreds of dollars on her Juanita's credit card.
