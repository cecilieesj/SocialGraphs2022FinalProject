'''Hugo''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is the human of [[Clarissa]].

== Personality ==
Hugo is loyally submissive his dog Clarissa whom he has spoiled rotten, as he complies to every single wishes she has.

He also seems to be a phone addict, as he is unwilling to let go of his phone in "[[Prank Pups]]".

== Appearance ==
Hugo is jokingly similar to his dog, Clarissa. He's short, with the same hair color than Clarissa's fur, a nose similar to Clarissa's muzzle, the same long eyelashes and even has a similar mole in his right cheek. Hugo looks like his dog in human form.
