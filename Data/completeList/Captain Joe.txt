|quote= }}'''Captain Joe Callisto''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. He is an intergalactic crime fighter uncle of [[Miles Callisto|Miles]] and [[Loretta Callisto]] and Brother to [[Leo Callisto]].

==Background==
Joe Callisto is Leo Callisto's younger brother. Joe is a space guard, and he is always on the trail of the alien outlaw [[Gadfly Garnett]]. Every time Gadfly steals the latest technology, Joe puts him in jail.

Captain Joe is Miles' and Loretta's favorite uncle - especially Miles' favorite uncle.
