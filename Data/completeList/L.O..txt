'''L.O.''' is a character from ''[[Happy Monster Band]]''.

==Appearance==
L.O. is a slender yellow monster with blue horns, hand and feet, green and red eyes, and fangs.

==[[Happy Monster Band]]==
L.O. is one of the main characters from the show. He can play guitar as well as Fred.
