==History==
She is the girl who tries to take [[Cody Martin|Cody]] away from [[Bailey Pickett|Bailey]]. She is very sassy, and she tries to make every move she can to get Cody closer to her. 

==Trivia==
*She is only found outside of the classroom once.
*She sweeps Cody off of his feet after Woody does to Bailey.
*She calls Cody "Sir Chef".
*Initially, she and the other girls in the class think he is not supposed to be there, and think of him weird.
