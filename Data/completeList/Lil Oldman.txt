He will be your first toon to visit in The Brrrgh for the final Gag Training Task.
==Toontasks==
His tasks gives toons the choice to pick the last 2 [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Gag gag] tracks.

#Defeat 10 Large [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs cogs] (Level 8+)

#Take down 2 of the tallest [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cog_buildings cog buildings] 4+ Floors (he rotates between the four cog types)
#Return to Lil Oldman
#Choose the last gag track you will train for in Toontown
Reward: Last Gag Track Training for the last 2 unpicked Gag Tracks.

==SOS Toon==
<span style="line-height:1.5em;">Lil Oldman is also an SOS Toon. He is a 5-star Lure SOS. When called upon, he will use </span>[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Hypno_Goggles Hypno Goggles]<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> to lure all cogs in battle.</span>

His Lure will last 4 rounds.
