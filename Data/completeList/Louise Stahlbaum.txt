'''Louise Stahlbaum''' is a character of the 2018 Disney film, ''[[The Nutcracker and the Four Realms|The Nutcracker and The Four Realms]]''. She is the elder sister of [[Clara Stahlbaum|Clara]] and [[Fritz Stahlbaum]] and is played by Ellie Bamber.

==Background==
===Personality===
Louise is a kind, polite, girly, and tidy girl.

===Physical Appearance===
Louise is a fair skinned girl with brown hair.

==Role in the film==
Clara and Fritz encounter Louise, telling Fritz that Clara is planning on catching mice on Christmas Eve. She, Clara, and Fritz approach their father, [[Benjamin Stahlbaum|Benjamin]] who gives them presents from their late mother, [[Marie Stahlbaum|Marie]], to which Louise receives her favorite dress. Upon wearing it, she interrupts Benjamin's discussion with Clara in a plan to go to the party, much to Clara's frustration in an attempt to open Marie's last gift.

Just as the Stahlbaum family leaves for [[Drosselmeyer]]'s mansion, Benjamin tells Louise that she should try to make new friends while dancing. She and her relatives are accepted into the invitation.

After Clara returns home, Louise happily reunites with her family upon seeing Benjamin dancing with her together just as Louise and Fritz dance together in couples. Back at home, the Stahlbaum family dances together to the waltz along with the other party guests.
