[[File:Master_Pho.jpg|thumb|168px|Master Pho as he appears in Season 1]]'''Master Pho''' was Agent K's karate teacher in ''[[The Replacements]]'', but he turned out to be evil and was defeated by Agent K.

==History==
He first appeared in "Master Pho" when Todd replaced his karate teacher with him and realizes he's one of Agent K's enemies. He later returned in "Puzzle Me Daring" wearing a disguise stealing many things like artwork and money, leaving riddles that nobody could solve "except for Todd" and together Agent K and Todd defeated Master Pho. His last appearance in the show was "[[Irreplaceable]]" when [[Dr. Scorpius]] had mind-controlled all of the replacements Todd and Riley asked for and joined Dr. Scorpius in the Arctic at the end of the episode.
