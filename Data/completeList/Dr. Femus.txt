'''Dr. Femus''' is a character, who appeared in the Tomorrowland attraction [[ExtraTERRORestrial Alien Encounter]]. She appeared in a duo with [[Spinlok]].

== Appearances ==

===ExtraTERRORestrial Alien Encounter===

Dr. Femus, along with [[Spinlok]], appear during the main show. They are shown on screens from the alien planet which holds X-S headquarters, interfering with the teleportation process.
