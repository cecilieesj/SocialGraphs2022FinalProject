'''Sabrina''' is a girl from the Nanju tribe that [[Brady Parker]] finds cute. It is later found out that she is one of [[Mikayla Makoola]]'s old enemies, they both went to fight school. Sabrina later kidnaps Brady. She fights Mikayla but loses and is locked up. She is seen in the episode Crouching Brady, Hidden Boomer.

==Trivia==
*She is portrayed by Skylar Vallo.
*She went to fight school with Mikayla.
*She has a scrap book called 'Know your enemy scrapbook' on Mikayla.
*She had a belt which enables her to fly.
*She is said to have 'Man feet'.
*Her full name is said to be Sabrina Giselle Naomi Van de Kamp.
