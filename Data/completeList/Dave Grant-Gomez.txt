'''Dave Grant-Gomez''' is a main character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. He is the father of Kevin and Gretel and the husband of [[Carolina Grant-Gomez|Carolina]].

==Background==

===Physical appearance===
Dave Grant-Gomez has light skin with light brown hair and beard. He wears black glasses, a green shirt and blue jeans.

==Trivia==
*His design is modeled after the show's creator [[Dan Povenmire]].
