'''King Larry''' is [[King Louie|King Louie's]] twin brother who appeared in the ''[[House of Mouse]]'' episode "[[King Larry Swings In]]".

Louie was supposed to star in that episode, but issues with the widow of [[Louis Prima]] (Louie's original voice actor) prompted the creation of Larry.

==Role in the episode==
King Larry makes a guest appearance in ''House of Mouse'', where he, Mickey and the [[Bandar-log]] perform "[[I Wanna Be Like M-O-You]]" featuring Larry singing about how he wants to be like [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] after chasing out [[Buzzie, Flaps, Ziggy, and Dizzy|the vulture band]] who was to perform earlier on, causing a mess in the House of Mouse and finally learning how to behave like a gentleman.

==Trivia==
*King Larry's name is a possible name switch of the American television and radio host, [[Larry King]].
