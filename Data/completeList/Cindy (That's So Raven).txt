'''Cindy''' is a recurring character on the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[That's So Raven]]''. She is the girlfriend of [[Cory Baxter|Cory]] and is portrayed by Jordyn Colemon.

==History==
Cory meets Cindy in "[[Double Vision]]". By the events of "[[Bend It Like Baxter]]" they're dating. By the series finale "[[Where There's Smoke]]" Cindy has started smoking. After the intervention that [[Raven Baxter|Raven]] and the gang hold for Cory when they think that he's smoking, Cindy tells the truth and promises to never smoke again.
