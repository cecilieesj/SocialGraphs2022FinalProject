'''Buffy and Muffy Vanderschmere''' are two red foxes and the antagonists of the ''[[TaleSpin]]'' episode "[[A Touch of Glass]]". They are married criminals who pose as the elite to attempt to nab whatever valuables they can get their hands on.

Muffy also makes a cameo appearance in "[[My Fair Baloo]]" as a passenger onboard the [[Spruce Moose]].
