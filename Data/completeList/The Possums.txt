'''The Possums''' appeared in the second part of the two-part ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]'' episode "[[Welcome to Woodland Valley]]." The large possum was voiced by [[Gilbert Gottfried]] while the small one was voiced by [[Brad Garrett]]. The pair of possums live in a tree that fell on the [[Woodland Valley Library]] during a storm, and express concern when [[Doc Hogg]] suggests chopping the tree for lumber.
