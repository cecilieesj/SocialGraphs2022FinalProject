==Relationships==
=== Love Interest(s) ===
====Judy Kling-Plank====
In ''[[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century]]'', Edward first met [[Zenon Kar]]’s Aunt Judy when she first came up to the Space Station with her niece [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] in 2049. Judy fell into Edward’s arm  when the Space Station was about to be destroyed. Then later at the Microbe Concert they were seen together and [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] gave Aunt Judy a thumbs up feeling happy that her Aunt found someone. 

A little over a year later in 2051, in ''[[Zenon: The Zequel]]'', Edward and Judy are official a couple. Edward is in a long-distanced relationship with his Earth Girlfriend Judy. He keeps a picture of him and Judy in his Space Commander Office.
