'''Ferret Jeeter''' is a [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Baseball baseball] star who went to Hazelnut High School with [[Bear (Bear in the Big Blue House)|Bear]] and played baseball with him on the same high school team. He was performed by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Jim_Kroupa Jim Kroupa].

Ferret Jeeter appears in the ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]'' episode "[[At the Old Bear Game]]." He is the shortstop for the [[Sequoia City]] Big Bats. He is a very talented player and the spokesperson for the soda Clammy Cola. [[Pip and Pop]] are big fans of his, and Bear gave them the first-ever copy of his rookie card.

The character is a spoof of Yankees shortstop [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Derek_Jeter Derek Jeter].
