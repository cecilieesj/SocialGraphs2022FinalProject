'''Glenda Molehill''' appeared in the ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_211:_Switched_at_Birth Switched at Birth]." She was voiced by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Mimi_Kennedy Mimi Kennedy], her body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Tom_Fisher Tom Fisher] and her face was operated by [[Bruce Lanoil]]. She was made from the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Needlenose Needlenose] Unisaur.

Glenda is the wife of [[Gus Molehill]] and mother of [[Aubrey Molehill]] and had been a fellow Lamaze class attendee with [[Frances Sinclair|Fran Sinclair]]. During an earthquake, their eggs rolled off their nests and had apparently been switched. [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Absolute_Proof_Laboratories Absolute Proof Laboratories] proved that [[Baby Sinclair]] was the real Molehill child. However, Glenda became disgusted with Baby's habits (including devouring the cat and spitting him up all over the carpet) and demanded that she get Aubrey back, even though Gus preferred Baby.{{MuppetsWiki}}{{Jim Henson's Dinosaurs}}{{DEFAULTSORT:Molehill, Glenda}}
