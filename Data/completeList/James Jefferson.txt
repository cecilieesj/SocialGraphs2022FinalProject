'''James Jefferson''' is a human character who appeared in ''[[The Muppets Present... Great Moments in American History]]'' stage show at the [[Liberty Square]] part of the [[Magic Kingdom]] area in the [[Walt Disney World]] theme park. James Jefferson (also known as "JJ") is a Revolutionary history buff who will serve as the Town Crier of Liberty Square at the Magic Kingdom.

A Disney Parks casting description described the role as follows posted in July 2016:
:''male improvisational actor (must portray 20-30 years old) who sings, 5’10” and taller, to be James Jefferson, "JJ", Town Crier of Liberty Square. JJ is a Revolutionary history buff who delights in sharing his knowledge of the Colonial era and Liberty Square with anyone who will listen, and he has teamed up with his good friends [[Sam the Eagle (Muppets)|Sam the Eagle]], [[Kermit the Frog]], [[Miss Piggy]] and others to share some great moments in [[United States|American]] history!''<ref>[https://disneyauditions.com/audition-calendar/?aud_id=7945 DisneyAuditions]</ref>
