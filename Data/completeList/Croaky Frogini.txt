'''Croaky Frogini''' is a character from the children's show ''[[JoJo's Circus]]''. She is a performer of the Flying Froginis and one of [[JoJo Tickle]]'s fellow classmates the Circus Town School. Croaky met JoJo on the first day of school, when she realized she had different abilities and agreed to help each other learn different tricks.
