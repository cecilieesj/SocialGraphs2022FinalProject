'''Anton Mogart''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Moon Knight (TV series)|Moon Knight]]'', played by the late Gaspard Ulliel.

Anton Mogart was created by Doug Moench and Bill Sienkiewicz.

He is a mysterious antiquities collector and an old friend of [[Scarlet Scarab|Layla El-Faouly]].

== Trivia ==
*This is Gaspard Ulliel's only [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] role, as well as one of his final roles, as he died in a skiing accident on [[January 19]], [[2022]].
