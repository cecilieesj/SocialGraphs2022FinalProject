|quote="Well well if it isn't Captain America"}}'''Rattler''' is a member of the Serpent Society.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
Rattler and the Serpent Society, led by [[King Cobra]], battled [[Avengers|the Avengers]] in a subway. Unfortunately, [[Henry Pym|Hank Pym]]'s paficist decisions cause the Serpent Society to escape.

In the wake of the [[Skrull]] invasion, Rattler and the rest of the Serpent Society face [[Captain America]] and [[Spider-Man]] to free King Cobra and [[Viper]].
==Powers & Abilities==
He has enhanced strength and can shoot bolts of electricity from his tail.
