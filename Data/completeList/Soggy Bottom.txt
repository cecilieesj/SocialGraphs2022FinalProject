'''Soggy Bottom''' is a character from the discontinued online game, ''[[Toontown Online]]''. He is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Horse Horse] and a shopkeeper toon who owns [http://toontown.wikia.com/index.php?title=Petal_Pusher_Bicycles&action=edit&redlink=1 Petal Pusher Bicycles], on [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Elm_Street Elm Street], in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Daisy_Gardens Daisy Gardens].

==SOS Toon==
ï»¿Soggy Bottom is also an [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon]. He is a 4-star Toons Hit. When called upon, he uses his [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Pixie_Dust Pixie Dust] to allow toons to hit [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs cogs] '''guaranteed'''.

This SOS lasts 1 round.

==Trivia==
*All SOS Toons Hit start with the letter "S".
**Coincidentally, two of the three toons both start with Soggy. He shares his name with [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Soggy_Nell Soggy Nell].
*He was the only SOS Toon Horse, until [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Doctor_Drift Doctor Drift] was remodelled.
