She is portrayed by [[Paulina Olszynski]].

==Appearance==
Tutor was hired by Jerry to be Max's tutor, to help him with his wizard studies. Justin develops a crush on her, and Alex wants to be her friend. They are both upset to find out that Tutor will leave after Max passes her wand test in Wiz Class, so decide to sabotage his progress to keep Tutor from leaving.

Max finds out, and tells Tutor while she is hanging out with Justin and Alex.

Back at the lair, they learn that Tutor is partly an elf; she has a magical attractive quality which makes people like her, a quality that's in babies and puppies and easter bunnies.

==Trivia==
*She has the appearance of a teenager, but indirectly mentioned that she's been tutoring wizards for "years."
