'''Leslie J. Clark''' is a character in the [[1945]] [[Donald Duck]] cartoon ''[[Duck Pimples]]''. He is a hot iron salesman, who offered hot irons to [[Paddy the Cop]] to torture Donald.

==Background==
==='''Physical Appearance'''===
Leslie is a slender man with a half bald head, black hair, a thick black unibrow, a black mustache and wears a light tan coat with a flower, orange rectangular hat with a red stripe, black bow, light pink business suit, cocoa brown pants, orange boots, holding a light tan suitcase with a handle.

==Appearances==
===''[[DTV|DTV Monster Hits]]''===
In ''DTV Monster Hits'', an original animation had him make a cameo, along with [[Dopey Davis]] and [[Pauline]], talking to [[Witch Hazel]] before Daryl Hall's "Dreamtime" started.

===''[[Quack Pack]]''===
He later appeared as '''E-Z Smith''' in the ''[[Quack Pack]]'' episode; "[[Can't Take a Yolk (Quack Pack)|Can't Take a Yolk]]", where he sells his retro-growth formula to [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]] as an easy way for the tree to shrink.
