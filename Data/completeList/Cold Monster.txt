}}The '''Cold Monster''' is a character in ''[[Future-Worm!]]'' He is a monster created from [[Danny Douglas|Danny]]'s cold, which soon became malevolent and caused havoc and pain at Sci-Fi-Fanti-Con, until it was defeated by [[Doug Douglas|Danny's father]].
==Appearance==
The Cold Monster is a giant sentient mass of mucus with multiple eyes: five on its "body" and two more on top of stalks. It has four arachnid-like legs which it uses to move around, and on top of its head is a large, crystalline brain of sorts.
