[[File:Chris_&_Craig_Eating.jpg|thumb]]'''Chris and Craig'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/chriscraig.htm</ref> are minor characters from the episode "[[A Ghost Story]]" of the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
Chris and Craig are twin campers in [[Camp Mugwomp]] who had [[Jake Long|Jake]], [[Trixie Carter|Trixie]], and [[Arthur "Spud" P. Spudinski|Spud]] as counselors. They are overweight. They wouldn't feel hungry all the time if it weren't for Jake's confidence.
