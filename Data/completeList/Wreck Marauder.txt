'''Wreck Marauder''' is the main antagonist of the ''[[Tangled: The Series|Tangled]]'' episode, "[[Challenge of the Brave]]" and he is considered the most feared warrior ever.

==Background==
Almost everyone is scared of Wreck Marauder. However, despite his reputation for being the most ruthless warrior ever (in fact, he is exaggeratedly fierce; even he intimidates almost all competitors of the "[[Challenge of the Brave]]"), only [[Cassandra (Tangled)|Cassandra]] knows that he exaggerates his ferocity.

==Trivia==
*Wreck Marauder is voiced by [[Danny Trejo]] who is best known for his roles in [[Disney Channel]] series. His other [[Disney]] roles include Ox in the ''The Spectacular [[Spider-Man]]'' episode "Probable Cause", Johnny-23 in the [[1997]] [[Touchstone Pictures]] film ''[[Con Air]]'', [[Venom]] in ''[[Phineas and Ferb: Mission Marvel]]'', [[Antonio Agama]] in ''[[Elena of Avalor]]'', Vasquez in ''[[Big City Greens]]'', and the lead piñata in the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode "[[¡Feliz Cumpleaños!]]".
*Wreck Marauder appeared as a cameo in "[[The Wrath of Ruthless Ruth]]".
