[[File:Stork.jpg|thumb|250px]]
The '''stork''' is a bird who has taken up residence and refuses to be moved from his perch in a high rise clock that needs cleaning in the [[1937]] animated short, ''[[Clock Cleaners]]''. [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] tries to get rid of the bird without any luck.
