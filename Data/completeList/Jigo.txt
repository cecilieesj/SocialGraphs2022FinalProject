[[File:Jigo.jpg|thumb|250 px|Jigo.]]'''Jigo''' was an antagonist in the [[1997]] [[Studio Ghibli]] film ''[[Princess Mononoke]]''. In the Japanese version, he was voiced by Kaoru Kobayashi and in the English dub, he was voiced by [[Billy Bob Thornton]].

== Personality ==
Jigo is not much of an antagonist in this movie. He just has this role as you can often see him interact and work with [[Lady Eboshi]] who her herself isn't much of a villain either.
