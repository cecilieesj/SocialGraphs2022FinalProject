'''Butchy''' is the leader of the motorcycle gang in the Wet Side Story film inside the movie ''[[Teen Beach Movie]]''. He is portrayed by John DeLuca.

==Background==
Butchy is the leader of the bikers.

===Personality===
Butchy is a leader, and likes to be in charge of the rough and tough biker gang. He's very hard on the outside but can be supportive at times. 

==Appearances==
===''[[Teen Beach Movie]]''===
Butchy first appears in the bikers' musical number ''Crusin' for a Bruisin<nowiki>'</nowiki>''. He leads the bikers throughout the film, and later decides to help Lela and Tanner defeat Les Camembert and Dr. Fusion. 

===''[[Teen Beach 2]]''===
Butchy finds Mack's necklace after Lela tosses it back into the waves so she can stay in Mack and Brady's world, he and the others go to Mack and Brady's world as well to bring Lela and Tanner back to stop their movie from destructing. He disappears immediately after performing "Gotta Be Me".
