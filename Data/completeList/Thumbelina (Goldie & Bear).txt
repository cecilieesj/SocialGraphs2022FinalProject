She babysat [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie]] and [[Bear (Goldie & Bear)|Bear]] while Mr. and Mrs. Bear were out for the evening. Everyday is an adventure for this small girl. While babysitting, she fell down the sink and nearly fell over a waterfall. She has a big sense of humor as she tricked Goldie and Bear with a thimble of milk and a single raisin. She loves building pillow forts and playing games like checkers. She is friends with a crow named Larry.
