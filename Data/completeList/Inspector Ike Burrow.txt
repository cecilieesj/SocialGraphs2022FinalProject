'''Inspector Ike Burrow''' is a one-time character featured in the ''[[TaleSpin]]'' episode "[[All's Whale That Ends Whale]]". This inspector works for the Agency Concerned with the Happiness of Oceanic Oddities (ACHOO).

==Role in the episode==
When [[Baloo#TaleSpin|Baloo]] and [[Kit Cloudkicker]] find out that [[Seymour (TaleSpin)|Seymour]] is neglecting to take proper care of the animals in the Seaquarium, they decide to call Inspector Burrow.
