==History==
Crockett appears in "South of Friendship, North of Honour" He wants to show his gratitude to [[Wayne Liggett|Wayne]] for saving his harvest. Wayne tells him to think nothing of it, but he insists. Crockett gives him a new set of comic books as an appreciation gift.
