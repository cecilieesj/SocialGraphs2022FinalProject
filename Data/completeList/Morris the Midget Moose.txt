|enemies = [[Thunderclap (moose)|Thunderclap]]}}'''Morris the Midget Moose''' is the titular character of [[Morris, the Midget Moose|the animated short of the same name]]. 

==Role in the film==
He is a young moose who is the laughing stock of his herd because although he has big horns, he has a small size. He makes friends with another moose who has a big size and small horns named [[Balsam]]. Together, they face the terrible [[Thunderclap (moose)|Thunderclap]], terror of all moose, and take away the fight by disguising themselves as a monster to scare Thunderclap away forever.
