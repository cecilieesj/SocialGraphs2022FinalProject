|occupation = Motorcycle gang leader}}'''Johnny T. Rex''' is a minor villain in ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''. He only appeared in the episode "[[Extinct Possibility]]". He is voiced by Chick Vennera.

==Background==
When [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing]] goes back in time to the age of the dinosaurs, he discovers that the ancient scientists are being threatened by a T-Rex and his gang. To keep himself and the citizens from becoming Johnny's next entree, he challenges him to a road race. Eventually, Johnny is encased in amber and, after eons pass, scientists recover his ageless body, still frozen and assumed to still be alive.

==Trivia==
*Johnny T. Rex is a playable character in the game ''Disney Adventures: Sport Goofy Olympic'', which was released on Game Boy and Panasonic 3DO and published by Konami in [[1994]].
