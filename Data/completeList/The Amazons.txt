|occupation = Racecar drivers}}'''The Amazons''' are a group of jumpsuit-wearing racecar driving ladies.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
[[File:The_Amazons_2.png|thumb|left|264px]]The Amazons all wear full racing uniforms with heart motifs.

Each Amazon has its own colored uniform; Foxy is orange or pumpkin, Lizzy is red or lava, Hair girl indigo is turquoise or robin egg blue, The girl orange hair is purple or dark violet, The black girl with deep purple hair is pink or royal fuchsia, The girl with brown hair is lime or inchworm and The girl with dark red hair is gold or sandstorm.

==Appearances==
===''[[Motorcity]]''===
When [[The Duke of Detroit]] set a bounty on The [[Burners]] heads, the Amazons were among those who tried to claim the reward.
