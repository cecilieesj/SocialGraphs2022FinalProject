'''Naya''' is a main character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is a cheerleader of the high school Kevin, Fred and Hiromi attend to.

== Background ==

=== Physical appearance ===
Naya has light skin and black hair. She wears her cheerleaders uniform with the colors of blue and gold, representing the high school's colors.
