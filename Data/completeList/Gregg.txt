{{NeedImage}}'''Gregg''' is a supporting character in ''[[Ernest Scared Stupid]]''. He was dressed as a Native American for Halloween when [[Trantor]] transformed him into a wooden doll. He reunites with the citizens of [[Briarville]] after [[Ernest P. Worrell|Ernest]] defeats Trantor once and for all.
