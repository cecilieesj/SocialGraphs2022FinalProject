'''Emma''' is awestruck when [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]] shows up at her school and tells the girls she’s going to throw a party for them at [[Cinderella's Castle|the castle]]. Emma can hardly wait! In fact, she’s so excited that she sneaks into the castle to get an early peek. She soon learns first-hand that being a princess isn't as easy as it seems. She first appears in the [[2006]] book, ''My Best Friend is Cinderella''.
