[[File:Glenn_Freeze_Frame.jpg|thumb]]'''Glenn''' is a man who works at the local dog shelter of [[Pasadena, California|Pasadena]]. He seems to be an animal person. He's portrayed by Harvey Guillen.

He was seen in the first series of ''[[Dog With a Blog]]'' when Avery and Tyler went to retrieve Stan from the Animal Shelter. However, he was not without being a protective man guarding his shelter, trying to prevent them from retrieving Stan.
