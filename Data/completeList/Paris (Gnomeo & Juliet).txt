}}'''Paris''' is a minor character from the animated [[Touchstone Pictures]] film ''[[Gnomeo & Juliet]]''.

==Role in the film==
Paris was arranged by his leader [[Lord Redbrick]] to be a suitor to his daughter [[Juliet (Gnomeo & Juliet)|Juliet]]. Juliet spurned Paris's advances and distracted him with her friend [[Nanette]], who had a crush on him.

After the Reds and Blues end their feud, Paris starts a relationship with Nanette.

==Trivia==
*Paris channels [[Elton John]] when he sings the song "[[Your Song]]" to Juliet.
