{{Infobox character|image = Kirk.jpg|shows = [[The Weekenders]]|voice = Jason Marsden|alignment = Good|family = Granny McQuarrie|parents=Unnamed mother<br>Unnamed father|siblings=Multiple brothers<br>[[Lor McQuarrie]] (younger sister)}}'''Kirk McQuarrie''' (voiced by [[Jason Marsden]]) is a secondary character from [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s animated series ''[[The Weekenders]]''. He is [[Lor McQuarrie|Lor]]'s oldest brother and is noted for wearing a collared t-shirt and a sweater and beige pants. He plays basketball and football. He also dated [[Carver Descartes|Carver]]'s sister [[Penny Descartes|Penny]] in the episode "[[Penny McQuarrie]]".
