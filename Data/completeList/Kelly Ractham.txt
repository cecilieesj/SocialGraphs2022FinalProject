==Background==
Kelly Ractham was born on May 26, 2005 and raised in Los Angeles CA. Kelly is a part of the Blue team consisting of three people; Kelly, Nolan, and Giana. Shopclass is an Adventure Family Game-Show consisting of a competition format that features teams of inventive students who are tasked with designing, building, and testing new contraptions. In the team Kelly's specialty is painting. At the beginning of the episode, there is a video of Kelly at her house explaining her love of Robotics and design. Over the course of the next days during the building, Kelly is tasked with painting the Blue Team's picnic table with clouds and sky similar to Disney's Toy Story design. At the end of the episode, Justin Long asks her "What do you think will happen" (gesturing to the boulder and table). Kelly responds with a witty remark about how her prediction is that the boulder will wreak the entire table.  
===Personality===
A sweet teenage girl from Los Angeles, CA.  
===Physical appearance===
Standing at 5'2 she is short and bubbly with a slim figure. 
===Powers and abilities===
Painting clouds and woodworking.
She appeared in the Netflix series The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina and the Award-winning Netflix movie Birdbox.
