'''Kingrobot''' is a giant robotic combination of robots in the likeness of [[Mickey Mouse]], [[Minnie Mouse]], [[Donald Duck]], [[Daisy Duck]], [[Goofy]], [[Pluto]] and [[Steamboat Willie |Steamboat Willie]].

==Components==
* Mickey Mouse - Jet Mickey
* Minnie Mouse - Sky Minnie
* Donald Duck - Diver Donald
* Daisy Duck - Aqua Daisy
* Goofy - Land Goofy
* Pluto and his kennel - Dash Pluto and "Inugoya"
* Steamboat Willie - Ace Willie
