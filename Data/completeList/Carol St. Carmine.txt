'''Carol St. Carmine'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/carol.htm</ref> is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
She's a preppie student in [[Millard Fillmore Middle School]], on who [[Jake Long|Jake]] accidentally spilled hot chili. She does not make any another appearance throughout the rest of the series.
