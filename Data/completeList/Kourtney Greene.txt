|quote = |status=Active|parents=Unnamed father<br>Michelle Greene (mother)|partner=[[Howie (High School Musical: The Musical: The Series)|Howie]] (boyfriend)<br>[[Jet]] (possible admirer)}}'''Kourtney Greene''' is one of the main characters in the Disney+ Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series|High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.]]'' She is the daughter of Michelle Greene. She is [[Nini Salazar-Roberts|Nini]]'s best friend. Kourtney was a junior at [[East High School]] in Season 1 and Season 2. In Season 1, Kourtney was the Costume Designer/Make-Up Artist for [[East High School]]'s fall production of ''[[High School Musical]]''. Kourtney filled in for [[Gina Porter]] by playing [[Taylor McKessie]] last minute in Act One when Gina wasn't there in Opening Night. In Season 2, Kourtney was casted as [[Mrs. Potts]] in [[East High School]]'s spring production of ''[[Beauty and the Beast (musical)|Beauty and the Beast]]''. In Season 3, she was cast as [[Elsa]] in Camp Shallow Lake's production of [[Frozen (musical)|''Frozen'']].

==Background==

===Development===
Dara Reneé who plays Kourtney originally auditioned for every character the casting director liked her so much they written Kourtney just for her. Originally Kourtney was suppose to be in only one episode then she ended up being one of the main characters.<ref>[https://www.thethings.com/high-school-musical-disney-plsy-kourtney-unknown-facts/]</ref>

===Personality===
Kourtney is obsessed with makeup tutorials and feminism in equal measure. Nini’s ride-or-die BFF, Kourtney also works on the costume crew of ''High School Musical''.<ref>[https://d23.com/meet-cast-high-school-musical-musical-series/ Meet the Cast of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series - D23]</ref>

In Season 1 she is mainly only Nini's sassy black friend she is very supportive, and is always there for her friends despite the fact that Nini is rarely there for her. She's also a feminist, and it is known that she loves make-up and clothes. She is dramatic, fun-loving, helpful, creative and supportive. In Season 2 she starts to do more things for herself like getting a job and auditioning for the musical.

===Physical appearance===
Kourtney is an African American girl with brown eyes, brown naturally curly hair and dark skin.

==Biography==

===Early Life===
Kourtney Greene was born sometime between 2002-2003 to Michelle Greene. At some point before seventh grade Kourtney met [[Nini Salazar-Roberts]]. In the fall of 2017, Kourtney started her freshmen year at [[East High School]].
