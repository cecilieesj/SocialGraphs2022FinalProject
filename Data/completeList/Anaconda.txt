||voice = [[Vanessa Marshall]]}}'''Blanche Sitznski''' (a.k.a. '''Anaconda''') is a [[Marvel Cinematic Universe|Marvel]] character and supervillainess from ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''. She is a member of the Serpent Society.
==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
Anaconda and the Serpent Society, led by the evil [[King Cobra]], battled [[Avengers|the Avengers]] in a subway. Unfortunately, [[Henry Pym|Hank Pym]]'s paficist decisions cause the Serpent Society to escape.

In the wake of the [[Skrull]] invasion, Anaconda and the rest of the Serpent Society face [[Captain America]] and [[Spider-Man]] in order to free King Cobra and [[Viper]].
