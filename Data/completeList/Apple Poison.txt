Apple Poison is a character from [[Tokyo DisneySea]] 's Halloween Time. He is the underling/minion/subordinate of the [[Evil Queen]] from Disney's [[Snow White and the Seven Dwarves|Snow White&nbsp;and the Seven Dwarves]]. He is the personification of the [[Poisoned Apple|poisoned red apple]] that the [[Evil Queen]] offered [[Snow White]].
