'''Christine''' is a character from the [[Disney+]] original series, ''[[Zootopia+]]''. She is a shrew who is one of [[Fru Fru]]'s friends.

==Background==
Christine is a shrew who wears a light blue dress and dark blue pants. She also wears a pair of teal-colored earrings on both of her ears.

==Role in the series==
In "[[The Real Rodents of Little Rodentia]]", [[Fru Fru]] approaches Christine and the rest of her friends to know who would be the Shrew of Honor to which the conversation is interrupted when [[Tru Tru]] arrives at Fru Fru's mansion which Christine and the rest of Fru Fru's friends react to see Tru Tru enter her mansion. Soon as Tru Tru enters the mansion, she and the rest of Fru Fru's friends observe Tru Tru talking to her cousin. Soon as Brianca attempts to approach Tru Tru, Christine and Charisma both hold her back. Christine then comments on Tru Tru where she thinks that Tru Tru is not related to Fru Fru. Soon as Fru Fru interviews about her not being interested in Tru Tru exploring the dining room in the mansion, Christine comments that she Fru Fru might engage in a shrew fight with Tru Tru which Mandy replies that she likes Tru Tru. Christine then tells Fru Fru that she could have picked Tru Tru. Soon as Charisma suggests going into Klawfeld, Christine, Fru Fru, and the rest of her friends arrive at Klawfeld.

Soon as Fru Fru wears a wedding dress at Klawfeld, Christine gives a negative reaction to Fru Fru's dress, but later changes her reaction to a positive one. Later when Tru Tru approaches Fru Fru which the latter confronts her and berates her, Christine and the rest of Fru Fru's friends react to Fru Fru fighting her cousin which Tru Tru furiously leaves in a fit of rage. Later, Christine and the rest of Fru Fru's friend go shopping.

Later during Fru Fru's wedding, Christine and the rest of Fru Fru's friends are present with Fru Fru preparing for her wedding to which as Fru Fru throws her bouquet of night howlers, the effect caused by the Night Howlers results in Christine and the rest of Fru Fru's friends in a side effect putting them into savage mode. It is unknown of what happened to her after that.

==Trivia==
*Christine's voice actress, Porsha Williams, is a reality television actress. Fittingly, the episode "The Real Rodents of Rodentia" where Christine appeared at is presented in the style of a reality television show.
