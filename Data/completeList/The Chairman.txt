'''The Chairman''' is the main antagonist in season 1 of the series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''. He is the tyrannical ruler of the Ghost World and the mysterious head of [[the Ghost Council]].

==Background==
===Personality===
The Chairman is described as an intimidating, sadistic evil spirit who enjoys bringing pain and misery to humans and punishing his fellow ghosts who fail at their jobs.

He doesn't talk, but he's able to show emotions through facial expressions such as giving Scratch an irritated look.

===Physical appearance===
The Chairman resembles an actual spirit, unlike the other ghost made of ectoplasm. His hands are really made of bones and his face resembles that of a skull.

He also is the tallest of all the ghosts.

===Powers and abilities===
He has the power to summon the Flow of Failed Phantoms and travel to human world like all the other ghost.
