'''General Tesler''' is the main antagonist of ''[[Tron: Uprising]]''.

==Personality==
General Tesler is a cold ruthless General Program serving Clu who intends to takeover Argon and the Grid. He shows no problem derezzing his own men should if they say the Renegade is Tron. He is shown to have a disliked towards [[Dyson]], he is even shown to have nightmares of the Renegade freeing Argon should he failed.
==Background==
Leading [[CLU 2.0]]'s forces in [[Argon City]], he tries to ensure control of the city and seeks to stamp out any resistance, coming up against [[Beck|the Renegade]].
