|home = Woodland Valley}}'''Ms. Henrietta Vanderpeen''' is an ostrich and the editor of ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Woodland_House_Wonderful Woodland House Wonderful]'', a magazine of interest to residents of [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Woodland_Valley Woodland Valley]. She was performed by [[Vicki Kenderes-Eibner]].

She paid a visit to the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Big_Blue_House Big Blue House] in "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_308:_Woodland_House_Wonderland Woodland House Wonderful]" to take photographs. Ms. Vanderpreen speaks very properly, but also likes to have fun. She was recycled from [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Lydia_the_Ostrich Lydia the Ostrich] from ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/The_Animal_Show The Animal Show]''.
