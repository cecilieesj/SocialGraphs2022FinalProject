[[File:Shiloh.png|thumb]]'''Shiloh''' is a character from the animated [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[Fish Hooks]]''. She is voiced by [[Vanessa Marshall]].
==History==
She is [[Angela Fishberger|Angela]]'s crazy best friend and [[Milo the Fish|Milo]]'s girlfriend ("[[Guys' Night Out]]"). It's possible that she and Angela are sisters, similar to how Milo (Shiloh's male counterpart) and [[Oscar the Fish|Oscar]] (Angela's male counterpart) are brothers.
==Appearance==
*"[[Guys' Night Out]]" (First appearance)
==Trivia==
*Her name is a combination of "she" and "Milo".
*Her name could also be an allusion to the titular character from the novel ''[[wikipedia:Shiloh (Naylor novel)|Shiloh]]''.
