'''Harper Schapira''' is a recurring character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]].'' She is the daughter of Margaret Goodwin and Mr. Schapira. She is currently a student at Westbrook School for Girls.

==Background==
===Personality===
According to Carolyn Smith, Harper is really smart and Sophisticated.<ref>TCKS</ref> Harper is very ambitious, artistic, creative, dramatic, energetic, talented, annoying and manipulative.
===Physical appearance===
Harper has blue eyes, long blonde hair and light skin.

==Biography==
===Early Life===
Harper Schapira was born to Margaret Goodwin and Mr. Schapira. Her mother Miss Goodwin had her keep her father’s last name so she wouldn’t “upstage” her.<ref>Everything To Me</ref>

===Season One===
====TCKS====
Harper is doing a Video Profile that she refers to as her "Scha-Profile" on Coach Korn and interviews the whole Westbrook School Basketball Team for her video to learn more about him something that she does for every teacher at Westbrook School for Girls. Then Marvyn Korn enters his office to drop something off, Marvyn finds Harper with her camera set up on a tripod ready to interview him for her "Scha-Profile" for the school's website. It’s not the first time he’s tried to reschedule and when he tries again, she shows him her phone. Principal Sherilyn Thomas is on a video call and tells him to do the interview now, reminding him that she pushed for his daughter Emma to enroll at Westbrook. Marvyn Korn wraps up his interview by telling Harper that he’s happy to be part of the Westbrook community then he goes back to practice after Harper is done interviewing him.

The next day Harper meets Marvyn Korn's daughter Emma Korn on her first day at Westbrook in Ms. Grint's Political Science Class. Harper approaches Emma Korn after she walks out Principal Sherilyn Thomas office introducing herself. Then Harper asks Emma Korn if she can do an interview for her Video Profile on Emma's father Marvyn Korn, begging her to have her story represented as the daughter of the new Basketball Coach. She says that she doesn't want to do right now, but Harper adds “Let me know when you change your mind.”

==Trivia==

*She has the same last name as Leslie Schapira one of the writers for [[Big Shot (TV series)|''Big Shot'']].
*She is Queer.
* She is in the musical ''Beth MacBeth'' with Lucas Gruzinsky and Emma Korn.
* She is one of the several people that went to Emma Korn's Housewarming Party along with Olive Cooper, Samantha Finkman, Louise Gruzinsky, Carolyn Smith, Destiny Winters, Dylan, Lucas Gruzinsky and Jake Matthews.
* It was revealed in Everything to Me that her mother is Miss Goodwin.
