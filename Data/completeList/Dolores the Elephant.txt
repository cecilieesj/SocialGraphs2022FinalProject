}}'''Dolores''' is an elephant who appears in ''[[Tiger Trouble]]'', ''[[The Big Wash]]'' and ''[[Working for Peanuts]]'', plus the ''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]'' short ''[[Around the World in Eighty Days]]''. She also made a cameo in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[The Money Vanishes]]", where she is shown to have a calf.
