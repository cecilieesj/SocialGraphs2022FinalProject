'''Councilor Omina'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/dragons/omina.htm</ref> is a minor character from the animated [[Disney]] television series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.
==History==

Councilor Omina is the dragon of Atlantis. Her dragon form resembles a large aqua blue dragon. She wears a yellow tiara and wrist plates. Her mane is the same yellow as her tiara and she has long black hair. Her underbelly is a slightly lighter blue compared to her scales. Her wings are similar to most dragons, having an arm-and-fingers bone structure. The outside of her wings match the blue of her scales, but the inside of her wings match the lighter blue of her underbelly.Â Initially, she wore mostly green and had blue hair, while later she wore mostly blue and had black hair.
