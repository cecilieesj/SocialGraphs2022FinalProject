The '''Bonecruncher''' is one of the nine man-eating giants that live in Giant Country. He has no hair and appears to have the darkest skin tone out of all the giants. He along with the [[Fleshlumpeater]], [[Bloodbottler]], [[Butcherboy|Butcher Boy]], [[Childchewer]], [[Meatdripper]], [[Gizzardgulper]], [[Manhugger]], and [[Maidmasher]] enjoy snatching and gobbling up little children while [[The BFG (character)|The BFG]] delivers dreams to them.
