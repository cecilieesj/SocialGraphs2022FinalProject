[[File:Flip_and_Flap.jpg|thumb]]'''Flip​''' and '''Flap''' are a comedy duo who appear in some episodes of ''[[Dumbo's Circus]]'' as part of the side-show. Flip & Flap are voiced by Will Ryan and Jim Cummings.

Flip has bunny ears, and Flap has a pig-like nose and ears.

Whereas most of the main characters are portrayed by people in costumes, Flip and Flap are portrayed by puppeteers in a similar fashion to the Muppets.
