'''Jack''' is a character based of the character from "Jack and the Beanstalk" who appears as one of the six tritagonists of the live-action musical fantasy film ''[[Into the Woods]]''.

==Background==
Jack is an absent-minded and carefree boy who trades his treasured cow, [[Milky White]], for five magic beans.
