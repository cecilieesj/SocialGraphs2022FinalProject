'''Walter "Skunky" Beaumont''' (voiced by Doug Preis) is a character from ''[[Doug]]''. He is a typical slacker surfer dude, with a voice that is reminiscent of the ''Fast Times at Ridgemont High'' character Jeff Spicoli. In the Nickelodeon series, he was commonly mentioned, but never seen (even though he was heard offscreen in the episode "Doug Didn't Do It").

== Appearance ==
Skunky has blonde hair, orange skin and freckles.
