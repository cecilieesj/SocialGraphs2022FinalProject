'''Sam McKeane''' is a character of ''[[Atlantis: Milo's Return]]''.

== Description ==
He's an old friend of [[Preston B. Whitmore|Mr. Whitmore]]. He found an Atlantean medallion in a pot in his hut, before being attacked by Sand Coyotes. He is then put in the clinic where he is taken care of by his nurse. When [[Milo Thatch|Milo]] and his friends go to inspect his hut, they see that the pot is missing, but McKeane tells them that it must have been stolen by [[Ashtin Carnaby]].
