'''Dopey Davis''' is a character in the [[1945]] [[Donald Duck]] cartoon, ''[[Duck Pimples]]''. He is a criminal and one of the ones who are responsible about the theft of [[Pauline]]'s pearls. When [[Paddy the Cop]] caught him, Davis sold Donald to him.

==DTV Monster Hits==
In [[DTV|DTV Monster Hits]], an original animation had him make a cameo, along with [[Leslie J. Clark]] and [[Pauline]], talking to [[Witch Hazel (Trick or Treat)|Witch Hazel]] before Daryl Hall's "Dreamtime" started.
