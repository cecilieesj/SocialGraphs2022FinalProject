|fate = Gets destroyed when kicked to the ceiling}}'''Cabby''' is an aging head of [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Cabbage cabbage] who appears in the ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[[When Food Goes Bad]]".

Although seemingly inanimate, Cabby is [[General Chow]]'s best friend and confidant. He comes to a tragic end when [[Robbie Sinclair]] knocks him to the ceiling, reducing Cabby to a shower of salad fixings.
