|shows = ''[[The Muppets (TV series)|The Muppets]]''}}'''Calico''' is a pinkish monocle-wearing character who appears in the film, ''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]'' as a pirate. He also appears in the video game, which is where his name comes from.

==History==
He first appears in the "[[Sailing for Adventure]]" number singing a solo in the first verse. He also appears in the "[[Cabin Fever]]" number, where he and an unnamed character take a verse. One of the pirates pulls a knife on him during "[[A Professional Pirate]]". Yet, he is still alive in the next scene. He also appears in the Muppet Treasure Island Sing Along performed by another puppeteer.

He reappears in ''[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]'' as one of the Flying Monkeys, along with several of his fellow pirates.

He also appears in ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]'' as a [[Gulag 38B]] inmate, and first appears in the song "[[Working in the Coal Mine]]", assisting [[Animal (Muppet)|Animal]], [[Fozzie Bear|Fozzie]], and [[Walter]] to dig a tunnel for [[Kermit the Frog|Kermit]].

==Trivia==
*He bears a striking resemblance to [[w:c:muppets:Count von Count|Count von Count]] from the children's puppet show ''[[wikipedia:Sesame Street|Sesame Street]]''.
