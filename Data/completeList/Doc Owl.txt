'''Doc Owl''' is a kindly owl who appears infrequently on ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. He was performed by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Jim_Kroupa Jim Kroupa]. He's fond of making standard owl-type quips, i.e. "whoooo's there?"

He is a recycled version of a background snowy owl puppet from "[https://muppet.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Willowby%27s_Christmas_Tree Mr. Willowby's Christmas Tree]" and ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/The_Animal_Show The Animal Show]''.
