|status = Active}}'''Aquanette "Aqua" Walker''', is one of the main characters from ''[[The Cheetah Girls]]'' series. She has a twin sister, Anginette "Angie" Walker, who does not appear in the films. She has a warm personality, and is strong-willed and speaks her mind. Her interests include horror movies, animals, and one day becoming a doctor. She is also interested in fashion, history, and acting. She is portrayed by [[Kiely Williams]].

==Background==
Aqua and her twin moved with their father to New York from Texas, and had a southern accent in the first film. She has three pets: two guinea pigs and a dog. Aqua is very religious, and attends church every Sunday.

===Physical Appearance===
Aqua has dark brown skin and brown eyes. Her hair is dark brown, and in the third film, black.
