'''Wolfie''' is the Hauntley's family dog in ''[[Vampirina]]'', who can turn into a werewolf during a full moon or whenever he drinks milk. He is a small dog with long purple and white fur that covers his legs, and when he is a werewolf he has a humanoid wolf appearance with a huge snout, and stands on two feet.
