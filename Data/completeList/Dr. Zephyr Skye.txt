'''Dr. Zephyr Skye''' (better known as '''Dr. Z''') is a top meteorologist and a storm-chaser who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. She is considered amongst the best in meteorology and as well the bravest. Her mission is to chase storms all over the galaxy and study them up close.

==Background==
===Physical Appearance===
Dr. Z has slender light blue skin figure with pointy ears. She has short purple hair, along with purple eyelashes and eyebrows. He eyes are dark blue and her lips tufts Blue. Her outfit includes a black and orange jacket and pants with pads on the knees and shoulders. She wears orange over the knee high boots, and a violet top underneath her jacket. Around her waist a silver utility belt.

==Trivia==
*Ginger Zee, who provides the voice of Dr. Zephyr Skye, is best known as the chief meteorologist on ''Good Morning America'' and ''[[ABC]] News''.
