'''Alectryon''' is a rooster who is [[Philoctetes|Phil]]'s former student, who chooses to fall asleep while on duty. He appears in ''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]'', the TV series.

==Role in the Series==
His first appearance in the show is in the episode "[[Hercules and the All Nighter]]", where [[Hercules]] makes multiple attempts to rely on his help to wake up [[Morpheus (Hercules)|Morpheus]], to which the rooster refuses. <span style="line-height:1.5em;">Meanwhile, at the same time, </span>[[Hades]]<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> dispatches his two demon minions, </span>[[Pain and Panic]],<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> to find a rooster that looks just like him. However, all the roosters look the same to them. After </span>[[Hades]]<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> grows frustrated with the time they're wasting, he decides to go see Alectryon himself. However, he is not too happy to see Hercules</span><span style="line-height:1.5em;"> there as well, and tries to strike a deal with the rooster. After his attempt fails, he grows frustrated, however despite this, he leaves the rooster with an omelet. </span>
