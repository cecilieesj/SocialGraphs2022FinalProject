|powers = Flight}}'''Vern''' is a minor character from the [[Disney Junior]] show ''[[Goldie & Bear]]'' who first appeared in the episode "[[Fairy Fly Adventure]]". He was an inchworm who was trying to get to the Fairy Fly Tree so he could become a fairy fly. [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie]] and [[Bear (Goldie & Bear)|Bear]] helped him get there by sundown. Vern later appeared in "[[Team Tiny]]", when Goldie and Bear teamed up with him, [[Itsy]], and [[Brian (Goldie and Bear)|Brian]] to retrieve [[Mama Bear]]'s ring from the Mouse King.
