'''Chicken Boy''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC yellow [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Duck Duck] who owns [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Pea_Soup_Cafe Pea Soup Cafe] on [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Walrus_Way Walrus Way], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/The_Brrrgh The Brrrgh]. He will have a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontask Toontask] that will reward Toons a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Laff Laff] Boost. He wears a yellow-sleeved and green shirt with a football on it.

==Toontask==
1. Visit Chicken Boy on Walrus Way, The Brrrgh.

2. Recover a Soccer Ball from one of the following [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs Cogs]:
*[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Head_Hunter Head Hunter] (Anywhere)
*[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Spin_Doctor Spin Doctor] (Anywhere)
*[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Money_Bags Money Bags] (Anywhere)
*[http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Two-Face Two-Face] (Anywhere)

3. Return Soccer Ball to Chicken Boy.

'''Reward:''' +1 Laff Boost

==Trivia==
*Wearing a Soccer Ball T-Shirt and having to recover a Soccer Ball from one of the Cogs may indicate his passion for Soccer.
*He wears an unreleased T-Shirt, meaning no Toon has the yellow-sleeved and green soccer ball T-shirt.
