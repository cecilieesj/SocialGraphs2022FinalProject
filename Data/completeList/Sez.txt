[[File:Hidden_worlds_sez.jpg|right]]'''Sez''' is the main antagonist from the online game [[Hidden Worlds]].

==Background==
He was created by The Inklings as a creature for [[Chrona]] to defeat. He then became out of hand and began to corrupt the different Disney worlds attempting to make a real body for himself.
