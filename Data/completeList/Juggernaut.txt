'''Juggernaut''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who is a villain with superhuman strength. Depending on the version, he may be a mutant or a normal human empowered by the magical Gem of Cyttorak.

Juggernaut was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Television Appearances==
===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===
Juggernaut caused rampage around New York and encountered [[Spider-Man]] for several days, but with his sheer strength was to overcome Spider-Man's webbings and escape. Juggernaut was in hiding and sat in a train where he encountered Spider-Man who was trying to stop [[Awesome Android]] . Juggernaut ambushed Spider-Man and fought against him and the other trainees. He was eventually defeated when his clothing was eaten by Awesome Android and was knocked unconscious by Spider-Man.

==Trivia==
*He, [[Wolverine]], [[Deadpool]],  [[Arcade]], and [[Sabretooth ]] are the only [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]] characters to appear in any of the Disney XD/Marvel animated shows.
*The character was portrayed by Vinnie Jones in X Men: The Last Stand and later in voice and motion capture by Ryan Reynolds in Deadpool 2 who also portrays the title character.
