'''Jack Johnson''' is a minor character in ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. He is the father of [[Jeremy Johnson]] and [[Suzy Johnson]]. He is the husband of [[Mrs. Johnson]], brother of Joe Johnson, and the son of [[Hildegard Johnson]].
==Present life==
Jack Johnson enjoys life with his wife Mrs. Johnson and his two kids, Jeremy and Suzy. He doesn't see his son very often due to his job at Mr. Slushy Burger. He enjoys watching his college sports team, the Polecats. Since his family is from Wisconsin, it is possible that the Polecats are a Wisconsin college sport team. It is unknown what sport they play or at what college.         

==Physical appearance==
Jack Johnson has blue eyes and blond hair, much like his son Jeremy, his daughter Suzy, and his wife.   

==Trivia==
*He shares a name of singer and musician [[Wikipedia:Jack Johnson|Jack Johnson]].
*It is shown that he likes to wear the same style shirt as his son, [[Jeremy Johnson|Jeremy]].
