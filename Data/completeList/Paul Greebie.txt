'''Paul Greebie''' is the school's guidance counsellor, from the show Life with Derek. 

==Character Description==
Paul Greebie is Casey's laid-back guidance counselor. Casey's constantly goes to him for advice when she gets stressed. Which is very often. He really doesn't do much. Although Casey has asked him, he won't tell her what to do, only give suggestions.Most of the time Casey will ask him questions and then immediately answer them herself. Once, when tipping his chair back to far, he fell and landed in the trash can. He is also very disorganized, and is also the computer/science teacher in addition to being Guidance Counselor.
