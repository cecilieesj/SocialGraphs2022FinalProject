==Role in the film==

In his version of the story, Jabber was to have been the one that reported the news of the ball to [[Bruno (Cinderella)|Bruno]], [[Major (Cinderella)|Major]], [[Birds (Cinderella)|the birds]], and the other barnyard animals. In some of his sketches, a beautiful white dove is present, suggesting that he was to have a love interest.
