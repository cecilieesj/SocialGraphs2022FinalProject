The '''Hot Dogs '''are characters in [[The Karnival Kid]]. They bark with [[Mickey Mouse]] during [[The Hoochie Coochie Dance]].

== Trivia ==
* The name has a pun. It's called Hot '''Dog''' because the hot dogs bark like a dog.[[File:The Hotdogs.png|thumb|220x220px]]
