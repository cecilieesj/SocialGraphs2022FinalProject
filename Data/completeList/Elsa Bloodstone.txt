'''Elsa Bloodstone''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who is a female monster-hunter. She made her [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] debut in ''[[Werewolf by Night (film)|Werewolf by Night]]''.

Elsa Bloodstone was created by Dan Abnett, Andy Lanning, Scott Hanna and Michael Lopez.
