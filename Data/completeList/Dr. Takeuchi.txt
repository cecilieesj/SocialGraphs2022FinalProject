'''Dr. Takeuchi''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]]''.

==History==
He was one of the human scientists who worked on the Prometheus Project and assisted in the creation of the [[Super Robot]]. His friend Professor Maezono wanted to transfer his consciousness into the Prometheus Five model, thus allowing him to live forever as the ultimate super-weapon. Dr. Takeutchi attempted to stop him but was killed in the process.
