'''Vanessa''' is a character from ''[[The Replacements]]''. She was voiced by [[Bonnie Wright]].

==Role in the series==
She was a student in the Royal Spy Academy and the thief of [[the Spyclopedia]], along with [[Clyde]]. But after [[Dick Daring|Dick]] shot one of Clyde's wings right in front of a rising draw bridge, (they wouldn't make across) he kicked her out of him and drove away from her. While the gang chased after Clyde, Car drove on a puddle and it splashed on Vanessa.
