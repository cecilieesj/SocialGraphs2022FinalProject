'''Ducklas "Sty" Styvesant''' is a character from the [[PKNA]] comic book. He is a famous movie producer and director in 23rd century.

Sty brings [[the Duck Avenger]] from the Twentieth Century, making him believe there is an invasion of Super-[[Evronians]], when he actually is filming a series and wants the hero to star in it. However, the Evronian animatronics are sabotaged and try to kill the Duck Avenger for real. {{Stub}}{{DEFAULTSORT:Styvesant, Ducklas}}
