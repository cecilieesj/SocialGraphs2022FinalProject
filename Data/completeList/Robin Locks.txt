|image = Robin Locks.png}}'''Robin Locks''' is [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie]]'s father in ''[[Goldie & Bear]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[When Goldie Met Bear]]".

==History==
In the episode, he, [[Mama Bear]] and [[Papa Bear]] tell the story of the meeting of Goldie and [[Bear (Goldie & Bear)|Bear]]. It is explained that he was sent out first with Goldie to find a location for their home. He invited everyone in Fairy Tale Woods to a house warming party.Â  During the party a notice came, saying that they where in the wrong woods. He replied back, telling [[Mrs. Locks]] that the plans have changed and that they are moving to Fairy Tale Woods. He and his wife appear more in Season 2.

==Trivia==
* His name resembles Robin of Locksley, better known as [[Robin Hood (character)|Robin Hood]]. This similarity was elaborated upon in [[Goldie's Do-Over Day]] when Robin's wife [[Mrs. Locks]] was revealed to have the first name Marian, like [[Maid Marian]].
