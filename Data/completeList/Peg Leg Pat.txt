'''Peg Leg Pat''' is a minor villain from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
Peg Leg Pat was once a Civil War soldier who died. He is part of [[Shackles Jack|Shackles Jack's]] gang in ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]'' in "[[A Ghost Story]]" who haunt [[Camp Mugwomp]].
