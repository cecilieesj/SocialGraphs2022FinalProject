|quote = |status=Active|parents=Mr. Mazzara (father)|partner=[[Miss Jenn]] (co-worker/crush/friend)}}'''Benjamin Mazzara''' mostly known as '''Mr. Mazzara''' is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''. Mr. Mazzara is a S.T.E.M Teacher<ref>[[The Auditions]]</ref> at [[East High School]] who teaches Engineering. Mr. Mazzara runs the Robotics and A.V. Club at [[East High School]].

==Background==
Mr. Mazzara is [[East High School|East High’]]<nowiki/>s somewhat stiff S.T.E.M. teacher who believes in preparing students for the future, which leads him to butt heads with the daydreaming [[Miss Jenn]].<ref>[https://d23.com/meet-cast-high-school-musical-musical-series/ Meet the Cast of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series - D23]</ref>

===Personality===
Mr. Mazzara is tough, smart and strong-willed. He is adamant on promoting his robotics club to the student in order to gain enough members to prevent it from termination.

Despite having a general disliking for the Drama Club and its teacher, Mr. Mazzara has a more compassionate side to him that he unveils only under certain circumstances. That being said, he is a tough egg to crack.

== Biography ==

=== Early Life ===
Benjamin Mazzara dream school was originally California Tech because they have a great Robotics program but, after applying multiple times he never got accepted. At some point before the events of Season 1, Mr. Mazzara got a job as a S.T.E.M. Teacher at [[East High School]] and has been working there ever since.
