The '''Pegasi Siblings''' are a batch of pegasi foals who appeared in the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" segment of the [[1940]] film ''[[Fantasia]]''. They are the energetic offspring of a [[Mother Pegasus|white pegasus]] and [[Father Pegasus|black pegasus]], and frequent with the other mythical fauna of their home.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
The pegasi are small winged foals with light muzzles and blue eyes. With the exception of their [[Peter Pegasus|youngest sibling]], each one has a pastel color - pink, yellow, blue, and orange - with their manes and tails being a darker shade of their color scheme.
