}}'''Agent Kwan Chen''' is a secondary character appearing only in the ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' episode "[[Girl in the Flowered Dress]]" and is portrayed by [[Tzi Ma]]. He is an agent for the [[Hong Kong]] branch of [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]

== History ==
He was tasked with keeping tabs on [[Scorch (Marvel)|Scorch]], who possesses pyrokinetic powers. When Scorch is kidnapped, Kwan, [[Phil Coulson|Coulson]], and [[Melinda May|May]] infiltrate the lab Scorch is in but because Scorch is under [[Extremis]]' effects he kills Kwan with a fireball through the chest.{{DEFAULTSORT:Chen, Kwan}}
