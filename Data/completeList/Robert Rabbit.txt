|quote = "We rabbits are a lot alike!"}}'''Robert Rabbit''' is the brother of [[Rabbit]] who appears in ''[[Welcome to Pooh Corner]]''.

==Personality==
Robert Rabbit is shown to be similar to [[Rabbit]], with a few differences - he wears a black hat, glasses, black bowtie, and a vest.

==Television appearances==
===''[[Welcome to Pooh Corner]]''===
Robert first appeared in the episode ''[[Hello, Hello There]]'', in which Piglet was nervous about meeting him.
