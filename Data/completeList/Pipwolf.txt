'''Pipwolf''' is a character from the ''[[X-Mickey]]'' comic book series from Italy.

Bearing a strong resemblance to [[Goofy]], he is a dimension hopping werewolf that serves as a guide to [[Mickey Mouse]] on his supernatural travels.
