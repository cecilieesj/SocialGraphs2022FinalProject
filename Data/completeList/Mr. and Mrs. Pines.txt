'''Mr. and Mrs. Pines''' are minor characters who are mentioned in the series ''[[Gravity Falls]]''. They are the parents of [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]] and [[Mabel Pines]].

In the pilot episode of the series, "[[Tourist Trapped]]", Dipper states that his parents sent him and Mabel to Gravity Falls, Oregon for summer vacation for them to get some "fresh air".

Their mother was mentioned in "[[The Inconveniencing]]", where it was revealed that she used to dress Dipper up in a lamb costume and make him do the "lamby dance".

They were mentioned in "[[Gideon Rises]]", when Grunkle Stan lied to their father on the phone that Dipper and Mabel were safe and were staying at a five-star hotel.
