'''Geoff''' is a supporting character in the series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
Geoff is a cheery dimwitted ghost, who unlike Scratch, is very sociable. He sees himself as Scratch's friend, while Scratch sees him as an "Annoying Co-Worker".

===Physical appearance===
Geoff is a green ghost with a large nose, yellow teeth, orange sclera, black pupils, a double chin, a large upper lip, and bald except for the hair on the sides of his scalp. 

===Powers and abilities===
He has standard ghost powers, such as floating and invisibility. Like Scratch, Geoff is able to morph his body into any shape at will.
