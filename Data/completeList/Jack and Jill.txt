{{Infobox character|image = Profile - Jack and Jill.jpg|shorts = ''[[Mother Goose Melodies]]''|alignment = Good|shows = ''[[Dumbo's Circus]]''<br>''[[Once Upon a Time]]''|status = Active}}'''Jack and Jill''' are the Mother Goose characters who ran up the hill only to fall down again in the 1931 ''[[Silly Symphony]]'','' [[Mother Goose Melodies]]''.

They appear in ''[[Once Upon a Time]]'' portrayed by Nick Hunnings and Tammy Gillis,where it is revealed in a flashback that they had their child stolen from [[Rumplestiltskin]] in a way for to attract the Black Fairy. [[Belle]] manages to bring the baby back to them.
