|inspiration=Kevin Kingston-Persons from ''Are We There Yet?''|family=Unnamed grandmother}}'''Michael Evers''' is [[Jim Evers|Jim]] and [[Sara Evers|Sara]]'s son, [[Megan Evers|Megan]]'s brother, and one of the two main tritagonists in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[2003]] comedy/horror film, ''[[The Haunted Mansion (2003 film)|The Haunted Mansion]]''. He has arachnophobia, the fear of spiders.

==Role in the film==
The Evers is going on a vacation but they get stuck in the Gracey Manor because of a heavy rainstorm. That night he is playing with a music box and sees a ghost orb. He and [[Megan Evers|Megan]] follow it to the attic where they see a picture of [[Elizabeth Henshaw|Elizabeth]], [[Master Gracey|Master Edward Gracey]]'s fiancee, who looks just like their mother. [[Ezra]] and [[Emma (The Haunted Mansion)|Emma]] quickly hide them before [[Ramsley]] can catch them. They go with their dad to the mausoleum, where he is left by himself to guard the door. When Jim and Megan try to leave, the door shuts in front of them and spiders begin crawling on the outside of the door. He soon faces his fear and lets them out. He and Megan get trapped in a box and are hung in the armory hall and Jim fights the knights to save them. In the end, the Evers, [[The Phantom Five|the Phantom Five]], and [[Madame Leota]] drive off into the sunset.
