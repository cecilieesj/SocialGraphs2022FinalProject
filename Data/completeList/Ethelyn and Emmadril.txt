'''Ethelyn''' and '''Emmadril''' are two witches who visit [[Lady Bane]] in her [[Lady Bane's Castle|castle]] in the episode "[[Rocking Chair Bear]]".

After Lady Bane leads them into her living room, they start a conversation about cooking.

Later, as Lady Bane looses her youth that she had previously stolen from [[Sunni Gummi]], the two witches see her running after her [[Troggles]] in the forest.
