'''Wigbert Ribbiton '''is [[One-Eyed Wally]]'s wealthy father. He is voiced by [[Hugh Bonneville]].

== Background ==
=== Personality ===
Wigbert values the life and wealthy status of the upper class, including his family business. He also put a lot expectations onto his son, Wally as he wanted him to take over the family business when he retires.

=== Physical appearance ===
Wigbert is a cyan blue frog with a lighter underbelly with slick back white hair. He has one yellow eye and another one that is actually a ball used for bug-polo which he convers with a monocle. He also wears upper-class styled clothing which consists of a brown shirt and white pants fastened with a black belt, along with black boots with golden buckles.
