'''Markl''' is the apprentice of Howl and the tritagonist in Studio Ghibli's film ''[[Howl's Moving Castle]]''. 

==Appearance==
Markl has ginger hair and brown eyes. Markl wears a green vest and he disguises himself as an old man wizard.

==Personality==
Markl is very obedient, doing what he is being told. Also he gives pointers to [[Sophie Hatter|Sophie]] when she first arrived, making sure to keep her out of trouble.

==Relationships==
===Sophie Hatters===
At first, Markl detests [[Sophie Hatter|Sophie]], thinking she was a witch and also for barging into [[Howl Jenkins Pendragon|Howl]]'s castle. But he quickly grows to appreciate her. When Sophie's mother visits Sophie, Markl insist Sophie not to leave because everyone living in the castle is now a family to him.
