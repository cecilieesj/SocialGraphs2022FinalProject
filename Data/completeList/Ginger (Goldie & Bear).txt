[[Big Bad]] (sometimes)|minions = Broom-my|dislikes = people thinking she is scary,her roof broken|enemies = [[Big Bad]], [[Pops Weasel]]|inspiration = The witch from the Hansel and Gretel fairy tale|voice = Maya Ritter}}'''Ginger''' or the Gingerbread Witch is a friendly witch that lives in [[Fairy Tale Forest(Goldie and Bear)|Fairy Tale Forest]]. In the beginning, kids believed that she is evil and transformed any kid that enters her yard into chocolate statues. Later it is found out that they are decoration and she loves kick ball. When she was younger she was a Kick Ball player. She owns a cat named Sprinkles, as well a bat named Batty and broom named Broom-my. She also works with magic powders that can do anything, and once mixed some together and ended up making Goldie invisible.
