==Trivia==
*He is likely a reference to the [[wikipedia:Fastech 360|FASTECH 360]], based on his shape and retractable air brake ears. He may be based on this type of train in real life.
* His name is French for fast.
*He is very proud of his high speed, and his basis top speed may be 251 Mph (405 km). He loves going very fast, he also imitates his own horn and he blows it and imitates the sound.
