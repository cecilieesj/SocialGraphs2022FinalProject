'''Tinku''' is a minor character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series [[Mira, Royal Detective]]. He is a Camel who resides in Jalpur with Mira and her friends.

== Appearance ==
Tinku is a full grown male Dromedary Camel. He's very strong and has brown eyes with long eyelashes.

== Personality ==
Tinku is very tame, loyal, gentle, lovable and extremely helpful. He loves his friends immensely and is always ready to help them whenever and however he can. He is also shown to be very affectionate, as seen when he is licking Babli and her kittens as they sleep next to him in [[The Mystery of the Fluppy Pillows|The Mystery of the Fluffy Pillows]].
