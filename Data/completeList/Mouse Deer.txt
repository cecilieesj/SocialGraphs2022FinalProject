The '''Mouse Deer''' is a character in the Disney Junior animated series, ''[[The Lion Guard]]''. He is an unnamed mouse deer (hence his name) who lives in the tropical forest alongside other animals.

==Background==
The mouse deer shows a serious personality. Despite him agreeing to help other animals, he is never fond of anyone who points at him.

==Role in the series==
The only time the mouse deer appears is in "[[Mama Binturong]]". During the part where the Guard tries to find Makini's missing staff, the Lion Guard walks across a civet named [[Tompok]] who would know about it to which he explains to talk to the mouse deer. Talking to the mouse deer who would know about Makini's staff, he tells the group to talk to [[Seisou]] living in the cave who knows about it. His further fate is unknonwn.

==Trivia==
*He is the only mouse deer in the [[The Lion King (franchise)|''Lion King'' franchise]].
**Despite this, he is one of the unique species to have a limited amount of screen time.
