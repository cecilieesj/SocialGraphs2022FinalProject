'''Mel Luster''' appeared in the ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "The Mating Dance." He was voiced by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Richard_Portnow Richard Portnow], his body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Jack_Tate Jack Tate] and his face was operated by [[Bruce Lanoil]].

Mel is the owner and sole instructor at [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Mel_Luster%27s_Mating_Dance_Academy Mel Luster's Mating Dance Academy]. He caters to both first-time students and also offers a $29.95 refresher course for old married guys. Mel's expertise leads the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Pangaea Pangaean] zoo to bring caveman [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Ling_Ling_and_Chang_Chang Chang Chang] to his school for emergency lessons.
