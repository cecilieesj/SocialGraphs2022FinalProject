'''Mrs. Anderson''' (also known as "'''Bonnie's mom'''")  is a character in the [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] [[Toy Story (franchise)|''Toy Story'' franchise]], first appearing in ''[[Toy Story 3]]''. She is [[Bonnie Anderson|Bonnie]]'s mother. 

==Appearances==
===''[[Toy Story 3]]''===
She is the receptionist at [[Sunnyside Daycare]]. She is also friends with [[Mrs. Davis]] and first seen when she is donating a box of toys to there. Later on, she is seen coming in the [[Caterpillar Room]] looking for Bonnie to tell her it's time to go home.

When she is seen at the end of the film, she is amazed at how [[Andy Davis|Andy]] has grown up to become a college student as Andy visits [[Bonnie's House]] to give away his toys. After Andy leaves, she takes Bonnie inside for some lunch.

She is last seen during the closing credits carrying another donation box of toys to the [[Butterfly Room]] which includes a [[Emperor Zurg]].

===''[[Toy Story Toons]]''===
===="[[Hawaiian Vacation]]"====
In the first episode of ''Toy Story Toons'', she is briefly heard when Bonnie packs up to go on vacation. Shortly, before [[Barbie]] and [[Ken Carson|Ken]] arrive.

===="[[Small Fry]]"====
Mrs. Anderson is seen taking Bonnie to [[Poultry Palace]].

===="[[Partysaurus Rex]]"====
Mrs. Anderson is heard telling Bonnie it is bathtime. She appears telling Bonnie that there is too much water in the bathtub and decides that they shall go to dinner at Bonnie's grandmother's house instead. She also appears, in the end, talking to the plumber, when the water in the bathtub at [[Rex]]'s party overflowed and flooded the house, which she didn't know what happened.

===''[[Toy Story of Terror!]]''===
Mrs. Anderson and Bonnie are driving along the countryside at night when a bump causes a flat tire, forcing them to go to the [[Sleep Well]] motel. Using the office phone, she calls for a tow truck, only to learn that they won't come until 6am, forcing them to stay the night.

The next morning, the tow truck arrives and replaces Mrs. Anderson's tire. As she and Bonnie began the check-out, the curtain behind [[Ronald Tompkins|the manager]]'s desk came off, revealing Bonnie's missing toys behind a glass cabinet. She reclaims them and Mrs. Anderson calls the police to arrest Ronald for theft before departing.

===''[[Toy Story That Time Forgot]]''===
In the special, Mrs. Anderson is first heard off-screen telling Bonnie to get ready to leave the house. She then is seen bringing her to [[Mason]]'s house.

During the final scenes, she is heard arranging the next time Bonnie will visit Mason, along with his father.
