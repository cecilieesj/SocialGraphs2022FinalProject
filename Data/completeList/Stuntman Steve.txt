'''Stuntman Steve''' is a character from ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. He is a stuntman that loves riding his motorcycle and doing tricks until he loses his helmet, causing his computer chip inside his head to come loose. [[Doc McStuffins (character)|Doc]] fixed him while [[Stuffy]] found his missing helmet.
