'''Trilla Suduri''' is a character in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' universe. She was first introduced in the video game, ''[[Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order]]''.

After the Great Jedi Purge, Trilla joined the [[Galactic Empire]], becoming an inquisitor known as the '''Second Sister'''. She was later executed by [[Darth Vader]] for failure to obtain Eno Cordova's [[Holocron|Jedi holocron]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Trilla Suduri was once a compassionate, caring individual during her time as a Jedi Padawan before Order 66 was commissioned. After her master gave up Trilla's location to the Empire, Trilla ended being tortured where her personality became twisted and dark. Once she became an Inquisitor, Trilla was cruel and ambitious, spending much of her time hunting down any surviving Jedi for the Empire. Trilla was also an exceptionally cunning and intelligent individual.

===Powers and Abilities===
====Powers====
*'''[[The Force]]:''' Trilla has a very strong connection to the Force.
**'''Telekinesis:''' Trilla can telekinetically move objects or other individuals without physically touching them, and she utilized it for offensive or defensive purposes.
***'''Force Push:''' Trilla can telekinetically send her opponents flying through the air, causing serious injury or temporarily incapacitating/knocking them unconscious, depending on how much power is unleashed.
***'''Force Pull:''' Trilla can telekinetically pull her opponents or objects towards him.
**'''Force Drain:''' Trilla can drain life-force energy from other individuals.
**'''Force Dash:''' Trilla can move at amazing speeds.

====Abilities====
*'''Expert Lightsaber Duelist:''' Trilla is a highly skilled lightsaber duelist.

===Weapons and Equipment===
====Lightsaber====
Trilla's valued weapon and possession is her red-bladed lightsaber.
*'''[[Lightsaber|Double-bladed spinning lightsaber]]:''' Trilla had built a red double-bladed spinning lightsaber. It would remain in Trilla's possession until her death at the hands of [[Darth Vader]], but it is unknown what happened to it after Trilla's death.
