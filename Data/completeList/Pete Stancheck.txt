'''Pete Stancheck''' is one of the main protagonists in ''[[Herbie Goes Bananas]]''. He's a race driver and [[Jim Douglas|Jim Douglas']] nephew.

==Background==
Pete and his partner, [[Davy Johns]], arrive n Puerto Vallarta, [[Mexico]] to pick up [[Herbie]] for the Grand Primeo of [[Brazil]]. At first, he shows less confidence in the little car but reluctantly takes possession and becomes his new owner. His opinion soon changes when Herbie shows off his incredible speed while trying to help [[Paco]] escape from three men who are after him.

Boarding a cruise ship he meets a young woman named [[Melissa (The Love Bug)|Melissa]] and they fall for one another. After losing Herbie, he and Davy find a job in Panama but soon go after Paco again when he steals his wallet. With Melissa's help, they commandeer a rundown bus and go after Herbie and Paco, they pick up Aunt Louise Trends and Captain Blythe along the way. In the end he gets Herbie back and starts a relationship with Melissa.
