'''Mrs. Price''' is a minor character of the [[M. Night Shyamalan]] films: ''[[Unbreakable]]'' and ''[[Glass]]''. She is the mother of [[Elijah Price]], who takes care of him right up to his adulthood.

==Appearances==
===''[[Unbreakable]]''===
In 1960, she gave birth to Elijah and learned that his bones break easily. Over the decades, she has given him plenty of comic books to read.
===''[[Glass]]''=== 
16 years after her son got arrested, she believed that a superhero and a supervillain would encounter each other. As her son died from Kevin Crumb's biting, she learned that her son recorded the entire escape that proves that superhumans exist.
