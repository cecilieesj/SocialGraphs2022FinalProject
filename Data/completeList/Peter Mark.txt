'''Peter Mark''' is a character from the [[1992]] Disney film, ''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''. He is a hockey player from Minneapolis, [[Minnesota]]. 

<br />

== Biography ==
Peter was a member of the [[District 5 Ducks]] and played as a defenseman as number 24. He is a player with an attitude and induces conflict; he leads a walkout on Coach Bombay when he overhears a sarcastic comment about the team out of context. Peter is friends with [[Jesse Hall]] and thinks he's a hotshot in his leather jacket. He also has a thing for overhearing things and misinterpreting them. He is portrayed by J.D. Daniels.
<br />

== Trivia ==

* Peter could be considered the biggest troublemaker of the ducks in the first film.
* He does not appear in the sequels nor is he mentioned, like Tammy, Terry, Tommy and Karp.
