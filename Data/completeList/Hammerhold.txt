'''Senator Hammerhold''' is a senator in the Galactic Alliance.

==Physical Appearance==
Senator Hammerhold has green skin, blue eyes, blond hair, and a pair of antennae growing from his head.

==Personality==
He is protective of [[Petra Hammerhold|his daughter]].
