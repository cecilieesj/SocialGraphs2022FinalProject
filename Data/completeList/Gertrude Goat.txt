'''Gertrude Goat''' or '''Gertie Goat''' is an anthropomorphic goat and a supporting character in the ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' comic strips of the 1930s. She is the wife of [[Gideon Goat]] and appeared in many early Mickey Mouse comics, primarily as a background character.
