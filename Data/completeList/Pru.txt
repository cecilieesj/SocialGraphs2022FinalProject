[[File:Pru.jpg|thumb]]'''Pru''' is a popular girl with blonde hair in school. She is in the Disney TV show ''[[The Weekenders]]''. When she has a fight with her friends because they forgot her birthday (again), she tries her hardest to make them jealous by hanging out with the gang. She is disliked by the gang because of her snobby attitude and general rudeness.
