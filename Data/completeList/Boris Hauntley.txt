'''Boris Hauntley''' is a main character in [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Vampirina]]''. He is [[Vampirina Hauntley|Vampirina's]] father and describes him to be "spookily silly" and a huge fan of the monster football team called the Transylvania Trolls.
