'''Sunny''' is a baby sloth from ''[[T.O.T.S.]]. She first appeared in [[Slippery When Wet]], and will later return in [[Some Assembly Required (T.O.T.S.)|Some Assembly Required]]. '' 


== Appearance==
Sunny is a brown and white furred sloth, with four claws on each of her paws. She has blue eyes, a similarly colored bow holding some fur fashioned like a pigtail, a black rounded nose, a yellow collar with the T.O.T.S. logo, and a diaper with both mountain and tree decals.
