'''Professor Rubicon''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. He is an alien scientist who operates the [[Trident Research Station]], and who [[Loretta Callisto|Loretta]] admires. His mentoring of Loretta and scientific aid led to the discovery of the Builders.
==Trivia==
*Professor Rubicon's voice actor, [[Bill Nye]], is best known as the host of the PBS children's science show ''[[Bill Nye the Science Guy]]''.
