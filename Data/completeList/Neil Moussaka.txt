'''Neil Moussaka''' is a character in the attraction [[Food Rocks]] at [[Epcot]].

A parody of Neil Sedaka hidden behind his piano, he performs "[[Vegetables are Good for You]]", a parody of "Breaking Up is Hard To Do".

==Trivia==
* Like other members of Food Rocks' cast, Neil Moussaka's remains were auctioned off on [[Ebay]] and briefly appeared at a [[2010]] Disney fan event at the [[Disneyland Hotel]].
