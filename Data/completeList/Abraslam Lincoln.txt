'''Abraslam Lincoln''' is the name of a wrestler in ''[[The Replacements]]''. He is a parody of [[Abraham Lincoln|President Abraham Lincoln]] and is extremely large and strong. He has a bitter rivalry with Canadian Canucklehead because he is married to his ex-girlfriend Lady Lady. He was replaced with [[Todd Daring]] by him (Todd).

==Wrestling Moves==
*'''Executive Power Slam:''' A move where he jumps miles and miles into the air and smash them into the ground. Used once against [[Donny Rottweiler]].

==''[[The Replacements]]''==
Abraslam appeared in the episode "[[Serf's Up]]", in which he was about to face off against Canadian Canucklehead, before [[Riley Daring|Riley]] interrupted the show with ''The Magnificent Princess''.
