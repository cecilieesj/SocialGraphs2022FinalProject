'''Black Knight''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character. He appears in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] film ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by Kit Harington

He is the boyfriend of [[Sersi]] and friend of the Eternals. He later finally accepts to wield the sword known as the Ebony Blade which is connected to the history of his family.

Black Knight was created by Roy Thomas and John Buscema.
