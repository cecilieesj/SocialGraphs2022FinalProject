==Personality==
Circe desperately searches [[Greece]] for romance, but her high standards usually lead to any potential suitors being transformed into animals.

==Role in the series==
Circe appears in the episode ''Hercules and the Song of Circe''.

She arrives and decides to make [[Icarus]] her new boyfriend, but after spending 5 minutes with him, sheÂ turns him into a platypus. By the end, thanks to Helen, she turns over a new leaf.

==Trivia==
*[[Circe (DuckTales)|Another version of Circe]], drawn as a pig, was shown in the series ''[[Ducktales]]''.
*Circe is the first Disney character to be portrayed by Idina Menzel, who would later portray [[Nancy Tremaine]] in ''[[Enchanted]]'', and [[Elsa]] in ''[[Frozen]]''.
*In the original Greek mythology, Circe is the daughter of Helios and Perse and is well versed in potions and herbs. Retellings of her story tend to either make her a goddess or simply an enchantress or other supernatural being. She is usually associated with Odysseus whom she attempted to seduce, but failed ultimately having to aid him in returning home.
