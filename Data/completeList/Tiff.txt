'''Tiff''' is a minor character in the [[Disney Channel Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. She is portrayed by Alexis Wilkins.

==Character History==
She is a teenager girl and a member of the cheerleading squad when Lindsay was talking to in Sumner Park. She and [[Avery Jennings]] were trying to convince her and the cheerleaders to give them as role of the High School mascot.

==Physical appearance==
Tiff is slender and with a regular heighth. She has blonde hair and brown eyes.
