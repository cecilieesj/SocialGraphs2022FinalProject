'''Fix-It''' is Manny's zoomorphic robotic "dog". She was made by [[Manny Garcia|Manny]] out of spare parts, lives in Manny's repair shop, and is most likely to be [[Squeeze]]'s best friend.

==Appearance==
Fix-It is blue-green, has orange ears, purple eyelids, a blue light up nose, blue wheels, a red tail, and light blue whiskers.

==Personality==
Fix-It is mostly hyperactive when the remote gets pressed like in the episode "[[Manny's Sick Day]]", she ruined the whole shop when the remote was pressed.
