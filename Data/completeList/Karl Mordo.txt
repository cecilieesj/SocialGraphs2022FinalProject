'''Karl Mordo''' is a character from [[Marvel Comics]]. He is a central character in ''[[Doctor Strange (film)|Doctor Strange]]''. An alternate form of his, originating from Earth-838, appears in its sequel, ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]''.

Karl Mordo was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Steve Ditko.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Doctor Strange (film)|Doctor Strange]]''====
Karl Mordo was a student of the Ancient One who overheard Stephen Strange asking people for directions to the Kamar-Taj. Strange was attacked by three muggers, but Mordo saved his life.

==== ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]'' ====
In an alternate universe, Karl Mordo joined the Illuminati, which was created by that universe's Doctor Strange. But when Strange corrupted himself with dark magic to defeat [[Thanos]], he was executed by the Illuminati, after which Mordo became the Sorcerer Supreme.
