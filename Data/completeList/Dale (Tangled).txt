'''Dale''' is a main antagonist of the episode, "[[Fitzherbert P.I.]]" in ''[[Tangled: The Series]]''. He is an actor who impersonated the famous artist giovanni.

==Role in the series==
===Season one===
In "[[Fitzherbert P.I.]]" Dale comes to Corona dressed as Giovanni to do an important painting, However, he secretly steals a real painting and replaces it with the fake one he made. Eugene notices that the painting is fake and rushes toward the docks to catch him just as the real giovanni arrives. He was arrested afterward.

===Season two===
In "[[Max and Eugene in Peril on the High Seas]]" he is one of the many criminals who took over a prison barge. However, their plan was foiled again and they were locked up again.

==Trivia==
*His real name was revealed in In ''Max and Eugene in Peril on the High Seas''.
*His disguise looks different from the real Giovanni.
