'''Canoe Henderson''' is [[Patti Randall]]'s boyfriend in ''[[That Darn Cat! (1965 film)|That Darn Cat!]]''. 

==Role in the Film==
Zeke Kelso is Patti Randall's boyfriend, he makes and eats sandwiches, surfs, and takes Patti to surfing movies. One night, Patti and Canoe came back from the movies to make a sandwich when suddenly, D.C. came to lied on him, then he saw Miss Miller's Watch, look it and then left believing it was late. Since Patti started getting [[Zeke Kelso]] on using [[D.C.]] to find [[Dan and Iggy]] and Margaret Miller, he starts to get suspicious and tries to investigate what is going on. On the night they caught Dan and Iggy, he got stuck in the garage door but eventually got out.
