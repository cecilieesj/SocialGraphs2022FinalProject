{{Infobox character|image = IMG 9440.JPG}}'''Precious and Treasure''' are two cats from a [[Disney Princess]] Story called ''Cinderella: The Great Cat-tastrophe''.

==Physical Appearance==
Precious is the white cat with a pink bow. Treasure is gray with a blue bow.
