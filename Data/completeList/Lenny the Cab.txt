'''Lenny the Cab''' is [[Benny the Cab|Benny the Cab's]] twin cousin who works for the [[Toontown (Who Framed Roger Rabbit)|Toontown]] Cab Company.

==Description==

=== Background ===
Lenny is the Cab is a cartoon taxi-car who is the identical-twin cousin of Benny the Cab.  While Benny appears to be somewhat freelance, Lenny is an employee of the Toontown Cab Company.

== Appearances ==

=== [[Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin]] ===
Lenny serves as the ride vehicle for the Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin attractions at [[Disneyland]] and [[Tokyo Disneyland]].  Lenny was employed to take humans on a tour of Toon Town only for the tour to be intercepted by the [[Toon Patrol]], attempting kidnap [[Jessica Rabbit]] and dousing the roads with the [[Dip]].

Benny then teams up with [[Roger Rabbit|Roger]] to try and pursue the Weasels through toon-town, bringing the guests along until the day is finally saved.
