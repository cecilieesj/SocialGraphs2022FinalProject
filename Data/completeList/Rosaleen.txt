'''Rosaleen''' is one of the two secondary antagonists from the upcoming [[2022]] Disney’s feature animated/live-action film, ''[[Disenchanted]]''. She is one of [[Malvina Monroe]]’s assistants, who is infected by [[Giselle]]’s wish to live a fairytale life.
