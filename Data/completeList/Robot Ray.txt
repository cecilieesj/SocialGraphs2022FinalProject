'''Robot Ray''' is a major character from ''[[Doc McStuffins]].'' He is Donny's toy robot who has robot vision, a sticky rope and a little light on top of his head.

==Background==
===Physical Appearance===
Robot Ray is a light gray robot with a blue chest and a yellow center. He has red lights on his head and blue ear pieces.

==Trivia==
* Robot Ray sang the ''I Feel Better ''song in 2 episodes: [[Out in the Wild]] and [[Diagnosis Not Even Close-Is|Diagnosis Not Even Close-is]].
*He could possibly be named after famous science fiction writer Ray Bradbury.
