|dislikes = Matt Murdock risking his life as Daredevil}}'''Foggy Nelson''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Daredevil (TV series)|Daredevil]]'', played by [[Elden Henson]]. He is [[Daredevil|Matt Murdock]]'s partner in their law firm. Unlike Matt, Foggy is kind, gentle, friendly, jovial, and good-natured. Foggy acts as a moral compass and voice of reason to Matt, keeping Matt from going off the deep end wih his friendly advice, his compassionate nature and his nurturing friendship. Foggy doesn't approve of Matt being Daredevil, but he respects Matt's decision and helps him as much as he can.
