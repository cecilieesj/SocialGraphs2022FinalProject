'''Gracie''' is a dog on ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Background==
Gracie is the daughter of [[Princess James|Princess]] and [[Stan Mordecai James|Stan]], and the sister of [[Freddy]]. Like her brother, Gracie has the unique ability to talk like her mother and father.

==Physical appearance==
Gracie remsembles her mother, Princess. She has a brown head and feet, and a white body.

==Trivia==
*Gracie was named after a receptionist who worked were Princess gave birth.
