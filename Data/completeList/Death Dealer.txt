'''Death Dealer''' is a Marvel Comics character that appears in ''[[Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings]]'', played by Andy Le. He's a former MI6 agent who joined the [[Ten Rings]], and who oversaw [[Shang-Chi]]'s training. Death Dealer has no superpowers, but has mastered several forms of kung fu and he's also an excellent sword fighter.

Death Dealer was created by Doug Moench and Gene Day.
