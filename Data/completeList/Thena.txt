'''Thena''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by [[Angelina Jolie]]. Thena is a member of theÂ Eternals. She can manifest weapons from cosmic energy and is a skilled warrior and scholar.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://comicbook.com/movies/news/eternals-toy-leaks-thena-role-revealed-angelina-jolie/|title=Eternals: New Action Figure Leaks Tease Angelina Jolie's Role in Movie|website=Comicbook.com|date=December 27, 2020}}</ref>
