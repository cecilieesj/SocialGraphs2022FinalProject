The '''Fire Demons''' are creatures and antagonists from Muspelheim that were responsible for the destruction of Beta Ray Bill's planet.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
In "The Ballad of Beta Ray Bill", they were led by [[Amora the Enchantress|Amora]] possessed by [[Surtur]]. They wanted to destroy the ship of Beta Ray Bill, where they traveled the last korbonites alive.
