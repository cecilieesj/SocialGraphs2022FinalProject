'''Sander Loyer''' is a dancer, and also likes to rap. He is friends with [[Barron James]], [[Caitlyn Gellar]], and [[Lola Scott]].

==Appearances ==
===''[[Camp Rock]] ''===
He sings the songs "Hasta La Vista", along with Barron and Ella and "Start The Party" with Barron.
===''[[Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam]] ''===
He returns to Camp Rock and assists his friends on the mission to save Camp Rock from closure.

==Personality==
He enjoys reggae music. Sander wants to be a musician when he grows up and his trademark move is the snake.
