'''Freddy''' is a dog on ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Background==
Freddy is the son of [[Princess James|Princess]] and [[Stan Mordecai James|Stan]], and the brother of [[Gracie]]. Like his sister, Freddy has the unique ability to talk like his father and mother.

==Physical appearance==
Freddy resembles his father, Stan. He has a white coat with black and brownish spots all over. He looks like Stan as a puppy, if not a resemblance to a Pomeranian.
