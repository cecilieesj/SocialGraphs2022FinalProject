|alignment = Neutral/Good}}'''Mr. B''' is an alien in the [[Pixar]] animated short, ''[[Lifted]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Lifted]]''===
He is regularly in control of the UFO, but lets [[Stu (Lifted)|Stu]] take control one night and attempt to capture a human, named [[Ernie]]. After several failures, Mr. B takes control instead, and puts everything in the household back to where it was.
