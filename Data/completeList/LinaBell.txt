'''LinaBell''' (リーナ・ベル) is a smart pink fox in the ''Duffy and Friends'' toyline introduced in 2021 at [[Shanghai Disneyland]]. She loves nature and solving mysteries.

==Background==
===Story===
One day, on an outing, [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] and [[Duffy the Disney Bear|Duffy]] got separated following butterflies. 

A fox passed by, "What's wrong?"

"I can't find my way back"

"Hmm. Sticky seed. Butterfly. Orchid. Got it. I can bring you back!"

"Wow, you're amazing!"

Excited to be reunited with Mickey, Duffy said, "She is really smart!", and gave her an orchid. "My name is LinaBell and I love solving mysteries."

What mysteries will you solve with LinaBell?

== [[Disney Parks, Experiences and Products|Disney Parks]] ==
LinaBell made her debut at [[Shanghai Disneyland]] on September 29, [[2021]], being introduced on social media two weeks earlier on the 16th. LinaBell also made her debut at [[Tokyo DisneySea]] on September 8, [[2022]] and at [[Hong Kong Disneyland]] on September 9, 2022. LinaBell made a surprise appearance at the 2022 [[D23|D23 Expo]], at the presentation for Disney Parks, Experiences and Products and at the Duffy and Friends panel alongside [[Duffy the Disney Bear|Duffy]].
