'''Drake DuCaine''' was a great hero from [[Puckworld]]'s ancient past.

==Character Biography==
Drake DuCaine created a special mask, a bio-mechanical cybernetic interface device, with the power to penetrate the Saurians dark magic and cloaking technology. He was eventually able to locate the Overlords and, with the help of his fellow ducks, eventually imprison the alien invaders in dimensional limbo.
