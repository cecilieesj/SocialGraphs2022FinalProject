'''Big Old Bullfrog''' is a frog who lives at the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Otter_Pond Otter Pond] in ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]''. He was performed by [[Peter Linz]].

He appears in the song "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Hello_Doctor Hello Doctor]" from "[[That Healing Feeling]]." He calls [[Doc Hogg]] because he has a stomachache from eating too many flies. He is also seen in "[[What's the Story?]]" and in the opening of "[[A Berry Bear Christmas]]." He is sometimes referred to as simply "Bullfrog." The puppet used for the character had first appeared in [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_204:_Toad_%26_Elephant_Seal episode 204] of ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/The_Animal_Show The Animal Show]'' as [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Bufo_the_Toad Bufo the Toad].
