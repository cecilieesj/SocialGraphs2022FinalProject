}}The '''Orange Monster''' is a character and antagonist in ''[[Future-Worm!]]''.

==Information==
The Orange Monster is a type of monster. It is unknown if it was in the past or the future but it's weaknesses are having eye drops. The Orange Monster is one of the recurring characters in the series.

==Weaknesses==
*'''Eye Drops'''- If there are any eye drops, the Orange Monster becomes blind.
