The '''Recurring Raccoon''' is a character who appears in the second season of ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''. The name implies that he makes recurring appearances in several episodes throughout the season. He will often show up unexpectedly, steal something, and then walk away along with a jingle playing in the background.

==Role in the Series==
The Recurring Raccoon first appears in "[[Picture Day (Milo Murphy's Law)|Picture Day]]", in which he steals a camera and runs away with it, leaving [[Milo Murphy|Milo]], [[Zack Underwood|Zack]], [[Melissa Chase|Melissa]], and [[Amanda Lopez|Lopez]] staring blankly in confusion. He appears again in "[[Game Night (Milo Murphy's Law)|Game Night]]" when he steals some dice from Milo while looking for a board game containing dice. He notes that he knew he was going to come back.

While watching TV in "[[Cake 'Splosion!]]", the Recurring Raccoon shows up an steals the TV remote from [[Martin and Brigette Murphy|Martin]] and [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz|Doofenshmirtz]]. He goes into the backyard and dunks it in the birdbath, causing the TV to malfunction. Doofenshmirtz tries to zap the raccoon with a inator, which will send him to a random location. However, he hits Martin by mistakes, sending him to Jefferson County Middle School, where the cooking show is taking place.
