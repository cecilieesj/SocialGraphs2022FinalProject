'''Donny Rottweiler''' is an antagonist in ''[[The Replacements]]''. He is a bad bully who is much taller than [[Todd Daring|Todd]], but still attends his school. He is Buzz's mentor and Todd's other nemesis, he is afraid due to his large size and makes his last appearance in "A Tale of Two Rileys".
