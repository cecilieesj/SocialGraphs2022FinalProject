[[File:Char_39159.jpg|thumb]]'''Chauncey Pesky''' ([[Brian Doyle-Murray]]) is the father of [[Maggie Pesky|Maggie]] and her siblings, of the animated show, ''[[The Buzz on Maggie]]'', and is proved to be strict but understanding when it comes to his children's situations. Chauncey, his sister Florence and his two unnamed brothers have the same head shape.
