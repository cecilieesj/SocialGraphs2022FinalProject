'''Spyder-Knight''' is a former vigilante and is now guardian of York.

==Background Information==
===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===
Peter Parker, the Spyder-Knight, operating from York in Yorkshire, England on his Earth. He became involved in the Web-Warriors' multiversal mission.

==Trivia==
*This is [[Christopher Daniel Barnes]]' third role as an alternate universe's Spider-Man, the others being Spider-Man Noir in ''Shattered Dimensions'' and Spider-Man 2099 in ''Edge of Time''.
*Spyder-Knight's armor is based on from the third Spider-Armor.
