'''Charmaine''' (voiced by [[Christine Cavanaugh]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Lloyd-in-Space/Charmaine/</ref> is a supporting character of [[Disney's One Saturday Morning]] ''[[Lloyd in Space]]''.

==Biography==
She is a student brain with glasses in school, with arms and legs. She is a Cerebellian and intelligent girl who often is involved in debates against other schools. She is a friend of [[Douglas McNoggin]], [[Cindy]], and Missy.
