The '''Mad Thinker''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] supervillain with an amazing ability to calculate the outcome of events.

The Mad Thinker was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
He was an inmate of the Big House along with [[Whirlwind]], who he tells he has a plan to escape. [[Henry Pym]] visited him regarding his conversation with Whirlwind. When the prison's security fails he escapes.
