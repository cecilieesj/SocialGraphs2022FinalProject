[[File:Char_39180.jpg|thumb]]'''Carmela Pesky''' ([[Tara Strong]]) is a butterfly at Buzzdale Academy.

==History==
Carmela is a butterfly that was [[Maggie Pesky|Maggie's]] little sister in the "Big Brother/Sister" program. She was in her cocoon until she learned how to be nice to people.
