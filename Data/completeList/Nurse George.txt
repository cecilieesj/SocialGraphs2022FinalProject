|friends = [[Nurse A.J.]]|status=Active|partner=Unnamed wife}}'''Nurse George''' is a minor character in the [[2009]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated feature film ''[[Up]]'' and one of the two main characters of the exclusive bonus animated short, ''[[George & A.J.]]''. He is mostly seen with his colleague [[Nurse A.J.|A.J.]] and works at [[Shady Oaks Retirement Village]]. He is married.
==Appearances==
===''[[Up]]''===
George is a nurse from Shady Oaks, that asks [[Carl Fredricksen|Carl]] to come with them. He, along with his colleague A.J., soon watches him in shock flying his house.

===''[[George & A.J.]]''===
George is one of the two main characters in this short film. After leaving [[Carl Fredricksen's House|Carl's residence]], where he flew his house away, George and his colleague, A.J., are still trying to ask the elderly to join Shady Oaks. But all of them escape, in different ways. For instance, one house got pulled away by cats, and another went to float on water. Soon after, they return to Shady Oaks, and soon come to realize that the elderly there are escaping also. As it goes off into the distance, Carl and [[Russell]], return in the [[Spirit of Adventure]] with [[Dug]] who speaks in front of George, making him and A.J. gasp.
