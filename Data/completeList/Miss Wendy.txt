'''Miss Wendy''' is a minor character in the [[2019]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated film, ''[[Toy Story 4]]''. She is [[Bonnie Anderson]]'s kindergarten teacher who welcomes her into her class. 

==Background==
{{Expand section}}
Miss Wendy's job is to teach kindergarten students. She met [[Bonnie]] at the front of the class door, and helped her on orientation day.

Not much is known about Miss Wendy prior to her debut.

=== Personality===
Miss Wendy is sweet and kind and is considerate of her studentâ€™s feelings and is willing to help them when needed.

==Role in the film==
Miss Wendy makes her first appearance in ''Toy Story 4''. When Bonnie enters the school for her first non-official day, she is first greeted by Miss Wendy, who nicely welcomes her into her class. She helps Bonnie into the classroom and to help her find her coatrack and a seat. Moments later, she is surprised with Bonnie when she created [[Forky]] and congratulates her on her creativity. 

==Trivia==
*Miss Wendy is the first teacher ever to be seen in the [[Toy Story (franchise)|''Toy Story'']] franchise.
*Miss Wendy is the first kindergarten teacher to appear in the franchise.
*Miss Wendy also appears in several ''Toy Story 4'' picture books.
