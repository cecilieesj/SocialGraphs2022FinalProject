'''Jack Savage''' was the main protagonist of the [[2016]] animated film ''[[Zootopia]]''. He was supposed to be the main character in the film before [[Judy Hopps|Judy]] was chosen for the final version of the film.

==Background==
===Physical Appearance===
Jack was a rabbit with grey fur and black stripes. He had a spy suit, in a nod to espionage films such as ''James Bond''.

===Personality===
It is assumed he was a serious character.

===Development===
During early development of the film, Jack was supposed to leave the city of [[Zootopia]] under the command of his boss where he would explore a world of dangerous animals. This led to Byron Howard's idea of films about rabbit spies. During development pitch, the idea for a modernized all-animal city in the first act, as the city was to be the film's setting. For the final version of the film, Jack was eventually replaced by [[Judy Hopps]], another rabbit protagonist.
