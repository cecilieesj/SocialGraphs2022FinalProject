'''Snap Carpenter''' is character and antagonist in ''[[The Buzz on Maggie]]''. He is a bully ant at Buzzdale Academy.

==History==
His parents call him Percy, and he has a lot of embarrassing secrets, like superhero underwear.

In "[[Hooligans]]", Snap almost murders [[Maggie Pesky|Maggie]] by punching her in the face, but his own mother saves her life.
