[[File:Leonard.jpg|thumb]]'''Leonard "Lenny" Cutler'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/leonard.htm</ref> is a one-time guest character on [[Disney Channel]]'s Original Series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
He is [[Judge Glamis Cutler]]'s son, who appears in "[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]", having his birthday party at [[Familio Festevedro]], where [[Arthur Spudinski|Spud]] and his [[Ms. Spudinski|mother]] works and they sing happy birthday to him.
