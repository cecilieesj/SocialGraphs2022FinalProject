|quote =  "Ludicrous!"}}'''The Megans''' are characters from the television series ''[[Recess]]''. They are four girls, all of whom are named Megan, who attend Mademoiselle Povlova's Salon of Independent Movement. Like [[the Ashleys]], they are very snobby. They appeared only in the episode "[[Dance Lessons]]".
