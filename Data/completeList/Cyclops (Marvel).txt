'''Cyclops''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who is a [[mutants|mutant]] superhero who can shoot blasts of concussive force from his eyes.

Cyclops was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

== Movie Appearances ==
===''Deadpool 2''===
Cyclops, along with a few other members of the [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]] appear in a brief cameo. They can be seen conversing in another room, while [[Deadpool|Wade]] is in the X-Mansion, complaining the [[20th Century Studios|studio]] hasn't allowed them to bring in one of the more famous members of the team.

===''[[Dark Phoenix]]''===
Cyclops appears in the film [[Dark Phoenix]] with his relationship with [[Jean Grey]] being a huge part of the emotional core of the movie. As Jean grows more unstable, he holds on to hope more tightly than anyone that they'll be able to save her.

==Television Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
Cyclops can be seen on [[Nick Fury]]'s wall of [[Skrull]] suspects in the episode "Infiltration".

===''[[Marvel Disk Wars: The Avengers]]''===
Cyclops is seen alongside [[Beast (Marvel)|Beast]] during the [[Avengers]]' party.
