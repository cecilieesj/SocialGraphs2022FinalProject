'''Princess Shivani''' is a minor character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Mira, Royal Detective]]''. She's the Crown Princess of [[Naiyapuram]], a neighboring kingdom of [[Jalpur]], the only daughter of the King and Queen of Naiyapuram and is the future queen of this country.

<br />

== Personality ==
Shivani is a very sweet, kind, generous and helpful young princess. This is shown when goes around Jalpur in disguise helping people all over the kingdom. This is also due to the fact that she likes being treated like a normal girl instead of just a princess. She and Mira have becomes great friends since their first meeting and Shivani always looks forward to seeing her when she comes to visit Jalpur or when Mira comes to visit her in Naiyapuram.
