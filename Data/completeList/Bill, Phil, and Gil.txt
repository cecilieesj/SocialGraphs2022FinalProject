'''Bill, Phil, and Gil''' are characters in ''[[Wander Over Yonder]]''. [[Rob Riggle]] voices Bill, [[Ken Marino]] voices Phil, and [[Bryan Callen]] voices Gil.

==Background==
In "[[The Family Reunion]]", they are the triplet brothers of [[Sylvia]] and the sons of [[Dorothy]]. They all like to fight with their sister as well as working out and constantly call Wander a cat or monkey. They all approve of Sylvia's success with helping the galaxy at the end.

==Appearances==
*"[[The Family Reunion]]" (first appearance)

==Trivia==
*Coincidentally, they are named after [[The Willie Brothers]] in ''[[Home on the Range]]''.
