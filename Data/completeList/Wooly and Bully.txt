'''Wooly and Bully''' are (respectfully) a toon sheep and a toon duck acting as [[Ma Parker]]'s evil but dim-witted henchmen and are supporting antagonists in the [[Bonkers (TV series)|Bonkers]] episode "[[Calling All Cars]]". Wooly is voiced by [[Rob Paulsen]], while Bully is voiced by [[Pat Fraley]].

== Trivia ==
* Despite being a bad toon, Bully was among the toons abducted by [[Warris and Donald]] in "[[Color Me Piquel]]".  
* Wooly and Bully are the bosses in the Studio level in the Super Nintendo version of the Bonkers video game.
