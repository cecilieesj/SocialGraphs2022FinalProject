'''Elliot Funston''' (voiced by Sam Gifaldi)<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Elliot/</ref> is a ''[[Fillmore!]]'' character and antagonist. He is one of the six guest characters and a Middle School criminal.

==History==
He serves as the Middle School mascot named, Lobstee, the Lobster in [[Cry, The Beloved Mascot]]. In the [[Test of the Tested]], he has stoled all of the S.A.T.T.Y. 9 Test and trying to impress [[Enid Quintara]] being captured by [[Cornelius Fillmore]] and [[Ingred Third|Ingrid Third]] was taken into custody.
