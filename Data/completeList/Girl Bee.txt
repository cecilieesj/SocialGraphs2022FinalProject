The '''Girl Bee''' is a seductive cutie-pie bee who attracts [[Spike the Bee|Spike]] and inspires his work in the [[1952]] animated short, ''[[Let's Stick Together]]''. Unfortunately, her seductive charms don't last into adulthood and marriage.
