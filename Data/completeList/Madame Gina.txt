'''Madame Gina''' is a singer at the Adriano Club who appears in the Japanese film ''[[Porco Rosso (film)|Porco Rosso]]''. She is Donald Curtis' love interest but she is in love with [[Porco Rosso|Porco]]. At the end of the movie it is hinted by Fio that Marco may have proposed to her.
