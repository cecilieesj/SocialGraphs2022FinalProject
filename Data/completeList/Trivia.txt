|quote = ''"Do you know who was the first Demigod to bowl a 300 game?"''}}'''Trivia''' (pronounced '''''TRI-vee-Ah''''') is a minor character of the Disney animated series, ''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]''. He is the [[Olympian Gods|Olympian God]] of when three roads meet and of trivial information. He is an extremely dull person who tends to bore others with his useless information. Even mortals, despite building him an impressive temple do not take him in high regard. 

==Powers & Abilities==
As a god, Trivia possesses the natural powers and abilities of an Olympian gods such as immortality omnipresence, vast strength, and potential to do magic such as the ability to shape-shift, to teleport, to manifest objects. As the god of the where and when three roads meet he possesses the ability to have an individual paved. As the god of trivia he is knowledgeable of trivial questions.

==Trivia==
*He is the one of the only Olympian gods in the animated series who is not based on an actual Greek God, the other being Narcissus.
*His name is the Latin name and Roman equivalent of the demigoddess of witchcraft [[Hecate]] who appeared in the episode ''[[Hercules and the Underworld Takeover]]''.
*His name is a pun on "Trivia Quizzes" since his name is referring to question game.
*Considering the fact that he can erase memories, he might be the reason why Hades didn't know Hercules was still alive at first and that Hercules did not remember Hades in the movie.
