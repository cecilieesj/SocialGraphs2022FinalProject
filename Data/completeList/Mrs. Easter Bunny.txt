Mrs. Easter Bunny is a original character only seen in Disney Parks with her husband [[Mr. Easter Bunny]].

==Background==
She is a white rabbit that wears a dress and hat with flowers and shorts. She appears with her husband in Easter Time in Disney Parks meeting guests and appearing in Easter shows and parades.
