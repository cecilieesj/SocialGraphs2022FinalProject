|status=Active}}'''Joyce McCarthy''' is a recurring character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[Big Shot (TV series)|Big Shot]]''. She is Marvyn Korn's rival. In Season 1, she was the Girl's Head Basketball Coach at Carlsbad Prep. In Marvyn's Playbook, she announces that is retiring at the end of the semester. In Season 2, she becomes the Principal at Carlsbad Prep.

==Background==
===Personality===
Joyce is ambitious, disrespectful mean, rude, and selfish. She doesn't care what anyone thinks.

===Physical appearance===
Joyce McCarthy has brown hair, blue eyes, and light skin.
