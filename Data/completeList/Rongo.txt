'''Rongo''' the God of Agriculture, is one of the Tiki Gods in the pre-show of [[Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room]].

== History ==
Rongo is the māori god of agriculture, in his spare time, he likes to fly kites.

== Appearances ==

=== [[Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room|The Enchanted Tiki Room]] ===
Rongo appears in the pre-show of the Enchanted Tiki Room along with the other tiki gods. When he introduces himself, he explains to the guests that his land was good for him, giving him time for sports like flying kites. The sign next to Rongo himself reads:
<poem>
''In tropic lands the legends tell''
''Astounding pioneers did dwell''
''This wise fella began all flight''
''For Rongo flew the world's first kite!''
</poem>

=== Trader Sam's ===
[[Pele]]<span>, </span>[[Ngendei]]<span>, [[Koro]] and </span>Rongo<span> all decorate the bar-taps in these bars and have glowing eyes.</span>
