'''Agent''' '''Gordon (Parallel Universe)''' is a government agent that appeared in the episode, Parallel Universe. In the Parallel Universe (Location), he is the member of the Department of Homeland Special Ops Unit. He and the government agents try to capture Parallel Leo, however, Leo escapes by geo-leaping.





It '''''could '''''be possible he is the parallel version of Agent Graham.
