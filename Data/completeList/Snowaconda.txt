The '''Snowaconda''' is a minor character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''. It is a large serpent of ice and snow created by [[ShiverJack]] to destroy [[Jake]] and his crew. Its vocal effects are provided by [[Dee Bradley Baker]].
