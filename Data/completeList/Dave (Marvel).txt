'''Dave''' is a character of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], who first appears in ''[[Ant-Man (film)|Ant-Man]]''. He is an ally of [[Scott Lang]] who helped him pull heists and then helped him and [[Hank Pym]] defeat the [[Darren Cross|Yellowjacket]].

== Appearances ==

===''[[Ant-Man (film)|Ant-Man]]''===
Dave is Scott Lang's gang member who drives their van when they escape their heists. He enjoys playing poker, and watching football. He later teases Hank Pym about the heist they pulled in his house before he became one of his employees. During the plan to steal and destroy The Yellow Jacket Suit, Dave creates a distraction by stealing Paxton and Gale's car when they plan to arrest Hank for breaking Scott out of Jail.
