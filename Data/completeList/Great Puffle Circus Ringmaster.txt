|friends = Other Puffles in Circus}}The '''Great Puffle Circus Ringmaster''' is a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Yellow_Puffle Yellow Puffle] who was the ringmaster for the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Great_Puffle_Circus Great Puffle Circus] during [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/The_Fall_Fair_2009 The Fall Fair 2009] and [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/The_Fair_2010 The Fair 2010]. He wore a red ringmaster top hat and a brown moustache. He also carried a baton, which he seemed to enjoy having, as he wiould twirl it around very often. The ringmaster and the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Purple_Puffle Purple Puffle] were the only [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffles Puffles] at the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Great_Puffle_Circus Great Puffle Circus] in 2009 and 2010 to do an act with two puffles. He seemed to also be a magician.

Yet, this puffle did NOT get an act in the circus by himself.
