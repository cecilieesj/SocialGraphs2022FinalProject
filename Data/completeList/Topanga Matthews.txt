'''Topanga Matthews''' (née '''Lawrence''') is a major character in ''[[Boy Meets World]]'' and ''[[Girl Meets World]]''.

==Character information==
Topanga is the mother of [[Riley Matthews|Riley]] and [[Auggie Matthews|Auggie]], and the wife of [[Cory Matthews|Cory]]. In ''Boy Meets World'', she was pursuing a career in law, and in ''Girl Meets World'', she is a lawyer.

==Trivia==
*"[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topanga,_California Topanga]" is a canyon in California that hippies would frequently go to. One of the writers got stuck in traffic while driving through there, and decided it was appropriate to the character.
*Topanga is overprotective of Riley and is closer to her than Cory.
