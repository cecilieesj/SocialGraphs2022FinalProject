Tiffany "The Songstress" Javelins is the group's ally on Teamo Supremo. She is a talented popstar.[[File:Songstress.gif|thumb]]

== Appearance ==
Tiffany is a blonde-haired, blue-eyed woman with a tan skin color. She wears a pink eyeshadow, red lipstick, amethyst earrings and bracelets, blue top that reveals her midriff (with matching heels) and ripped white pants. She has a left-arm tattoo and a belly button piercing.<br />
