'''Agent G''' (voiced by [[Michael York]]) is the father of [[Agent K]], father-in-law of [[Dick Daring]] and adoptive grandfather of [[Todd Daring]] and [[Riley Daring]] in ''[[The Replacements]]''. He's the head scientist and inventor of the British Spy Service and the husband of [[Agent B]]. He appeared in the episodes "[[London Calling]]" and "[[Dick Daring's All-Star Holiday Stunt Spectacular V|Dick Daring's All-Star Christmas Spectacular]]".
