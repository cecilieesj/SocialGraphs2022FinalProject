'''Emcee''' is a minor character in the [[Disney]]/[[Pixar]] [[2017]] animated feature film [[Coco (film)|''Coco'']]. She is a resident of the Land of the Dead, and hosts a musical talent show, in which the winner would have a chance of meeting [[Ernesto de la Cruz]].

== Background ==
Little to nothing is known about Emcee prior to ''Coco'' other than the fact that her spirit has moved to the [[Land of the Dead]], and is now a hostess who appears to host shows. 

=== Appearance ===
Emcee has the appearance of an extravagant celebrity, who dresses in bight, vivid, and colorful clothing and apparel. She wears a bright pink dress with a blue collar, while also having dark cyan hair with various objects attached to it. 

== Appearances ==

=== ''[[Coco]]'' ===
Emcee first appears in ''Coco''. She hosts a talent show at a chance for the winner to meet Ernesto de la Cruz. This draws the attention of [[Miguel Rivera|Miguel]], who attempts to perform during the show to which he and [[Héctor]] perform the musical number "[[Un Poco Loco]]" in front of the audience, while his family continues to search for him so he can return home. Emcee is then asked to ask the audience to keep a look out for a living boy who needs to return to the living world. 

=== ''[[Pixar Popcorn]]'' ===

==== “[[A Day in the Life of the Dead]]” ====
Emcee appears as a silent character in ''Pixar Popcorn.'' She appears in the episode “A Day in the Life of the Dead,” where she is hit by a speeding bike ridden by Óscar and Felipe. Once hit by the bike, she immediately takes part of the speeding down the street and zooms by various characters, including [[Imelda]] and [[Héctor]], who are seen dancing. 

== Trivia ==
* Given her appearance and celebrity-like personality, Emcee might have been a icon/celebrity during her time in the living.
