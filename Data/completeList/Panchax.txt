'''Panchax''' is a Bathyosian officer and the Chief of Security of the crystallic crystal mine on [[Bathyos]].

==Physical Appearance==
He is a red fish-like alien with thick lips and two maroon fins above his yellow eyes as "eyebrows".

==Abilities==
Panchax is a capable fighter, holding his own against the shark-like [[Gularis|Minister Gularis]] and eventually arresting him.
