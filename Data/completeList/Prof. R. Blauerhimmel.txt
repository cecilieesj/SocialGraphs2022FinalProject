|model = Robert Coltrin}}'''Professor R. Blauerhimmel''' is a pilot and member of the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]].

==Background==
Little information is available about Blauerhimmel, having only made a few minor portrait appearances. It is known that he was an aviator who became a member of S.E.A. during the late 19th century and was active throughout the early 20th century.

==Appearances==

===[[Mystic Manor]]===
At Mystic Manor, his portrait is on display in the queue as well as appearing in the 1899 Society meeting painting alongside [[Henry Mystic|Lord Henry Mystic]], [[Harrison Hightower III]] (with [[Shiriki Utundu]]), [[Mary Oceaneer|Captain Mary Oceaneer]], and Dr. [[Dr. J.L. Baterista|J.L. Baterista]].

===Bengal Barbecue===
The profile painting of Blauerhimmel can also be found at Disneyland's Bengal Barbecue. It seen with a photograph of Blauerhimmel in the cockpit of a biplane and a collection of postcards.

===[[Skipper Canteen]]===
A book in the ''[[Jungle Navigation Company|Jungle Navigation Co.]] Ltd. Skipper Canteen'' called "Traditional native holiday d√©cor" is written by Blauerhimmel. This book's name is an apparent allusion to the seasonal [[Jingle Cruise]] overlay of the [[Jungle Cruise]] though it is unknown why this is affiliated with the professor.

===[[The Tropical Hideaway|Tropical Hideaway]]===
At The Tropical Hideaway, one of the expedition paddles is marked with his name and displays a 1904 expedition on the Ucayali River in Peru.

==Trivia==
*Blauerhimmel is German for "Blue Sky", the term Imagineering uses for early "no limits" conceptual exploration for an attraction.
*The professor is modelled after imagineer Robert Coltrin.
