'''Hiromi Tanaka''' is a recurring character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is comic shop employee and Kevin's crush.

== Background ==

=== Physical appearance ===
Hiromi has light skin with pink hair, two ponytails and earrings. She wears a gray shirt with a Duck Boyz logo on it, yellow pants with a turquoise belt on it and green shoes.
