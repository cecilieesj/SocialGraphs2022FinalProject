|fate =}}The '''Fairies''' are magical creatures that appear in the film ''[[Maleficent (film)|Maleficent]]'' and live in the magical forest known as the [[Moors]].

==Background==
The Fairies once lived in harmony with the humans but after [[Maleficent]] was ruthlessly betrayed and became the evil ruler of the forest, things changed for the Moors and the fairies lived in fear of their now wicked queen, yet they still remain loyal. They are all known to be friendly creatures and warmly welcome Aurora when Maleficent brings her to the forest.

*'''Wallerbog''' - The Wallerbog's all tiny elf like creatures with small trunks. They are known to be quite playful and enjoy mud fights.
*'''Mushroom Fairy''' - Elderly elf looking fairies covered in mushrooms.
*'''Water Fairy''' - Tiny blue mermaid like fairies that glow and fly and live in the waters of their forest home.
*'''Leaf fairy''' - A small green leafed fairy with leafs for wings.
*'''Hedgehog fairy''' - A half-hedgehog/half-elf looking fairy.
