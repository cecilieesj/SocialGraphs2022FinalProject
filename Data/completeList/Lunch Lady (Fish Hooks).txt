[[File:TDSOTF25.jpg|thumb]]The '''Lunch Lady''' is [[Freshwater High]]'s lunch lady.

==Personal Life==
When [[Oscar the Fish|Oscar]] turned into a goth fish and he and [[Razor V. Doom|Razor]] were in the lunch line, Razor wanted the blackened chicken, the black beans and the black olives, but before he could say anything else, the Lunch Lady stuffed black foods onto his tray ("[[The Dark Side of the Fish]]").

She attended the Freshwater High musical. ("[[Fish School Musical]]")
