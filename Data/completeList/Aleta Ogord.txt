'''Aleta Ogord''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'', played by [[Michelle Yeoh]]. She is the wife of [[Stakar Ogord]] and a member of his same race of alien beings.

==Appearances==
===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]''===
She is [[Stakar Ogord]]â€˜s wife and was one of the members and a founder of her husband's [[Ravagers|Ravager]] Clan, and she participated to many adventures and fight across the galaxy. Since [[Yondu Udonta]] was exiled on the Clan, the team itself was disbanded and she never saw his team-mates again for many years.

Some years later, after the battle on Ego's Planet, Yondu died and sacrificed for [[Star-Lord]]. Stakar finally forgived Yondu and later the Clan reunited and assembled again as a team to steal some stuff.

==Trivia==
*In the comics, Aleta Ogord is merged with her adopted brother and husband [[Stakar Ogord]] by the [[w:c:marvel:Hawk God|Hawk God]] to form the superpowered hero '''Starhawk'''.
