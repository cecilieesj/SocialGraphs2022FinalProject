'''Billy''' is a bunny boy who appears in the ''[[Bear in the Big Blue House]]'' episode "[[A Trip to the General Store]]". He lives with his mother [[Bonnie (Bear in the Big Blue House)|Bonnie]], and their home was damaged by flooding from the storm in "[[Welcome to Woodland Valley]]." Many of their possessions were damaged as well, and Billy lost some of his favorite toys. He accepts an invitation to play at [[The Big Blue House]]. Billy is generally seen wearing a cap. He bears a passing resemblance to the character of [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Snort Snort] from ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/The_Tale_of_the_Bunny_Picnic The Tale of the Bunny Picnic]''.
