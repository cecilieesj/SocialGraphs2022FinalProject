|paraphernalia=Gun}}'''Aurora Mayflower''' is the LT (Lieutenant) of the RLS ''Lyoness''. She is also less formerly known as Captain (later Admiral) [[Captain Amelia|Amelia]]'s niece. She is not seen in the movie ''[[Treasure Planet]]'', only appearing as a character in ''[[Treasure Planet: Battle at Procyon]]'' (the ''Treasure Planet'' PC game released in November 2002 alongside the movie).
While it is known she is Amelia's niece, it is not known if her actual last name is Mayflower, since there is another game character with her same looks that goes by 'Mayflower', and another with the same looks that goes by 'Aurora'.
