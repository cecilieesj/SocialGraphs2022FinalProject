[[File:Huggerman.jpg|thumb|248x248px]]'''Dr. Huggarman''' (voiced by [[Efrem Zimbalist Jr.]]) from ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]'' is a scientist and friend of Tanya's. He turned his back on humanity when they abused his inventions. He briefly returns to help the Ducks stop the energy creatures.
