[[File:Mrs._Horton.jpg|thumb]]'''Mrs. Horton '''(voiced by [[Tress MacNeille]]) is the wife of [[Frank Horton]] and the mother of [[Eddie Horton]] who appears in the [[Disney's One Saturday Morning]] show ''[[Lloyd in Space]]''. She has minor roles in the series.

==Physical Appearance==
She has caucasian pale/tan skin and peach blonde hair.
