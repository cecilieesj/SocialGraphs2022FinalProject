'''Patricia''' "'''Trish" Walker''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]/[[Netflix]] series ''[[Jessica Jones (TV series)|Jessica Jones]]''.

Trish Walker was created by Stuart Little and Ruth Atkinson.

==Background==
Patricia Walker was born to a low income family. After her father left her mother, Dorothy Walker began putting her daughter through acting school, which would later land Patricia the role in the TV show "It's Patsy", making her the most famous child star in the world at the time. For a publicity move, Patricia's mother adopts a classmate of Patricia's who has been in a car accident, making [[Jessica Jones]] her adoptive sister.

In her adult years, Patricia reinvents herself as "Trish Walker", trying to wash away her teen star years, running a talk show by the name of ''Trish Talk''.

Later, she injects herself with chemicals that give him superhuman strength, superhuman speed and sharp nails, which she uses to help Jessica fight crime.

===Personality===
Trish is endlessly supportive and a true friend. She always has the best interests of her [[Jessica Jones|best friend]] at heart, even if the situation between the two of them becomes strained.

She has an impulsive side, with a personal mission to make herself strong enough so as she can protect herself from the dangers of the world, and be a hero like how she knows Jessica is.
