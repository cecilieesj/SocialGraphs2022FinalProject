'''Z-Bot''' is the main antagonist of the ''[[Bonkers]]'' episode "[[Tokyo Bonkers]]".

==History==
Z-Bot is an evil mechanical Toon robot who can merge with any mechanical device. His Toon body is destroyed in the [[United States]], and his brain gets escorted to [[Tokyo]] by [[Bonkers D. Bobcat]] and [[Miranda Wright]].
