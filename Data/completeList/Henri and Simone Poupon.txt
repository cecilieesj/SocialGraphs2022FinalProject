|status = Active}}'''Henri and Simone Poupon''' are one-time characters from the sitcom ''[[Dinosaurs]]''. They are a pair of ''Archaeopteryx'' and the parents of [[Francois Poupon]]. They appeared in the episode "[[Getting to Know You]]".

==Role in the episode==
When [[Charlene Sinclair|Charlene]] sings up for an exchange program, she goes to live in the Poupons' tree, while their son Francois goes to live with the Sinclairs. Henri and Simone's idea of getting a laugh is by making fun of Charlene and her culture, and when Charlene accidentally snaps off a branch, the couple throw an onslaught of insults and squawks.

The Poupons call [[The Sinclair Home|the Sinclair house]] and discuss what has happened. When they find out their son has been eaten by [[Baby Sinclair|Baby]], they request Earl give them his TV to fill the sudden void in their lives.
