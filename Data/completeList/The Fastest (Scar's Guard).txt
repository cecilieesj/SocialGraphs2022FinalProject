The '''Fastest Member of Scar's Lion Guard''' is a character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[The Lion Guard]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
Nothing is known about this member. As a member of the Lion Guard, he was devoted to protecting the Pride Lands and the [[Circle of Life]], so he refused the order of his leader, Scar, to aid him in usurping [[Mufasa]].

===Abilities===
*'''Animal Speed:''' The Fastest is the fastest member in Scar's Lion Guard, as he can run really fast and endure long distances without any problems.

*'''Animal Stamina:''' The Fastest has the highest stamina in Scar's Lion Guard, as he is she able to travel very long distances.
