[[File:Guardian_Dog.png|thumb|200px|Guardian Dog]]The '''Guardian Dog''' is a character involved in the play ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secrets_of_the_Bamboo_Forest Secrets of the Bamboo Forest] ''in [[Club Penguin]]. He is a dog that guards the castle, and only lets [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Penguins penguins] who have the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Feather_Pin Feather Pin] in. He is also [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Phoenix_Queen Phoenix Queen]'s guard. When he waves, he becomes a statue.

==Description==

"A loyal guard to the Phoenix Queen and when he waves, he becomes a statue!"

==Appearances==

He appeared in a sketch for the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/What%27s_New_Blog What's New Blog] as a sneak peek. When the sketch was shown, some penguins thought he was a lion, as shown in the blog's comments.
