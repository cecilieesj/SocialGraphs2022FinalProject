'''Isaiah "Zay" Babineaux''' is a semi-regular character in ''[[Girl Meets World]]'' who was introduced in Season Two as [[Lucas Friar|Lucas]]' best friend from [[Texas]] and later became the fifth member of [[Riley Matthews|Riley]]'s circle of friends.

==Information==
Originally from Austin, Texas, Zay moves to [[New York City]] and reunites with his old friend, Lucas. He then forms new friendships with Riley, [[Maya Hart|Maya]], and [[Farkle Minkus|Farkle]] (with whom he shares the title of Lucas' best friend).
