}}'''Silver''' is [[John Reid|The Lone Ranger]]'s white stallion in the film ''[[The Lone Ranger]]''.

==Personality==
Silver is the trusty steed of the highest order, frequently saving the day, even if that includes climbing to the top of roofs or galloping along moving trains, even if Tonto is convinced that he might just be stupid.

==Role in the film==
The "spirit horse" pops up while [[John Reid]], [[Dan Reid]], and a group of Texas Rangers are searching for the evil outlaw [[Butch Cavendish]].
Silver is indicating that John must return from the dead and become The Lone Ranger.
