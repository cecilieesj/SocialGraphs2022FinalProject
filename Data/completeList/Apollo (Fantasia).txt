'''Apollo''' is a minor character in the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" segment of the [[1940]] film ''[[Fantasia]]''.

==Role in the film==
Apollo is seen during the end of the segment after the storm clears (as said by [[Deems Taylor]] during the introduction), where he drives his chariot during the evening. All the inhabitants of [[Mount Olympus]] bid farewell to him during the end of the day as [[Morpheus (Fantasia)|Morpheus]] arrives to bring in the night across Mount Olympus for the inhabitants to sleep.
