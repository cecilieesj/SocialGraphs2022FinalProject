'''Captain George Stacy''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', voiced by Robert Clotworthy.

He is the captain of the [[New York City]] Police Department, and [[Spider-Gwen|Gwen Stacy]]'s father.

Captain Stacy is unaware that Gwen is Spider-Gwen and is using a fleet of androids to help him hunt down vigilantes. When [[Wolf Spider]] arrives at the police station, Captain Stacy tries to arrest him, but he is unsuccessful. After Wolf Spider is defeated, Captain Stacy has a talk with his daughter Gwen and realizes that she is Spider-Gwen.

Captain George Stacy was created by [[Stan Lee]], John Romita, and Don Heck.
