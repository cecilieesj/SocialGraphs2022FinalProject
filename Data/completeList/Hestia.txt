'''Hestia''' is a minor character in Disney animated series, ''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]''. She is the [[Olympian Gods|Olympian Goddess]] of Family and Hearth. She is [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]]' aunt.

==Background==
The goddess of family and the hearth is seen as a cheery housewife type. She is often seen cooking or marveling at her own confections, most of which end up being ruined in a comical fashion. She is the sister of [[Hera]], [[Demeter]], [[Poseidon]], [[Hades]] and [[Zeus (Hercules)|Zeus]]. Hestia is known as the peacemaker of the Olympian family. ===Powers and Abilities===
As a Goddess, Hestia Possesses:
*Immortality
*Superstrength
*Teleportation
*Metamorphosis (Shapeshifting)
*Vast magical abilities

Other
*Great Cook
*Orange/Dark Yellow Aura

==Role in the [[Hercules (TV series)|series]]==
In "[[Hercules and the Tiff on Olympus]]", Hestia appeared cooking for the gods but her food were mostly destroyed in comical fashion.

==Trivia==
*Betty White, who plays Hestia, previously played Sue Ann Nivens, The "Happy Homemaker" on the Mary Tyler Moore Show, a character similar to Hestia.
