|family = Jason (Twin Brother)}}'''Mason''' is [[Chloe James|Chloe]]'s boyfriend in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He is portrayed by Griffin Kunitz.

==Personality==
Mason is a kind and nice boy who Chloe made to be his girlfriend. He is also very absent-minded with Chloe. His personality resembles that of Bennett. He doesn't really understand how relationships work, as he has playdates with other girls.

==History==
Chloe makes friends with a boy named Mason from her school and then becomes his girlfriend. They copy an argument Chloe's parents have and this results in a temporary breakup, though they reconcile, and Mason is called gallant by Chloe.

==Trivia==
*He became a recurring character in Season 3.
*In ''Stan Has Puppies'', he has a twin brother name Jason.
*He has also dated girls named Tiffany and Molly.
*His catchphrase is "I don't understand".
*He has a running gag which is him wandering to random place spouting "How did I get here?".
