|status = Active}}"'''Ennuisha'''" is a character in ''[[Future-Worm!]]''. She debuted in "[[The Forever Five]]", where she took [[Danny Douglas|Danny]] and [[Megan Douglas|his mother]] to her realm.

==Appearance==
Ennuisha has the appearance of a small, grey-scaled girl with big black eyes. She wears a grey crown and dress. After she becomes happy, her hair turns auburn and her dress turns pink.

==Trivia==
*Her name is derived from ''ennui'', which means "boredom".
