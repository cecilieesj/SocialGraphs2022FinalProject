|voice = [[Kath Soucie]]}}'''Andromeda''' is a character in the TV series ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series|Hercules]]''. She appeared in ''[[Hercules and the Big Lie]]''. She is a new student who attracts [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]]' attention. She is voiced by [[Kath Soucie]].
== Trivia ==
* In the original Greek mythology, Andromeda was the daughter of Cepheus and Cassiopeia. When the former claimed that her daughter was more beautiful than Nereids, [[Poseidon]] sent the sea monster [[Cetus]] to attack the kingdom until Andromeda was chained to a rock to be sacrificed. However, she was rescued by Perseus.
