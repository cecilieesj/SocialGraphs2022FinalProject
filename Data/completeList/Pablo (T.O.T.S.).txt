==Appearance==
Pablo is a grey colored dog with lighter colored areas between his eyes, on his muzzle and tummy, with a lighter colored tail tip and light paws. He has blue eyes and a big black nose. He wears a red collar with the T.O.T.S. logo on it and a white diaper with corn cobs and barns.

==Personality==
Pablo is playful and loves to dance and dig.

==Trivia==
*He is voiced by Max Mitchell who also voiced Bobby the Bear. Max is also known for playing younger versions of certain characters, such as Baby Eggs in the Laika stop-motion film ''Boxtrolls'', Baby Panda and Baby Ice Bear in the Cartoon Network series ''We Bare Bears'', and Young Sam in the ''Hideo Kojima'' (''Metal Gear Solid'') game ''Death Stranding''.
