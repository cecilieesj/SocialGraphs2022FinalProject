|status= Active}}'''Hattie Hatter''' is one of the main characters of the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Alice's Wonderland Bakery]]'' He is a descendant of the original [[Mad Hatter]] and one of [[Alice (Alice's Wonderland Bakery)|Alice]]'s best friends and her silliest  friend.

==Physical Appearance== 
Hattie has white hair like original Mad Hatter, brown eyes, and wears a green hat similar to that of his predecessor with the iconic "10/6" tag, green fingerless gloves, a yellow jacket with dual green striped cuffs and pockets, a green plaid bow tie, green plaid pants, yellow dual-striped socks, dark green sneakers with lime green stripes and golden rod shoelaces. 

At bedtime he wears lime green footie pajamas with dark green hats and has his gloves off.

When out for a beach day, he wears a 1920's yellow swimsuit with a dark green anchor on the top and green open-toed flippers.
