'''Gemma''' is one of the twelve Star Darling students at Starling Academy and also one of the supporting characters and protagonists in [[Disney]]'s ''[[Star Darlings]]''.

==Background==
===Personality===
Gemma is Tessa's very funny and energetic little sister.

== Appearance ==
Gemma’s theme color is orange. She is a preteen girl with dark skin, orange eyes, lips, and hair held into mini ponytails. In the series her hair is held into pigtails. Her outfit consists of a yellow dress with an orange belt, orange-striped stockings, and sandals. Her accessories consists of gold bracelets on her wrists and her star-charmed earrings.

On earth, Gemma’s appearance changes to look like a human. Her hair color changes to black with only a streak of orange, and her skin loses its sparkle. Her outfit consists of an orange short-sleeved dress with golden flowers for its design and a pair of sandals to complete her look. The only item that remains the same is her star-charmed earrings.

==Trivia==
*Gemma is named after [[wikipedia:Gemini (astrology)|Gemini]] (♊), the third of the twelve astrological signs of the zodiac, which means the twins.
