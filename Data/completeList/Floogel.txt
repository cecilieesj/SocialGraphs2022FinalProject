'''Floogels''' are gigantic insects featured in the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' episode "[[Thornberry to the Rescue]]".

==Background==
[[Cavin]] and [[Sir Thornberry]] have a run-in with one of the creatures, and briefly take it for a flight.
