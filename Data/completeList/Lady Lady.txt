'''Lady Lady'''  is a minor character from Disney's ''[[The Replacements]]''. She's a professional wrestler who settled [[Tasumi]] and Riley's feud for Todd in "[[Best Friends For-Never?]]". She got married to the Canadian Knucklehead in "Serf's Up" before being interrupted by a rude guest "Abraslam Lincoln" for reasons not known. Also appeared as one of Dr. Scorpius' minions in "Irreplaceable".
