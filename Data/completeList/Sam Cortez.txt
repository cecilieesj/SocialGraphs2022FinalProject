'''Sam Cortez''' is a character from ''[[Beverly Hills Chihuahua]]'' and its sequels. He is [[Rachel Ashe]]'s husband, [[Vivian Ashe]]'s nephew-in-law, and Mr. and Mrs. Cortez's son. He's a landscaper who use to care for Vivian's garden and now works at the Langham Hotel.
