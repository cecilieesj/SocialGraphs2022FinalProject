The '''Nutcracker Sentries''' were characters deleted from the "[[Piano Concerto No. 2]]" segment of ''[[Fantasia 2000]]''.

==Role in the film==
The Sentries would have stood guard outside the toy castle and would have assisted the [[Jack-in-the-Box (Fantasia)|Jack-in-the-Box]] in his mission to woo the [[Ballerina]] and keep away the [[Tin Soldier]].
