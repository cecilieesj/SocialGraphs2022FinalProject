|imagesize = 110px}}'''Eli Excelsior Pandarus''' is an antagonist from the animated television show, ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.
==History==
[[File:Eli_Pandarus.jpg|thumb|left|Eli Pandarus in Season One]]Pandarus is the Wizard of Wall Street and desires to be the most powerful magical being. He is first seen working with a couple of [[Trolls (American Dragon)|Troll]] henchmen, plotting to steal gold from Leprechauns.<ref>[[The Heist]]</ref> In Season 1, Pandarus is fairly cowardly, as he cowers and begs not to be hurt after he is defeated.<ref>[[Eye of the Beholder]]</ref> In Season 2, he appears less cowardly and is much more wicked, being more willing to kill his enemies and those who get in his way.<ref>[[A Befuddled Mind]]</ref>

Pandarus' Manhattan headquarters are located in the luxurious Pandarus Towers.
