==History==
He was the third mate to Captain Grant of the Britannia, but he planned a mutiny on him and his two other mates, McNabb and [[Bill Gaye]] and sent them off to the Maories. He did the same thing to Mary and Robert Grant, [[Jacques Paganel]], Lord Glenarvin, and his son John. But then he got arrested along with his men.
