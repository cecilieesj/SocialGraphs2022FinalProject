==Role in the [[Sofia the First|series]]==
Sassofras first appears after [[Clover]], who was left behind by accident, arrives in the forest. After Clover tells her he needs to get back to the castle, she agrees to help him. Along the way, she teaches Clover the importance of valuing what people have. When they finally arrive at the castle, they learn from [[Rex (Sofia the First)|Rex]] that [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]] had learned from [[Barley]] that Clover had been left behind and went out to go look for him. They head out to find the pair. Upon doing so, Sassafras takes Barley home with her.
