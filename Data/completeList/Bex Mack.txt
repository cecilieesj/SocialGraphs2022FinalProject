== Background ==
Bex Mack is the free-spirited daughter of the Mack family. She has a notable sense of adventure. After a long absence, she returns home on her alleged younger sister [[Andi Mack (character)|Andi's]] thirteenth birthday to get her life back in order.

She later reveals to Andi that she is not her sister, but her mother.
