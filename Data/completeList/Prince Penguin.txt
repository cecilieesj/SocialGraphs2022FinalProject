==Background==
===Personality===
Selfish, he loves all thing beautiful and wishes to own them. He hates the cold, but believes he will melt if not kept cold due to some legends of his people.

===Physical appearance===
He is a large penguin dressed in elaborate royal clothes and an odd shaped crown. He has a moustache on his beak and wears a monocle. He wears several rings. Oddly, he also seems to have thick lips, despite having a beak.
