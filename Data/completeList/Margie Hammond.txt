'''Margery "Margie" Leslie Hammond''' is one of the main characters in [[Disney Channel]]'s [[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century|''Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century'']] Trilogy. At first she was [[Zenon Kar]]'s enemy but, by the end of [[Zenon: The Zequel]] they became friends.

== Background ==

=== Personality ===
Margie is a very smart girl. At first Margie was mean to others because she doesn’t know how to fit in. She later became nice and ended up becoming friends with [[Zenon Kar]]. She is very independent.

== Biography ==

=== Early Life ===
Margery Leslie Hammond was born on Earth between 2035-2036. She is the daughter of a General in the Military General Hammond. Her father General Hammond has been in the Military her whole life growing up she moved around a lot due to her father's job. She had hard time fitting moving around a lot.

== Appearances ==
===''[[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century]]''===

Margie was sitting with her friends Andrew, Greg and Matt at a restaurant at a table next to [[Zenon Kar]] and Aunt Judy. Margie goes up to [[Zenon Kar]] and starts making fun of her outfit. Then Margie later ends up seeing [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] again on [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]]’s first day of school on Earth when [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] and Margie ended up in the same class. Margie gives [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] a hard time by making fun of her. One day in Gym class when the class is practicing swimming in the school’s pool Margie who is standing right behind [[Zenon Kar]] in line pushes [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] into the pool on purpose. Then one day in science class Margie and [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] get paired up as lab partners for a science experiment. [[Zenon Kar|Zenon]] ends up blowing up their science experiment by causing a fire.
