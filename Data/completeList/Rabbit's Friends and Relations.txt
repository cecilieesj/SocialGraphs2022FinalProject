|alignment = Good}}'''Rabbit's Friends and Relations''' are deleted characters from ''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]''. They originally appeared in the original books by A.A. Milne.

Rabbit's Friends and Relations are also present in the [[Hundred Acre Wood]] in ''Pooh's Neighborhood'', a book in the ''My Very First Winnie the Pooh'' line. In this title, [[Winnie the Pooh]] passes by nine of Rabbit's friends and relations, all living in trees, while out for a walk in the Wood.
