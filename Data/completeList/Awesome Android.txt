'''Awesome Android''' is a brick-headed android.

==History==
The Awesome Android was lying in the [[S.H.I.E.L.D. Helicarrier]]'s lab taken by [[Spider-Man]] as a project for the science fair. The Awesome Android was brought to the science fair, there he formed a body and began eating up inorganic materials, making him bigger and stronger. Then he wreaked havoc around Midtown High School. But he was stopped by Spider-Man and his team-mates.

==Powers and abilities==
Awesome Android's powers increases when he eats inorganic materials he could find but has weaknesses on ice. He shows to be a formidable fighter even in his childish attitude.
