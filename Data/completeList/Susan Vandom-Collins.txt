== Comics ==
[[Susan Vandom-Collins]] is the mother of central character, [[Will Vandom]]. She divorced Tony Vandom (in the comics, his gambling addiction was the result) and later moved to Heatherfield with Will. She begins dating Will's history, [[Dean Collins]] after taking an immediate interest in him. Their relationship gets more serious to the point where Susan and Dean get married, making Dean Will's stepfather. In Issue 80 (Emotions), Susan gives birth to her and Dean's son, [[William Collins]]. She is shown to be very protective of her two children.
