Dave Karp is a hockey player from Minneapolis, [[Minnesota]]. His first and only appearance was in the film,Â ''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''. He played for the [[District 5 Ducks]].

<br />

== Biography ==
Karp plays as a defenceman and wears number 11. He is comedic and light-hearted; Karp loves to play pranks. At first, he is sarcastic toward Coach Bombay when the latter appears to start coaching the team, but eventually comes to accept him. He is portrayed by [[Aaron Schwartz]].
<br />

== Trivia ==

* Karp does not appear or is mentioned in the sequels, along with these characters: Tammy Duncan, Peter Mark, Terry Hall and Tommy Duncan.
