}}'''The Little House''' is a character and the small country house who survives encroaching life in the big city in the [[1952]] animated short, ''[[The Little House]]'' (based on Virginia Lee Burton's classic book).

==Background==
==='''Physical Appearance'''===
She is a beautiful white anthropomorphic Gothic Revival architectural house with red lip-like steps, a door with a golden doorknob, two window-like eyes with blue curtains and black window openings and eyelids, a brick chimney in the middle of her, a gable roof with red roof tiles, two Nautilus shell-like corbels with two hipped cross gables and two pop-out-like eyebrows.
