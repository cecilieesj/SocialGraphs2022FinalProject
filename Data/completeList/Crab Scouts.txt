The '''Crab Scouts''' are three child crabs (Clawdia, Tuffy & Buster) who attended [[Sebastian]]'s Crab Scout activity in ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]'' television series. They were voiced by J.D. Daniels, [[Anndi McAfee]], Whitby Hertford and Malachi Pearson.
