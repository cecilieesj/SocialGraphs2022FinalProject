'''King Cobra''' (real name Klaus Vorhees) is the leader of the [[Serpent Society]]. He was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Don Heck.

==Powers & Abilities==
He has tasers on his wrists, and can shoot missiles from his chest. He can form a metallic tail from his belt which covers his legs.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===
During the [http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Breakout_(Event) Breakout], he broke out of the [http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/The_Big_House Big House] and tried to attack [[Henry Pym|Hank Pym]]. Hank Pym managed to escape from his grasp and escape. Later, he and the Serpent Society battled the Avengers in a subway.

Unfortunately, Hank Pym's pacifist decisions cause the [http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Serpent_Society Serpent Society] to escape.

Eventually, he was captured by the [http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Skrull Skrulls] and replaced with [http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/King_Cobra_(Skrull) an impostor]. When [[Captain America]] and [[Viper|Madame Hydra]] led an escape from the Skrull spaceship, he joined them and fought against the [[Super Skrull]].

During the battle in Washington, D.C., King Cobra helped the Avengers against the Skrulls. After the battle, both King Cobra and Madame Hydra were imprisoned by [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]
