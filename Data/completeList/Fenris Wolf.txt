The '''Fenris Wolf''' is a large [[Asgardians|Asgardian]] wolf originating from [[Marvel Comics]]. It is an adversary to the Norse god [[Thor Odinson|Thor]]. A live-action version of the character appears in ''[[Thor: Ragnarok]]''.

This version of the mythological Fenris Wolf was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Thor: Ragnarok]]''====
Fenris wolf appears in the film as an ally to [[Hela]], although this version of the character is female. She is the loyal pet of Hela, and is resurrected alongside Hela's army to aid Hela in ruling Asgard. Later, Fenris battles the [[Hulk]] and after a fierce brawl, gets tossed off the side of Asgard to her death into the void below.

===Marvel Animated Universe===
====''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''====
The Fenris Wolf appears in the episode "Meet Captain America". It was one of the mythical specimens that were captured by [[Red Skull]] upon discovering an entrance to [[Asgard]]. Due to a control collar around its neck, the wolf was forced to obey Red Skull; after it is released however, it ends up fighting [[Captain America]] and [[Bucky Barnes|Bucky]].

====''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''====
The Fenris Wolf appears in the two-part episode "Avenging Spider-Man" as a host for the [[Venom]] symbiote among the other Asgardian creatures as part of a takeover conducted by [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]] and [[Doctor Octopus]]. After [[Spider-Man]] switches minds with Loki, the Venom-wolf attacks Spider-Man in Loki's body until it is blasted away by Loki's scepter. It runs into Spider-Man once again (after Spider-Man had returned to own body). This time, Spider-Man was aided by [[Sam Wilson|Falcon]], so they managed to remove the symbiote from Fenris and shock it into unconsciousness. After Loki is transferred to another realm, the Fenris Wolf, [[Ulik]], and the rest of the Asgardian creatures confront Loki as revenge for Loki controlling them.

====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====
In the episode "A Friend in Need", the wolf is shown incarcerated in Asgard prison, while its history with Tyr is briefly mentioned by Thor.

==Trivia==
*In Marvel Comics and in Norse mythology, Fenris and [[Hela]] are actually [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]]'s children.
