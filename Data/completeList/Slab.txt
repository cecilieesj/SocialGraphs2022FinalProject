}}'''Slab''' is a one-shot character from the TV show ''[[Quack Pack]]''. He appears in the episode "[[Can't Take a Yolk (Quack Pack)|Can't Take a Yolk]]".

==Role in the episode==
Slab first shows up when he sees [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]] talking to [[Alexandra and Cassandra Mergancer]]. He grabs the nephews, claiming that the girls are his and are just unaware of it. Just as he's about to give the nephews the boot, [[Donald Duck|Donald]] walks in, looking like he did in the early days.
