'''Professor Ichabod Grogg''' is one of the main antagonists in ''[[Return to Halloweentown]]''. Grogg is one of the professors in [[Witch University]] and a member of [[The Dominion|the Dominion]], an evil organization who wants to rule [[Halloweentown (place)|Halloweentown]].

He is played by Scott Stevenson. He is 70 years old as of May 4, 2021. His first and final Disney Original Movie is [[Return to Halloweentown]].
