'''Princess Alrgelbaccch Blunkenthorttthhhph''' is the princess of "Alien World" from ''[[Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero]]''.

==Background==
==Appearance==
She has a slender alien body with lilac purple skin, long lilac purple hair, with the violet wind and wears a blue-green headband, 4 long and pointed ears, gold necklace, gold armband, blue earrings

===Personality===
The Princess is extremely snobbish, spoiled, deluded, and self-important, assuming everyone is infatuated with her, whereas most seem annoyed at her very presence, except for [[Penn Zero| Penn]] and [[Boone]].
