|likes = Doc and Donny, working in the McStuffinsville Hospital}}'''Grandma McStuffins''' is a recurring character who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. She is Doc and Donny's grandmother.

==Role in the series==
At the start of "[[Bringing Home Baby]]", Grandma McStuffins arrives to take care of Doc and Donny when Mom and Dad get the call that [[Maya McStuffins|the new baby]] is on the way. In "[[Toy Hospital: Welcome to McStuffinsville|Welcome to McStuffinsville]]", she reveals her own magical stethoscope and teleports her and Doc to McStuffinsville and puts Doc in charge of the McStuffinsville Hospital.
