|dislikes = Her Haley's Comet model sabotaged.}}'''Bernice Beachmont'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0395124/</ref> is a ''[[Fillmore!]]'' character and an X Middle School student. She is voiced by Rachel Crane.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0186414/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Bernice/</ref>

==Appearance==
She's a caucasian teen girl with pale blue, beady eyes and brown hair with a fringe, and pigtails, each with an aquamarine hair band. She wears goldenrod, horn-rimmed glasses, a purple jacket with orange plaid lines, a yellowish green shirt, turquoise pants and brown shoes.

==History==
As seen in <ref>"The Shreds Fell Like Snowflakes,"</ref> papers that were part of her model of Haley's Comet that she has on display at a science exhibit were shredded. When she finds that her work was sabotaged, she freaks. When the Safety Patrol arrive at the scene of the crime, she signs a form pertaining to the accounts of the crime.
