'''Amber Dearly''' is a minor character from ''[[101 Dalmatians: The Series]]'' who appeared in the episode "[[Beauty Pageant Pandemonium]]". She is [[Roger Radcliffe|Roger Dearly]] and [[Anita Radcliffe|Anita]]'s niece.

==Background==
===Personality===
She is sweet, caring and kind to the Dalmatians on the farm. She is also very talented, training the pups to jump through a hoop being one of her talents.

===Physical appearance===
Amber is a young girl around eight or nine years of age. She has fair skin, blue-green eyes, long strawberry-blonde hair wrapped in a high ponytail with a turquoise hair tie and wears a turquoise shirt with a yellow flower on it, a violet skirt, turquoise socks and violet flats.

==Trivia==
*It is unknown which side of the Dearly couple Amber comes from. Her appearance suggests that she is likely from Anita's side of the family, but her surname suggests that she is likely from Roger's side of the family because they share the same surname.
