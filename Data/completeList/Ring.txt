==Role==

Ring is a very minor character who only appeared in "[[Ring Around the Planet]]".

==Trivia==

*He is able to grow and shrink when ever he wants.
*Like a lot of characters in the show, his voice is a musical instrument.
*June watches him every night through her telescope.
* In real life, the rings lie in orbit around Saturn, but the other three gas giants (Jupiter, Uranus and Neptune) also possess ring systems.
