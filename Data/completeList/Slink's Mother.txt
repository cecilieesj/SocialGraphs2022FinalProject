'''Slink's Mother''' is a minor character from ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''. She is a giant sea serpent who lives in the Cave of Neptune at the bottom of the Never Sea with her baby [[Slink]].

==Role in the Series==
While [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]]Â and his crew were exploring the wonders of the Never Sea with Stormy they reach the Cave of Neptune when they hear Slink's Mother roar mistaken it for [[Slink]] on their first meeting. Not wanting to invoke the rage of Slink's mother, they decide to return to [[Pirate Island (Jake and the Neverland Pirates)|Pirate Island]] unknown to them that the baby sea serpent has followed them back to the island and later gets captured by [[Sharky and Bones]] as the main attraction of [[Captain Hook]]'s petting zoo. Slink's Mother appears later in the episode thanks to Stormy to prevent Hook and his crew from escaping with her baby.
