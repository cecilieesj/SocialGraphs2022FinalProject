'''Tiffany Willson''' is a character from ''[[The Replacements]]''. She is the younger sister of [[Abbey Willson]]. She dated [[Todd Daring]] for a short time in "[[A Little Tiff]]". She was voiced by Imani Hakim, who is best known for playing Tonya in the live-action sitcom ''Everybody Hates Chris''.
