'''Maria Stark''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character, portrayed by [[Hope Davis]].

Maria Stark was created by Bill Mantlo, George Tuska, Frank Giacoia, Mike Esposito and Dave Cockrum.

==Background==
Maria Collins Carbonell Stark, also known as Maria Stark, was the wife of [[Howard Stark]].

On [[May 29]], [[1970]], she and her husband had a son together. His name was [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]].

On [[December 16]], [[1991]], she and her husband were assassinated by [[Bucky Barnes]] in [[Russia|Siberia, Russia]].

==Relationships==

===[[Howard Stark]]===
Maria married [[Howard Stark]], who became a devoted husband despite being a womanizer during his youth. He is also the CEO of [[Stark Industries]] before.

===[[Iron Man|Tony Stark]]===
Maria's intelligent son, Tony Stark was born on [[May 29]], [[1970]]. After Maria and Howard's death, Tony became the CEO of [[Stark Industries]].

===[[Bucky Barnes|Winter Soldier]]===
Maria was killed by Barnes who was known as the Winter Soldier. Her husband, Howard was also killed. Barnes was the ally of [[Captain America]] and the [[Howling Commandos]] during their battle against [[H.Y.D.R.A.]].
