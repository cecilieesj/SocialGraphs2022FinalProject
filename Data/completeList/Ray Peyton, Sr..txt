|likes=Maggie involved in races (currently), his family, racing}}'''Ray Peyton, Sr.''' is the owner of Team Peyton and [[Maggie Peyton|Maggie]] and Ray Jr.'s father in ''[[Herbie: Fully Loaded]]''.

== Personality ==
Ray Peyton, Sr. is very over-protective of Maggie due to his families past traumas dealing with injuries and possible death, especially how accidental Ray Jr. is and the death of his wife.

He appears to be super sensitive and hates being being lied to.

Despite his problems, he is genuinely caring, well-meaning, and fatherly.

== Trivia ==
*Michael Keaton would later voice [[Chick Hicks]], the main antagonist of ''[[Cars]]'', who is a dirty racer. However, Ray Peyton Sr was a crew chief, not a racer.
