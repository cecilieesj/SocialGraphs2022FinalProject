'''Wolfie''' is a special agent and is one of the two deuteragonists of the [[2009]] animated [[Playhouse Disney]]/[[Disney Junior]] show ''[[Special Agent Oso]]''. He assists [[Oso]] (his best friend) in his training exercises.

==Appearance==
Wolfie is blue wolf, he has golden-brown eyes.

==Trivia==
*Wolfie's line "Now pay attention, Oso" is an oblique reference to Q's line in ''James Bond'', "Now pay attention, 007."
*His favorite color is blue.
*He is the only Special Agent did not shown his jetpack.
