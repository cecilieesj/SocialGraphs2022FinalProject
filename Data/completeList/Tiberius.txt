}}'''Tiberius''' is a tiger prince who appears in the episode "[[Temple of the Tiger]]".

==Appearance==
Tiberius is a baby tiger with gray fur and dark blue stripes. He has blue irises and a dark pink nose. He also wears a collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol. He spots a diaper with green trim and leaf symbols on them.Â As of "[[Adventures In Tigersitting]]", he wears a gold crown on his head.

==Personality==
He is a mischievous and very playful tiger who likes to run around and get dirty despite Pip and Freddy's insistence on keeping him clean. According to Freddy, he likes "Long naps, playing with yarn, and that cozy snugly feeling of a fresh diaper".

==Trivia==
*He is voiced by Julian Edwards, who also voiced Jed, one of the three young storks in the episode Training Daze. Julian is best known for his performance of "Boy" in the Netflix movie Bird Box staring Sandra Bullock
*While not based on a specific breed of tiger, he and his parents could be based on the reported but unproven breed of tiger known as the Maltese Tiger https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maltese_tiger
*He speaks for the first time in "[[Adventures In Tigersitting]]"
