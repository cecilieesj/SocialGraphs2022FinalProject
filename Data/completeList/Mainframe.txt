'''Mainframe''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]'', voiced by Jeffrey Combs, and in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'', voiced by [[Miley Cyrus]]. Mainframe is an android built by [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]] in order to help him protect the universe and defeat the forces of evil. Mainframe is equipped with all the powers and weapons of the [[Iron Man (armor)|Iron Man armor]]. Mainframe is connected to [[F.R.I.D.A.Y.]] so he can stay in contact with Tony Stark no matter how far away he is.

In the MCU, she befriended [[Yondu Udonta|Yondu]] and formed the original team of Ravagers alongside [[Stakar Ogord]], Martinex, [[Aleta Ogord]], [[Krugarr]] and [[Charlie-27]].

==Trivia==
*In the comics, Mainframe is a future version of [[Vision]] in the [[w:c:marvel:Earth-691|alternative 31st Century of Earth-691]] and a companion of the original Guardians of the Galaxy.
*[[James Gunn]] chose to cast [[Miley Cyrus]] as the voice of Mainframe after watching her in The Voice.
*The structure of the character is similar to the Cybermen from ''Doctor Who''.
