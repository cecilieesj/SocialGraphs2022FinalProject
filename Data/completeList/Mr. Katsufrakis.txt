[[File:Char_32103.jpg|thumb]]'''Mr. Katsufrakis'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398846/</ref> is  character from [[The Weekenders]]. He is voiced by [[Jeff Bennett]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/The-Weekenders/Tishs-Dad/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0071818/</ref>

A college professor, his English is a lot clearer than his wife's. He was nicknamed "The Brain" until he grew a beard and was thus nicknamed "Beardy-man".
