|dislikes = His sons not following his orders and attacking Alamut}}'''King Sharaman''' is a minor character from the film ''[[Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time]]''. He is portrayed by [[Ronald Pickup]].



==Background==
King Sharaman was the king of Persia and father to [[Garsiv]], [[Tus]], and [[Prince Dastan|Dastan]], the princes of Persia. He exhibited many of same qualities, such as bravery, a sense of honor and a love for his sons, including Dastan, whom he adopted from the streets of Persia. He was murdered by his brother [[Nizam]], but he was returned to life when Dastan triggered the Grand Rewind near the end of the film.
