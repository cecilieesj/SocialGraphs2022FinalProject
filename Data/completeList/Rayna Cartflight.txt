|parents=[[Mrs. Cartflight]] (mother)}}'''Rayna Jane Cartflight''' is [[Maggie Pesky|Maggie]]'s best friend from ''[[The Buzz on Maggie]]''. She is voiced by [[Cree Summer]].

==Life style==
===Personality===
<p style="line-height:21px;">Rayna is a pretty smart student at Buzzdale Academy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hotfortutor_1-0">[http://buzzonmaggie.wikia.com/wiki/Rayna_Cartflight#cite_note-hotfortutor-1 [2]]</sup> She's also fashionable and very perky.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hotfortutor_1-1">[http://buzzonmaggie.wikia.com/wiki/Rayna_Cartflight#cite_note-hotfortutor-1 [2]]</sup> Unlike Maggie's selfish plans, Rayna's are nearly impossible to perform.</p>

===Appearance===
Rayna has a big pair of yellow cat-eye glasses, a horizontal-striped shirt and a green skirt. Her hair is dark blue.

===Family===
Rayna's family is unknown, only her mother appears in a few episodes. It is unknown if she has any siblings.
