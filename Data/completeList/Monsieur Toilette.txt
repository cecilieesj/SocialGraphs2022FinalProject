'''Monsieur Toilette''' is a minor character in the [[2017]] remake film, ''[[Beauty and the Beast (2017 film)|Beauty and the Beast]]''. His scene was cut from the film, but is featured in the deleted scenes.

==Appearances==
===''[[Beauty and the Beast (2017 film)|Beauty and the Beast]]''===
In the first deleted scene, [[LeFou]] tries to take refuge in a room during the villagers' fight with the servants only to have a frightful encounter with Monsieur Toilette. After the battle against [[Gaston]] stops, Monsieur Toilette and all the other castle residents are transformed into inanimate objects.

When [[Enchantress|Agathe]] uses her magic to restore [[The Enchanted Rose|the Enchanted Rose]], Monsieur Toilette is transformed back into his human form, along with the other castle servants. In another deleted scene LeFou encounters Monsieur Toilette in his human form who he identifies from the bathroom smell and was shown in a traumatized/relieved state. When LeFou asks him what he is going to do now, Monsieur Toilette states that he's going to go brush his teeth. He walks away pleased that he is human again as he passes by [[Chef Bouche|Cuisiner]] and [[Coat Rack|Chapeau]].
