'''Melina Bianco''' is a recurring character in the tv series ''[[Lizzie McGuire]]''. Melina is a friend of [[Matt McGuire]] who loved getting [[Lanny Onasis]] and Matt into trouble and eventually became Matt's girlfriend. She was first introduced as Matt's enemy, but they both began to have feelings for each other.

==Trivia==
*Melina's birthday is June 12.
