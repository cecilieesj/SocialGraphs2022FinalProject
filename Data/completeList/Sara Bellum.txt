'''Dr. Sara Bellum''' is the head of [[S.H.U.S.H.]]'s research division and somehow both profoundly intelligent and spectacularly stupid at the same time. Her goal is to win the Nobel Prize with the inventions she creates for S.H.U.S.H.

==Trivia==
*Her name is a play on "cerebellum", Latin for "brain".
*There is [[w:c:powerpuffgirls:Sara Bellum|a character]] in ''[[w:c:powerpuff:The Powerpuff Girls (1998 TV Series)|The Powerpuff Girls]]'' with the same name.
*There is also a character with the same name in ''Carmen Sandiego''.
