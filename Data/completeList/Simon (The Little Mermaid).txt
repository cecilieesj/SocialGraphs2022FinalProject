'''Simon''' is a [[Dragons|sea dragon]] and a one-time character of the episode "[[Message in a Bottle]]" in [[Disney Television Animation|Disney]]'s animated series, ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]''.

==Appearances==

===''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid: The Series]]''===

===="[[Message in a Bottle]]"====
Imprisoned in cave and looking to be rescued. He writes a message and puts it in a bottle and with some luck, [[Ariel]], [[Sebastian]], and [[Flounder]] find the letter, brave danger in route to the cave, and then they face a giant sea dragon. The dragon turns out to be Simon himself, the writer of the message, who's looking to have a party with some new friends. Bringing Simon home to [[King Triton]] poses another challenge, until Simon helps King Triton to save [[Atlantica]] and its merpeople from an invasion of evil sea anemones.
