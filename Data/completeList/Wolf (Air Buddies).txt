The '''Wolf''' is a minor character from ''[[Air Buddies]]''. He is a gray wolf who helps the Buddies find shelter and later takes them to Wine Country to find [[Air Bud (dog)|their]] [[Molly (Air Bud)|parents]].

==Trivia==
*He is one of the few live-action [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] wolves to be friendly in nature.
