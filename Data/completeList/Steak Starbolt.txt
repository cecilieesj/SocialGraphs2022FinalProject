"'''Steak Starbolt'''" is a sentient piece of steak who is also the star of [[Danny Douglas]]'s favorite tv show.
==Appearance==
Steak Starbolt is a steak with a humanoid body. He wears a blue bodysuit with yellow boots, and he has a blaster for a left hand.
