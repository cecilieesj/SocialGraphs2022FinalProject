'''Chain Gang Chuck''' is a minor character from the episode "[[A Ghost Story]]" from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.
==History==
Chain Gang Chuck was once a Civil War soldier who died in battle. He later became part of [[Shackles Jack|Shackles Jack's]] troop of ghosts that haunts [[Camp Mugwomp]].
