|quote = |status=Active}}'''Princess Caterina of Cariza''' is a character who appears in the Disney Channel animated series, ''[[Elena of Avalor]]''. She's the daughter of [[King Joaquin]] and Queen Teresa.



==Background==
===Personality===
Caterina is a precocious girl whose very adventurous. She also loves being called "Cat". She also admits to not being into sports.

'''Physical Appearance'''

Princess Caterina has a  brown skin , brown hair and big blue eyes . She wears a green dress with a orange strip and earring that matches her dress

==Role in the Series==
In her debut appearance, Caterina arrives in the Kingdom of Avalor with her family. Cat becomes fast friends with Elena's sister Princess Isabel, who she goes on a tour with. In town, they come across the ''Voladores''. Isabel confides in her how she's always wanted to see how the ''Voladores'' spinning tower works. The next day, a bored Cat sneaks away with an equally bored Isabel to the ''Voladores'' tower. They ride to the top where they get stuck. They get rescued by Gabe.
