'''Martian Mickey''' is an alien counterpart of [[Mickey Mouse]], who appears in the ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]'' episodes "[[Mickey's Message from Mars]]", "[[Mickey's Show and Tell]]", "[[Space Adventure]]", "[[Mickey's Sport-Y-Thon]]", and "[[Martian Minnie's Tea Party]]". He would later return in the ''[[Mickey Mouse Funhouse]]'' episodes "[[Spaced Out!]]" and "[[Polka Dots and Don'ts]]".
