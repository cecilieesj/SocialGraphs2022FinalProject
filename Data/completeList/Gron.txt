'''Gron''' is a minor protagonist in the [[Disney XD]] animated series ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''. He is a male Lasat who served under captain [[Garazeb Orrelios]] in the Lasan Honor Guard.

==Background==
At some point in his life, Gron became part of the Lasan Honor Guard, and was one of the few that survived the Fall of Lasan. He later escaped with a Lasat elder named Chava, and became her traveling companion.
