{{Infobox_character
|image = King_Yo.png}}'''King Yo''' is formerly the ruler of planet Bling in [[The 7D]] season two episode ''[[Take Me to Your Grumpy]]''.

He took over the planet, forcing its residents Nabble and Freem to search for a new home. After they crash land in [[Jollywood]], they meet [[Grumpy]], who helped them to be like him in order to get their planet back from King Yo.
