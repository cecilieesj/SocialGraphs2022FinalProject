A piece of paper}}'''Mother Goose''' is [[Ludwig Von Drake]]'s grandmother, who appeared in the ''[[Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color]]'' episode'' [[The Truth About Mother Goose (Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color episode)|The Truth About Mother Goose]]'' (first aired in [[1963]]).

In the episode, centered around the classic "Mother Goose rhymes", Ludwig brags about the famous author Mother Goose being his own grandmother. She is seen sitting in her rocking chair on a painting in her grandson's library, along with Ludwig himself as a child and the latter's "childhood companion", [[Herman the Bootle Beetle]].
