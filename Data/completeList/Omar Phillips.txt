'''Omar Phillips'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0215029/</ref> (voiced by [[Kevin Michael Richardson]]) is a supporting character from ''[[The Proud Family]]''.

==History==
He is one of the football players at [[Willy T. Ribbs Middle School]] and one of [[Sticky Webb]]'s friends. He has messy hair and wears a black jacket. Omar first appears in <ref>Rumors</ref> as the school quarterback [[Penny Proud|Penny]] and most other girls in the school have a crush on. Omar appears to be dimwitted, but is a good quarterback. Omar is mostly seen with [[Frankie White|Frankie]], [[Sticky Webb|Sticky]], and [[Duke Anoi|Duke]] mocking Penny and her friends.

==Physical Appearance==
He has crinkly or curly hair, a white t-shirt, black jacket with the collar up, black jogging pants, and black and white athletes shoes.
