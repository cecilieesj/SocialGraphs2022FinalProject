|status=Active}}'''Bimini''' (also spelled '''Biminy''') is a minor character in ''[[Beverly Hills Chihuahua]]'' and [[Beverly Hills Chihuahua 2|its sequel]]. She is absent from the [[Beverly Hills Chihuahua 3: Viva la Fiesta|third film]] along with [[Delta]].

==History==
Bimini's role is very small in the first film. Here she is only seen at the beginning, where she is at the dog groomer's with [[Chloe]] and Delta. She is then seen at the pool with them, and is later mentioned by Chloe as having a date with a dog named Scooter (who is said to chase parked cars and is "crazier than a Labradoodle") She is seen again at the end with Delta and [[Sebastian (Beverly Hills Chihuahua)|Sebastian]], having been invited to a dance show.

In the sequel, her role is a bit larger. She attends Papi's wedding with Sebastian and Delta, and later watches the dog show on TV.
