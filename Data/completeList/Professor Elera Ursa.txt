|voice =April Stewart }}'''Professor Elera Ursa''' is a character in Disney's ''[[Star Darlings]]''. She is the significantly old teacher of the Star Darlings concerning Wishers.
==Appearance==
Ursa wears a purple headress and a dress that is filled with constellations. She has a physical appearance implying advanced age, with many wrinkles around her face and long white hair.
