|alignment = Evil}}'''Bread Baron''' is a villain in [[Future-Worm!]] who appears in the episode "[[Old Man Duck Head]]" as the main antagonist.

==Appearance==
Baron Bread has a loaf of bread for a head, with a piece of butter on the top resembling a beret. He's wearing a red business suit, white shirt, a dull yellow tie and brown shoes with yellow lines.

== Trivia ==
His name is a pun on Red Baron.
