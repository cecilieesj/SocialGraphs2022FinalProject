|fate = Arrested for his crimes and sent to the island prison}}'''Pocket''' is a minor antagonist in ''[[Tangled: The Series]]''. He serves as the right-hand man of [[Lady Caine]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]''===
Pocket assists Lady Caine in her plan to disrupt Rapunzel's coronation and imprison all the guests. Eventfully he is defeated and sent to prison with his boss.

===''[[Tangled: The Series]]''===
In "[[Max and Eugene in Peril on the High Seas]]" he is one of the many criminals who took over a prison barge. However their plan was foiled again and they were locked up again.

==Trivia==
*His name is a pun on the action of "Pickpocketing" where one steals stuff from pockets without anyone noticing.
*He seems to serve as Lady Caine's right hand man.
