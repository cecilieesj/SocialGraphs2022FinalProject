'''Gargantos''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appeared in ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]''.

He was a powerful demon summoned by [[Scarlet Witch]] to capture [[America Chavez]], but she was saved by [[Doctor Strange]], who defeated the demon.

Gargantos was created by Roy Thomas, Marie Severin, and Joe Sinnott.

==Trivia==
*Gargantos bears notable similarities with Shuma Gorath, who couldn't be used in the movie due to the rights of the character belonging to the Robert E. Howard estate.
