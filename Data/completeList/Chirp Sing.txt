|friends = The [[Rescue Rangers]]}}'''Chirp Sing''' is [[Emperor Dim Sun]]'s favorite nightingale in ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]''. He considers himself a curse because he believes he's responsible for driving the Emperor insane. He appears in the episode [[Song of the Night 'n Dale]].



==Trivia==
*His name is a pun, as he is a bird and the noise birds make is known as a chirp.
*When creator [[Tad Stones]] originally pitched the idea for the ''Rescue Rangers'' show, it included a cricket with the same name.
