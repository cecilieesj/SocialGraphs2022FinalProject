'''[[File:Tuck-disneyscreencaps.com-5897.jpg|250px|right]]Miles Tuck''' is an ageless character from ''[[Tuck Everlasting]]''. He is first seen wearing a black top hat an a brown coat. He is fond of gambling. He got into a fight at a bar with his opponent and was thrown out. He got married and had two children, Beau and Anna. His wife left him and took Anna and Beau with her because she thought he sold his soul to the devil but he kept the ring. She died in an insane asylum. Anna died of influenza before she turned fifteen. Beau was eighty if he were alive.
