[[File:Lance.jpg|thumb]]'''Lance''' is a character of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Avalon High]]''. He is portrayed by Christopher Tavarez.
==History==
He is the football jock who is Will's best friend and He goes behind Will's back to cheat with Jen. He seems to have no real intention to hurt Will, like Jen, however he can't seem to deny his attraction to Jen.
