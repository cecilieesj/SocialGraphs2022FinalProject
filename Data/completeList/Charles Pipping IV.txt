||shows = ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''}}'''Charles Pipping IV''' is a boy [[Candace Flynn|Candace]] meets in [[England]]. She develops a crush on him. Charles is oblivious to it, but is nice to Candace. He has a British accent that Candace adores. Interestingly, Charles bears a remarkable similarity to Candace's normal crush [[Jeremy Johnson]]. He has a good sense of humor. Charles competed in a jousting tournament against [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]] and [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]].
==Background Information==
*His family lives next door to [[Reginald Fletcher|Reginald]] and [[Winifred Fletcher]], Ferb's grandparents.
*His castle used to be owned by the Black Knight.
*He looks remarkably similar to [[Jeremy Johnson]], as they both have a round head and blond hair.
