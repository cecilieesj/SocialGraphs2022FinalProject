'''Camryn Barnes''' (biological name: '''Apolla''') is the main protagonist of ''[[Twitches]]'' and ''[[Twitches Too]]''. Camryn is the daughter of Aron and Miranda DuBaer. She is [[Alexandra Fielding]]'s twin sister. She is played by [[Tamera Mowry]].

==Story==
Camryn was born on Coventry island along with [[Alexandra Fielding]].
