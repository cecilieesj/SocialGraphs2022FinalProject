'''Fritz''' is a minor character in the [[2015]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]•[[Pixar]] animated feature film, ''[[Inside Out]]''. He is a blue-green Mind Worker with a mustache and no hair who installs an upgraded console, which includes a large button labelled "PUBERTY", in [[Emotion Headquarters|Headquarters]] when [[Riley Andersen|Riley]] turns 12.

==Role in the [[Inside Out|film]]==
Fritz only appears at the end of the film where he explains to the emotions that he and the other mind workers have built a new console in Headquarters after Riley adapts for having a new life in [[San Francisco]]. After talking, he explains to them that the emotions are ready to use the new console as he and the other mind workers leave.
