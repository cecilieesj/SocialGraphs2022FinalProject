==Appearance==
Bug is a human-sized fairy with fair skin, buck teeth, green eyes and triple colored wings going from yellow to green to blue. They have purple hair styled as an afro, a yellow headband with a pair of bouncy antenna, a green jumpsuit and brown boots.
