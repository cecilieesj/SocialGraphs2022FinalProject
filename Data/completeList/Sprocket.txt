'''Sprocket''' is a character from one of [[Jim Henson]]'s properties, ''[[w:c:muppets:Fraggle Rock|Fraggle Rock]]''. He appeared with Doc in ''[[w:c:Muppets:A Muppet Family Christmas|A Muppet Family Christmas]]'', and he would later appear as a background character in ''[[The Muppets at Walt Disney World]]'' and ''[[The Muppet Christmas Carol]]''.
