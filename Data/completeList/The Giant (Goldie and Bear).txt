He is a large humanoid character that lives in the clouds at the top of a beanstalk over fairy tale forest. He has a tendency to lose his stuff like his shoe and rubber ducky. He is a big fan of Buttercup ball. He used to be the owner of a [[Goosey]] that laid the golden egg and singing harp until it was taken away by [[Jack (Goldie & Bear)|Jack]].
