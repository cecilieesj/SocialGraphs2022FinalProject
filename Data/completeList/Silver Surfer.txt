'''Silver Surfer''' is an [[Marvel Comics]] character who is an alien being and an ally of Team S.M.A.S.H. He is voiced by [[Brent Spiner]].

Silver Surfer was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==Powers and Abilities==
*'''Cosmic Energy''': Has the power to generate cosmic energy.
*'''Cosmic Ray''': Can shoot cosmic rays from his hands.

==Appearances==
===''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]''===
The Agents of S.M.A.S.H. encounter Silver Surfer where he is fighting Null and is accidentally knocked out by the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. When Silver Surfer regains consciousness, he helps the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. fight the Null. After Null is defeated, Silver Surfer closes the wormhole to Earth from the other side where he vows to the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. that he will tell all of Earth about their heroics against Null.

==Trivia==
*Silver Surfer is mentioned at the beginning of ''[[Flora & Ulysses]]''.
