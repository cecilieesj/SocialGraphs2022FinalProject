'''Officer Hobble''' is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He wears a khaki long-sleeved shirt, brown vest, black gloves, and dark sunglasses.

==History==
Hobble is a law enforcement officer who is usually seen arresting the villains in the episodes that he was in. He has a hard time believing [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]] is capable of saving the day.

==Appearances==
*"[[Sink or Swim]]"
*"[[Royal Pain (episode)|Royal Pain]]"
*"[[October 31st]]"
*"[[Oh Boyz (episode)|Oh Boyz]]"

==Trivia==
*His uniform coloration is consistent with most Middle Western Sheriff's Departments. Which makes sense taking into consideration the locations he mostly appears, such as out in the country at Camp Wannaweep.
