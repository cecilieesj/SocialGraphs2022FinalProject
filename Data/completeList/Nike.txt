[[File:Nike_Goddess_of_Victory,_hercules_disney.jpg|thumb|Nike with Helios, Hermes and Athena]] '''Nike''' (known in Roman as '''Victoria''') is the winged goddess of victory personified by her. She was planned to be in the [[Hercules: The Animated Series|series]], but never appeared, but only mentioned in the Hercules and the Poseidon Adventure. She also appears in a cameo on [[Mount Olympus]] in the film ''[[Hercules (1997 film)|Hercules]]'' in the company of the other [[Olympian Gods|gods]] in the celebration of the birth of [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]].
