'''Frankie''' is a small, yellow canary who appeared in the [[1947]] animated short, ''[[Figaro and Frankie]]''. He was the bane of [[Figaro]]'s existence.

==Role in the short==
Figaro is hungry for Frankie, but the cat must first cross a barrier; [[Minnie Mouse]], the canary's owner. Frankie is also met with [[Butch the Bulldog]], who would also like to eat him.
