==History==
Fred is a student in [[Jake Long|Jake]]'s class at [[Millard Fillmore Middle School]], and is typically an outcast from the "cool" groups. Once, in the 4th grade, he had to go to the nurse's office when he'd gotten a cricket stuck up his nose.

In the episode "Siren Says" Fred enters a bachelor auction, and the only person who bid on (and bought) him was his mother.
