'''Good Ol' Joe''' is a minor character of ''[[Big City Greens]]'', who appeared in "[[Friend Con]]".

==Background==
Good Ol' Joe is a large man with light purple skin and dark purple hair. He has big, black eyebrows and no visible eyes.
