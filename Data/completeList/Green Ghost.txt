The '''Green Ghost''' is a minor character from the episode "[[A Ghost Story]]" from the animated series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
The Green Ghost was once a Civil War soldier who died in prison. He later became part of [[Shackles Jack]]'s troop of ghosts that haunts [[Camp Mugwomp]].

==Appearance==
Unlike Shackles Jack, [[Hook Hand Ghost]], [[Solitary Sam]], [[Chain Gang Chuck]], and [[Peg Leg Pat]], the Green Ghost is the only ghost who has eyes while the rest of the ghosts don't have eyes. He has green skin.
