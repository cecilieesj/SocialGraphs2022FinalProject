{{Infobox_character|image = Firefly (PJ Masks)-0.jpg|name = Firefly|shows = ''[[PJ Masks]]'' (deleted)|appearance = Spiky pink hair, pink eyes, black costume with a bright aqua lines, wings, violet glasses with white-green horns|occupation = Villain|alignment = Bad|enemies = [[Catboy]], [[Owlette]], [[Gekko]], [[PJ Robot]]|voice = [[Jessica DiCicco]]}}'''Firefly '''is a scrapped villain who was supposed to be the overarching antagonist in the second season of [[Disney Junior]] show ''[[PJ Masks]]'', but was never made.
