'''Cacahuate''' is a Spirit sloth who appears in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Elena of Avalor]]''. He is [[Mateo]]'s Spirit Guide who once felt incapable of carrying out his job until some encouragement and determination built up his confidence in becoming a good Spirit Guide.
