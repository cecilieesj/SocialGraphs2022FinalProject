'''Miss Baker''' is [[Loretta Callisto|Loretta's]] pet Robo-Monkey who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. She is a T.T.A. robot who disappeared a few years ago while on a mission until she was found and adopted by the Callisto family.

==Abilities==
*'''Holographic Symbol Projection:''' One of her basic features is projecting holographic symbols above her head which tell people what she is thinking.
*'''Extendable Tail:''' Similar to [[M.E.R.C.]]'s extendable neck, Miss Baker has a tail that can extend to great lengths and has a built in laser on the end.

==Trivia==
*Miss Baker is named after the real monkey that was sent into space by the [[United States]]. She was the first animal sent into space by the U.S. to return alive.<ref name="Twitter">{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/saschapaladino/status/883522546540269569|title=Sascha Paladino post|website=Twitter|date=July 7, 2017}}</ref>
*She is the Callisto's second robot pet, alongside [[M.E.R.C.]]
