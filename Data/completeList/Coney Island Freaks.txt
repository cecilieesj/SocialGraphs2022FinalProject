'''The Coney Island Freaks''' (a muscle man, a bearded lady, and a fire breather) are the main villains of the ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]'' episode "[[Flight of the Unicorn]]".

==History==
They are a sideshow attraction at [[Coney Island]]. They captured a Unicorn for their show, "Master Morgan's Boardwalk Sideshow" but she must go back to her herd before the full moon rises.
