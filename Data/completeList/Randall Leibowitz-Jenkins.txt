'''Randall Leibowitz-Jenkins''' is a new character in the [[Disney+]] Original Series ''[[The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder]]''. He is the banker husband of [[Barry Leibowitz-Jenkins]] and the adopted father of [[Maya Leibowitz-Jenkins|Maya]] and [[KG Leibowitz-Jenkins|KG]].

==Background==
===Personality===
Randall is a 40-year-old, tailored suit-wearing banker and the self-proclaimed “brother in charge” at Wizard Kelly Bank. He’s a sharp-tongued intellectual and dedicated foodie. He works overtime to keep up with his kids, but, most importantly, he encourages them to be who they need to be.<ref>[https://d23.com/meet-the-new-characters-of-the-proud-family-louder-and-prouder/ Meet the New Characters of The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder]</ref>

===Physical appearance===
Randall is a short African-American man who is slightly round with short black hair, a small mustache and round glasses. He is usually seen wearing a grey suit with a pink undershirt and a dark pink tie and dark black pants and shoes.
