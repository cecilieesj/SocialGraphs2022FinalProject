'''Godfrey''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is, as his job implies, a guide dog.

==Appearance==
Godfrey is a Golden Retriever dog with a yellow collar around his neck and is mostly seen with a tired expression on his face.

==Personality==
He is loyal to his unknown owner.
