'''Hermod Odinson''' is a character from [[Marvel Comics]] that appeared in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'', voiced by [[Troy Baker]]. He is the Norse god of speed, the messenger of the Asgardians, and the brother of [[Thor Odinson|Thor]]. He is always ready to help his brother fight the forces of evil and protect Asgard and the rest of the universe.
