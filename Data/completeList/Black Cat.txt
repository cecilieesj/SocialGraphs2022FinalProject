|weapons = Black Cat costume<br>Grappling hooks<br>Retractable claws<br>Equilibrium Earrings<br>Electromagnetic Contact Lenses}}'''Felicia Hardy''' (better known as '''Black Cat''') is a [[Marvel Comics]] anti-hero. She appears as a mission giver in ''[[Disney Infinity: 2.0 Edition|Disney Infinity 2.0]]'' and appears in the ''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]'' animated series.

Black Cat was created by Marv Wolfman, Frank Giacoia and Keith Pollard.

==Background==
Felicia is an attractive young woman with white hair. Her costume has seen many designs through her history, but has always kept some trademark details, like the total black color, white fur and a domino mask around her eyes.

=== Powers and abilities ===
====Powers====
*'''Peak human agility:''' Black Cat is a skilled gymnast capable of olympic level feats of agility, though still limited by human physiology;

====Abilities====
*'''Skilled burglar:''' Felicia in an expert thief, well trained in breaking into secured facilities and stealing items without been seen or caught

==Appearances==
===''[[Spider-Man (TV series)|Spider-Man]]''===
Spider-Man first encounters Black Cat having infiltrated Horizon High to steal the [[Venom|V-252]] for an unknown client. [[Spider-Man]] had a hard time stopping Black Cat's heists due to her bad luck abilities. Eventually, Spider-Man managed to turn the tables on Black Cat's abilities where he ends up immobilizing her with his webs and handing her over to the police.
