'''Phastos''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Eternals (film)|Eternals]]'', played by Brain Tyree Henry.

Phastos was created by [[Jack Kirby]], Peter Gillis, Sal Buscema and Al Gordon.

==Trivia==
*Phastos is the fourth LGBTQ character to be introduced in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
**In the comics, Phastos's sexuality was never mentioned.
