'''Eureka''' is the main protagonist of the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Eureka!|with the same name]]''. A young cavegirl from Rocky Falls, Eureka is a smart girl very much ahead of her time, who often engages in "thinkering" (thinking and tinkering).

==Background==
Eureka lives during the Stone Age, specifically in the location known as Rocky Falls. She lives with her mother Roxy (the owner of Paleo, the only restaurant in Rocky Falls) and her father Rollo (the owner of a pottery shop). She hangs out with her friends Pepper and Barry, and attends classes lead by Ohm.
