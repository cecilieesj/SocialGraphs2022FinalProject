The '''Duke and Duchess of Drear''' are the parents of [[Hildy Gloom]] in the [[Disney XD]] animated series ''[[The 7D]]''.

==Trivia==
*The voices of the Duke and Duchess are provided by Sharon and Ozzy Osbourne, who are in real life the parent's of Hildy Gloom's voice actress, [[Kelly Osbourne]].
**The Duke and Duchess also share some references to their voice actors.
