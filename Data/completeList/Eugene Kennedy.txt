'''Eugene Kennedy'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/eugene.htm</ref> is a minor character from the ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]'' episode "[[A Ghost Story]]".

==History==
Eugene is one of the campers at [[Camp Mugwomp]] who had [[Jake Long|Jake]], [[Trixie Carter|Trixie]], and [[Arthur Spudinski|Spud]] as counselors. He is timid, nervous, and anxious, similar to [[Piglet]]. When he is first seen he is hiding under his camp bed, he wouldn't be brave if it weren't for Jake's confidence.
