'''Crocosaurus''', or '''Crock''', is the main villain in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s ''[[The Wuzzles]]''. He is half-crocodile, half-dinosaur.
==Personality==
Crock is bad-tempered, lazy, vile, ignorant, a bully, and does anything to get what he wants. He always wants the best of what the Wuzzles have, but does not want to apply the effort to acquire it himself. He also has two sidekicks named [[Brat]] and [[Flizard]].
