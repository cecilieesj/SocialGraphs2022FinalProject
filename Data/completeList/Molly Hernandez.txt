'''Molly Hernandez''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in the Hulu series ''[[Runaways (TV series)|Runaways]]'', played by Allegra Acosta. She is a teenager with the power of superhuman strength, with her strength level being nearly the same as [[Hulk]] or [[Thor Odinson|Thor]]. When she finds out that her father and mother are supervillains, she joins the team of superhero fugitives known as [[the Runaways]]. Molly is kind, gentle, noble, and caring, always ready to use her massive strength to protect the innocent and defeat the forces of evil.
