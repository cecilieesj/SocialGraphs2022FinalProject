==Mickey and the Roadster Racers/Mickey Mouse Mixed-Up Adventures==
Mr. Doozy is the manager of the Snoozy Doozy Bed and Breakfast who also works as its bellhop, cook, and repairman.

==Trivia==
*Mr. Doozy was Fred Willard's final role following his death in May 2020.
