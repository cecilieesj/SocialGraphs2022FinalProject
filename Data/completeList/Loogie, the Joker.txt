'''Loogie''' has a funny personality and has a dirty green catfish puppet named '''Dr. Pinch''' he wears on his left hand with a bizarre, alter-personality that constantly criticizes himself (appears to be disassociative identity disorder). Loogie sports rocket-powered in-line skates as transportation. Loogie wears a green costume.

Loogie's the 2nd oldest of the Dojo Team, being younger than Burn but older than Deets, Fizz and Ed.

*He is voiced by Peter Cugno
