|alignment = Good/Neutral|parents=[[President Richard Martinez|Richard Martinez]] (father)<br>Elizabeth Martinez (mother)}}'''Sophia "Sophie" Martinez '''(portrayed by [[Madison Pettis]]) is the daughter of President Richard Martinez. She is a mischievous 8 year-old girl who loves to annoy people, especially [[Cory Baxter|Cory]]. She has two best friends, Haley and Tanisha, who often compete with Sophie at random things, like class president. She is also known as "'''America's Angel'''" by all of [[United States|America]], (though she is not one to Cory) and when her nickname is mentioned she often responds using her catchphrase: "That's what they call me!"
