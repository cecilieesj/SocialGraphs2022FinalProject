'''Mrs. Chen''' is a [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] character who appears in ''[[Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings]]'', played by Jodi Long. She is [[Katy Chen]]‘s mother.

==Appearances==
===''[[Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings]]''===
Mrs. Chen is the mother of [[Katy Chen]], first seen, [[Shang-Chi|Shaun]] goes to her apartment for breakfast, like he does every day as it’s mentioned by her son. She is shown to be very strict as she is seen discussing Katy's career choices with Katy, as Katy said her mom wishes that she was a journalist and do something with her life, after seen a girl writing a research paper on a bus and makes Ruihua help her husband at their family shop.
