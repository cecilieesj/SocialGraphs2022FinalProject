'''Mick''' is [[Bash Johnson]]'s best friend. He is a football player on Norrisville High Football Team. He was part of Bash's band (Bash & the Bros) in the episode "[[So U Think U Can Stank]]". He also appeared in "[[The Tale of the Golden Doctor's Note]]" and bit a volleyball twice, which was an issue that bothered Bash. He also appeared in "[[Secret Stache]]" as a bouncer for the Mustache Society's secret club party.

==Personality==
Mick rarely ever speaks, and acts as the strong, silent type. He's a popular kid, but is a part of Bash's group, meaning he also is a part of the bullying.

==Appearance==
Mick is big as Bash, His hair is grayish black which covers his eyes.

==Trivia==
*He's one of Bash's bros.
*He seems to like biting into dodgeballs, causing them to deflate, much to Bash's annoyance.
*In the episode Secret Stache he has a mustache that resembles Wario's mustache.
*The first time anyone sees him talk is in "Secret Stache".
