|quote = |voice = [[Bernard Fox]]}}{{Quote|Gorillas can be trained to rescue people.|The Professor}}'''The Professor''' is a scientist from the animated series ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]''. 

==Appearance==
The professor is an elderly man with gray hair and a gray mustache. He wears glasses, a lab coat, and black pants. He is also short.

==Information==
The professor works with a gorilla named [[Kookoo and Boots|Kookoo]]. While working with Kookoo, he tries to prove that gorillas can save people. He appeared in the episode "[[Gorilla My Dreams]]."
