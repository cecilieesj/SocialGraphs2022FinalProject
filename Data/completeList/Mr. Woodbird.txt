'''Mr. Woodbird''' is a woodpecker who is the janitor at T.O.T.S. He takes care of maintaining T.O.T.S. HQ, as well as managing the park outside. He enjoys inventing things that can make things easier for the fliers or K.C. in the nursery. He loves making loveys for the babies the most.

He enjoys eating sandwiches while he works. He has a habit of adding long numbers to his inventions even if they're the first one of the kind. He sometimes gives someone his inventions even if he hasn't tested them out himself.  He also loves babies a lot like everyone at T.O.T.S. He achieved his dream of delivering a baby and seeing the happy parents in Season 2.

==Appearance==
He is a woodpecker with a gray tip. He wears a red hair with red eyebrows, He has blue irises, green glasses, a t-shirt yellow, a golden yellow beak and a golden yellow feet.
