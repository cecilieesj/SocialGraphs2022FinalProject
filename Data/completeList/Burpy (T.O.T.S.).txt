==Appearance==
Burpy is a bunny who has grey fur, darker grey hair, and amber eyes, as well a white puffy tail. He has a teal collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol. A white diaper with teal lining, patterned with pine trees and mountains.

==Personality==
Burpy does not have many notable traits. He loves to hop as well as burp a lot.{{T.O.T.S.}}
