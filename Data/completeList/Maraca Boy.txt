[[File:Maraca_boy.png|thumb]]'''The Maraca Boy '''was seen in the episode "Got Stank". He is a minor character and a member of the [[Norrisville High]] band.

<p style="line-height:19.5px;">He was in a scene where he almost got crushed by an instrument shelf that Bucky pushed, and luckily, the ninja stopped from it falling onto the boy.</p>

==Attire==
*'''Civilian Attire''' - khaki shorts and light blue t-shirt with a dolphin
*'''Band Attire''' - blue pants, black shoes, a blue and yellow hat, blue shirt with yellow buttons and lines
*'''Instrument Used'''- maracas
==<span style="line-height:19.5px;">Trivia</span>==
*Maraca boy also had a non-speaking cameo in "Dawn of the Driscoll" where he was speaking to a pumpkin-like girl.
*The Maraca Boy has a brief cameo in "30 Seconds to Math" where he was being hypnotized by Heidi's song.
*Maraca Boy also has a non-speaking cameo in "Viva El Nomicon".
