[[File:Javier.jpg|thumb|250px]]
'''Javier''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil]]''. He is a young, undefeated and intelligent speller of supposedly Spanish descent. After "[[Bee Awesome!]]", he is seen as one of [[Kick Buttowski (character)|Kick]]'s fans.

==Trivia==
*He pronounces the letter R with a Spanish accent. This may prove that he might be of Spanish descent.
