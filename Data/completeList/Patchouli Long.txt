|imagesize = 190px|alignment = |siblings=[[Jonathan Long]] (brother)}}'''Patchouli "Patty" Long'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/auntpatchouli.htm</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0388169/</ref> is a minor character from the television show, ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. She appears in the episode "[[Adventures in Troll Sitting]]". 
==Appearances==
*"[[Act 4, Scene 15]]" (flashback)
*"[[Adventures in Troll Sitting]]" (first appearance)

==Personality==

She is Jonathan's sister, and Jake and Haley's paternal aunt. Like her brother, she is unaware that her sister-in-law and niece and nephew are from a long line of dragons, or that the "man" she is dating—[[Stan Lipkowski]]—is actually a Sewer Troll. Not much else is known about her.

==Trivia==
*Patchouli is the only known paternal relative of Jake's that is seen in the series, although his paternal grandfather is mentioned in Season 2.
