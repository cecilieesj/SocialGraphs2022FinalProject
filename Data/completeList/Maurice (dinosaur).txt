|status = Active}}'''Maurice''' is a one-time character from the sitcom ''[[Dinosaurs]]''. [[Allan Trautman]] voiced him.

==Role in the episode==
Maurice appeared in the episode "[[Charlene's Tale]]". After [[Charlene Sinclair|Charlene]]'s tail had grown, she went out with Maurice on her first date. [[Fran Sinclair|Fran]] was proud of her daughter, but [[Earl Sinclair|Earl]] felt dejected and said Maurice should spend time at [[The Sinclair Home|the Sinclair Home]] instead of going out. Fran distracted Earl by telling him to order ribs and then told Charlene to have a good time. Maurice and Charlene then went out on their date.

At the end of the episode, it was revealed that Maurice had run off with another girl whose tail was bigger than Charlene's. Heartbroken, Charlene walked all the way home.
