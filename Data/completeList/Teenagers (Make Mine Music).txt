|likes = Dancing, upbeat music, ice cream}}A group of '''Teenagers''' appears in the "[[All the Cats Join In]]" segment of the [[1946]] Disney animated feature film ''[[Make Mine Music]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Make Mine Music]]''===
A male teenager is first seen following the artist drawing the scene related to ''All the Cats Join In''. When the couple rides on the car drawn by the artist, many other teenagers hang out in the malt shop the artist draws as they dance to the song. They continue to dance until the jukebox explodes at the end of the segment.

===Other appearances===
They also make a cameo appearance in the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode "[[Couple Sweaters]]" where Mickey and Minnie encounter them dancing at the dancing event much to Mickey not getting used to the sweater he wears. They become overjoyed when they see Mickey's impressive moves. They are not seen in the episode afterward.
