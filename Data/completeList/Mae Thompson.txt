'''Mae Thompson''' is [[Russ Thompson, Sr.|Russ Sr.]]'s wife and [[Russ Thompson, Jr.|Russ Jr.]] and [[Ron Thompson|Ron]]'s mother in ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids]]''.

Mae is rather friendly with the Szalinskis, particularly [[Diane Szalinski|Diane]]. She doesn't like getting into huge arguments, even if she tries to discuss things a little more properly with Russ. She also supports her sons in not being pushed so much, and  constantly tries to reassure Russ of some things, while even telling him that there are some things he'd need to know of himself.{{CharacterStub}}
