'''Fan''' is [[Ebenezer Scrooge]]'s younger sister and [[Fred (A Christmas Carol)|Fred]]'s mother in ''[[A Christmas Carol]]''. She is voiced by [[Robin Wright Penn]], who also voiced [[Belle (Disney's A Christmas Carol)|Belle]].

==''[[A Christmas Carol]]''==
It was Fan that brought Scrooge back from boarding school after being neglected by his father. Years later, Fan died giving birth to Fred.

Fan was the sister to Scrooge and the mother of Fred.
