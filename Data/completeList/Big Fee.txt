|quote = |enemies=[[Cruella De Vil]]}}'''Big Fee''' is a supporting character in the TV series ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. She is one of the members of the Canal Crew, along with [[Fergus Fox|Fergus the Fox]] and Sid the Squirrel.
==Background==
===Personality===
Witty and articulate peddler about town, "Canal Crew" member Big Fee can get you anything for, well, a big fee. Information, goodies, take your pick...just pay her in shiny things, ring pulls, old coins; the shinier the better!

===Physical appearance===
Big Fee is a dark gray rat with hot pink head fur and tattoos, which look like flowers, on each of her front legs.
