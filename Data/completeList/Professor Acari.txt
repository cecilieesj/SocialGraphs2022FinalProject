'''Professor Acari'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398744/?ref_=tt_cl_t9</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005606/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0071818/</ref> is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He appears in the episode, "[[Tick-Tick-Tick]]", and then again in "[[The Full Monkey]]".

==History==
Professor Acari is a very talented entomologist who also has some experience in robotics. Professor Acari runs an advanced lab somewhere in South America. Professor Acari called [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim Possible]] when [[Shego]] broke into his lab and stole his plans for a robotic tick.
