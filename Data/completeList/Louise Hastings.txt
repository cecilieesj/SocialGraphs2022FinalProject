'''Louise Hastings''' is a character of the Marvel [[Hulu]] series, ''[[Helstrom]]''. She is a psychologist with a strong faith but an open mind, Hastings is the head psychiatric hospital where Victoria is being monitored. She has watched over the Helstrom family for years, caring for them as best she can.

Louise Hastings was created by Christopher Cooper and Richard Case.
