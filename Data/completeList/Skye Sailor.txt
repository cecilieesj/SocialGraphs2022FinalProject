[[File:Cloud-9-kiersey-clemons-december-2-2013-1.jpg|thumb]]'''Skye Sailor''' is the main antagonist of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Cloud 9]]''. She is portrayed by [[Kiersey Clemons]].

==History==
She is known to be focused on snowboarding, and is a famous snowboarder that has traveled and replaces [[Kayla Morgan]], after being booted out of the Swift Team by Sebastian Swift.
