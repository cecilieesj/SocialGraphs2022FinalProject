
[[File:James_Sardine.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''James Sardine''' is a male fish whom [[Bea Goldfishberg|Bea]] dated in the ''[[Fish Hooks]]'' episode "Doris Flores Gorgeous". He is the ''Fish Hooks'' equivalent to James Dean.

==Background Information==
*He appears to be the smallest fish in [[Freshwater High]].
