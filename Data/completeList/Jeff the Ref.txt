|friends = }}'''Jeff the Ref''' is a penguin. He wears a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Referee_Jersey Referee Jersey] and a [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Silver_Whistle Silver Whistle].

==Appearances==
===Team Blue Rally 2===
Jeff the Ref made his first appearance as a character in the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Stage Stage] play [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Team_Blue_Rally_2 Team Blue Rally 2]. He was known as simply "Jeff".
===Team Blue vs. Team Red===
Jeff made his second appearance in the sequel of Team Blue's Rally 2; [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Team_Blue_vs._Team_Red Team Blue vs. Team Red].
====Costume Trunk description====
''Off the court, Jeff is an average citizen. But on the court, he is The Law. Watch out!''
"When this ref calls foul, you best throw in the towel."
===Club Penguin: Game Day!===
Jeff also appears in ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Game_Day! Club Penguin: Game Day!]''. When you ask the captain of your team (depending on the team color) the first question, the captain will mention Jeff the Ref. Jeff is seen at the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Town Town], the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Snow_Forts Snow Forts], the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Dock Dock], the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Beach Beach], the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Ski_Hill Ski Hill] and the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Ski_Village Ski Village].{{Club Penguin}}
