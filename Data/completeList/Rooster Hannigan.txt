'''Rooster Hannigan''' is the secondary antagonist, [[Miss Hannigan]]'s brother, and [[Lily St. Regis]]' boyfriend in ''[[Annie (1999 film)|Annie]]''.

==Role in the film==
Serving as Miss Hannigan's henchman in the plot, Rooster tries to steal money from [[Oliver Warbucks]] by dressing up like [[Annie (character)|Annie]]'s fake father along with Lily dressed up as her fake mother. He later wants to kill her along with Miss Hannigan but Lily, the [[The Orphans (Annie)|orphans]], and F.D.R. stop and arrest him, Miss Hannigan, and Lily at the end.
