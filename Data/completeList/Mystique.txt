'''Mystique''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Marvel Disk Wars: The Avengers]]'' and the X-Men Cinematic Universe.

Mystique was created by Chris Claremont, Dave Cockrum, Jim Mooney, Frank Springer, and Terry Austin.

==Appearances==
===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===
[[File:Spiderman Mutant and the Beast.png|250px|thumb|left]]
Mystique's name can be seen on one of the news tickets, noted being questioned in a string of bank robberies.
[[Spider-Man]] imagines the musical "[[Beauty and the Beast (musical)|Mutant and the Beast]]" starring Mystique and [[Beast (Marvel)|Beast]].

===''[[Marvel Disk Wars: The Avengers]]''===
Mystique was hired by [[Red Skull]] to steal the DISKS and give them to [[Crossbones]]. She disguised herself as [[Pepper Potts]] and got the DISKS containing [[Baron Zemo]], [[Abomination]], [[Graviton]], [[M.O.D.O.K.]], and Tiger Shark, but after she handed them to Crossbones, he betrayed her and knocked her out to be found by the authorities.
