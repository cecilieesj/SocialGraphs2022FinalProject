'''Dean Eugene Higgins''' is a character from a trilogy of films, beginning with ''[[The Computer Wore Tennis Shoes]]'', followed by ''[[Now You See Him, Now You Don't]]'' and ''[[The Strongest Man in the World]]''.
He is the long suffering Dean of [[Medfield College]], and often his own worst enemy when he finds himself or the college in a bad situation.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Computer Wore Tennis Shoes]]''===
Dean Higgins is first seen speaking with board that they cannot afford a computer for Medfield, unaware to him and the board, the students had planted a bug in his office and were listening to him. He even talks about the budget problems that the college has been facing.
