'''R.A.L.F.''' (also known as '''Robotic Assistant Labor Facilitator''') is a service robot who delivers mail and supplies around the NASA base in ''[[Flight of the Navigator]]''. He is often operated by [[Carolyn McAdams]] and according to her, he messes up once in a while.

R.A.L.F. helps [[David Freeman]] escape from his room and sneak inside the hanger where the [[Trimaxion Drone Ship|ship]] is being kept. He is last seen being loaded with mail by Carolyn.
