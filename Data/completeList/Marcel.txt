|status = Active}}'''Marcel''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2009]] animated feature film, ''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''. She is [[Charlotte La Bouff|Charlotte]]'s pet cat.

==Background==
Marcel is Charlotte's pet cat. Though she doesn't speak, she dislikes being  squeezed on too tight during the moment where Charlotte puts a frog hoodie on Marcel, causing her to be launched into the ceiling just before [[Eudora]] puts her down from the ceiling.

==Role in the film==
Marcel is seen at the beginnig at the film where she is seen listening to [[Eudora]] telling the story of the "Frog Prince" to [[Tiana]] and [[Charlotte La Bouff|Charlotte]] who is holding her on her arms to which while she is narrating, Marcel escapes from her owner's arms. after the story, Charlotte puts a frog costume on her cat pretending for her to be a frog, urging Tiana to "kiss" her which she refuses. Charlotte then squeezes Marcel tight, launching him up to a ceiling. Eudora tells the two to stop tormenting Marcel, telling the two to be kind to her just before [[Eli La Bouff|Big Daddy]] arrives while Eudora takes her daughter home.
