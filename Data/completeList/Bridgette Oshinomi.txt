'''Bridgette Oshinomi'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0399149/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Phineas-and-Ferb/Bridgette-Oshinomi/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0934589/</ref> is a reporter who has appeared in a few episodes and has reported some breaking news in [[Danville]].

==Physical appearance==
She wears a blue headband and gold earrings, a light blue shirt with a ribbon and long sleeves, and dark blue pants with a black belt with a gold-gray buckle. She also has a circle-shaped head and brown hair.

==Background information==
In "Backyard Aquarium", she stated that she has a daughter and is the author of a series of books called ''You and Your High-Strung Teen''.
