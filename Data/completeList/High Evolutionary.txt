'''High Evolutionary''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3]]'', played by Chukwudi Iwuji. High Evolutionary was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

==History==
He is a scientist obsessed with creating the perfect race of beings through evolution.
