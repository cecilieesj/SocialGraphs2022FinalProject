'''Lucy Miller''' is [[Neil Miller|Neil]] and [[Laura Miller|Laura]]'s daughter, [[Scott Calvin]] and [[Carol Newman-Calvin]]'s honorary niece, and [[Charlie Calvin]]'s half sister in ''[[The Santa Clause 2]]'' and ''[[The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause]]''.

==Personality==
Lucy Miller is a sweet girl who is 6 years old and lives with her mother and father. In the second movie, she founded out that her Uncle Scott is Santa and was very happy about it. In the third movie, Lucy had a chance to go to the North Pole with Scott and meets The Tooth Fairy, Sandman & the Easter Bunny.
