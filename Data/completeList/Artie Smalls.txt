}}'''Arthur "Artie" Leonard Smalls''' is the main antagonist of the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[Liv and Maddie]]''.

==History==
He is [[Joey Rooney|Joey]]'s nemesis and is also a Space Wolverines fan. He used his minions to do stuff for him, since he was little.

==Personality==
He is an clever and over competitive teenage boy. He tends to be overbaring and strives to be the best. Also, he has a major crush on Liv.
