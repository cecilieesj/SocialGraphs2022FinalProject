'''Hop the Grasshopper''' is the main character of the [[1934]] [[Silly Symphony]] cartoon, ''[[The Grasshopper and the Ants]]''.

==Role in the film==
The Grasshopper fiddles and plays through his day, tempting some of the worker ants to play with him, unaware that winter is coming and food will soon be scarce.

==Trivia==
*Pinto Colvig would go on to become the voice of [[Goofy]] whose signature tune is the song Hop made famous: "[[The World Owes Me a Living|The World Owes Us&nbsp;a Livin']]." Goofy can be heard humming it as late as [[1950]].
