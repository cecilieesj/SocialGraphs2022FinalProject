'''Yori''' is a character in the film, ''[[Tron (film)|Tron]]''.

==History==
She is the confidante of [[Tron (character)|Tron]]. Just like Tron resembles his creator [[Alan Bradley]], Yori resembles her creator, [[Lora Baines]], the ex-girlfriend of [[Kevin Flynn]], whom Bradley is replacing as her boyfriend.

==Trivia==
*In a deleted scene on the DVD, there was a love scene between [[Tron (character)|Tron]] and [[Yori (Tron)|Yori]]. but it was cut for being too sentineled.  [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzZmvAMN3OA]
