}}'''Rockhead Rooster''' is a character who first appeared in a ''Daisy's Diary'' comic story by [[Carl Barks]]. He later became a recurring minor character, making various other printed appearances in recent years.<ref>https://inducks.org/character.php?c1=date&c=Rockhead%20Rooster&view=4</ref>

==Background==
He is [[Clara Cluck]]'s pen pal, and when he comes to [[Duckburg]], Clara and [[Daisy Duck|Daisy]] decide to have a double date, Clara taking Rockhead and Daisy taking [[Donald]]. Donald and Rockhead get along so well that they don't pay enough attention to their girlfriends, as he agrees with Donald on everything.
