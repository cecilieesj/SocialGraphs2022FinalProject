[[File:SNL2011-06.jpg|thumb|250px|Segel and the Muppet in a bumper shot for ''Saturday Night Live''.]]A Muppet version of '''[[Gary (The Muppets)|Gary]]''' appears in the film ''[[The Muppets (film)|The Muppets]]'' during the song "[[Man or Muppet]]". The puppetry and singing voice for the character was provided by [[Bill Barretta]]. His speaking voice, resembling that of [[Johnny Fiama]], can be heard in a [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Muppet_Bloopers blooper reel] from the film.<ref>[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1_QNkOSAvXs#t=0m23s Disney's "The Muppets" - Blooper reel (Blu-ray preview)]</ref>

==Outside appearances==
Segel showed the puppet off in his home during an appearance on ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Nightline Nightline]'', along with the puppets from ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Forgetting_Sarah_Marshall Forgetting Sarah Marshall]''.

On November 13, 2011, Segel [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Twitter tweeted] about the disappearance of the Muppet: "Landed in NY and someone took my bag by mistake. He is going to be confused when he opens it and finds a Jason Segel Muppet. [[w:c:muppet:Saturday Night Live|SNL]] Saturday!"<ref>[https://twitter.com/#!/jasonsegel/status/135849448063504384 Tweet]</ref> But one day later the problem was solved, Segel upload a new picture with the Muppet and wrote: "Good news world, the bag and muppet have been returned safe and sound. Now off to [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/David_Letterman Letterman]. Always an honor to be on his show. Then SNL SAT."

The puppet appeared in the final commercial bumper for the episode of ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Saturday_Night_Live Saturday Night Live]'' hosted by Segel (promoting the movie), while several Muppets characters were in the studio.

Segel talked about the puppet in detail during his November 28, 2011 appearance on ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Conan Conan]''.
