'''Chairol Burnett''' is a minor character in [[Toy Story 4|''Toy Story 4''.]] She is one of Bonnie's old toys (along with [[Bitey White]], [[Carl Reineroceros]] and [[Melephant Brooks]]) that are stashed away in her closet. Her name is a pun on her voice actor, [[Carol Burnett]].

== Appearance ==
She is a toy chair who has a green color, googly eyes, a black mouth, along with a simple rainbow, a flower and a mushroom design on her stump legs.
