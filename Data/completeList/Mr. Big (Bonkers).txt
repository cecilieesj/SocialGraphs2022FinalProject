'''Mr. Big''' is a one-time character, an anthropomorphic Toon mouse and the main antagonist of the episode, "[[Hamster Houseguest]]" from Disney's ''[[Bonkers]]''.

==Background==
Despite his name, Mr. Big is actually small. He was one of the two actors on ''The Mr. Big and Tiny Show''. Mr. Big hadn't had a show in 12 years since he and his co-star Tiny the Hamster had retired.

===Physical appearance===
Mr. Big is a short and slender magenta anthropomorphic mouse with yellow eyes and a black nose, and also, he wears green shorts with blue overalls, green hat with blue band.

==Trivia==
*Mr. Big is one of the four criminals that [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]] needs to arrest in the Sega Genesis game.
