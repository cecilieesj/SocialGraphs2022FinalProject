'''Red Beard '''is one of [[Two Peg]]'s helpers. 
[[File:Defeat and Da arms of Red Beard.png|thumb|220x220px]]
Red Beard is first seen when the pirates capture Brady, Boomer, Mikayla, Mason, and Lanny. He then comes to The Castle and captures the guards. When Mason and Mikayla come, Mason fights him and wins. (Oh Brother, Where Arr Thou)

When Boomer and Brady each make their own Junga Ball team, Boomer chooses Red Beard on his team. He later joins the national team. (Junga Ball)
