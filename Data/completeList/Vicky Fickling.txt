|animator = Jeff Goode}}'''Vicky Fickling'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/vickie.htm</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0388179/</ref> is an antagonist from the [[2005]] animated television series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
Vicky is a teenage geeky girl who turns out to be a siren. She tricks [[Jake Long|Jake]] and company into thinking [[Danika Hunnicutt]] was the Siren, but is later discovered. Vicky snatches the necklace that has siren powers and controls [[Arthur "Spud" P. Spudinski|Spud]], [[Jake]], and [[Fu Dog]]'s minds. [[Trixie Carter|Trixie]] knocks her out with a shield, and after she is taken away, she is never seen in person again<ref>[[Siren Says]]</ref>.

She suffers from swimmer's ear, as a result of being a sea creature and spending so much time in the water.
