'''Alice Wartheimer''' is a minor character of [[Disney Channel]]'s Original Series ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''. She is portrayed by Haley Holmes. 

==History==
She is a very sweet, but high-stunning, and submissive girl from Teddy's French class who was an employee at the Reddi Mart until she was fired.

*Charlie Did It
*Sleepless in Denver
