
[[File:Mrwolfe.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Mr. Barry Wolfe''', voiced by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/David_Leisure David Leisure], appeared in the second season ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[[What "Sexual" Harris Meant|What 'Sexual' Harris Meant]]".

A member of the [[WESAYSO]] corporate hearing board, Mr. Wolfe asks [[Monica DeVertebrae]] for intimate details of her garment habits.

In the closing credits, the character is listed as "Mr. Teddy Wolfe."
