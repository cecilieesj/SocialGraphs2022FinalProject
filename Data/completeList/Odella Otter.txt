}}'''Odella Otter''' is a character from the children's show ''[[PB&J Otter]]''. She is a famous pop star singer, and [[Jelly Otter]] is a big fan of hers.
Her only true appearance was in the episode "[[The Singin' Kid]]". She appeared on stage at a fairground in Lake Hoohaw, where she sang one of her greatest songs to the audience. In the same episode, her face was seen on the cover of an Otter Beat magazine Jelly was reading.
