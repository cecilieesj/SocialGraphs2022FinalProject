The '''Town Crier''' is a minor character in the [[2015]] adaptation of ''[[Cinderella (2015 film)|Cinderella]]''.

==History==
In this version, the town crier is theÂ liaison between the royalty and the common folk of the kingdom. He appears in the movie on three separate occasions. The first in the market when he announces a royal ball which every maiden in the kingdom is invited to. The second at the ball itself when he is announcing each person attending the ball. The third and final time he appears is when he informs every maiden to try on the [[Glass Slipper]].
