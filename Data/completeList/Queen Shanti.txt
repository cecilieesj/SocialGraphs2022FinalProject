'''Queen Shanti''' is a character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Mira, Royal Detective]]''. She is the queen of Jalpur who appoints [[Mira]] as the kingdom's Royal Detective.

==Trivia==
*She has the same name as [[Shanti]] from ''[[The Jungle Book]]'', who is also an Indian character.
