'''Fisherman Freddy''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Rabbit Rabbit] Fisherman [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/NPC NPC] in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontown Toontown]. He is located in the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontown_Central Toontown Central] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Playground Playground ]''(He is found next to the gazebo near the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Fishing Fishing Pond.]). ''
Like all Fisherman NPC's, you can talk to him to sell fish in your fishing bucket. For every 10 new fish species you sell, he will award you with a trophy and a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Laff Laff boost]. You can also receive Jellybeans from him when you sell fish.
