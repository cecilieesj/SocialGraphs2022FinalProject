'''Gilbert''' is a very fast kindergartener (both in speed and speech) in the show ''[[Recess]]''. Gilbert is [[King Bob]]'s pick to win the Kindergarten Derby in the episode "The Kindergarten Derby". He loses the derby to [[Tubby]].
