'''Laufey''' is known as the King of the [[Frost Giants]]. He is the secondary antagonist in the [[2011]] film, ''Thor''. For centuries, he wishes to kill [[Odin Borson|Odin]] and take over [[Asgard]]. He abandoned his own son for being too small. 1000 years later, he finally entered Asgard when [[Loki Laufeyson|his own son]] let him in but was betrayed and killed when he was about to kill Odin.
