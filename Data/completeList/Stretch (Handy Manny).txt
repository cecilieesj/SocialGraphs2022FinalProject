|weapons = His tape (as his hand/tongue)}}'''Stretch''' is a 10-year-old tape measure from Disney's [[2006]] television series ''[[Handy Manny]]''.

==Biography==

Stretch loves to measure things. He can speak out accurate measurements, can count well, and use his tape as an extendable hand. He bonds with every tool, except Turner and Felipe. Â 
==Appearance==
Stretch is a magenta tape measure with yellow tape. Due to the tip being too big, the tape resembles buck teeth.
==Personality==
Stretch is very smart. Stretch is shown to be a perfectionist. He's always right, knows everything, has a mathematical mind, and a good memory. He also makes good remarks and loves to be held by Manny. He also loves to talk.
