Agent '''Franklin''' is a character in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], featured in the [[Marvel Studios|Marvel]] [[Disney+]] series, ''[[WandaVision]]''. He is a [[S.W.O.R.D.]] agent, who transforms into the '''Beekeeper''', upon going through the [[Westview]]'s Hex, and comes into contact with [[Scarlet Witch|Wanda Maximoff]] and [[Vision]]. He makes his debut in the [[Don't Touch That Dial|second episode]], and is portrayed by Zac Henry.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[WandaVision]]''====
Following the disappearance of one their agents, [[S.W.O.R.D.]] set up a base outside [[Westview]], the town [[Monica Rambeau]] disappeared in. No is able to pass through the borders, as Westview is barricaded. S.W.O.R.D. discover that the subjects of the town, are placed in an alternate reality, in the style of a classic 1950s sitcom, and [[Scarlet Witch|Wanda Maximoff]] and [[Vision]] are the subjects. Franklin is tasked with retrieving Rambeau, and is given a hazmat suit, attached to a wire, so the other agents can keep track of him, as he travels through underground. Franklin's suit is altered upon entering, into that of a beekeeper, and his wire becomes detached. Franklin manages to find his way outside the home of Wanda Maximoff and Vision, through the sewer system, and climbs outside out the manhole, with a hoard of bees surrounding him. He comes face to face with the two Avengers, and when Wanda says "No", and time is rewound to before he managed to break through.

==Trivia==
*Zac Henry, who portrayed the Beekeeper, has also appeared in other [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] projects. He appeared in the uncredited role of TAC Team Agent #1, in the ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' episode "[[Turn, Turn, Turn]]", and also worked in stunts for ''Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.'', as well as ''[[Runaways (TV series)|Runaways]]'', ''[[Ant-Man and the Wasp]]'', ''[[Avengers: Infinity War]]'', ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'', ''[[Captain America: Civil War]]'' and ''Thor''.
