==Toot, Whistle, Plunk and Boom==
They are the titular characters in ''Toot, Whistle, Plunk and Boom'' to show us the beginning of music, for it came to be during the Stone Age.

== Marsupilami ==
Toot, Whistle, Plunk and Boom make recurring appearances on ''Marsupilami''. They have somehow relocated to the jungle (essentially making them a jungle tribe) and now have speech patterns modeled after [[The Beatles]].

==Hercules: The Animated Series==
Toot, Whistle, Plunk, and Boom made a cameo in "[[Hercules and the Prometheus Affair]]" in the song telling the story of Prometheus as cavemen dancing around a campfire before being struck by Zeus' lightning.

==House of Mouse==
They appeared in ''House of Mouse'' as a fill-in for the club band.

== Trivia ==

* Each of the cavemen's respective sounds birthed the four musical families:
** Toot: Brass
** Whistle: Woodwind
** Plunk: String
** Boom: Percussion
