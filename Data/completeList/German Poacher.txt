|status=Defeated}}The''' German Poacher''' is a character from ''[[The Legend of Tarzan]]'' who serves as the main antagonist in the episode, "[[Tarzan and the Rift]]".

==Role in the series==
The Poacher and his men are first seen when [[Tarzan (character)|Tarzan]] gets caught by a net. Tarzan escapes before the poachers see him trapped and they go back to their tents.

The poachers later capture [[Terkina|Terk]] and put her in a cage on their boat. Tarzan and his friends find Terk and the poachers and they have a battle. When the German poacher is about to defeat Tarzan and Terk, [[Tantor]] grabs him and throws him out of the boat and he lands on a shore next to his men.
