'''Rusty''' is a monkey wrench (though he looks more like a pipe wrench) who is not very brave and usually worried. He sometimes needs Manny's assistance to get courage. He bonds with [[Pat]], [[Dusty]], and [[Squeeze]].

==Appearance==
Rusty is all orange with white eyes, a jaw and is strong.
==Personality==
Rusty is generally a worrywart and a scaredy cat. He's afraid of everything especially animals. Secretly, Rusty wants to be brave but it will never happen.
==Trivia==
*Rusty resembles the Scaredy-shroom from ''[[wikipedia:Plants vs. Zombies|Plants vs. Zombies]]'' as they are both cowardly.
*Rusty resembles [[Fear]] from ''[[Inside Out]]'' they are both cowardly.
*Rusty is similar to Lofty since they are a member of the crew and are both a coward, but sometimes give courage.
