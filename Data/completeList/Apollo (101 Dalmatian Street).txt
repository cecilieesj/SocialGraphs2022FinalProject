'''Apollo''' is a character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is a police horse.

==Personality==
He is a serious, no-nonsense character.

==Appearance==
Apollo is a stocky, cream colored horse with blonde, curvy mane and tail. He wears black horse boots and a red saddle pad.
