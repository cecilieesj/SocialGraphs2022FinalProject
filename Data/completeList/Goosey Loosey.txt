'''Goosey Loosey''' is an antagonist in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2005]] animated feature film ''[[Chicken Little (film)|Chicken Little]]''. She doesn't really speak, she just quacks, honks and squawks.

==Background==
===Personality===
Goosey Loosey isn't a real villain because she doesn't have evil intentions, but she bullies unpopular kids such as [[Chicken Little (character)|Chicken Little]] with her best friend is [[Foxy Loxy (2005)|Foxy Loxy]]. Goosey Loosey was in the same baseball team as Chicken Little, and after Chicken Little won the baseball match, Goosey Loosey isn't seen bullying him anymore.

===Physical appearance===
Goosey Loosey is a white goose with a long neck and a beak. She wears a red dress, with a red and white checkerboard collar and red and white checkerboard lace sleeves, and she's got some red bowties in her hair with pigtails. She's wears some make-up on her eyes. Despite the fact she's a goose, she has fingers instead of feathers on her hands.

==Appearances==
===''[[Chicken Little (film)|Chicken Little]]''===
Goosey Loosey is first seen on the school bus while she's giving acorns to her best friend Foxy Loxy to pour them in Chicken Little's path. After that, she's seen in her school in gym class. Goosey Loosey was in the popular team with dodge-ball. When Chicken Little was protecting [[Abby Mallard]], Foxy Loxy snaps her fingers, and Goosey Loosey throws Chicken Little against the window. Chicken Little's friends try to save him, but Goosey blocks the way and chases them away. Later, Goosey Loosey is seen in the baseball match. She was in the same team as Chicken Little and after their team won she was exalting Chicken Little. During the alien invasion, she and Foxy attempted to throw rocks at the invaders to stop them, however after Foxy was vaporized by the aliens, Goosey ran away from them screaming in horror. She was then seen in the cinema watching Chicken Little's movie while she was sitting behind Runt. Her last appearance was when she was dancing to the song "[[Don't Go Breaking My Heart]]" during the credits.
