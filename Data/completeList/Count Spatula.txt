'''Count Spatula''' is a monster truck, who first appears in ''[[Cars (video game)|Cars: The Video Game]]'', in which he comes to [[Radiator Springs]] alongside [[Ginormous]] and [[The Crippler]], to challenge [[Lightning McQueen]] to a monster truck rally race.
