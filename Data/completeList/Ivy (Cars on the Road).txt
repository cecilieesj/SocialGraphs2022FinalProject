'''Ivy''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney+|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated series ''[[Cars on the Road]]''. She is a monster truck who loves to perform for an audience.

==Background==

===Personality===
Ivy is a very defensive vehicle. She was proud and happy to perform for an audience, but a supposed accident forced her to live like a hermit in the woods and stay away from everyone. Despite this, she secretly loved to perform again and with the help of [[Lightning McQueen]] and [[Mater]], she is able to overcome her insecurities and step out into the world again. She becomes very open and fun-loving, showing an adventurous side that matches that with her friends. She is also very opportunistic about wanting to take the stage, but is not vain or conceited about it. She is a car filled with passion for what she loves best.

===Physical appearance===
Ivy is a monster truck and thus possesses giant tumbler wheels and has five headlights on her head. Her usual design and appearance is an acrylic, sparkling aquamarine blue with an orange fire design on her sides. While living in the woods, she looked greyish-brown and was covered in green moss. When she joined the film industry, she was painted entirely yellow and had a giant rubber yellow and purple brain on her head.

==Trivia==
*Ivy is the only character in ''Cars on the Road'' besides [[Lightning McQueen]] and [[Mater]] to appear in more than one episode.
