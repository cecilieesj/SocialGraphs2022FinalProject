'''Cheri Shotwell'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0395120/</ref> is an [[X Middle School]] cheerleader. She is also a reporter/interviewer for the school's newscast.

==Personality==
Naturally, being a cheerleader, she is very chipper and bubbly, and smiles most of the time. Catering to the blonde stereotype, she may also be a little ditzy.

==Appearance==
She is a caucasian girl with a blonde ponytail and blue eyes. She is most often seen in a mostly red cheerleader outfit.

==History==
Not only is she seen as a member of the school's cheerleading squad, but she has also been seen co-anchoring the school's news program with [[Philsky Richards|Philsky]]. Such as covering a parade on the football field that includes Principal Folsom in <ref>Cry, The Beloved Mascot</ref> and an organization at the school called B.A.G.A.S.T., in <ref>Two Wheels, Full Throttle, No Breaks</ref>.

When Ingrid is seen going through the school's hallway as a new student at X in Ingrid Third, Public Enemy Number One, she meets Cheri, who is the first to greet her as part of the welcome bandwagon committee.
