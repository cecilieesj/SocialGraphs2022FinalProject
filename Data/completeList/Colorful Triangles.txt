|status = Active}}The '''Colorful Triangles''' are abstract shapes and the main characters of the first segment, ''[[Symphony No. 5]]'' in Disney's [[2000]] animated film, ''[[Fantasia 2000]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Fantasia 2000]]''===
They represent the cause of good and act like butterflies. The most significant of these are a large orange butterfly and a smaller pink butterfly. The Colorful Triangles then witness darkness arriving in their area noticing that danger is emerging. When their [[Black Triangles|enemies]] emerge from the ground, the colorful shapes gather together and use the power of the sun to vanquish their foes. Later when the Colorful Triangles use the power of the sun to defeat their enemies, they manage to get inside the power of the sun.

===''[[Fantasia: Music Evolved]]''===
There are also rumors for the Colorful Triangles to appear in ''[[Fantasia: Music Evolved]]''.
