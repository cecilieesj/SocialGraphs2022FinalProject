'''Thor''' is the Asgardian god of thunder in the ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]'' episode "[[Hercules and the Twilight of the Gods]]". He is the son of [[Odin (Hercules)|Odin]], and older brother of [[Loki (Hercules)|Loki]].

In the episode, Loki hatches a scheme to have Hercules take Thor's place as God of Thunder to ensure that Asgard is unprepared for the coming of Ragnarok.

==Powers and Abilities==
*Standard powers of an Asgardian/Aesir deity.
*Extraordinary super-strength.
*Unleash thunder with his hammer, [[Mjolnir]].
