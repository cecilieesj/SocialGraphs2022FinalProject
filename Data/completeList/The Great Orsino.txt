'''The Great Orsino''' is a minor character from the Disney Junior animated series [[The Rocketeer (TV series)|The Rocketeer]]


==Information==
The Great Orsino is a magician on a traveling carnival truck who has one goal in his mind: to steal Kit's rocket pack and use it for his greatest trick of all time: The Flying Orsino.
