}}'''Queen Regina''' is the Queen of Really Royal Land who appeared in the ''[[JoJo's Circus]]'' Season 3 episode, "[[Princess for a Day]]". She is [[King Regis]]' wife. She is also [[Princess Josephina|Josephina]]'s mother and she's JoJo's aunt. She is voiced by Mariska Hargitay.
== Trivia ==
* Regina means Queen in Latin.
* [[The Evil Queen|Queen Regina]] is a character in ''[[Once Upon a Time]], ''who is more villainous that the'' JoJo's Circus ''Queen Regina''.''
