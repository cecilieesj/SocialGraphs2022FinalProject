'''Geegaw Hackwrench''' is [[Gadget Hackwrench|Gadget]]'s rarely-mentioned father on ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]'', and an old friend of [[Monterey Jack]]'s. His only appearances were limited to a mention and photo cameo in one episode, as well as in a flashback at the beginning of one comic book story.
