|occupation = Basketball player|fullname=Astrid Stanislowski}}'''Astrid “Stains” Stanislowski''' is a recurring character on ''[[Liv and Maddie]]''. She is a part of the girls basketball team, and is known for getting stains all over herself and being clumsy, such as walking into a locker.



==Trivia==

*She was the first (and possibly only) person to try on the "new" basketball uniforms that the principal gave them.
