==Appearance==
Cobby has a light blue-colored skin, and dark blue wings, with yellow and orange striped stomach and horns. He is two years older than his brother, Henry Hugglemonster, and one year older than his sister, Summer Hugglemonster. He is around eight years of age.

== Trivia ==

* Cobby was voiced by Chiara Zanni, Actually, Zanni was the only Canadian actress to voice this character.
