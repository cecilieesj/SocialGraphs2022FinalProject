'''Marian Locks'''<ref>in [[Goldie's Do-Over Day]] Bear calls her Mrs. Locks while her mother May calls her Marian</ref> is a character who appears in [[Disney Junior]]'s animated series, ''[[Goldie & Bear]]''. She is [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie Locks]]' mother and businesswoman who travels the world collecting things for the people of her home [[Fairy Tale Forest (Goldie & Bear)|forest]]. She is handicapped so she moves around in a customized wheelchair.Â She was first mentioned at the end of season one as scouting ahead looking for a new place for her family to live. She later sent a letter that she was in another forest and asked why her the family was not there. She fully appears in season two in episodes: "[[Goldie's Great Adventure]]", "[[Sprites on the Loose]]" and "[[Winterchime Day]]".

==Trivia==
*The names of Marian and her husband Robin Locks resemble the literary characters Maid Marian and Robin of Locksley (Robin Hood).
