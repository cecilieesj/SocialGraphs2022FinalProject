'''Gloria Swansong''' is a famous actress featured in the ''[[Darkwing Duck]] episode [[Hush, Hush Sweet Charlatan]].'' She's been featured on stage, screen, and countless deodorant commercials. Her name is a reference to Gloria Swanson. She is voiced by [[B.J. Ward]].

==Trivia==
*She is named after the 20th century actress Gloria Swanson.
