'''Captain Daniel Turner'''<ref>{{Cite_web|url=http://www.disneyabcpress.com/disneychannel/pressrelease/fact-sheet-elena-of-avalor/|title=Fact Sheet: Elena of Avalor|website=DisneyABCPress.com|date=December 12, 2016}}</ref> is [[Naomi Turner|Naomi's]] father in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series, ''[[Elena of Avalor]]''. He is the Harbor Master in the Kingdom of [[Avalor]]. He is very encouraging toward his daughter, telling her that she is special by just being herself. Traditionally, he and Naomi, along with the rest of the boat captains, celebrate Felize Navidad by taking all the ships out to sea to watch the stars and spot the occasional whale.
