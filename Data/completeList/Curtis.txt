|alignment = Good}}'''Curtis''' is a supporting character from ''[[Phil of the Future]]''. He is a caveman that stowed away on the Diffys' time machine. He got stuck in [[2004]] with the Diffys and lived with them.

As the series progresses, he pursues a more modern attitude like cleaning his home, less grunts and more English type but his caveman instincts cause the Diffy family a trouble or two, Curtis loves rocks and knows a lot about them.
