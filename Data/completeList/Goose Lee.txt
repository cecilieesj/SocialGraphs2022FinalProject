'''Goose Lee''' (voiced by [[Robert Ito]]) is a one-time character from the show, ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''. This master of martial arts taught [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing Duck]] the art of Quack Fu long ago. Master Lee appeared only in the episode "[[Kung Fooled]]". He is voiced by [[Robert Ito]].

==Role in the episode==
After chasing [[Professor Moliarty]] underground and emerging in Kung Pow City, Darkwing decides to pay a visit to his old master to try and impress him. Upon reaching Master Lee's abode, Darkwing finds his old teacher has abandoned his humble lifestyle. Master Lee now believes he can spread the wisdom of Quack Fu through marketing. But his ultimate dream is to build Ninja Land, a martial arts theme park.

Unbeknownst to Darkwing, however, Master Lee is plotting to drive out the people from the neighborhood where he plans to build Ninja Land.

==Trivia==
*His name is a spoof on martial arts master Bruce Lee.
*At times, his accent switches from stereotypical Asian-American to stereotypical Italian-American.
