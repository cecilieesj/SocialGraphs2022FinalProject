'''Halley Brandon''' is one of the main characters in the film, ''[[Frenemies (2012 film)|Frenemies]]''.

== History ==
She runs the blog GeeklyChic with her best friend [[Avalon Greene|Avalon]]. She is a geek girl. She became Avalon's frenemy when only one of them could stick with the job of head writer for a famous magazine. She and Avalon tell the stories of the film.
