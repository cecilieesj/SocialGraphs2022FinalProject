'''Falcon Graves''' is a professional corporate saboteur who appears in the ''[[DuckTales (2017 series)|DuckTales]]'' episodes "[[The Infernal Internship of Mark Beaks!]]" and "[[Louie's Eleven!]]".

==Personality==
Falcon Graves is a ruthless and violent falcon who takes his job deathly seriously. He is shown to have no qualms with critically injuring innocents or even children if it means accomplishing his goal.

==Trivia==
*Falcon Graves' shadow was among the shadows that were encountered in "[[The Shadow War!]]".
