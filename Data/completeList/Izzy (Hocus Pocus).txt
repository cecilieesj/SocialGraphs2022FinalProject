'''Izzy''' is the tritagonist in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s  [[2022]] film, ''[[Hocus Pocus 2]]''. She is one of [[Becca]]'s friends alongside [[Cassie Traske|Cassie]], who is also an aspiring witch, and will help her wish with the [[Black Flame Candle]] to bring the [[Sanderson Sisters]] back after 29 years.
