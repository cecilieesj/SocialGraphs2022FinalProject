'''Maya Muscles''' is a character from the [[Playhouse Disney]] series ''[[JoJo's Circus]]''. She is the daughter of Mr. Aldeberto Muscles, the circus strong man. Maya can be rather shy at times, but she loves to get up and go. She is known by her nickname "Little Mouse" by her dad and her favorite "happy place" is a daisy patch.
