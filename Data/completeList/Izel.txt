'''Izel''' is a character in the [[Marvel Television|Marvel]] series ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'', and the main antagonist of season six.

Originating from the Fear Dimension, Izel's belongs to a species of non-corporeal beings, who long for a physical host. Her former partner [[Sarge|Pachakutiq]] had taken over a physical duplicate body of [[Phil Coulson]], and she arrived on Earth, to not only find him, but to use the Di'Allas in order to create a gateway between her home and Earth, so her fellow species could take over.
