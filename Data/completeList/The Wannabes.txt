
'''[[File:0hhgk.jpg|thumb|250px|Gigi's wannabes on the left and right of her]]The Wannabes''' are two snobby girls on ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]] ''who follow [[Gigi Hollingsworth]] around and try to be as much like her as possible. They are portrayed by Heather Trzyna and Kelsey Sanders.
