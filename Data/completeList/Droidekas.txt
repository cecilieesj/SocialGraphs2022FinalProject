|goal = To fight against the Republic|fate = Deactivated at the end of The Clone Wars|affiliations=Trade Federation<br>[[Confederacy of Independent Systems]]|status=Deactivated}}'''Droidekas''', also known as '''destroyer droids''', were a type of droid used by the Trade Federation in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' universe. They were used in combat by the Trade Federation during the Invasion of [[Naboo]] and later by the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems]] during the Clone Wars.    

== Background ==
Droidekas were dangerous and deadly, designed to exterminate their adversaries with extreme prejudice. They could transform their shape by curling into a ball and moving up to 75 kilometers per hour across a surface, or stand on three legs and utilize a shield generator while firing at a target.
