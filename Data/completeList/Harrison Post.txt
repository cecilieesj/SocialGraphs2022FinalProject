'''Harrison Post''' (voiced by Gary LeRoi Grey)<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Harrison/</ref> is one of the guest characters and antagonists of ''[[Fillmore!]]''.

==History==
He suspected and framed [[Vern Natoma]] for stealing and abducting the school mascot Lobstee, the Lobster.
