Space Cadet (by Jace)|alignment = Good|home = Miami|family = Piper (sister)}}'''Carrie''' is a character that appears in [[Austin & Ally|''Austin & Ally'']]''. ''She first appears in Cupids & Cuties, the episode where she met Dez and after the two hit it off, and they became a couple. She was a recurring character in Seasons 3 and 4. Carrie was also an employee of Sonic Boom.

== Trivia ==
* Carrie works at Shredder's Beach Club as a waitress but was fired in Records & Wrecking Balls.
* Carrie is the only recurring character from Season 3, to appear in the finale, Relationships & Red Carpets.[[Category:Austin & Ally characters]]
