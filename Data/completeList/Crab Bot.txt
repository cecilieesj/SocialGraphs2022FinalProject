'''Crab Bots''' are minor characters in the series'' [[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''. 

They are a crab-based robots created by [[Doctor Undergear]] which make up a part of Undergear's foot soldiers. They are usually stationed at Undergear's lair on Gear Island.
