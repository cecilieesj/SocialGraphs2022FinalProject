'''Grimsley''' is a minor antagonist in ''[[Tinker Bell and the Lost Treasure]]''. He is a troll and [[Leech]]'s brother. The two guard a bridge (called the "Troll Bridge") on an island north of [[Neverland]], and he always argues with Leech.
