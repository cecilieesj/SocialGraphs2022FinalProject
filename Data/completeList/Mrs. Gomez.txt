'''Amy Gomez''' is one of the main characters of ''The Gumazing Gum Girl''. She is very dedicated to her family and would do anything in her power to keep them comfortable and safe.

== Appearance ==
She wears a pink dress, a black skirt, some black Mary-Janes, and some bracelets.

== Personality ==
Amy Gomez cares very deeply for her family, so much that she would do anything to express her love for them. She is also very protective of her family, including Gum Girl.
