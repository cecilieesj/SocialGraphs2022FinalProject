'''Love'''  is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]'', portrayed by India Rose Hemsworth. Love is the biological daughter of [[Gorr the God Butcher]] and the adoptive daughter of [[Thor Odinson]].

Love is a composite character based on '''Agar''', who was created by Jason Aaron and Jackson Guice, '''Mistress Love''', who was created by J.M. DeMatteis and Don Perlin, and '''Singularity''', who was created by Marguerite Bennett, G. Willow Wilson and Jorge Molina.

==Appearances==
===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===
====''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]''====
Love is the daughter of [[Gorr the God Butcher|Gorr]], who died of starvation despite Gorr's pleas to their god, [[Rapu]], to save her. After her father had arrived at Eternity, [[Thor Odinson]] convinced him to wish for her resurrection, and Thor adopted her. Discovering she had been empowered with superpowers, Thor decided to train her to be a hero. During their adventures, the duo was nicknamed "Love and Thunder."
