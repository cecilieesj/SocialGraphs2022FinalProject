'''Barbara "Betty" Bluff '''is a minor character from the series [[Disney's Doug]]. She is the beautiful wife of [[Bill Bluff]] and the mother of [[Beebe Bluff]]. She has a major role the series and ''[[Doug's 1st Movie]]''. She is the richest woman in Bluffington and teaches at a ballet studio.
