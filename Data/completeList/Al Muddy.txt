The '''Al Muddy''' are recurring antagonists in the [[Aladdin (TV series)|''Aladdin'' TV series]].

==Role in the series==
They are a race of subterranean carnivorous mud-monsters ruled by an evil giant [[Al Muddy Sultan|Sultan]] of their race, who was later defeated by [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]] and his friends in "[[Mudder's Day]]". Although in "[[Sneeze the Day]]", due to [[Genie]]'s magical sneezing illness, The Al Muddy Sultan made an appearance along with [[Fashoom]] from "[[The Prophet Motive]]" and [[Mechanicles]]' large mechanical bug from "[[Getting the Bugs Out]]" until Genie, after being cured, defeated them. The Al Muddy later returned in the episode, "[[Night of the Living Mud]]".
