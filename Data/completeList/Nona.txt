[[File:Char_72862.jpg|thumb]]'''Nona'''<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/The-Weekenders/Nona/</ref> is an African-American girl that goes to the gang's school. She is noted for being tallest girl in school (sometimes her face isn't shown onscreen), and she had a crush on Carver until she realized his head was shaped like a pineapple. She also disliked Tino for a while because he said her feet were big. Nona always be seen on any background throughout the series.

Nona was voiced by [[Grey DeLisle]].
