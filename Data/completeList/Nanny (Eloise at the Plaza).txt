'''Nanny '''is a character based on the ''Eloise'' books by Kay Thompson from the films: ''[[Eloise at the Plaza]]'' and ''[[Eloise at Christmastime]]''. She is Eloise's English nanny.
==Personality==
Nanny is supportive, wise, and motherly to Eloise, but can be a bit stressful and scolding at times.
