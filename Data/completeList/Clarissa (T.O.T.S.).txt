[[File:Unnamed_(14).jpg|thumb]]Clarissa is a cow in [[T.O.T.S.]].



==Appearance==
Clarissa is a cow with black and white fur. She has green irises. The arms, hands and the feet are white. She wears a red colored collar with the T.O.T.S. icon and a white diaper with a red trim and a cactus symbols on it.
