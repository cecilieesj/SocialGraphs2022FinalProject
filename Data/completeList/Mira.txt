'''Mira''' is the titular protagonist of the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Mira, Royal Detective]]''. She is a brave and resourceful commoner who becomes the royal detective after solving a mystery that saved the kingdom's young prince. As the royal detective, Mira travels throughout the kingdom helping royals and commoners alike, and will stop at nothing to solve the case.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
Mira has olive skin, hazel eyes, and long black hair. She is around eight years of age. Her outfit is a blue dress, magenta pants, pink earrings, and blue shoes. She wears a gold magnifying glass.

=== Personality ===
Mira is a kind and intelligent girl, and she's willing to help with any case that comes her way whether it's searching for a missing bicycle or the reason for something strange to happen. When it comes to solving a case, she's not one to jump to conclusions, and she won't come to a conclusion until she gets all the facts.

==Trivia==
*Mira's mother is first mentioned in "[[The Great Diwali Mystery]]", and it's hinted that she passed away prior to the series.
