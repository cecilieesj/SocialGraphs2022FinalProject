[[File:Starstruck-04.jpg|thumb]]'''Alexis Bender''' is a recruiting character of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Starstruck (2010 film)|Starstruck]]''. She is portrayed by [[Chelsea Kane]].

==History==
She is an ex-girlfriend of [[Christopher Wilde]] who dumps, break-up, and humiliated her for [[Jessica Olson]].
