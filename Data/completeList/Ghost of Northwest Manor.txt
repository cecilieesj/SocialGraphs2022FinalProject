'''Archibald Corduroy''' (also known as the '''Ghost of Northwest Manor''') is the main antagonist of the ''[[Gravity Falls]]'' episode "[[Northwest Mansion Mystery]]".

==History==
When he was alive, Archibald Corduroy became cold-hearted after losing his land to the Northwest family and was then killed by his own axe. Years later, his ghost returned to haunt those responsible for his past by turning them into wooden statues. He is also the ancestor of [[Wendy Corduroy]] and her brothers.
