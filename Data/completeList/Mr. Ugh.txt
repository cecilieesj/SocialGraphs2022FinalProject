'''Mr. Ugh''' appeared on ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' as the title character of the television show ''Mr. Ugh'', a parody of ''Mister Ed''. He was played by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Michelan_Sisti Michelan Sisti].

Mr. Ugh is a talking caveman, owned by a dinosaur named [[Woodrow]].

Mr. Ugh appeared in a commercial for his own show, seen in "[[Hurling Day]]", where he is advised not to open a very important package addressed to his owner. According to the commercial, the show airs at 4:00pm on TV3. The same scene used in the commercial was later re-used in "[[ The Clip Show]]".{{MuppetsWiki}}{{Jim Henson's Dinosaurs}}{{DEFAULTSORT:Ugh}}
