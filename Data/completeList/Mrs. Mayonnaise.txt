{{Infobox character|image = File:Mrs. Mayonnaise.jpg|name = Mrs. Mayonnaise|shows = [[Disney's Doug]]|personality = Motherly, loving|alignment = Good|family = |fate = Has died in a car accident before the series|partner=[[Chad Mayonnaise]] (husband)|children=[[Patti Mayonnaise]] (daughter)<br>Art Mayonnaise (son)}}'''Mrs. Mayonnaise '''is a deceased minor character from the series [[Doug]]. She is the deceased wife of [[Chad Mayonnaise|Chad]] and the deceased mother of [[Patti Mayonnaise|Patti]]. She was mentioned by [[Skeeter Valentine|Skeeter]] throughout the Nickelodeon series until her appearance in "Patti's Dad Dilemma". It was revealed that Mrs. Mayonnaise was killed in a car accident that left her husband Chad in a wheelchair when Patti was just a little girl.
