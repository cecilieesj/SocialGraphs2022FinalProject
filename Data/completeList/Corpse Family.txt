==Background==
They are seen through various intervals, and are seen as being very close to [[Jack Skellington]]. The names of the three were revealed in the trading cards released based on the film. The Corpse Kid is named Ethan, the Corpse Dad is named Ned and the Corpse Mom is named Bertha.Â
