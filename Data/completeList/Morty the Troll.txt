'''Morty the Troll'''<ref>http://jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/morty.htm</ref> (voiced by [[Dee Bradley Baker]])<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0048389/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t2</ref> is a fictional character from [[Disney]]'s animated series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. He was seen in "[[Keeping Shop]]" in Season 1. He never makes a reappearance afterwards, not even in Season 2.
==History==
He is a friend of [[Fu Dog]]'s from the sewers. But it shows Morty is tough on Fu Dog, because Fu didn't pay the money back to him. [[Arthur Spudinski|Spud]] said a friend of Fu Dog's stopped by and Spud said "''Come on in, Morty"''. Morty arrives with a baseball bat and says "''Where's the Money''?"!
