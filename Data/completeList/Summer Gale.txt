'''Summer Gale''' is a minor villain from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]'', and a media news weather reporter in [[Middleton]]. 

== Appearances ==

=== ''[[Kim Possible]]'' ===
Her major concern is getting more airtime. To accomplish this, she went so far as to purchase or lease a used Weather Machine from Wacky Wally's Weather Machines to create a surprise overnight snowstorm over the Tri-City Area.<ref name="ep217b">[[Day of the Snowmen]]</ref>. She used the tainted water from [[Lake Wannaweep]] with the unexpected side-effect of snowmen made from the storm's snow coming to malevolent life and attacking the populace. 
==Physical Appearance==
Summer Gale has blonde hair and blue eyes. She wears a purple and lavender winter coat along with pink high heels.
==Personality==
Summer Gale is shown to be rather disregarding of the chaos that she caused. Her only real priority was making herself look good no matter what.
