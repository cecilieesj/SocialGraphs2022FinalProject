==History==
In<ref>Nappers Never Sleep</ref>, Mr. Almaden is first seen when [[Cornelius Fillmore|Fillmore]] and [[Ingrid Third|Ingrid]] head to the Almaden's' house, wanting to see Lorraine for questioning. He informs them that she's not there, so they ask him some questions about her instead. But the safety patrolling duo are able to literally catch her when she does return later and following a chase. He's seen once more, attempting to finish a book he's reading after [[Mrs. Konquist]], [[Everett Konquist]]'s mother, who was at the Almaden residence and found hiding in a cabinet, admits and confesses that she caused some balls to roll out of a cage, and having the two safety patrollers caught in the ball stampede. In attempt to prevent them from solving the case. Everett and Lorraine happen to be not only schoolmates, but neighbors as well and they both have single parents. It's never explained or mentioned whether each of their parents is divorced, separated or widowed.
