'''Samantha Goldberg '''is a minor character on the Playhouse Disney (later Disney Junior) animated series [[Stanley (TV series)|Stanley]]. She is [[Lester Goldberg|Lester]]'s little sister.

She is allergic to dogs, as revealed in "[[Little Dog Lost (Stanley episode)|Little Dog Lost]]". Samantha first appears in "The Tooth About Teeth", where she is afraid of seeing the dentist. She is prone to throwing tantrums, as seen in "Tasmanian Tantrum".
