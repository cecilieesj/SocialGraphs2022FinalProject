"Wasn't that a fun game"|parents=[[Ratso Ratzkiwatzki]] (father)}}'''Buffy Ratzkiwatzki''' is a minor character who appeared in the series ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]''. Her only appearance was in the episode "[[Out of Scale (Rescue Rangers episode)|Out of Scale]]".
==Role in the Series==
Buffy Ratzkiwatzki is the daughter of the crime lord [[Ratso Ratzkiwatzki]] who brings her the Arts Museum and General Fogerty's statue shrunken by the [[Gigantico Raygun|Gigantico Gun]]. [[Chip and Dale]] are captured and brought to her and she uses them as her pets.
