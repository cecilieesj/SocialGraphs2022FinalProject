'''Mrs. Steinmetz''' is one of the three main characters in ''[[Herbie Rides Again]]''.

==Background==
She is [[Tennessee Steinmetz|Tennessee's]] aunt who lives in the [[The Firehouse (The Love Bug)|Firehouse]] with [[Herbie]] and [[Nicole Harris]].

She has a strong bond to her home as it was where she and her late husband, Captain Steinmetz, were married. When [[Alonzo Hawk]] tries to tear it down she is determined to protect it with the help of Herbie and [[Willoughby Whitfield]].
