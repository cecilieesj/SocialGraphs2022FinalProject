'''Captain Frost''' is the captain of the frozen Never North from the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]'', voiced by [[Harland Williams]]. Although Captain Frost was supposed to appear in "[[Captain Frost (Episode)|Captain Frost]]" first, he made his debut in "[[ShiverJack]]".
