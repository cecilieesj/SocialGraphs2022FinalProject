'''Mitesh "Tesh" Cheena''' is the deuteragonist of ''[[The Rocketeer (TV series)|The Rocketeer]]''. He is voiced by Callan Farris.

He is the gadget-minded best friend of Kit, and serves as her Ground Control when she's The Rocketeer.

==Appearance==
Tesh is an 8-year-old boy with brown skin, wavy black hair, and thick eyebrows. For his Ground Control outfit, he wears a yellow and black jacket with grey pants. For his civilian outfit, he wears a blue sweatshirt with a white lightning bolt on it, as well as grey pants and blue sneakers.

===Powers and Abilities===
*'''Gadgets''': He makes these to help upgrade Kit's rocket pack.
