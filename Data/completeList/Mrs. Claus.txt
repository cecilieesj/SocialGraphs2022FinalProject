She often appears alongside him in numerous Christmas specials and movies, featuring him. In [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] media, she appears as a character in ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]'', the ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]'' episodes "[[Mickey Saves Santa]]" and "[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse: Choo-Choo Express|Mickey's Choo-Choo Express]]", ''[[Future-Worm!]]'', and the ''[[Prep & Landing]]'' short, ''[[Operation: Secret Santa]]''.
