'''DZ''' is Jonas' neighbor, who comes over unannounced, and uninvited, and finds himself being thrown out by [http://jonas.wikia.com/wiki/Big_Man Big Man]. He claims to have lots of Hollywood connections, encouraging the boys to throw a party ([[House Party]]), and offering to introduce Macy to Stone Stevens ([[Back to the Beach]]). He lives with his mom who goes to pilates classes. His real name is Dennis Zimmer, but he says only the haters call him that.
