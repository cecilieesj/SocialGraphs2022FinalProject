'''Dolf''' appeared in the third season ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[[Steroids to Heaven]]."

A jockish athlete at [[Bob LaBrea High School]], Dolf is a known user of [[Thornoids]].

He was made from the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Unisaurs spikeback unisaur]. He was voiced by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Sam_McMurray Sam McMurray], his body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Jack_Tate Jack Tate] and his face was operated by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Allan_Trautman Allan Trautman].
