[[File:NicholasGimmlestaubpic.jpg|thumb]]'''Nicholas Gimmlestaub''' is the younger brother of [[Brie Gimmlestaub]] in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.

==Character History==
Not much is known about him. He's the younger brother of Brie and their mother is the mayor of Pasadena.

He doesn't seem to have the best relationship with his sister, because she hides in the most unusual places.

==Trivia==
*Brie often hides him anywhere.
