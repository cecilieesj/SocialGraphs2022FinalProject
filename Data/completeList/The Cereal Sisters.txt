|inspiration = [[The Andrews Sisters]]}}'''The Cereal Sisters''' are a trio of packaged products who performed in the former [[Kitchen Kabaret]] show at [[Epcot]].

The group, comprised of '''Ronnie Rice''', '''Connie Corn''', and '''Mairzy Oats''', sang in the style of the [[The Andrews Sisters]].

They sang a song about the "Toast of the Town": the [[Boogie Woogie Bakery Boy]].
