'''Please Des Traction''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC aqua [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Duck Duck]. He is a Field Office SOS Toon that can be called on. He is a no-star [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Lure_Gags Lure] SOS Toon. When called upon, you will take a big magnet that is given by Des and lure the cogs in battle.

==Trivia==
*﻿His name is a pun on "Distraction", the only difference is the first vowel.
