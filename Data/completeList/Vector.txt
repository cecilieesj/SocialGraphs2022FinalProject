==History==
Simon Utrecht was a successful businessman, industrialist, and politician who craved more power. He used his resources to copy everything about the Fantastic Four's flight into space, and hired a crew in the hopes of gaining superpowers. He is an [[HYDRA]] scientist who got exposed to gamma radiation while on a trip through space. The gamma radiation mutated him, giving him telekinesis. He uses his newfound power to fight [[Avengers|the Avengers]] as the leader of the [[U-Foes]]. Vector is arrogant and impulsive, and prone to make snarky remarks. He is a very wealthy and successful businessman and industrialist.
