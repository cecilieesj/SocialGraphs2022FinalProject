|quote = }}'''Corrie''' ([[Vanessa Hudgens]]) is one of [[Maddie Fitzpatrick|Maddie]]'s best friends in ''[[The Suite Life of Zack & Cody]]''. She studies in Our Lady of Perpetual Sorrow with Mary-Margaret and London. She loves London and everything about her. She is the third ''Suite Life'' actor from ''High School Musical'' to appear on the show (not counting Zac Efron, who was merely a guest). She is often considered an annoyance to London, who often scorns her admiration. She is often seen with one of her best friends, [[Mary Margaret]].
