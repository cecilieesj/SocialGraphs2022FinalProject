==Background==
===Physical appearance===
===[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]===
She is a dog with white skin and pink nose with blue eyes. She wears a pink sutter, gray shirt, and a black dress.

===[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]===
In the the Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse she is pretty much the same but wears purple classes. She wears the same clothing that she wears in the [[Mickey Mouse (TV series)]] but with a pink hat with purple flowers and black shoes.

==Appearances==
===[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]===
She first appeared in the episode [[No]]. When [[Mickey Mouse]] learns to say No. The Old Lady appears asking to see if Mickey can help her cross the street. But when he says No, she turns her simple upside down and Mickey runs away and leaves her by herself.

===[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]===
She appears in the episode [[The Brave Little Squire]] looking at papers. But when Mickey Mouse and [[Mortimer Mouse]] show up Mortimer picks up the Old Lady and throws her away.
