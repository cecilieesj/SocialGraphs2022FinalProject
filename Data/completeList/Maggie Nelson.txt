|home = [[Anaheim]], [[California]]|inspiration=Sister Edwitha from the original 1951 "Angels In the Outfield"}}'''Maggie Nelson''' is the sole female major character in ''[[Angels in the Outfield]]''. She is the keeper of the foster home where she raises [[Roger Bowman]], [[J.P.]], and for less time, [[Miguel (Angels in the Outfield)|Miguel]]. For some reason, she does not go to baseball games, but she does believe in angels.
