|partner = [[Star-Lord]] (ex-boyfriend)}}'''Supergiant''' is a character and supervillainess from [[Marvel Cinematic Universe|Marvel Comics]]. She is a psychotically crazy member of [[Thanos]]' [[Black Order]], always ready to carry out Thanos' schemes with sinister glee. She has telepathy, telekinesis, mind control, and the ability to grow to giant size at will.

==Appearances==
===[[Avengers Assemble]]===
Supergiant appears with the rest of the [[Black Order]] to free there [[Thanos|master]]. Supergiant encounters [[The Avengers]] after they freed Thanos. While their master receives new powers, Supergiant fought [[Hulk|The Hulk]], discovering he is immune to her mind control. Supergiant and the Black Order were present in the battle for Earth. After Thanos' defeat she and the rest of the Black Order where in custody by [[Guardians of the Galaxy (team)|the Guardians of the Galaxy]].
===[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]===
Supergiant is a member of the Black Order, who has the ability to expand her size and figure into ginormous height. She is one of [[Star-Lord]]'s ex girlfriends, who had dated, however it ended badly and she has resented him ever since. She constantly teams up with Star-Lord's other ex girlfriend [[Lucy the A'askvarian]] to get revenge on Star-Lord.
