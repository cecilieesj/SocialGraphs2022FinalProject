}}'''Nancy Margaret Clancy''' is the protagonist of the [[Disney Junior]] animated series ''[[Fancy Nancy]]''.

==Background==
Nancy is a seven-year-old girl who enjoys fancy things and is a bit of a Francophile. Her favorite color is fuchsia, she adores butterflies, has an adorable dog named Frenchy, her own secret delivery mailbox to share all her party invitations with her best friend Bree, and her own playhouse. She lives in a town called Plainfield, Ohio with her family.

===Physical appearance===
Nancy has fair skin, green eyes, and curly, red hair that she usually wears up. Usually, she is seen wearing a yellow-gold tiara with three blue flowers, a yellow T-shirt with pink trim, a pink tutu with a blue bow belt, purple beads, a few bracelets, yellow, pink, and blue pastel striped tights, pale pink socks, and pink strapped Mary Jane shoes.
