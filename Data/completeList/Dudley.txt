'''Dudley''' is [[King Triton]]'s best friend in ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]'' television 
series of whom [[Sebastian]] is often jealous.

==Appearances==
===''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid: The Series]]''===
Dudley mumbles and speaks very slowly and does not swim like other sea turtles in this show, instead he walks slowly along the sea floor like a land turtle and keeps important documents within his shell when he retracts his head. His conversation is always interrupted by his employer as he understands what the former is about to say.
