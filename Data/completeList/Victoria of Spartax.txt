'''Princess Victoria''' (or known as '''Victoria of Spartax''' is the daughter [[J'son]] and sister of [[Star-Lord|Peter Quill]].

== Personality==
After first meeting her brother, she started cold, serious, and not so loving (not even a good welcome to him). During her time with her brother, she started to see why he posses the title, "Star-Lord". At the end of her debut episode, she tried to have her brother help with the meeting and ended with Peter and Victoria both saying "OK, don't get mad".
