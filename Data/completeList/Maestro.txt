== Description ==
In the future, after a devastating nuclear war Hulk has absorbed a large amount of radiation, which increased his physical strength, but also made it possible to maintain a brilliant intellect, Benner. Maestro Dr. Doom stole from his time machine and went back in time. But he was defeated when Benner sent him via his own time machine in the center of the explosion of gamma-bomb that spawned the Hulk.
