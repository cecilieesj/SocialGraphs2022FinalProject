}}'''Margo''' is a stuck-up stickbug who lives in the Amazon Rainforest. She is above [[Brandy Harrington|Brandy]] in the social chain of the Amazon Rainforest.

==Personality==
Margo is described as often being "the popular one." She is also said to be "malicious" and loves fashion and boys. Margo also appears to be very stuck-up, selfish and vain. Despite the fact that she hangs out with Brandy, in some instances she is not afraid of belittling her in order to improve her own social standing.
