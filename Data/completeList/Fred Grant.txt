'''Winifred "Fred" Grant''' is a main character of the  [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Hamster & Gretel]]''. She is the tech-savvy cousin of Kevin and Gretel.

== Background ==

=== Physical appearance ===
Fred has light skin and brown bangs that cover up one her eyes. She wears a purple shirt with an red jacket, black pants and pink glasses.
