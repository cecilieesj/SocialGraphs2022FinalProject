{{Infobox Character|image = Screen Shot 2013-04-01 at 5.06.03 PM.png|shows = ''[[Teacher's Pet]]''|likes = [[Ian Wazselewski]]|name = Ruth|alignment = Good}}'''Ruth''' is a secondary character in ''[[Teacher's Pet]]''. She is the only minor character who has a name. She is [[Ian Wazselewski|Ian]]'s crush and he constantly chases her until she becomes "Mrs. Ian Wazselewski" and she runs away in disgust. In the episode "[[A Dog for All Seasons]]", it is revealed she is Jewish. Ruth loves her blanket.
