|occupation = News reporter}}'''Shandra Jimenez''' is a supporting character in the TV animated series ''[[Gravity Falls]]''. She is the town's news reporter, whom [[Toby Determined]] has a huge crush on.

==Background==
===Physical appearance===
She is a tall, tan woman with reddish-brown hair. She wears a light blue shirt with a slightly darker blue vest and a black skirt that goes down to her knees. She has very high, very distinct cheekbones, a 'beauty mark' mole on her face, and wears dark pink lipstick. She usually carries a microphone in her hand.

==Role in the series==
Shandra makes her debut in season 1 episode "[[Headhunters]]". She is present at [[Stan Pines|Stan Pine's]] grand reopening of the [[Mystery Shack]] wax museum and asks him if he will give them free pizza as promised.

It is later revealed that Toby Determined has a crush on her and owns a cardboard cutout of her, which he sometimes makes out with.

In "[[The Hand That Rocks the Mabel]]", she is mentioned by Toby when [[Li'l Gideon|Gideon Gleeful]] gave him Shandra's phone number as part of a deal to get back at [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]].

In "[[Boyz Crazy]]", Shandra is shown on TV reporting on Ergman Bratsman's arrest, describing how he was pulled over and arrested for not having a rear license plate.

Her latest appearance is in the season finale, "[[Gideon Rises]]". She is first seen giving a report about the Mystery Shack which is now under Gideon's control and shows up again at the end, reporting about Gideon's arrest and congratulating Grunkle Stan for his brave actions, calling him a town hero.
