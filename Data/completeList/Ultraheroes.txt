|leaders = [[Eega Beeva]]}}The '''Ultraheroes''' are a fictional team of superheroes whose rivals are the [[Bad-7]]. They were assembled by [[Eega Beeva]] to defeat the Bad-7 and save the world. These heroes are actually the alter egos of their original selves and include [[Mickey Mouse & Friends]].
