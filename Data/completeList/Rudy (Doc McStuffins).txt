|quote = ''"(barks)"''|alignment = Good}}'''Rudy''' is a minor character from the Disney Junior series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. He is Rudy and Alma's terrier puppy.
==Appearance==
Rudy is a small brown terrier puppy with white paws and a white belly, and wears a blue collar with a yellow diamond-shaped license.
==Personality==
Rudy is always very mischievous and energetic, but he can be a handful to Emmie and Doc. He likes to cause trouble and bother Doc's toy crab, Hermie.
