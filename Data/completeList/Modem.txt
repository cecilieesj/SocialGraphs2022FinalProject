==Appearances==
===''[[The Brave Little Toaster to the Rescue]]''===
The Modem plays a major role in this film. When the gang sets up their rescue cart, he tells them that he has orders to come with them for power connection to track down the truck to Tartarus Laboratories. In the end, they catch up with the truck and fall inside the cab.
