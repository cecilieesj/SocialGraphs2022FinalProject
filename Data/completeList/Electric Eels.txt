|friends = [[Marina Del Rey]]|dislikes = Being outsmarted.}}The '''Electric Eels''' are the pets, henchmen, and minions of [[Marina Del Rey]] and the secondary antagonists from ''[[The Little Mermaid: Ariel's Beginning]]''.

==Background==
They are minions of Marina Del Rey. Ariel and her friends fight Marina and her eels to save the kingdom and then, they tied them up into a shape of a knot.

==Trivia==
*Marina's Eels were represented from [[Flotsam and Jetsam]] in the [[The Little Mermaid|first film]] and [[Cloak and Dagger]], the two of [[Morgana]]'s minion manta rays from the [[The Little Mermaid II: Return to the Sea|second film and sequel]].
*Electric eels in real life are actually freshwater fish native to the Amazon River with nearly no fins. Their teeth are also inside the mouth, whereareas in the movie, they have exposed sharp teeth.
