'''Fun Meal Emperor Zurg''' is a character in the ''[[Toy Story Toons]]'' episode "[[Small Fry]]".

==Description==
Fun Meal Emperor Zurg is a miniature version of [[Emperor Zurg]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Small Fry]]''===
He's first seen with [[Fun Meal Buzz Lightyear]] when he gets the idea to replace [[Buzz Lightyear]]. When asked to go out, Fun Meal Emperor Zurg remains behind in order to not anger "the chicken people" while Fun Meal Buzz escapes.
At the credits, he laughs at a joke said by a utility belt toy.
