|powers = Super strength, super extending arms, can control anything mechanical}}'''Armstrong''' is a robot created by [[Gyro Gearloose]], which appeared in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' [[Armstrong (episode)|episode of the same name]].

==Appearances==
===''[[DuckTales]]''===
Armstrong is introduced as Gyro's latest invention. He is helpful for a while, until he takes over [[Launchpad McQuack|Launchpad]] and [[Duckworth]]'s jobs. Then he turns bad, steals Scrooge's money and holds him and Gyro hostage. He was defeated when water from Launchpad's plane was dumped on him, causing him to short-circuit and fall to pieces.
===[[DuckTales (2017 series)|''DuckTales'' (2017 series)]]===
In the first episode "[[Woo-oo!]]", Armstrong's head is shown to be kept inside Scrooge's garage. Also, in "[[The Great Dime Chase!]]", Armstrong's name can be seen in Gyro's notes, indicating that he was among the robots built by Gyro that ended up going rogue.

==Trivia==
*The robots in "[[Robot Robbers]]" bear a strong resemblance to Armstrong.
*Armstrong was mentioned in the episode "''Robot Robbers''" as "''The Last Robot''".
