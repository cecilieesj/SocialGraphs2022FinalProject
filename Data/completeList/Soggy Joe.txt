'''Soggy Joe''' is a supporting character in the [[2019]] series ''[[Amphibia]]''. as well as the main character of the "Wild Amphibia" shorts.

== Background ==
=== Personality ===
Loud and raspy,  yet helpful.

=== Appearance ===
He has Muscular tattooed arms, a beard with bones stuck in it and light blue skin.

==Trivia==
*It is revealed in "[[Truck Stop Polly]]", that he is also a who likes to be referred to as "Speedy Joe" when driving his beetle.
