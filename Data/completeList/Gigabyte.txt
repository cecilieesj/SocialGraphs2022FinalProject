'''Gigabyte''' is a minor antagonist in the attraction ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Audience]]''. He is [[Nick Szalinski]]'s pet snake, who almost eats the audience after finding them shrunken.

==Appearances==
===''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Audience]]''===
Gigabyte appears near the end of the attraction, where he approaches the shrunken Nick and audience in an attempt to eat them, as he had not been fed that day. Fortunately, [[Quark]] arrives and scares the snake away with his barking, just in time for [[Wayne Szalinski|Wayne]] to fix his [[Szalinzki's Shrinking Machine 2.0|shrinking machine]] and restore them back to normal.

===''[[Kingdom Keepers III: Disney in Shadow]]''===
Gigabyte appears as a member of [[The Overtakers]]. He confronts the Kingdom Keepers when they go to the ''Honey, I Shrunk the Audience'' attraction while looking for a sword needed to help find [[Wayne Kresky]].

Gigabyte does not appear in the 2020 version of the book, due to ''Honey, I Shrunk the Audience'' having closed down by then.
