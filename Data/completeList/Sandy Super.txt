[[File:Sandy_Super.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Sandy Super''' is a one-time guest character and antagonist of ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''.

==History==
She is the daughter and creator of Super Adventure Land. She seems to have a crush on [[Spencer Walsh|Spencer]], who is [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]]'s current boyfriend. She has only to be seen flirting with Spencer once.
