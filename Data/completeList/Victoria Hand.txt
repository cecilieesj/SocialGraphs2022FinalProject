'''Victoria Hand''' is a  [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared on ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''. She appears in the first season, and is portrayed by Saffron Burrows. Victoria was a [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] agent who was killed by [[Grant Ward]] after she discovered [[Deathlok (John Garrett)|John Garrett]] was the Clairvoyant.

Victoria Hand was created by Brian Michael Bendis and Mike Deodato.

==Alternate Timeline==
After [[Phil Coulson (LMD)|Coulson]] and his team travel back in time, they end up creating a branched timeline. Victoria Hand of [[1983]] is a young S.H.I.E.L.D. agent. In this new timeline, she and her peers travel to a S.H.I.E.L.D. safehouse, after the [[Triskelion]] is destroyed. When [[Deathlok (John Garrett)|John Garrett]] teleports in, she shoots him.

==Trivia==
*Victoria Hand is listed amongst the dossier of [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] assets, who pose a threat, on [[HYDRA]] agent, [[Freddy Malick|Wilfred Malick]]'s computer, in the ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' episode, "[[A Trout in the Milk]]".
**Those also included are; [[Isabelle Hartley]], [[Hulk|Bruce Banner]], [[Robert Gonzales]], and [[Peggy Carter]].
