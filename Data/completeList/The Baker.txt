|alignment = Neutral|likes = Baking}}'''The Baker''' is another minor character from Disney's [[1978]] animated short, ''[[The Small One]]''.

==The Small One==
The Baker is first seen doing his job; baking cakes and treats when all of a sudden, [[The Boy|a boy]] comes by to ask him if he wants buy his donkey, [[Small One (Character)|The Small One]] but the Baker refuses; stating that he's "a scrawny, undernourished little beast" and that he since his wife's "a healthy size, would rather buy a horse at least" and then, [[The Three Merchants|three Merchants]] come out of nowhere, take him seriously and literally, and start emptying his pockets full of gold coins into one of their sacks while singing [[The Merchant's Song|their song]], dancing, and presenting him with a funny-looking scrawny horse. The horse takes a sudden liking to the baker by licking him but the Baker rejects his way of showing affection to him.
