The '''HQ Officers''' are found in Toon HQs in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Playgrounds playgrounds] and the streets. They give you a choice of [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontask ToonTasks] to complete. The first HQ Officer you meet is HQ Harry during the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontorial Toontorial]. He guides you through your [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Shticker_Book Shtickter book] and helps you to use it.

==Trivia==
*HQ Harry is the only HQ Officer to have a name.
*The HQ Officers have no names unlike HQ Harry, rather they are merely called HQ Officer.
*In the district Toon Valley many toons like to get behind the window on the right(left if you enter through the other door)this can happen on any district that has a lot of toons.
