'''Rebecca Reid''' is a character in Disney's [[2013]] film ''[[The Lone Ranger]].'' She is the wife of [[Dan Reid]].

==Background==
Made for finer things yet possessing the grit and independence of any frontiersman, Rebecca is a woman whose spirit is caught between two men, her husband Dan and his more refined younger brother John.
