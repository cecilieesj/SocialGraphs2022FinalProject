==Role in the film==
Vulcan assists [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]] in creating a storm to ruin the party of the creatures on the foothills of [[Mount Olympus]] by creating lightning bolts to throw down to the Earth below. When he uses his mallet to hit one, it makes thunder. He puts his tools away when Zeus goes to sleep after a stormy night of mischief. He is not seen again for the rest of the segment.

==Trivia==
* Vulcan is the god's Roman name. In [[Greece]], he is called Hephaestus.
