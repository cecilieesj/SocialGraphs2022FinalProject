'''''Rintrah''''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] film ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]''. 

Rintrah was created by Peter Gillis, Chris Warner, Scott Williams and Randy Emberlin.

==Background==
===Powers and Abilities===
Rintrah is a sorcerer and a master of the mystic arts. 

===Personality===
Rintrah is a minotaur-humanoid creature, who serves as [[Doctor Strange]]'s apprentice.
