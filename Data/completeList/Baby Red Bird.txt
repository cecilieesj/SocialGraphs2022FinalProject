'''Baby Red Bird''' is a little bird in ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]''. He first appeared in ''[[Goofy's Bird]]'' when he hatched out of an egg from a nest that [[Goofy]] found. He originally lived with his mommy in the forest. But now they live in a birdhouse next to the clubhouse.
