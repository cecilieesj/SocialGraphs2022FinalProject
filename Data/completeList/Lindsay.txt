'''Lindsay''' is a recurring character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''.


==Personality==
She is a very sassy and girly, and very similar to [[Avery Jennings|Avery]] talking fast a lot, which is why the two are BFF. She also portrayed to be a little dumbstruck. Lindsay helps Avery any problems in every episodes.
==Physical Description==
She is about the same height as Avery, and has long curly hair. She often wears hats. She wears a dresses with jackets and flats with girly designs.
==Relationship==
===[[Avery Jennings]]===
She is Lindsay's friend. It is unknown how long they have been friends, though it been revealed that they became friends they both talked fast and bit smarter.
===Karl Fink===
She seems to dislike Karl, as he is nemesis. However they do not seem to have a bad past history.
