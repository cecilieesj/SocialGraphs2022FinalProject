'''Bea Sharpe''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC sea green [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Monkey Monkey]. She is a Field Office SOS Toon. She is a no-star [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Sound_Gags Sound] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon].

==Trivia==
*Her name is a pun on ''"b-sharp"'', a common musical designation; it is denoted by a hash mark in the ''b'' position.
*She is the weakest SOS toon, doing only 10 damage to all cogs.
