'''Faucet''' is one of the major characters from ''[[The Brave Little Toaster Goes to Mars]]''.

==Role in the film==
She is first seen when her nozzle sprayer son, [[Squirt (The Brave Little Toaster)|Squirt]] helps her unscrew something stuck on her end. Before the gang leaves to go to [[Mars]], she fills up [[Robbie McGroarty|Robbie]]'s bottle and tells them to make sure he drinks plenty of water, and also tell him she said hi. Near the end of the film, when she notices Squirt dripping, she thinks he's crying, but he tells her that he actually needs a new washer. She then tells him that maybe [[Santa Claus|Santa]] will bring him one. At the end of the film, Faucet and her son are asleep.

==Trivia==
*[[Farrah Fawcett]], Faucet's voice actress, is the real-life mother of Squirt's voice actor, Redmond O'Neal.
