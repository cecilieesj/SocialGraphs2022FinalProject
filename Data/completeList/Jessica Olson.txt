'''Jessica Olson''' is a main character of [[Disney Channel Original Movies|''Disney Channel's Original'' ''Movie'']] ''[[Starstruck (2010 film)|Starstruck]]''. She is portrayed by [[Danielle Campbell]].
==History==
She is a 15 year old girl who lives in Kalamazoo, [[Michigan]] and her family are going to [[Los Angeles]], [[California]] to visit her grandmother.
'''{{DEFAULTSORT:Olson, Jessica}}'''
Jessica meets christopher at his concert when he hits her with a door. They then go to the doctor but the paparazzi are following them so he stays in her grandmothers garage. As the movie goes on Jessica and Christopher get closer but after spending time together he tells her that she is a nobody. Jessica leaves for home and her sister convinces her to go to the school dance. Jessica is still sad about christopher at this point. Christopher shows up at the school dance and apologizes to Jessica.
