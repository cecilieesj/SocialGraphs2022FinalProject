'''Griff Halloran''' is a character who appears in the animated series, ''[[Star Wars Resistance]]''. He is one of the Aces that flies defense for the [[Colossus (location)|Colossus]] platform.

==Background==
As an ex-Imperial TIE pilot, Griff flies an extensively modified TIE-based racer that emphasizes raw power. No one is quite certain whether or not Griff is a veteran of the Imperial Navy, or whether he just flies old Imperial colors to intimidate his opponents.
