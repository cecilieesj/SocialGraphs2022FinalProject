'''Kayla Morgan''' is the protagonist of the [[Disney Channel]][[Disney Channel Original Movie| Original Movie]], ''[[Cloud 9]]''.

==Background==
===Physical Appearance===
Kayla has curly blonde hair and green eyes. She wears girly outfits with a coat.

==Role in the film==
She is a 17-year-old diva snowboarder, who enjoys fashion and styling. Her life is turned upside down when she is kicked from the Swift Team and working at Dog Kennels with [[Will Cloud]].
