'''Killbot 86''' is a character from [[Wander Over Yonder]] who first appeared in "[[The Bounty]]". He is a bounty hunter who formerly worked for [[Lord Hater]] to capture [[Wander]] and [[Sylvia]], but [[Commander Peepers]] kept messing with his plans.

A different Killbot (Killbot 85) later appeared in "[[The Waste of Time]]", in the day Wander and Sylvia time traveled back to where they met. There, Killbot 85 and Sylvia were righting on who would be the one who captures Wander.
