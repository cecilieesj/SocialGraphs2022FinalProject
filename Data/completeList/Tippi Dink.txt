'''[[File:Char_33070.jpg|thumb]]Tippingdale "Tippi" Dink''' (voiced by Doris Belack) is [[Bud Dink]]'s sarcastic and normally monotonous, but generally well-intentioned and friendly, wife in the series ''[[Doug]]''. Near the end of the Nickelodeon series, she defeated [[Bob White]] in election as mayor. This role, which toned down her sarcasm, continued into the Disney series.
