[[File:Mike_Moe.png|thumb]]'''Mike Moe''' (or Junpei Takayama) is a student at [[Norrisville High]]. He wears glasses all the time.

==Trivia==
*In the episode "[[Weinerman Up]]", Mike Moe is shown on the leaderboards by the name of "Junpei Takayama". Junpei Takayama is one of the character designers for the show.
*As revealed in [[McFear Factor]], Mike Moe is afraid of evil bananas.
*In [[Night of the Living McFizzles]], he is dressed up as a panda, and was on the [[Whoopee World]] Frightacular with [[Buttermaker]], S. Renisewn, and Nameless Girl with Big Eyelashes.
**Big Eyelashes held him because she was scared, and carried him when she exited the ride.
**He also is dressed up like a panda in [[So U Think U Can Stank]], probably for the Norrisville High Talent Show.
*While running out of the library in [[Monster Drill]], Mike caught and used the megaphone that [[Principal Slimovitz]] dropped, becuase Glenn had pushed him.
*Mike went to [[Ninja Camp]]. He wrestled Howard, using the choke-hold on him at one point.
*Mike is one of the kids who's wheeled by Randy and Howard, having failed to catch Pitch Kickem's shot in [[The McHugger Games]].
*In "[[Sorcerer in Love 2: The Sorceress' Revenge]]", he was with Jed Elinoff (Background Character), Scott Thomas (Background Character) and another guy who were angry at Randy for playing Manfred Musterman for eight hours while they have been waiting in the line.
*He had his first speaking role in Julian's Birthday Surprise.
