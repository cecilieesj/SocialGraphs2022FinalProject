|status = Active}}'''Dinah''' replaced [[Fifi (Mickey Universe)|Fifi the Peke]] as [[Pluto|Pluto's]] girlfriend in the later [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]] cartoons.

She appears as Pluto's girlfriend although she sometimes dates [[Butch the Bulldog]] as well. She first appeared in ''[[The Sleep Walker]]'', and made appearances in several other cartoons: ''[[Canine Casanova]]'', ''[[In Dutch]]'', ''[[Pluto's Heart Throb]]'', ''[[Wonder Dog]]'', ''[[Pluto's Arrow Error]]'', ''[[Presto Pluto]]'', and ''[[Pluto's Magic Paws]]''. It is shown that she had puppies of her own, in ''The Sleep Walker'' and ''Canine Casanova''. She also appeared in the ''[[House of Mouse]]'' episode "[[Big Bad Wolf Daddy]]".

==Trivia==
*Pluto and Dinah kissed each other two times (the first time in "Canine Casanova" with Pluto licking his lips covered with saliva after a big, wet one, and the second time in "Presto Pluto") - not counting Dinah licking Pluto's lips at the end of "Canine Casanova". (Note: Real-life dogs kiss by licking each other's mouths, unlike some cartoon dogs kissing each other like humans.)
*Dinah dumped Butch in "Pluto's Heart Throb" after the latter gave her a big, slobbery kiss and pushed her into the swimming pool.
