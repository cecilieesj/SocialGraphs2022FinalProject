'''Padma Shinivasanviswanthan''' is a minor character on [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]] ''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''. She is portrayed by [[Tiya Sircar]].

==Background==
She was born in a small village in [[India]] to her mother Gitanjail Shinivasanviswanathan (later Buffy) and her unnamed father. She is known to have three brothers.
===Physical appearance===
Padma has tan skin and dark hair. She has dark eyes and mildly colored lips.
