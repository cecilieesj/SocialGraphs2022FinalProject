[[File:Rayon.png|thumb]]'''Rayon''' is the owner of the [http://motorcitydisneyxd.wikia.com/wiki/Skylark_Motel Skylark Motel], and a regular client of the [[Burners]].

==Description==
A sharp dressed professional in cool shades. On the back of his suit is the number 1, and he's often seen with two other men who wear a number 2 and number 3.

==Personality==
Rayon is cool and collected, and has been known to put business before friends.
