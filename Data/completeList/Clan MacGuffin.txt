|fate = }}'''Clan MacGuffin''' is a Scottish clan in ''[[Brave]]''. It is one of the four clans that have united to form the DunBroch Kingdom. Its leader is called the [[Lord MacGuffin]]. The clan's tartan is green and orange. Its emblem is the cauldron. According to the promotional clip ''A Royal Bash'', the MacGuffins have a legend relating to their symbol, which states that a with one potato, a magical cauldron fed the entire clan and gave them the force to slay 10,000 [[Clan Macintosh|Macintosh]]'s. The term MacGuffin is best known as referring to an object, goal that the protagonist is willing to do anything in order to achieve.{{PixarWiki}}
