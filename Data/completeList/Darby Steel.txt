'''Darby Steel''' is a character in the [[2022]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]â€¢[[Pixar]] animated film, ''[[Lightyear]]''. She is a gruff, no-nonsense, and not-exactly-voluntary member of the Junior Zap Patrol. She was doing time in prison until she joined the unit in exchange of having her sentence reduced. Darby is known to be an explosives expert, who can take any device and turn it into something that will blow up.
