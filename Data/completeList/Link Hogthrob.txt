'''Link Hogthrob''' is a blond-haired, cleft-chinned Muppet pig with a high opinion of himself and he is not too bright.
He debuted during the second season of ''The Muppet Show'' as the not-so-fearless captain of the spaceship Swinetrek in the popular "Pigs in Space" skits.
