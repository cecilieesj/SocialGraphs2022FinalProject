|designer = [[Hayao Miyazaki]]|children=3 unnamed sons}}'''Mr. Piccolo''' is a supporting character from the 1992 Miyazaki animated film ''[[Porco Rosso (film)|Porco Rosso]]''. He is a retired plane mechanic, who is a old friend of the main character, [[Porco Rosso|Porco]], as well as the grandfather of the main female lead, [[Fio Piccolo|Flo]]. 

He often makes it clear he loves money, but its also very clear that he cares about Porco and his granddaughter much more than his payment.
