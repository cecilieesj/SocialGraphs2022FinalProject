{{Infobox character|name = Jared|shows = ''[[I'm in the Band]]''|model = Aaron Albert|personality = Nerdy, smart, clever, odd|image = Jared.jpg|alignment = Good|likes = Rockets, robots}}'''Jared''' is a character from ''[[I'm in the Band]]'' played by Aaron Albert. He is [[Tripp Campbell|Tripp]]'s nerdy, exceptionally smart, and odd friend. He screams like a girl when he gets freaked out. He also went to bird calling camp. He seems to love rockets and robots. He also has been to a bird calling camp, has a crush on the lunch lady and has a father who builds robots.
