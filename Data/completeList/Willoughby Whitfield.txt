'''Willoughby Whitfield''' is the protagonist in ''[[Herbie Rides Again]]''.

==Background==
Willougby is a lawyer and [[Alonzo Hawk]]'s nephew. He was given the assignment to get [[Mrs. Steinmetz]] to move out of the [[The Firehouse (The Love Bug)|firehouse]]. However, after spending time with [[Nicole Harris]], he finally sees what kind of person his Uncle Alonzo really is, and decides to help Mrs. Steinmetz protect her home.

During his time with Nicole, they fall in love, and get married at the end of the film.
