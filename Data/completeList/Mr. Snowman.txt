|friends = [[Rippen]]}}'''Mr. Snowman''' is a snowman and antagonist from ''[[Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero]]''.

==History==
At first, he is just a head when [[Penn Zero|Penn]], [[Boone]], and [[Sashi Kobayashi|Sashi]] find him. Penn takes his advice on sneaking into the workshop through the back door, but they end up getting trapped. Mr. Snowman, his body returning to him, reveals that he tricked the heroes and works for [[Rippen]].
