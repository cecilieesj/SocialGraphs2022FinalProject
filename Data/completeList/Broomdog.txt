The '''Broomdog''' is a minor character and tertiary antagonist in ''[[Alice in Wonderland]]''.



==Background==
The Broomdog is shown to be a busy, hard-working dog-like creature.

==Role in the film==
The Broomdog first appears in [[Tulgey Wood]] clearing the path [[Alice]] is walking on. After that, he steps behind her to finish of the rest of the path with his broom like-tail. He is not seen again for the rest of the film.

==Trivia==
*The Broomdog is one of the few Tulgey Wood creatures who did not approach a tearful Alice during the musical number "[[Very Good Advice]]".
