[[File:Char_39175.jpg|thumb]]'''Mrs. Cartflight''' ([[Kevin Michael Richardson]]) is Rayna's mother. She appears rarely on the show. Her husband, Mr. Cartflight, is in the army and never appeared on the show.

==Appearances==
All Mrs. Cartflight's appearances are only cameos, she has only one or two lines in each episode.
