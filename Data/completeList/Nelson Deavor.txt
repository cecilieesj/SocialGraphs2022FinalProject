|alias = Screenslaver}}'''Nelson Deavor''' is a deleted character from ''[[Incredibles 2]]'', who was originally set to be the main antagonist of the film, but was replaced by [[Evelyn Deavor]], as creators thought it better to have a female villain.

Clay sculpts and early iterations of the movie included Nelson as [[Winston Deavor|Winston's]] brother instead of Evelyn. He looked a lot like Winston, but with hair that wasn't slicked back.
