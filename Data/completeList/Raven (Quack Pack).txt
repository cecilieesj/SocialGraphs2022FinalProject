|voice = [[Frank Welker]]}}'''Raven''' is a cat who appears in the ''[[Quack Pack]]'' episode "[[Cat & Louse]]".

==Physical appearance==
Raven has the typical appearance of a black cat. He has black fur with a white mouth, white paws, yellow eyes, and a white stripe at the end of his tail. He also has pink ears and yellow eyes with green pupils.

==Personality==
Raven can be cowardly at times, but he's mostly smart, brave, daring, nice, and friendly.

==Role in episode==
Raven is one of [[Andre Demouche]]'s pet cats. Andre impresses [[Daisy Duck|Daisy]] by showing her that he can train Raven. Huey is shown to be afraid of Raven and tries to avoid him every time he sees him. Dewey and Louie discover that Huey has been afraid of domestic cats ever since Halloween when the boys were younger. After Andre locks the ducks in his rooms after being discovered that he's a thief who hides treasures, he makes Raven chase Huey. When Raven does so, he is about to fall off a cliff and land in a ravine. Huey rescues him and he's not afraid of him anymore. He then puts Raven on a toy jet plane and makes him attack Andre until he goes to the kitchen. When Andre gets attacked by [[Andre's Cats|his jungle cats]], it is revealed that Huey is actually allergic to Raven.
