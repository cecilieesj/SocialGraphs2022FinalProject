'''Ranginald Bagel''' is a student at [[Norrisville High]]. He was a background character until he got a major appearance in "[[Bring Me the Head of Ranginald Bagel!]]."

==Personality==

==Appearance==
Ranginald has black hair and black eyes and he has a unibrow.

==Trivia==
*Randy Cunningham makes up the same name when dealing with [[Catfish Booray]] in "[[Swampy Seconds]]," yet he doesn't know that there's a real Ranginald Bagel until "[[Bring Me the Head of Ranginald Bagel!]]."
*Ranginald thinks he's a successful stand-up comedian, but is really considered by his fellow classmates as merely the most annoying kid in school.
*Ranginald Bagel is voiced by comedian [[Gilbert Gottfried]], who also voiced [[Iago]] in Disney's [[Aladdin (1992 film)|Aladdin]] and Nicolai Technus in ''Danny Phantom'', among others. Ranginald's character design resembles Gottfried's real-life appearance.
*Since "Bring Me the Head of Ranginald Bagel" Ranginald has not had a speaking role.
