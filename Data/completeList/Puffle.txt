|status = Presumably active}}{{Quote|Squeak!|Onomatopeia for puffle noises}}'''Puffles''' are creatures from the ''[[Club Penguin]]'' franchise. Penguins keep many of them as pets.

==Appearance==
Their body generally looks spherical, with spiky hair of the same color on top, and have a face formed (from top to bottom) by two hidden brows, two big parallel eyes with dark pupils, two hidden nostrils, two cheeks, along with a mouth with lips, white teeth that sometimes seem to have an overbite, and a tongue.

==[[Disney Parks, Experiences and Products|Disney Parks]]==
Various puffles appeared as plush toys on top of a barrel labelled "CREAM SODA", at a themed spot in [[Disney's Hollywood Studios]], from [[April 30]], [[2009]] to [[May 13]], 2009.

[[w:c:clubpenguin:Enrique|Enrique]] (wearing a pirate hat) and 5 other [[w:c:clubpenguin:Blue Crystal Puffle|blue crystal puffles]] appeared as decorations on the ''Club Penguin'' parade float of the [[Disney Parks Magical Christmas Celebration|Disney Parks' Frozen Christmas Celebration]] in [[2014]].

==Trivia==
*They are known for eating [[w:c:clubpenguin:O' Berry|O'berries]], which taste very spicy to penguins.
*Puffles do not like fish, coffee, nor eating moss.
*Every puffle species has different tongue color.
*The faces of the "meeps" from the ''[[Roblox]]'' game, ''Meep City'', resemble the faces of puffles.
