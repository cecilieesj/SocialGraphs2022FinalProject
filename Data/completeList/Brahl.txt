'''Brahl''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'', played by Stephen Blackeheart. He is a member of an alien race called the Achernonians, and he can turn intangible, turn other things intangible, and fire energy beams from his hands. He is the newest recruit of [[Ravagers|the Ravagers]].
