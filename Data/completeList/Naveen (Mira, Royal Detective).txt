'''Naveen''' is a character in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Mira, Royal Detective]]''. He is the father of Meena, Priya, and Chotu and [[Mira]]'s uncle.


==Background ==
He hasn't been seen until the episode "[[Mystery at the Marketplace]]". He was very happy to be reunited with his wife and children, and he was also happy to see his niece Mira.


== Trivia ==

* He shares the same name as the character [[Prince Naveen]] from the [[2009]] Disney animated feature, ''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''.
