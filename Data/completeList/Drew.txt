'''Drew''' is a character in ''[[The Suite Life of Zack & Cody]]''. He is a pre-teen who appeared as an antagonist in the episode "[[Hotel Hangout]]".

==History==
[[Zack Martin|Zack]] and [[Cody Martin]] met Drew on their first day of school in Boston, but as Drew was one of the "cool kids", the twins were shunned. When Drew finds out that Zack and Cody live in the Tipton Hotel, he inducts them into his crew. But after they discover that he was just using them to get to the hotel, they force him to leave, instead choosing to hang out with [[Max (The Suite Life)|Max]] and Tapeworm.
