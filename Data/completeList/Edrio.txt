|affiliations = Partisans|fate = Dies at the hands of the Death Star on Jedha|likes=Rebellion|dislikes=The Galactic Empire, his men getting killed|status=Deceased}}'''Edrio "Two Tubes"''' is a character who appears in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' movie ''[[Rogue One]]''. He is a mercenary pilot who flies alongside his eggmate, Benthic. They share the nickname derived from the breathing apparatus that allows Tognath physiology to process oxygen atmospheres. Edrio’s native world of Yar Togna was conquered and occupied by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], forcing him to flee as a refugee. With a desire to strike back at the Empire, Edrio and Benthic have allied with [[Saw Gerrera]].
