|alignment = Good}}'''Bootle Beetle''' is an elderly bootle beetle who is [[Donald Duck]]<span>'s arch enemy in a number of </span>''Donald Duck''<span> cartoon shorts. He is old and sage, and </span>gives a runaway beetle a dose of reality in the 1949 animated short, ''[[The Greener Yard]]''. In later appearances, he appears as a friend of [[Bucky Bug]] who lives in Junkville and is often asked for his wise advice by the town's residents.
