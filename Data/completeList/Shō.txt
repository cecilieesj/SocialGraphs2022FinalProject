'''Shō''' (known as '''Shawn''' in the US version) is the male lead of ''[[The Secret World of Arrietty]]''. He is a 12-year-old human boy who becomes [[Arrietty]]'s friend. He meets Arrietty while recuperating from an illness at the old house in which his mother was raised. He returns to the home a year later in the next summer, indicating that the heart operation had been successful. He is happy to hear rumors of objects disappearing in his neighbors' homes.

==Appearance==
Sho has black hair and brown eyes. He wears a white shirt and blue trousers, and is also seen wearing pajamas.
