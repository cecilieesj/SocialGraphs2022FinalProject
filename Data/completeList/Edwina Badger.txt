'''Edwina Badger''' is a musician who also goes by the name "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_419:_The_Great_Bandini The Great Bandini]". She was voiced by [[Whoopi Goldberg]]. Although she has a great talent for music, Edwina has an even greater talent for helping others to realize their own musical abilities.
