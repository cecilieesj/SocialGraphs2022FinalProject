'''James "Pops" Lafayette Sr.''' is a upcoming character of the upcoming [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Moon Girl and Devil Dinosaur]]''. He is the grandfather of [[Moon Girl|Lunella Lafayette]], and the owner of the Roll With It, skating rink run along with his family.

== Background ==
=== Official description ===
''Described as Lunella’s cool grandpa and the proud owner of Roll With It. He is a clever tinkerer and meticulous craftsman, constructing custom skates and reviving old pairs until they’re better than new.''
