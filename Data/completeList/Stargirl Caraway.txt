'''Susan "Stargirl" Caraway''' is the titular protagonist of [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[2020]] musical romantic-drama film, ''[[Stargirl (film)|Stargirl]]'' and its [[2022]] [[Hollywood Stargirl|sequel]]. She is a high schooler who befriends and later starts dating [[Leo Borlock]]. She is musically gifted and displays a free spirited attitude on life. Throughout the film, she tries to help Leo be more open and expressive while also having to deal with some of the consequences about her actions.

==Trivia==
*In the original novel, Stargirl's tastes and personality where a little more outrageous, such as cheering for people for throwing litter away, attending funerals uninvited, and favoring simplistic songs, such as "The Bunny Hop".
