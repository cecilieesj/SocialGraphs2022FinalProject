'''Conrad Mundy''' is a recurring character in ''[[Recess]]''. He is one of the troublemakers in the school.

== Personality ==
He is a mischievous character. He also wants to do something so horrible, he ends up on the news. Later he decides to become a better person 

== Appearance ==
He is a skinny red haired kid with a chipped tooth. He wears a sleeveless jacket.
