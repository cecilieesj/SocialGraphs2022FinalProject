[[File:Phillis.png|thumb]]
'''Phyllis''' is the caretaker for the part-time heroes. She assigns the missions to the hero team and is the owner of the movie theater. She is always in a sour mood all the time.

In "[[At the End of the Worlds]]", it is revealed that Phyllis is a cosmic being whose duty is to make sure the Multiverse stays in balance, split in two, with her other half being Phil. Her representing good and Phil representing evil.

She is voiced by [[Sam Levine]].

==Trivia==
*Phyllis was voiced by co-creator Sam Levine.
