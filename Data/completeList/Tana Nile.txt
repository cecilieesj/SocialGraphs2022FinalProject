|quote = }}'''Tana Nile''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', voiced by [[Grey DeLisle]], and in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'', voiced by [[Jessica DiCicco]]. She is the princess of Rigel-3. She has superhuman strength, invulnerability, and the ability to control minds. She wears a special suit of armor that lets her disguise as a human, and she wields a stasis gun that can fire energy beams. She is an ally of the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]], helping them fight the forces of evil.

Tana Nile was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].
