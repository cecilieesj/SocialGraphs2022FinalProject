[[File:Dr._Gooberman.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Dr. Kevin Gooberman''' is a doctor from the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is voiced by [[Kevin Michael Richardson]].

==History==
Dr. Gooberman is a surgeon at the [[Middleton Medical Center]] in the town of [[Middleton]]. He works with [[Ann Possible]].
