'''Taron Malicos''' is a character in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' universe. He is first introduced in the video game, ''[[Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order]]''. He is a Jedi Master and one of the few Jedi who survived the Great Jedi Purge.

==Background==
===Powers and Abilities===
====Powers====
*'''[[The Force]]:''' Taron has a very strong connection to the Force.
**'''Telekinesis:''' Taron can telekinetically move objects or other individuals without physically touching them, and he utilized it for offensive or defensive purposes.

====Abilities====
*'''Master Lightsaber Duelist:''' Taron is a highly skilled lightsaber duelist.
**'''Jar'Kai:''' Taron is highly skilled in Jar'Kai.

===Weapons and Equipment===
====Lightsaber====
Taron's valued weapon and possession is his red-bladed lightsaber.
*'''[[Lightsaber|First Lightsaber]]:''' Taron had built a red-bladed lightsaber.
*'''[[Lightsaber|Second Lightsaber]]:''' Taron had built a second red-bladed lightsaber.
