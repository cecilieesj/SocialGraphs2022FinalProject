|quote = "I'm not Wonder Woman you know."}}'''Principal Powers''' is a minor character in the [[2005]] film, ''[[Sky High]]''. She is a former superheroine, and the current principal of [[Sky High School]].

==Background==

===Personality===
Powers seems to be quite calm and collected, not getting too angry when [[Will Stronghold]] and [[Warren Peace]] nearly destroyed the cafeteria.

===Powers and Abilities===
Powers has the ability to transform herself into a comet. By spinning around at incredible speeds, she can turn into a comet and fly to wherever she desires.

==Role in the film==
When Royal pain and her gang trapped everybody in the gym including her,she told Boomer to get all the kids out then got turned into an infant and they did then after she was back to normal she put Gwen and her minions in the detetion room to lock them in to prevent them from escaping since it keeps them from using their powers while she went off to call the police to take them to prison.

==Trivia==
*Lynda Carter's quote "What a waste. I can't do anything more to help you. I'm not Wonder Woman, you know." was actually a joking reference due to the fact that she played the role of [[wikipedia:Wonder Woman (TV series)|Wonder Woman]] on television during the 1970s.
