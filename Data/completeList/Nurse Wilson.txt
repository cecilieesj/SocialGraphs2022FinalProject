'''Nurse Wilson''' is a major antagonist appearing in the [[1985]] live-action film, ''[[Return to Oz]]''. She is a nurse who wanted to erase [[Dorothy Gale|Dorothy]]'s "imaginations" of [[Land of Oz|Oz]] upon her patient's arrival. She is the real-world counterpart of [[Mombi]], a wicked witch from Oz.

==Appearances==
===[[Return to Oz|''Return to Oz'']]===
After the hospital was burned down after getting struck by lightning along with [[Dr. Worley]] is dead and Nurse Wilson was arrested by the polices for abusing the patients.
