'''Alan Twitty''' is the best friend of [[Louis Stevens]] in ''[[Even Stevens]]''. He is very sporty and was a great pitcher of Lawrence Junior High's baseball team in "[[Head Games]]". He is also very talented at playing the guitar, and made a band called The Alan Twitty Project, but soon combined it together with The Louis Stevens Experience. He is portrayed by [[A.J. Trauth]].

In one episode of Even Stevens, He was attracted by a lot of girls.
