'''Moosel '''is one of the main characters of The Disney 1985 show ''[[The Wuzzles]]''.

==General Description==
===Physical Appearance===
A half-moose, half-seal creature from ''[[The Wuzzles]]'', '''Moosel''' is a small aquatic Wuzzle with the body and flippers of a seal and the head and antlers of a moose. He is blue with a lavender tummy, and his antlers and flippers are pink.
===Home===
Moosel lives in a lighthouse off the coast of the Isle of Wuz and pilots his own chugboat.
===Personality===
He has an affable spirit, and a very vivid imagination, which makes him believe in monsters. He and [[Rhinokey]] are best friends. He was voiced by [[Bill Scott]].
