'''Leah Stein-Torres''' is a supporting character in the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''. She is the mother of [[Libby Stein-Torres|Libby]], the best friend to [[Molly McGee]].

==Background==

===Personality===
Leah is very supportive and caring towards her daughter. As a matter of fact, she is not afraid to show her love and devotion towards her and is very reliable. She however shows a very rambunctious side and has a strong interest in slam poetry; revealing that she is very eccentric. 

===Physical appearance===
Leah is a tall woman with a brown complexion and olive brown hair that appears messy. She usually wears a white shirt, a brown jacket, an olive green scarf, denim jeans and grey slip on shoes. Occasionally, she will wear a beige dress with a pink coat, white shoes and a gold necklace.

==Trivia==
*Leah's first name was revealed via a tweet.<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20220423035007/https://twitter.com/TGAMMwriters/status/1517628132164915200</ref>
*It is not known what became of Leah's spouse, or if she even had one to begin with. Based on her name, it is possible that she married and either divorced or became a widow. Another possibility is that her name was inherited and that she had Libby on her own either intentionally or unintentionally.
