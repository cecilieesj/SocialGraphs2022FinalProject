'''Burt Quackarach''' is a singer who appeared in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[The Uncrashable Hindentanic]]", where he served as the crooner on board the [[Hindentanic]].

He could also be seen among the guests at Scrooge's wedding in "[[Till Nephews Do Us Part]]".

He was voiced by [[Terry McGovern]].

==Trivia==
*He is based on American singer Burt Bacharach.
*Despite his duck-related name, he is, in fact, a lizard.
**This trait is also shared with [[Duckworth]], who is a dog with a duck-related name.
*Burt bears a resemblance to [[Bill the Lizard]] from ''[[Alice in Wonderland (1951 film)|Alice in Wonderland]]''.
