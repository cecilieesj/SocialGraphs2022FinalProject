'''Boreas''' is a minor character in the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" segment of the [[1940]] film ''[[Fantasia]]''. He also makes a cameo appearance with recycled animation from the live-action/animated film ''[[So Dear to My Heart]]''.

==Role in the film==
Boreas is a two headed-cloud deity sent by [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]]. His task is to get rid of [[Bacchus (Fantasia)|Bacchus]], [[Jacchus]], and his honorers by blowing them away. After Zeus falls asleep, he is no longer needed and therefore isn't seen for the rest of the segment.
