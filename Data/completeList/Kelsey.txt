[[File:Kelsey.webp|thumb]]
'''Kelsey''' is a minor character in ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''. She is a member of the volleyball team, very over dramatic, and she is shown to be one of [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]]'s friends. Some other friends are Teddy and [[Vonnie]]. She doesn't like Mr. Hammerstone, and she is very hyper too. She is portrayed by [[Coco Jones]].
