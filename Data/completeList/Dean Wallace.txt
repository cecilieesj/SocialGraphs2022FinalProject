'''Dean Wallace''' is a minor character and antagonist in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He is portrayed by Drew Wicks.

==History==
Dean and his wife, June, were at the picnic where Bennett and Ellen arrived for theirs. Bennett claims that he tries to copy his life, such as going on the same picnics and marrying a similar woman. They were once accused of stealing their meat but they didn't know that Stan followed them and took it. Bennett and Ellen found out when they Stan eating it, along with his puppies, meaning they accused the Wallaces for nothing.

==Trivia==
*Bennett claims that he tries to copy his life.
