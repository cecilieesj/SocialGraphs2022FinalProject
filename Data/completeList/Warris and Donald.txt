}}'''Warris and Donald''' are toon men in ''[[Bonkers]]'' who appear as the main antagonists of "[[Color Me Piquel]]." Warr is voiced by [[Jess Harnell]] and Donald was voiced by [[Corey Burton]].
==Role in the episode==
Warris and Donald are two out-of-work comedians whose colors have all but faded. They seek to steal color from other Toons and transfer it to themselves.
