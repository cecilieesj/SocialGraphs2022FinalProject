'''Ursula''' is a character from the anime film ''[[Kiki's Delivery Service]]''. She is voiced by [[Janeane Garofalo]] in Disney's English dub.

She is a young woman that is a good artist who adores painting, particularly many magpied crows, and lives in the middle of the forest in a log cabin. Kiki meets her while attempting to retrieve a stuffed cat she dropped while flying overhead on her broomstick.{{CharacterStub}}
