[[Jasmine|You]] put my old score to shame! But I'm just so proud!"}}'''Scara''' is a character in the ''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]]'' TV series. She is [[Queen Hippsodeth|Queen Hippsodeth's]] "right-hand woman" and one of the Galifem warriors, a goofy comic relief character. In the end, she reforms and falls in love with [[King Pector]] in the episode "[[From Hippsodeth, with Love]]".

==From Hippsodeth, With Love==
