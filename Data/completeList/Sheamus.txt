'''Sheamus''' is a minor character who appeared in ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]''. He only appeared in the episode, [[Minnie's Rainbow]].

Sheamus was used by [[Pete|Leprechaun Pete]] to stop the [[The Sensational Six|Sensational Six]] from following the rainbow by dispensing a fog machine as his "Mystery Leprechaun Tool". Fortunately, [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] called [[Toodles]] who dispensed a giant fan to blow the fog away so they could keep going.

==Looks==
Sheamus is a flat floating green disk that is shaped like a four-leaf clover and has a green screen with four leaf clovers that come in various shades of green.

==Trivia==

*Unlike [[Toodles]], [[Quoodles]], [[Boodles]], and [[Goofles]], but like [[Woodles]], Sheamus doesn't have a face or voice.
*Unlike Toodles, Quoodles, Woodles, and Boodles, but like Goofles, Sheamus doesn't have its own Mousekedoer.
*Unlike Toodles, Quoodles, Woodles, Boodles, and Goofles, he can only hold up to one Mouseketool, which will always be the Mystery Mouseketool.
*Sheamus's Mouseketools are referred to as "Leprechaun Tools".
