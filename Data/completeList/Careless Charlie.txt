'''Careless Charlie''' is the main character [[Disney]] used to present health issues in some of the CIAA shorts.

At the start of each short, Careless Charlie- a poor Latin husband and father- does everything wrong that is causing health problems. By the end of each short, Careless Charlie comes to understand what needs to be done and becomes Careful Charlie.{{DEFAULTSORT:Charlie, Careless}}
