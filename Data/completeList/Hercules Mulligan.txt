'''Hercules Mulligan''' is a musical dramatized version of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hercules_Mulligan the real life figure of the same name.] He is a supporting character in the [[Disney+]] film, ''[[Hamilton]]'' taken from the staged theater production. He is usually portrayed by the same actor who plays James Madison.
