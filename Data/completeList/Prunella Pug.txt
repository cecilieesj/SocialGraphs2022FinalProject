'''Prunella Pug''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. She is one of [[Clarissa]]'s friends.

==Appearance==
She is a tan dog with a black collar with a pink flower.

==Personality==
She is snobby and loyal to Clarissa.
