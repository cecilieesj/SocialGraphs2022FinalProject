==Biography==
===Early Life===
At some point in Terri Porter's life she had her first child Jamie Porter. Then a few years later at some point in 2004, she had her daughter [[Gina Porter]].

===Season One===
====Homecoming====
The night after homecoming, she sees Gina in the car with Ricky and she flashes the lights on to let her know to come inside.

====Thanksgiving====
After Gina calls her to ask is she can spend the night at Ashlyn's with the girls, Terri reveals that they are being relocated to Washington D.C. in a few days. She comforts Gina as she packs and begins to cry because of the fact that she has to move again just as she was finally making friends.

====The Tech Rehearsal====
In The Tech Rehearsal, Terri and Gina move to Washington D.C.

===Season Two===
====New Year's Eve====
Terri gives Gina her blessing to stay in Salt Lake City with her friends.

====Valentine’s Day====
Terri finds a way to send Gina chocolates, even though their Valentine's Day tradition is giving her a stuffed bear.

====The Quinceañero====
Terri Porter's daughter Gina Porter calls her during Carlos birthday party and tells her that she is ready to come home.

====Yes, And...====
After hearing that Gina want to go back with her, Terri tells her to wait and think about it before she makes any big decisions.

TBA

====Showtime====
Terri Porter texted her daughter Gina Porter telling her that she can't make it to Salt Lake City to watch her daughter Gina Porter performed in ''Beauty and the Beast'' she texts Gina Porter that she sent another surprise instead. Terri sends her son Jamie to go watch his sister Gina Porter performed in ''Beauty and the Beast.''

===Season 3===
====No Drama====
Terri Porter tells her daughter, Gina, that she is moving back to Salt Lake City to be with her.

==Trivia==

* She lived in Salt Lake City, Utah from The Auditions to Thanksgiving. Then she moved to Washington D.C. in The Tech Rehearsal. She is moving back to Salt Lake City at the end of Season 3.
* She moves around a lot due to her job.
* According to Jamie Porter she thinks E.J. Caswell is "charming" and "walks on air."
* She still keeps in touch with her both her children Jamie Porter and Gina Porter despite living in different cities.
