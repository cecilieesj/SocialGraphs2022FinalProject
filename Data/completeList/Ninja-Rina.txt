She was a masked ballerina with mad ninja skills.

== Appearance ==
Ninja-Rina usually wears a black suit, a black mask, a pink tutu, and some black shoes.

== Personality ==
Ninja-Rina was capable of strength and speed. She was really good at jujitsu, weaving baskets, and ceramics.

== Relationships ==

=== Friends ===

==== [[Gum Girl (Book Character)|Gum Girl]] ====
Gum Girl and Ninja-Rina are close friends. Although Ninja-Rina wants her mother to win more trophies, as seen in ''Popped Star'', in which she messes up with Gum Girl by weaving her into a basket, shaping her into a vase, and even distorts her with her trophy. Gum Girl and Ninja-Rina later become friends. Later, she helps Gum Girl save everyone, even Rico, Dr. Gomez, Mrs. Gomez, Natalie, and the boy, from the Ferris wheel. She also saves her mom from pulling the key from the stand.
