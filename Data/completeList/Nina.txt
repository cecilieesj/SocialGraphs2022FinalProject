|image = Nina (Imagination Movers).jpg}}'''Nina''' is one of the main characters from the Playhouse Disney (now Disney Junior) series ''[[Imagination Movers]]''. Nina is a fun-loving 15-year-old neighbor of the Imagination Movers and likes helping them solve their problems. She has the opposite personality from her uncle, Knit Knots.
==Physical appearance==
Nina is slender with long black hair and fair skin. She also wears different pink and red outfits with red shoes. She usually wears a pink and red polka-dotted swimsuit and straw hat in a beach sequence and red winter accessories with a pink coat in a winter sequence.

==Trivia==
*She may be similar to [[Demi Lovato]].
*Her other family relatives are occasionally never shown in the series.
*She may have a crush on Smitty.
