'''Microphone Mike''' is an anthropomorphic [[toon]] microphone who serves as the announcer at the ''[[House of Mouse (nightclub)|House of Mouse]]'', mainly introducing [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] at the beginning of every show, introducing some of the onstage acts, as well as announcing the show's sponsors at the end of most shows. He is voiced by [[Rod Roddy]], best known as the announcer of the popular game show ''The Price is Right''.
