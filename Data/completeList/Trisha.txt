'''Trisha'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/trisha.htm</ref> is a secondary character from [[Disney Channel]]'s animated series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.

==History==
She is a member of the varsity cheerleading squad at Millard Fillmore Middle School. She is currently dating Joshua the goth guy from the school's Valentine's Day "Love Cruise" field trip event.
