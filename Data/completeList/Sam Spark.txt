[[File:Sam_Spark.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Sam Spark'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/samspark.htm</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0389363/</ref> is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[Family Business (American Dragon: Jake Long)|Family Business]]".
