'''James Lafayette Jr.''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that will appear in the upcoming [[Disney Channel]] series, ''[[Moon Girl and Devil Dinosaur]]''. He is the father of [[Moon Girl|Lunella Lafayette]].

James Lafayette Jr. was created by Amy Reeder Hadley, Brandon Montclare and Natacha Bustos.
