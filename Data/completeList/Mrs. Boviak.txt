[[File:Mrs._Boviak.png|thumb]]'''Mrs. Boviak''' is the mother of [[Brittany Boviak]].

==Background==
At some point, she met and married Mr. Boviak and the two had one daughter: Brittany Boviak. Since she was their only child they spoiled her rotten, causing the snobby attitude she has today. She and her husband also enjoy attending any fancy-do that is available.
