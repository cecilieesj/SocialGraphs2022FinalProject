'''Pitts''' is a character and antagonist from the TV show ''[[Bonkers]]''.

==History==
He is a toon dog, and the former co-star of the TV show "Pitts and Smarts". After his show and career are cancelled, he threatens a bank with his signature bomb in exchange for his job. After multiple chances gone awry, [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]] blows him up at an airport with his own weapon. He was voiced by the late [[Hamilton Camp]].
