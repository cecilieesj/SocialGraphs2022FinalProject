==Appearance==
Gozan is an obese dark brown mandrill with a cream brown chest and Hungo is a skinny mandrill with a long tail.

==History==
[[Tarzan (character)|Tarzan]], [[Terkina|Terk]], and [[Tantor]] first encountered Zutho and his friends as kids at the forbidden side of the river. Tarzan taught the mandrills how to make spears, but then a fire broke out and nearly destroyed the entire jungle.
