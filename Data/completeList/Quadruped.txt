'''Quadruped''' is a minor character in the film, ''[[The Brave Little Toaster]]''. He is a dog owned by [[Elmo St. Peters]].

==Role in the film==
Right after Elmo loads [[Toaster]], [[Blanky]], [[Lampy]], [[Radio]], and [[Kirby]] in his monster truck, he climbs in the driver seat, pets Quadruped on the head and says "Good boy Quadruped."

When all the appliances escape Elmo's store, Quadruped escapes, too, by driving his master's monster truck away.
