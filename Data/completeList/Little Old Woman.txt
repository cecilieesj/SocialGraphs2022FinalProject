'''Little Old Woman''' is a character from the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Goldie & Bear]]''. She lives in [[The Giant (Goldie and Bear)|the Giant's]] shoe, she has so many children. Her belief system is first come, first serve, hand me downs so hand them down. She is tough to negotiate with and spite being tough she is kind to all.
