'''Marquis de Lafayette''' is a musical dramatized version of the real life figure of the same name. He is a supporting character in the [[Disney+]] film, ''[[Hamilton|Hamilton,]]'' taken from the staged theater production. He is usually portrayed by the same actor who plays [[Thomas Jefferson]].
