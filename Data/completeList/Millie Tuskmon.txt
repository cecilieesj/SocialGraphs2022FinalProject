'''Millie Tuskmon''' is the mother of [[Tylor Tuskmon]] in the [[Disney Television Animation|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated series, ''[[Monsters at Work]]''.

== Physical Appearance ==
Millie has a slender figure which is wide at her hips along with arms that are similarly delicate looking. She has blue green skin with slight dark turquoise patterns on her skin. She also has three hazel eyes and blue pod-like legs with a teal fin for hair. Her accessary is her gold headband which is the only thing she wears.
