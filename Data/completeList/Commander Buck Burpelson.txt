==Background==
===Physical Description===
Commander Burpelson is a muscular man with tan skin, thick brown eyebrows, dark purple-brown bags on his eyes and wears a dark beige suit-jacket with medals, cream white long sleeved shirt underneath with a mahogany red tie, a black belt from underneath his suit-jacket, dark gray pants and dark cool-gray shoes. Also, he wears a dark blue-gray hat with a golden A-letter shaped medal and and the end of the episode, he even wears red and white striped underwear and a tattoo with an arrow and a red heart.

===Personality===
Describing Burpelson's personality as a military officer, he is deceiving, stern, mean-spirited, rough, tough, nasty, double-crossing, rotten to the core (described to by Mrs. Roborson), and villainous.
