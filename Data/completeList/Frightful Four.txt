[[File:Frightful_Four_USM.png|thumb|250px]]The '''Frightful Four''' is a team of supervillains. They are hired by anyone who has money like [[Doctor Octopus]] and [[Green Goblin|Norman Osborn]]. They are a strong team when they are working together.

The Frightful Four were created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].
