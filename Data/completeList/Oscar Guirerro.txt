'''Oscar Guirerro''' is and antagonist and one of the three guest characters from the animated series ''[[Fillmore!]]''. He is voiced by Nico Matinata.

==Personality==
He appeared in [[Next Stop, Armageddon|same episode]] where his dad works a model train convention when the train crashed and demolished and was captured by [[Cornelius Fillmore]] and [[Ingred Third|Ingrid Third]] and was brought to justice and served a detention.
