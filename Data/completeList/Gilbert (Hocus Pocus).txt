'''Gilbert''' is a supporting character in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s  [[2022]] film ''[[Hocus Pocus 2]]''. He is the owner of the magic store who inadvertently reawakens the [[Sanderson Sisters]].

==Background==
On Halloween night in 1993, Gilbert saw the [[Sanderson Sisters|Sanderson sisters]].
