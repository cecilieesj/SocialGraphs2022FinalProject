'''Caryn "Ma" Pines''' is Stan Pine's mother in ''[[Gravity Falls]]''. She only appeared in "[[A Tale of Two Stans]]".

== History ==
In the 1960s, Mrs. Pines lived in Glass Shard Beach, New Jersey, where she worked as a phone psychic, a vocation greatly aided by her pathological need to lie.

=== Season 2 ===
[[Stan Pines|Stan]]'s mother makes several appearances in his flashbacks throughout the episode. She is seen working as a phone psychic and meeting with the Pines twins' principal about [[Ford Pines|Ford]]'s future, during which she expresses concern for their "free spirit", Stanley. She also asks what is happening when [[Filbrick Pines|Filbrick]] kicks Stanley out of the house.
