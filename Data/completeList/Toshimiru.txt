'''Toshimiru '''is a minor character in the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''.

==History==
He was the original master of Monkey Kung Fu, and had Mystical Monkey Power.

Toshimiru is the founder and creator of the secret ninja school, [[Yamanouchi Ninja School]], which very few even know exists. He used The [[Lotus Blade]] in order to carve the school. A statue of him is later placed at the school.
