}}'''Mother Goose''' is a large goose that lives in [[Fairy Tale Forest (Goldie & Bear)|Fairy Tale Forest]]. She helps out the kids with activities like who will ride the Cow when it is jumping over the moon. On odd occasions, she likes to hang out with her kin. One of her favorite pastimes is spending an afternoon swimming with the geese and resting on the shore of the river.
