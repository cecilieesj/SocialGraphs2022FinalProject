'''Mindy''' is the best friend of [[Charlene Sinclair]] on ''[[Dinosaurs]]''. She was voiced by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Jessica_Lundy Jessica Lundy], her body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Star_Townshend Star Townshend] and her face was operated by [[Julianne Buescher]].

She was one of the few specifically female full-body supporting [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Unisaurs Unisaur] puppets also used for [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Caroline_Foxworth Caroline Foxworth] and [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Wendy_Richfield Wendy Richfield].

She was mentioned in [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/License_to_Parent?action=edit&redlink=1 License to Parent] and [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Driving_Miss_Ethyl Driving Miss Ethyl].

The puppet used for Mindy in '''Slave to Fashion''' is different from the one used in '''Charlene and Her Amazing Humans''' and '''Scent of a Reptile''' while both puppets have the same name.
