[[File:Knights_of_Destruction.jpg|thumb|250px|The Knights of Destruction]]The '''Knights of Destruction''' are a band of [[Nerissa Crossnic|Nerissa]]'s minions.

In the comics, they are created from Nerissa's despair, pain and hatred (Shagon and Khor are actually a boy and his dog who wondered on Mount Thanos). Also in the comics, they are also called the '''Four Knights of Revenge'''.

In the television series, Shagon and Khor were [[Matt Olsen|Matt]]'s and Mr. Huggles' dark sides.

The Knights are:
*[[Shagon, the Hate]]
*[[Khor, the Destroyer]]
*[[Tridart, the Despair]]
*[[Ember, the Pain]]

[[File:Nerissa's_Knights_of_Destruction.jpg|thumb|250px|Nerissa and the Knights of Destruction.]]In the [[W.I.T.C.H. (TV series)|animated show]], the Knights of Destruction under Nerissa's command, consists of, Matt as Shagon, Mr. Huggles as Khor, [[Tridart, the Despair|Tridart]] and [[Ember, the Pain|Ember]] are made of lava and ice. Shagon loves making [[Will]] hate him more everyday. Just as Shagon's target is Will, Tridart targets [[Hay Lin]], Ember targets [[Irma Lair|Irma]], and Khor targets [[Taranee Cook|Taranee]].
