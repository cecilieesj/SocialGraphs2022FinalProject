[[File:Ruby_the_Darkslayer.jpg|thumb]]'''Ruby the Darkslayer''' is [[Chuck Ryans|Chuck]]'s friend and a part of his LARPing team in the series ''[[Motorcity]]''. She is loyal to Chuck, her 'King', but has great respect for [[Mike Chilton|Mike]] after seeing him in action.

==History==

Ruby the Darkslayer and the other the LARPers came to the [[Burners]] for help when two of her LARPer friends, Sam and Philip, went missing.
