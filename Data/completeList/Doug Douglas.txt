'''Doug Douglas''' is the father of [[Danny Douglas]] and husband of [[Megan Douglas]] in [[Future-Worm!]]. He works as a doctor.
==Appearance==
Doug has brown hair, wears glasses like his son, and commonly wears a long-sleeved blue shirt, grey pants, and black shoes.
