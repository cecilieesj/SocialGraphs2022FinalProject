Argus Blinks is a minor supporting character from ''[[Monsters at Work]]''. He is a M.E.R.C. investigator.
==Background==

===Personality===
In the series, he is shown to be very committed to his job, never have taken a day off in 20 years. After he got bump by scream Canister he is happy mood and got a day off having on the beach for the first time.

=== Appearance===
He is a pale cyclops-like monster with thin tentacles. He also has a bushy eyebrow and mustache, and wears a pair of one-lens glasses.
