'''Havel Capek''' is a main character in the ''[[The Mighty Ducks: Game Changers]]'' since the episode "[[Spirit of the Ducks]]". A member of the Don't Bothers, alongside his brother [[Tibor Capek|Tibor]]. He and his brother are both from the [[Czechia|Czech Republic]] and don't know much English.
