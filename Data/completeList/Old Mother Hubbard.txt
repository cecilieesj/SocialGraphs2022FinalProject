'''Old Mother Hubbard''' is a character who appeared in Disney's [[Silly Symphony]] short ''[[Old King Cole]]''.

==Appearances==
===''[[Silly Symphonies]]''===
In ''[[Old King Cole]]'', Old Mother Hubbard was one of the citizens of Storyland who attended a jazz party thrown by Old King Cole with her dog. She and her dog come out from their book and then go to the party. Later on, Old Mother Hubbard starts dancing with Old King Cole. After the party she returns to the book with her dog.
