|fate = Lives life with Alex|actress = }}'''Mason Greyback''' is a character and [[Alex Russo]]'s love interest from ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]''. He is a purebred werewolf.

==Background==
Mason was a transfer student from [[England]], who fell in love with Alex. They date, and she later finds out he is a werewolf.

Wanting to help her brother, [[Justin Russo|Justin]], find his girlfriend, he sniffs her dental floss. It brings them all the way to Transylvania, and his past is revealed when [[Juliet Van Heusen|Juliet]] announces they used to date. He blurts out that he still loves her, and Alex transports home with her heart broken.
