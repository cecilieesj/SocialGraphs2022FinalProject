==History==
Sarkus is the evil high priest who reigns over the Desert City [[Garbabble]]. He became jealous over [[Donald Duck]] who will possess him with the ancient spirit of a king named [[The Garbled One]] and awakening a mummy named [[Ka-Hoo-Fu]] from his casket with a spell, commanding him to destroy him. He is later arrested for his treason.
