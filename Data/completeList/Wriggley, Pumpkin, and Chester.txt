==Background==
===Wriggley===
Wriggley is the pet weasel of [[Grimtrix|Grimtrix the Good]] who knows [[Wormwood]] from their days in Hexley Hall. She likes to mock him for having Cedric for a master.

===Pumpkin===
Pumpkin is the pet cat of a sorcerer. He likes to play with balls of yarn. He knows Wormwood from their days in Hexley Hall.

=== Chester ===
Chester the bulldog is the pet of another sorcerer. He is the only familiar of the trio to have an accent. Sofia used him to transform into a bulldog.
