'''Stinky Ned''' is a [[Toon]] NPC brown Duck who owns [[King Neptoon's Cannery]] on Lighthouse Lane in Donald's Dock.

==SOS Toon==
Stinky Ned is also an SOS Toon. He is a 3-star Lure SOS. He uses Hypno Goggles to lure cogs into "sleep" mode.

This SOS's Lure will last 3 Rounds.
