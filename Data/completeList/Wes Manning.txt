[[File:Dog-with-a-blog-monstober-oct-3-2013-4-150x150.jpg|thumb]]'''Wes Manning''' is a recurring character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He's portrayed by [[Peyton Meyer]]. He previously attended Tyler and Avery's school. He and Avery broke up when he and his family moved away.

==Biography==
He is a new guy that attends at [[Tyler James|Tyler]]'s and [[Avery Jennings|Avery]]'s school that she has a crushed on him.

==Personality==
Wes is an intelligent boy who is friends with Avery Jennings. He also has a crush on her.

==Physical Description==
He has a brown short hair and tan skin and very tall inches than Avery and is pretty tall for his age.

==Relationships==
===[[Avery Jennings]]===
Avery and Wes are mutual crushes who met in the episode Too Short. Avery immediately developed a crush on him. In Howloween, the two connect over their shared love of Halloween. In Lost In Stanslation, Avery attempts to tutor Wes in Spanish, with the help of Tyler and Nikki. In the end, he asks Avery if it would be inappropriate if he asked her on a date, and she unknowingly replies that it is. In Avery B. Jealous, Wes asks Lindsay out on a date, making Avery extremely jealous. In Avery-body Dance Now, they go to the school dance together, Wes gets jealous that Avery is spending so much time with Karl and they reveal their feelings to each other and they become a couple. In Love, Loss and a Beanbag Toss, they break up when Wes and his family move away.

===[[Lindsay]]===
In Avery B. Jealous, Wes asks Lindsay out on a date, to which she agrees. This almost nearly strains Lindsay's friendship with Avery but the two eventually make up.
