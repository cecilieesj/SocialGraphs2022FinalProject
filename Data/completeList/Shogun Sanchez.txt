'''Shogun Sanchez''' is the older step brother of Papercut Peterson who only appears in "[[Drop Kick]]". After he beat Papercut, he took his wrestling academy from him banned younger brothers from studying there. Then [[Kick Buttowski (character)|Kick]], [[Gunther Magnuson|Gunther]], and Papercut have a fight with him and he gets [[Brad Buttowski|Brad]] to team up with him. Eventually, Kick, Gunther, and Papercut win and Papercut gets his academy back. {{Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil}}
