'''Korg''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]'' and in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. He is a member of the Kronan, a race of alien beings whose body consist of a rock-like substance, which gives him superhuman strength and invulnerability. Since [[Hulk]] and [[Thor Odinson|Thor]] freed him from his enslavement when on the planet Sakaar, Korg is a valuable ally and a friend of Hulk and Thor, and he helps them fight tyranny and injustice.

Korg was created by Greg Pak and Carlo Pagulayan.
