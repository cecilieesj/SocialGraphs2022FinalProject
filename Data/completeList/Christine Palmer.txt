'''Christine Palmer''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Doctor Strange (film)|Doctor Strange]]'' and ''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]''. SheÂ is a surgeon that holds romantic feelings towards her colleague [[Doctor Strange|Stephen Strange]], but ended her relationship with him after his mental breakdown and losses. However, she becomes close to Stephen again when he returns from his training and apologises to her for his arrogance.

Christine Palmer was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Al Hartley.

==Appearances==

===Marvel Cinematic Universe===

====''[[Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness]]''====
Because she survived the Snap five years ago, she married a man named Charlie. She invited Stephen Strange to their wedding in New York City.
