The Thief is a minor antagonist in the cartoon ''[[Timon and Pumbaa]]''. He is a raccoon.

==Appearance ==
He is the stereotypical raccoon appearance. He wears a black shirt with a green stripe. He also has a brownish red hat. 

==Personality ==
As his name implies he is a thief. He is dishonest and sneaky.
