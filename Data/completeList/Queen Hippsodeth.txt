==Appearances==
===''[[Aladdin (TV series)]]''===
In "[[A Sultan Worth His Salt]], Queen Hippsodeth and her Galifem warriors first appeared in the episode "A Sultan Worth His Salt" as the main antagonist of the episode sneaking into [[The Sultan's Palace|the palace]], besting [[Razoul]], [[Royal Guards|the royal guards]], and [[Rajah]], and escaping with [[Jasmine]] because they want her to join them and won't accept no for an answer. When [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]] and the others try to rescue her, [[The Sultan]] insists on going along.

Queen Hippsodeth and her warriors return in the episode "[[From Hippsodeth, with Love]]" now in love with the Sultan for defeating her.
