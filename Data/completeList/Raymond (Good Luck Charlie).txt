[[File:Raymond_finally.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Raymond'''<span style="line-height:21px;"> is [[Ivy Wentz]]'s crush in "Let's Potty." He later becomes Ivy's boyfriend. He is in government class with Ivy and [[Teddy Duncan|Teddy]].</span>

==<span style="line-height:21px;">Relationships</span>==
===[[Ivy Wentz]]===
<p style="line-height:21px;">Ivy has a crush on Raymond. At the Duncan house, they hug and become a couple. Ivy calls Raymond Ray-Ray, and Raymond calls her I-I. Raymond seems to like Ivy back.</p>
===[[Teddy Duncan]]===
<p style="line-height:21px;">Teddy plays matchmaker to hook Ivy and Raymond up together. ("Let's Potty").</p>
===[[Spencer Walsh]]===
<p style="line-height:21px;">Raymond and Spencer are good friends. ("The Singin' Dancin' Duncans").</p>
