[[File:Char 33894.jpg|thumb|210px|right|Principal Cutler as seen in the first season of the Replacements]] '''Principal Cutler''' is a character on ''[[The Replacements]]''. He's the eskimo principal of George Stapler Middle School, where [[Todd Daring]] and [[Riley Daring]] go to school. He loves money and the cold. He's voiced by [[Jeff Bennett]].
