|partner = [[Petra Hammerhold]] (girlfriend)}}'''Plasma Boy''' is [[Petra Hammerhold|Petra Hammerhold's]] boyfriend and currently training to become a Space Ranger.
==Physical Appearance==
He has a red slim body and has a yellow spot on his chest which is his heart. He also has orange hair, a pointy chin, and yellow eyes. 
==Personality==
He's a laid-back teenager who has strong feelings for Petra. He also can get easily irritable especially when being called a Plasma Monster.

==Abilities==
'''Plasma Manipulation:''' He can generate and manipulate plasma in the form of electricity.

'''Size Manipulation:''' He can control his own size and mass.
