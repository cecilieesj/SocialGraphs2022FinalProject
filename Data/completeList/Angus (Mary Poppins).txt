|quote ="Come along - join us in a bit of kick and prance."}}'''Angus''' is a minor character and [[Jack (Mary Poppins)|Jack]]'s fellow lamplighter who first engages in the "leerie speak" with him in the film ''[[Mary Poppins Returns]]''.

==Background==
Tarik Frimpong makes his feature film debut as Angus in the film. As an Australian actor, singer, and dancer based in [[London]], he was born in Melbourne. Tarik trained from a young age at his mother's performing arts school "Dance Explosion".

==Role in the film==
Angus is first seen during the musical number "[[Trip a Little Light Fantastic]]" where he dances alongside a whole chorus line of lamplighters led by Jack. Furthermore, he plays an important role toward the end of the film when the Banks family, along with the help from [[Mary Poppins (character)|Mary]], tries to stop [[William Weatherall Wilkins]] just as he helps Jack stop [[Big Ben]] from striking midnight to which the plan was successful, foiling Wilkins' plan. He later appears at the end of the film playing a ring toss game and later having fun with a balloon alongside everyone else during the musical number "[[Nowhere to Go But Up]]".

==Trivia==
*Frimpong previously played the role of young [[Simba]] in the Australian production of ''[[The Lion King (musical)|The Lion King]]''
