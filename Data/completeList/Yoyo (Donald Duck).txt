}}'''Yoyo''' is a character that was introduced and made an appearance in a few issues of ''The Force of Three'' series within the [[Donald Duck in comics|Donald Duck line of comics]] from Disney and the [[Egmont Group]] in [[2014]] and [[2015]], and is based off of [[Yoda]], his counterpart from within the [[Star Wars]] universe. His arch-nemesis is Duck Vader, and he is also an ally to [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]].
