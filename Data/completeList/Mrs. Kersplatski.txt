|quote = "I'm okay!"}}'''Mrs. Kersplatski''' is a major character from the children's show ''[[JoJo's Circus]]''. She is [[JoJo Tickle|JoJo]]'s teacher and the ringmaster of the Big Top. A warm and encouraging teacher, Mrs. Kersplatski gives the circus kids a strong sense of mastery and confidence, with her combination of hard work and play. Often she has a tendency to be rather clumsy and often says, "I'm okay!" after tripping up.

In the special "[[A Circus Town Wedding]]", she gets married to Mr. Muscles.
