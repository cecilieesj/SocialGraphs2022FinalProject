'''Everett Ross''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appeared in ''[[Captain America: Civil War]]'', ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'', and ''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]'' played by [[Martin Freeman]]. He is the Deputy Task Force commander, working with [[Thaddeus Ross]], to bring in [[Bucky Barnes]] who was suspected for bombing the United Nations meeting. Later, when [[Baron Zemo]] was revealed to be the one for all the incidents that occurred Zemo was given over to Ross into custody by the [[Black Panther]].

Everett Ross was created by Christopher Priest, Anibal Rodriguez and Kenny Martinez.

==Appearances==
===''[[Captain America: Civil War]]''===
Working with Thaddeus Ross, he attempted to control and regulate the actions of the Avengers; he came into conflict with Captain America when he captured the framed Winter Soldier. In the end, upon learning that Helmut Zemo was responsible for the recent terrorism, he imprisoned Zemo in his headquarters.

===''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]''===

Everett is called in when [[Ulysses Klaue]] is captured by [[Black Panther|T'challa]]'s people. However, he suffers a spinal injury when [[Erik Killmonger]] breaks into the holding facility and frees Klaw. Knowing Ross can't survive without [[Wakanda]]'s medical technology, T'challa has him brought with and has [[Shuri]] repair his spine with [[Vibranium]]. Grateful for the help, Ross returns the favor by helping Black Panther stop Killmonger's coup and keep the secret of how advanced Wakanda is; becoming T'challa's ally in the outside world.

===''[[Black Panther: Wakanda Forever]]''===
Everett Ross appears in the film.

==Trivia==
*Despite sharing the same surname, he is not a relative of Thaddeus Ross.
*Everett Ross is played by [[Martin Freeman]] known for playing Bilbo Baggins from "The Hobbit" Trilogy.
