'''Jack Case''' is a minor character who appeared in the ''[[TaleSpin]]'' episode "[[A Spy in the Ointment]]".

== Biography ==
He is a a semi-villainous rabbit and wannabe spy with a French accent who likes to be sly on all topics, though he isn't overly the main antagonist of the said episode.

== Appearance ==
He has creamy fur with a pink nose, white molar teeth, and has bushy black-grey eyebrows due to age. He occasionally wears a white undershirt with a red tie, a blue sweater-vest and a blackish hat and jacket.

== Personality ==
He is manipulative and cryptic, but not truly evil.

== Trivia ==
* He is the second notable rabbit character in ''TaleSpin'', the first being [[King Amok]].
