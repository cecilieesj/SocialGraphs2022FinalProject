{{Infobox_character|image = Steel_Toe.jpg|fullname = Steel Toe|appearance = Muscular}}'''Steel Toe''' is one of the two Pro-wrestlers, which appear in the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He appeared in the episode "[[Pain King vs. Cleopatra]]" He is tall, strong and well-built on the top half of his body.
==History==
Steel Toe visited the [[Middleton Mall]], with the Global Wrestling Association. They were holding a wrestling event there and were in the middle of the match when the wrestling promoter, Jackie Oakes, bitter at not being able to be taken seriously as a wrestler because of his short stature, used a magic talisman to turn himself into a monster,

Team Possible were able to defeat the monster, and with Pain King, he beat up Jackie.
==Trivia==
*[[Ron Stoppable]] and [[Rufus (Kim Possible)|Rufus]] are huge fans of Steel Toe.
*According to [[Jim & Tim Possible]], [[Pain King]] never defeats Steel Toe.
*He is voiced by late pro wrestler Andrew (Test) Martin.
