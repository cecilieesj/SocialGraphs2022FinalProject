}}'''Mr. Block''' is a commander for the Bureau of Time Travel (also known as '''B.o.T.T.''' or '''B.O.T.T''') on ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''.
==Background==
Mr. Block is the rude commander of [[Balthazar Cavendish|Cavendish]], [[Vinnie Dakota|Dakota]], Brick, and Savannah that works for the Bureau of Time Travel. His job is to call the Time Travelers and inform them on what mission they are being given.

==Personality==

He is stern and is fond of belittering his employees, no matter the position.

==Physical appearance==

Block has a square face, eye bags, bright blue eyes, curly black hair.

He wears a black futuristic suit with red outlines with matching boots. He also wears grey pants.
