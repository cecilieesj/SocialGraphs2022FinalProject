[[File:Linds.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Linds''' is a character of [[Disney Channel]]'s [[Cloud 9]].
==History==
She is a Friend to [[Kayla Morgan]] and [[Pia]], she is very silent, and text communicate with them.
==Physical Appearance==
Linds has caramel coloured skin and straight brown hair, with brown dark eyes.
