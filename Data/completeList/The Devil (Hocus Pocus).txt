'''"The Devil"''' (also referred to by the [[Sanderson Sisters]] as '''"The Master"''') is a minor character in ''[[Hocus Pocus]]''. He is a man wearing a devil costume whom the [[Sanderson Sisters|Sanderson sisters]] initially assume to be their "master", the real Satan.
 
==Appearances==
===''[[Hocus Pocus]]''===
"The Devil" is first seen giving candy to children and is overheard by the [[Sanderson Sisters]], who are close by and assume it’s their "master" the real devil. Flattered by this, he wholeheartedly welcomes them into his home. There, he introduces them to his [[The Devil's Wife|wife]], who they believe is Medusa. He later dances with [[Sarah Sanderson]], but is caught by his wife and the sister are chased out of the house by his dog, Ralph.

===''[[Hocus Pocus 2]]''===
"The Devil" and his wife make a cameo in this film on a TV. There are also some citizens of Salem dressed up as them.

==Trivia==
* [[Garry Marshall]] and Penny Marshall, the actors who portrayed "The Devil" and his wife were siblings in real life.
