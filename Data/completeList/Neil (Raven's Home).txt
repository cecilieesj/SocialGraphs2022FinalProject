'''Neil''' is a main character on the [[Disney Channel]] show ''[[Raven's Home]].'' He is [[Victor Baxter|Victor]]'s neighbor and Booker's classmate at [[Bayside High School]]. He is portrayed by Felix Avitia.

==Personality==
Neil is seen as a caring, comforting friend, who is sometimes seen as dim-witted. In "[[The Big Sammich]]", [[Booker Baxter-Carter|Booker]] states he would tip Neil in two-pounds of salami and Neil took it literal, though Booker was joking.

== Trivia ==
*It's revealed that since the events of "[[The Wrong Victor]]", '''Neil''' has been fired from the hospital and now works at the Chill Grill.
*He is the backup quarterback on the football team, according to [[Ivy Chen|Ivy]] in "[[Clique Bait]]".
