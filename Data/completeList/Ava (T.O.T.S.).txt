==Background==
===Personality===
Ava is eccentric and is always ready to deliver. She loves being super fast and showing off her new flying moves. She is also helpful when Freddy or Pip have questions about the job or babies.

===Physical Appearance===
Ava is a short necked stork. She has purple and white hair. She wears her T.O.T.S. uniform, which is a blue shirt with white buttons. She also has a light blue cap.
