'''Judge Austin Gorg''' is a minor character from the [[2003]] live-action film, ''[[Holes]]''. He is portrayed by Michael Cavanaugh.
==History==
Austin is the Judge that sentenced [[Stanley Yelnats IV]] to [[Camp Green Lake]], a juvenile detention camp instead of going to prison.
