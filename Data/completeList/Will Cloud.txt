'''Will Cloud''' is a character and the deuteragonist of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Cloud 9]]''. He is portrayed by [[Luke Benward]].
==History==
Will is very laid back and an ordinary kid who used to have a passion for snowboarding until a big incident. Will gets back on his feet thanks to [[Kayla Morgan]].
==Physical Appearance==
Will has light brown hair with hazel eyes and tan skin. Will is mostly seen wearing a coat and a t-shirt with jeans.
