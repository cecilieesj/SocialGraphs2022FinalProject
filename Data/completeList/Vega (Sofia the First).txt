==Role in the Series==
After [[Sofia the First (character)|Princess Sofia]] successfully stopped [[Prisma]] from harming the Isles, both Vega and [[Orion (Sofia the First)|Orion]] invited her to receive training as a Protector of the [[EverRealm]], which she accepted. She and Orion later encourage Chrysta to be Sofia's Protector trainer in order to teach them both.
