|shows = ''[[Future-Worm!]]''}}The '''Triplets''' are characters in ''[[Future-Worm!]]''. They appear in "[[The Very Hungry Killa-Pillah]]".

==Appearance==
The trio have pale skin, red hair, grey pants and black shoes.

The girl has her hair tied in a pair of pigtails with pink scrunchies and her shirt sleeves are pink.

The boy has disheveled hair and his shirt's sleeves are red.

The youngest looks like a shorter version of the boy.
