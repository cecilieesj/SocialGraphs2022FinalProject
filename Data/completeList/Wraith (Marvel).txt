|quote = }}'''Wraith''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appeared in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'', voiced by [[Jeff Bennett]].

He is a [[Kree]] who was genetically modified by his father. He has superhuman strength, superhuman speed, a healing factor, the ability to control shadows, and a weapon that can morph into any weapon in the universe. He is loyal to the Kree, but he is not fond of [[Ronan the Accuser|Ronan]]. [[Grandmaster|The Grandmaster]] kidnaps him and pits him in a fight against [[Gamora]].
