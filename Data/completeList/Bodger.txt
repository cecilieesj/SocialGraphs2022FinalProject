== Background ==
Bodger is a wise, old bull terrier. He is very loyal and obedient to his owner named Peter, a teenage boy who loves him very dearly. Given his age, he sees the way of a dog as his own law. His pet comrades are Luath, a young, immature Labrador retriever and Tao, a Siamese cat.
