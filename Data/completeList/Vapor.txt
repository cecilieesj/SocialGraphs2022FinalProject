}}'''Vapor''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and supervillainess who appeared in ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]'', voiced by [[Colleen O'Shaughnessey]], and in ''[[Avengers Assemble]]'', voiced by [[Catherine Taber]].

Vapor was created by Bill Mantlo and Sal Buscema.
==History==
She is an [[HYDRA]] scientist who got exposed to gamma radiation while on a trip to space. The gamma radiation mutated her, turning her into a being of pure gas. She uses her newfound powers to fight [[Avengers|the Avengers]] as a member of the [[U-Foes]]. She is brave, headstrong, and confident.
