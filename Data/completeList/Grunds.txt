'''Grunds''' are a race tiny blue aliens from [[Marvel Comics]], who appear in the [[Disney+]] original animated series ''[[I Am Groot]]''.

The Grunds were created by Steve Gerber and Jim Starlin.

==Appearances==
===''[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]''===
====''[[I Am Groot]]''====
In the [[The Little Guy (I Am Groot)|second episode]], [[Groot]] accidentally uncovers their civilization. At first intimidated by the seemingly enormous tree toddler and start attacking him, but the Grunds soon discover he’s the hero they’ve been waiting for.

==Trivia==
*In the comics, the Grunds are yellow human-like creatures, who can use their antennae atop heads to absorb energy for concussive bolts.
