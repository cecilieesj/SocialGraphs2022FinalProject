|headquarters = [[St. Canard]]}}The '''Justice Ducks''' are a group of five heroes in the television series ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''. The group was formed during the rise of the [[Fearsome Five]].

The group is comprised of [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing Duck]], [[Morgana Macawber]], [[Stegmutt]], [[Fenton Crackshell|Gizmoduck]], and [[Neptunia]].

==Trivia==
*The Justice Ducks were inspired by the Justice League.
*There was originally a plan for there to be a series about the Justice Ducks, with the team being comprised of Darkwing Duck, Gizmoduck, [[Rubber Chicken]], and two new characters called "Muck-Draker" and "The Duckhunter".
