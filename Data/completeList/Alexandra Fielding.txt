'''Alexandra Fielding''' (biological name: '''Artemis''') is a character in ''[[Twitches]]'' and ''[[Twitches Too]]''. She is played by [[Tia Mowry]].

=='''Powers & Abilities'''==
*'''Spell Casting''': Alex can cast spells; a "spell" being the word used to signify the means employed to carry out a magical action. This allows users to magically alter reality to a wide number of effects or to evoke forces to change their surroundings. A spell could consist of ritual action, a spoken component, a verse, a gesture or any combination of these.
**'''Potion Brewing''': A spell may require the use of certain tools, ingredients (herbs, stones, and minerals) or the use of additional power such as from celestial events.
*'''Intangibility''': Alex is able to move through solid objects and ignore most physical effects in her way, exact means how this is done when she can walk through walls.

==Story==
She was born in Coventry with her sister, Camryn Barnes.
