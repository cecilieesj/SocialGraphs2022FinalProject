{{NeedImage}}'''Igor Drenkov''' was Bruce Banner's old lab assistant and a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Avengers Assemble]]'', voiced by [[André Sogliuzzo]]. He leads his group called the SteelCorps in a fight against [[Avengers|the Avengers]] at [[Stark Industries]] and uses a gamma energy-stealing weapon on [[Hulk]]. After depowering Bruce, Drenkov plans to use the stolen gamma energies to become the hero he wanted to be.

Igor Drenkov was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].
