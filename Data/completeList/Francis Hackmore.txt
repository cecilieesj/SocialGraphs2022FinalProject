==Role in the film==
Francis is a local superstitious hermit. She finds [[Ernest P. Worrell|Ernest]] and three children building the tree house after [[Mike Murdock|Mike]] and [[Matt Murdock]] bullied [[Kenny Binder]] by ruining his cardboard haunted house. Ernest tries to talk to her and she reads the legendary story about [[Trantor]]. Also at that moment, she finds out he is the direct descendant of [[Phineas Worrell]] who put a curse on Trantor two hundred years earlier. After Ernest defeats Trantor, she reunites with her family, especially her sister, who was one of Trantor's original four victims.
