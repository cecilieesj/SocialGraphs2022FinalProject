Santa Claus is the helper of [[T.O.T.S.]].
==Appearance==

Santa Claus is a helper of [[T.O.T.S.]] . He has a red hat with a white tip, white beard, a red fat belly, a red jacket, black boots, a black belt with a gold tip, a magic red sled and a reindeer team.
