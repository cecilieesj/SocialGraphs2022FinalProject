'''Karolina Dean '''is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in the Hulu series ''[[Runaways (TV series)|Runaways]]'', played by [[Virginia Gardner]]. She is a member of an alien race called the Majesdanians who was raised on Earth. When she finds out that her parents came to Earth in order to conquer it, she joins the team of superhero fugitives known as [[the Runaways]]. Karolina has the ability to shoot beams of energy, and the ability to fly. She is generous, optimistic, gentle, and kind.
