'''Spinlok''' is a character who appeared in the Tomorrowland attraction [[ExtraTERRORestrial Alien Encounter]]. He is the sidekick of [[Dr. Femus]].

== Appearances ==

===ExtraTERRORestrial Alien Encounter===
Spinlok appears during the main show alongside [[Dr. Femus]]. He appears on screens from the alien planet which holds X-S headquarters, interfering with the teleportation process.
