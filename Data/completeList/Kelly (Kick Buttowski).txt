[[File:Kelly.png|thumb]]'''Kelly''' (voiced by [[Emmanuelle Chriqui]]) is [[Brad Buttowski|Brad]]'s girlfriend in the ''[[Kick Buttowski]]'' episode "[[Love Stinks!]]", but she doesn't really like him. She just uses him for a cheerleading initiation. But Kick destroys her plan and as a result, she and Brad break up when he sees her true colors.
