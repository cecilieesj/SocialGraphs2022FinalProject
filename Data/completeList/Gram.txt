== Background ==
Gram first appears in [[The Family Reunion]]. Gram is the mother of [[Dorothy]] and the grandmother of [[Sylvia]] and [[Bill, Phil & Gil]]. She is very distrustful of [[Wander]] thinking he's after her pearls which she heavily guards. At the end of the episode she approves of Sylvia saving the galaxy as she is shown with tears of joy in her eyes.
