==History==
Hank Carter is [[Trixie Carter|Trixie]]'s father. It was mentioned that he was deployed to an Air Force base in Greenland<ref>[[Hairy Christmas]]</ref>. Not much is known about him except that despite his busy work, he would always be there for his family on Christmas Day to do their usual traditions, which include drinking eggnog.
