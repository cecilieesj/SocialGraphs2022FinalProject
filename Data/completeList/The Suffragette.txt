[[File:Sufferin.png|thumb]]'''The Suffragette''' (voiced by (Essra Mohawk) is a woman in the [[Schoolhouse Rock!]] song: [[Sufferin' Till Suffrage]].
<br />

== Appearance ==
The Suffragette has long red hair with curly bangs that is styled into a low ponytail. She wears a long-sleeved blue top with a star design that reveals her midriff (no innie drawing), red bellbottoms and white platform boots.{{Schoolhouse Rock!}}
