}}'''Druella O'Midas''', also known as the '''Queen of the Banshees''', is the primary antagonist of the ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers|Rescue Rangers]]'' episode, "[[The Last Leprechaun]]".

Druella is a banshee, a malevolent spirit, who lived in [[Ireland]]. She enslaved leprechauns and forced them to mine gold.

==Trivia==
*Druella's last name is a reference to the legend of [[King Midas]], who had the power to turn things into gold with a single touch.
*The name Druella rhymes with [[Cruella De Vil|Cruella]].
*Her hair is similar to [[Lady Tremaine]]'s.
