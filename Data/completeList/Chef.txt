|alignment = Good/Neutral}}The '''Chef''' at [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Le_Predateur Le Predateur] appears in the fourth season ''[[Dinosaurs]]'' episode "[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Episode_410*:_Working_Girl Working Girl]". His face was operated by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/David_Greenaway David Greenaway], his body was puppeteered by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Pons_Maar Pons Maar] and his voice was provided by [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Thom_Sharp Thom Sharp].

This culinary worker is taken aback by [[Charlene Sinclair|Charlene's]] application for the job of busboy, as the term implies specific gender expectations. He remains convinced that the whole thing is an elaborate prank planned by Al, the maitre d'.
