==History ==
He is the father of [[Steve Barkin|Steven Barkin]]. He is the mayor of the living history museum of [[Port Mystic Cove Haven]]. He is speaks in an older English accent. He has a pet parrot named Remy. He also temporally replaced Mr. Littleton as the town blacksmith. He also acts as the town constable. He is a sailor and captain the ship that chased Captain Blackeye Brown's Ship.
