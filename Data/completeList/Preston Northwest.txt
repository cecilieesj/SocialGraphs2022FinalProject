==Background==
He is [[Priscilla Northwest]]'s husband, [[Pacifica Northwest]]'s father, and patriarch of the Northwest family. He takes pride in the fact that he is related to the supposed town founder, Nathaniel Northwest. A rich and noble resident of [[Gravity Falls (location)|Gravity Falls, Oregon]], he believes he is above everyone else in the town and passes these ideas down to his daughter.
