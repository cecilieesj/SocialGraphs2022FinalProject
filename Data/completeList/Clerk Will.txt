'''Clerk Will''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Rabbit Rabbit] and a clerk in [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Donald%27s_Dock Donald's Dock's] [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Goofy%27s_Gag_Shop Goofy's Gag Shop].

==SOS Toon==
ï»¿Clerk Will is also an [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/SOS_Toon SOS Toon] toons have to rescue in the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/VP VP] battle. He is a 3-star [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Trap_Gags Trap] SOS, and when called upon summons [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Trapdoor Trapdoors] directly in front of all unlured [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Cogs cogs]. His Trapdoors can do a damage of 50 for each trapped cog.

As with all Trap Gags, this will always activate before [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Lure_Gags Lure Gags].

==Trivia==
*All Trap SOS Toons are Clerks ([[Clerk Penny]], [[Clerk Clara]], [[Clerk Ray]], and himself)
*Trap SOS do not trap cogs that are already Trapped or will be trapped (for that round).
*Trap SOS Toons are the only offensive SOS Toons that do not use Level 6 Gags. These Toons use Trapdoors, which is a Level 5 Gag.
