{{Infobox_character|image = 2017-01-05_22-59-15.png|alignment = Neutral|films=''[[The Game Plan]]''|actress=[[Kyra Sedgwick]]}}'''Stella Peck''' is the child hating agent of [[Joe Kingman]] in ''[[The Game Plan]]''. She views [[Peyton Kelly]] as a distraction for Joe.

==Role in the film==
She confronts Joe about Peyton and attempted to hire a babysitter for her. But she ends up firing the sitter. By the end of the film, she falls in love and has the meal that gives gas.

==Trivia ==
*She seems to be the main antagonist of the film.
