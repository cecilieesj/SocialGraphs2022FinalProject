==Background==
Ellie is a rebellious 16-year-old girl who has best friends named Karl and Monica. Then Adam becomes her boyfriend, she and her mother, [[Katherine Blake|Katherine]] don't get along, and her brother, [[Fletcher Blake|Fletcher]] keeps playing with her stuff like her hourglass, with which he does magic tricks.
