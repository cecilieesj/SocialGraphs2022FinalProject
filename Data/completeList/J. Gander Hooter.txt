'''Director J. Gander Hooter''' is the director of [[S.H.U.S.H.]]. He is depicted as very well spoken and smart. He regards [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing]] very highly and often calls him in on cases, much to the protest of his top agent [[Vladimir Gryzlikoff]].

==Trivia==
* J. Gander Hooter is named after former [[wikipedia:FBI|FBI]] director [[wikipedia:J._Edgar_Hoover|J. Edgar Hoover]].
* He first appeared in "[[Dirty Money]]".
* His personality seems to be based on [[w:c:jamesbond:Q|Q]] from the [[wikipedia:James_Bond|James Bond]] series.
