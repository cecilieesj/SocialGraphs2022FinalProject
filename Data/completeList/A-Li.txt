'''A-Li''' is an alien from the ''[[Zombies]]'' franchise. She is one of the main characters in the [[Disney+]] and [[Disney Channel]] original movie ''[[Zombies 3]]''. She is portrayed by Kyra Tantao.
==Background==
===Personality===
Also super-intelligent and telepathic, opinionated A-Li has a more intense outlook on life that her fellow extraterrestrials.<ref>[https://lumiere-a.akamaihd.net/v1/documents/zombies_3_character_descriptions_93f5f82c.pdf Zombies 3 Character Descriptions]</ref>
===Physical Appearance===
A-Li is an alien with short blue hair and bangs, brown eyes and light skin. A-Li wears a blue and silver jumpsuit, blue dress, Luma Lens bracelet and silver boots. Like all Aliens, A-li has silver markings on the side of her face.
