
[[File:Screenshot_2020-01-27_Bouncy.png|thumb]]Bouncy is a male bunny from the [[Disney Junior]] series, ''[[T.O.T.S.]]''
==Appearance==
Bouncy is a lavender bunny with yellow eyes, purple hair that sways back and a white puffy tail. His collar is yellow with the T.O.T.S. symbol. He wears a white diaper with yellow lining and a pattern of mountains.
==Personality==
Bouncy is a bunny that loves to bounce more than others.
