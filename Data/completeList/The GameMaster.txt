'''The GameMaster''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. She is an alien who designs holo-games. She is known to be a creative tech genius.
==Trivia==
*The GameMaster has a pet robot Kangaroo known as Grace, named after computer scientist [[Wikipedia:Grace Hopper|Grace Hopper]].
