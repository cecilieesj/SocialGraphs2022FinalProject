'''The Klutter Tutters''' were a group of performing mice in [[Tutter]]'s family. A trio, they played beautiful clarinet music. [[Klutter Tutter]] would work the "silver things" (buttons) on the clarinet with Skitter Tutter and [[Nutter Tutter]] would blow to produce the music.
