'''Dr. Gomez''' is one of the main characters in ''The Gumazing Gum Girl!''. He is a dentist and the father to Gum Girl.

== Appearances ==
He usually wears a white shirt, black pants, and black shirt.

== Personality ==
He work in the job as a dentist in his appointment, Gomez Dental.
