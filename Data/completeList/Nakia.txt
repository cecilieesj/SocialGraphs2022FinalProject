'''Nakia''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who first appears in the film, ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]''. She serves [[Wakanda]] as a spy around the world, and is also an ally and girlfriend of [[Black Panther|T'Challa]]. She is played by [[Lupita Nyong'o]].

Nakia was created by Don McGregor, Gil Kane, and Klaus Janson.

==Trivia==
*In Marvel comics, Nakia is a super-villain enemy of Black Panther known as Malice.
*In the comics, she was a former [[Dora Milaje]]. It is unknown if such is the case in the MCU.
