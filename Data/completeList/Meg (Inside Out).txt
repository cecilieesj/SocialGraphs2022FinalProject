'''Meg''' is a minor character in the [[2015]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]â€¢[[Pixar]] animated feature film, ''[[Inside Out]]''. She is [[Riley Andersen]]'s former old best friend from [[Minnesota]].

==Role in the film==
When Riley and her parents move to [[San Francisco]], they part their ways in a tearful goodbye. A few days later, they start video chatting and Meg reveals she has found a brand new friend she made who she continuously talks endlessly about, unknowingly angering Riley. Riley becomes fed up and hangs up on her, ending her friendship with her. Meg is not seen again afterwards.
