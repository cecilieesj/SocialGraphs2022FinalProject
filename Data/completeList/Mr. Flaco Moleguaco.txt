'''Mr. Flaco Moleguaco''' (voiced by [[Curtis Armstrong]]) is a teacher in Kuzco Academy. He teaches [[Kuzco]], [[Malina]], and [[Kronk]]'s class. His main goal in the series is to applicate tests to help Kuzco become humble and respectful to others. He is very short and seems to be middle-aged.

==Trivia==
*His first name, Flaco, is Spanish for thin or skinny, while his surname, Moleguaco, is the word Guacamole flipped around and with the second 'a' replaced with an 'o'.
**Similarly, his cousin, Mr. Nataempa, is the word Empanada flipped around, but with the 't' replacing the 'd'.
