The '''Human Cannonball''' is a tech based criminal. He wears a dome shaped suit with rockets on the bottom and lasers as a weapon. He will tackle the heroes from the air. As the name goes, he is the human cannonball for the [[Circus of Crime]]. He and his other members where caught by S.H.I.E.L.D. when Hawkeye betrayed them and joined there captors. He returned to take vengeance against [[Hawkeye]] and his new partner [[Sam Wilson|Falcon]].

After his deafeat him and all organisation are arrested by the SHIELD.

The Human Cannonball was created by [[Stan Lee]] and Steve Ditko.
