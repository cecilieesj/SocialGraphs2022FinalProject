|status = Active}}'''Jelly Otter''' is the middle child of the three Otter siblings, [[Peanut Otter|Peanut]] and [[Baby Butter Otter|Baby Butter]]. She is 6 years old and is immensely kind. Her best friend is [[Pinch Raccoon]]. Jelly is an idea person and loves to help out her friends. She is usually the only one that suggests doing a "[[Noodle Dance]]" and is often the one to come up with ideas.

==Personality==
Jelly is somewhat athletic and enjoys a variety of sports. She can also sometimes be girly, but usually leaves the really girly stuff, such as pretty lace or ribbons, to Pinch. Jelly's normal tendencies are to be kind and caring, For some reasons, though she can let her achievements go to her head, such as in "[[The Singin' Kid]]".

== Trivia ==
* Jelly is the only child who does not have a belly button.

==Disney's Hollywood Studios==
*The puppet versions of Peanut and Jelly appeared on the Playhouse Disney car during the [[Disney Stars and Motor Cars Parade]].
