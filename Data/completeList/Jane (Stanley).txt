'''Jane '''(last name unknown) is a minor character on the Playhouse Disney (later Disney Junior) series ''[[Stanley (TV series)|Stanley]]''.
She is in [[Stanley Griff|Stanley's]] class at school. In "Guess What's Coming for Dinner", she invites Stanley to her house for dinner, and the latter tries Chinese food.

According to "A Happy Chinese New Year", Jane is of Chinese-American descent. In that same episode, she is very excited about visiting her grandfather in China, but disappointed when her mother has to call off the trip due to extra work. Stanley and his friends want to cheer her up.
