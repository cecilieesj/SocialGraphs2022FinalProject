'''Wade Ripple''' is one of the two main protagonists from the upcoming [[2023]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]] animated feature film, ''[[Elemental]]''. He's described as a go-with-the-flow guy.

==Physical appearance==
Wade's body is composed of translucent blue water. He is seen with various types of clothes, such as a blue and white t-shirt with a water drop, a light purple and blue short-sleeved shirt and a white striped yellow wave shirt with a mauve necktie.

==Trivia==
*A drink labeled "Wade Water", showing Wade as its mascot, was seen in ''[[Lightyear]]'' as a cameo appearance.
