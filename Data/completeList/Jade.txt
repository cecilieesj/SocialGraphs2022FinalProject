|status = Active}}'''Jade''' (pronounced 'Ha-day') is a [[Gargoyle (creature)|gargoyle]] member of the [[Mayan Clan]]. He appears in the [[Disney Television Animation|Disney animated]] TV series, ''[[Gargoyles]]''. His name is derived from the Mayan pendant he wears.


==Role in the series==
In [[1996]], Cyberbiotics began to encroach on the Mayan Clan's territory, bringing Jade into direct conflict with them. [[Preston Vogel]] hired [[Jackal]] and [[Hyena (Gargoyles)|Hyena]] to remove the gargoyles by any means necessary. When the [[Avalon]] Travelers arrived, [[Zafiro]] enlisted their help in defending their territory.

After the Mayan Clan and their new allies had defeated Jackal and Hyena, [[Elisa Maza|Elisa]] took Jade and [[Turquesa]] to Avalon to transplant some rare rainforest plants.
