[[File:Char_33538.jpg|thumb]]'''Prince Borg''' (voiced by [[Rob Paulsen]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Mighty-Ducks-The-Animated-Series/Borg/</ref> in the Disney television series ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]''. He is the Prince regent of the dimensional Anaheim. The Ducks helped him take back his throne and fought against [[Asteroth]]. He soon returns to warn the Ducks of Asteroth's search for his amulet, and he helps [[Nosedive Flashblade|Nosedive]] and [[Duke L'Orange|Duke]] find the Star Sword, a weapon capable of destroying Asteroth's amulet.
