'''Yardarm''' is a one-shot character who only appeared in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode "[[Pearl of Wisdom]]".

Yardarm appears to be based on [[Pete]]'s sidekick [[Scuttle (Mickey Mouse)|Scuttle]] from [[Paul Murry]]'s [[Mickey Mouse]] comics.
