|quote = "It's a fashion no-no!"}}'''Dress Up Daisy''' is a character from the [[Disney Junior]] series, ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. She is a fashionable doll (similar to a [[Barbie]] doll) who loves to dress up in different clothes (hence her name) that once belonged to a babysitter named Katherine, who babysat for [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]] and [[Donny McStuffins|Donny]], but Doc learned that Katherine never had time to play with her doll, as she gave Dress Up Daisy to Doc.
