}}The unnamed '''Lion''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]''.

== Personality ==
The lion is wild, rough, tough, savage and mean.

===''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]''===
He first appears in [[Daisy Visits Minnie]] where he escapes from the zoo and tries to attack Minnie and Daisy in [[Minnie's House]] but ends up being washed away back to the zoo.
