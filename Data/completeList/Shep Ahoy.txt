'''Shep Ahoy''' is a [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toon Toon] NPC and owner of [http://toontown.wikia.com/index.php?title=All_for_Nautical&action=edit&redlink=1 All for Nautical] on [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Seaweed_Street Seaweed Street], [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Donald%27s_Dock Donald's Dock]. Shep Ahoy will give Toons a series of [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Toontask Toontasks] for the [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Bossbot_Cog_Suits Bossbot Cog Suit Parts]. When all 16 sets of Tasks have been completed, Toons may earn the last Cog Suit Part from [http://toontown.wikia.com/wiki/Flippy Flippy].

==Toontasks==
Shep Ahoy's Toontask features a series of Toontasks for each Bossbot Cog Suit Parts.
