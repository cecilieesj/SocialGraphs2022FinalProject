[[File:Whizzer_Marvel.jpg|thumb]]'''Whizzer''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] characterÂ that was featured in ''[[Jessica Jones (TV series)|Jessica Jones]]'', played by Jay Klaitz. He was injected with the same chemical that gave [[Jessica Jones]] her powers, giving him superhuman speed, which he uses to protect the innocent and fight the forces of evil. When Jessica arrives in order to seek answers regarding her origin, Whizzer is startled and runs into the path of falling debris, which kills him.

In the ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'' animated series, he was voiced by Robert Patrick.
