|quote= "The stars have vanished!"}}'''Master Erwin''' is an astronomer who appears in the ''[[Sofia the First]]'' episode "[[Clover Time]]".

==Background==
Erwin is the royal astronomist of [[Enchancia]] and he sometimes helps [[Princess Amber|Amber]], [[Prince James|James]] and [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]] with their astronomy homework. He's also the one who sparked Amber's love for astronomy.
