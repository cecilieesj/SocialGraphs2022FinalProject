|alignment = Neutral/Bad}}'''All Hands and Mr. Starfish''' are characters from the animated series ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]''. They only appear in the episode "[[A Creep in the Deep]]".

==Appearance==
All Hands is a pink octopus who wears a sailor hat and a red bandana on his neck.

Mr. Starfish is a yellow/orange starfish who wears a red hat, an eyepatch, and a white T-shirt with blue stripes.

==Roles==
All Hands and Mr. Starfish serve as secondary antagonists in the episode of the series. They are members of [[Captain Finn]]'s crew. All Hands appears to be idiotic and he usually discharges all kinds of land job while Mr. Starfish is Captain Finn's starfish quartermaster.
