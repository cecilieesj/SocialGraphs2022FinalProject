'''Esteban Julio Ricardo Montoya de la Rosa Ramírez''' is the current assistant night manager and former bellhop at the Boston Tipton. He speaks in a Spanish accent, so not many people can understand him. He has a pet chicken named Dudley whom he considers as his best friend. He gives Zack and Cody Martin, the nickname of "Little Blond Peoples".
