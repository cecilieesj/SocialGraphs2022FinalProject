|status=Active}}'''Squeaky & Squirmy Smee''' are the twin sons of [[Mr. Smee]], in the [[Disney Channel]] film, ''[[Descendants 3]]''. They, along with [[Dizzy Tremaine|Dizzy]] and [[Celia Facilier|Celia]], are the chosen four to leave the [[Isle of the Lost]] and attend [[Auradon Prep]].

==Biography==

===Early Life===
Squeaky and Squirmy Smee were born of the [[Isle of the Lost]] on July 29th. 

===''[[Descendants 3]]''===
Squeaky and Squirmy get picked to go to [[Auradon Prep]] to have a better life.

==Background==

===Personality===
Squeaky and Squirmy are both very shy.

===Physical Appearance===
Squeaky and Squirmy both have long platinum blonde hair and wear glasses.
