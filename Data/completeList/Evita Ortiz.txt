[[File:Evita.png|thumb|250px]]'''Evita Ortiz''' is [[Nikki Ortiz]]'s Pomeranian in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. Stan finds her annoying because she yips a lot. Stan acts as a wingman to Tyler by hanging out with Evita while Tyler tries to woo Nikki.

==History==
Evita is a Pomeranian that belongs to Nikki. When Nikki first brought her to introduce to Stan, he instantly finds her annoying because of her consent yipping.

Tyler wanted Stan to his wingman to woo Nikki and he reluctantly agrees. At the park, Stan thought it was bad way for a wingman to eat her and he digs a hole to bury her.
