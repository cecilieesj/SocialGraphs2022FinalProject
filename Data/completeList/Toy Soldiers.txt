The '''Toy Soldiers''' are toys in'' [[Babes in Toyland]]''. 

==Disney Parks==
The Toy Soldiers are seen in parades at many Disney theme parks around the world during the Christmas season.

==Trivia==
The animation used for the toy soldiers marching was used in ''[[Mary Poppins (film)|Mary Poppins]]'' for when [[Michael Banks]]' toy soldiers marched into the toy chest.
