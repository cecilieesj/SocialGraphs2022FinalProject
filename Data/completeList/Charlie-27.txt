'''Charlie-27 '''is a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'', played by [[Ving Rhames]]. He is a member of an alien race who has superhuman strength. He is a skilled pilot and marksman, and an expert in hand to hand combat. He uses his powers and skills to protect the innocent and fight the forces of evil. He is a good friend of [[Yondu Udonta|Yondu]].

==Appearances==
===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]''===
Charlie-27 was an old associate of both [[Stakar Ogord]], a renowned leader among the [[Ravagers]], and [[Yondu Udonta]], a Ravager captain who had once disgraced himself when he betrayed the Ravager code by trafficking children. Upon Yondu's death participating in a self-sacrificing effort to save the galaxy, Ogord summoned a fleet of Ravager ships to pay tribute to their now redeemed comrade, and Charlie-27 was among those who answered the call. He joined with Stakar and several others in paying tribute to Yondu.

==Trivia==
*In the [[w:c:marvel:Earth-691|alternate 31st Century of Earth-691]], Charlie-27 was a genetically engineered soldier from Jupiter, who joined the Guardians of the Galaxy after the Badoon exterminated his people.
