'''Tippy-Toe''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] characters, and the pet of [[Squirrel Girl]]. She first appeared in ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', and appears in the film, ''[[Marvel Rising: Secret Warriors]]''. She was set to make her live-action debut in the canceled TV series ''[[New Warriors (TV series)|New Warriors]]''.
