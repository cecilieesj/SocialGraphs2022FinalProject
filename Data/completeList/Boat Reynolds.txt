'''Boat Reynolds''' is a character in [[Disneytoon Studios]]' [[2014]] animated feature film, ''[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''.

==Background==
===Official Description===
:''Well known for his roles as a good ol' boat who bucks authority, Boat Reynolds is an accomplished actor, director, and voice artist who started out as a star athlete. He was an all-conference boat racer in college when an injury ended his professional racing aspirations. Instead of becoming a police boat like his father, he went to [[New York City|New York]] to pursue acting. Now he is [[United States|America]]'s favorite leading boat!''<ref name="meettheplanes">Disney (2014). ''Meet the Planes''. Disney Press. ISBN 978-1423175674</ref>

===Physical appearance===
Reynolds is a white boat who has brown stripes painted on him, a black shape over his mouth that represents a mustache, brown eyes, and a see-through hat.

==Appearances==
===''[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''===
Boat Reynolds only makes one appearance in the film, where he comes into the Grand Fusel Lodge at the time a party is being held for the grand reopening. [[Cad Spinner]] is pleased to see him, having loved his acting, especially in ''Best Little Boathouse in Texas''.
