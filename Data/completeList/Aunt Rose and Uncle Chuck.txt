'''Aunt Rose and Uncle Chuck''' are characters in the [[Disney XD]] series ''[[Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero]]''. Their nephew, [[Penn Zero]], lives in their house while his [[Brock and Vonnie Zero|parents]] are trapped in the Most Dangerous World Imaginable. It is unclear how they are related to Penn. Aunt Rose owns a pet Chinchilla whom she likes to dress in costumes, but the chinchilla seems to dislike her and Chuck for it.{{CharacterStub}}{{Penn Zero: Part-Time Hero}}
