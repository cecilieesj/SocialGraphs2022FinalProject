'''Parker''' is a minor character from the [[1993]] film, ''[[The Three Musketeers]]''.

==History==
Parker is one of [[Milady de Winter]]'s servants. He helps her with her mission for [[Cardinal Richelieu]]. Along the way, he helps kidnap [[D'Artagnan]]. Later, he is seen trying to fight [[Porthos]] on the ''Persephone'' only to be easily defeated by him.
