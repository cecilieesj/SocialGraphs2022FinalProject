'''Valentino''' is a character in the [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s upcoming [[2023]] animated feature film, ''[[Wish]]''. He is [[Asha|Princess Asha]]'s pet goat who wishes for the ability to communicate.<ref name="Comicbook">{{Cite web|url=https://comicbook.com/movies/news/disneys-wish-animated-musical-wishing-star-announced-2023-ariana-debose/|title=Disney's Wish, Animated Musical About Wishing Star, Announced For 2023 With Ariana DeBose|website=Comicbook|accessdate=September 10, 2022}}</ref>
