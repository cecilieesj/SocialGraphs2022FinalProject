[[File:Dixon.png|thumb|250px]]'''Dixon Herbert Ticonderoga''' is a minor character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]], ''[[ANT Farm]]''. He's portrayed by Ben Winchell.

==Biography==
He is a new student at Z-Tech in Secret agANT. Immediately [[Chyna Parks|Chyna]] and [[Lexi Reed|Lexi]] grew crushes on him and fought his attention. Later this episode [[Olive Doyle|Olive]] has a crush on him. Olive ends up being the one he chooses to be with and they ride off into a helicopter together.
