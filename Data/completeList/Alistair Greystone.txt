'''Alistair Greystone'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0513187/</ref> (voiced by Kiel Holmes)<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Alistair/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3420307/</ref> is an antagonist and two of the six guest characters of the animated series ''[[Fillmore!]]''.

==History==
He was a student and a resident psychic who appeared in [[Cry, the Beloved Mascot]] when he was the possible suspect of kidnapping Lobstee, the Lobster the X Middle School Mascot during the school Spirit Week with News reporters [[Philsky Richards]] and [[Cheri Shotwell]].
