'''Jackal Johnson''' is a well-known record producer and the main antagonist of the film, ''[[The Cheetah Girls]]''.

==Physical Appearance==
Jackal is a white male. He has short spiky blond hair and a medium build. He also wears dark shades.

==Events==
===The Cheetah Girls (Movie)===
When he first watches the Cheetah Girls perform at school, he signs them on to his record label. His ulterior motive is to change the girls into something they're not. His plan ultimately fails when the Cheetah Girls decide that they're better off being their own band.
