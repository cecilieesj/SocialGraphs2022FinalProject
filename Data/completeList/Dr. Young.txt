[[File:DrYoung.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Dr. Young''' is Stan's veterinarian. He is portrayed by Rizwan Manji.

==Character History==
Dr. Young sees himself as a funny doctor, and it seems like he doesn't take his job seriously, although he does in a humorous way. He makes bad jokes and puns that only Ellen finds it funny.

==Trivia==
*He has been a veterinarian for 20 years.
*He doesn't want to be a people doctor.
*According to Stan, he's a laugh-a-minute, which it doesn't mean he's funny, its just he laughs every minute.
