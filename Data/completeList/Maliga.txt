==History==
Maliga was a sea nymph who was banished by her people to the Foglands forever for being cruel, selfish, and terrible but thanks to [[Princess Chloe|Chloe]] she learned what it meant to be a good friend. This breaks the curse and the other sea nymphs let Maliga return home.

==Trivia==
*Maliga's voice actress, [[Sofia Carson]] previously worked on ''[[Descendants]]'', ''[[Descendants 2]]'', ''[[Descendants 3]],'' and ''[[Descendants: Wicked World]]'' as the daughter of [[the Evil Queen]], [[Evie]].
