|status = Active}}'''Morpheus''' is a minor character in the "[[The Pastoral Symphony|Pastoral Symphony]]" segment of the [[1940]] film, ''[[Fantasia]]''.

==Role in the film==
After the inhabitants of [[Mount Olympus]] bid farewell to [[Apollo (Fantasia)|Apollo]] during the end of the segment, Morpheus is seen briefly covering the sky with her cloak to bring in the night and the time for the inhabitants to sleep. Then [[Diana]] uses the crescent moon as a bow to fire an arrow to spread stars across the sky.

==Trivia==
*Despite her gender, Deems Taylor erroneously refers to Morpheus as a male (which is her usual gender).
*Morpheus is the god's Greek name. In Rome, she is called Somnus.
