[[File:TomFairbanksDWaB.jpg|thumb]]'''Tom Fairbanks''' is one-guest character in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He is portrayed by [[Charles Shaughnessy]].

==Character History==
Tom Fairbanks is a wealthy business tycoon. He wanted to buy Stan from the James-Jennings family to use him as a spokes-dog for his animal rescue campaign.

==Trivia==
*He's the second person to be introduced to be known as a businessman. Followed by [[Monty Cathcart]].
