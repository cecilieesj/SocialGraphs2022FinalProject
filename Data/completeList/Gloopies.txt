|enemies = [[Danny Douglas]], [[Future-Worm]], [[Bug]], [[Neil deGrasse Tyson]]}}The '''Gloopies''' are an alien race that appear in ''[[Future-Worm!]]''. They're the reason of the stockout of Captain Cakerz cereal, and they're defeated thanks to [[Danny Douglas|Danny]], [[Future-Worm|Fyootch]], [[Bug]] and Neil deGrasse Tyson.

==Appearance==
The Gloopies look like giant, pale yellow-green masses of gelatin, with sickly green eyes, orange pupils and notable eyebags.
