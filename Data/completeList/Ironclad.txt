|quote = }}'''Ironclad''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character and supervillain who appeared in ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]'', and in ''[[Avengers Assemble]]'', voiced by Eric Ladin.
==History==
He is a [[HYDRA]] scientist who was exposed to gamma radiation during a trip to space. The gamma radiation mutated him, turning his body into iron and giving him superhuman strength and invulnerability. He uses these powers to fight [[Avengers|the Avengers]] as a member of the [[U-Foes]]. Ironclad is gruff, antisocial, and aggressive.
