'''The Mother''' is the main antagonist of the 2022 horror movie ''[[Barbarian]]''. The tragic result of years of repeated rape and incest, the Mother is a twisted creature obsessed with capturing people who move into her house and forcing them to take on the roles of her children.

==Background==

===Personality===

===Physical appearance===
The Mother is a severely deformed woman, warped by the decades of parent-child incest between Frank and his lineage. Her body is frail, her face is malformed, and her hair is long and raggedy.

===Powers and abilities===
She is shown to have superhuman strength, being able to bust down walls and cause physical harm to others with ease.
