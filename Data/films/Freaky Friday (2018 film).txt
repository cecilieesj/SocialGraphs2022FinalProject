'''''Freaky Friday''''' is a [[Disney Channel Original Movies|Disney Channel Original Movie]] which premiered on the¬†[[Disney Channel]] on [[August 10]], [[2018]]. It is a television adaption of the [[Freaky Friday (musical)|''Freaky Friday'' musical]], which premiered in [[Washington, D.C.]], which in turn is based on the¬†[[1976]] [[Freaky Friday (1976 film)|film]] and on the [[Wikipedia:Freaky Friday|novel]]¬†by¬†Mary Rodgers.

==Synopsis==
With the help of a magical hourglass, a woman and her teenage daughter swap their bodies. However, they find themselves in trouble when the hourglass inadvertently breaks.

==Trivia==
*This is the second film adaptation of ''Freaky Friday'' to start with an opening animated sequence after the [[1976]] [[Freaky Friday (1976)|film adaptation]].
*To make the film more age-appropriate for a Disney Channel Original Movie the lyrics from the film's songs were altered to remove inappropriate language, mature situations, and copyrighted material.
**A few songs were cut entirely in order to reduce the length of the film.<!-- Which songs? -->
