'''''Trees''''' is an animated short and a song from the film ''[[Melody Time]]''. It was later re-released as the first of two segments in the 1955 short, ''[[Contrasts in Rhythm]]''.

==Synopsis==
A lyrical setting of the famous poem follows trees through the seasons.
