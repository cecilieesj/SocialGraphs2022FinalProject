'''''The Birthday Party''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[January 7]], [[1931]].

==Summary==
[[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]], [[Horace Horsecollar|Horace]], and [[Clarabelle Cow|Clarabelle]] join hordes of others for a big birthday celebration. Mickey and Minnie jam on "I Can't Give You Anything But Love, Baby," then Horace and Clarabelle perform a dance and some scat-singing to "Darktown Strutters' Ball."
