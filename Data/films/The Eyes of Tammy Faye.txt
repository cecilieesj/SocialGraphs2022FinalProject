'''''The Eyes of Tammy Faye''''' is an American drama film directed by Michael Showalter and based on the documentary of the same name by Fenton Bailey and Randy Barbato. The film stars [[Jessica Chastain]] (who also produces the film), [[Andrew Garfield]], Cherry Jones, and [[Vincent D'Onofrio]]. The film was released on [[September 17]], [[2021]] by [[Searchlight Pictures]].

==Synopsis==
The film features the history of controversial televangelists Tammy Faye Bakker and Jim Bakker.

==Release==
The film was initially scheduled to be released on [[September 24]], [[2021]], by [[Searchlight Pictures]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/01/searchlight-dates-sundance-acquisition-the-night-house-nightmare-alley-more-for-later-this-year-1234677690/|title=Searchlight Dates Sundance Acquisition ‘The Night House’, ‘Nightmare Alley’ & More For Later This Year|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Anthony|last=D'Alessandro|date=January 21, 2021|accessdate=January 21, 2021}}</ref> It was then moved up by a week to be released on [[September 17]].
