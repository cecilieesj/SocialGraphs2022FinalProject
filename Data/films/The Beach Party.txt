'''''The Beach Party''''' is an animated ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short cartoon. It was first released on [[November 5]], [[1931]], and was directed by Burt Gillett.

==Summary==
In this cartoon, Mickey and his friends head to the beach for a day of fun and mishaps. [[Clarabelle Cow]] falls into the water and is rescued by [[Pluto]]. Later, an octopus washes up on the shore and chases everyone away, laughing from the water as they run.
