'''''Goofy and Wilbur''''' is an animated cartoon that was released on [[March 17]], [[1939]]. It was notably Goofy's first solo cartoon.

==Summary==
[[Goofy]] enlists the help of his friend, Wilbur, a grasshopper, in a fishing expedition, but then has second thoughts when he realizes that his friend might actually get eaten by a fish.

==Characters==
*[[Goofy]] (voiced by Danny Webb)<ref name=Canemaker>{{cite book|author=Canemaker, John|year=2006|title=Paper Dreams: The Art And Artists Of Disney Storyboards|publisher=Disney Edition|page=86|isbn=978-0786863075}}</ref>
*Wilbur

==Note==
*Although the original theatrical poster states the cartoon as "A 'Mickey Mouse'", it was originally released as a Goofy cartoon, and the first in his own series.
