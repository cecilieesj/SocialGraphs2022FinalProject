'''''Alice in the Big League''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. This is the final entry in the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' series and Lois Hardwick's final role as Alice.

==Summary==
Alice attempts to umpire a big league baseball game where the animals begin to take exception to her bad calls.
