|release = [[1957]]}}'''''You and the Living Machine''''' is a "You and Your..." short that originally aired on ''[[The Mickey Mouse Club]]''. It was updated in the early 1990s.

==Synopsis==
[[Jiminy Cricket]] shows how the "human machine" converts food into energy. He compares the human digestive system to a factory production line while explaining the importance of healthy food and exercise. He tells the story of a young boy who wants to stay healthy so that he can play and do all the fun things of life.

==Trivia==
*The animation of the beating heart in this short was later featured in the opening of the Disney music video version of [[Huey Lewis]]' "The Heart of Rock 'n' Roll", which was shown on both ''[[DTV]]'' and the primetime special ''Disney's DTV Valentine'' (later renamed ''Disney's DTV Romancin<nowiki>'</nowiki>'').
