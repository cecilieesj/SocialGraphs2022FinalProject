'''''Nature's Half Acre''''' is a [[1951]] American short documentary film directed by [[James Algar]]. It won an Academy Award in [[1952]] for Best Short Subject (Two-Reel). The film was produced by [[Walt Disney]] as part of the [[True-Life Adventures|''True-Life Adventures'']] series of nature documentaries.
