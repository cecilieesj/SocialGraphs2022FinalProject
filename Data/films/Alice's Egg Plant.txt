'''''Alice's Egg Plant''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent. This is the only time that [[Anne Shirley|Dawn O'Day]] played the role of Alice.



==Synopsis==
Julius, the boss of Alice's chicken farm, has to find a way to deliver 5000 eggs to Sinkem and Soakem when the hens go on strike.
