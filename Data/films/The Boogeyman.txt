'''''The Boogeyman''''' is an upcoming American horror film based on the 1973 short story of the {{WPS|The Boogeyman (short story)|same name}} by {{WPS|Stephen King}}. It will be released on [[Hulu]] in the United States and [[Disney+]] Star in other countries in [[2023]].

==Synopsis==
The film follows a 16-year-old and her younger sister, still reeling from the death of their mother, as they’re targeted by a supernatural boogeyman, after their psychologist father has an encounter with a desperate patient in their house.<ref name="BoogeyMan">[https://deadline.com/2022/02/the-boogeyman-david-dastmalchian-marin-ireland-more-join-stephen-king-film-1234957566/ ‘The Boogeyman’: David Dastmalchian, Marin Ireland, Vivien Lyra Blair & Madison Hu Board 20th Century Studios’ Stephen King Adaptation]</ref>
