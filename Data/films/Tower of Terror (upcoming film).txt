'''''Tower of Terror''''' is an upcoming American horror fantasy film directed by [[Taika Waititi]]. It serves as a reboot of the [[1997]] television film of the [[Tower of Terror (film)|same name]].

==Production==
On September 30, 2021, after [[The Walt Disney Company]] settled their lawsuit dispute with [[Scarlett Johansson]], they started working on a new ''Tower of Terror'' film, with Johansson producing.

On November 1, 2021, it was reported that [[Taika Waititi]] was set to direct the film and [[Josh Cooley]] would write the screenplay.<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2021/11/01/exclusive-taika-waititi-to-direct-disneys-tower-of-terror/ Exclusive: Taika Waititi to Direct Disney’s ‘Tower of Terror’]</ref>
