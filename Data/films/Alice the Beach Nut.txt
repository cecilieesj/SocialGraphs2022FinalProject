'''''Alice the Beach Nut''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Alice plays on the beach while Julius engineers a dramatic rescue as a lifeguard.
