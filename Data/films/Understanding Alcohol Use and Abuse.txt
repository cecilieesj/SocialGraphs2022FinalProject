'''''Understanding Alcohol Use and Abuse''''' is a [[1979]] American educational animated short film. It is the final installment of Upjohn's Triangle of Health series, produced by the Upjohn Company.

==Synopsis==
This film shows the dangers of alcohol abuse and yet why people drink as a means of fitting in.

==Cast==
*Ralph Story: Narrator
*[[Daws Butler]]: Reason
*Richard Bakalyan: Emotion
