'''''Mother Goose Melodies''''' is a black-and-white [[Silly Symphony|''Silly Symphony'']] that was released on [[April 17]], [[1931]].

==Synopsis==
Various storybook characters come to life in song and dance, to the amusement of [[Old King Cole]].

==Characters==
*Old King Cole (voiced by Allan Watson)
*[[Jack and Jill]]
*[[Simple Simon]]
*[[Humpty Dumpty]]
*[[Little Bo Peep]]
*Three Blind Mice
*Mother Goose
*Little Miss Muffett
*Little Jack Horner (voiced by [[Walt Disney]])
*Little Boy Blue
*Baa Baa Black Sheep
*The Cat and the Fiddle
*The Little Dog
*The Cow Who Jumped Over the Moon
