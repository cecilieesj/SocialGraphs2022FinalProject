An upcoming '''''Indiana Jones''''' film is being directed by James Mangold, set for release on [[June 30]], [[2023]]. It will be the final theatrical installment in the ''[[Indiana Jones (franchise)|Indiana Jones]]'' franchise (as well the first film with Disney's involvement), and will star [[Harrison Ford]] returning as [[Indiana Jones|the title character]].

==Production==
The film was officially announced in [[2016]] after years of rumors and speculation with a release date of [[July 19]], [[2019]], but the date was pushed back twice following delays surrounding the script and story. Originally intended to be directed by [[Steven Spielberg]], it was announced in February [[2020]] that he would be stepping down and replaced by James Mangold.<ref>https://variety.com/2020/film/news/steven-spielberg-indiana-jones-5-james-mangold-harrison-ford-1203515698/</ref> On April 8, 2021, Lucasfilm announced that Phoebe Waller-Bridge would star alongside Ford, and that [[John Williams]] would return to score the film,<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.lucasfilm.com/news/phoebe-waller-bridge-joins-indiana-jones-cast/ |title= Phoebe Waller-Bridge Joins ''Indiana Jones'' Cast |publisher= Lucasfilm |date= April 8, 2021}}</ref> but that this film would be his final project before retiring.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.nme.com/news/music/john-williams-to-retire-from-scoring-films-after-indiana-jones-5-3255180 |title= John Williams to retire from scoring films after ‘Indiana Jones 5’ |publisher= NME |date= June 24, 2022}}</ref>
