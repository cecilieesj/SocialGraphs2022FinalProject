==Production==
On June 24, 2019, after multiple attempts, it was reported that [[Taika Waititi]] had signed on as the director of the Flash Gordon reboot.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2019/06/taika-waititi-flash-gordon-animated-movie-thor-ragnarok-jojo-rabbit-fox-disney-1202637059/ Taika Waititi To “Crack” ‘Flash Gordon’ As Fox/Disney Animated Film]</ref>

Initially believed to be an animated film, producers John Davis and John Fox reiterated that the film will be in live-action.<ref>[https://collider.com/taika-waititi-flash-gordon-movie-live-action-new-details/ Exclusive: Taika Waititi's Animated 'Flash Gordon' Movie Is Now Live-Action]</ref>
