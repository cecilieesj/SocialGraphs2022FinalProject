|time = 111 minutes}}'''''Northern Lights'''''<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> is a </span>[[1997]]<span style="line-height:1.5em;"> film </span>based upon the [[1988]] stage play of the same name by John Hoffman <span style="line-height:1.5em;">starring Academy Award-winner [[Diane Keaton]]</span><span style="line-height:1.5em;">.</span>

Some sources identify the film as the first [[Disney Channel Original Movies|Disney Channel Original Movie]], though it was not included in the [[Disney Channel]]'s 100 Original Movies celebration that aired in May–June [[2016]], and the Disney Channel considers [[1997]]'s ''[[Under Wraps]]'' to be the first official Disney Channel Original Movie.  

==Plot==
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">A stranger's call informs Roberta (Diane Keaton) that her estranged brother Frank has died in a small town under bizarre circumstances. Ben, his best friend from college, also gets the call.</p>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Arriving just in time for his funeral, it becomes quickly obvious to them that the little slice of small-town America in which they find themselves is like an episode of ''The Twilight Zone'' featuring Frank's eccentric friends, his amazing secrets and his stunning final request.</p>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Both Ben and Roberta are shocked to discover that Frank not only had a child, but that he has left them responsible for his son's care. Through a series of comic events, each tries to foist off responsibility on the other.</p>
