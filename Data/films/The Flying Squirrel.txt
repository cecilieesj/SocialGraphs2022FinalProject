}}'''''The Flying Squirrel''''' is a [[1954]] animated short starring [[Donald Duck]].

==Synopsis==
Donald enlists the help of a [[Flying Squirrel|flying squirrel]] to hang a sign in a nearby tree for his peanut stand, but the squirrel has to take revenge when Donald cheats on his half of the deal.
