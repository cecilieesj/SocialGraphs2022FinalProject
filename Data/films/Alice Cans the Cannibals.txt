'''''Alice Cans the Cannibals''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius drive their car into the sea where they become stranded on an island occupied by cannibals.
