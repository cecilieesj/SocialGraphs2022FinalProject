'''''More Kittens''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that was released on [[December 19]], [[1936]]. As the title suggests, it is a follow-up to ''[[Three Orphan Kittens]]''.

==Synopsis==
The three kittens once again get into trouble, tormented by a turtle and a bluebird. This time, they find a friend and protector in a big Saint Bernard dog.

==Voice Cast==
*[[Billy Bletcher]] - Bernard Dog
*[[Clarence Nash]] - Kittens
*Purv Pullen - Birds
*Lillian Randolph - [[Mammy Two Shoes|Mammy Two-Shoes]]
