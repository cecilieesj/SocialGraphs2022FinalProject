'''''In Beaver Valley''''' (often referred to as just '''''Beaver Valley''''') is a [[1950]] American short documentary film directed by [[James Algar]] and narrated by [[Winston Hibler]]. The film was produced by [[Walt Disney]] as part of the [[True-Life Adventures|''True-Life Adventures'']] series of nature documentaries.

==Cast==
*[[Winston Hibler]] as Narrator

==Awards==
It won an Academy Award in 1951 for Best Short Subject (Two-Reel). At the 1st Berlin International Film Festival it won the Golden Bear (Documentaries) award.
