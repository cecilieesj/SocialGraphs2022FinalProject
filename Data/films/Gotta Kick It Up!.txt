'''''Gotta Kick It Up!''''' is a [[Disney Channel Original Movie]]. In the US, it first aired [[July 26]], [[2002]]. It is based on a true story of a middle school dance team.

==Plot==
A young executive turned teacher helps a group of young Latina girls find themselves and overcome societal obstacles through their dance troupe.
