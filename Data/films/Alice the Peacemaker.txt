'''''Alice the Peacemaker''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice tries to break up a fight between two newsboys by telling them a story of a feuding cat and mouse.
