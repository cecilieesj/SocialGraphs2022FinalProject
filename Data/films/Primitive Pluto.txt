'''''Primitive Pluto''''' is an animated short starring [[Pluto]].

==Synopsis==
Primo, a wolf and Pluto's primitive instinct, convinces Pluto to go native and go out hunting for his own food like a 'real' dog.

==Characters==
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Primo]] (voiced by [[Paul Frees]])

==Releases==

== Trivia ==
* [[Mickey Mouse]] does not appear in this cartoon, but is mentioned at the very beginning of the cartoon on the sign "Mickey's Wildlife Retreat", the place which Pluto resides in.
