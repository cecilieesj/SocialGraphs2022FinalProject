'''''Mickey in a Minute''''' is a [[2022]] animated short produced by [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]].

==Synopsis==
While touring the halls of the Disney Animation building, Mickey Mouse gets sucked into one of his old cartoon posters and takes a "whirlwind" trip through his past performances.

==Characters==
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Bret Iwan]])

==Trivia==
*This short was created as a segment of the Disney documentary feature, ''[[Mickey: The Story of a Mouse]]'', which also contains looks at the making of the short.
*The designs/costumes Mickey takes on during the short are from, in reverse chronological order: ''[[Mickey's Christmas Carol]]'', ''[[Mickey Mouse Club|The Mickey Mouse Club]]'' (specifically, his "Talent Round-Up" outfit), ''[[Fantasia]]'', ''[[Brave Little Tailor]]'' (with an appearance by the Giant), ''[[The Band Concert]]'', ''[[Mickey's Gala Premier]]'', and ''[[Steamboat Willie]]''.
