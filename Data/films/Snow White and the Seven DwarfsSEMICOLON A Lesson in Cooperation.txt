'''''Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs: A Lesson in Cooperation''''' is a [[1978]] American educational live-action/animated short film, part of the ''Disney's Animated Classics: Lessons in Living'' series. The short features clips from ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]''.



==Synopsis==
Cooperating, even in chores, can be a rewarding experience.
