'''''PRIDE To Be Seen: A Soul of a Nation Presentation''''' is a 2022 Soul of a Nation documentary film documenting the lives and situations of the LGBTQ+ Community today.

==Synopsis==
It’s the resilience and love that keeps this community marching to the beat of its own drum; each generation redefining what it means to be queer and to be seen.

==Plot==
Cara Delevingne and other hosts takes us through the modern climate and lives of the lives and history of the LGBTQ+ Community and their PRIDE.

==Releases==
PRIDE: To Be Seen: A Soul of a Nation Presentation premiered on [[Disney+]] and [[Hulu]] on [[June 24]], [[2022]].
