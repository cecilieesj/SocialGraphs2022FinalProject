'''''Sister Act 3: Kicking the Habit ''''' is an upcoming sequel to ''[[Sister Act 2: Back in the Habit]]'' and a [[Disney+]] original film.

==Cast==
*[[Whoopi Goldberg]] as [[Deloris Van Cartier]]
*Wendy Makkena as [[Sister Mary Robert]]
*[[Kathy Najimy]] as Sister Mary Patrick

==Production==
After a Broad City cameo in 2016, Whoopi expressed doubts about a sequel based on missing cast members, but said she thought it would be fun.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.out.com/out-exclusives/2016/9/20/whoopi-goldberg-weed-being-ally-and-sister-act-3|title=Whoopi Goldberg on Weed, Being an Ally, & Sister Act 3|accessdate= September 20 2016}}</ref> On December 7, 2018, it was confirmed that Regina Y. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/sister-act-3-regina-hicks-karin-gist-disney-1203083508/|title=‘Sister Act 3’: ‘Insecure’ Executive Producer, ‘Star’ Showrunner to Write Sequel|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref>

In December 2020, it was revealed that Tyler Perry would be producer on the film.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/disney-investor-day-2020/|title=Disney Investor Day 2020|accessdate=December 10, 2020}}</ref>
