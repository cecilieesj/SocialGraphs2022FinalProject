'''''Corn Chips''''' is a [[1951]] animated short starring [[Donald Duck]] and [[Chip and Dale]].

==Synopsis==
Donald tricks Chip 'n' Dale into shoveling his snowy sidewalk, so the chipmunks decide to get their own back with Donald ending up shoveling popcorn.

==''Have a Laugh!'' changes==
The following scenes were cut from the short version due to time constraints:
*Donald getting pelted by a bit of snow from the chipmunks and exclaiming, "So!"
*Donald getting the idea to trick the chipmunks into shoveling the snow for him.
*Donald noticing his path is only half-shoveled and tricking the chipmunks into shoveling that as well.
*The chipmunks taking to take a breath from shoveling the snow.
*The chipmunks puzzling over a fire hydrant they crashed into.
*The chipmunks vowing to get revenge on Donald and making a snow version of him which they proceed to demolish before they head for his house.
*Dale looking through the keyhole upside-down.
*The chipmunks sampling some of the kernels which they can't seem to get down, then kick some of them into the fireplace, where they pop into popcorn.
*The chipmunks shoveling the popcorn back into the box and hiding from Donald in it.
*Donald taking a handful of popcorn in his mouth and almost getting Chip along with it.
*Donald thinking that it's snowing before realizing it's actually the popcorn and goes to retrieve it.
*Donald swiping the popcorn back from the chipmunks and hiding it under his hat.
*The entire scene of the chipmunks and Donald vying for the popcorn, wrapped in Donald's hat.
*Donald, in chasing the chipmunks into their tree, rams his head against his shovel, bending into the shape of his head, and deciding to smoke out the chipmunks.
*Donald walking off with the popcorn and saying, "That'll hold 'em," as he starts walking off.

==Trivia==
*This is the first short in which a majority of Chip 'n Dale's dialogue is original to this short, although Chip's first line is still reused from "[[Private Pluto]]".
