'''''Knights''''' is an upcoming family-adventure film, produced by [[Walt Disney Pictures]]. While the film was announced on September 18, 2019, no release date was announced, though it is set to be released theatrically.<ref name="Announcement">[https://deadline.com/2019/09/disney-knights-movie-live-action-matt-orton-operation-finale-1202736804/ Disney In Development On Live-Action Adventure Pic ‘Knights’ With ‘Operation Finale’ Scribe]</ref>

==Plot==
A headstrong young woman joins forces with a knight in order to destroy a dangerous artefact before it falls into the wrong hands.<ref name="Announcement" />
