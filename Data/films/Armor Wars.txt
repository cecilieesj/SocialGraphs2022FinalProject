'''''Armor Wars''''' is an upcoming American superhero film based on the character of the [[War Machine|same name]] and a sequel to ''[[Secret Invasion]]''. It is an installment of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]].

==Synopsis==
[[War Machine|James Rhodes]] must confront one of [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]]'s greatest fears when Stark's tech falls into the wrong hands.

==Cast==
*[[Don Cheadle]] as [[War Machine|James Rhodes/War Machine]]
*[[Sam Rockwell]] as [[Justin Hammer]]<ref>Nathan Earley is listed as his assistant on IMDb</ref>
*[[Walton Goggins]] as [[Sonny Burch]]<ref>Rex Riddick is listed as his stunt double on IMDb</ref>

==Production==
On [[December 10]], [[2020]], [[Kevin Feige]] officially announced the series at the Disney Investors Day.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2020/12/disney-to-launch-marvel-series-ironheart-moon-knight-armor-wars-secret-invasion-1234654568/ Disney+ To Launch Marvel Series ‘Ironheart’, ‘Armor Wars’, ‘Secret Invasion’ & ‘Moon Knight’]</ref>

On [[August 20]], [[2021]], ''Variety'' reported that Yassir Lester was enlisted as head writer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2021/tv/news/armor-wars-disney-plus-series-don-cheadle-yassir-lester-head-writer-1235045820/|title=‘Armor Wars’ Disney Plus Series Starring Don Cheadle Enlists Yassir Lester as Head Writer|website=Variety|date=[[August 20]], [[2021]]}}</ref>

On April 16, 2022, it was revealed that filming is scheduled to begin this fall.<ref>[https://cinemastudies.sas.upenn.edu/events/2022/04/23/wolf-conference-2022-idea-film Wolf Conference 2022 | From Idea to Film]</ref>

On September 10, 2022, it was announced that filming will begin in 2023, and that the series is directly tied into ''[[Secret Invasion]]''.<ref>[https://www.cbr.com/secret-invasion-lead-into-armor-wars-mcu-disney-plus/ Marvel’s Secret Invasion Will Lead Into Armor Wars]</ref>

On September 29, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that ''Armor Wars'' was being redeveloped as a feature film instead of a television series.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/marvels-iron-wars-to-be-movie-don-cheadle-1235230012/ Marvel Shakes Up ‘Armor Wars’: Don Cheadle Series Now Being Developed As a Movie (Exclusive)]</ref>

==Trivia==
*This is the first appearance of [[Justin Hammer]] since ''[[Marvel One-Shot: All Hail the King]]''.
