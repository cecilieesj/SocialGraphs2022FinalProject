'''''The Last Duel''''' is a [[2021]] American-British historical drama film based on the book ''The Last Duel: A True Story of Trial'' by Combat in Medieval France by Eric Jager. The film is directed by [[Ridley Scott]], from a screenplay written by Nicole Holofcener, [[Ben Affleck]] and [[Matt Damon]]. Damon stars opposite [[Adam Driver]] and [[Jodie Comer]], with Affleck in a supporting role.

It was released on [[October 15]], 2021, by [[20th Century Studios]], its world premiere at the 2021 Venice Film Festival on September 10, 2021.

==Synopsis==
In 14th century France, best friends Jean de Carrouges and Jacques Le Gris are being asked to fight to the death after Carrouges accuses Le Gris of raping his wife Marguerite. The events leading up to the duel are divided into three chapters, reflecting the perspectives of de Carrouges, Le Gris, and Marguerite, respectively.

==Production==
Filming began on February 14, 2020 in Dordogne, France<ref>[https://www.francebleu.fr/amp/infos/culture-loisirs/dordogne-ridley-scott-cherche-des-doublures-pour-les-stars-de-son-prochain-film-1579014852 Recherche doublures pour Matt Damon, Adam Driver et Jodie Comer pour un tournage en Dordogne]</ref><ref>[https://figurants.com/figuration-cinema/figuration-long-metrage/casting-doublures-matt-damon-adam-driver-et-jodie-comer-pour-film-de-ridley-scott_i11180 #casting doublures Matt Damon, Adam Driver et Jodie Comer pour film de Ridley Scott]</ref><ref>[https://www.francebleu.fr/infos/culture-loisirs/discretion-de-mise-a-sarlat-autour-du-dernier-riddley-scott-1581627414 Discrétion de mise à Sarlat autour du dernier Ridley Scott]</ref> and continued on March 12, 2020 in the medieval castle of Berzé-le-Châtel (near Mâcon), Burgundy, France (with a film crew of 300 people including 100 extras).<ref>{{cite web |title= #Macon #casting femmes et hommes 16/80 ans pour tournage film avec Matt Damon et Adam Driver Macon - castings figurants : Casting cinéma, Casting chant, casting télé |url= https://figurants.com/figuration-cinema/figuration-long-metrage/macon-casting-femmes-et-hommes-1680-ans-pour-tournage-film-avec-matt-damon-et-adam-driver_i11217 |website= Figurants.com |language= fr }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title= Le JSL > "The Last Duel" by Damien VALETTE (23 January 2020) |url= https://www.lejsl.com/edition-macon/2020/01/23/the-last-duel-cinq-a-six-jours-de-tournage-au-chateau-de-berze |website= www.lejsl.com |language= fr}}</ref>

Filming was to have taken place in County Meath, Dublin, County Wicklow and Cahir Castle (in County Tipperary), Ireland from March 23, 2020 to March 30, 2020.<ref>[https://www.meathchronicle.ie/news/roundup/articles/2020/02/11/4185567-hollywood-legends-coming-to-meath-for-major-movie-shoot/ Hollywood legends coming to Meath for major movie shoot]</ref>

However, on March 13, 2020, Disney announced that the studio had to delay the shoot indefinitely amid concerns for the cast and crew in light of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic as well as travel restrictions in Europe.<ref>[https://variety.com/2020/film/news/disney-fox-production-halts-coronavirus-the-little-mermaid-the-last-duel-1203531990/ Disney Halts Production on Films Including ‘The Last Duel’ – Variety<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> Filming resumed in late August, and wrapped on October 14th.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2020/08/mgm-ridley-scott-lady-gaga-gucci-film-robert-de-niro-jared-leto-al-pacino-adam-driver-reeve-carney-1202997254/ MGM’s Ridley Scott Lady Gaga Gucci Film Eyes Robert De Niro, Jared Leto, Al Pacino, Adam Driver, Jack Huston & Reeve Carney]</ref>

==Release==
The film was originally scheduled to begin a limited theatrical release on December 25, 2020, before going wide on January 8, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/11/disney-fox-release-schedule-the-last-duel-the-kings-man-nimona-1202787773/|title=Disney Dates A Ton Of Pics Into 2023 & Juggles Fox Releases With Ridley Scott's 'The Last Duel' To Open Christmas 2020, 'The King's Man' Next Fall – Update|last=D'Alessandro|first=Anthony|website=Deadline Hollywood|date=November 15, 2019|access-date=May 15, 2020}}</ref>

However, as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, the release date was pushed back to October 15, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/07/mulan-avatar-star-wars-release-date-changes-disney-coroanvirus-1202993719/|title=‘Mulan’ Off The Calendar; Disney Also Delays ‘Avatar’ & ‘Star Wars’ Movies By One Year As Studio Adjusts To Pandemic|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Anthony|last=D'Alessandro|date=July 23, 2020|accessdate=July 23, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=Rubin|first=Rebecca|date=July 23, 2020|title=‘Star Wars’ Films, ‘Avatar’ Sequels Pushed Back a Year in Disney Release Calendar Shakeup|work=Variety|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/box-office/star-wars-films-avatar-sequels-pushed-back-a-year-in-disney-release-calendar-shakeup-1234715104/|url-status=live|access-date=July 23, 2020}}</ref>
