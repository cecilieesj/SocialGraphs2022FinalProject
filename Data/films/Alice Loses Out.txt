'''''Alice Loses Out''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice manages a hotel where Julius does all the work, including janitor, barber, and manicurist.
