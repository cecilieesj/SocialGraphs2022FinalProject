'''''A Celebration of the Music from Coco''''' is a concert film directed by Ron de Moraes released to [[Disney+]] on [[April 10]], 2020. The film showcases the Disney/Pixar ''[[Coco]] – A Live-to-Film Concert Experience'' at the Hollywood Bowl in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].

==Synopsis==
This concert film showcases not just the music from ''Coco'', but also traditional Mexican songs and musical pieces. The program features the Hollywood Studio Symphony under the direction of Maestro Sarah Hicks with special appearance by Mariachi Divas. Because Miguel's original voice actor, Anthony Gonzalez, had hit puberty since the film was released, his younger brother, Alex Gonzalez sings Miguel's songs.
