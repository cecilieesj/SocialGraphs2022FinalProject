'''''Frank Duck Bring 'em Back Alive''''' is a [[Donald Duck|''Donald'']] and [[Goofy|''Goofy'']] animated short released on [[November 1]], [[1946]]. It was directed by [[Jack Hannah]], written by [[Dick Kinney]] and animated by Hugh Fraser, Jim Moore, Al Coe, and [[Andy Engman]].

==Synopsis==
Goofy is a "wild man of the jungle", and Donald DuckÂ arrives to capture him and make him part of his circus.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Goofy]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*Lion (voiced by Clarence Nash)

==Notes==
*Donald's alias in this short, Frank Duck, is a parody of Frank Buck, a famous big-game hunter who wrote a bestselling book titled ''Bring 'Em Back Alive''.
*Although the original theatrical poster states the cartoon as a ''Donald Duck'' cartoon, it was actually released as a ''Donald & Goofy'' cartoon.
