'''''Untitled Taika Waititi Film''''' is an upcoming untitled ''[[Star Wars]]'' film by [[Lucasfilm]]. It will be released in late [[2023]].

==Premise==
A brand-new ''Star Wars'' feature with acclaimed filmmaker and Academy Award-winner [[Taika Waititi]] is in development. “Taika’s approach to ''Star Wars'' will be fresh, unexpected, and unique,” said Kennedy. “His enormous talent and sense of humor will ensure that audiences are in for an unforgettable ride.”<ref>[https://www.starwars.com/news/future-lucasfilm-projects-revealed Future Lucasfilm Projects Revealed]</ref>

==Production==
===Development===
On January, The Hollywood Reporter revealed that Taika Waititi was approached for a ''Star Wars'' movie. It was announced on May 4, 2020.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/taika-waititi-courted-star-wars-movie-1269996 Taika Waititi Courted for ‘Star Wars’ Movie (Exclusive)]</ref>

On May 27, 2022, in an interview with ''Total Film'', [[Kathleen Kennedy]] confirmed that the film would be released in late [[2023]].<ref>[https://www.gamesradar.com/star-wars-movies-kathleen-kennedy-celebration-interview/ Kathleen Kennedy on the future of Star Wars movies: "We need to create a whole new saga"]</ref>
