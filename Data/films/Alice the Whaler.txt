}}'''''Alice the Whaler''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It was released as part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Synopsis==
Alice and Julius board a whaling ship where they have to deal with a temperamental cook as well as harpooning a whale.
