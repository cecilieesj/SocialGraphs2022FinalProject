'''''Three Little Wolves''''' is a [[1936]] ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' cartoon starring [[The Three Little Pigs (characters)|The Three Little Pigs]].

==Synopsis==
A Disney take on "The Boy Who Cried Wolf" as Fifer Pig and Fiddler Pig continually set off the wolf alarm to Practical Pig's annoyance. Unbeknownst to them, they are being stalked by the Big Bad Wolves' sons.

==Characters==
*[[The Three Little Pigs (characters)|The Three Little Pigs]] (voiced by Dorothy Compton, Mary Moder, [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Big Bad Wolf]] (voiced by [[Billy Bletcher]])
*[[Three Little Wolves (characters)|The Three Little Wolves]] (voiced by Alyce Ardell, Leone Ledoux)
