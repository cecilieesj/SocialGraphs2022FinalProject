"'''Snow Bear'''" is a [[1970]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[November 1]], and [[November 8]], 1970. It was later edited into a Disney educational short film in March [[1972]] under the title '''''The Track of the Giant Snow Bear'''''. The telefilm was directed by Gunther von Fritsch.
