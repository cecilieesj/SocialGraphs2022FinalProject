'''Men Against the Arctic''' is a [[1955]] short documentary film directed by Winston Hibler. It won an Academy Award in [[1956]] for Documentary Short Subject. It was also entered into the 6th Berlin International Film Festival.

This short film was released in a double bill with both [[The Littlest Outlaw]] and [[The Great Locomotive Chase]].
