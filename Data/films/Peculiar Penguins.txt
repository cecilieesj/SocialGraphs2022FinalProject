'''''Peculiar Penguins''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that was released on [[September 1]], [[1934]].

==Synopsis==
During a dance piece from the South Pole called "[[The Penguin Is a Very Funny Creature]]", [[Peter Penguin|Peter]] catches a fish to show his love for [[Polly Penguin|Polly]]. The fish expands when she eats it, making her look fat, and Peter foolish. Along comes a shark out to end the whole thing -- you even feel sorry for the shark at the end.
