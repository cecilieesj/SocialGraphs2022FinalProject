'''''No Hunting''''' is an animated short released on [[January 14]], [[1955]], starring [[Donald Duck]].

==Synopsis==
Donald is inspired by the spirit of his forefathers to take up a gun and go hunting for his food.

[[Bambi]] and [[Bambi's mother|his mother]] make a cameo appearance.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Grandpa Duck]] (Donald's grandfather) (voiced by [[Bill Thompson]])
*[[Bambi]] (cameo)
*[[Bambi's mother]] (cameo) (voiced by unknown)
*[[Flannery]] (cameo)
*Usher (cameo) (voiced by Milt Schaffer)
*Moose (cameo) (voiced by [[Jack Hannah]])

==Trivia==
* With only 5 minutes and 25 seconds of footage (not counting the credits and cards), "No Hunting"' is the shortest Donald Duck short ever made in the Golden Age of American animation.
* The shot of Donald attempting to get on the highway is reused from ''[[Motor Mania]]''.
