}}'''''Hollywood Party''''' is a musical film starring Jimmy Durante and was originally released to theaters on [[June 1]], [[1934]] by [[Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]].

The film features an animated cameo appearance by [[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Walt Disney]]). In the film sequence, he harassed Jimmy Durante, plays the piano and introduces a Technicolor [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] cartoon that was made for the film entitled "[[The Hot Chocolate Soldiers]]".

==Copyright info==
The film's copyright was renewed by MGM on [[June 6]], [[1961]].<ref>[https://archive.org/details/catalogofcopyr3151213libr/page/62/mode/1up Catalog of Copyright Entries]</ref>
