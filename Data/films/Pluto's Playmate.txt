'''''Pluto's Playmate''''' is a [[1941]] animated short starring [[Pluto]].

==Synopsis==
Pluto fights with a baby seal during a visit to the beach, but the seal soon has to come to his rescue from an attacking octopus.

==Characters==
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by Lee Millar)
*[[Salty the Seal]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])

==Trivia==
