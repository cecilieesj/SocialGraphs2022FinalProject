| Trivia                  = * This attraction will occupy the [[Wikipedia:Universe of Energy|Energy Pavilion]]. The Energy Pavilion original attraction, ''Universe of Energy'', opened along with [[Wikipedia:Epcot|Epcot]] on October 1st, 1982. The original attraction operated from 1982-1996 when it was renovated and reopened as ''Ellen's Energy Adventure''. This second iteration finally closed on August 13, 2017.<ref name="announcement">{{cite web|url=https://www.ign.com/articles/2017/07/15/new-guardians-of-the-galaxy-ride-announced-for-epcot-center|title=New Guardians of the Galaxy Ride Announced for Walt Disney World|work=IGN|first=Seth & Eric|last=Macy & Goldman|date=15 July 2017|accessdate=15 Feb 2021}}</ref>
* This attraction was first announced at the D23 Expo on July 15th, 2017.<ref name="announcement"/> The official name of the attraction was revealed two year later at the D23 Expo in August, 2019.<ref name="named">{{cite web|url=
https://disneyparks.disney.go.com/blog/2019/08/live-exciting-announcements-for-disney-parks-resorts-cruise-ships-and-beyond-at-d23-expo-2019/|title=Exciting Announcements for Disney Parks, Resorts, Cruise Ships and Beyond at D23 Expo 2019|work=Disney Parks Blog|first=Jennifer|last=Fickley-Baker|date=12 September 2019|accessdate=15 Feb 2021}}</ref>
* Filming for this attraction began in January, 2021. Filmmaker [[Taika Waititi|Taika Waititi]] will directing the scenes and filming with the cast will occur during the filming of [[Thor: Love and Thunder]].<ref name="filming">{{cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2021/01/04/taika-waititi-to-film-sequences-for-guardians-of-the-galaxy-cosmic-rewind-at-epcot/#:~:text=%E2%80%9CThis%20is%20the%20first%20time,will%20likely%20debut%20in%202022.|title=Taika Waititi to Film Sequences For ‘Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind’ at EPCOT|work=Disney Parks Blog|first=Skyler|last=Shuler|date=21 January 2021|accessdate=15 Feb 2021}}</ref>
* This is the second permanent Disney Theme Park attraction inspired by the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]], with the first being [[Guardians of the Galaxy - Mission: BREAKOUT!]] in [[Wikipedia:Disney_California_Adventure|Disney California Adventure]] which opened on May 27, 2017. This is also the first permanent attraction at the [[Disney World|Walt Disney World resort]] to be inspired by [[Marvel Comics|Marvel Comics characters]] or the [[Earth-199999|Marvel Cinematic Universe]].
