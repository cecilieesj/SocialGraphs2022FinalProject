|language       = English}}'''''Nature's Strangest Creature''''' is a 1959 nature documentary short about strange creatures in [[Australia]]. Though not an official entry in the series, it was given a home release as an extra on the ''[[True-Life Adventures]]'' Walt Disney Legacy Collection DVD series.

==Synopsis==
The viewer witnesses a quick overview of the most amazing Australian animals in the world: koala, kangaroo, wallaby and platypus.
