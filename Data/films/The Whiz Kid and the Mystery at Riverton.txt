"'''The Whiz Kid and the Mystery at Riverton'''" is a [[1974]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[January 6]], and [[January 13]], 1974. The telefilm was directed by Tom Leetch.

==Synopsis==
"The Whiz Kid and the Mystery at Riverton" is about a sixth-grader (Eric Shea) who tries to expose a land swindle engineered by a city official (John Fiedler)
