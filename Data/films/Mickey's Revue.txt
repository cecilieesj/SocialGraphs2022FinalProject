'''''Mickey's Revue''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' cartoon that debuted on [[May 27]], [[1932]]. It is notable as the first appearance of [[Goofy]], then known as Dippy Dawg.

==Synopsis==
[[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]], [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]], [[Horace Horsecollar|Horace]], and [[Clarabelle Cow|Clarabelle]] put on another big show, with an elder audience member named Dippy Dawg as the running gag - eating peanuts and laughing to the annoyance of the audience.
