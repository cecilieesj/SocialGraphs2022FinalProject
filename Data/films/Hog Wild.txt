|next = "[[Carlo, the Sierra Coyote]]"}}"'''Hog Wild'''" is a [[1974]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode of ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[January 20]], and [[January 27]], [[1974]]. It was directed by [[Jerome Courtland]].

==Synopsis==
A [[Chicago]] man and his family move to an [[Idaho]] pig farm, where he's crippled by an angry sow.
