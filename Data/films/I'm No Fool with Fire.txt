|release = [[December 1]], [[1955]] (television)<br>April [[1956]] (16mm)<br>September [[1986]] (updated version)}}'''I'm No Fool with Fire''' is an "I'm No Fool" short that originally aired on ''[[The Mickey Mouse Club]]''Â in [[1955]].

==Synopsis==
[[Jiminy Cricket]] gives a history of man's reliance on fire, then offers safety techniques to handle and control it properly.

==Voices==
===Original===
*[[Cliff Edwards]] - Jiminy Cricket
===Remake===
*[[Hal Smith]] - Jiminy Cricket

==Trivia==
*In [[1986]], an updated version of this short was released by the Walt Disney Educational Media Company, which updated the safety information on this short and showed kids with a real live fire fighter showing them the modern safety features such as smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, and the 911 emergency phone number.
