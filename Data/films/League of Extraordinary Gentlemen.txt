'''''League of Extraordinary Gentlemen''''' is an upcoming American adventure film that serves as a reboot for the franchise. It is scheduled to be released on [[Hulu]].

==Production==
On May 17, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that [[20th Century Studios]] is developing a reboot of ''League of Extraordinary Gentlemen'', to be released as a Hulu exclusive.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/league-of-extraordinary-gentlemen-reboot-in-the-works-at-twentieth-hulu-1235148573 ‘League of Extraordinary Gentlemen’ Reboot in the Works at Twentieth, Hulu]</ref>
