'''''Antlers''''' is a supernatural horror film. It is directed by Scott Cooper and written by C. Henry Chaisson, Nick Antosca, and Scott Cooper. The film is based on the short story "The Quiet Boy" by Nick Antosca. The film stars [[Keri Russell]], Jesse Plemons, Jeremy T. Thomas, Graham Greene, Scott Haze, Rory Cochrane, and Amy Madigan.

The film was scheduled to be released by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]] through the [[Searchlight Pictures]] banner on [[April 17]], [[2020]], but has been delayed to [[February 19]], [[2021]], and again, it was delayed to [[October 29]], [[2021]], due to the coronavirus outbreak.<ref name="Delayed">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nydailynews.com/snyde/ny-disney-delays-mulan-release-amid-coronavirus-concerns-20200312-uvouoxesc5dixmvxrathzd6y2a-story.html|title=Disney delays ‘Mulan’ release and two other movies amid coronavirus concerns|first=Lauren|last=Thiesen|date=March 12, 2020|accessdate=March 12, 2020|publisher=New York Daily News}}</ref>

==Synopsis==
Julia Meadows, an Oregon teacher, and Paul, her sheriff brother, became entangled with a young student at her school who is revealing a risky secret with scary consequences.
