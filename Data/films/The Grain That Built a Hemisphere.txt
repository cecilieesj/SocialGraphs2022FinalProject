'''''The Grain That Built a Hemisphere''''' is a [[1943]] short animated propaganda film about corn produced by [[Walt Disney]] for the U.S. Coordinator of Inter-American Affairs.<ref name=ref=Shale82>Shale, Richard. (1982). Donald Duck joins up: The Walt Disney Studio during World War II (Studies in cinema). Ann Arbor: UMI Research Press.</ref> It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Documentary in 1943.
