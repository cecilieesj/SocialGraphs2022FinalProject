| Notes                   = * This film has had multiple changes to its US release date due to the [[wikipedia:COVID-19 pandemic|COVID-19 pandemic]]:
** Originally slated to be released on May 1, 2020 in the US, the release was pulled on March 17, 2020.{{r|{{cite web|url= https://variety.com/2020/film/box-office/black-widow-release-coronavirus-1203532996/|title= ‚ÄòBlack Widow‚Äô Release Pulled Amid Coronavirus Pandemic|work= Variety|last= Rubin|first= Rebecca|date= March 17, 2020|accessdate= March 17, 2020}}}} On April 3, 2020, it was announced that the release date for this film had been pushed to November 6, 2020.{{r|{{cite web|url= https://variety.com/2020/film/box-office/disney-mulan-black-widow-release-dates-coronavirus-1203550173/|title= ‚ÄòBlack Widow,‚Äô ‚ÄòEternals,‚Äô ‚ÄòIndiana Jones 5‚Äô and More Disney Films Get New Release Dates|work= Variety|last= Rubin|first= Rebecca|date= April 3, 2020|accessdate= April 3, 2020}}}} 
** In September 2020, the film was pushed back again to May 7, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/news/black-widow-west-side-story-eternals-release-date-delay-1234773491/|title=‚ÄòBlack Widow,‚Äô ‚ÄòWest Side Story,‚Äô ‚ÄòEternals‚Äô Postpone Release Dates|accessdate=September 24, 2020|last=Lang|first=Brent|coauthors=Rubin, Rebecca|date=September 23, 2020|work=Variety}}</ref>
** In March 2021, the film was pushed back for a third time to July 9, 2021, releasing in theaters and on Disney+ with Premier Access on the same day.<ref name="Twitter">[https://twitter.com/MarvelStudios/status/1374429041877389320 Black Widow in theaters July 9, 2021]</ref>
* This was originally intended to be the Marvel Studios property that launched Phase 4 of their [[Earth-199999|cinematic universe]]. However, due to the film's release date delays, the Disney+ series ''[[WandaVision]]'' began Phase 4 instead, with ''Black Widow'' instead being slated as the first film of Phase 4.
