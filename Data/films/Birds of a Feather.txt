'''''Birds of a Feather''''' is a black-and-white ''[[Silly Symphony]]''Â animated short that debuted on [[February 10]], [[1931]].

==Synopsis==
A flock of birds are out, just having a good time singing and dancing. As the flock takes flight, they are attacked by a hawk who steals one of the baby birds. All of the birds join together in a joint attack to rescue the little bird.
