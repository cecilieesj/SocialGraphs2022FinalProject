'''''Avatar 5''''' is an upcoming epic science fiction film directed by [[James Cameron]] and produced by [[20th Century Studios]]. It is a direct sequel to ''[[Avatar 4]]'' and the final film in the franchise. It is set to be released on [[December 22]], 2028.

==Production==
On July 31, 2017, ''Variety'' reported that [[20th Century Studios]] would develop sequels to ''Avatar'', with the fifth one being the final one in the franchise.<ref>[https://variety.com/2017/artisans/news/avatar-sequels-progress-to-next-level-as-weta-digital-begins-visual-effects-work-on-the-films-james-cameron-1202510639/ ‘Avatar’ Sequels Progress to Next Level as Weta Digital Begins Visual Effects Work]</ref>

Filming on ''[[Avatar 4]]'' and ''Avatar 5'' have not begun yet, as James Cameron is waiting for the audience response to ''[[Avatar: The Way of Water]]'' and ''[[Avatar 3]]''.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2017/09/avatar-james-cameron-four-consecutive-sequels-1-billion-budget-1202176798/ ‘Avatar’ Starts Production Today On Four Consecutive Sequels At Over $1 Billion Budget]</ref>
