'''''Shrunk'' '''will be the fourth movie in the ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids]]'' franchise, following ''[[Honey, We Shrunk Ourselves]]''.

==Plot==
The movie will feature an older [[Nick Szalinski]] following in [[Wayne Szalinski|Wayne]]'s footsteps as he continues Wayne's size-changing experiments while they both deal with [[Diane Szalinski|Diane]]'s death. Nick now has two children, Theo, who has a gloomy disposition and is sensitive about his weight, and Maddie, who hosts a YouTube show where she showcases Nick's inventions. His sister, [[Amy Szalinski|Amy]], will also return for this movie.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cbr.com/shrunk-character-breakdowns-story-details/|title=Shrunk Character Breakdowns, Story Details Surface|website=Comic Book Resources|date=February 17, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/film/news/honey-i-shrunk-the-kids-reboot-josh-gad-joe-johnston-1203425344/|title='Honey, I Shrunk the Kids' Director to Return for Reboot Starring Josh Gad|website=Variety|date=December 5, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.slashfilm.com/honey-i-shrunk-the-kids-reboot/|title='Shrunk' in the Works With Josh Gad to Star|website=Slashfilm.com|date=May 13th, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/02/honey-i-shrunk-the-kids-reboot-rick-moranis-1202858344/|title=Rick Moranis Closes Deal To Return To 'Honey, I Shrunk The Kids' Franchise With 'Shrunk' At Disney|website=Deadline|date=February 12, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2020/02/03/exclusive-disney-may-bring-rick-moranis-out-of-retirement-for-honey-i-shrunk-the-kids-reboot-shrunk/|title=Rick Moranis Out Of Retirement For 'Shrunk'|website=DisnInsider|date=February 4, 2020}}</ref>
