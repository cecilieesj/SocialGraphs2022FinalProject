'''''Magic and Music''''' is a [[1974]] Disney educational animated short. It is an edited version of the ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Disneyland]]'' episode of [[Magic and Music|the same name]].

==Synopsis==
The Wizard of the Mirror hosts as three songs are played, accompanied by cartoons: "Flight of The Bumble Bee," "Sometimes in Winter Time," and "Pastorale" with the dancing centaurs.

==Trivia==
*The music in the opening titles is taken from ''[[Eyes in Outer Space]]''.
*This program was released on videocassette in the [[United Kingdom]] on the fifth volume of "The Disney Channel", paired with ''[[Peter and the Wolf]]''.
