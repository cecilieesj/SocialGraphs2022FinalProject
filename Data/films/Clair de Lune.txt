|time = 6 minutes}}'''''Clair de Lune''''' is an unused ''[[Fantasia]]'' segment. It is set to Claude Debussy's classical musical piece "Clair De Lune". It was produced in [[1941]], but did not see a release until 1996. However, it appeared on the original broadcast of ''[[Music for Everybody]]''. In 1970, broadcasts replaced it with a montage of clips from ''[[Sleeping Beauty]]''.

Footage from this segment was re-scored and re-edited as the "[[Blue Bayou]]" sequence in ''[[Make Mine Music]]''.

==Synopsis==
A tone poem featuring an egret looking for companionship in the loneliness of the bayou.
