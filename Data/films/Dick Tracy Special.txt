'''''Dick Tracy Special''''' is a [[2010]] television special starring [[Warren Beatty]] and [[Leonard Maltin]]. In the special, Maltin (as himself) interviews Tracy (Beatty) about his long career, and the comic strips and films "starring" him, including the [[Dick Tracy|1990 film]] that Beatty starred in and directed for [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Touchstone Pictures|Touchstone]]. Maltin also narrates clips showing the history of the character and clips 

[[Mindy Sterling]] and [[Wendi McLendon-Covey]] play studio personnel waiting for Tracy to arrive at his interview. 

The special, which broadcast once on [[TCM]], was produced by Beatty in order to retain the film and television rights to the character, which would expire if he did not use them.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.avclub.com/article/our-option-atlas-shrugged-expires-two-days-6-plus--212280 |title=Our Option on ''Atlas Shrugged'' Expires in Two Days: 6-Plus Copyright Extensions Disguised as Movies |last=Vishnevetsky |first=Ignatiy |author-link=Ignatiy Vishnevetsky |date=December 1, 2014 |publisher=The A.V. Club}}</ref> In an interview about the special, Maltin said that Beatty had pitched the special to Disney Home Video, wanting to produce something similar in style to ''{{WikipediaLink|The Charlie Rose Show}}'', and someone there suggested Maltin for the job.<ref name="ohmydisney">{{cite web|title=Remembering That Weird TCM Dick Tracy Special|url=http://blogs.disney.com/insider/2015/06/29/remembering-that-weird-tcm-dick-tracy-special/|website=Disney Insider|publisher=[[The Walt Disney Company]]|access-date=July 8, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150703150940/http://blogs.disney.com/insider/2015/06/29/remembering-that-weird-tcm-dick-tracy-special/|archive-date=July 3, 2015|date=July 2015}}</ref>

The special was set and partially filmed at the [[Walt Disney Studios (Burbank)|Walt Disney Studios]] in Burbank. The interview was shot on a stage at [[Walt Disney Imagineering]] in Glendale. The interview was shot by acclaimed cinematographer {{WikipediaLink|Emmanuel Lubezki}}, to the surprise of Maltin: "I said, 'Oh my god, Chivo is shooting this little conversation!' He’s a lovely man and I’ve seen him many times since and we always laugh about it."<ref name="ohmydisney"/>
