'''''Paul McCartney: The Music and Animation Collection''''' is a collection of three animated shorts produced by {{WikipediaLink|Paul McCartney}}. It was released in the U.S. on DVD by [[Miramax Films]]. The film includes ''{{WikipediaLink|Rupert and the Frog Song}}'', ''{{WikipediaLink|Tuesday (book)|Tuesday}},'' and ''{{WikipediaLink| Tropic Island Hum}}''.

==Release==
This film was released on DVD in August [[2004]] in libraries and released in September at Amazon.com. This film was also re-released on DVD in [[2011]] by Echo Bridge Home Entertainment.
