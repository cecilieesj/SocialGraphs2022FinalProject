|language = English|image = File:Mickeys-birthday-party-show-movie-poster-1978-1020193324.jpg}}'''''Mickey's Birthday Party Show''''' is a [[1978]] feature-length compilation film.

==Synopsis==
In celebration of [[Mickey Mouse]]'s fiftieth birthday, the film contains five classic cartoons starring Mickey and his friends, as well as a special guest appearance by the 1970s Mouseketeers. Also on the program is the 1955 live-action feature ''[[Davy Crockett, King of the Wild Frontier]]''.
