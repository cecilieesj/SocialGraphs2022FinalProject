'''''Alice Foils the Pirates''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Julius discovers Alice being held captive on Pete's pirate ship and once again has to take on Pete to rescue her.
