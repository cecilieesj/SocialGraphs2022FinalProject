'''''Lilo & Stitch''''' is an upcoming American, live-action science fiction film adaptation of [[Lilo & Stitch|the 2002 film of the same name]]. The project was announced in October [[2018]], although a release date is still to be announced.

==Cast==
*[[Chris Sanders]] as the voice of [[Stitch]].<ref name="DisInsider February 2020"/>

===Development===
A live-action adaptation of ''Lilo & Stitch'' was first reported in [[October 3]], 2018, to be under development at [[Walt Disney Pictures]], with Dan Lin and Jonathan Eirich of production studio [[Rideback]] producing, and Mike Van Waes writing the film's script.<ref name="Hollywood Reporter October 2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/lilo-stitch-live-action-disney-remake-works-1148811?|title='Lilo & Stitch' Live-Action Remake in the Works at Disney (Exclusive)|last=Kit|first=Borys|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=October 3, 2018|accessdate=July 18, 2022}}</ref>


On [[November 13]], [[2020]], it was reported that Jon M. Chu was in talks to direct the film, while Disney was looking for a new writer to re-write Van Waes's script.<ref name="Variety November 2020">{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/news/jon-m-chu-lilo-stitch-movie-disney-1234831529/|title=Jon M. Chu to Direct 'Lilo & Stitch' Live-Action Movie|website=Variety|first=Rebecca|last=Rubin|date=November 13, 2020|accessdate=July 18, 2022|archivedate=February 9, 2021|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20210209132226/https://variety.com/2020/film/news/jon-m-chu-lilo-stitch-movie-disney-1234831529/}}</ref><ref name="Hollywood Reporter November 2020">{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/jon-m-chu-in-talks-to-direct-live-action-lilo-stitch-movie-exclusive|title=Jon M. Chu in Talks to Direct Live-Action 'Lilo & Stitch' Movie (Exclusive)|website=The Hollywood Reporter|first=Borys|last=Kit|date=November 13, 2020|accessdate=July 18, 2022|archivedate=November 13, 2020|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20201113202914/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/jon-m-chu-in-talks-to-direct-live-action-lilo-stitch-movie-exclusive}}</ref> However, it was later reported in May 2021 that Chu decided to leave production to focus on his film adaptations of ''Wicked'' and ''China Rich Girlfriend''.<ref name="DisInsider May 2021">{{Cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2021/05/29/upcoming-disney-live-action-remakes-adaptations/|title=Upcoming Disney Live-Action Remakes/Adaptations|first=Skyler|last=Shuler|work=The DisInsider|date=May 29, 2021|accessdate=July 18, 2022}}</ref>

On [[July 14]], [[2022]], ''Deadline Hollywood'' reported that Dean Fleischer Camp will be the film's director, while Chris Kekaniokalani Bright was negotiating with Disney to rewrite the script.<ref name="Deadline July 2022">{{Cite web|last=Kroll|first=Justin|date=2022-07-14|title=‘Lilo & Stitch’ Live-Action Pic At Disney Taps ‘Marcel The Shell With Shoes On’s Dean Fleischer Camp To Direct|url=https://deadline.com/2022/07/lilo-stich-disney-marcel-and-the-shell-director-1235064201/|access-date=July 14, 2022|website=Deadline Hollywood|accessdate=July 18, 2022}}</ref>

===Filming===
Filming will take place in [[Hawaii]]. It was supposed to begin in fall 2020.<ref name="DisInsider February 2020"/>

==Release==
''The DisInsider'' reported on [[February 4]], 2020, that the film will be a streaming release through [[Disney+]].<ref name="DisInsider February 2020"/> However, ''Variety'' and ''The Hollywood Reporter'' contradicted this in November 2020, saying that Disney has yet to decide on either a theatrical or streaming release.<ref name="Hollywood Reporter November 2020"/><ref name="Variety November 2020"/> The release format remains unclear as of July 2022.<ref name="Deadline July 2022"/>

==Trivia==
*This is the first remake of a [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] film that is from the post-Renaissance era.
*[[Chris Sanders]] previously voiced [[Stitch]] in [[Lilo & Stitch|the original film]]. Sanders is the seventh actor to reprise his role for a Disney remake of a previous Disney animated production after [[Jim Cummings]], [[Brad Garrett]] (both of who reprised [[Winnie the Pooh]], [[Tigger]], also voiced by Cummings since the late [[1980]]s, and [[Eeyore]], whom Garrett voiced previously in ''[[Animated StoryBook: Winnie the Pooh and the Honey Tree]]'' and recently in ''[[Ralph Breaks the Internet]]'', in [[2018]]'s ''[[Christopher Robin (film)|Christopher Robin]]''), [[Nancy Cartwright]] and [[Patton Oswalt]] (both of who reprised [[Rufus (Kim Possible)|Rufus]] and [[Professor Dementor]] in the live-action ''[[Kim Possible (film)|Kim Possible]]'' film), [[Frank Welker]] (who reprised the vocal effects for [[Abu]] and [[Rajah]] and the voice of the [[Cave of Wonders]] in ''[[Aladdin (2019 film)|Aladdin]]''), and [[James Earl Jones]] (who reprised his role as [[Mufasa]] in ''[[The Lion King (2019 film)|The Lion King]]'').
