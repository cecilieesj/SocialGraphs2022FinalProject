'''''Descendants: The Pocketwatch''''' (working title) is an upcoming American musical fantasy comedy film directed by Jennifer Phang. The film is the fourth installment and speculated spin-off to the ''[[Descendants (franchise)|Descendants]]'' franchise and is scheduled to be released on [[Disney+]] in the future, with production beginning in early [[2023]].<ref name="announcement">[https://deadline.com/2022/05/descendants-sequel-the-pocketwatch-movie-disney-plus-1235020234/ ‘Descendants’ Sequel ‘The Pocketwatch’ Movie Greenlighted By Disney+; Jennifer Phang To Direct]</ref> 

==Synopsis==
The story, which introduces Red, the whip-smart and rebellious teenage daughter of the [[Queen of Hearts]] (''Alice in Wonderland'') and Chloe, the perfectionist and athletic daughter of [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]] and [[Prince Charming]] (''Cinderella''). In order to prevent an impending coup in [[Auradon]]–one that looms during the celebration of a new royal baby–Red must join forces with Chloe to travel in back in time, via the White Rabbit’s pocket watch, to stop an event that would lead to grave consequences.<ref name="announcement" />

==Production==
On May 10, 2022, Disney+ announced a new ''Descendants'' film was in the works, set to release on the streaming platform.<ref>https://deadline.com/2022/05/descendants-sequel-the-pocketwatch-movie-disney-plus-1235020234/</ref>

On November 21 2022, it announced 7 other cast members joined the cast of ''The Pocketwatch'' since 2022's D23

Filming is set to begin in early 2023.

==Trivia==
*The film is still in production, under the working title ''<nowiki/>'The Pocketwatch'''.<ref name="announcement" />
*[[Auradon]] appears for the first time since ''[[Descendants 3]]''.
*Seven new songs and Disney classic songs will advance the story.<ref name="announcement" />
*Returning to the franchise is production designer Mark Hofeling, who previously designed the sets for the entire ''Descendants'' franchise.<ref name="announcement" />
*This is the first ''Descendants'' film to air on Disney+ in contrast to the other films in the franchise.
*This is the first adaptation of the ''Descendants'' franchise to feature new characters instead of the original ones.
*Brandy reprises her role as Cinderella from the 1997 television production of ''[[Rodgers & Hammerstein's Cinderella]]''.
