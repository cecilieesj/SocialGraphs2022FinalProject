'''''The Art of Keeping Cool''''' is a live-action short promoting the [[Walt Disney World Resort]] starring [[Goofy]].

==Synopsis==
[[Goofy]] embarks on a guided tour of locations where guests can cool off during the summer season at the Walt Disney World Resort.

==Trivia==
