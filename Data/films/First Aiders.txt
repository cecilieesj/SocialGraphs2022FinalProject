}}'''''First Aiders''''' is an animated short starring [[Pluto]], [[Minnie Mouse]], and [[Figaro]].

==Synopsis==
Minnie practices first-aid on Pluto, to Figaro's delight and amusement.

==Characters==
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Minnie Mouse]] (voiced by [[Ruth Clifford]])
*[[Figaro]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
