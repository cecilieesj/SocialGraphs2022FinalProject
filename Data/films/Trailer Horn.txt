'''''Trailer Horn''''' is a [[1950]] animated short starring [[Donald Duck]] along with [[Chip and Dale|Chip 'n' Dale]].

==Plot==
Chip 'n' Dale come across Donald camping in the woods and amuse themselves by bouncing on Donald's car horn. Donald, however, is not amused but tries to enjoy his holiday, anyway and the usual war ensues. In the end, it's Donald's car that gets the worst of it!

==''Have a Laugh!'' changes==
The following scenes were deleted from the short release due to time constraints:
*Chip discovering one of Donald's webfoot prints in the ground and paces across it.
*Chip shaking Dale out of the tree and telling him about the webfoot prints, then forcing Dale along with him to see it when Dale is dismissive of this.
*The chipmunks seeing Donald asleep, only for him to be awakened by them jumping on his car's horn, honking.
*Donald giving one of the chipmunks a noogie, and the other biting him on his foot.
*The chipmunks imitating the act of biting Donald on the foot.
*The chipmunks' first attempt at thwarting Donald jumping off the diving board, by extending it so that he lands on it.
*The chipmunks smashing Donald's cup of coffee with a pine cone.
*Donald repeatedly slamming his car into the chipmunks' tree.
