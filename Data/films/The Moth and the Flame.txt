'''''The Moth and the Flame''''' is a [[Silly Symphonies|Silly Symphony]] that was released on [[April 1]], [[1938]].

==Synopsis==
A swarm of moths head out to "Ye Olde Costume Shop" through the window, and live high on the hog.

==Characters==
*Moth
*The Flame
