'''''Billposters''''' is a [[Donald Duck|Donald]] and [[Goofy]] cartoon that was released on [[May 17]], [[1940]].

==Synopsis==
Donald Duck and Goofy attempt to post bills; Goofy gets into it with a windmill while Donald has it out with an angry goat.

==Voice Cast==
*Danny Webb - [[Goofy]]<ref name="Canemaker">{{cite book|author=Canemaker, John|year=2006|title=Paper Dreams: The Art And Artists Of Disney Storyboards|publisher=Disney Edition|page=86|isbn=978-0786863075}}</ref>
*[[Clarence Nash]] - [[Donald Duck]]

== Trivia==
*At the beginning of the short, Donald and Goofy can be heard singing "[[Whistle While You Work]]" from ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]''.
*Although the original theatrical poster states the cartoon as a Donald Duck cartoon, it was actually released as a Donald & Goofy cartoon.
