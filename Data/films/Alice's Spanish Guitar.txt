'''''Alice's Spanish Guitar''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
[[Pete]] once again kidnaps Alice as she plays guitar in a Spanish cafe. [[Julius the Cat|Julius]], once again, comes swashbuckling to the rescue.
