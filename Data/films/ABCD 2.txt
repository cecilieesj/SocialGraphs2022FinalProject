'''''ABCD 2''''' is a [[2015]] Indian 3D dance film, directed by Remo D'Souza and produced by Siddharth Roy Kapur and [[Walt Disney Pictures]]. The film stars Varun Dhawan and Prabhu Deva in lead roles with Lauren Gottlieb, Raghav Juyal, and Dharmesh Yelande in supporting roles. It is the sequel to the 2013 film ''[[ABCD: Any Body Can Dance]]''. ''ABCD 2'' was released on [[June 19]], 2015.

== Premise==
The film will explore the real life struggle of choreographers Vishnu and Suresh from [Vasai] (Mumbai suburb), who went on to win the World Hip-Hop dance championship.

== Release ==
The film has been released by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]] on June 19, 2015. However, the film's choreographer & director Remo D'Souza wanted to pre-release it on June 18, 2015 due to the beginning of the Muslim holy month of Ramadan. He stated, ''We just come to know that the Holy month of Ramazan starts on June 19, the day we have scheduled the release of ABCD2. I've been receiving so many emails and messages from my Muslim friends requesting not to release on that day, as they would be unable to go for the film.''
