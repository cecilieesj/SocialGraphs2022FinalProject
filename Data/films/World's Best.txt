'''''World's Best''''' is an upcoming American musical comedy film. It is set to be released on [[Disney+]] in [[2023]].

==Cast==
*Manny Magnus as Prem
*[[Utkarsh Ambudkar]] as Suresh
*Punam Patel as Priya

==Production==
On April 21, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[Disney+]] was developing a musical comedy film with Roshan Sethi directing. Filming is scheduled to begin in May.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/04/ghosts-utkarsh-ambudkar-rap-musical-comedy-feature-disney-1235007534/ ‘Ghosts’ Star Utkarsh Ambudkar Sets Rap Musical Comedy Feature At Disney+]</ref>
