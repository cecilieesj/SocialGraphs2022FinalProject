}}'''''Africa Before Dark''''' is an [[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit]] animated short released on [[February 20]], [[1928]].
This was one of many Oswald shorts long thought to be lost. In 2009, a fragment of ''Africa Before Dark'' was found and by 2015 a full copy was found in a German film archive and was shown at a screening at Disneyland on January 14, 2015.<ref>[http://www.laughingplace.com/w/articles/2015/01/21/wednesdays-with-walt-oswald-the-lucky-rabbit/ Laughing Place entree for "Wednesdays With Walt: Oswald the Lucky Rabbit"]</ref>

==Synopsis==
Oswald goes big game hunting in Africa. He tries to capture a tiger cub before having a run-in with a ferocious lion.
