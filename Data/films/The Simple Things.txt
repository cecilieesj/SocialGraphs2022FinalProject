'''''The Simple Things''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' cartoon that was released on on [[April 18|April 18,]] [[1953]], produced by [[Walt Disney]] and directed by [[Charles A. Nichols|Charles Nichols]]. This cartoon is notable for being the last short for Mickey Mouse theatrical cartoon series.
==Synopsis==
When Mickey and [[Pluto]] go to the beach for a relaxing fishing trip, they have to deal with a crabby clam and a greedy seagull.

==''Have a Laugh!'' changes==
The following scenes were deleted from the short release due to time constraints:
*The opening shot is halved, cutting out the seagulls flying away as Mickey and Pluto pass by.
*Pluto's encounter with a starfish.
*The clam hiding at the sight of Pluto.
*Pluto struggling to get the clam out of his mouth.
*Pluto, in trying to explain he has a clam in his mouth, Mickey interprets that as him wanting to something to eat and giving him a sausage, which the clam eats instead, after which it spits water in Mickey's face.
*Mickey giving Pluto another sausage, only for the seagull to eat it instead.
*The seagull's first attempt at catching one of Mickey's fish used for bait, resulting in it getting hit on a rock.
*The entire scene with Mickey and Pluto's collective struggle with the seagull; it cuts from the seagull hovering over Mickey and Pluto to it diving into the bucket of bait.
*Mickey throwing a rock into the bucket of bait as the seagull flies off with it so that it weighs down the bucket.
*The seagull telling the other seagulls about the "free fresh fish".
*Pluto barking at the seagulls as they come to attack Mickey and Pluto, before Mickey decides they should beat a retreat.

==Trivia==
*This was the last Mickey Mouse cartoon to be produced by [[Walt Disney]] himself and also the last to feature [[Jimmy MacDonald]] as the voice of the lead character.
*This would be the last Mickey Mouse cartoon for 30 years, until [[1983]]'s ''[[Mickey's Christmas Carol]]'' was released.
*This is one of the few Mickey Mouse cartoons to feature an original song called "[[The Simple Things (song)|The Simple Things]]".
