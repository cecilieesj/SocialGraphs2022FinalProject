'''''The Bikeriders''''' is an upcoming American drama film written and directed by Jeff Nichols.

==Synopsis==
Depicts the evolution of a motorcycle club in Midwestern United States over the course of decades, as it evolves into a more menacing organization from its more earnest early days.

==Production==
On August 4, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that that Jeff Nichols would write and direct the film, which will feature an ensemble cast that first announced [[Jodie Comer]], Austin Butler and Tom Hardy as part of it. Michael Shannon, Boyd Holbrook and Damon Herriman were added to the cast later in the month.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/08/jodie-comer-austin-butler-tom-hardy-jeff-nichols-new-regency-1235084092/ Jodie Comer, Austin Butler & Tom Hardy To Lead Ensemble For Jeff Nichols’ ‘The Bikeriders’ At New Regency]</ref><ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/08/michael-shannon-boyd-holbrook-damon-herriman-jeff-nichols-the-bikeriders-new-regency-1235094214/ Michael Shannon, Boyd Holbrook and Damon Herriman Latest To Join Jeff Nichols ‘The Bikeriders’ At New Regency]</ref>

Filming is expected to take place in Cincinnati in late 2022.<ref>[https://www.citybeat.com/arts/tom-hardy-austin-butler-and-jodie-comer-to-begin-filming-new-movie-in-cincinnati-13627462 Tom Hardy, Austin Butler and Jodie Comer to Begin Filming New Movie in Cincinnati]</ref>
