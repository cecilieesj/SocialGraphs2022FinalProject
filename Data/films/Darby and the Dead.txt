'''''Darby and the Dead''''' (formally titled '''''Darby Harper Wants You to Know''''') is an upcoming American teen horror comedy film. It is produced by [[20th Century Studios]] and Footprint Features and will be released on [[Hulu]] on [[December 2]], [[2022]].

==Synopsis==
The story follows Darby Harper, who after suffering a near-death experience as a child is granted the ability to see ghosts. To combat the existential boredom of high school, she runs a side business counseling local spirits in her spare time. When an unexpected occurrence happens between Darby and Capri, the most popular girl at her high school, Darby reluctantly agrees to help her and in the process learns how to fit in with the living world again.<ref name="TheWrap">[https://www.thewrap.com/riele-downs-aulicravahlo-asher-angel-chosen-jacobs-derek-luke-darby-harper-wants-you-to-know/ Riele Downs, Auli’i’ Cravalho, Asher Angel, Chosen Jacobs & Derek Luke Join ‘Darby Harper Wants You to Know’ (Exclusive)]</ref>

==Production==
On October 22, 2021, ''Deadline'' reported that [[20th Century Studios]] acquired the rights to the film and [[Storm Reid]] was initially cast as Darby Harper, but later dropped out of the project due to a scheduling conflict.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/10/20th-century-darby-harper-wants-you-to-know-strom-reid-1234860836/ 20th Century Lands Rights To ‘Darby Harper Wants You To Know’ Starring Storm Reid]</ref>

On February 10, 2022, ''The Wrap'' reported the main cast and crew members of the film, with Riele Downs replacing Reid. The film has already started production.<ref name="TheWrap" />
