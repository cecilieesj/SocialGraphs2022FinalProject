== Trivia ==
*This is the first Disney Channel Original Movie to be a remake of another DCOM.
*It got added to [[Disney+]] on [[October 8|October 8,]] [[2021]], just a week after the premiere on [[Disney Channel]].
*This is the first [[Disney Channel Original Movies|Disney Channel Original Movie]] to feature LGBTQ+ representation with Amy being a child of a gay divorce with her dads.
*Thriii's cover of [[China Anne McClain]]'s hit song "Calling All the Monsters" from the ''[[A.N.T. Farm]]'' episode "mutANT farm" was playing during the Halloween party.
*There was a dog dressed up as Shrimpy from ''[[Zombies (film)|Zombies]]''.
*At the Halloween carnival, there were two kids dressed up as [[Zed]] and [[Addison Wells|Addison]] from ''[[Zombies (film)|Zombies]]''.
