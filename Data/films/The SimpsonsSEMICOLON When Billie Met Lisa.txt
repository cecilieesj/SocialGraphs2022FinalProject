'''''The Simpsons: When Billie Met Lisa''''' is a [[Disney+]] short. It premiered on [[April 22]], [[2022]].

In “When Billie Met Lisa,” Lisa Simpson is discovered by chart-topping artists Billie Eilish and FINNEAS while searching for a quiet place to practice her saxophone. Billie invites Lisa to her studio for a special jam session she’ll never forget.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://faroutmagazine.co.uk/billie-eilish-guest-the-simpsons/|title=Billie Eilish to make guest appearance on 'The Simpsons'|website=Far Out Magazine|date=[[April 15]], [[2022]]}}</ref>

==Trivia==
*At the end of the short, one of the still images features Disney characters such as [[Ursula]] from ''[[The Little Mermaid]]'', [[Minnie Mouse]], [[Goofy]], [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]] from the [[Cinderella (1950 film)|film of the same name]], [[Gaston]] from ''[[Beauty and the Beast (1991 film)|Beauty and the Beast]]'', and [[Tweedle Dee and Tweedle Dum]] from ''[[Alice in Wonderland (1951 film)|Alice in Wonderland]]'' can be seen dancing with, Homer, Marge, Bart, and Selma and Patty respectively with Lisa, Billie Eilish, and FINNEAS performing on stage together.
