}}'''''Pest of the West''''' is a [[Pluto]] cartoon. The plotline of the cartoon short is a parody of the story, which is loosely based on the fairy tale ''The Wolf and the Seven Young Kids'' by the Brothers Grimm, which is in turn based on Aesop's fable "''The Dog and the Sheep''".
==Synopsis==
Pluto guards the chicken house from a hungry coyote and his hungrier son.
