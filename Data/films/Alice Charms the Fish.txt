'''''Alice Charms the Fish''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Alice and Julius go fishing where Alice charms the fish ashore with a tune from her flute.
