'''''Hungry Hobos''''' is an [[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit (series)|Oswald the Lucky Rabbit]] short that was first released on [[May 14]], [[1928]]. It was rediscovered in 2011.

==Summary==
The film follows the misadventures of [[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit|Oswald]] and his roughneck pal, Putrid [[Pete]], as they ride a freight car loaded with animals and devise ways to cook a meal using the tools at hand. When a policeman chases them off the train, the duo poses as a hurdy-gurdy man and a monkey as part of their escape.

==Purchase by Disney==
On March 23, 2012, Warren Spector confirmed that the "anonymous bidder" that bought the short for $31,250 was Disney. When Warren was asked if the short would appear in ''[[Epic Mickey 2: The Power of Two]]'',Â Warren refused to comment.

==Trivia==
* ''Hungry Hobos'' was rediscovered in the collection of the Huntley Film Archives in Herefordshire, England in November 2011.
* The short was auctioned off in December 2011 to an anonymous bidder which Warren Spector later revealed was [[The Walt Disney Company]].
* This short premiered exactly a day before the first preview screenings of ''[[Plane Crazy]]''.
* The short was released as a [[Disney Movies Anywhere]] exclusive short on the ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]'' page.
