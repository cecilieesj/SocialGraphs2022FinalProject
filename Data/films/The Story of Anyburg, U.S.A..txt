'''''The Story of Anyburg, U.S.A.''''' is an animated Disney short that was originally released to theaters on [[June 19]], [[1957]]. Just like ''[[Susie the Little Blue Coupe]]'', it was the inspiration for the design of the characters in Disney/Pixar's ''[[Cars]]'', with the eyes on the windshield.

==Synopsis==
The problem of traffic are examined by the city of Anyburg in judicial court as several automobiles are tried for various crimes and declared "not guilty," since it is the people who drive them who must admit their guilt and promise to drive carefully.
