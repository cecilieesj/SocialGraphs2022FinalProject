'''''Mickey's Mechanical Man''''' is a black and white ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short released on [[June 17]], [[1933]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey builds a robot named [[Champ]] to fight against [[Beppo the Gorilla]], the Kongo Killer, in a boxing match. Champ doesn't work as well as Mickey expects him to, however, and he only manages to defeat Beppo because [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie's]] car horn has the power to invigorate and resuscitate him.

This cartoon was featured in ''[[Epic Mickey]]'' as a transition level.

== Trivia ==

* This cartoon was technically the last to use the "Minnie's Yoo Hoo" theme in the opening titles. All subsequent shorts used more original fanfares relating to the short in the opening titles.
