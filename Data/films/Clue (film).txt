'''''Clue''''' is an upcoming American comedy mystery film, directed by [[James Bobin]] and written by Rhett Reese and Paul Wernick. It is based on the board game of the {{WPS|Cluedo|same name}}.

==Cast==
*[[Ryan Reynolds]] as TBA

==Production==
On January 11, 2018, ''Variety'' reported that that a new adaptation of the popular Hasbro board game was in development at 20th Century Fox, with Ryan Reynolds producing while Rhett Reese and Paul Wernick write the screenplay.<ref>[https://variety.com/2018/film/news/ryan-reynolds-clue-movie-fox-1202672829/ Ryan Reynolds Signs First-Look Deal at Fox With ‘Clue’ Movie in the Works]</ref>

On February 10, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[James Bobin]] had been hired to direct the film.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2020/02/james-bobin-clue-movie-ryan-reynolds-1202856873/ James Bobin In Talks To Direct ‘Clue’ Movie At 20th Century]</ref>
