'''''Alice's Circus Daze''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]]. This is the first time that Lois Hardwick played the role of Alice.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius as circus acrobats, up on the high wire, and taming the lions.
