'''''Noah's Ark''''' is a 20-minute [[Disney]] short originally released theatrically to theaters on [[November 10]], [[1959]] by Buena Vista Film Distribution with ''[[Third Man on the Mountain]]''. It was a retelling of the Biblical story of Noah and the Ark with Disney's first use of stop-motion animation.
