'''''Jungle Rhythm''''' is a [[Mickey Mouse|''Mickey Mouse'']] cartoon that was released on [[November 15]], [[1929]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey travels through a jungle riding an elephant and plays music with the animals.

==Notes==
*The film's copyright was renewed on [[December 31]], [[1956]].<ref>[https://archive.org/details/catalogofcopyrig3101213li/page/120/mode/1up Catalog of Copyright Entries (1956 renewals)]</ref>
*This short was featured as a transition level in ''[[Epic Mickey]]''.
