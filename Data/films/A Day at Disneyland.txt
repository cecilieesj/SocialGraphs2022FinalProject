'''''A Day at Disneyland''''' is a [[1982]] souvenir film about [[Disneyland]].

==Synopsis==
===Summary===
Viewers are taken on a tour of Disneyland.

===Plot===
This video takes viewers on a tour of Disneyland, as they watch their favorite Disney characters experience the many rides, shops, restaurants and attractions there.

*[[Tony Anselmo]] - Donald Duck
*[[James Avery]] - Br'er Bear
*[[Kathryn Beaumont]] - Alice
*[[Mary Kay Bergman]] - Snow White
*[[Corey Burton]] - Captain Hook, Dale, Mad Hatter, Mr. Smee
*[[Bill Farmer]] - Goofy
*[[J.D. Hall]] - Br'er Fox
*[[Tress MacNeille]] - Chip
*[[Diane Michelle]] - Daisy Duck
*[[Russi Taylor]] - Minnie Mouse

==Trivia==
*A remake of this video was released in [[1991]] with the then-current voice cast consisting of [[Wayne Allwine]] ([[Mickey Mouse]]), [[Russi Taylor]] ([[Minnie Mouse]]), [[Tony Anselmo]] ([[Donald Duck]]) and [[Bill Farmer]] ([[Goofy]] and [[Pluto]]). Clips from more recently added attractions such as [[Splash Mountain]], [[Star Tours]], [[Mickey's Toontown (Disneyland)|Mickey's Toontown]], and [[Fantasmic!]] are also shown.
**Some of the clips of the original were also recycled in the [[1990]] video ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Disneyland Fun]]''.
