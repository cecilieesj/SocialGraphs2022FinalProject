'''''Alice's Three Bad Eggs''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Alice and Julius protect their western fort from the Three Bad Eggs and a band of attacking Indians.
