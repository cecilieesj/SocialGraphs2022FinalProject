An '''Untitled female ''Pirates of the Caribbean'' film''' is a cancelled American action fantasy film. It was intended as a spin-off of the [[Pirates of the Caribbean]] franchise.

==Cast==
*Margot Robbie

==Production==
On June 26, 2020, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that a female centric [[Pirates of the Caribbean]] film was in development, with Margot Robbie in the lead role.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/margot-robbie-christina-hodson-team-new-pirates-caribbean-movie-1298722/ Margot Robbie, Christina Hodson Reteam for New ‘Pirates of the Caribbean’ Movie for Disney (Exclusive)]</ref>

On November 14, 2022, in an interview with ''Vanity Fair'', Margot Robbie revealed that the film is no longer in development.<ref>[https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2022/11/margot-robbie-cover-babylon-star-on-navigating-hollywood Margot Robbie Is Nobody’s Barbie: The Babylon Star on Navigating Hollywood]</ref>
