'''''Alice Picks the Champ''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius visit Pete's gym where he takes on all comers in the boxing ring. Julius, of course, gets into the ring with him in a no holds barred boxing match.
