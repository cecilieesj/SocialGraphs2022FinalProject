|release = [[1955]]}}'''''You and Your Sense of Touch''''' is a "You and Your..." short that originally aired on ''[[The Mickey Mouse Club]]''. It was updated in the early 1990s.

==Synopsis==
[[Jiminy Cricket]] explains the sense of touch and how it is actually four sensations all together: warmth, cold, pressure and pain.

==Trivia==
* A scene from this short has been reused in ''[[Disney Sing Along Songs: Circle of Life]]'' (where Jiminy introduces ''[[Everybody Wants to Be a Cat]]'' from ''[[The Aristocats]]'').
