'''''Shanghaied''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[January 13]], [[1934]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey and Minnie have been kidnapped by Pete and his pirate crew and must devise a way to escape from confinement and defeat the pirates.

==Characters==
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Walt Disney]])
*[[Minnie Mouse]] (voiced by [[Marcellite Garner]])
*[[Pete]] (voiced by [[Billy Bletcher]])
*Pete's pirate crew (one crew member imitating female voice, voiced by [[Elvia Allman]])

==Trivia==
*This short is notable for being the first short to have [[Billy Bletcher]] as the voice of Pete.
*The basic plot of this short was expanded by [[Floyd Gottfredson]] for the ''Mickey Mouse'' comic strip storyline "[[The Captive Castaways]]".
*The music that plays during Mickey and Pete's duel and during the ending was later used in ''[[Thru the Mirror]]''.
*A clip of Mickey and Pete's duel was used for the scene of [[Goofy]] pardoning other people while he's watching in the theater in the ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Disneyland]]'' episode "[[The Goofy Success Story]]".
*This short appears in ''[[Epic Mickey]]'' as one of the projector levels.
*Minnie's screaming and Pete's evil laughter were later reused for ''[[Get A Horse!]]''
