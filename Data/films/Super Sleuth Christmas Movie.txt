'''''Super Sleuth Christmas Movie''''' is a [[2007]] direct-to-video Christmas film featuring the characters of ''[[My Friends Tigger & Pooh]]''. It was first released to DVD on [[November 12]], [[2007]] in the [[United Kingdom]], and on [[November 20]] in the [[United States]].

''Super Sleuth Christmas Movie'' introduces [[Santa Claus]]'s reindeer-trainee, [[Holly (Reindeer)|Holly]], who is discovered trapped in a bush in the [[Hundred Acre Wood]] by [[Roo]] and [[Lumpy]]. After they rescue her, she reveals that she is searching for Santa's special magic bag in which he keeps all of the Christmas toys. The Super Sleuths find the bag and then together most of the members of Wood set out on a quest to the North Pole to return the lost bag to Santa.
