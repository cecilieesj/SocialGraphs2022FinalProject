'''''Canvas Back Duck''''' is a [[Donald Duck|''Donald Duck'']] short.

==Synopsis==
Donald's pride gets him into trouble again when he is lured into the boxing ring after proving his prowess at various circus games and his opponent turns out to be dangerous professional heavyweightÂ Pee Wee Pete.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]] (voiced by Clarence Nash)
*[[Pete]] (voiced by [[Billy Bletcher]])
*Referee (voiced by Unknown)
*Audience Member (voiced by Unknown)

== Trivia ==
* This cartoon marks Pete's final pairing with Donald Duck in the Golden Age of American animation; they would never face each other again until [[Music Store Donald]] in 2001.
* This is the only cartoon to have Pete, Donald, and Huey, Dewey, and Louie pairing together, until 1983 in [[Mickey's Christmas Carol]].
*While Pete is talking with his ringside announcer when the nephews spy on him, he uses his old "big-eyes" design. During his match with Donald, he uses his newer design seen in many of his appearances in the late 40's through early 50's.
