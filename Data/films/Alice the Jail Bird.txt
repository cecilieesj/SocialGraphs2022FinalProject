'''''Alice the Jail Bird''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
After stealing a pie, Alice and Julius are put on a chain gang, where they escape with the help of a friendly ostrich.
