'''''Lady Gaga & the Muppets' Holiday Spectacular''''' is a Christmas special starring [[Lady Gaga]] and [[the Muppets]]. The 90-minute special first aired on Thanksgiving night, Thursday, [[November 28]], [[2013]] on [[ABC]].

Filmed in [[Los Angeles]], the special features Gaga performing her song, "Applause," as well as a duet with [[Kermit the Frog]]. [[The Swedish Chef]], [[Animal]] and [[Beaker]] also performed versions of "Jingle Bells" and "Deck the Halls." Other guest stars include [[Elton John]], [[Joseph Gordon-Levitt]], [[Kristen Bell]], and [[RuPaul]].

The special also features a sneak peek of the [[2014]] film, ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]'', which features a cameo by Lady Gaga.
