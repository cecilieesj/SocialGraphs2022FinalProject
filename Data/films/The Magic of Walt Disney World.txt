|time = 29 minutes}}'''''The Magic of Walt Disney World''''' is a [[1972]] short film promoting [[Walt Disney World Resort|Walt Disney World]]. It was originally released to theaters by [[Buena Vista Distribution]] on [[December 22]], 1972 alongside ''[[Snowball Express]]'' and ''[[Saludos Amigos]]''. An expanded and updated version aired on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' in [[1974]].

==Synopsis==
A look at the many attractions, resort hotels, and other amusements at Walt Disney World in its first year of operation.
