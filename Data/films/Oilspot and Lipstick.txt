'''''Oilspot and Lipstick''''' is a computer-generated animated short film screened at the SIGGRAPH conference held in Anaheim, California in [[1987]]. It was the earliest animated film from Disney that used computer animation. The group that made the short film called themselves the "Late Night Movie Group".

The film has not been made available to the general public.

==Summary==
An experimental film from Disney's first computer graphics artists tells the story of Oilspot, a mechanical junkyard dog made of spare parts, who mistakenly wakes a junk monster. When the monster grabs his girlfriend, Lipstick, he rushes to her rescue.
