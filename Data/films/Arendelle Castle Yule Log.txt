'''''Arendelle Castle Yule Log''''' is an animated program that premiered on [[Disney+]] on [[December 13]], [[2019]]. It features [[Olaf]] and the fireplace in the library of [[Arendelle]] [[Arendelle Castle|Castle]] in the background.

A new version, ''Arendelle Castle Yule Log: Cut Paper Edition,'' was released on Disney+ on December 17, 2021:
<blockquote>
''Hand-crafted from paper art by Brittney Lee, Walt Disney Animation Studios’ acclaimed visual development artist on “[[Frozen]]” and “[[Frozen 2]],” this warm and inviting hearthside scene sets a festive holiday mood. Stylized cut-out paper flames combine with imaginative visual effects (including a touch of snow provided by Elsa) and silhouetted images of Anna, Elsa, Olaf, Kristoff and Sven to add to your holiday fun and enjoyment.''
</blockquote>

==Trivia==
*The film replays for 3 hours.
*This is the first ''Frozen'' production not to have [[Anna]], [[Elsa]], [[Kristoff]], or [[Sven]] in it. [[Olaf]] is the only character from the franchise to appear.
