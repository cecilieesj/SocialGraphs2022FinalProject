"'''The B.R.A.T. Patrol'''" is an episode of ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|The Disney Sunday Movie]]''. It originally aired on [[ABC]] on [[October 26]], [[1986]].

==Synopsis==
A group of military brats uncovers a plot to steal base equipment.
