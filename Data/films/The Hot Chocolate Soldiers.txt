'''''The Hot Chocolate Soldiers''''' is an animated segment of MGM's feature film ''[[Hollywood Party]]'', which was released on [[June 1]], [[1934]]. The segment was introduced by [[Mickey Mouse]], who harassed Jimmy Durante in the film and plays the piano.

==Synopsis==
A group of confectionery soldiers go to war against a neighboring cookie castle.

==Trivia==
*One of the primary animators of this short was Ben Sharpsteen, who would be promoted to director not long after making this short and make the Silly Symphony ''[[The Cookie Carnival]]'' similar in style to this work.
