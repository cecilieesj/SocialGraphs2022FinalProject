'''''Once on This Island''''' is an upcoming [[Disney+]] original film, based on the Broadway musical of the same name.

==Plot==
A peasant girl falls in love with aristocrat. Against this backdrop, social class differences play out while the island gods wager a bet of what is stronger, love or death.
