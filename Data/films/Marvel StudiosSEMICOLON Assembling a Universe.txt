|language = English}}'''''Marvel Studios: Assembling a Universe''''' is a television special that document the history of [[Marvel Studios]] and the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], and includes exclusive interviews and behind-the-scenes footage from all of the films, [[Marvel One-Shots|One-Shots]] and ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'', and sneak peeks of ''[[Avengers: Age of Ultron]]'', ''[[Captain America: The Winter Soldier]]'', ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy]]'', and unaired episodes of ''Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.''. The special aired on [[March 18]], [[2014]] on [[ABC]].
