'''''Alice Wins the Derby''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
A combination auto race and horse derby. Pete tries to send Julius on a false detour, but Julius comes from behind to win the race!
