'''''Four Artists Paint One Tree''''' is a [[1958]] Disney educational animated film. It was originally broadcast on [[ABC]]'s ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Disneyland]]'''s "[[An Adventure in Art]]" sub-series.

==Synopsis==
Four Disney artists - [[Marc Davis]], [[Eyvind Earle]], Joshua Meador, and Walt Peregoy - using different media, paint the same subject. Each artist explains his approach and his interpretation.
