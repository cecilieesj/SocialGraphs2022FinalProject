'''''Disney's Noah''''' is a [[1998]] television film directed by Ken Kwapis. The movie premiered on [[October 11]], 1998 and stars [[Tony Danza]] as a modern-day carpenter tasked with building an ark. It also stars [[Wallace Shawn]] and Jane Sibbett.

==Plot==
In order to save his family and home town, carpenter Norman Waters ([[Tony Danza]]) is tasked by an angel named Zach ([[Wallace Shawn]]) to rebuild Noah's Ark in 40 days to prepare for a great massive flood.
