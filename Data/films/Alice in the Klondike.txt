'''''Alice in the Klondike''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Alice and Julius strike gold while prospecting in the Klondike and need to protect it from a thieving Pete.
