'''''Alice's Auto Race''''' is a [[1927]] animated short film by MJ Winkler Productions, and as part of the [[Alice Comedies]]. This was Margie Gay's final role as Alice before she was replaced by Lois Hardwick for the rest of the series.

==Summary==
A madcap auto race. [[Pete]] tries to cheat by switching some road signs, but [[Julius the Cat|Julius]] as usual comes in a winner.
