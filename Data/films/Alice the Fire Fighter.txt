'''''Alice the Fire Fighter''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Many comic misadventures ensue as Alice and Julius attempt to put out a fire at a local hotel.
