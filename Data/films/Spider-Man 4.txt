'''''Spider-Man 4''''' is a placeholder title for an upcoming superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero [[Spider-Man|of the same name]]. The film is a sequel to ''[[Spider-Man: Homecoming]]'', ''[[Spider-Man: Far From Home]]'', and ''[[Spider-Man: No Way Home]]''. It is expected to be part of Phase Six or Seven of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. It will be produced by [[Marvel Studios]] and distributed by [[Sony Pictures Entertainment]].<ref name="NewTrilogy">[https://www.fandango.com/movie-news/spider-man-no-way-home-producer-amy-pascal-reveals-more-about-the-historic-film-and-confirms-tom-hollands-future-as-spider-man-754461 'Spider-Man: No Way Home' Producer Amy Pascal Reveals More About the Historic Film and Confirms Tom Holland's Future as Spider-Man]</ref>

==Production==
On November 29, 2021, in an interview with ''Fandango'', Amy Pascal confirmed that a new trilogy set in the Marvel Cinematic Universe is in development with Holland reprising his role, in an extended deal with [[Marvel Studios]].<ref name="NewTrilogy" />

==Trivia==
*This will be the third film to make a superhero trilogy into a tetralogy; first being ''[[Thor: Love and Thunder]]'' and second being ''[[Captain America: New World Order]]''.
*This will be the first Spider-Man movie in history to be the fourth installment in its respective series.
*[[Vincent D'Onofrio]] hopes to appear in this movie as the villain [[Kingpin]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedirect.com/article/spider-man-4-kingpin-vincent-donofrio|title=
Kingpin's Vincent D'Onofrio Reveals Spider-Man 4 Hopes|website=The Direct|date=January 29, 2022|access-date=January 29, 2022}}</ref>
*On [[May 17]], 2022, [[Tom Holland]] was asked if he would return as [[Spider-Man]] and he replied with "Maybe one day" with a smirk, hinting he had signed a new contract.<ref>[https://mobile.twitter.com/tomhollandsdaya/status/1526647090494963712 (Video) Tom Holland is Asked About His Return as Spider-Man]</ref>
