}}'''''Big Business''''' is a [[1988]] American comedy film starring [[Bette Midler]] and [[Lily Tomlin]]. It is the story of two sets of twins who were mismatched at birth, resulting in one being raised by a poor family and the other by a wealthy family.

==Trivia==
* The cost of building a set, recreating the Plaza Hotel, proved so high, that Disney decided to re-use it to recoup the money. This resulted in the short-lived sitcom ''The Nutt House''. The show starred [[Harvey Korman]] and [[Cloris Leachman]].
