|imdb_id = 0206446}}'''''Yellowstone Cubs''''' is a [[1963]] Disney short film. It was originally released to theaters by [[Buena Vista Distribution]] on [[June 1]], 1963 alongside both ''[[Savage Sam]]'' and ''[[Summer Magic]]''.

Digitally remastered in widescreen, it is the only live-action Disney featurette to retain its Buena Vista logo on digital download along withÂ ''[[Frankenweenie (short)|Frankenweenie]]''.

==Synopsis==
Two bear cubs, Tuffy and Tubby, are separated from their mother Nokomis and spend an entire summer romping through Yellowstone National Park. In the meantime, the mother bear follows their trail as she searches for them, with the three bears finally converging in the kitchens of the Old Faithful Inn.
