'''''Save U''''' was a planned sequel to the [[2005]] Disney live-action superhero comedy film, ''[[Sky High]]''.

==Production==
In November of [[2016]], it was announced that Disney is developing a sequel, and that the film is in early development stages.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehollywoodnews.com/2016/10/17/exclusive-writer-revealed-dreamworks-shrek-5-sky-high-2-coming/|title=Exclusive: Story writer revealed for Dreamworks’ ‘Shrek 5’ – ‘Sky High 2’ coming?|publisher=The Hollywood News|last=Heath|first=Paul|date=October 17, 2016}}</ref> In January 2019, Mike Mitchell revealed about earlier plans to make a sequel; but due to the film's box-office performance, nothing came to be. The sequel would have been called ''Save U'' (as in Save University) and would have featured the characters graduating high school and attending college.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://io9.gizmodo.com/the-unrealized-sky-high-sequel-could-have-been-called-s-1832054122|title=The Unrealized Sky High Sequel Could Have Been Called Save University|website=io9|last=Lussier|first=Germain|date=January 25, 2019|accessdate=January 26, 2019}}</ref>
