'''''1001 Nights''''' is an upcoming American film written by Arash Amel, based on the collection of folk tales of the same name.

==Production==
On October 10, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that a live-action ''1001 Nights'' film was in development with Arash Amel writing the screenplay, and would also serve as an executive producer.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/10/disney-fantasy-sci-fi-movie-arabian-nights-1001-rise-arash-amel-1235139732/ ‘One Thousand And One Nights’ Fantasy & Sci-Fi Reimagining In Works At Disney; ‘Rise’ Scribe Arash Amel To Pen Movie]</ref>
