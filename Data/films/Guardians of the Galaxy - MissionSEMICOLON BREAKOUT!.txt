| Plot                    = The [[Guardians of the Galaxy]] have been captured by [[Collector|The Collector]] to become the newest prized attraction in the [[Collector's Museum|The Collector’s Fortress]]. However, [[Rocket Raccoon|Rocket]] was able to escape. With the help of [[Earth-1218#Residents|numerous unnamed Terrans]], Rocket was able to hack into the Collector's system, take over the gantry lifts, free the other Guardians, and play music from Star-Lord's [[Awesome Mix]].

After being freed, the Guardians were picked up by [[Mantis]] in the [[Milano]].

| Trivia                  = * The attraction originally opened as the [[Wikipedia:The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror|''The Twilight Zone'' Tower of Terror]] in 2004 before being rethemed in 2017.
* Despite the ride's soundtrack, there are six songs:
** One featured in Shrek Forever After
** One featured in Undercover Brother
** One featured in Rayman Raving Rabbids: TV Party
** One featured at the end of Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)
** One featured in Henchmen (2018)
** One featured in the trailer of Planes: Fire & Rescue
* Lead Imagineer Joe Rohde worked with [[James Gunn]], [[Kevin Feige]], [[Joe Quesada|Joe Quesada]], [[Tyler Bates]], and many others in developing the story experience of the attraction.
* This attraction was the second Disney attraction to be based on a Marvel property, following [[Iron Man Experience]], which opened at [[Wikipedia:Hong Kong Disneyland|Hong Kong Disneyland]] in January 2017.
