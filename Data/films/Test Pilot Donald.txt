'''''Test Pilot Donald''''' is a [[1951]] [[Donald Duck|''Donald Duck'']] and [[Chip and Dale|''Chip and Dale'']] animated short.

==Synopsis==
Dale dreams of flying and finds a perfect opportunity when Donald's remote control airplane gets stuck in their tree.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Chip and Dale]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]] and [[Dessie Flynn]])

==Trivia==
*The ''Walt Disney Cartoon Classics: The Continuing Adventures of Chip & Dale with Doanld Duck'' VHS uses the original opening and closing titles of this cartoon. They were used on Disney Channel and Toon Disney airings up until 2002. The original titles were restored for the ''Walt Disney Treasures: The Chronological Donald, Vol. 4'' DVD.
