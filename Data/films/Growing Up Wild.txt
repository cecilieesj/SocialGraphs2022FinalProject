'''''Growing Up Wild''''' is a [[2016]] [[Disneynature]] documentary film that was released direct to video and streaming.

Narrated by [[Daveed Diggs]], it is a compilation film consisting of footage from previous entries in the Disneynature series including [[African Cats]], [[Bears]], [[Monkey Kingdom]], and [[Chimpanzee]], focusing on the stories of the baby animals from those films.
