|time = 6 minutes}}'''''The Ballad of Nessie''''' is an animated short that was released theatrically with ''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]''. It premiered on [[March 5]], [[2011]] at Anima 2011, an International Animation Film Festival in Brussels, BelgiumÂ before its full theatrical release with ''Pooh''.

The short was directed by Stevie Wermers-Skelton and Kevin Deters and the short itself is narrated by [[Billy Connolly]].

==Summary==
When the lake monster [[Nessie]] is forced out of her home by a greedy developer wanting to turn her home into a miniature golf course, she travels through Scotland to find a new home.

==Voice Cast==
*[[Billy Connolly]] as Narrator, [[MacFroogle]], Seagull, Rat
*Kevin Deters as Tycoon Assistants

==Behind the Scenes==
The look of the film was specifically designed to capture the feel of 1940s and 1950s Disney shorts, particularly ''[[The Adventures of Ichabod and Mr. Toad|The Legend of Sleepy Hollow]]'' and ''[[Melody Time|Johnny Appleseed]]'' along with some influence from the art of [[Mary Blair]] and [[Ward Kimball]].
