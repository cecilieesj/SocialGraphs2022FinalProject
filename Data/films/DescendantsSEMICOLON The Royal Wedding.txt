'''''Descendants: The Royal Wedding''''' is a [[Disney Channel]] animated television special that aired on [[August 13]], [[2021]]. Based on the ''[[Descendants (franchise)|Descendants]]'' franchise, the special brings viewers back to the United States of [[Auradon]] and features [[Mal]] and  [[Ben (Descendants)|Ben's]] royal wedding. The animated television special reached 0.65 million viewers.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thetvratingsguide.com/2021/08/friday-cable-ratings-81321-spin-is.html?m=1|title=Friday Cable Ratings 8/13/21: Spin is Lowest-Rated Disney Channel Original Movie Ever|website=thetvratingdgyide.com|accessdate=2021-08-16}}</ref>

==Trivia==
*Since [[Cameron Boyce]] had passed away on [[July 6]], [[2019]], the absence of [[Carlos De Vil]] was acknowledged in the storyline and Boyce's memory is honored in the Broadcast.
*This special aired right after on the [[Disney Channel Original Movies|Disney Channel Original Movie]], ''[[Spin (film)|Spin]]'' and before the premiere of ''[[Disney's Magic Bake-Off]]''.
**Coincidentally, [[Anna Cathcart]], who voiced [[Dizzy Tremaine]] in the special, had also played Molly in ''Spin''.
*This is currently the final film in the ''Descendants'' franchise to focus on the original cast. The next film, ''[[The Pocketwatch]]'', features a brand new cast.
