'''''The Jungle Book: A Lesson in Accepting Change''''' is a [[1981]] Disney educational live-action/animated short, part of the ''Disney's Animated Classics: Lessons in Living'' series. The short features clips from ''[[The Jungle Book]]''.

==Synopsis==
How change, in friends and environment, can be faced.
