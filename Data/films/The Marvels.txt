'''''The Marvels''''' is an upcoming American superhero film and serves as a sequel to the [[2019]] films ''[[Captain Marvel (film)|Captain Marvel]]'', ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]'' and the [[2022]] [[Disney+]] series ''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]''. It is produced by [[Marvel Studios]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]]. It is the thirty-third film of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] and the third film of Phase Five. It is scheduled to be released on [[July 28]], [[2023]].

==Synopsis==
Marvel Studios’ ''The Marvels'' will feature Brie Larson returning to the role of Captain Marvel/Carol Danvers. In the film, she’ll be joined by Teyonah Parris, who was first introduced as adult Monica Rambeau in Marvel Studios’ ''[[WandaVision]]'', along with Iman Vellani, who will appear as [[Ms. Marvel]] in the upcoming Disney+ [[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|series of the same name]]. 
Prepare to experience it and fly higher, further, and faster with the film, directed by Nia DaCosta!<ref>[https://www.marvel.com/articles/movies/captain-marvel-the-marvels-title?linkId=117790029 ‘The Marvels’ Arrives in Theaters November 11, 2022]</ref>

==Production==
===Development===
On [[July 20]], [[2019]], [[Kevin Feige]] announced that the sequel is in development at San Diego Comic Con.

On [[August 5]], 2020, it was announced that Nia DaCosta is set to direct the film.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2020/08/captain-marvel-sequel-nia-dacosta-director-1202992213/ 'Captain Marvel 2': 'Candyman's Nia DaCosta To Direct Sequel]</ref>

On [[May 3]], [[2021]], it was revealed that the title had been changed from ''Captain Marvel 2'' to ''The Marvels''. The same day, [[Marvel Studios]] released a video, which revealed the new logo for the film.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/general-news/the-marvels-captain-marvel-2-title-4177593/|title=‘The Marvels’ and the Future of ‘Captain Marvel’|website=The Hollywood Reporter|date=[[May 3]], [[2021]]}}</ref>

===Writing===
On [[January 22]], [[2020]], it was reported that the screenplay will be written by Megan McDonnell, a staff writer for ''[[WandaVision]]'', with a tentative 2022 release date.

===Casting===
On [[December 10]], 2020, it was confirmed that Teyonah Parris and [[Iman Vellani]] will reprise their roles as [[Monica Rambeau]] and [[Ms. Marvel|Kamala Khan]], respectively. A new release date of [[November 11]], [[2022]] was also announced.

On [[February 12]], [[2021]], Zawe Ashton was reported to be playing the villain.

On [[June 15]], 2021, it was reported that Korean actor Seo-Joon Park had joined the cast of the film in an undisclosed role.

On [[August 11]], 2021, ''Collider'' reported that [[Samuel L. Jackson]] would reprise his role as [[Nick Fury]] in the film.<ref name="Collider"/>

===Filming===
On [[August 10]], 2021, Larson confirmed that the film had begun production.<ref>[https://comicbook.com/marvel/news/marvels-filming-brie-larson-captain-marvel/ The Marvels: Brie Larson Confirms Captain Marvel Sequel Is Currently Filming]</ref>

On May 12, 2022, it was reported that filming had officially wrapped.<ref>[https://www.cbr.com/the-marvels-wrapped-filming-captain-marvel-mcu/ Captain Marvel's MCU Ensemble Sequel Has Wrapped Shooting]</ref>

===Music===
On [[January 13]], [[2022]], ''Variety'' reported that five-time Emmy winner, [[Laura Karpman]], would be the composer for the film. She is the second female composer ever to score a Marvel movie, following Pinar Toprak, who scored ''[[Captain Marvel (film)|Captain Marvel]]''. She previously scored the music for the [[Disney+]] series, ''[[What If...?]]'' and ''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]''.<ref name="Var">{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2022/artisans/news/the-marvels-mcu-what-if-composer-1235152926/|title=‘The Marvels’ Lands Laura Karpman From ‘What If…?’ as Composer (EXCLUSIVE)|website=Variety|date=[[January 13]], [[2022]]}}</ref>

==Release==
On [[April 3]], 2020, the film was given a release date of [[July 8]], [[2022]].

On [[October 18]], 2021, ''Deadline'' reported that the release date for the film had been pushed back to [[February 17]], [[2023]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/10/doctor-strange-2-thor-love-and-thunder-black-panther-wakanda-forever-release-date-changes-1234857549/|title=Marvel Movie Shift: ‘Doctor Strange 2’ To Kick Off Summer 2022, ‘Thor: Love And Thunder’ Heads To July & More|website=Deadline|date=[[October 18]], [[2021]]}}</ref>

On April 29, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that the film swapped release dates with ''[[Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania]]'' and will now be released on July 28, 2023.<ref>[https://variety.com/2022/film/news/the-marvels-ant-man-the-wasp-quantumania-release-date-swap-1235254707/ ‘The Marvels’ and ‘Ant-Man 3’ Swap Release Dates in 2023]</ref>
