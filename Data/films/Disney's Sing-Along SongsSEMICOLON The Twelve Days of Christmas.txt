'''''Disney's Sing-Along Songs: The Twelve Days of Christmas''''' is a sing-along video produced by [[Walt Disney Studios Home Entertainment|Walt Disney Home Video]] in [[December 24]], [[1993]]. It was re-released on [[December 14]], [[1994]]. It is the twelfth video in the ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs]]'' video series, the third ''Sing-Along Songs'' live action video, and the second Christmas-themed video in the series, before ''[[Mickey's Fun Songs]].''

==Description==
''Sing, dance, and play along with your favorite Disney songs! Learning the words is fun and easy as you sing along to the on-screen lyrics and join your favorite Disney characters!''

''Get into the holiday spirit with Mickey, Minnie, Donald, Goofy, and friends in this live-action songfest that captures the Christmas season's warmth and cheer! Sing along to traditional Christmas carols and learn new songs, too-while visiting the North Pole, sleigh riding through the wintry woods and dancing to a yuletide country hoedown!''

==Summary==
Mickey and the gang spend Christmas at Mickey's Log Cabin and at Disneyland.

==Trivia==
*This is the first ''Disney's Sing-Along Songs'' live-action video not to be directed by Gregory Sills and the second ''Disney's Sing-Along Songs'' live-action video to be located at Disneyland.
*This is also the only ''Disney's Sing-Along Songs'' live-action video where no one says goodbye at the end. Instead, the characters use hand throw streamers and celebrate Christmas with the people.
*[[Daisy Duck]]'s voice can be heard in the [[Japan]]ese version.
*This tape marks the final appearance of the ''Disney's Sing-Along Songs'' logo from 1986.
*[[Mickey Mouse]], [[Minnie Mouse]], [[Pluto]], [[Donald Duck]], [[Daisy Duck]], [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]], [[Scrooge McDuck]], [[Goofy]], [[Chip and Dale]], Grandma Duck, [[The Three Little Pigs (characters)|the Three Little Pigs]], [[Snow White]], [[Doc]], [[Happy]], [[Bashful]], [[Sneezy]], [[Grumpy]], [[Sleepy]], [[Dopey]], [[Pinocchio (character)|Pinocchio]], [[Jiminy Cricket]], [[Geppetto]], [[Honest John]], [[Stromboli]], [[Zummi Gummi]], [[Dumbo (character)|Dumbo]], [[Br'er Rabbit]], [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]], [[Prince Charming]], [[Alice]], [[Mary Poppins (character)|Mary Poppins]], Fifer Pig, [[Baloo]], [[Colonel Hathi]], [[Robin Hood (character)|Robin Hood]], [[Winnie the Pooh]], [[Tigger]], [[Eeyore]], [[Roger Rabbit]], [[Belle]], and the [[Beast]] are featured characters in this.
*During the "Dear Santa" song, a clip from the ''Who Framed Roger Rabbit'' MS-DOS video game ''[[Hare Raising Havoc]]'', and the front covers of three [[Disney Comics]]-published comic issues (''[[Walt Disney's Comics and Stories]]'' #570, ''[[DuckTales (comic books)|DuckTales]]'' #13, and ''[[Roger Rabbit (comic book)|Roger Rabbit]]'' #5) can be seen on Huey, Dewey and Louie's computer.
*The actual still with the logo is from the reprise of "[[When You Wish Upon a Star]]" from ''[[Pinocchio (film)|Pinocchio]]''.
*This marks the final appearance of Pluto's [[1978]] [[Disneyland]] costume with larger pupils.
*This is the only ''Disney's Sing-Along Songs'' video to not feature the usual theme music in the title card.
*The parade footage for "[[Here Comes Santa]]" is from the [[1990]] parade at the [[Magic Kingdom]] and the [[1991]] parade from the then [[Disney's Hollywood Studios]].
*There is also a German release of this video but this rare. A Japanese version was also made.
**The page for "The Twelve Days of Christmas" shows Huey, Dewey, and Louie holding presents again instead of Mickey and Minnie caroling.
*This volume is tied with [[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Zip-a-Dee-Doo-Dah|Zip-a-Dee-Doo-Dah]] as one of the least internationalized volumes in the series, not counting US-exclusive volumes.
