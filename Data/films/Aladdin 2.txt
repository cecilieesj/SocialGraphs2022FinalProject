'''''Aladdin 2''''' is an upcoming American live-action fantasy film, and sequel to ''[[Aladdin (2019 film)|Aladdin]]''.<ref name="Variety">{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/news/aladdin-sequel-1203502713/|title='Aladdinâ€™ Sequel in the Works (EXCLUSIVE)|website=Variety|date=[[February 12]], [[2020]]}}</ref>

==Production==
On [[February 12]], [[2020]], it was announced via ''Variety'', that a sequel was in development, for the live-action remake of ''[[Aladdin (2019 film)|Aladdin]]''. Dan Lin and Jonathan Eirich, who produced the original, were confirmed to return, with Ryan Halprin, also acting as an executive producer. The film was also confirmed to be a theatrical release and not a [[Disney+]] title.<ref name="Variety"/>

==Trivia==
*The sequel will be an original story, and not a remake of ''[[The Return of Jafar]]'', the sequel to the [[Aladdin (film)|original animated film]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://comicbook.com/movies/2020/02/13/aladdin-2-to-be-original-story-wont-be-based-on-return-of-jafar/|title=Aladdin 2 to Be Original Story, Won't Be Based on Return of Jafar|website=Comic Book|date=[[February 13]], 2020}}</ref>
*This will be the third sequel to a live-action [[Disney Remakes|Disney remake]], following ''[[102 Dalmatians]]'' and ''[[Maleficent: Mistress of Evil]]''.
