'''''Diamonds on Wheels''''' is a family comedy film directed by Jerome Courtland and starring [[Patrick Allen]], George Sewell and Derek Newark and Barry Jackson. It was originally released in the [[United Kingdom]] on [[August 19]], [[1973]], on a double bill with ''[[The World's Greatest Athlete]]''. It later aired in the United States in three parts on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' starting on [[March 10]], [[1974]].

==Plot==
A million-dollar diamond robbery takes an unexpected turn when one of the gang decides to double-cross his partners and take the gems for himself.
