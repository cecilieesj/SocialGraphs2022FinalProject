'''''All Night Long''''' is an upcoming American live-action musical film based on the songs by {{WPS|Lionel Ritchie}}.

==Production==
On June 9, 2020, it was announced  that Walt Disney Pictures had put into development a musical film that would use the songs of musician Lionel Richie, who will also produce the film. Peter Chiarelli was hired to write the screenplay.<ref>[https://variety.com/2020/film/news/disney-lionel-richie-movie-musical-all-night-long-1234629473/ Disney Developing Original Movie Musical ‘All Night Long’ From Lionel Richie Song Catalog (EXCLUSIVE)]</ref>
