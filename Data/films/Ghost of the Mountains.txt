'''''Ghost of the Mountains''''' is a [[2017]] nature documentary about snow leopards in [[China]]. Directed by Ben Wallis<ref>{{cite news|last=Kessler|first=Rebecca|title=Stalking snow leopards: Q&A with the director of "Ghost of the Mountains"|url=https://news.mongabay.com/2017/09/stalking-snow-leopards-qa-with-the-director-of-ghost-of-the-mountains/|work=Mongabay|accessdate=23 November 2017|date=20 September 2017}}</ref> and narrated by Antoine Fuqua. ''Ghost of the Mountains'' was released theatrically by [[Disneynature]] on [[June 30]], 2017. It is the eleventh nature documentary released under that label.<ref>{{cite news|title=Disneynature Releases Ghost of the Mountains|url=https://www.laughingplace.com/w/news/2017/06/30/disneynature-releases-ghosts-mountains/|work=LaughingPlace.com|accessdate=23 November 2017|date=30 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Reif|first=Alex|title=Digital Review: Disneynature Ghost of the Mountains|url=https://www.laughingplace.com/w/articles/2017/07/05/digital-review-disneynature-ghost-mountains/|work=LaughingPlace.com|accessdate=23 November 2017|date=5 July 2017}}</ref>

''Ghost of the Mountains'' focuses on the journey that the filmmaking team behind ''[[Born in China]]'' embarked on to find the very elusive leopards in their mountain habitats.
