'''''Out of the Frying Pan Into the Firing Line''''' is a wartime cartoon starring [[Pluto]] and [[Minnie Mouse]].

==Plot==
[[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]] is taught the importance of conservation and recycling of bacon grease, which can be turned into glycerin for the war effort.

==Characters==
*[[Minnie Mouse]] (voiced by Thelma Boardman)
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*Radio Announcer (voiced by Art Gilmore)
*Butcher (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]])
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (cameo on portrait)
