'''''Jack and Old Mac''''' is an animated [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]] short that was originally released theatrically on [[July 18]], [[1956]].

==Synopsis==
This is a two-part short: the first part is a telling of "The House that Jack Built". The second part had a hillbilly family sing the song "Old MacDonald" as "Old MacDonald Had a Band."

==Notes==
*This short reuses some animation from the "[[All the Cats Join In]]" segment of ''[[Make Mine Music]]''.
*'''Running gag''': The hillbilly on the right hits Old Mac's son's head to get him to sing "e-i-e-i-o". The last time he does this Mac Junior kicks the hillbilly in the shin.
