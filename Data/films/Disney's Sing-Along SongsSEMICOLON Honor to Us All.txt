'''''Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Honor to Us All''''' is a ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs]]'' video released on [[June 28]], [[1998]].
==Description==
''Sing, dance and play along with your favorite Disney songs! It's fun and easy as you read the on-screen lyrics and join your favorite characters in their most memorable musical moments!''

''Join Disney's newest heroine on an adventure of magic and music! "Honor to Us All" features memorable scenes and songs from Disney's 36th animated hit, Mulan, including "Honor to Us All" and "I'll Make a Man Out of You". Plus "Where Do I Go from Here" from Pocahontas II: Journey to a New World, "The Siamese Cat Song" from Lady and the Tramp", "Zero to Hero" from Hercules and others! Also includes a sneak peek at The Lion King II: Simba's Pride- the upcoming sequel to Disney's hit The Lion King!''

==Trivia==
*Hosted by [[Professor Owl]] (voiceover only, before first song).
*Features the return of "The Siamese Cat Song", cut from the 1994 edition of ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Heigh-Ho|Heigh-Ho]]''. This song features newly-updated lyrics.
*This volume uses the remixed opening and closing themes, first used on ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Friend Like Me|Friend Like Me]]''. However, most copies and the Laserdisc edition uses the remixed opening and the original closing.
*[[Mushu]] appeared on the last fan transition revealing the last song, "I'll Make a Man Out of You".
*The "Honor to Us All" laserdisc features ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Pongo & Perdita|Pongo and Perdita]]'' on side 2.
*The song "Zero to Hero" was previously featured on ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs from Hercules]]'', and ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Collection of All-Time Favorites: The Modern Classics|The Modern Classics]]''. "A Guy Like You" was previously featured on the former volume. Also, "On the Open Road" and "Father and Son" are reused from the non-US release of ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Topsy Turvy|Topsy Turvy]]''.
