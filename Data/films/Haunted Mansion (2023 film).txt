'''''Haunted Mansion'''''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://wdwnt.com/2022/04/logo-revealed-for-2023-the-haunted-mansion-film/|title=Logo Revealed for 2023 ‘The Haunted Mansion’ Film|website=WDW News Today|last=Michaelsen|first=Shannen|date=April 27, 2022|access-date=April 27, 2022}}</ref> is an upcoming film based on the [[The Haunted Mansion (attraction)|attraction of the same name]]. It is a reboot of the [[The Haunted Mansion (film)|2003 film adaptation]]. It it set to be released on [[August 11]], [[2023]].

==Premise==
''Gabbie, a single mother, and her young son recently purchased a New Orleans estate at auction only to discover it isn’t quite as empty as they expected. Gabbie hires a grieving widower who works as a ghost tour guide, a psychic, a priest, and a local historian to exorcise the vengeful spirit from the Haunted Mansion.''<ref name="CastPlot" />

==Production==
A ''Haunted Mansion'' reboot was announced as far back as 2010 with the intent to have [[Guillermo del Toro]] write, produce and direct the film.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.collider.com/2010/07/27/sdcc2010-guillermo-del-toro-interview-dont-be-afraid-of-the-dark-the-haunted-mansion-frankenstein-hellboy/|work=Collider|title=SDCC2010: Guillermo del Toro Interview DON'T BE AFRAID OF THE DARK; Plus Info on THE HAUNTED MANSION, FRANKENSTEIN and HELLBOY|last=Radish|first= Christina|date=July 27, 2010|access-date=July 29, 2010}}</ref> His take would have featured the famous [[Hatbox Ghost]] in a major role and be legitimately terrifying and funny.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1644374/20100724/story.jhtml|work=MTV|title=Guillermo Del Toro Dishes On 'Haunted Mansion' Remake|author=Schwartz, Terri|date=July 24, 2010|access-date=July 25, 2010}}</ref> Imagineer Jason Surrell was brought on as a creative consultant.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://knowtheartist.com/author-jason-surrell-assembles-art-of-marvels-the-avengers/|title=Jason Surrell assembles ''The Art of Marvel's The Avengers''|author=Armstrong, Josh|date=May 14, 2012|access-date=June 21, 2012|publisher=KnowTheArtist.com |url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120521011917/http://knowtheartist.com/author-jason-surrell-assembles-art-of-marvels-the-avengers/|archive-date=May 21, 2012}}</ref> However, due to numerous delays, del Toro dropped out as director, but would remain as a writer and producer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://collider.com/justice-league-dark-news-haunted-mansion-news-guillermo-del-toro/|title=Guillermo del Toro Gives Updates on JUSTICE LEAGUE DARK and HAUNTED MANSION; Confirms He Won't Direct HAUNTED MANSION|author=Chitwood, Adam|date=July 10, 2013|accessdate=April 22, 2021}}</ref> [[Ryan Gosling]] had entered early negotiations to star, while D.V. DeVincentis was hired to rewrite the script.<ref name="Gosling_Variety">{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2015/film/news/ryan-gosling-to-star-in-guillermo-del-toros-haunted-mansion-1201469772/|work=Variety|title=Ryan Gosling to Star in Guillermo del Toro's 'Haunted Mansion'|author=McNary, Dave|date=April 9, 2015|accessdate=April 22, 2021}}</ref> Ultimately, nothing came of this version.

In August 2020, Katie Dippold was hired to pen a new screenplay, while Dan Lin and Jonathan Eirich would co-produce.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/haunted-mansion-movie-in-the-works-with-writer-katie-dippold-exclusive|work=The Hollywood Reporter|title='Haunted Mansion' Movie in the Works With Writer Katie Dippold (Exclusive)|author=Kit, Borys|date=August 28, 2020|access-date=August 28, 2020}}</ref> The following year, Justin Simien signed on as director,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/04/disneys-haunted-mansion-dear-white-people-helmer-justin-simien-1234741251/|title=Disney’s ‘Haunted Mansion’ Pic Finds Its Director In ‘Dear White People’ Helmer Justin Simien|work=Deadline|last=Kroll|first= Justin|date=April 22, 2021|access-date=April 22, 2021}}</ref> while [[Tiffany Haddish]] and Lakeith Stanfield entered negotiations to star.<ref name="CastPlot">{{cite web|url=https://www.theilluminerdi.com/2021/07/26/haunted-mansion-tiffany-haddish/|title=HAUNTED MANSION: INTEREST IN TIFFANY HADDISH AND LAKEITH STANFIELD TO STAR AND EXCITING STORY DETAILS: EXCLUSIVE|website=The Illuminerdi|last=Tyrrell|first=Caitlin|date=July 26, 2021|access-date=July 27, 2021}}</ref>

In August 2021, it was revealed that casting was underway for the roles of [[Master Gracey]] and his wife, the [[Mariner]], the butler, the [[Bride]] (based on the [[Constance Hatchaway]] version), and a [[Duelists|duelist]]. Additionally, roles for the portrait ghosts are also underway.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thathashtagshow.com/2021/08/17/these-are-the-ghosts-youll-find-in-disneys-new-haunted-mansion-movie/|title=THESE ARE THE GHOSTS YOU’LL FIND IN DISNEY’S NEW ‘HAUNTED MANSION’ MOVIE [EXCLUSIVE]|website=That Hashtag Show|last=Lancaster|first=Jessica|date=August 17, 2021|access-date=August 17, 2021}}</ref>

Production is expected to begin in October 2021 under the working title ''Joyride''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2021/09/01/disneys-haunted-mansion-reboot-begins-filming-later-this-month/|title=Disney’s ‘Haunted Mansion’ Reboot Begins Filming Later This Month!|website=The Disinsider|last=Pillot|first=Dempsey|date=September 1, 2021|access-date=September 1, 2021}}</ref> [[Owen Wilson]] and [[Rosario Dawson]] joined the cast in early September.<ref name="WilsonCast">{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/owen-wilson-to-star-in-disneys-haunted-mansion-movie-tiffany-haddish-lakeith-stanfield-1235009953/|title=Owen Wilson to Star in Disney’s ‘Haunted Mansion’ (Exclusive)|website=The Hollywood Reporter|last=Kit|first=Borys|date=September 8, 2021|access-date=September 8, 2021}}</ref><ref name="DawsonCast">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/09/rosario-dawson-haunted-mansion-movie-casting-1234828943/|title=Rosario Dawson Books A Room In Disney’s ‘Haunted Mansion’ Movie|website=Deadline|last=Hipes|first=Patrick|date=September 9, 2021|access-date=September 9, 2021}}</ref> In mid-October, [[Danny DeVito]] joined the project.<ref name="DeVitoCast">{{cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2021/10/18/disneys-haunted-mansion-adds-danny-devito/|title=Disney’s ‘Haunted Mansion’ Adds Danny DeVito|website=The Disinsider|last=Pillot|first=Dempsey|date=October 18, 2021|access-date=October 18, 2021}}</ref>

On February 25, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that the film is set to be released on March 10, 2023.<ref>[https://twitter.com/Borys_Kit/status/1497280296563068928 Borys Kit on Twitter - February 25, 2022]</ref>

On September 15, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that the film was delayed to [[August 11]], [[2023]].<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/star-wars-rogue-squadron-removed-2023-1235221389/ Disney Pushes ‘Haunted Mansion’ to Summer 2023, Removes ‘Star Wars’ Movie ‘Rogue Squadron’ From Calendar]</ref>
