'''''The China Shop''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that was released on [[January 13]], [[1934]].

==Synopsis==
After the doors close at the China Shop, the sculpture pieces come to life in to dance, move, and song.

==Releases==
*''[[The Mickey Mouse Club]]'', November 2, 1955
*''[[The Mickey Mouse Club#1970s revival: The New Mickey Mouse Club|The New Mickey Mouse Club]]'', November 4, 1977
*''[[Mickey's Mouse Tracks]]'', episode #49
*''[[Donald's Quack Attack]]'', episode #67
*''[[The Ink and Paint Club]]'', episode #1.24: "Symphonic Silly Symphonies"

===Home video===
*''[[Walt Disney Cartoon Classics: Limited Gold Editions]] - Silly Symphonies'' (VHS)
*''[[Walt Disney Treasures]]: More Silly Symphonies'' (DVD)

==Trivia==
*The five-dollar fine china sculptures convert to $96 in 2019 present dollars from an inflation estimator.
*The ten-dollar rare antique pieces convert to $192 in 2019 present dollars. The antiques are more expensive in value year after year.
*The short is inspired by the [[Hans Christian Andersen]] fairy tale ''The Shepardess and the Chimney Sweep''.
