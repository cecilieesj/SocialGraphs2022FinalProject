'''''A Haunting in Venice''''' is an upcoming American mystery thriller film. It is directed by [[Kenneth Branagh]], written by Michael Green, and based on the 1969 novel ''Hallowe'en Party'' by Agatha Christie. The film is a sequel to the [[2022]] film ''[[Death on the Nile]]''. It is scheduled to be released on [[September 15]], [[2023]].

==Synopsis==
[[Hercule Poirot]], now retired, must solve the murder of a guest at a séance he is in attendance for.

On October 10, 2022, it was announced that Jamie Dornan, [[Tina Fey]], [[Michelle Yeoh]] and more were cast in the film, with production scheduled to begin on October 31.<ref name="Cast">[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/a-haunting-in-venice-cast-revealed-kenneth-branagh-1235238310/ Jamie Dornan, Tina Fey, Michelle Yeoh, Jude Hill Join Kenneth Branagh in Agatha Christie Mystery ‘A Haunting in Venice’]</ref>
