An '''''Untitled National Treasure: Book of Secrets sequel''''' is an upcoming American adventure film and serves as a sequel to the [[2007]] film, ''[[National Treasure: Book of Secrets]]''. It is produced by [[Jerry Bruckheimer]].

==Cast==
*[[Nicolas Cage]] as [[Benjamin Gates]]

==Production==
On May 28, 2022, it was reported that [[Jerry Bruckheimer]] was developing a third ''National Treasure'' film, with [[Nicolas Cage]] returning as [[Benjamin Gates]].<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2022/05/27/jerry-bruckheimer-confirms-they-are-working-on-a-national-treasure-3-script-with-nicolas-cage-returning/ Jerry Bruckheimer Confirms They Are Working on a ‘National Treasure 3’ Script With Nicolas Cage Returning]</ref>
