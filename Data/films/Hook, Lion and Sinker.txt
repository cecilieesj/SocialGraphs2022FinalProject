'''''Hook, Lion and Sinker''''' is animated short starring [[Donald Duck]] and [[Louie the Mountain Lion]].

==Synopsis==
Louie and his son attempt to steal the prizes from Donald's fishing expedition. 

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Louie the Mountain Lion]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]])
*Little Louie
