'''''Muppet Man''''' is an upcoming biopic. The film chronicles the life of famed creator of ''[[The Muppets (franchise)|The Muppets]]'', [[Jim Henson]].

==Synopsis==
''Muppet Man'' will focus on Henson's journey to convince broadcasters that ''The Muppets'' was a great idea and how he worked to get the characters on air where they became a comedy staple.

==Production==
The script is being written by Michael Mitnick, based on a previous draft by Aaron and Jordan Kandell.<ref name="deadline2021-04-21">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/04/jim-henson-movie-muppet-man-sesame-street-muppets-michael-mitnick-1234740866/|title=Jim Henson Biopic: Michael Mitnick Writing ‘Muppet Man’ For Disney & The Jim Henson Company|website=Deadline|date=April 21, 2021}}</ref>

==Notes==
The Mitnick project is unrelated to a script of the same name by Christopher Weekes which was picked up by the Henson Company after it made first place on the 2009 Black List.<ref name="deadline2021-04-21" />
