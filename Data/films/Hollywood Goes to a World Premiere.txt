'''''Hollywood Goes to a World Premiere''''' is a [[1964]] featurette promoting the world premiere of ''[[Mary Poppins (film)|Mary Poppins]]'' in August 1964 at {{WikipediaLink|Grauman's Chinese Theater}} in Hollywood. It features on-the-spot interviews with [[Julie Andrews]], [[Dick Van Dyke]], and [[Walt Disney]], and a parade of most of the distinguished guests attending the event.
