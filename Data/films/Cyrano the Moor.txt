'''''Cyrano the Moor''' ''is an upcoming swashbuckler film starring [[David Oyelowo]] as the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyrano_de_Bergerac eponymous character]. The film is written by ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moonlight_(2016_film) Moonlight]'' author [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tarell_Alvin_McCraney Tarell Alvin McCraney] and combines elements from Edmond Rostand's play ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyrano_de_Bergerac_(play) Cyrano de Bergerac] ''with William Shakespeare's ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Othello Othello]''.

== Trivia ==
*This is the second time that David Oyelowo stars in a Disney-related property after playing [[disney:Peter Pan (character)|Peter]] and [[disney:Alice|Alice]]'s father in the 2020 fantasy drama [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Come_Away ''Come Away''].
