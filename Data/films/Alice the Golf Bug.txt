'''''Alice the Golf Bug''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
[[Alice]], [[Julius the Cat|Julius]], and [[Pete]] compete in a manic golf tournament.
