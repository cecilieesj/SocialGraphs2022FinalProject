}}'''''Cinderella''''' is a [[1922]] film directed by [[Walt Disney]] which was based on the book by Charles Perrault.

==Synopsis==
The traditional story with Cinderella as a 1920's flapper, featuring Susie (as Cinderella), [[Jack (Laugh-O-Gram shorts)|Jack]] (as the Prince), and [[Julius the Cat]].
