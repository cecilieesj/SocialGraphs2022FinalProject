'''''Mockingbird''''' is an upcoming American science-fiction film, directed by Alma Har’el based on the novel of the same name by Walter Tevis.

==Production==
On April 20, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[Searchlight Pictures]] was developing a ''Mockingbird'' film to be released theatrically, with Alma Har’el directing.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/04/honey-boy-alma-harel-mockingbird-searchlight-1235006100/ ‘Honey Boy’ Director Alma Har’el To Helm Adaptation Of ‘Mockingbird’ For Searchlight]</ref>
