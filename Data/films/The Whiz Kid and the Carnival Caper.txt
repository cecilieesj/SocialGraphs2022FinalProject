|next       = "[[The Bears and I]]"}}"'''The Whiz Kid and the Carnival Caper'''" is a [[1976]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[January 11]], and [[January 18]], [[1976]]. The telefilm was directed by Tom Leetch.

==Synopsis==
Daffy Fernald loses her brother's toy rocket when it flies into a storm drain. She knows Alvin will be upset if he finds out, so she climbs into the dark tunnel to search for it. She spots a stranger, Ernie Nelson, and sees that he's carrying a gun. She races home and tells Alvin, but the would-be inventor refuses to believe her.
