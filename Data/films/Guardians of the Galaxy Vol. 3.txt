'''''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3''''' is an upcoming American superhero film and serves as a sequel to ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'' and ''[[The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special]]''. It was confirmed in April [[2017]], by [[James Gunn]] who will return to write and direct the third film. It is produced by [[Marvel Studios]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]]. It will be released on [[May 5]], [[2023]] and will be the thirty-second film in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] and the second film of Phase Five.

==Synopsis==
In [[Marvel Studios]] "''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3''" our beloved band of misfits are looking a bit different these days. [[Star Lord|Peter Quill]], still reeling from the loss of [[Gamora]], must rally his team around him to defend the universe along with protecting one of their own. A mission that, if not completed successfully, could quite possibly lead to the end of the Guardians as we know them.<ref>[https://www.marvel.com/movies/guardians-of-the-galaxy-volume-3 Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 - Marvel]</ref>

==Production==
In November 2014, Gunn stated that, in addition to having the "basic story" for ''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2'' while working on the first film, he also had ideas for a potential third film.<ref>[http://www.indiewire.com/2014/11/interview-james-gunn-talks-guardians-2-small-vs-big-budgets-and-being-betrayed-by-lost-270558/ Interview: James Gunn Talks ‘Guardians 2,’ Small Vs Big Budgets And Being “Betrayed” By ‘Lost’]</ref> Despite this, Gunn stated in June 2015 that he was unsure if he would be involved with a third ''Guardians'' film, saying that it would depend on how he felt after making ''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2''.<ref>[http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-characters-stories/ ‘Guardians of the Galaxy 2’ Has Fewer Characters, One New]</ref> In April 2016, Feige said on future MCU films, "it is still a big chess board for 2020 and beyond, but certainly I would say Guardians 3 is [one film that's] up there. I don't know what exactly the order will be."<ref>[http://io9.gizmodo.com/avengers-infinity-war-parts-1-and-2-will-be-very-diffe-1770201271 Avengers: Infinity War Parts 1 and 2 Will Be Very Different Movies]</ref> In March 2017, Gunn stated there would be a third film "for sure. We're trying to figure it out,"<ref name="complex">[http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2017/03/james-gunn-belko-experiment-guardians-of-the-galaxy ‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ Director James Gunn Teases The Guardians’ “Integral Part” In ‘Infinity War’]</ref> also adding, "There are no specific plans for ''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3''. But we know unless something goes horribly — which is always possibly, you never know — I think that Marvel would want to make another movie."<ref>[https://www.yahoo.com/movies/james-gunn-explains-what-he-really-meant-about-guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-214412871.html James Gunn Explains What He Really Meant About 'Guardians of the Galaxy: Vol. 3']</ref> He also reiterated he was still unsure if he would be involved with the film and that he was going to figure out his involvement and his next project "over the next couple of weeks."<ref name="complex">[http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2017/03/james-gunn-belko-experiment-guardians-of-the-galaxy ‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ Director James Gunn Teases The Guardians’ “Integral Part” In ‘Infinity War’]</ref> The following month, Gunn announced he would return to write and direct ''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3''.<ref name="THR">[http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/guardians-galaxy-3-james-gunn-returning-write-direct-994662 'Guardians of the Galaxy 3': James Gunn Returning to Write and Direct]</ref>

On July 20, 2018, Disney and Marvel severed ties with Gunn after conservative commentators began circulating old controversial tweets surrounding topics such as rape and pedophilia, and called for his firing.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/07/james-gunn-fired-guardians-of-the-galaxy-disney-offensive-tweets-1202430392/fa|title=James Gunn Fired From ‘Guardians Of The Galaxy’ Franchise Over Offensive Tweets|website=Hollywood Reporter|accessdate=February 17, 2019}}</ref> However as of March, 15, 2019 he has been rehired by Disney to direct ''Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3'' once again and would resume working on the film after he completes 2021's ''The Suicide Squad'' for Warner Bros. Pictures and DC Films.

On [[June 28]], [[2021]], [[Chris Pratt]] revealed that filming would start in the Autumn of that year and will end in April 2022.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedirect.com/article/guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-chris-pratt-filming|title=Chris Pratt Reveals When Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 Starts Filming|website=The Direct|date=June 28, 2021|access-date=June 2021}}</ref>

Filming began on [[October 19]], [[2021]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedirect.com/article/chris-pratt-guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-filming-start|title=Chris Pratt Celebrates Guardians of the Galaxy 3 Filming Start|website=The Direct|date=October 19, 2021|access-date=October 19, 2021}}</ref> and would finish around the middle of May 2022.<ref>[https://mobile.twitter.com/getFANDOM/status/1509248773842563072 Fandom on Twitter - March 30, 2022]</ref>

On May 6, 2022, James Gunn announced that they officially wrapped production.<ref>[https://twitter.com/JamesGunn/status/1522731151143616512 James Gunn on Twitter - May 6, 2022]</ref> [[Dave Bautista]] announced he had finished filming as [[Drax the Destroyer|Drax]], saying goodbye to the character.<ref name=Drax>[https://thedirect.com/article/dave-bautista-guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-drax-endhttps://thedirect.com/article/dave-bautista-guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-drax-end  Dave Bautista Teases the End of Drax In Guardians of the Galaxy 3]</ref>

*According to [[Dave Bautista]] this is likely going to be the end of [[Drax the Destroyer|Drax]], implying he might die.<ref>[https://thedirect.com/article/guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-dave-bautista-drax-marvel-future-role Guardians of the Galaxy 3 Will 'Probably' Be the End of Dave Bautista's Drax Role]</ref>

*Part of the plot was nearly used for a ''What if..?'' episode.
*The plot is very emotional. Actresses [[Pom Klementieff]] and [[Karen Gillan]] both cried when reading the script.<ref> [https://comicbook.com/movies/news/marvel-guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-script-emotional-karen-gillan-pom-klementieff-floods-of-tears/ "So Emotional" Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 Script Left Stars in "Floods of Tears"]</ref>
*The movie will be focused on the relationship of Gamora and Nebula.<ref>[https://thedirect.com/article/guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-gamora-nebula-story-plot-rumors Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 Will Reportedly Be Gamora & Nebula's Story]</ref>

*According to James Gunn, this is the final appearances of all the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (team)|Guardians]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thedirect.com/article/marvel-chris-pratt-guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-director|title=Marvel Is Done With Chris Pratt's Guardians of the Galaxy After Vol. 3, Says Director|website=The Direct|date=January 28, 2022|access-date=January 28, 2022}}</ref>
