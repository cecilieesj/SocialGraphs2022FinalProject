The '''''untitled Nova film''''' is an upcoming American television special, set within the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] centering on [[Nova (Marvel)|Nova]]. It is the third television special for [[Disney+]].

==Production==
On [[March 24]], [[2022]], ''Deadline'' reported that a Nova project would be in the works, for [[Marvel Studios]]. Sabir Pirzada, who was a scribe for ''[[Moon Knight (TV series)|Moon Knight]]'', ''[[Ms. Marvel (TV series)|Ms. Marvel]]'' and [[Marvel Comics]], was hired as a writer, for the upcoming project.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2022/03/marvel-nova-moon-knight-sabir-pirzada-1234985015/|title=Marvel Developing ‘Nova’ Project With ‘Moon Knight’ Scribe Sabir Pirzada|website=Deadline|date=[[March 24]], [[2022]]}}</ref>

On October 13, 2022, it was reported that the project would become a [[Disney+]] television special, following ''[[Werewolf by Night (film)|Werewolf by Night]]'' and ''[[The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special]]''.<ref>[https://www.thecosmiccircus.com/exclusive-marvels-nova-to-be-a-special-presentation-on-disney/ Exclusive: Marvel’s Nova to Be a Special Presentation on Disney+]</ref>
