'''''Something You Didn't Eat''''' is a [[1945]] Disney animated short film. It was sponsored by the Cereal Institute (Office of War Information, War Food Administration).

==Trivia==
*One odd note comes in the form of several scenes whose background is a dead ringer for the Warner Bros. cartoon main title "bullseye" motif.
