| Plot                    = The [[X-Men (Earth-TRN608)|X-Men]] confronted the [[Sentinels]] at the DAVE school. The Sentinels were easily defeated. On their way back to [[Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters]], [[Charles Xavier (Earth-TRN608)|Professer X]] contacted them and had them divert their course to an oil rig in the [[Atlantic Ocean]] as one of the team's members, [[Ororo Munroe (Earth-TRN608)|Storm]], had been lost there. On the oil rig, the X-Men came into conflict with [[Max Eisenhardt (Earth-TRN608)|Magneto]]'s [[Brotherhood of Mutants (Earth-TRN608)|Brotherhood of Mutants]] who was trying to stop the Earth's core to kill [[Humans (Homo sapiens)|humanity]]. Magneto's Brotherhood were defeated by the team.
