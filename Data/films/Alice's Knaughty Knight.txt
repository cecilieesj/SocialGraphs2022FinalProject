'''''Alice's Knaughty Knight''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
[[Pete]], in a suit of armor, steals away the object of [[Julius the Cat|Julius]]' affection. So Julius makes himself a suit of armor out of junk to fight back.
