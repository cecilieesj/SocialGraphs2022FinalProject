'''''Ride 'Em Plowboy''''' is an [[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit]] animated short released on [[April 15]], [[1928]]. This is one of several Oswald shorts that are "lost".

A handful of surviving story sketches and backgrounds were published in ''[[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit: The Search for the Lost Disney Cartoons]]''.

==Synopsis==
In a foreshadowing of what was to become a staple of the Mickey shorts, Oswald manages his farm along to a musical beat.
