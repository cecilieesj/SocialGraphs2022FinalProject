"'''Donovan's Kid'''" is a [[1979]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[January 7]], and [[January 14]], 1979. The telefilm was directed by Bernard McEveety.

==Synopsis==
Two Con Men (Darren McGavin, Mickey Rooney) team up to rescue One's Family in circa-1900s San Francisco.
