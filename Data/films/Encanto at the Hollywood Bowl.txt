'''''Encanto at the Hollywood Bowl''''' is an upcoming concert film that will be released to [[Disney+]] on [[December 28]], 2022. The film is an edited taping of the ''[[Encanto]] Live-to-Film Experience'' at the Hollywood Bowl in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]], which was performed on [[November 11]] and [[November 12|12]], 2022.

==Synopsis==
The concert at the Hollywood Bowl had most of the entire cast reprising their roles from the film on the stage. The show will also include a special introduction by songwriter [[Lin-Manuel Miranda]], a special appearance by score composer [[Germaine Franco]], and appearances by singers Carlos Vives and Andrés Cepeda. Conductor Anthony Parnther leads a full symphony orchestra as well as a Colombian band.
