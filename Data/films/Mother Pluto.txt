'''''Mother Pluto''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphonies]]'' cartoon starring [[Pluto]] that was released on [[November 14]], [[1936]].

==Synopsis==
Pluto plays mother hen to a handful of chicks who have just hatched in his doghouse.

==Characters==
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*Mother Chicken (voiced by [[Florence Gill]])
*Father Chicken
