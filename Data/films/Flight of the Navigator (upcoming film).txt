'''''Flight of the Navigator''''' is an upcoming American science-fiction adventure film. It serves as the remake of the [[1986]] film of [[Flight of the Navigator|same name]] with a female lead.

==Production==
On September 15, 2021, ''Deadline'' reported that [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]] is developing a remake of ''Flight of the Navigator'' with [[Bryce Dallas Howard]] directing for [[Disney+]].<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/09/flight-of-the-navigator-female-reboot-in-the-works-at-disney-with-bryce-dallas-howard-directing-producing-1234834039/ ‘Flight Of The Navigator’ Female Reboot In The Works At Disney With Bryce Dallas Howard Directing & Producing]</ref>
