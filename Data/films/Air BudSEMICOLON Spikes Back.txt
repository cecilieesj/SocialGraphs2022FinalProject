'''''Air Bud Spikes Back''''' is the fifth and final film in the original [[Air Bud (series)|''Air Bud'' series]]. The film series itself was followed by a spin-off series - the ''[[Air Buddies]]'' franchise.

==Plot==
In Disney's fifth and final installment to the franchise, [[Air Bud (dog)|Buddy]] finds that he also has the uncanny ability to play volleyball. Throughout this experience, he and a talking parrot stop some crooks and make some new friends.
