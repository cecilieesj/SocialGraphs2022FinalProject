'''''Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Flik's Musical Adventure at Disney's Animal Kingdom''''' is a ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs]]'' video released on VHS in [[June 8]], [[1999]] and then on DVD in [[September 27]], [[2005]].

==Description==
''Sing, dance and play along in fun-filled musical adventures! It's easy as you read the on-screen lyrics to favorite kid songs!''

''With Flik-the outrageously fun hero from Disney and Pixar's A Bug's Life-as your guide and Mickey at your side, jump aboard the Wildlife Express on a journey bound the fun at Disney's newest theme park, Animal Kingdom! You'll sing and dance your way through an African safari, mysterious Asian ruins, DinoLand U.S.A. and more. Flik's Musical Adventure at Disney's Animal Kingdom-it's a wild experience you'll really dig!''

==Summary==
[[Flik]], [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] and the rest of the gang spend the day at [[Disney's Animal Kingdom]].

==Trivia==
*Hosted by [[Flik]] from ''[[A Bug's Life]]''.
*This is the first time where Flik isn't voiced by [[Dave Foley]] (in this case, being voiced by [[Wally Wingert]]). Wingert later voiced Flik in the former [[The Ugly Bug Ball (attraction)|Ugly Bug Ball]] attraction at [[Disney California Adventure]].
*Episode #8 of ''The Tiara Talk Show'' podcast marks the first time that the entire cast spoke together since the filming of this video in 1998.
*There are a couple of deleted scenes that are only seen in the sneak peek of this video.
*It uses early CGI animated techniques that were made in [[Wikipedia:Autodesk Maya|Alias Wavefront Maya]].
* This is the only sing-along video in the parks where Mickey and Minnie appear but don't speak.
