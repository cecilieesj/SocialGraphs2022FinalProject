}}'''''The Thrifty Pig''''' is a promotional animated short of the wartime from [[1941]], starring [[The Three Little Pigs (characters)|the Three Little Pigs]] and the [[Big Bad Wolf]].

==Synopsis==
Reused and reconfigured animation from ''The Three Little Pigs'' is used to show the advisability of buying Canadian War Bonds to support the war effort. The Nazi Wolf does the same as the normal Wolf does in ''The Three Little Pigs''. He blows the first two houses. At the third house, he blows off his own pants to show his nude German lower. Practical takes a few bricks and throw them at the Wolf's dong, in the hope to hit it.
