'''''Young Woman and the Sea''''' is an upcoming American biographical drama film based on ''Young Woman and the Sea: How Trudy Ederle Conquered the English Channel and Inspired the World'' by Glenn Stout. It will be released on [[Disney+]].

*[[Christopher Eccleston]] as TBA<ref name="Deadline2" />

==Development==
On December 9, 2020, ''Deadline'' reported that a biopic centering on American competition swimmer and Olympic champion {{WPS|Gertrude Ederle}} was in development at [[Disney+]].<ref name="Ederle">[https://deadline.com/2020/12/daisy-ridley-disney-plus-movie-young-woman-and-the-sea-gertrude-ederle-joachim-ronning-jerry-bruckheimer-jeff-nathanson-first-woman-swim-english-channel-1234653227/ Disney Takes Plunge On ‘Young Woman And The Sea’; ‘Star Wars’ Daisy Ridley, ‘Kon-Tiki’s Joachim Rønning Team On Tale Of First Woman To Swim English Channel]</ref>

On March 14, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that Tilda Cobham-Hervey and [[Stephen Graham]] joined the cast of the film.<ref name="Deadline">[https://deadline.com/2022/03/tilda-cobham-stephen-graham-daisey-ridley-disneys-young-woman-and-the-sea-1234978450/ Tilda Cobham-Hervey And Stephen Graham Join Daisy Ridley In Disney’s ‘Young Woman And The Sea’]</ref>

On May 4, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[Christopher Eccleston]] joined the cast of the film in an undisclosed role, with filming beginning in early May.<ref name="Deadline2">[https://deadline.com/2022/05/christopher-eccleston-joins-daisy-ridley-in-young-woman-and-the-sea-1235016337/ Christopher Eccleston Joins Daisy Ridley In Disney+ Film ‘Young Woman And The Sea’]</ref>

On June 18, 2022, [[Daisy Ridley]] announced that filming had officially wrapped.<ref>[https://www.instagram.com/p/Ce8zJarL9DH/ Daisy Ridley on Instagram - June 18, 2022]</ref>
