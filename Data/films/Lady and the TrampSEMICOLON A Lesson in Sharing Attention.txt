'''''Lady and the Tramp: A Lesson in Sharing Attention''''' is a [[1978]] Disney educational live-action/animated short, part of the ''Disney's Animated Classics: Lessons in Living'' series. The short features clips from ''[[Lady and the Tramp]]''.

==Synopsis==
The film stresses the importance of sharing attention when a new baby joins a family.
