'''''The Simpsons: The Good, the Bart, and the Loki''''' is a crossover short film between the ''[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]'' and [[Wikipedia:The Simpsons|''The Simpsons'']]. It was released on [[July 7]], [[2021]] on [[Disney+]].

==Official description==
:''Banished from Asgard once again, Loki must face his toughest opponents yet: the Simpsons and Springfieldâ€™s mightiest heroes. The God of Mischief teams up with Bart Simpson in the ultimate crossover event paying tribute to the Marvel Cinematic Universe of Super Heroes and villains.''

==Cast==
* [[Dan Castellaneta]] as Homer Simpson, Barney Gumble (voice)
* [[Nancy Cartwright]] as Bart Simpson, Ralph Wiggum (voice)
* [[Yeardley Smith]] as Lisa Simpson (voice)
* [[Maurice LaMarche]] as [[Odin Borson|Odin]] (voice)
* [[Dawnn Lewis]] as [[Ravonna Renslayer]] (voice)
* [[Tom Hiddleston]] as [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]] (voice)

==Music==
Some of the music provided by [[Marvel Studios]] featured in the short was from Alan Silvestri's soundtrack in ''[[The Avengers (film)|The Avengers]]''.

==Trivia==
*This short film was released alongside "[[Journey into Mystery]]", the fifth episode of ''[[Loki (TV series)|Loki]]''.
*During the Springfield Avengers scene, Rainier Wolfcastle, as Giant Man, holds up a sign reading "This Is What Happens When Disney Buys Marvel And Fox", referencing the [[The Walt Disney Company|company]]'s acquisitions of [[Marvel Entertainment]] and [[21st Century Fox]].
*The poster is a parody of ''[[Avengers: Endgame]]''.
*The production team originally had plans to include a cameo of [[Stan Lee]], but were dissuaded by [[Marvel Studios]] who informed them that they no longer feature Lee in recent Marvel Studios films following his death.
