'''''Hello Aloha''''' is a Walt Disney cartoon released [[February 19]], [[1952]] starring [[Goofy]].

==Characters==
*[[Goofy|George Geef]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*Mr. Geef's Boss (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]])
*[[wikipedia:Harry Owens|Harry Owens]] - Narrator

==Plot==
George Geef (Goofy) has been overworked for weeks and all he can think about is vacationing. Geef takes a cruise ride and moves to [[Hawaii]]. Now he adapts to the Hawaiian lifestyle and becomes a sacrifice for a volcano goddess.
