'''''Little House on the Prairie''''', also known as '''''Laura Ingalls Wilder's Little House on the Prairie''''', is an American television miniseries which was broadcast on [[ABC]] as part of [[Walt Disney anthology series|The Wonderful World of Disney]] anthology series. It was produced in [[2004|2005]] and directed by David L. Cunningham it is based on the novel of the same name and it is a remake to the 1974 television series. The miniseries is filmed at Alberta, Canada.

==Plot==
The plot was based on the text from Laura Ingalls Wilder's novels ''Little House in the Big Woods'' and ''Little House on the Prairie''.

==Differences from the books==

There were several changes from the book when it was adapted

*According to the book the two horses were traded to the man in Iowa, while the adaptation tells that Pa shot one and traded the other for Pet and Patty.
*The meeting between Pa and Mr. Edwards was added to the adaptation.
*Mr. Scott was the one who helped Pa dig the well in the book, not Mr. Edwards.
*In the book, Mr. Edwards did chores for the Ingalls while Pa went to town, while Mrs. Scott attended the family while they were sick.
*The Indian war dance was changed heavily, to the settlers building the stockade, instead of just letting it go without a fight, as the book says
*The Ingalls see a wagon that is not moving and filled with sick people at the end of the book, while the adaptation portrays this at the start.
