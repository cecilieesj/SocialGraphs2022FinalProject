'''''The Walt Disney Christmas Show''''' is a Christmas television special, originally broadcast on Christmas Day [[1951]]. The then-upcoming Disney film,Â ''[[Peter Pan (film)|Peter Pan]]'' was promoted in this special, along with a reissue of ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]''.

==Segments==
*''[[The Band Concert]]'' (1935) (short film shown in almost its entirety as part of this TV special)
*''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]'' (1937) (short clips from the movie are shown in ten different languages)
*''[[Bambi (film)|Bambi]]'' (1942) (the scene where Bambi meets Thumper is shown in Hindi, and set to authentic Indian music)
*''[[Donald and Pluto]]'' (1936) (short film shown in its entirety as part of this TV special)
*''[[Song of the South]]'' (1946) (a clip is shown)
