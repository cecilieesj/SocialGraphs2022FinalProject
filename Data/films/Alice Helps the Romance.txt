'''''Alice Helps the Romance''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
When [[Julius the Cat|Julius]]' sweetheart is stolen by a rival, Alice arranges a devious plot to discredit his rival and bring them back together.
