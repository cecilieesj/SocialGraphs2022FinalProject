'''''Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Collection of All-Time Favorites: The Magic Years''''' is a ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs]]'' video released on [[July 22]], [[1997]].

==Description==
''Presenting the second volume of the finest collection of Sing-Along Songs available anywhere! Now kids of every age can sing, dance and play along with their favorite characters from the greatest Disney films ever made. And the collection features restored footage and exciting new graphics never before seen!''

''This volume contains magical musical moments from such unforgettable feature-length films as Cinderella, Peter Pan and Mary Poppins, as well as the title song from TV's Mickey Mouse Club, which became a cultural phenomenon. It's the best of the best from The Magic Years (1950-1973) of the Disney Studio!''

==Trivia==
*Hosted by [[Professor Owl]] (voiceover only, before all songs).
*Features songs from [[1950]]â€“[[1973]].
*This volume with the other ''Collection of All-Time Favorites'' videos in the UK were released in a set instead of separately.
*All the songs except "That's What Makes the World Go Round", "Once Upon a Dream", "Oo-De-Lally", "Bella Notte", "Higitus Figitus" and "A Spoonful of Sugar" are updated with new lyrics.
*Unlike ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Zip-A-Dee-Doo-Dah|Zip-A-Dee-Doo-Dah]]'', this video features the full version of the "[[Mickey Mouse March]]".
*Unlike ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs: The Bare Necessities|The Bare Necessities]]'', [[Jiminy Cricket]]'s voice was not heard during "[[I Wan'na Be Like You]]".
