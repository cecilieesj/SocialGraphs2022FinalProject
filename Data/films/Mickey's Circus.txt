'''''Mickey's Circus''''' is an animated [[Mickey Mouse]] short that was released in [[1936]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey's circus has a performance that's dominated - and wrecked - by the orphans and by Donald's destructive trained sea lions. The short marks the first appearance of [[Salty the Seal]].

==Characters==
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Walt Disney]])
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Salty the Seal]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[The Orphans]]
