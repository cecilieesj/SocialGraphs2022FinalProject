'''''Luck Luck Ki Baat''''' is an [[India]]n produced ‘made-for-TV’ movie on Disney Channel India. It is a remake of the [[2001]] [[Disney Channel Original Movies|Disney Channel Original Movie]] ''[[The Luck of the Irish]]''.

==Plot==
The movie centers around a 16-year old Amit who is a class hero and an incredibly lucky student who attributes all his luck to his special gold coin. In a bid to find out about his heritage, he visits a local fair styled around Arabian Nights where the mystery deepens as he encounters a series of mystical and sinister characters; amidst colourful stalls, Arabian dancers and magical tricks. By the next day his luck starts to wear thin as he enters the unknown yet familiar world of magic, genies and more.
