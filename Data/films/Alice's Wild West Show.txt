'''''Alice's Wild West Show''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Synopsis==
Alice and her friends put on a Wild West Show for the neighborhood kids where she regales the audience with her tales of cowboys and Indians.
