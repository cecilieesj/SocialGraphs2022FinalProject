'''''Untitled Damon Lindelof Star Wars film''''' is an upcoming ''[[Star Wars]]'' film.

==Production==
On March 14, 2022, it was reported that Damon Lindelof was writing a brand new Star Wars feature film.<ref>[https://www.slashfilm.com/799024/is-damon-lindelof-making-a-star-war/ Is Damon Lindelof Making A Star War?]</ref>

On October 24, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy]] was set to direct the film.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/10/star-wars-damon-lindelof-lucasfilm-ms-marvel-sharmeen-obaid-chinoy-1235152896/ Secret ‘Star Wars’ Film From Damon Lindelof And Lucasfilm Sets ‘Ms Marvel’s Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy As Director]</ref>

Later that day, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that Justin Britt-Gibson would co-write the screenplay, and that the film would take place after the events of ''[[Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker]]''.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/star-wars-inside-damon-lindelofs-movie-1235247453/ A Secret Writers Room, a Rising Scribe and a Post-‘Skywalker’ Timeline: A Look Inside Damon Lindelof’s ’Star Wars’ Movie (Exclusive)]</ref>
