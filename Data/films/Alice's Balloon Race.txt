'''''Alice's Balloon Race''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]''.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius enter a madcap balloon race where Julius gamely attempts to keep their balloon aloft.
