'''''Arizona Sheepdog''''' is a documentary short film that was originally released to theaters by [[Buena Vista Distribution]] on [[May 25]], [[1955]] as a double-bill with ''[[Davy Crockett, King of the Wild Frontier]]''. It was later included in the ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Disneyland]]'' episode, "[[The Best Doggoned Dog in the World]]".
