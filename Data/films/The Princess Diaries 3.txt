'''''The Princess Diaries 3''''' is a placeholder title for an upcoming American romantic comedy film, and a sequel to [[2004]]'s ''[[The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement]]''.

==Production==
On November 15, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that a third film in the franchise is officially in development, with Aadrita Mukerji writing the screenplay, though [[Anne Hathaway]] had not yet signed a deal to return.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/princess-diaries-3-in-the-works-disney-1235261402/ New ‘Princess Diaries’ Movie in the Works at Disney (Exclusive)]</ref>
