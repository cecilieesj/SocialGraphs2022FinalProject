'''The Prince and the Pauper''' is a [[1962]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally broadcast on [[NBC]] as a three-part episode on ''[[Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color]]'' on [[March 11]], [[March 18]], and [[March 25]], 1962. The telefilm was directed by Don Chaffey.

==Plot==
Based on [[Mark Twain]]'s classic tale, this lively 16th-century-set comedy drama chronicles the misadventures and the lessons learned by two disparate and discontented look-a-likes who swear that the grass really is greener on the other side of the fence.
