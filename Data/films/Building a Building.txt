'''''Building a Building''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[December 20]], [[1932]] for a limited run before its widespread release on [[January 7]], [[1933]].

==Summary==
Mickey Mouse operates a steam shovel; Pegleg Pete is his foreman; Minnie Mouse sells box lunches. Mickey must save the day when Pete makes advances to Minnie.

==Characters==
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Walt Disney]])
*[[Pete]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Minnie Mouse]] (voiced by [[Marcellite Garner]])
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by Pinto Colvig)

==Notes==
*This is a remake of the ''[[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit]]'' short ''[[Sky Scrappers]]''.
*The "LUNCH!!!" call was later used in the 1945 ''[[Goofy]]'' cartoon ''[[Tiger Trouble]]'' and in the ''[[Mickey Mouse Works]]'' short ''[[Sandwich Makers]]''.
*This short was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Short Subject (Cartoon). It lost to ''[[Three Little Pigs]]''.

==Video game appearances==
*''Building a Building'' is an area in the [[Timeless River]] world in ''[[Kingdom Hearts II]]''. Other shorts that are featured as areas of that world are ''[[Steamboat Willie]]'', ''[[Gulliver Mickey]]'', ''[[Mickey's Orphans]]'' and ''[[The Fire Fighters]]''.
*One of the projector transition levels in ''[[Epic Mickey 2: The Power of Two]]'' is based on this cartoon.
*This cartoon serves as the inspiration for one of the minigames in the Classic Kingdom games in ''[[Kingdom Hearts III]]''.
