}}'''''Islands of the Sea''''' is a [[1960]] Disney nature documentary short. Though not an official entry in the [[True-Life Adventures|''True-Life Adventures'']] series, it was featured as a bonus feature on their Walt Disney Legacy Collection DVD release.

==Synopsis==
The viewer witnesses a panorama of animals inhabiting the islands among the least known of the world: Galapagos, Guadalupe, Falkland and Midway.
