'''''A Disney Cartoon Jubilee''''' (originally titled '''''Walt Disney's 50th Anniversary Show''''') is a [[1973]] Disney compilation film featuring animated shorts. It was released theatrically in Europe on a double bill with ''[[The Magic of Walt Disney World]]''.
