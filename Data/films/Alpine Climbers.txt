'''''Alpine Climbers''''' is a [[Mickey Mouse]] cartoon that was released on [[July 25]], [[1936]].

==Synopsis==
Up in the Swiss Alps, Mickey Mouse tangles with a mother eagle, Donald Duck scraps with an edelweiss-stealing goat and Pluto gets inebriated with a St. Bernard.

==Characters==
*[[Mickey Mouse]] (voiced by [[Walt Disney]])
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Pluto]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Bolivar]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]])
*Mountain Goat Kid
*Mountain Goat Father (voiced by Clarence Nash)
*Eagle Chicks (voiced by Jimmy MacDonald)
*Mother Eagle (voiced by Jimmy MacDonald)

==Trivia==
*''Alpine Climbers'' is one of the shorts featured as a level in ''[[Epic Mickey]]''.
*On the ''Walt Disney Cartoon Classics: Limited Gold Editions - Life with Mickey'' VHS, the cartoon uses the 1948 reissue titles.
