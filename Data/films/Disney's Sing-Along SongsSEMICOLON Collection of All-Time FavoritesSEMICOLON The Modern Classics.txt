'''''Disney's Sing-Along Songs: Collection of All-Time Favorites: The Modern Classics''''' is a ''[[Disney's Sing-Along Songs]]'' video released on [[July 22]], [[1997]].

==Description==
''Presenting the third volume of the finest collection of Sing-Along Songs available anywhere! Now kids of every age can sing, dance and play along with their favorite characters from the greatest Disney films ever made. And the collection features restored footage and exciting new graphics never before seen!''

''The art of animation underwent a major Renaissance in the late 1980s when a whole new world of extraordinary films was introduced. State-of-the-art animation, great storytelling and unforgettable music combined to produce one smash hit after another-including The Little Mermaid, Beauty and the Beast, and The Lion King! This volume contains some of your favorite tunes from the most recent Disney movies, as well as two fun-filled new songs from Disney's 35th Animated Masterpiece, Hercules! Here's the best of the best from The Modern Classics (1989-1997) of the Disney Studio.''
