'''''Alice's Brown Derby''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Another horse race with Julius riding a mechanical horse and Pete unsuccessfully trying to cheat to win.
