'''''Playful Pan''''' is a black-and-white ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that debuted on [[December 27]], [[1930]].

==Synopsis==
The Greek God Pan comes to Earth, coaxing music and dance out of the flora and fauna. Lightning strikes start a fire that nearly burns everything. Pan uses his music to lure the flames into a nearby pond where they are safely extinguished.
