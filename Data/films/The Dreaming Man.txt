}}'''''The Dreaming Man''''' (Chinese: '''假如王子睡着了 ''Jiǎ rú wáng zǐ shuì zhe le''''', lit: "If the Prince falls asleep") is a Chinese romantic comedy film starring Chen Bolin, Lin Yun and Zhang Yunlong. This film is a loose remake of the [[1995]] [[Hollywood Pictures]] film ''[[While You Were Sleeping]]''.

It is the first Chinese film produced by SMG Pictures and [[Walt Disney Pictures]] China.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://chinafilminsider.com/disney-unveils-first-chinese-language-rom-com/|title=Disney Unveils Its First Chinese-Language Rom-Com|publisher=China News Sider|access-date=2017-06-23|language=en}}</ref> The film was released on [[December 8]], [[2017]].<ref name="IE 2">{{cite news|title=《假如王子睡着了》定档 陈柏霖林允演绎童话爱情|url=http://ent.sina.com.cn/m/c/2017-11-02/doc-ifynmzrs6012706.shtml?cre=tianyi&mod=pcentc&loc=11&r=97&doct=0&rfunc=26&tj=none&tr=97|accessdate=11-02-2017|work=Sina|date=11-02-2017}}</ref>
