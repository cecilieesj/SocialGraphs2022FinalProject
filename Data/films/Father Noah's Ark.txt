'''''Father Noah's Ark''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that debuted on [[April 8]], [[1933]].

==Synopsis==
A musical retelling of the Biblical story of Noah and his ark, with a boatload of animals setting sail on the most famous voyage of all.

==Characters==
*[[Noah (character)|Noah]] (voiced by Allan Watson)
*Noah's Wife (voiced by [[Lucille La Verne]])
*Hem, Shem, and Japeth (voiced by Delos Jewkes)
*Mrs. Hem, Shem, and Japeth (voiced by Dorothy Compton)
*Anthropomorphic Sun
