'''''Dragon Around''''' is a ''[[Donald Duck]]'' and [[Chip and Dale|''Chip 'n' Dale'']] animated short, released on [[July 16]], [[1954]].

==Synopsis==
Construction worker Donald tries to oust Chip 'n' Dale out of their home with a dragon-looking steam shovel to build a highway, so the chipmunks become knights in shining armor to fight back.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Chip and Dale|Chip 'n' Dale]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]] and [[Dessie Flynn]])
