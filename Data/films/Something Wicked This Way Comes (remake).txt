{{NeedImage}}'''''Something Wicked This Way Comes''''' is a remake of [[Something Wicked This Way Comes|the 1983 Disney horror fantasy film and Ray Bradbury's novel of the same name]].

==Remake==
According to Deadline, Disney is making a remake of ''Something Wicked This Way Comes'' with [[Seth Grahame-Smith]] writing the script, making his directorial debut, and producing with David Katzenberg from their producing banner KatzSmith Productions, also, Grahame-Smith wants to focus mostly on Ray Bradbury's source material from the book.<ref>{{cite web|last=Fleming|first=Mike|title=Disney, Seth Grahame- Smith Making New Film Of Ray Bradbury’s ‘Something Wicked This Way Comes’|url=http://deadline.com/2014/03/disney-seth-grahame-smith-making-new-film-of-ray-bradburys-something-wicked-this-way-comes/|publisher=Deadline|accessdate=2014-03-12}}</ref>
