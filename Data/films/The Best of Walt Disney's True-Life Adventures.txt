}}'''''The Best of Walt Disney's True Life Adventures''''' is a [[1975]] compilation film constisting of select moments from Disney's Academy Award-winning ''[[True-Life Adventures]]'' series.
==Plot==
The film begins with a tribute to Walt Disney and his love of animals, as evidenced by characters from his early cartoons and his foray into feature-length fairy takes, and how his curiosity for animals in their natural habitats led him from fantasy to fact. Afterward, we delve into scenes from Disney's [[True-Life Adventure|''True-Life Adventure'']]''s'', including ''[[The Living Desert]]'', ''[[The African Lion]]'' and ''[[White Wilderness]]''.
