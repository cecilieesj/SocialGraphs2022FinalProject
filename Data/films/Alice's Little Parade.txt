'''''Alice's Little Parade''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
Julius leads an all-out war against the mice; but eventually has to defeat them single-handedly.
