'''''How to Have an Accident in the Home''''' is a ''[[Donald Duck]]'' short that was released on [[July 8]], [[1956]]. It was produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures|Buena Vista Film Distribution]].

==Summary==
J.J. Fate follows Donald through his day, showing how accidents can lurk in everyday occurrences.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*J.J. Fate (voiced by [[Bill Thompson]])

== Trivia ==
* This is Donald Duck's first appearance in an educational cartoon short. Donald would continue appearing in educational shorts until 1967.
* This is the final Donald Duck cartoon to be produced in CinemaScope, as well as the only educational Donald short produced in that process.
