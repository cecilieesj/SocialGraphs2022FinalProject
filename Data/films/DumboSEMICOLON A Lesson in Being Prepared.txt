'''''Dumbo: A Lesson in Being Prepared''''' is a [[1981]] Disney educational live-action/animated short, part of the ''Disney's Animated Classics: Lessons in Living'' series. The short features clips from ''[[Dumbo (1941 film)|Dumbo]]''.

==Synopsis==
[[Timothy Q. Mouse|Timothy Mouse]] pricks [[Dumbo (character)|Dumbo]] with a pin to make him perform despite the fact that Dumbo feels uncertain about his act, and the result is a crashing failure. The sequence makes a point of over-eager youngsters who try things before they are prepared.
