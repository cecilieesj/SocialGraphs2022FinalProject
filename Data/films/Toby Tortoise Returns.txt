'''''Toby Tortoise Returns''''' is a ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' cartoon starring [[Max Hare]] and [[Toby Tortoise]], and the sequel to ''[[The Tortoise and the Hare]]''.

==Synopsis==
A return bout between Max Hare and Toby Tortoise, this time mismatched in the boxing ring. After dominating the slow-moving Toby in the first round, Max finds himself unable to get a punch past Toby's powerful shell and resorts to dirty tricks which inevitably backfire.
