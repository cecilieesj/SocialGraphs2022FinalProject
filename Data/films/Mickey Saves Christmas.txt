'''''Mickey Saves Christmas''''' is an upcoming stop-motion Christmas special starring [[Mickey Mouse]] and friends, produced by [[Disney]] Television Animation and Stoopid Buddy Stoodios (''Robot Chicken''). It will premiere simultaneously on [[ABC]], The [[Disney Channel]], [[Disney Junior]], and [[Disney XD]] on November 27, 2022, and will be available to stream on [[Disney+]] the day after. This will notably make it the first TV special produced by Disney Television Animation to air on ABC since [[1999]]'s ''[[Winnie the Pooh: A Valentine for You]]''.

==Summary==
[[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]], [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]] and their pals attempt to celebrate the perfect Christmas at their snowy cabin. However, when [[Pluto]] causes [[Santa Claus|Santa]] to lose all the presents on his sleigh, the friends travel to the North Pole on a quest to save Christmas and find the true meaning of the holiday.

==Trivia==
*This is the first Mickey Mouse special to be in stop-motion.
**However, it is not the first time Mickey has appeared in stop-motion; [[2019|three years prior]], he and the gang appeared in two shorts of stop-motion Christmas interstitials on [[Disney Junior]].
