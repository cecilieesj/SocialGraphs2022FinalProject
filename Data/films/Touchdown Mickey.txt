'''''Touchdown Mickey''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[October 7]], [[1932]].

==Synopsis==
It's the big game of American Football with Mickey's Manglers vs. the Alley Cats. Sportscaster Goofy keeps track of what's going on (badly) as Mickey tries to lead his team to victory. Things start looking bleak with the surprisingly roughness of the Alley Cats, but some encouraging support from Minnie gives Mickey the confidence he needs to win the game.
