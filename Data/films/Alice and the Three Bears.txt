'''''Alice and the Three Bears''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
In a twist on the traditional story, Alice comes across three bears operating a still, and she and Julius have to fight their way free.

==Trivia==
*The title is a reference to the fairy tale Goldilocks and the Three Bears.
