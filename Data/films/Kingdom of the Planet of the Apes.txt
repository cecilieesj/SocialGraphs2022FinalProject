'''''Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes''''' is an upcoming American science fiction action film directed by Wes Ball from a screenplay by Bell and Josh Friedman, and produced by Peter Chernin, Joe Hartwick Jr., David Starke, Rick Jaffa, and Amanda Silver. It is intended to be the sequel to ''War for the Planet of the Apes'' (2017) and the fourth installment in the ''Planet of the Apes'' reboot series. It is scheduled to be released on [[May 24]], [[2024]].

==Production==
On December 3, 2019, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that Wes Ball would write and direct the film, after having previously worked with Reeves on a ''Mouse Guard'' film. After that film was canceled by Disney following the merger, Disney approached Ball on developing a new ''Planet of the Apes'' film.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/new-planet-apes-movie-works-maze-runner-filmmaker-1258816/ New ‘Planet of the Apes’ Movie in the Works With ‘Maze Runner’ Filmmaker Wes Ball (Exclusive)]</ref>

On February 20, 2020, it was reported that  that the film would not be a reboot, instead chronicling "Ceaser's legacy".<ref>[https://collider.com/new-planet-of-the-apes-movie-not-a-reboot-says-director-wes-ball/ Wes Ball Suggests His ‘Planet of the Apes’ Movie Won’t Be a Reboot]</ref>

On August 22, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that Owen Teague was cast in the lead role.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/08/planet-of-the-apes-owen-teague-20th-century-1235097948/ ‘Planet Of The Apes’: Owen Teague To Star In New Film Installment For 20th Century]</ref>

On September 29, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that the officially was revealed to be ''Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes'', and that Freya Allen and Peter Macon had joined the cast in undisclosed roles. Filming is set to begin in October 2022, with a [[2024]] release window.<ref name="THR">[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/kingdom-of-the-planet-of-the-apes-cast-1235230071/Next ‘Planet of the Apes’ Cast, Title Unveiled]</ref>
