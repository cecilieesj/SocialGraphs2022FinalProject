'''''Steamboat Willie Redux''''' is a new hand-drawn short produced by [[Disney Consumer Products]]. It's basically a remake of [[Steamboat Willie|the original short]] in various graphic styles by different artists. It premiered at the [[D23|D23 Expo]] on [[August 10]], [[2013]]. It was shown at the "Mickey: The True Original" Exhibition in New York.
