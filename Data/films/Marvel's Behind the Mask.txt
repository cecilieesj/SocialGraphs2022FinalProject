'''''Marvel's Behind the Mask''''' is a documentary [[Marvel Entertainment|Marvel]] film. It is produced by Marvel New Media and distributed by [[Disney Platform Distribution]].

==Premise==
Marvel's Behind the Mask will feature guests from across Marvel's 80-year legacy, including the writers and artists behind the rise of characters like [[Black Panther]], [[Spider-Man (Miles Morales)|Miles Morales]], [[Ms. Marvel]], [[Luke Cage]], the [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]], [[Captain Marvel]], and many other characters from [[Marvel Comics]], highlighting Marvel's impact on pop culture and media.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.marvel.com/articles/tv-shows/marvel-behind-the-mask-now-streaming-disney-plus|title=Tune in Today: 'Marvel's Behind the Mask' Is Now Streaming Exclusively on Disney+|accessdate=February 12, 2021}}</ref>
