'''''The Litterbug''''' was the final theatrical short to star [[Donald Duck]], released on [[June 21]], [[1961]].

==Synopsis==
Donald shows various type of conscious and unconscious litterbugging.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]]
*[[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]]
*[[Mr. Busy]] from ''[[Lady and the Tramp]]''

==Voice Cast==
*[[John Dehner]] - Narrator

==Releases==
'''Television'''
* ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color]]'', episode #9.22: "[[A Square Peg in a Round Hole]]"
* ''[[The Mickey Mouse Club|The New Mickey Mouse Club]]'', episode D-066, April 17, 1978
'''DVD'''
* ''[[Walt Disney Treasures]]: The Chronological Donald, Volume Four''

==Notes==
* ''The Litterbug'' was the final "Donald Duck" short that was released theatrically; it is not, however, Donald's last appearance. One 'commercial' featurette (''[[Steel and America]]'') and two educational shorts, ''[[Donald's Fire Survival Plan]]'' and ''[[Family Planning]]'' would be produced four, five, and six years later.
* This short along with [[Donald and the Wheel]] were the first "Donald Duck" shorts to be made with the Xerox process.
