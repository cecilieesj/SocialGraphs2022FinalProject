'''''Elemental''''' is an upcoming American computer-animated film produced by [[Walt Disney Pictures]] and [[Pixar|Pixar Animation Studios]]. It will be Pixar's 27th animated feature and is scheduled to be theatrically released on [[June 16]], [[2023]].<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/05/pixar-elemental-release-date-concept-art-1235024683/]</ref>

==Synopsis==
In a city where fire, water, earth, and air residents live together, a fiery young woman and a go-with-the-flow guy are about to discover something elemental: how much they actually have in common.

==Release==
''Elemental'' is scheduled to be released in theaters in the [[United States]] on [[June 16]], [[2023]] by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]].

Footage of the film was shown at the [[Disney D23]] on [[September 9]].<ref>[https://d23.com/pixar-and-walt-disney-animation-studios-to-share-never-before-seen-footage-and-experiences-at-d23-expo-2022/ Pixar and Walt Disney Animation Studios to Share ... - D23https://d23.com › pixar-and-walt-disney-animation-stud...]</ref>

The Teaser Trailer for the film was released on [[November 17]], [[2022]].

==Trivia==
*This is the second Pixar film to be released on [[June 16]], after ''[[Cars 3]]''.
*This is the second Pixar film to be directed by [[Peter Sohn]], after ''[[The Good Dinosaur]]''.
*Wade makes a cameo appearance in ''[[Lightyear]]'' on the label of a bottled drink called "Wade Water".
*This will be the first Pixar film to use the Disney's 100th anniversary logo.
**This is also the first Pixar film to use the new 2022 Disney logo.
*This is the third Pixar film of the 2020s to be produced in a 1:85:1 aspect ratio after ''[[Luca]] and [[Turning Red]].''
*This is the fourth Disney animated film of the 2020s to be produced in a 1:85:1 aspect ratio, after ''[[Luca]]'', ''[[Encanto]]'' and ''[[Turning Red]]''.
*The air elementals in this film resemble the cloud characters from ''[[Partly Cloudy]]'', a short film Peter Sohn directed for.
