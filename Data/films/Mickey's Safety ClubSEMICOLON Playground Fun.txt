'''''Mickey's Safety Club: Playground Fun''''' is part of ''[[Mickey's Safety Club]]'', a series of educational films, released in [[1989]] and [[1990]]. [[Mickey Mouse]], [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]]. and [[Daisy Duck]] teach about the importance of safety while having fun at the playground.
