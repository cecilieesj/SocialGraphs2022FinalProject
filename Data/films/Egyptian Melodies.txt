'''''Egyptian Melodies''''' is a black-and-white ''[[Silly Symphony]]'' that was released on [[August 21]], [[1931]].

==Summary==
When a curious spider makes his way into the tomb of an ancient Egyptian, he discovers the strange wall paintings (and Mummies) have come to life for him.

==Trivia==
*The background animation of the trip through the pyramid was re-used in the [[Mickey Mouse|''Mickey Mouse'']] cartoon ''[[The Mad Doctor]]''.
