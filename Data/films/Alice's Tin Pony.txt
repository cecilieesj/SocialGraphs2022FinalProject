'''''Alice's Tin Pony''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius are driving a train carrying a large payroll which attracts the attention of Pete the Bear and his gang.
