'''''How to Be a Detective''''' is an animated short starring [[Goofy]], released on [[December 12]], [[1952]].

==Synopsis==
Goofy takes on the role of "Johnny Eyeball, Private Eye" as he takes on the case of the missing "Al."

==Characters==
*[[Goofy]] (voiced by [[Pinto Colvig]])
*[[Peg Pete|Dame]] (voiced by [[June Foray]])
*[[Pete|Al Muldoon]] (voiced by [[Billy Bletcher]])
*[[Weasels|Weasel]] (voiced by Alan Reed)
