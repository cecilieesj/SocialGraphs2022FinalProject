'''''Lion Around''''' is a [[Donald Duck|''Donald Duck'']] and [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie|''Huey, Dewey, and Louie'']] cartoon. The plotline of the short is a parody of the book, that was loosely based on the novel ''The Mountain Lion'' by Robert William Murphy.

==Synopsis==
Donald's nephews disguise themselves as a mountain lion to scare Donald, when a real mountain lion comes wandering along.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]] (voiced by Clarence Nash)
*[[Louie the Mountain Lion]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]])
