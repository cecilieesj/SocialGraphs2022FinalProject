'''''Orphans' Picnic''''' is a [[Mickey Mouse|''Mickey Mouse'']] and [[Donald Duck|''Donald Duck'']] cartoon that was released on [[February 15]], [[1936]].

==Synopsis==
Donald is supervising the ill-behaved orphans at Mickey's picnic and sticks to his task, even when the brats steal the food and give the Duck a bee sandwich.

==Trivia==
* The song that Donald leads the orphans in is "A Stein Song". The original lyrics are "For it's always fair weather / When good fellows get together / With a stein on the table / And a good song ringing clear." To get the film accepted by the Hays Code, the "stein on the table" lyric, which is in reference to a beer mug, is changed to "a handshake of friendship".
