Henry Jackman|release = October 31st, 2019|time = 87 mins}}The Tuck And Rowley Movie is a 2019 American computer-animated action comedy musical film about two brothers named Tuck and Rowley (Jesse Bradford and Leonardo DiCaprio). It is directed by Rob Letterman and Justice Smith. 

== Plot ==
Tuck (Jesse Bradford) and Rowley (Leonardo DiCaprio) are two brothers. 24 mins later, Tuck meets Joey (Blake Shelton), a cool kid who watches Fresh Beat Band Of Spies. Willard (Harland Williams) faces Jack (Tom Cruise) for a demolition race. 

Tuck and Rowley plays Tennis until a mean coach named Amber (Macaulay Culkin) arrives and pushes Tuck in the face. One day, evil villain named Momotoro (Tom Holland) plans about rule the world. One day earlier,  Tuck meets a nerd girl in class named Jon (Ashley Tisdale).

Tuck and Rowley sings together ( "Tuck And Rowley Theme Song" ) but evil Momotoro comes to rule the world. Will Willard saves the world? find out.
