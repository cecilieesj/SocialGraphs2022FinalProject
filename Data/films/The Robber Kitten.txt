'''''The Robber Kitten''''' is a [[1935]] ''[[Silly Symphonies]]'' short.

==Synopsis==
Ambrose the kitten renames himself Butch and runs away from home with plans of seeking adventure as a highwayman. A scary encounter with a genuine robber, however, soon sends him flying home to Mom.

==Characters==
*[[Ambrose]] (voiced by Shirley Reed)
*[[Dirty Bill]] from Cootie Hill (voiced by [[Billy Bletcher]])
*Ambrose's Mom (voiced by Shirley Reed)
*Tarzan (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*Horse (voiced by Clarence Nash)
