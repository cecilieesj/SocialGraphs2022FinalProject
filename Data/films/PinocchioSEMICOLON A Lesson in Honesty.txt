'''''Pinocchio: A Lesson in Honesty''''' is a [[1978]] Disney educational live-action/animated short, part of the ''Disney's Animated Classics: Lessons in Living'' series. The short features clips from ''[[Pinocchio (film)|Pinocchio]]''.

==Synopsis==
A boy who plays hooky from school learns that lies only increase a problem.
