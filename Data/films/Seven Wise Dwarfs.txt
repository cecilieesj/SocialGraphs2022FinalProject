'''''Seven Wise Dwarfs''''' (also known as '''''7 Wise Dwarfs''''') is a promotional animated short of the wartime from [[1941]], starring the Seven Dwarfs.

==Synopsis==
Reworked animation from ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]'' has the dwarfs investing the proceeds of their diamond mine into Canadian War Bonds.
