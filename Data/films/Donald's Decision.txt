'''''Donald's Decision''''' is a wartime cartoon starring [[Donald Duck]] and a remake of the [[1938]] cartoon ''[[Donald's Better Self]]'' which additionally uses animation from ''[[Self Control]]''.

==Synopsis==
Donald wrestles with his conscience in a decision of whether or not to buy war bonds. He is hounded by two spirits a "Devil Donald" who encourages him to be stingy and that war bonds are a scam and an "Angel Donald" who says that he'll be helping the war effort by making a loan to the US government.
