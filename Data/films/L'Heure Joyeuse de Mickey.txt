'''''L'Heure Joyeuse de Mickey''''' ('''''Mickey's Happy Hour''''') is a [[1934]] Disney compilation film that played in France and Belgium and featured assorted ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' and ''[[Silly Symphonies]]'' cartoons.

==Cartoons==
* ''[[Giantland]]''
* ''[[The Pied Piper]]''
* ''[[Lullaby Land]]''
* ''[[The Grasshopper and the Ants]]''
* ''[[Mickey's Gala Premier]]''
* ''[[The Three Little Pigs]]''
* ''[[Funny Little Bunnies]]''

==Trivia==
* On his grand tour of Europe in 1935, [[Walt Disney]], while in Paris, ran across a Lord Byron cinema on the Avenue des Champs-Élysées. It was his discovery of this cinema's program that convinced Walt that feature-length cartoons would be accepted.
