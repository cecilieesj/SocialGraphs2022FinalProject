'''''The Lottery''''' is a [[1989]] film directed by [[Garry Marshall]] shown at the then [[Disney's Hollywood Studios|Disney MGM Studios]] in line for the first versions of the [[Studio Backlot Tour]]. It was a short comedy film, demonstrating special effects and production making, it stars [[Bette Midler]] as a lady who chases a lottery ticket around New York.

The film was shot at Disney MGM Studios on the New York Set. It took a crew of over 100 people to produce.<ref>[http://vimeo.com/6108702/]</ref>
