'''''Director by Night''''' is a documentary film written and directed by Anthony Giacchino, as a behind the scenes for the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] television special ''[[Werewolf by Night (film)|Werewolf by Night]]''. It was released on [[November 4]], [[2022]].

==Synopsis==
''Director by Night'' tells the story of two Academy Award winning brothers and their different ways of telling stories. [[Michael Giacchino]], renowned film composer, makes his directing debut with ''Werewolf by Night''. His brother, veteran documentary filmmaker Anthony Giacchino, chronicles the experience, and explores the duo's filmmaking origins in the small [[New Jersey]] town where they grew up.<ref>[https://www.disneyplus.com/en-gb/movies/director-by-night/7LAI8wd0sRWN Director by Night Synopsis]</ref>
