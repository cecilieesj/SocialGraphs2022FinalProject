'''''Casey at the Bat''''' is an animated short of a segment in ''[[Make Mine Music]]''. It was reissued as a stand-alone short on [[July 16]], [[1954]].

==Synopsis==
The Disney version of the poem about baseball slugger Casey, and his valiant stand to try to win the game for Mudville.

==Characters==
*[[Casey (Make Mine Music)|Casey]]
*Cooney
*Flynn
*Jimmy Blake
*Pitcher
*Umpire
*[[Ladies from the Stand]]

==Voice Cast==
*[[Jerry Colonna]] - Narrator
*[[Jimmy MacDonald]] - Umpire, Mudville Coach
*[[Dessie Flynn]] - Cutie from the Stands

==Trivia==
*A close-up view of the musicians in the band reveals some of the Disney animators including four of [[Disney's Nine Old Men|the Nine Old Men]], [[Ward Kimball]] (tubist), [[Frank Thomas]] (drummer), [[Ollie Johnston]] (trombonist), and [[Marc Davis]] (flautist), and producer [[Walt Disney]] (triangle player).
