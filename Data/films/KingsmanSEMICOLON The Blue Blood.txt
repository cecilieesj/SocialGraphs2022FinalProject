'''''Kingsman: The Blue Blood''''' is an upcoming action spy comedy film. Based on the comic book series ''The Secret Service'' (later retitled to ''Kingsman'') by Mark Millar and Dave Gibbons, the film serves as the fourth installment in the Kingsman film series and the sequel to ''Kingsman: The Golden Circle'' (2017). The film is produced by [[20th Century Studios]] and Marv Films.

==Cast==
*[[Colin Firth]] as Harry Hart
*Taron Egerton as Gary "Eggsy" Unwin

==Production==
On May 5, 2017, Matthew Vaughn stated that a sequel to Kingsman: The Golden Circle is currently in development, adding that he and co-writer Jane Goldman mapped out the plot during production on the second film.<ref>[https://variety.com/2017/film/news/matthew-vaughn-third-kingsman-film-already-planned-1202410329/ Matthew Vaughn Says Third ‘Kingsman’ Film Already Planned]</ref>

On September 18, 2020, it was given the title of ''Kingsman: The Blue Blood''.<ref>[https://screenrant.com/kingsman-prequel-excited-nervous/ The King's Man: 5 Things Fans Are Excited To See In The Prequel (& 5 That Make Them Nervous)]</ref>

On December 13, 2021, it was reported that filming is set to begin in September 2022.<ref>[https://comicbook.com/movies/news/kingsman-3-september-2022-filming-sequel/ Kingsman 3 Starts Filming in September (Exclusive)]</ref>

On July 2, 2022, it was reported that the filming schedule had been pushed to 2023.<ref>[https://comicbook.com/movies/news/kingsman-3-taron-egerton-says-sequel-starts-shooting-next-year/ Kingsman 3: Taron Egerton Says Sequel Starts Shooting Next Year]</ref>
