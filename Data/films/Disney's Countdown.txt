'''''Disney's Countdown''''' is a [[1988]] Disney educational animated short film.

==Synopsis==
This film uses Disney animation and rhyme to stimulate interest in numbers. [[Mickey Mouse]], [[Minnie Mouse]], [[The Three Little Pigs (characters)|the Three Little Pigs]], the Seven Dwarfs and others demonstrate what numbers and their sets mean.
