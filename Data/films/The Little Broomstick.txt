}}'''''The Little Broomstick''''' was a cancelled [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]] animated feature film that would have been released in [[1980]] and it was based on the [[1971]] novel by British writer, Mary Stewart. 

It was instead released under the name ''Mary and the Witch's Flower'' in 2017 by Japan-based animation studio Studio Ponoc in conjunction with Toho.

==Synopsis==
The tale centered on a little girl, Mary, who was led by a black cat. Tib, to discover a magical broomstick that took her to Endor College, a dark and scary training ground for witches.
