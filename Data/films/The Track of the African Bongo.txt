"'''The Track of the African Bongo'''" is a [[1977]] telefilm produced by [[Walt Disney Productions]]. It was originally aired on [[NBC]] as a two-part episode on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'' on [[April 3]], and [[April 10]], [[1977]]. The telefilm was directed by Frank Zuniga.

==Synopsis==
A young warrior of the Kikuyu Tribe discovers the whereabouts of a rare antelope, the bongo, and starts a three-fold race between hunters and rescuers.

*Johnny Noava
