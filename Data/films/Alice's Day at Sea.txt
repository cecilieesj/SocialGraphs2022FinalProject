}}'''''Alice's Day at Sea''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' series. It was animated by the [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney Brothers Cartoon Studio]] and released by M. J. Winkler in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Synopsis==
Alice is entranced by an old sailor's stories of surviving a shipwreck which inspires a dream of her visiting the denizens of an underwater zoo.

==Theatrical poster==
According to the Guinness Book of World Records, the poster for this film was sold for $36,534 at Christies auction, the most valuable cartoon poster of all time.

* This is the very first animated short film in general to be produced by [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] instead of [[Laugh-O-Gram Studio]].
* This is also the first ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' short to be produced by [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|WDAS]].
