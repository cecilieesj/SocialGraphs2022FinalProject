'''''A Dream Called Walt Disney World''''' is a [[1981]] Disney short promotional film travelogue of [[Walt Disney World]].

==Trivia==
*The footage of [[Pirates of the Caribbean]] is actually from the [[1968]] ''[[Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color]]'' episode "[[From the Pirates of the Caribbean to the World of Tomorrow]]", and is footage of the [[Disneyland]] version.
