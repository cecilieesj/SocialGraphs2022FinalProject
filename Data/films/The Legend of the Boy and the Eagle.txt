'''''The Legend of the Boy and the Eagle''''' is a [[1967]] American live-action [[The Walt Disney Company|Walt Disney]] film.

== Plot ==
A Hopi Indian boy is banished from his village after he defies tribal law and frees a sacred, sacrificial eagle. After surviving in the wilderness, he returns to his village where he is again rejected. Fleeing, the boy climbs a cliff and jumps off but before he reaches the ground, he turns into an eagle.

==Cast==
* [[Stanford Lomakema]] as The Eagle Boy
* Frank Dekova as Narrator (voice)
