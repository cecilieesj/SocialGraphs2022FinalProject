'''''Project Florida''''' is a [[1971]] film documenting the construction progress of the [[Walt Disney World]]Â with archival clips of Walt Disney's Florida presentation, concept art, footage of the Walt Disney World Preview Center, and construction of the resort.

The film was presented on ''[[The Wonderful World of Disney (NBC)|The Wonderful World of Disney]]'', following the conclusion of ''[[The Adventures of Bullwhip Griffin]]''. An 15-minute excerpt of the film along with relevant footage of its presentation was released on DVD with ''[[Magic Kingdom: Imagineering the Magic]]''. The full film has not been released on home video.
