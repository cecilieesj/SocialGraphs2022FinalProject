}}'''''Petal to the Metal''''' is a cartoon produced by [[The Walt Disney Company]] in [[1992]] starring [[Bonkers D. Bobcat]]. It was released theatrically with ''[[3 Ninjas]]''.

==Summary==
In delivering a bouquet of flowers across town to a stunning starlet named [[Fawn Deer]], a delirious delivery cat named [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]] turns a relatively easy assignment into a catalog of catastrophes. Racing against the clock to meet his five-minute delivery deadline, Bonkers encounters a wide array of ridiculous roadblocks, ranging from banana peels to the world's slowest taxi driver, in his frantic attempts to meet the deadline and keep his job.
