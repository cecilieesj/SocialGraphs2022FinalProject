'''''Alice's Channel Swim''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
[[Julius the Cat|Julius]] and [[Pete]] match swimming skills in a race across the English Channel with [[Alice]] as the referee.
