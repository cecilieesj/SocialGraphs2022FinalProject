'''''Alice Chops the Suey''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice and Julius have an adventure in Chinatown. Alice is captured by a jinn and is rescued by Julius in a madcap chase.
