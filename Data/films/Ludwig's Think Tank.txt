'''''Ludwig's Think Tank''''' is a television special that aired on the [[Disney Channel]] on [[September 1]], [[1985]]. [[Ludwig Von Drake]] hosts this look at learning. As he notes, everyone can learn something &ndash; even an expert on as many subjects as he is. To prove his point, he uses several educational cartoons.

==Featured cartoons==
*''[[Harold and His Amazing Green Plants]]''
*''[[Scrooge McDuck and Money]]''
*''[[Toot, Whistle, Plunk and Boom]]''
*''[[Winnie the Pooh Discovers the Seasons]]''
*''[[Comets: Time Capsules of the Solar System]]''
*''[[Donald in Mathmagic Land]]''
