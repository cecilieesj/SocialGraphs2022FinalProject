'''''Gulliver Mickey''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[May 29]], [[1934]]. As the title suggests, it is an adaptation of ''{{WikipediaLink|Gulliver's Travels}}'' with Mickey in the title.

==Synopsis==
Mickey tells the orphans how he once landed in Lilliput, was first attacked by its tiny citizens, but later saved them all from a giant spider monster.
