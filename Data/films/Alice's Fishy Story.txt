'''''Alice's Fishy Story''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice steals away from piano practice to go fishing and tells her friends a tale about fishing at the North Pole.
