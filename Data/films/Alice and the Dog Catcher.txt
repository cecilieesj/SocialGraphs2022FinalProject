'''''Alice and the Dog Catcher''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Alice presides over a secret club which proposes to rid the town of dog catchers and free the dogs!
