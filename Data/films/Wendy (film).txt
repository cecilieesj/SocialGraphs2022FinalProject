'''''Wendy''''' is an American drama film written and directed by Benh Zeitlin and star Devin France. It was released on [[February 28]], [[2020]], by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]] through the [[Searchlight Pictures]] label. The film serves as a re-imagining of the story of ''[[Peter Pan (1953 movie)|Peter Pan]]'', from the point of view of [[Wendy Darling]].<ref>[https://www.theadvocate.com/gambit/new_orleans/film/article_b68ae6c8-f12d-11e9-ade1-0bad40543141.html 'Wendy,' new film from 'Beasts of the Southern Wild' director Benh Zeitlin, to open in Feb. 2020 | Film coverage | theadvocate.com]</ref>

==Synopsis==
Wendy finds herself stranded on a mysterious island where aging and time have come unstuck. She must fight to save her family, her freedom and the joyous spirit of youth.

==Production==
In August 2015, it was announced Benh Zeitlin would write and direct the film.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2015/08/beasts-of-the-southern-wild-director-benh-zeitlin-gearing-up-new-film-wendy-261090/|title=‘Beasts Of The Southern Wild’ Director Benh Zeitlin Gearing Up New Film ‘Wendy’|website=IndieWire|first=Kevin|last=Jagernauth|date=August 6, 2015|accessdate=March 21, 2019}}</ref> Production began in March 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2017/03/benh-zeitlin-wendy-beasts-of-souther-wild-new-film-production-photos-1201789239/|title=Benh Zeitlin May Finally Be Shooting His Follow-Up to ‘Beasts of the Southern Wild’ in the Caribbean|website=IndieWire|first=Zack|last=Sharf|date=March 2, 2017|accessdate=March 21, 2019}}</ref>

==Release==
In April 2018, [[Searchlight Pictures]] bought the North American distribution rights to the film.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxsearchlight.com/post/4300/fox-searchlight-pictures-announces-searchlight-television-for-broadcast-cable-and-streaming/|title=FOX SEARCHLIGHT PICTURES ANNOUNCES SEARCHLIGHT TELEVISION FOR BROADCAST, CABLE AND STREAMING|website=[[Fox Searchlight Pictures]]|date=April 11, 2018|accessdate=March 21, 2019}}</ref> It was released on February 28, 2020 by Searchlight Pictures.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/kylebuchanan/status/1184863943199641600|title=Benh Zeitlin is back! It’s been 84 years, etc|website=Twitter|date=October 17, 2019|accessdate=October 17, 2019}}</ref>
