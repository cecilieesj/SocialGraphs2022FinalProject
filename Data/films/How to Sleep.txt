'''''How to Sleep''''' is a [[1953]] animated ''[[Goofy]]'' cartoon from [[Walt Disney Productions]].

==Synopsis==
Goofy (George Geef) falls asleep everywhere — at work, in the car — everywhere except when his head hits the pillow at night.

==Plot==
After a short history of man's eternal quests for sleep (from the stone ages to modern times) as well as a demonstration of what puts man to sleep (like walking, talking, squawking, rocking and socking (getting punched in the face as shown by boxing)) and how man sleeps (like the frog, dog, identical to [[Bruno (Cinderella)|Bruno]] from ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]'', the ostrich, the mole, the bear and the owl), we see G.G. Geef's attempts to sleep despite insomnia. He tries opening a window, using an electric blanket, drinking hot milk and counting sheep (including a stampede), but nothing works at all. He consults a scientist who studies Mr. Geef's sleeping habits and finally finds a cure for Mr. Geef's insomnia.
