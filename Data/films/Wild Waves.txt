'''''Wild Waves''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[December 21]], [[1929]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey rescues Minnie from drowning, then plays the song "Rocked in the Cradle of the Deep" with various beach animals.

==Cast==
*Walt Disney - Mickey Mouse
*Carl Stalling - Mickey's singing voice, Walrus
*[[Marjorie Ralston]] - [[Minnie Mouse]]

==Notes==
*The film's copyright was renewed on [[September 23]], [[1957]].<ref>[https://archive.org/details/catalogofcopyrig3111213li/page/120/mode/1up Catalog of Copyright Entries (1957 renewals)]</ref>
*Some of the animation was reused in ''[[The Castaway]]''.
*This was only one of three shorts to feature [[Marjorie Ralston|Marjorie Norton]] as the voice of Minnie Mouse, before she begged to stop as she was too shy to do any more. Afterwards, starting with ''[[The Cactus Kid]]'', [[Marcellite Garner]] (another Disney employee) would take over as Minnie.
*Footage of this cartoon (using the colorized version) was featured in the "By the Beautiful Sea" segment in the ''[[Disney Sing Along Songs]]'' video ''[[Disney Sing Along Songs: Under the Sea|Under the Sea]]''.
