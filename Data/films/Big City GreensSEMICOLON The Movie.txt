'''''Big City Greens: The Movie''''' is an upcoming animated musical [[Disney Channel Original Movies|film]] based on the [[Disney Channel]] animated series, ''[[Big City Greens]]''. It will premiere on both [[Disney Channel]] and [[Disney+]]. <ref name=":0">https://deadline.com/2022/01/big-city-green-movie-disney-fourth-season-disney-channel-1234917171/</ref>

==Production==
On January 21, 2022, it was announced that a musical spinoff movie for ''[[Big City Greens]]'' has been ordered, along with a fourth season renewal. <ref name=":0" /> On October 6, 2022, the film's title and trailer were revealed at New York Comic Con.

==Trivia==
* This is the 5th Disney Channel Original Movie to be an animated film after ''[[Kim Possible Movie: So the Drama]]'', ''[[The Proud Family Movie]]'', ''[[Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension]]'', and ''[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]''.
* This is the 10th Disney Channel Original Movie based on a Disney Channel series, after [[Jett Jackson: The Movie|''Jett Jackson: The Movie'']], [[The Even Stevens Movie|''The Even Stevens Movie'']], ''[[Kim Possible Movie: So the Drama]]'', ''[[The Proud Family Movie]]'', ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place: The Movie]]'', ''[[The Suite Life Movie]]'', ''[[Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension]]'', ''[[Good Luck Charlie, It's Christmas!]]'', and ''[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]''.
** This is also the tenth feature film based on a Disney Television Animation show, after ''[[DuckTales the Movie: Treasure of the Lost Lamp]]'', ''[[A Goofy Movie]]'', ''[[Doug's 1st Movie]]'', ''[[Recess: School's Out]]'', ''[[Teacher's Pet: The Movie]]'', ''Kim Possible Movie: So the Drama'', ''[[The Proud Family Movie]]'', ''Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension'', and ''[[Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Candace Against the Universe]]''.
*This is the first 2020 April Fools joke from Disney to be real. However, in that joke, it was a road trip to Europe.
