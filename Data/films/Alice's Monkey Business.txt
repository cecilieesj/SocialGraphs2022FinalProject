'''''Alice's Monkey Business''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]''.

==Summary==
While hunting for big game, Alice and Julius find themselves brought into the court of the King Lion.
