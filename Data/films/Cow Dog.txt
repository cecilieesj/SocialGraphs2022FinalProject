'''''Cow Dog''''' is a [[1956]] Disney featurette about the importance of dogs in the cattle.

==Synopsis==
This featurette demonstrates how trained dogs are instrumental in helping round up an outlaw Brahma bull which has been plaguing a ranchers pure-bred Hereford cattle.

==Cast==
*[[Rex Allen]] as Himself - Commentator
*Slim Pickens as Cowhand
*Jay Sisler as Australian Shepherd Owner
*Luann Beach as Horse Owner
