'''''Alice at the Rodeo''''' is a [[1927]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
[[Julius the Cat|Julius]] wins a bucking bronco contest at the rodeo, but when [[Pete]] steals his winning he is forced to take on the Bear to get them back.
