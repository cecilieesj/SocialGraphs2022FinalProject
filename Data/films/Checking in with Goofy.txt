'''''Checking in with Goofy''''' is a promotional web-toon for the [[Disney Cruise Line]] starring [[Goofy]].

==Synopsis==
Goofy checks in with the Disney Cruise Line website to avoid the hassle and stress of other cruises.
==Notes==
*This video is made in the style of the "How To..." Goofy shorts.
*The title is styled as '''Checkin' in with Goofy'''.
*When Goofy is filling in paperwork, he does so betweenÂ [[Roger Radcliffe|Roger]] & [[Anita Radcliffe|Anita]] from ''[[One Hundred and One Dalmatians]]''.
*There is a [[Hidden Mickey]] head on Goofy's computer.
*According to his check-in, Goofy lives on "500 South Buena Vista Street, Burbank, California, 91506", which is where [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] is located.
*Goofy takes his cruise on the ''[[Disney Dream]]'' cruise ship.
