'''''The First Omen''''' is an upcoming American horror film, directed by Arkasha Stevenson. It is a prequel to the [[1976]] film, ''{{WPS|The Omen}}''.

==Cast==
*Nell Tiger as TBA

==Production==
On May 10, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that [[20th Century Studios]] was developing a prequel film to the original from 1976, with Arkasha Stevenson making her feature directorial debut.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/05/the-omen-prequel-the-first-omen-sets-arkasha-stevenson-as-director-for-20th-century-studios-movie-1235020725/ ‘The Omen’ Prequel ‘The First Omen’ Sets Arkasha Stevenson As Director For 20th Century Studios Movie]</ref>

On August 31, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that Nell Tiger was cast in the film in an undisclosed role.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/08/servant-star-nell-tiger-free-omen-prequel-first-omen-20th-century-1235103698/ ‘Servant’ Star Nell Tiger Free To Star In ‘Omen’ Prequel ‘First Omen’ For 20th Century]</ref>
