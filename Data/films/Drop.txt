|music =Nathan Curtis |preceded = ''[[Jing Hua]]''|followed = ''[[Zenith (Short Circuit)|Zenith]]''|producer = Nicholas Russell}}'''Drop''' is an animated short film produced by [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] that was released as a [[Disney+]] exclusive on [[January 24]], [[2020]].

==Synopsis==
A newly-formed raindrop falls to earth for the first time and has an unlikely and heartfelt encounter with a young girl that proves to be uplifting for both.

== Plot ==
Drop, a small water droplet, awakens high up in the sky to greet his new surroundings as well as his fellow water droplets. He suddenly begins to fall and is hit by lightning. Desperate to hang onto something, he lands on a rain gutter and surfs down, hitting things along the way.

Drop lands on the roof of a car and slides down the window where he spots a little girl playing with a toy. The girl takes notice of him and touches the window. Drop is suddenly reabsorbed into the sky and flies back upward. Now happy about his new life, Drop prepares to rain down once again.

In a post credits scene, Drop lands on a high structure of some kind, turns and happily [[Breaking the Fourth Wall|waves at the audience]].

==Cast==
* Michael Herrera as Drop

== Trivia ==
* The girl in the short reuses child [[Moana (character)|Moana]]'s character model.
* The post credit animation was actually the first and only animation test.
* Shortly after its premiere, the short film had to be temporarily removed from Disney+ to be re-edited. According to the director Trent Correy, there have been minor issues in the introduction segment of the short.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B7yuLScBVH7/|title=Trent Correy on Instagram|website=Instagram|date=January 28, 2020}}</ref> It was added back a couple of weeks later.
