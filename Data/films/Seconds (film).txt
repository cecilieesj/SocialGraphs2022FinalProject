'''''Seconds''''' is an upcoming American film based on the graphic novel of the same name by Bryan Lee O’Malley. 

==Production==
On April 29, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reporter that [[Searchlight Pictures]] was developing a ''Seconds'' film based on the graphic novel with Blake Lively making directorial debut, and [[Edgar Wright]] writing and producing.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/blake-lively-directorial-debut-seconds-1235138164/ Blake Lively to Make Film Directorial Debut With Adaptation of Bryan Lee O’Malley Graphic Novel ‘Seconds’ (Exclusive)]</ref>
