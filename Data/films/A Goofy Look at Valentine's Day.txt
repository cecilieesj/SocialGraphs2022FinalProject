'''''A Goofy Look at Valentine's Day''''' is a [[1983]] Disney educational animated short. It is a compilation of classic Disney animation paying tribute to love and Valentine's Day. Intermixed throughout are numerous clips from many different Disney cartoons, as well as many images of Valentine's Day cards.
