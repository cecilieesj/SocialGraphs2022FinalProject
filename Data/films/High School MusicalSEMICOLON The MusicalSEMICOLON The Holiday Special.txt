'''''High School Musical: The Musical: The Holiday Special''''' is a [[Disney+]] original special based on ''[[High School Musical: The Musical: The Series]]''. It was released on Disney+ on Friday, [[December 11]], [[2020]]. The special features the cast of the series performing Christmas, Hanukkah, and New Year’s songs and sharing their favorite holiday memories. Plus, there's a sneak peek performance from the second season. All show regulars appear in the holiday special, singing new renditions of classic holiday songs and sharing their favorite childhood holiday memories, stories about their best and most embarrassing presents, their family traditions, photos, and New Year's resolutions.
