'''''Alice Rattled by Rats''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
Julius is put in charge of keeping the house free from rats. But when he falls into a still and gets drunk, the rats soon begin to take over.
