}}'''''Alice Plays Cupid''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
While serving as a lifeguard, [[Julius the Cat|Julius]] falls in love and then saves the life of a cute female cat. Alice helps out by driving the car they eventually elope in.
