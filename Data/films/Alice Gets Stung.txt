'''''Alice Gets Stung''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent. This was the last Alice Comedies film in which [[Virginia Davis]] played the role of Alice.

==Summary==
Julius goes rabbit hunting while Alice goes bear hunting.
