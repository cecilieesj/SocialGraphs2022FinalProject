'''''Polar Bear''''' is a [[Disneynature]] documentary film, centered around polar bears in the Arctic.

The film was released on [[Disney+]] on [[April 22]], [[2022]], accompanied by a feature-length documentary on its production titled ''Bear Witness''.

==Summary==
The film follows the story of a new mother whose memories of her own youth prepare her to navigate motherhood in the increasingly challenging world that polar bears face today.

==Trivia==
*This film marks the first Disneynature film to be rated PG by the MPA, unlike all of the previous Disneynature films, which were rated G.
*This film marks the first Disneynature film to be produced in a 2:39:1 aspect ratio since ''[[Oceans]]''.
*While there was another Polar Bear movie called Arctic Tale, this one had a different storyline and different issues.
