'''Swiss Family Robinson''' is an upcoming live-action remake of the [[Swiss Family Robinson|1960 adventure film of the same name]]. It's made for [[Disney+]] and in development at [[Walt Disney Pictures]] and [[Mandeville Films]].

==Production==
===Development===
On July 2005, it was reported that studio veteran Lindsay Lohan was being considered for a role.
Production on the remake never began, and the film was believed to be shelved until early 2009, when it was still in the works, and had been renamed ''The Robinsons''.
