}}'''''The Ol' Swimmin' Hole''''' (also called '''''The Ol' Swimmin' 'Ole''''') is an ''[[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit (series)|Oswald the Lucky Rabbit]]'' animated short released on [[February 6]], [[1928]]. Dave Bossert, the author of the book ''[[Oswald the Lucky Rabbit: The Search for the Lost Disney Cartoons]]'', has reported that the short has been found and uploaded an image of the short to [https://www.instagram.com/p/BSJtt2XBNWD/ his instagram]. 
The short has been found in a film print in the Royal Belgian Film Archive, with Disney having a deal to get a 4K scan of the print.

==Synopsis==
A pastoral outing as Oswald and his friends take in the delights on a swimming hole.
