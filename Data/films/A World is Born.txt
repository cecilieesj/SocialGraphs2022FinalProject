==Synopsis==
Narrated by Holling C. Holling and set to an interpretation of Igor Stravinsky's ''[[The Rite of Spring|Rite of Spring]]'', taken from ''[[Fantasia]]'', this film depicts the first two billion years of the earth's existence, showing its early convulsions and upheavals and the first prehistoric monsters who inhabited it. The music is played by the Philadelphia Orchestra and conducted by [[Leopold Stokowski]].
