'''''Out on a Limb''''' is a [[1950]] [[Donald Duck|''Donald Duck'']] and [[Chip and Dale|''Chip 'n' Dale'']] animated short.

==Synopsis==
Donald is pruning Chip 'n' Dale's tree home. He doesn't want to chase them away like he usually does; just to have some fun with them which, of course, backfires.

==Characters==
*[[Donald Duck]] (voiced by [[Clarence Nash]])
*[[Chip and Dale|Chip 'n' Dale]] (voiced by [[Jimmy MacDonald]] and [[Dessie Flynn]])
