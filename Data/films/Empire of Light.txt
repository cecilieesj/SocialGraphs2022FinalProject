'''''Empire of Light''''' s an upcoming romantic drama film written and directed by Sam Mendes.

==Synopsis==
A love story that takes place in an English coastal cinema during the 1980s.

==Cast==
*[[Olivia Colman]] as Hilary Small
*Micheal Ward as Stephen
*[[Colin Firth]] as Mr. Ellis
*[[Toby Jones]] as Norman
*Tom Brooke as Neil
*Tanya Moodie as Delia
*Hannah Onslow as Janine
*Crystal Clarke as Ruby
*Monica Dolan as Rosemary Bates

==Production==
It was announced in April 2021 that Sam Mendes had set his next film, which he will write and direct all by himself this time around, at Searchlight Pictures. [[Olivia Colman]] was in talks to star in the film and Roger Deakins was announced to serve as cinematographer. Colman would be confirmed in July, with Micheal Ward also joining the cast. In December, [[Colin Firth]], [[Toby Jones]], Crystal Clarke and Tanya Moodie were added to the cast.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/12/empire-of-light-adds-colin-firth-toby-jones-crystal-clarke-tanya-moodie-sam-mendes-searchlight-pictures-1234900001/ ‘Empire Of Light’: Colin Firth, Toby Jones, Crystal Clarke & Tanya Moodie Join Sam Mendes’ Searchlight Pictures Drama]</ref>
