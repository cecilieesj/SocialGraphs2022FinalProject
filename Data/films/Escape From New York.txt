'''''Escape From New York''''' is an upcoming American science fiction action film, directed by Radio Silence, and serves as a reboot of the [[1981]] film of the same name. It is produced by [[20th Century Studios]].

==Production==
On November 17, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that a reboot of ''Escape From New York'' is in development, with the team Radio Silence directing. John Carpenter, the director of the original film serves as an executive producer.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/11/radio-silence-escape-from-new-york-pic-1235175637/ Radio Silence To Direct New ‘Escape From New York’ Movie For 20th Century Studios]</ref>
