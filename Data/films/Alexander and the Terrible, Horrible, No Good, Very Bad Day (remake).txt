'''''Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day''''' is an upcoming American film based on the best-selling children’s book by Judith Viorst. It is a remake of the [[Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day|2014 film]].

==Cast==
*Eva Longoria as Kelly Cooper
*[[George Lopez]] as Alexander Cooper's grandfather

==Production==
On October 4, 2022, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that [[Disney+]] was developing a remake of ''Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day'' with Eva Longoria and [[George Lopez]] cast in the film.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/eva-longoria-george-lopez-alexander-terrible-horrible-movie-1235233162/ Eva Longoria, George Lopez to Star in New ‘Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day’ Movie (Exclusive)]</ref>
