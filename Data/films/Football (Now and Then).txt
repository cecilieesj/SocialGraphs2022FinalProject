'''''Football (Now and Then)''''' is a Disney animated short that was originally released on [[October 2]], [[1953]].

==Synopsis==
An old-timer tells his grandson that old-time football players could take a modern team, so we see a game with just that match-up: Bygone U. vs. Present State. Even the stadium, fans, and press are modern vs. old-time. The game is close, and fiercely fought.
