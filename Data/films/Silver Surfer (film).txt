'''''Silver Surfer''''' is an upcoming American television special based on the [[Marvel Comics]] character of the [[Silver Surfer|same name]]. It is expected to be part of Phase Six or Seven of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. It will be produced by [[Marvel Studios]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]]. It is the fourth television special for [[Disney+]].

==Production==
On December 20, 2018, ''The Hollywood Reporter'' reported that Adam McKay was interested in making a ''Silver Surfer'' film set in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]], due to both the potential visuals and the character's comic book story.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/silver-surfer-movie-adam-mckay-wants-direct-1171299 ‘Vice’ Director Adam McKay Is Interested in a ‘Silver Surfer’ Movie]</ref> Several weeks later, McKay had already discussed the idea with Marvel and said the studio was excited about it. He reiterated his own excitement for the project, and said he might return to it "in a couple months" after he finished promoting his film, ''Vice'' (2018).<ref>[https://screenrant.com/silver-surfer-movie-adam-mckay-marvel/ Adam McKay Has Discussed Directing Silver Surfer Movie With Marvel]</ref>

On September 14, 2019, after the acquisition of [[20th Century Studios]], it was reported that a ''Silver Surfer'' film was officially in development at [[Marvel Studios]].<ref>[https://thegww.com/exclusive-silver-surfer-movie-in-the-works-at-marvel-studios/ Exclusive: 'Silver Surfer' Movie in the Works at Marvel Studios]</ref>

On April 23, 2021, McKay said he was still interested in making the film, but he was not able to when he spoke to Marvel about it due to related work already being done at the studio for the [[Fantastic Four]] franchise. He said that one of the reasons he wanted to make the film was because he felt he could easily turn it into an allegory for environmental issues.<ref>[https://screenrant.com/mcu-silver-surfer-movie-adam-mckay-interest-direct/ Adam McKay Still Interested In Directing MCU Silver Surfer Movie]</ref>

On October 22, 2022, it was reported that the project would be redeveloped as a television special for [[Disney+]].<ref>[https://www.thecosmiccircus.com/what-i-heard-marvels-silver-surfer-to-be-a-disney-special/ What I Heard: Marvel’s Silver Surfer to Be a Disney+ Special]</ref>
