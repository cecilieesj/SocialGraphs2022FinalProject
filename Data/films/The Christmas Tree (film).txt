==Synopsis==
A story about a forming friendship between an elderly nun, Sister Anthony (Julie Harris), and [[New York City]]'s Rockefeller Center's head gardener Richard Reilly (Andrew McCarthy), who wants to fell a tree she's been growing for decades and move it to Rockefeller Center for Christmas display.
