'''''Strange World''''' (formerly titled '''''Searcher Clade''''') is an  American animated adventure film produced by [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]]. It was released on [[November 23]], [[2022]] and is the 61st animated feature in the [[Disney Animated Canon]]. The film is directed by [[Don Hall]] and written/co-directed by Qui Nguyen.

==Synopsis==
The original action-adventure journeys deep into an uncharted and treacherous land where fantastical creatures await the legendary Clades, a family of explorers whose differences threaten to topple their latest ‚Äî and by far ‚Äî most crucial mission.

==Plot==
Jaeger Clade and his son Searcher are adventurers who brave the wilderness to explore new worlds. While trying to traverse a mountain that surrounds the land of Avalonia, Searcher discovers a plant that gives off energy. Jaeger insists on continuing the quest, but Searcher and the rest of the expedition team believe that the new plant, dubbed Pando, is good enough. Jaeger angrily continues his mission alone. 25 years later, Searcher has made a name for himself by harvesting Pando and turning it into a fuel resource for Avalonia. He is married to fellow harvester Meridian and they have a son named Ethan who gets embarrassed whenever Searcher tries talking to his crush Diazo.

One day, Callisto Mal, the leader of Avalonia, shows up in a giant flying ship called the Venture. She informs the Clades that the Pando is losing its power and that they need Searcher to help find the cause. He joins a new expedition team and they travel into a giant sinkhole where giant roots of Pando have been located. On the way down, Meridian tries catching up in her crop duster after Ethan and their dog Legend have snuck aboard the ship. They are attacked by wyvern-like creatures and continue going down until they break through sensitive crust and end up in a subterranean world. During the crash, Searcher and Legend are separated from the group.

While chasing off a creature called Splat, Searcher and Legend are rescued by Jaeger who has been living underground all these years. He reveals that he has tried taking advantage of traversing under the mountain, but is blocked off by an acidic ocean that he cannot cross. He decides to help look for the Venture as a means to cross it. Meanwhile the group tries to prepare the ship, but Ethan sneaks away to find Searcher and discovers Splat who tries taking him to his father. They end up getting attacked by creatures called Reapers, but reunite with Searcher and Jaeger. They are soon rescued by Meridian and Callisto who outrun the creatures and return to the Venture.

Searcher insists on completing their mission while Jaeger wants to continue journeying across the Strange World. Callisto agrees to help fix the ship so that they can continue traveling. During this time, Ethan realizes that Searcher and Jaeger do not see eye-to-eye and he becomes frustrated with the two of them and their opposing views. After another wild encounter, Searcher and Jaeger finally have a heart-to-heart talk with one another and realize that they do respect each other's goals in life. They finally encounter a root cluster of where the Pando is as it is being attacked by the creatures. Believing that the Pando is fighting back, they decide to try and help it.

Searcher and Jaeger have another argument that ends with Ethan angrily leaving. Searcher follows him aboard a small flying vehicle, but as they are making up, they realize that they are floating above an ocean and in front of a giant eye with a mountain on top of it. The two realize that Avalonia has been on the back of a giant creature and that they have been traveling inside its body, meaning that the Pando is an illness that needs to be destroyed. They head back to inform everyone about it, but they are in disbelief with Jaeger angrily leaving to see for himself and Callisto and the team locking up the Clades to prevent them from stopping their mission. Luckily, Legend and Splat release the family.

Searcher and Ethan head to the root cluster, which is actually the heart, while Meridian takes over the ship and finally convinces Callisto. Jaeger, after having a change of heart, returns and they break through the Pando. Creatures suddenly appear and bring the heart back to life, saving the land, but losing the Pando power source. Searcher and Ethan show everyone the giant creature which is actually a turtle. One year later, Ethan is in a relationship with Diazo as they and their friends collect resources from the Strange World, Avalonia has shifted from Pando energy to wind turbines, Jaeger revisits his ex-wife who has remarried, and Searcher and Jaeger's relationship has improved.

===Development===
On [[April 3]], [[2020]], an animated feature was announced for a late 2022 release.<ref>[https://whatsondisneyplus.com/walt-disney-studios-announces-updated-release-schedule/ Walt Disney Studios Announces Updated Release Schedule]</ref> On [[May 31]], [[2021]], auditions began for the role of Ethan with production set to commence on [[July 1]].<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2021/07/05/details-released-for-next-walt-disney-animation-studios-film/ Details Released for Next Walt Disney Animation Studios Film]</ref> In August, information on the film, alongside its working title, ''Searcher Clade'' was revealed.<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2021/08/22/title-confirmed-for-2022-animated-disney-film/ Title Confirmed for 2022 Animated Disney Film]</ref> On [[December 8]], 2021, The official logo for the film, with the updated title of ''Strange World'', was published on Disney's press platform.<ref name=":0" /> A day later, the film was formally announced by Disney, along with the reveal of some concept art.<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2021/12/09/walt-disney-animation-studios-announces-new-movie-strange-world/ Walt Disney Animation Studios Announces New Movie ‚ÄòStrange World‚Äô]</ref> On [[June 6]], [[2022]], a teaser trailer for the film was released along with the reveal of Jake Gyllenhaal as the voice of the main protagonist, Searcher Clade.<ref name="Gyllenhaal">[https://thedisinsider.com/2022/06/06/jake-gyllenhaal-joins-disneys-strange-world-teaser-and-poster-released/ Jake Gyllenhaal Joins Disney‚Äôs ‚ÄòStrange World‚Äô; Teaser and Poster Released]</ref> Eleven days later on [[June 17]], the rest of the main cast for the film was announced at France's '''Annecy Animation Festival''' with [[Jaboukie Young-White]] as Ethan Clade, Gabrielle Union as Meridian Clade, Lucy Liu as Callisto Mal and Dennis Quaid as Jaeger Clade.<ref name="FullCast">[https://thedisinsider.com/2022/06/17/strange-world-rounds-out-cast/ ‚ÄòStrange World‚Äô Rounds Out Cast]</ref>

==Trivia==
*This is the most recent film in the Disney Animated Canon to be helmed by a single director, with the last film being ''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]'' ten years prior in [[2012]].
*This is the second Disney Animated Canon film to be released on November 23, after ''[[Moana (film)|Moana]]''.
*[[File:Strange World easter egg Encanto.jpg|thumb|250px|The Clade Ship during ''Encanto'' credits]] On December 8, 2021, the official title was revealed to be ''Strange World'' after previously going under the working title of ''Searcher Clade''.<ref name=":0">[https://disneyplusbrasil.com.br/logo-de-strange-world-proxima-animacao-da-disney-e-divulgada-confira/ Logo for 'Strange World', Disney's next animation, is released; check out!]</ref>
*The Clades' ship from the film appeared as an Easter egg during the end credits of ''[[Encanto]]'', similar to the [[Wreck-It Ralph (character)|Ralph]] Easter egg during Moana's end credits and the butterflies from ''Encanto'' during ''[[Raya and the Last Dragon]]''<nowiki/>'s end credits.
*This is the first film in the Disney Animated Canon to be produced in a 2:39:1 aspect ratio since ''Raya and the Last Dragon''.
*The film was originally set for a theatrical release in [[France]], but it was later moved to a Disney+ release as an original film due to the country's regulations on film release windows, which Disney has opposed.<ref>[https://dapsmagic.com/2022/06/disney-pulls-release-of-strange-world-from-theaters-in-france-and-french-exhibitors-arent-happy/ Disney Pulls Release Of Strange World From Theaters In France And French Exhibitors Aren‚Äôt Happy]</ref> Locally, this makes it the first film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios to go directly to streaming.
*One type of creature in this film is obviously based on a type of extinct [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panarthropoda panarthropod] known as ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hallucigenia Hallucigenia]''.
**Two other creatures are based on two types of {{WikipediaLink|dinosaur}}s known as ''{{WikipediaLink|Alamosaurus}}'' and ''{{WikipediaLink|Haopterus}}''.
***With that being said, the creatures swinging/sticking to trees are based on a sea {{WikipediaLink|Asteroidea}} known as ''{{WikipediaLink|Linckia laevigata}}''.
*This is the first Disney Animated Canon film to have an openly gay main character.<ref>[https://www.animationmagazine.net/events/disney-offers-exclusive-looks-at-strange-world-baymax-zootopia-at-annecy/ Disney Offers Exclusive Looks at ‚ÄòStrange World,‚Äô ‚ÄòBaymax!‚Äô & ‚ÄòZootopia+‚Äô at Annecy]</ref>
**Because of this, the movie is banned in China, Turkey, Malaysia, Indonesia, Pakistan, Vietnam, Tanzania, Uganda, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, Nepal and Bangladesh. <ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/11/box-office-wakanda-forever-strange-world-disney-bob-iger-thanksgiving-1235179433/ Disney Will Dominate the Thanksgiving Box Office With 'Wakanda Forever' and 'Strange World']</ref>.
*Both actors [[Jake Gyllenhaal|Jake Gyllenhaa]]<nowiki/>l and [[Dennis Quaid]] had previously co-starred in the [[2004]] film ''The Day After Tomorrow''.
*According to a recent assumption, reference points for the story include slight and imaginable inspiration from {{WikipediaLink|Jules Verne}} and {{WikipediaLink|H. G. Wells}} stories.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-features/disney-strange-world-trailer-concept-art-1235223223/ Behind Disney‚Äôs Jules Verne-Inspired ‚ÄòStrange World‚Äô]</ref>

*This is [[Dennis Quaid]]'s third Disney film after ''[[The Parent Trap (1998 film)|The Parent Trap]]'' and ''[[The Rookie]]''.
*This is [[Jaboukie Young-White]]'s second Disney film after ''[[Ralph Breaks the Internet]]''.
