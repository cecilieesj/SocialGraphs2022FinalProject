'''''Everybody's Talking About Jamie''''' is an LGBT musical drama film directed by Jonathan Butterell and written by Tom MacRae. The film is based upon the musical of the same name, inspired by the real-life story of Jamie Campbell. It stars Max Harwood, Sarah Lancashire, Lauren Patel, Shobna Gulati, [[Ralph Ineson]], Adeel Akhtar, Samuel Bottomley, with Sharon Horgan, and [[Richard E. Grant]].

''Everybody's Talking About Jamie'' was released by Amazon Studios on [[September 17]], [[2021]] on the Prime Video streaming service. This is Regency Enterprises' first film under the [[20th Century Studios]] banner.

==Release==
The film was released by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]] through [[20th Century Studios]] banner.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://deadline.com/2020/06/the-one-and-only-ivan-disney-release-walt-disney-studios-release-date-change-1202957814/|title= ‘The One And Only Ivan’ Heads To Disney+; ‘Beatles: Get Back’ Moves To 2021 & More: Disney Release Date Changes|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Anthony|last=D’Alessandro|date=June 12, 2020|accessdate=June 12, 2020}}</ref> It was previously scheduled to be released on 23 October 2020,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/08/emma-stone-cruella-amy-adams-woman-in-the-window-disney-fox-release-date-changes-1202677451/|title=Amy Adams ‘Woman In The Window’ Will Now Open In Early Summer, ‘Cruella’ Moves To 2021|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Anthony|last=D'Alessandro|date=August 20, 2019|accessdate=August 20, 2019}}</ref> but was postponed to January 21, 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.broadway.com/buzz/199441/everybodys-talking-about-jamie-movie-musical-shifts-release-date-to-january-2021/|title=''Everybody's Talking About Jamie'' Movie Musical Shifts Release Date to January 2021|last=Sullivsn|first=Lindsey|work=[[John Gore Organization|Broadway.com]]|date=June 12, 2020|accessdate=August 10, 2020}}</ref>. On October 11, 2020, the film was delayed again to February 26, 2021, while the first trailer and poster were released. However, in December 2020, it was then taken off the release schedule.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Disney delays Everybody's Talking About Jamie musical film release {{!}} WhatsOnStage|url=https://www.whatsonstage.com/london-theatre/news/disney-delays-everybodys-talking-jamie-musical-film_53041.html|access-date=December 16, 2020|website=www.whatsonstage.com}}</ref> In May 2021, it was announced that Amazon Studios had acquired the worldwide distribution rights for the film and will release it on the Prime Video streaming service on September 17, 2021, with the exception of China, where it will be released theatrically.
