|image = File:TheLondonConnection.jpg}}'''''The London Connection''''' is a [[1979]] film directed by Robert Clouse. It stars Jeffrey Byron and Larry Cedar. It was renamed '''''The Omega Connection''''' when it aired on TV.

==Plot==
It's about a young American government agent who witnesses the kidnapping of a noted European scientist in [[London]]. The only item left behind in this startling incident is a gold ring, inscribed with an address, that leads the youth into a massive conspiracy of international proportions.
