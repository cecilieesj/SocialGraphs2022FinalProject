'''''Shang-Chi 2''''' is a placeholder title for an upcoming superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero [[Shang-Chi|of the same name]]. The film is a sequel to ''[[Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings]]''. It is expected to be part of Phase Six or Seven of the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. [[Destin Daniel Cretton]] is returning to direct and write. It will be produced by [[Marvel Studios]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]].<ref name="Deadline">[https://deadline.com/2021/12/destin-daniel-cretton-marvel-hulu-onyx-collective-deal-shang-chi-sequel-1234885502/ Destin Daniel Cretton Inks Overall Deal With Marvel Studios & Hulu’s Onyx Collective; Set For Disney+ MCU Series & ‘Shang-Chi’ Sequel]</ref>

==Cast==
*[[Simu Liu]] as [[Shang-Chi]]

==Production==
On December 6, 2021, ''Deadline'' reported that a second ''Shang-Chi'' film was in development with [[Destin Daniel Cretton]] set to direct and write. It was also reported that [[Simu Liu]] would reprise his [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] role of [[Shang-Chi]] in the film.<ref name="Deadline"/>
