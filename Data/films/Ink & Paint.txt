'''Ink & Paint''' is an upcoming documentary mini-series set for release on [[Disney+]].<ref>[https://deadline.com/2018/10/abc-studios-alternative-division-best-bite-ever-ink-paintabc-series-disney-streaming-service-fernando-hernandez-1202474547/ ABC Studios Ramps Up Alternative Division, Sets Cooking Pilot At ABC & Series ‘Ink & Paint’ At Disney OTT Service]</ref> Based on the non-fiction book of the same title by Mindy Johnson, ''Ink & Paint'' tells the story of women's role in Disney animation history "from ''[[Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs]]'' to ''[[Coco]]''" in eight episodes.

However, as of September 2022, there is no update on the series' progress.
