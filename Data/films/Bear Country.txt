'''''Bear Country''''' is a [[1953]] American short documentary film directed by [[James Algar]]. It won an Academy Award at the 26th Academy Awards in [[1954]] for Best Short Subject (Two-Reel). The film was produced by [[Walt Disney]] as part of the [[True-Life Adventures|''True-Life Adventure'']] series of nature documentaries.
