'''''Jiminy Cricket: Storyteller''''' is a television special that aired on the [[Disney Channel]] on [[January 1]], [[1986]]. It is an updated version of the ''[[Walt Disney anthology series|Disneyland]]'' episode ''[[From Aesop to Hans Christian Andersen]]''. It features [[Jiminy Cricket]] playing emcee and introducing some cartoons based on stories by Aesop, [[the Brothers Grimm]], [[Hans Christian Andersen]] and others. It was released on home video internationally as ''Jiminy Cricket's Fabulous Fables, Fairy Tales and Other Wonderful Stories''.

==Featured cartoons==
*''[[The Tortoise and the Hare]]''
*''[[The Grasshopper and the Ants]]''
*''[[Old King Cole]]''
*''[[Gulliver Mickey]]''
*''[[Brave Little Tailor]]''
*''[[The Pied Piper]]''
*''[[The Ugly Duckling (1939 film)|The Ugly Duckling]]''
*''[[Little Hiawatha]]''
*''[[Elmer Elephant]]''
