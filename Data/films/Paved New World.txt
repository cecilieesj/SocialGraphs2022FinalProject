'''''Paved New World''''' is Disney live-action film produced by [[Walt Disney Studios]] and distributed by [[Walt Disney Pictures]]. It was directed by Alexander Garcia and written by Kip Koenig and Scott Marcano.

==Synopsis==
The film takes place in summer [[1995]]. YouTube star and skateboarder Andy Schrock stars as a hometown skateboarding hero named Gibby caught in the middle of a mid-life crisis. It centers on two teens who have just graduated from high school, heading off in different directions in life. Before they can embark on their new journeys, they take their boards on a final trek across town to witness Gibby attempt to skate “unskateable” Widow Maker Mountain on his skateboard, as his last attempt to prove he is still the idol he once was.<ref>[https://variety.com/2017/film/news/claudia-lee-skateboarding-movie-paved-new-world-exclusive-1202525729/ Claudia Lee Joins Skateboarding Movie ‘Paved New World’ (EXCLUSIVE) - Variety]</ref>
