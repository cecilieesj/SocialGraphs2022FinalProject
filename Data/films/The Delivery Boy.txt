'''''The Delivery Boy''''' is a ''[[Mickey Mouse]]'' short that was released on [[June 13]], [[1931]].

==Synopsis==
Mickey's delivering musical instruments, but stops his wagon to take them out and "test" them with [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]] - a project that involves a beehive, a bucket of tar, and dynamite, and leaves her farm and most of the instruments in ruins, and Mickey's mule without his hair.
