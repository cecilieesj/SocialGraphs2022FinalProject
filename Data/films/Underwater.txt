'''''Underwater''''' is a [[2020]] American science fiction thriller film directed by William Eubank, and written by Brian Duffield and Adam Coaz. The film stars [[Kristen Stewart]], Vincent Cassel, Jessica Henwick, John Gallagher Jr., Mamoudou Athie, and [[T.J. Miller]].

==Plot==
An underwater crew must swim to safety after an earthquake destroys their subterranean laboratory.

==Cast==
*[[Kristen Stewart]] as Norah Price

*Jessica Henwick as Emily
*John Gallagher Jr. as Liam Smith
*Mamoudou Athie as Rodrigo
*[[T.J. Miller]] as Paul
*Gunner Wright as Lee

==Trivia==
*This is the last film to be released under the name 20th Century Fox, due to Disney renaming the studio "20th Century Studios" as of [[January 17]], 2020; a week after the film's release.
**In additional, this is the last film to be distributed by 20th Century Fox's distribution banner before it fully merged into [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]].
*This also mark as the final home video release to be distributed by [[20th Century Studios Home Entertainment|20th Century Fox Home Entertainment]] before it was handed over to [[Walt Disney Studios Home Entertainment]] as their future home video distributor using 20th Century Studios as their in-name label/banner.
*This is the first Disney movie of 2020.
