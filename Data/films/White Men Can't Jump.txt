'''''White Men Can't Jump''''' is an upcoming American sports film. It is a remake of the [[1992]] film of the same name. 

==Cast==
*Jack Harlow as TBA
*Sinqua Walls as Kamal 

==Production==
On January 17, 2017, Kenya Barris was developing a ''White Men Can't Jump'' remake with NBA star Blake Griffin and NFL player Ryan Kalil producing. Nothing came up about the project until November of 2021, when Calmatic was revealed to be the director.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/11/white-men-cant-jump-20th-century-calmatic-1234882192/ ‘White Men Can’t Jump’ Remake At 20th Century Sets Calmatic As Director]</ref> 

On March 3, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that rapper Jack Harlow joined the cast in the lead role.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/03/jack-harlow-white-men-cant-jump-remake-woody-harrelson-nail-tech-20th-century-1234969619/ Jack Harlow To Star in 20th Century’s ‘White Men Can’t Jump’ Reboot Off His First Ever Screen Audition]</ref>

On April 13, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that Sinqua Walls joined the cast as Kamal, who was portrayed by Wesley Snipes in the original film.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2022/04/white-men-cant-jump-reboot-adds-sinqua-walls-1235001573/ ‘White Men Can’t Jump’: Sinqua Walls Joins 20th Century Studios Reboot]</ref>

Filming is expected to begin in May 2022.<ref>[https://thedisinsider.com/2022/04/12/20th-centurys-white-men-cant-jump-remake-adds-sinqua-walls-to-the-team-will-film-next-month/ 20th Century’s ‘White Men Can’t Jump’ Remake Adds Sinqua Walls To The Team, Will Film Next Month]</ref>
