'''''Alice the Piper''''' is an animated short film that is part of the [[Alice Comedies]] that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1924]]. It was in black and white and silent.

==Summary==
A take on the story of "The Pied Piper on Hamelin" as Alice and Julius are hired to rid the town of rats.
