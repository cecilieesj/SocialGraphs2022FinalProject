'''''The Aristocats''''' is an upcoming American live-action/CGI-animated romantic musical comedy adaptation of [[Walt Disney]]'s [[1970]] animated feature film [[The Aristocats|of the same name]]. A release date has yet to be set.<ref name="Deadline">[https://deadline.com/2022/01/live-action-aristocats-pic-disney-1234916566/ Live-Action ‘Aristocats’ Pic In Development At Disney]</ref>

==Production==
On January 20, 2022, ''Deadline'' reported that a live-action adaptation of ''[[The Aristocats]]'' is in development, yet to-be-determined if it's a theatrical release or a [[Disney+]] release.<ref name="Deadline" />
