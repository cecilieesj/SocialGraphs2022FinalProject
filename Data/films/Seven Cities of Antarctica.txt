'''''Seven Cities of Antarctica''''' is a documentary film that originally released to theaters by [[Buena Vista Distribution]] on [[December 25]], [[1958]]. It won a 1959 BAFTA Award for Best Documentary. The film focuses on Operation "Deep Freeze" - the exploration of Antarctica and the establishment of seven bases by the United States Navy.
