'''''Alice in the Jungle''''' is an animated short film that is part of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' that were released by Winkler Pictures in [[1925]]. It was in black and white and silent. This was [[Virginia Davis]]' final official role as Alice in the series.

==Synopsis==
Alice and Julius on safari in Africa; Alice gets chased by a lion after playing with a lion cub and Julius (as usual) has to come in and rescue her.
