'''''Alice the Lumberjack''''' is a [[1926]] short film by the Walt Disney Studio and Winkler Pictures. It is part of the [[Alice Comedies]].

==Summary==
The regular story of Pete trying to kidnap Alice, this time as she and Julius work as lumberjacks on the river.
