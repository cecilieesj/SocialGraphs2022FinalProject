'''''True Love''''' is an upcoming American science-fiction film written, directed and produced by Gareth Edwards. It is scheduled to be released on [[October 6]], [[2023]].

==Production==
On February 6, 2020, ''Variety'' reported that Gareth Edwards had been hired to direct the film at New Regency.<ref>[https://variety.com/2020/film/news/gareth-edwards-rogue-one-new-regency-1203495962/ ‘Rogue One’ Director Gareth Edwards Sets Sci-Fi Film at New Regency (EXCLUSIVE)]</ref>

On May 5, 2021, ''Deadline'' reported that John David Washington had been cast in the lead role, with other cast members the following month.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/05/john-david-washington-gareth-edwards-true-love-new-regency-1234750641/ John David Washington To Star In Gareth Edwards Next Film ‘True Love’ At New Regency]</ref><ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/06/danny-mcbride-gemma-chan-benedict-wong-john-david-washington-gareth-edwards-new-regency-1234781073/ Danny McBride, Gemma Chan & Benedict Wong Eyed To Join John David Washington In Gareth Edwards’ Next Film At New Regency]</ref>

On January 17, 2022, ''The Wrap'' reported that filming had officially began.<ref>[https://www.thewrap.com/gareth-edwards-greig-fraser-new-movie-tr Gareth Edwards to Reunite With ‘Rogue One’ Cinematographer Greig Fraser on New Film ‘True Love’]</ref>
